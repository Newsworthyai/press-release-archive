# Wendt Partners Announces Major Q3 Client Wins, Promotions and Global Expansion Activities

NEW YORK - (NewMediaWire) - October 1, 2024 - Wendt Partners, a HubSpot Elite Solutions Partner headquartered in New York, announced today the conclusion of Q3 with a series of major client wins, significant staff promotions, and further execution of the firm’s global growth strategy.

About Wendt Partners:

 Wendt Partners is a HubSpot Elite Solutions Partner headquartered in New York City with offices in Toronto, London, and Melbourne. Specializing in the implementation of the HubSpot CRM platform, Wendt Partners works with sophisticated B2B companies across various verticals, including industrial/manufacturing, professional services, technology, and more. The firm’s services encompass Platform Solutions, Technical Solutions, Enablement Solutions, Digital Solutions, and Growth Solutions. Wendt Partners is SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and HIPAA compliant.
