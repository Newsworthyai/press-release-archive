# Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex joins Orlando CityPASS offering an extraordinary space adventure

(NewMediaWire) - October 30, 2024 - Orlando, FL — CityPASS®, the renowned seller of discounted ticket packages to top attractions in North America, announces the addition of the Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex to the Orlando CityPASS® program. This exciting new partnership offers travelers a unique opportunity to explore the wonders of space exploration alongside other premier attractions in the Orlando area.

With the Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex joining the Orlando CityPASS lineup, visitors can enjoy a comprehensive, curated experience of the area’s best attractions that will now include a journey through space history and innovation. As an integral part of NASA and the United States’ advancement in space technology, Kennedy Space Center is one of the ten field centers of NASA and has been functioning as a primary launch center of human spaceflight and is the only place in the U.S. where you can see rocket and space shuttle launches.

The complex offers a variety of immersive exhibits, such as the Space Shuttle Atlantis, the Space Shuttle Launch Experience, the Apollo/Saturn V Center, IMAX® space films and interactive displays that celebrate the achievements of NASA and the space program.

"We are thrilled to expand our Orlando CityPASS offerings to include the Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex," said Megan Allen, CEO of City Pass Inc. "This partnership allows us to offer an extraordinary mix of attractions that cater to a wide range of interests, from thrilling theme parks to awe-inspiring space exhibits. It's a perfect addition for families and travelers looking to make the most of their Orlando experience while saving money."

The Orlando CityPASS program provides an incredible degree of flexibility, customization and savings. Travelers can buy discounted tickets to one attraction or a combination of attractions to create a individualized, easy-to-use ticket bundle delivered directly to their email.

Attraction options include:

* Walt Disney World Resort
* Universal Orlando Resort
* SeaWorld Orlando
* LEGOLAND Florida Resort
* NEW Kennedy Space Center Complex

Orlando CityPASS ticket prices and savings vary, depending on which parks and ticket types are chosen, and for how many days. Find more information at Orlando CityPASS.

Tickets can be purchased online at CityPASS.com, arriving moments later via email. Customers can use the My CityPASS app toconveniently store their tickets, make reservations at those attractions that require or recommend them, and find entry instructions, special offers and upgrades, and more to plan their visits.

And don't worry, should your travel plans change, City Pass Inc. offers a risk-free 365-day return policy on any nonactivated tickets.
