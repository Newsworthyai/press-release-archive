# Blackwell 3D Welcomes Experienced M&A Specialist to Its Board of Directors

DUBAI - (NewMediaWire) - December 31, 2024 - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce the appointment of Mahesh Krishnamurthy to its Board of Directors. With an exceptional career as a practicing Chartered Accountant and extensive expertise in M&A, structured debt, audit and taxation, private equity, and SPAC transactions, Mahesh brings unparalleled insights and value to the Company.

 Mahesh is a practicing Chartered Accountant with decades of experience across key financial domains, specializing in M&A and structured debt. A recognized leader in the renewable energy and green technologies space, he has played a pivotal role in numerous high-profile transactions and strategic initiatives.

 Mahesh’s active involvement in renewable and green technologies positions him as a visionary in advancing sustainable solutions. His strategic acumen in navigating complex financial landscapes and emerging industries will play a pivotal role in Blackwell 3D’s continued growth and innovation.

 “Mahesh’s appointment to our board underscores our commitment to excellence and sustainability,” stated Mohammedsaif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell 3D. “His deep knowledge of structured finance, combined with his leadership in green technologies, aligns seamlessly with our strategic objectives. We are confident that his expertise will drive significant value for our stakeholders and enhance our competitive edge in the market.”

 Commenting on his appointment, Mr. Krishnamurthy "is honored to join the board of Blackwell 3D. It is an exciting opportunity to contribute to a company that is not only committed to innovation and growth but also to fostering sustainable practices. I look forward to collaborating with the board and management team to deliver meaningful outcomes for the Company and its shareholders.”

 Mahesh’s contributions are expected to have a transformative impact, particularly in areas like strategic investments and financial structuring for long-term resilience. His extensive background in special situations financing and green technology adoption will bolster Blackwell 3D’s ability to stay ahead in today’s rapidly evolving marketplace.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D and its Series D Preferred Shares, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
