# Red Planet MMA Amplifies Diversity With New Transgender Boxing Coach and Expanded Martial Arts Services

BROOKLYN, NY - (NewMediaWire) - October 14, 2024 - To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

 Key Takeaways: * Red Planet MMA, a Brooklyn-based Mixed Martial Arts training center, has expanded its roster with the addition of a new boxing coach, demonstrating its commitment to inclusivity and diversity.
* The training center has affiliated with Masterskya Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, enhancing the variety and quality of training opportunities available to its students.
* Red Planet MMA is deeply involved in community service, offering women-only classes and contributing positively to the local community through various initiatives.
