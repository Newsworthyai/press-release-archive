# B2i Digital Named Marketing Partner for Maxim Group's 2024 Healthcare Virtual Summit

NEW YORK, NY - (NewMediaWire) - October 11, 2024 - B2i Digital, Inc. continues to support Maxim Group's virtual conference series with its role as a Marketing Partner for the 2024 Healthcare Virtual Summit. The event is scheduled for October 15 - 17, 2024, beginning at 9:00 AM ET each day.

 Key Takeaways: * B2i Digital is the Marketing Partner for Maxim's 2024 Healthcare Virtual Summit
* Presenting companies will be introduced to B2i Digital's 950,000+ institutional and retail investor community
* Maxim's event joins 12 other B2i Digital Featured Conferences in Fall '24
