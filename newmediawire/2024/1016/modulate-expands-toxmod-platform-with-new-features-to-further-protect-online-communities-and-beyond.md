# Modulate Expands ToxMod Platform With New Features to Further Protect Online Communities and Beyond

‍SOMERVILLE, Mass. - (NewMediaWire) - October 16, 2024 - Modulate, the prosocial voice intelligence company, today announced significant new features for its groundbreaking ToxMod platform. ToxMod, the world’s most advanced proactive voice moderation solution, has empowered community teams in gaming, social platforms, and other online environments to protect users from toxic behavior. To-date, Modulate’s ToxMod technology has moderated over 160 million hours of voice data, helping customers take 80 million+ moderator actions against harassment and toxicity. With the addition of Prosocial Behavior Detection, integrated text moderation, and phone number anonymization features, ToxMod expands its reach and impact to more industries like delivery, ride-share, gig work, social platforms, and more.
