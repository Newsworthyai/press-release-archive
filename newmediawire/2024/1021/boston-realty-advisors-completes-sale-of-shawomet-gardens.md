# Boston Realty Advisors Completes Sale of Shawomet Gardens

BOSTON - (NewMediaWire) - October 21, 2024 - Boston Realty Advisors, the Boston area’s largest independent commercial real estate brokerage, represented Audubon Road Associates in the sale of Shawomet Gardens, a community of 240 handsome garden residences in Somerset, MA.

 To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

 Key Takeaways: * At a time when high interest rates have slowed real property transactions, Boston Realty Advisors advised the owners over about four months, leading to the closing of the sale of the market-rate apartments on Oct. 8.
* The apartments, in 60 well-maintained brick buildings with extensive green space over 12 acres, were built in the late 1940s by the U.S. Government and have been owned for decades by the same family.
