# Museum Teams Up to Launch New Samurai Game

![samurai brothers](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cms.ipressroom.com/26/files/20248/samurai+brothers.jpg)

 (NewMediaWire) - September 01, 2024 - Glendale, CA – The Martial Arts History Museum is excited to announce a new partnership with Moon Rock Games for the upcoming Kickstarter launch of the highly anticipated tabletop game, “Samurai Brothers: Escape from Exile.”

 The Kickstarter campaign is set to launch on Sept. 10, 2024. Supporters are encouraged to sign up early to be the first to know when the game goes live.

 Samurai Brothers: Escape from Exile is an action-packed addition to the Samurai Brothers universe blending strategic gameplay with the rich history and lore of the samurai era. Developed by Moon Rock Games, this new game transports players to the mysterious Exile Island, where they must assemble a team consisting of a wise sensei, a brave guard, and a precious heirloom to escape and secure victory. This is a fast-paced game with immersive adventure.

 The Martial Arts History Museum is proud to support this dynamic and unique game that captures the spirit of the samurai and brings an exciting new experience to tabletop gaming fans. By partnering with Moon Rock Games, both are offering the community something fresh and engaging that resonates with the themes of discipline, strategy, and honor.

 Kickstarter supporters who sign up for early notification will have the chance to receive exclusive rewards that range from free stickers, the Escape from Exile game, and even the original Samurai Brother game.

 Five percent of the gross sales from the Kickstarter will be donated to the Martial Arts History Museum! Sign up today to be notified as soon as the Kickstarter campaign launches and join us in bringing Samurai Brothers: Escape from Exile to life!!!

 To sign up, visit https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/moonrockcomics/samurai-brothers-escape-from-exile
