# Business Owners and Public Relations Leaders Celebrate National Entrepreneur Month 2024: Empowering Careers and Communities

MURRIETA, Calif. - (NewMediaWire) - October 4, 2024 - As National Entrepreneur Month kicks off, two influential award-winning business owners, Liselle DeGrave, APR, of DeGrave Communications, Inc. and Rachel Dickman, MBA, APR, of RMG Communications, are highlighting their entrepreneurial success and also dedicating this time to helping others build their careers and live their professional lives to the fullest.
