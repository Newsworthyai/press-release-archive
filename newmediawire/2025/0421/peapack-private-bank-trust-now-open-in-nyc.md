# Peapack Private Bank & Trust Now Open in NYC

BEDMINSTER, NJ - April 21, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation (NASDAQ Global Select Market: PGC) and Peapack Private Bank & Trust are excited to announce the opening of their flagship location in New York City at 300 Park Avenue, at the corner of Park Avenue and 50th Street.

 This first-floor Financial Center spans 6,000 square feet and offers a deeply personal and unique private banking experience.  It is designed to provide an alternative to the larger institutions in NY by focusing on bespoke client service and exceptional attention to detail. The Financial Center complements the 18,000 square feet of office space on the 13th floor, which was opened in 2023. This office space houses over 100 private bankers and relationship managers who specialize in offering high-end commercial and private banking solutions.

 The premier facility combines best-in-class banking and state-of-the-art technology with a client-focused approach, built on a foundation of over 100 years of exceptional service. Peapack Private Bank & Trust is ready to welcome new clients looking for an elevated banking experience. Visit the Financial Center at the corner of Park Avenue and 50th Street to meet our team of relationship managers or visit peapackprivate.com to schedule a private appointment.

 Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation, a New Jersey bank holding company, has total assets of $7.0 billion and assets under management and/or administration of $11.9 billion as of December 31, 2024. Founded in 1921, Peapack Private Bank & Trust offers a client-centric approach to banking, providing high-quality products along with customized and innovative wealth management, investment banking, commercial, and personal banking solutions. The Bank’s wealth management division offers comprehensive financial, tax, fiduciary, and investment advice and solutions to individuals, families, privately held businesses, family offices, and not-for-profit organizations, helping them establish, maintain, and expand their legacy. Peapack Private Bank & Trust is committed to unparalleled client service. Visit peapackprivate.com for more information.
