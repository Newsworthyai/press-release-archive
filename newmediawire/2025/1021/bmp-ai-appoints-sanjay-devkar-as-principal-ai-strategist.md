# BMP AI Appoints Sanjay Devkar as Principal AI Strategist

DUBAI, UAE - October 21, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - BMP AI Technologies Inc. (OTC: BMPA), a next-generation artificial intelligence and blockchain development company, today announced the appointment of Mr. Sanjay Devkar as Principal AI Strategist.

 Mr. Devkar brings over 25 years of global experience in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and large-scale program management. Based in Dubai, he has led high-impact initiatives at Meta Platforms Inc. (Reality Labs - AI & VR), Amazon Web Services (AWS), IBM, and Infosys, driving innovation at the intersection of product development, applied research, and infrastructure engineering.

 At Meta, Mr. Devkar led a 300-member cross-functional organization focused on perception and personalization models for VR/AR devices. His team standardized MLOps practices across multiple product surfaces, reduced inference latency by 18 percent through runtime optimization, and advanced privacy-by-design frameworks in coordination with global regulators. Prior to Meta, he oversaw Big Data and AI portfolios at AWS and enterprise automation programs at IBM and Infosys.

 Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of BMP AI Technologies Inc., stated: “We’re pleased to welcome Sanjay to BMP AI. His extensive experience in AI systems architecture, governance, and scalable deployment aligns with our strategic vision for enterprise AI adoption. His leadership in AI and VR at Meta, combined with deep understanding of MLOps and compliance, will strengthen our capability to deliver robust, trustworthy AI solutions for global clients.”

 Strategic Focus

 Mr. Devkar will support BMP AI’s continued growth as a trusted provider of AI infrastructure and solutions for regulated industries such as fintech, healthcare, and enterprise automation. His advisory scope includes:

 * Developing reliable and cost-efficient AI models for real-time decision support and predictive insight.
* Establishing AI governance frameworks that balance innovation with privacy and safety requirements.
* Expanding R&D partnerships with academic and industry collaborators across the MENA region.
* Guiding scalable deployment of BMP AI’s flagship chatbot and blockchain platforms for enterprise clients.

 Mr. Devkar commented, “AI adoption is entering a phase where reliability and accountability matter as much as accuracy. I look forward to working with BMP AI to help build systems that are both impactful and responsible.”
