# Beeline Holdings (BLNE) Reports 35% Sequential Growth in Q3 Originations and 37% Revenue Increase

LOS ANGELES, CA - November 6, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), an emerging digital mortgage lender and SaaS platform, reported third-quarter 2025 originations of $69.8 million, up 35% from Q2, with net revenue rising 37% to $2.3 million. Operating expenses declined 8% to $5.2 million, and adjusted EBITDA improved to $(2.0) million from $(2.8) million in Q2. Beeline posted a Q3 net loss of $(4.0) million, including a $718,000 one-time disposition expense related to its spirits business.
