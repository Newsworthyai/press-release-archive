# KYN Capital Group Updates and Recaps KOINFOLD™ - A Revolutionary Non-Custodial Crypto Wallet

RANCHO CORDOVA, CA - September 11, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - KYN Capital Group, Inc. (OTC: KYNC), a trailblazer in fintech innovation, recent updates and recap for KOINFOLD™, a state-of-the-art non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet designed to redefine security, flexibility, and user empowerment. Powered by cutting-edge AI and expansive blockchain integration, KOINFOLD™ sets a new standard for managing digital assets with confidence and ease.

 Sleek Design, Unmatched Performance

 KOINFOLD™ released a new modern, user-friendly interface that simplifies digital asset management without compromising security. Integrated with MoonPay’s lightning-fast payment system, this non-custodial wallet ensures users retain full control over their assets while enjoying seamless, private, and secure transactions.

 Broad Token Support and Seamless Connectivity

 KOINFOLD™ now supports an extensive array of tokens, including DOT, FLOW, PEPE, PIXEL, SHIB, SNX, MKR, MATIC, MANA, KAVA, JASMY, and GRT, all swappable on Solana’s high-speed network. With USDT compatibility across Polygon, Ethereum, and Tron, plus fiat-to-crypto conversions via Moon Pay, KOINFOLD™ delivers unmatched flexibility for both novice and seasoned traders.

 Virtual Banking: The Future of Finance

 Set to launch in Q4 2025, KOINFOLD™ will introduce virtual bank accounts, bridging traditional finance and DeFi. This innovative feature will allow users to manage fiat and crypto assets seamlessly within the app, offering unparalleled convenience and redefining financial empowerment.
