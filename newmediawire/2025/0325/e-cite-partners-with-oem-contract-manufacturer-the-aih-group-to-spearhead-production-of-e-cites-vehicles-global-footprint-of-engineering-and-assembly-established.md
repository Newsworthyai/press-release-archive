# E-Cite Partners With OEM Contract Manufacturer "The AIH Group" to Spearhead Production of E-Cites Vehicles - Global Footprint of Engineering and Assembly Established

BOTHELL, WA - March 25, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Innovative EV Technologies, Inc. formerly VaporBrands International, Inc. dba E-Cite Motors Group, (OTCPINK:VAPR) announced that it has executed an MOU with The AIH Group to oversee implementation of production.

 The AIH Group is a globally renowned manufacturing and logistics consultancy firm which also offers contract manufacturing to its customers https://autoih.co.za/. Some of the functions that AIH may provide include automotive and industry consulting, logistics and supply chain planning, engineering, contract assembly, built environment, infrastructure and plant and equipment, ERP systems, human capital and economic development for E-Cites vehicles.

 Corrie Kotze, Managing Director and Group CEO of The AIH Group, remarked: “We have been observing E-Cite and providing guidance for some time from the sidelines. Due to their recent developments and clear advantages, it is time for us to formerly join the effort and to provide any and all resources at our disposal. This is a unique opportunity, and we are thrilled to be intimately involved on an official basis.”

 Gene Langmesser, E-Cite COO, stated: “The AIH Group has long been among the most widely respected best in class automotive consulting and contract manufacturing companies in the world. Their vast experience in logistics, supply chain administration, and specifically efficient manufacturing management, are unparalleled in the industry.”

 Barry Henthorn, E-Cite CEO, added: “E-Cite is now able to focus on delivering its vehicles without the burden of establishing systems and software that have been well refined by AIH. We are confident that this partnership will allow E-Cite to ensure our ability to track and control cost while opening up our resources around the Globe.”

 The AIH Group has offices or assembly plants in more than 10 cities and countries across the globe. A list of some of the facilities can be found at https://autoih.co.za/offices/ .

 THE AIH GROUP: The companies within the AIH Group’s ecosystem offer automotive and industry consulting, logistics and supply chain planning, engineering, contract assembly, built environment, infrastructure and plant and equipment, ERP Software solution, human capital and economic development. Our network of international offices covers a wide global reach and combines a local presence with a key client contact point while providing access to the full scope of services in the AIH Group.

 Innovative EV Technologies dba E-Cite Motorswww.ecitemotors.com (OTC PINK:VAPR) is a publicly traded company based in Bothell, WA that is developing for manufacturing, state of the art electric and ICE vehicles utilizing the latest in technologies with a flare of some of the iconic autos of the past. Innovative (VAPR) acquired 100% ownership in E-Cite Motors, Acclaimed Automotive www.acclaimedauto.com, and N2A Motors www.n2amotors.com a California-based custom auto manufacturer and car factory specializing in designing, engineering and building prototype, concept, and limited production vehicles for OEMs, corporations, movies, and private owners. N2A was led by legendary designer Gene Langmesser who now serves as the COO of the combined operations.
