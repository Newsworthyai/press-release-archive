# NeuralBase's BMP AI Platform Expands Pilot Program With Three Leading Dubai Restaurants

LAS VEGAS, NV - June 25, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating through its enterprise brand BMP AI, today announced the launch of a strategic pilot program with three of Dubai’s most dynamic establishments: Lahori Chatkhara, Kiraak Hyderabadi, and Biryani Bunch. These renowned restaurants will serve as early adopters of BMP AI’s next-generation enterprise chatbot platform.

 This collaboration represents a pivotal step in NeuralBase AI’s ambition to revolutionize service-driven industries with intelligent automation. Over the coming months, BMP AI’s conversational agent will be integrated into each restaurant’s core operations to elevate customer engagement, optimize order management, enhance supplier coordination, and streamline internal communication.

 “This partnership represents more than a trial - it’s an opportunity to reshape hospitality,” said Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase AI. “Working directly with high-traffic venues gives us crucial real-world insights and validates our platform's potential to transform service delivery.”

 Highlights of BMP AI Chatbot Capabilities:

 - 24/7 Conversational Engagement – Delivering personalized customer service via chat

 - Seamless Order Routing – Automating the handoff from guest queries to kitchen operations

 - Supplier Integration – Minimizing delays and errors across the supply chain

 - Team Collaboration Tools – Improving internal flow with real-time staff messaging

 These partner restaurants, each celebrated for their vibrant cuisine and busy operations, will actively shape BMP AI’s evolution. Through live deployment, user feedback, and performance metrics, NeuralBase AI plans to refine platform functionality ahead of wider global rollout.

 Already in active development, the next version of the chatbot will introduce:

 - Advanced analytics for operational insights

 - Multi-channel support, including voice and messaging apps

 - Enhanced automation for inventory, scheduling, and procurement

 NeuralBase AI remains dedicated to delivering scalable, secure, and compliance-ready automation solutions for global enterprises.

 Additional information about the Company is available at www.neuralbase.ai or by visiting www.sec.gov.
