# Take Your Big Tech Investing To The Next Level With The Magnificent 10 Index From Cboe - Learn How At This Webinar

By Meg Flippin, Benzinga

 * Cboe and Benzinga will host a virtual webinar introducing the Cboe Magnificent 10 Index (MGTN) on November 13 at 11 am ET.
* The Magnificent 7 paradigm is getting an upgrade with the addition of Advanced Micro Devices Inc. (AMD), Broadcom Inc. (AVGO) and Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR).
* Henry Schwartz, Vice President, Derivatives Market Intelligence at Cboe and Benzinga’s Gianni DiPoce will share insights about making the most of the index.

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - November 7, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Cboe and Benzinga will host an exclusive pre-launch webinar on November 13 from 11 a.m. to 12 p.m. ET to introduce the Cboe Magnificent 10 Index (MGTN) - a new innovative benchmark capturing the pulse of U.S. large-cap technology stocks driving overall growth. Through this pre-launch webinar, investors will get an early look at Cboe’s take on the most influential tech stocks in the market.

 Henry Schwartz, Vice President, Derivatives Market Intelligence at Cboe and Gianni DiPoce, analyst and contributor at Benzinga, will lead the session on Cboe’s latest thematic, equal-weighted index and show investors how this product suite enables efficient exposure to growth leaders.

 Learn All About The MGTN

 The webinar will provide an overview of the Cboe Magnificent 10 Index, why Cboe decided to expand its index beyond the original seven and the impact these technology titans have on the markets.

 Through this webinar, you’ll learn about the design and methodology behind the MGTN Index, how this product suite enables efficient exposure to growth leaders and what to expect from the upcoming launch of options and futures on MGTN, particularly when you can trade the derivatives products 24 hours a day five days a week.

 Live Q&A

 Capping off the webinar will be a live Q&A session in which attendees can submit questions directly to Schwartz and Di Poce about MGTN, the methodology behind MGTN, the importance of AI stocks to MGTN, how you can leverage options and futures on MGTN and more.
