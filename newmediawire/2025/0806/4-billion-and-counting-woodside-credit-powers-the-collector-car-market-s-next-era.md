# $4 Billion and Counting: Woodside Credit Powers the Collector Car Market's Next Era

NEWPORT BEACH, CA - August 6, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Woodside Credit, the nation's leading lender for classic, collector, and exotic vehicles, proudly announces that it has exceeded $4 billion in loans originated. This milestone highlights the continued strength of the company's purpose-built financing model and its commitment to delivering the lowest monthly payments in America for car enthusiasts, collectors, and dealers across the country.

 As the collector car community gathers for the prestigious Pebble Beach Concours d'Elegance® this month, Woodside Credit's achievement reflects the company's consistent performance and trusted position in the collector car financing market, particularly its focus on the lowest payments in America.

 "This milestone reflects the caliber of our clients, the confidence of our partners, and the strength of our lending program," said Mitch Shatzen, President and COO. "Our continued growth is driven by a singular focus: delivering the lowest payments in the industry with unmatched value. Events like the Pebble Beach Concours showcase the passion and expertise of our community, and we're proud to be an engine powering transactions throughout the week."

 Found Where Serious Buyers Shop

 Woodside Credit's loan program is available through the channels where enthusiasts buy cars, including dealerships nationwide, at Barrett-Jackson auctions, and anywhere buyers and sellers meet. Whether you're working with your trusted dealer or bidding at auction, Woodside's proven financing platform delivers industry-leading low payments and straightforward service.

 Designed for collectors and enthusiasts, Woodside's lending program offers up to 180-month terms, no early payoff penalties, and loans up to $1 million. This structure allows clients to keep more cash in their pocket, maximize their buying power, and enjoy ownership with payments that make sense.

 It's not just about financing a vehicle; it's about working with a lender that truly understands the collector and exotic car world. With over 30,000 enthusiasts served and $4 billion in loans originated, Woodside Credit has earned its reputation as the go-to financing partner for car buyers.

 Known for Speed, Backed by Experience

 Woodside Credit gets deals done quickly and efficiently. Backed by a team of specialists who know the market and understand the needs of buyers and sellers, the company delivers fast approvals, clear communication, and smooth funding, often in a few business days. This streamlined process means you can move quickly when you find the right car.

 Woodside Credit will be onsite at the Concours Village during the Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance, August 14–17, and at the Werks Reunion on August 15. Attendees can learn more about how Woodside Credit’s collector car financing program supports enthusiasts in purchasing their next vehicle.

 About Woodside Credit

 Founded in 2003 and headquartered in Newport Beach, California, Woodside Credit is the trusted name in collector car financing, having funded more than $4 billion in loans. Its focus on low monthly payments, an unmatched loan program, and expert service has made it the preferred lender for car enthusiasts and collectors nationwide. Woodside Credit is the exclusive loan provider for Barrett-Jackson.
