# TAG1 Inc. to Participate in Jones Las Vegas Technology and Innovation Conference on April 8-9, 2025

Pioneering the Future of Cancer Treatment With Lead-212 innovation, TAG1 Inc. to Join an Expert Panel on Radiopharmaceuticals at the Jones Las Vegas Technology and Innovation Conference

 CHESTERFIELD, MO - March 31, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - TAG1 Inc., a radioisotope company focused on strengthening the global supply chain for Targeted Alpha Therapies (TATs) by providing high-purity Lead-212, today announced its participation in the Jones Las Vegas Technology and Innovation Conference, to be held April 8–9, 2025, at The Venetian Resort in Las Vegas, Nevada.

 The Jones Conference is recognized for bringing together a select group of innovative companies and institutional investors for a two-day event. The event will be focused on one-on-one (1x1) meetings, fireside chats, panels, and networking. Organized by JonesTrading, with marketing support from B2i Digital, Inc., the event offers an opportunity for direct dialogue between senior company executives and investors seeking to engage with leadership teams.

 As part of the program, TAG1 CEO Sumit Verma will speak on a featured panel titled "Radiopharma Isotope Roadmap: Clinical & Logistical Considerations," alongside executives from Lantheus Holdings (LNTH) and Radiopharm Theranostics (ASX: RAD), moderated by JonesTrading’s Justin Walsh. The panel will take place on April 9 at 9:00 AM local time.

 In addition to the panel, TAG1 will deliver a virtual company presentation hosted via MeetMax event platform on April 9 at 3:00 PM, which will also be made available on TAG1’s corporate website following the event. The company will also participate in one-on-one investor meetings throughout the conference.

 “TAG1 is honored to be part of the Jones Las Vegas Technology and Innovation Conference and to join a panel of leaders shaping the future of radiopharmaceuticals,” said Sumit Verma, CEO of TAG1 Inc. “This is an exciting opportunity to share TAG1’s progress, meet with investors, and contribute to the broader dialogue on how we can address the growing clinical demand for critical isotopes like Lead-212. As our field continues to evolve, strengthening the supply chain for targeted alpha therapies will be essential to delivering next-generation treatments to patients worldwide.”

 “JonesTrading is pleased to host a conference that brings together companies and investors for thoughtful and in-depth discussions,” said Alan Hill, CEO of JonesTrading. “We remain focused on creating a forum where public companies can communicate directly with the investment community.”

 “As the Marketing Partner, B2i Digital is working to ensure that companies participating in the Jones Conference have a platform to reach investors who are actively seeking new ideas and opportunities,” said David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital, Inc. “We partner with the leading investor events in the US and are honored to be a part of this conference with its cutting-edge companies, serious investors and valued sponsors.”

 The conference will feature keynote addresses from Dr. Charity Dean, CEO of PHC Global, and Eric F. Trump, Executive Vice President of The Trump Organization, along with a Jones-hosted Golf Event and Cocktail Reception.

 For more information about the Jones Las Vegas Technology and Innovation Conference, including a list of participating companies, visit their B2i Digital Featured Conference page.

 About TAG1 Inc.TAG1 Inc. is a radioisotope company dedicated to advancing the future of cancer treatment through the production and supply of high-purity medical isotopes for Targeted Alpha Therapies (TATs). Leveraging its proprietary portable generator technology, TAG1 enables nuclear radiopharmacies, hospital networks, and research institutions to access a reliable, scalable supply of Lead-212 (Pb-212). Through strategic partnerships and a commitment to innovation, TAG1 supports the development of life-saving radioligand therapies and aims to strengthen the global radiopharmaceutical supply chain.
