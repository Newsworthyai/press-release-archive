# MWG Holdings Expands West Coast Cannabis Footprint With Perfect Union Store in San Diego

Grand Opening Oct. 22 Marks Flagship Retail Brand’s Entry into Southern California Market

 SACRAMENTO, CA - October 14, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - MWG Holdings Group, Inc. (“MWG Holdings”), a leading purpose-driven, locally-focused cannabis company with award-winning roots in the California medical and recreational markets, announced today the opening of its 10th California location in San Diego, continuing its momentum across the Golden State.

 The new Perfect Union San Diego dispensary is located at 909 Cardiff Street, San Diego, and will operate from 9am - 9pm.

 MWG invites the San Diego community to celebrate the grand opening on Wednesday, Oct. 22 for the Ribbon Cutting at 9 a.m. with exclusive promotions, doorbusters, music, food, brand demos, and a first look at their curated product offerings.

 MWG Holdings will celebrate its San Diego Grand Opening with a four-day sale offering 52% off, running Wednesday, October 22 through Saturday, October 25. Known for standout service, curated product offerings, and a commitment to uplifting the communities it serves, MWG is rapidly emerging as a leader in California cannabis retail. With locations now spanning from Northern California to the southern coast, the Company is proud to introduce its top in-house and partner brands - along with its signature customer-first retail model - to San Diego.

 “Bringing MWG to San Diego represents the next step in our mission to deliver exceptional cannabis experiences across the state,” said Tom Sheridan, Esq., CEO of MWG Holdings. “We’re proud to introduce Southern California customers to the best of what we offer - from award-winning products to passionate, knowledgeable staff who understand what a great cannabis experience should be.”

 MWG’s reputation is backed by consumer-driven accolades. In the 2025 NuggMD Patient Choice Awards, Perfect Union’s Northside Sacramento store earned Gold honors in four major categories: Best Overall Dispensary, Best Prices, Best Selection, and Best Budtenders. MWG’s beverage brand, 5G Pop, was also voted Best Cannabis Beverage in California.

 These honors underscore the Company’s continued leadership in delivering product excellence, value, and customer service. In 2024, Perfect Union was also named a Silver Winner for Best Dispensary in the Sacramento Favorites Awards, further reinforcing MWG’s trusted community presence.

 “We’ve built a strong foundation by listening to what customers want and curating experiences that reflect the unique character of each community we serve,” said Nicole Hanratty, VP of Marketing at MWG Holdings. “San Diego is a vibrant, diverse city, and we’re thrilled to bring Perfect Union here and contribute to its dynamic culture.”

 About MWG Holdings

 MWG Holdings Group, Inc. is a California-based cannabis holding company with a portfolio spanning award-winning consumer brands and retail operations, including Perfect Union, one of the state’s top five retail chains.

 Founded in 2017, MWG owns and operates 10 retail locations, including nine Perfect Union stores and one Wild Seed Wellness store, which have achieved record-setting sales this year. The retail stores have experienced a 32.2% year-over-year increase in transaction count and a 26.7% increase in revenue during the first half of 2025.

 MWG's retail stores are consistently recognized for their award-winning brands and community-driven approach. Its management team brings over a decade of experience building profitable cannabis businesses, having raised millions in capital, and bringing the Company to cash flow positivity.

 MWG’s diverse and growing portfolio, featuring top brands like Blazy, 5G Pop, Cap City, 530 Grower, and Shake That, solidifies its standing as an industry leader.
