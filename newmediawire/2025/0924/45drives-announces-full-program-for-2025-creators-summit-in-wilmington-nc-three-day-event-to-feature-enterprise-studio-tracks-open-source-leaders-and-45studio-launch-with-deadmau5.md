# 45Drives Announces Full Program for 2025 Creators Summit in Wilmington, NC Three-Day Event to Feature Enterprise &amp; Studio Tracks, Open Source Leaders, and 45Studio Launch With deadmau5

WILMINGTON, NC and SYDNEY, NOVA SCOTIA - September 24, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - 45Drives, the leader in big, strong, fast open source data storage solutions, today announced the complete schedule for the 2025 Creators Summit, a three-day gathering that unites enterprise technologists, filmmakers, digital creators, and open-source leaders. Taking place September 10–12 across Wilmington, NC, the Summit will culminate in a landmark 45Studio product launch featuring globally renowned electronic artist deadmau5.

 “The Creators Summit is where worlds collide - in the best way possible,” said Dr. Doug Milburn, President of 45Drives. “We designed it to bring enterprise technologists, filmmakers, musicians, and creators together under one roof to share ideas and push boundaries. From open-source pioneers to industry-shaping artists like deadmau5, this year’s program reflects our belief that creativity and technology are inseparable forces driving the future. Wilmington has quickly become a hub for these conversations, and we’re proud to host the community here again in 2025.”

 The 2025 Creators Summit is open to industry professionals, students, creators, and media. To register for the event or to request press credentials, please visit: https://www.45drives.com/creators-summit/

 The event is proudly supported by Michael Best Law as a sponsor, with community partners including the Skyline Center, UNCW, and Cape Fear Community College, along with additional sponsorship from Seagate.
