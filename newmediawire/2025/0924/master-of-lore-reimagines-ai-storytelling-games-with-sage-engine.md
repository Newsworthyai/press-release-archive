# Master of Lore Reimagines AI Storytelling Games With SAGE Engine

Narrative-rich world sets new standard in AI-powered role-playing

 DOVER, DE - September 24, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Master of Lore, an immersive AI-powered storytelling experience, has officially launched its platform built on the proprietary SAGE engine. This innovative game delivers structured, dynamic narratives within an ecosystem of handcrafted, evolving worlds. Following its debut on September 18, Master of Lore positions itself as the first platform to merge cutting-edge AI with meaningful story depth, deliberate pacing, and real stakes - marking a new era in narrative role-playing.

 Unlike most AI games that rely on large language models for reactive text generation, Master of Lore introduces Loremaster, an intelligent AI narrator trained to guide, challenge and remember. Instead of indulging every user prompt, Loremaster balances player freedom with curated tension, making every session feel like part of a living, persistent world. Players can explore this in the platform’s debut universe, “Loom - Woven Worlds,” where the story begins in the magical city of Kesi, a realm of mystic creatures and choice-driven storytelling.

 “Most AI games today only scratch the surface of what is possible with this technology,” said Tomasz Gusciora, the project’s lead developer and one of the architects of the SAGE engine. “Master of Lore isn't just another AI-storytelling prompt; it is a structured, story-rich universe where AI doesn’t just respond, it resists. With the SAGE engine, players don’t just generate content, they live inside a world that remembers what they’ve done and grows alongside them.”

 Built for role-players, writers and fans of immersive storytelling, Master of Lore offers an ecosystem of evolving worlds, each with its own internal logic, character arcs and lore. With real consequences, limited retries and a pacing model that simulates the experience of playing with a live game master, the platform blends AI innovation with storytelling tradition. Future updates will expand the platform to include multiplayer support and entirely new universes for collaborative exploration.

 To learn more or request early access to Master of Lore, visit: https://masteroflore.com.

 About Master of Lore

 Master of Lore is an AI-powered storytelling and entertainment platform that blends intelligent narrative systems with the timeless appeal of role-playing games. Inspired by the spirit of tabletop RPGs, it delivers extraordinary, choice-driven adventures enhanced by cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Built on the proprietary SAGE engine, each journey unfolds in richly structured, handcrafted worlds that evolve with every decision. Founded by a multidisciplinary team of writers, game designers, and AI engineers, Master of Lore is on a mission to deepen immersion, spark creativity, and bring dynamic, interactive storytelling to life - anytime, anywhere, across devices.
