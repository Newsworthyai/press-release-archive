# Dealers Cloud and Everyware Partner to Revolutionize Automotive Dealership Management with Integrated Payment Solutions

AUSTIN TEXAS - August 7, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Dealers Cloud, a leading provider of cloud-based Dealer Management Software (DMS), has announced a strategic partnership with Everyware, a prominent provider of contactless payment and customer engagement solutions. This partnership will integrate Everyware’s Pay By Text and Point-of-Sale (POS) device solutions directly into Dealers Cloud’s comprehensive DMS platform, offering dealerships a powerful and seamless payment experience for both in-person and remote transactions.

Dealers Cloud delivers a full suite of tools to streamline dealership operations, including inventory management, CRM, and internet lead management. With the addition of Everyware’s Pay By Text and POS device capabilities, dealerships can now provide their customers with secure, flexible payment options - whether in the showroom or from their mobile device - meeting the rising demand for convenient, contactless payment experiences.

 “Our mission at Dealers Cloud has always been to empower dealerships with innovative technology that drives efficiency and revenue,” said Nafees Abbasi, CEO of Dealers Cloud. “By integrating Everyware’s Pay By Text and POS device solutions into our DMS, we’re delivering a modern payment infrastructure that aligns with consumer expectations and simplifies transactions for our clients.”

Everyware’s payment tools enable dealerships to send secure invoices via text and accept real-time payments through in-store POS terminals. This combined solution not only enhances the customer experience but also accelerates payment collection, improves cash flow, and reduces manual billing efforts.

“We’re thrilled to collaborate with Dealers Cloud to bring our Pay By Text and POS capabilities to their national network of automotive dealers,” said Austin Talley, CEO & Founder of Everyware. “This partnership supports our shared vision of enabling seamless, secure, and customer-friendly payment interactions that drive stronger engagement and operational success.”

This integration is part of a larger initiative by Dealers Cloud to modernize automotive retail through technology. By offering both contactless mobile and in-store POS payment options, dealerships are better equipped to meet today’s consumer preferences, enhance customer satisfaction, and maintain a competitive edge in a rapidly evolving market.

About Dealers Cloud

Dealers Cloud is an industry-leading developer of cloud-based Dealer Management Software (DMS), offering solutions for inventory management, customer relationship management, and internet lead management. Their mission is to provide easy-to-use, innovative solutions that enhance productivity and revenue growth for automotive dealerships nationwide. DealersCloud.com
