# NeuralBase Appoints Pawan Kumar as Sales Director to Drive Global Expansion and Strategic Partnerships

LAS VEGAS, NV - August 7, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI, proudly announces the appointment of Pawan Kumar as its new Sales Director. With over 13 years of proven success in fintech marketing, forex operations, and real estate business development, Mr. Kumar brings a results-driven leadership style and deep market expertise to the role.

 Pawan Kumar's career spans key roles at leading fintech and real estate firms in Dubai, including Sarwa, DAMAC Properties, and Emaar. His background includes managing high-net-worth client portfolios, scaling digital and direct sales channels, and leading high-performance teams across the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) and India.

 As Sales Director, Mr. Kumar will lead NeuralBase AI’s global sales strategy, focusing on expanding its footprint in enterprise AI solutions, financial analytics platforms, and next-generation customer engagement tools powered by machine learning and natural language processing.

 “We are thrilled to welcome Pawan to our executive team,” stated Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase AI. “His unique blend of fintech and real estate experience, coupled with his track record of driving sales growth through relationship-building and team leadership, makes him the perfect fit to lead our next phase of expansion.”

 Pawan Kumar’s appointment is set to supercharge NeuralBase AI's go-to-market strategy through:

 * Client-Centric Sales Leadership: His ability to nurture long-term relationships will strengthen NeuralBase AI’s position in high-value enterprise accounts.
* Market Expansion Expertise: Having led regional growth initiatives in Dubai’s competitive fintech and property markets, Mr. Kumar will help penetrate new verticals and geographic regions.
* Team Building & Mentorship: His leadership experience will support the development of a high-performance sales force equipped to scale alongside the company.
* Strategic Alliances: Leveraging his strong network across the GCC, he will drive key partnerships to expand NeuralBase AI’s reseller and integration ecosystem.

 “I’m excited to join NeuralBase AI at this transformative stage,” declared Pawan Kumar. “AI is redefining how businesses operate, and I look forward to contributing to NeuralBase’s mission to democratize intelligent technology for organizations of all sizes.”

 Mr. Kumar’s appointment marks a significant milestone in NeuralBase AI’s growth as the company accelerates its roadmap across financial services, healthcare, real estate, and government sectors.
