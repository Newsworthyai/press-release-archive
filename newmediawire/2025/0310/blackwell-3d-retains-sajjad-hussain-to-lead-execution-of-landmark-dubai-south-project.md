# Blackwell 3D Retains Sajjad Hussain to Lead Execution of Landmark Dubai South Project

DUBAI, UAE - March 10, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce the appointment of Sajjad Hussain, a renowned consultant in Dubai’s real estate sector, to spearhead the execution of the Company’s newly signed Dubai South Project. Hussain, a visionary in the industry, has been instrumental in delivering some of Dubai’s most ambitious large-scale developments and is respected by major developers in the region.

 With a reputation for not just building structures, but shaping the visions of real estate development companies, Hussain’s expertise will be a key asset in ensuring the success of the Dubai South Project. His strategic insight, coupled with Blackwell’s commitment to innovation and excellence, is set to redefine the standards of urban development in the region.

 Mitesh Rasaikar, Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Blackwell, expressing his enthusiasm about Hussain’s appointment, declared that “Sajjad Hussain’s deep understanding of Dubai’s real estate landscape and his ability to bring visionary projects to life make him an invaluable addition to our team. His leadership will be crucial in navigating the complexities of the Dubai South Project and ensuring it is executed with precision, efficiency, and excellence.”

 Echoing this sentiment, Mohammed Saif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell, stated that “at Blackwell, we believe in working with the best, and Sajjad Hussain’s track record speaks for itself. His ability to transform ambitious concepts into reality aligns perfectly with our vision for Dubai South. With his expertise, we are confident that this project will set new benchmarks in urban development.”

 Dubai South is set to be one of the most dynamic and strategically important developments in the UAE, playing a pivotal role in the city’s future expansion. Blackwell’s partnership with Hussain underscores the Company’s commitment to excellence and innovation in real estate development.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov. Additional information regarding the details of this project in Dubai South will be made available soon.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
