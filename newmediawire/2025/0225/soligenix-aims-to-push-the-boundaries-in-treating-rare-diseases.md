# Soligenix Aims To Push The Boundaries In Treating Rare Diseases

By Johnny Rice Benzinga

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - February 25, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Christopher J. Schaber, Ph.D, president and CEO of Soligenix Inc. (NASDAQ: SNGX), was recently a guest on Benzinga’s All-Access.

 Soligenix, a late-stage biopharmaceutical company, is focused on developing and commercializing treatments for rare diseases. The company's key focus is on HyBryte(TM), a novel photodynamic therapy for cutaneous T-cell lymphoma, and the expansion of synthetic hypericin into psoriasis and other inflammatory diseases.

 Schaber shared exciting updates from the company’s phase 3 study of HyBryte. Preliminary results are expected in mid 2026.
