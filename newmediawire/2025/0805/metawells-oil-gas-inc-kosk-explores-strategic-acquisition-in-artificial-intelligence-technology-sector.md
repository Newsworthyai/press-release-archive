# Metawells Oil &amp; Gas Inc. (KOSK) Explores Strategic Acquisition in Artificial Intelligence Technology Sector

HARRISON, NEW YORK - August 5, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Metawells Oil & Gas Inc. (OTC Markets: KOSK), a development-stage energy company, is pleased to announce that it is actively pursuing a strategic acquisition in the artificial intelligence (AI) technology sector.

 This initiative marks a significant milestone as Metawells explores diversification opportunities beyond traditional oil and gas operations. The company is currently engaged in advanced discussions with a prospective target in the AI field, reflecting its vision to align with high-growth technology sectors and enhance long-term shareholder value.

 “Our pursuit of this acquisition is part of a broader strategy to embrace innovation while maintaining our roots in energy,” said Ronald Minsky, Chairman and CEO of Metawells. “We believe that combining traditional resource expertise with emerging AI technologies can unlock new operational efficiencies and create future-facing opportunities for our company.”

 Further updates will be shared as the company progresses through due diligence and negotiation phases.

 About Metawells Oil & Gas Inc.

 Metawells Oil & Gas Inc. (OTC Markets: KOSK) is a holdings company founded in the State of Nevada and engaged in two sectors, the energy sector and the sector of technology. The Company is set to forge strategic collaborations and develop both business segments to accelerate the company growth.The company is also expanding its portfolio to include emerging technology ventures with the aim of building a diversified and resilient business model.
