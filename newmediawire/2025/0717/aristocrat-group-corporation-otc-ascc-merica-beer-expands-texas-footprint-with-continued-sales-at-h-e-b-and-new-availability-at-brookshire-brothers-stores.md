# Aristocrat Group Corporation (OTC:ASCC) Merica Beer Expands Texas Footprint With Continued Sales at H-E-B and New Availability at Brookshire Brothers Stores

HOUSTON, TEXAS - July 17, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Aristocrat Group Corporation (OTC: ASCC) - Merica Beer, the American-made craft Pilsner known for its bold flavor and patriotic branding, is proud to announce the continued statewide availability of its products at all H-E-B stores across Texas. In addition, Merica Beer is now available at select Brookshire Brothers Grocery Stores, further expanding its reach to customers across the Lone Star State.

 H-E-B, Texas’ largest grocery retailer, has been a strong supporter of local and regional brands, and Merica Beer’s continued presence on their shelves is a testament to the brand’s popularity among Texan beer lovers. Now, with the addition of Brookshire Brothers - a trusted name in community-focused grocery stores - Texans have even more convenient locations to pick up their favorite American Pilsner.

 “Texas has shown us tremendous support, and we’re excited to deepen our roots in the state,” said Derek Sisson, CEO of Aristocrat Group Corporation. “With the continued partnership of H-E-B and the new opportunity to serve Brookshire Brothers customers, we’re making it even easier for Texans to crack open a cold Merica Beer wherever they are.”

 Merica Beer is brewed with high-quality ingredients and a passion for American craftsmanship. It continues to grow in popularity across Texas for its smooth finish, iconic branding, and unapologetically American attitude.

 Texans can now find Merica Beer at their nearest H-E-B and select Brookshire Brothers locations. To locate a store or learn more, visit www.mericabeers.com.
