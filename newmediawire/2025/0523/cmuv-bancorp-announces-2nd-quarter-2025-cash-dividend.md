# CMUV Bancorp Announces 2nd Quarter 2025 Cash Dividend

EL CENTRO,CA - May 23, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - The Board of Directors of CMUV Bancorp, the bank holding company for Community Valley Bank, has approved a $0.12 2nd Quarter cash dividend per common share. The dividend will be payable on June 30, 2025, to all shareholders of record on June 9, 2025.
