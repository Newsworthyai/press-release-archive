# Accredited Solutions, Inc. (OTC: ASII) Engages Partner Capital Group, LLC  (PartnerCap)to Explore Strategic Alternatives to Maximize Shareholder Value

NEW YORK - March 6, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Accredited Solutions, Inc. (OTC: ASII) ("Accredited Solutions" or the "Company"), a diversified holding company focused on fintech and technology-driven businesses, today announced that it has engaged PartnerCap, a premier advisory firm specializing in corporate finance and strategic transactions, to explore new opportunities aimed at enhancing shareholder value.

 Through this engagement, PartnerCap will assist the Company in evaluating strategic alternatives, including potential mergers with companies listed on national exchanges such as Nasdaq, business combinations with operating companies or SPACs, and others. The objective of this initiative is to unlock the true value of ASII’s assets, including its subsidiary GlobeTopper, and position the Company for sustainable growth.

 “We are excited to partner with Partner Capital Group as we explore pathways to create meaningful value for our shareholders,” said Eduardo Brito, CEO of Accredited Solutions, Inc. "Despite the strong fundamentals and growth trajectory of our business - especially with the GlobeTopper acquisition - the market has not fully recognized its value. This engagement is a proactive step in identifying opportunities that could help us maximize our potential, whether through strategic mergers, acquisitions, or other alternatives.

 "We are committed to pursuing the best outcome for our shareholders and will leave no stone unturned in exploring all viable options. We deeply appreciate the continued support and patience of our shareholders as we navigate this process and work toward delivering long-term value.”

 The Company will provide updates as developments unfold and strategic opportunities materialize.
