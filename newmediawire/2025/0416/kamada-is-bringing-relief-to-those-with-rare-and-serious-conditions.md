# Kamada Is Bringing Relief To Those With Rare And Serious Conditions

By Johnny Rice Benzinga

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - April 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Amir London, CEO of Kamada Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ: KMDA), was recently a guest on Benzinga’s All-Access.
