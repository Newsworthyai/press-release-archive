# Assimina "Mina" Nikiforos Joins Peapack Private Bank & Trust

BEDMINSTER, NJ - May 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation (NASDAQ Global Select Market: PGC) and Peapack Private Bank & Trust announce that Assimina “Mina” Nikiforos has joined the Bank as a Senior Managing Director with the Bank’s Commercial Banking division.

 Mina, who is based out of the Bank’s newest location in Garden City, New York, is a successful banker with more than 20 years of experience in financial services. She joins Peapack Private Bank & Trust from Flagstar Bank, Astoria, NY, where she most recently served as a vice president and group director, responsible for enhanced customer satisfaction through the development of service improvement initiatives and ensuring compliance with company regulations and industry standards through regular audits.

 Prior to her 20 years at Flagstar, formerly Signature Bank, Mina worked at Marathon Bank as a Branch Manager, where she provided guidance and support to team members, focusing on customer service, and building strong client relationships.

 About the Company

 Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation is a New Jersey bank holding company with total assets of $7.1 billion and assets under management and/or administration of $11.8 billion as of March 31, 2025. Founded in 1921, Peapack Private Bank & Trust is a commercial bank that offers a client-centric approach to banking, providing high-quality products along with customized and innovative wealth management, investment banking, commercial and personal banking solutions. The Bank’s wealth management division offers comprehensive financial, tax, fiduciary and investment advice and solutions to individuals, families, privately held businesses, family offices and not-for-profit organizations, which help them to establish, maintain and expand their legacy. Peapack Private Bank & Trust offers an unparalleled commitment to client service. Visit www.peapackprivate.com for more information.
