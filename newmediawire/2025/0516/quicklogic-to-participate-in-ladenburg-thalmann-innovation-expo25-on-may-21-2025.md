# QuickLogic to Participate in Ladenburg Thalmann Innovation EXPO25 on May 21, 2025

SAN JOSE, CA - May 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - QuickLogic Corporation (NASDAQ: QUIK) (“QuickLogic” or the “Company”), a developer of embedded FPGA (eFPGA) IP, ruggedized FPGAs and Endpoint AI solutions, today reminded investors of its participation in the Ladenburg Thalmann Innovation EXPO25, to be held on May 21, 2025, at Convene, 101 Park Avenue, New York, NY.

 “We look forward to participating in the Ladenburg Innovation EXPO25, which provides an opportunity to showcase our business momentum, including the latest eFPGA Hard IP, and allow us to connect directly with the investment community,” said Brian Faith, Chief Executive of QuickLogic.

 The Ladenburg Thalmann Innovation EXPO25 is a premier event bringing together a diverse group of innovative companies and institutional investors for a full day of presentations, one-on-one meetings, and networking opportunities. The conference will feature approximately 50 technology companies that will present their business strategies and innovations through three dedicated presentation tracks and demonstrate their products live in the “Ladenburg Expo format.”  Organized by Ladenburg Thalmann with marketing support from B2i Digital, Inc., the EXPO25 provides a unique platform for companies to engage directly with institutional and high-net-worth investors, public company executives, and industry professionals.

 The livestream of the Company’s presentation will be broadcast on QuickLogic’s IR Site Events Page at https://ir.quicklogic.com/ir-calendar and also on https://b2idigital.com/ladenburg-innovation-expo/livestream.

 “Ladenburg Thalmann is committed to building valuable connections between innovative companies and discerning investors,” said Mark Green, Managing Director, Investment Banking at Ladenburg Thalmann.

 “As the Marketing Partner, B2i Digital is focused on helping companies participating in the Ladenburg EXPO25 maximize their exposure to the investment community before, during and after the event,” said David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital, Inc. “Ladenburg Thalmann has carefully curated the presenting companies to include only the highest caliber disruptors in the technology and AI space. Our investors will value the insights they share.”

 For more information about the Ladenburg Thalmann Innovation EXPO25, visit: https://b2idigital.com/ladenburg-innovation-expo.
