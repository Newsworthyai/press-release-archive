# UAPC Launches KidSportsLive.com Nationwide

ORLANDO, FL - August 15, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - United American Petroleum Corp. (OTCMKTS: UAPC) today announced the nationwide rollout of KidSportsLive.com, a new streaming and fundraising platform for youth sports leagues and travel ball venues.

 

 KidSportsLive.com installs HD cameras at no cost to participating venues, enabling fans to watch games live or on demand through a pay-per-view system. By pairing local viewers with local business sponsors, KidSportsLive.com ensures long-term success while giving families the convenience of never missing their child’s sporting events. Revenue generated helps leagues fund field and facility upgrades, uniforms, travel expenses, and other needs - creating a sustainable fundraising model that benefits both the community and local sponsors. See Florida demo: https://kidsportslive.com/channels/florida/

 The company will be headquartered in the Kalamazoo, Michigan area and is seeking experienced regional sales representatives nationwide to partner with leagues and venues in communities of 15,000 or more residents.

 In addition to KidSportsLive.com, UAPC is expanding into restaurant and golf course management in Michigan and Indiana, with plans for national growth. The company also intends to launch an Airbnb-focused division within the next year, targeting popular U.S. vacation destinations such as Tennessee, Montana, and Maine.
