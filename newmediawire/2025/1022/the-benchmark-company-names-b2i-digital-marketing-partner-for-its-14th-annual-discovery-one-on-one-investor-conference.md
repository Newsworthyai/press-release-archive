# The Benchmark Company Names B2i Digital Marketing Partner for Its 14th Annual Discovery One-on-One Investor Conference

B2i Digital will Expand Awareness of Benchmark’s Discovery Conference to B2i Digital’s 1.4 Million-Investor Network Across the Capital Markets

 The Conference features one-on-one, in-person meetings with dynamic publicly traded micro- and small-cap companies in an intimate conference setting

 NEW YORK, NY - October 22, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - B2i Digital, Inc. has been selected as a Marketing Partner for Benchmark’s 14th Annual Discovery One-on-One Investor Conference, taking place December 4, 2025, at the New York Athletic Club in New York City. The exclusive event offers qualified institutional investors direct access to the management teams of more than 50 dynamic micro-cap and small-cap companies, many within Benchmark’s research coverage universe.

 The Discovery Conference facilitates meaningful discussions between emerging growth companies and investors through focused one-on-one and small group meetings. The day features eight 40-minute meeting slots from 8:45 AM to 3:40 PM ET, along with a luncheon panel titled “Trends & Themes in Today’s Capital Markets.” The format is entirely dedicated to direct investor engagement.

 As the Marketing Partner, B2i Digital will highlight the conference to its network of institutional and sophisticated investors across digital and social channels.

 “We are pleased to support Benchmark’s Discovery Conference, which has become a vital connection point between innovative micro- and small-cap companies and institutional investors,” said David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital. “Our collaboration reflects B2i Digital’s mission to combine face-to-face investor engagement with the scale of digital marketing - connecting sophisticated investors with today’s most promising emerging-growth companies.”

 This is an invite-only event; registration is subject to approval by Benchmark. Institutional investors may contact their Benchmark representative or Vince Curatola, Managing Director – Capital Markets at vcuratola@benchmarkcompany.com for participation details.
