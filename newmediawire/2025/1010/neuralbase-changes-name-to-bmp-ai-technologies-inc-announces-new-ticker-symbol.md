# NeuralBase Changes Name to BMP AI Technologies Inc. &amp; Announces New Ticker Symbol

DUBAI, UAE - October 10, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - BMP AI Technologies Inc. (OTC: BMPA), a next-generation artificial intelligence and blockchain development company, today announced that its common stock began trading on the OTC Markets under its new ticker symbol "BMPA" at the open of the market on October 9, 2025. The Company was formerly known as NeuralBase AI Ltd. and traded under the ticker symbol "NBBI".

 “The ticker change to ‘BMPA’ is an exciting and important step for our company, aligning our market identity with our corporate brand,” stated Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of BMP AI Technologies Inc. “We look forward to the future as we continue to build out our company and deliver innovative AI and blockchain solutions to the enterprise market. This is a significant milestone, and we are committed to driving long-term value for our shareholders under our new symbol.”

 The ticker symbol change follows the Company's corporate name change from NeuralBase AI Ltd. to BMP AI Technologies Inc. The Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) announced the effectiveness of both the name and ticker symbol change on the daily list on October 8, 2025. The Company's CUSIP number will remain unchanged.

 "We're entering an incredibly dynamic phase of growth and innovation," Dobale added. "Our new website at www.bmpaitech.com, while still under development, should be completed soon and is expected to showcase the cutting-edge technology we're developing and the transformative impact our AI and blockchain solutions are having across multiple industries. We invite investors and partners to explore our vision and see firsthand how BMP AI is positioned at the forefront of the enterprise technology revolution. This is just the beginning of what we believe will be an extraordinary journey."

 Additional information about BMP AI Technologies Inc. is available at www.bmpaitech.com or by visiting www.sec.gov.

 About BMP AI Technologies Inc.

 BMP AI Technologies Inc. (OTC: BMPA) is a next-generation artificial intelligence and blockchain development company specializing in enterprise automation, data integrity, and decentralized business solutions. Through its technology alliances, BMP AI delivers transformative products such as HeartEase (AI-powered health diagnostics), BMP AI (enterprise chatbots), and proprietary blockchain platforms designed for secure information distribution and internal enterprise payments.
