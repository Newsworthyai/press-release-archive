# Don't Miss Cboe's Educational Webinar On Derivatives And Volatility Trading

By Johnny Rice Benzinga

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - March 26, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Join Cboe® for an educational online webinar at 11 a.m. ET Thursday, Mar. 27. The webinar will explore the diverse range of derivatives products offered by Cboe, with special emphasis on volatility instruments. Participants will be able to gain critical insights and could come away better able to navigate the current market's unpredictability.

 Cboe senior vice president and head of global derivatives Kristin Boyd will lead the discussion and will cover Cboe's product suite, including its Cboe Volatility Index® products. Boyd will deliver an expert perspective on leveraging volatility products to capture movements in implied volatility as market expectations shift.

 Comprehensive Coverage Of Market Dynamics

 The webinar will address several key areas of interest to today's traders, including:

 * Current trends in trading volume that reflect the market's directional uncertainty
* The growing activity in volatility products and how traders can capitalize on these movements
* Cboe's ongoing commitment to increasing women's representation in financial markets
* Educational initiatives designed to empower traders of all backgrounds

 Something For Everyone

 This event could appeal to a diverse audience. From new traders to experienced market gurus looking to refine their strategies, this webinar will like cover many issues of importance. Don’t miss your chance to level up your trading knowledge this Thursday, March 27.
