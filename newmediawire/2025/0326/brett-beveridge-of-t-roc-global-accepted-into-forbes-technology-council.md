# Brett Beveridge of T-ROC Global Accepted Into Forbes Technology Council

MIAMI, FL - March 26, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Brett Beveridge, the founder and CEO of The Revenue Optimization Companies (T-ROC Global), the leading provider of people and technology solutions for the global retail market, has officially joined the Forbes Technology Council – an invitation-only group for top tech leaders from around the world.

 Brett was chosen for this honor because of his deep experience in the tech and retail industries along with his proven track record of driving real business results. The community includes a select group of CEOs, CIOs, and other tech execs.

 As part of the Council, Brett will have the chance to connect with other industry leaders and share his expertise in articles and expert panels on Forbes.com. He will get to connect and collaborate with other respected leaders to help shape the future of both the retail and tech worlds.

 T-ROC is growing fast, and Brett is excited to use this platform to share the company’s experience working with top-tier clients and to keep pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in tech and retail.

 "I’ve always believed T-ROC’s success has been driven by a blend of exceptional talent and transformative technology, and I look forward to bringing fresh insights to the Forbes Council," said Brett Beveridge, CEO and Founder of T-ROC Global. “Joining the Forbes Council is an opportunity to bring fresh insights to the table and learn from other experts. I’m looking forward to what we can all achieve together."

 Want to learn more about  T-ROC Global? Visit www.trocglobal.com. You can also connect with T-ROC on Facebook, X, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

 About The Revenue Optimization Companies (T-ROC Global)

 T-ROC Global is a retail branding and consulting partner that supports companies in navigating through today's retail shopping experience, redefining the power of people and technology. T-ROC Global offers a unique combination of people-based services, applications, technology management, mystery shopping programs, actionable market research and competitive insights that support the complex needs of assisted selling. T-ROC Global's expertise and next-generation technology is delivered by a team that’s all in to drive sales, optimize performance and deliver measurable ROI for businesses every single day.

 About Forbes Councils

 Forbes Councils is a collective of invitation-only communities created in partnership with Forbes and the expert community builders who founded Young Entrepreneur Council (YEC). In Forbes Councils, exceptional business owners and leaders come together with the people and resources that can help them thrive.

 To learn more about Forbes Councils, visit https://councils.forbes.com.
