# Louisiana Dental Association Expands Endorsement With Cloud Software Leader iCoreConnect

OCOEE, FL - February 3, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - iCoreConnect Inc. (NASDAQ: ICCT), a leading provider of cloud-based software and technology solutions, has announced an expanded endorsement from Louisiana Dental Services, Inc. (LDS), the for-profit subsidiary of the Louisiana Dental Association (LDA).

 iCoreConnect became part of the LDA Member Perks program in 2015 with the endorsement of iCoreExchange encrypted HIPAA-compliant email and referral network. In 2023, recognizing the growing challenges of insurance verification, the LDA endorsed iCoreConnect’s iCoreVerify automated insurance verification software. Now, as part of a shared commitment to helping members improve workflow efficiency, security, and profitability, the LDA has endorsed four additional iCoreConnect products:

 In addition to iCoreVerify and iCoreExchange, the LDA now endorses:

 * iCoreRx – Cloud ePrescribing + Electronic Prescribing for Controlled Substances (EPCS)
* iCorePay – Digital Patient Statements, Billing & Payments
* iCoreClaims – Dental Insurance Claims & Billing Services
* iCoreCodeGenius – Rapid Medical Coding for Dentists

 Dr. Bill Hadlock, President of LDS, shared his enthusiasm for the expanded endorsement: “For nearly a decade, we’ve worked with iCoreConnect and witnessed their unwavering commitment to innovation tailored to dental practices. These additional solutions will empower our members to utilize a more efficient and compliant prescription platform, optimize their revenue cycle management workflow, and access increased security programs to transport protected data.”

 Robert McDermott, CEO of iCoreConnect, echoed the sentiment: “The Louisiana Dental Association has been a valued partner from the beginning. Expanding our collaboration enables us to deliver even greater value to their members while advancing our mission to streamline dental practice operations and improve patient care.”

 With over 200 product endorsement agreements nationwide, iCoreConnect continues to set the standard for cloud solutions that drive efficiency, productivity, and compliance across dental practices in the U.S.

 About iCoreConnect Inc.

 iCoreConnect Inc. is a SaaS leader in cloud-based software and technology solutions, designed to enhance workflow productivity and profitability in healthcare.

 About the Louisiana Dental Association

 Established in 1878, the Louisiana Dental Association (LDA) is the largest professional health organization for dentists in Louisiana. It is a statewide grassroots organization whose purpose is to promote, advocate, and protect the dental profession. Louisiana Dental Services, Inc. (LDS) is the for-profit subsidiary of the LDA. Through LDS, the LDA endorses many high-quality products and services.
