# Beeline Holdings (BLNE) Sets Record Loan Volume Following Fed Rate Cut Announcement

LOS ANGELES, CA - October 30, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), the digital mortgage lender built for next-generation homeowners, locked 21 loans totaling nearly $8 million on Oct. 29, the same day the Federal Reserve announced its second consecutive rate cut, marking record highs for the company’s key performance indicators. The Rhode Island-based lender, which declared itself debt-free in September and expects to be cash-flow positive by Q1 2026, credits its AI-driven proprietary platform for its ability to rapidly scale as market conditions improve.
