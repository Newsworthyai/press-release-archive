# Blackwell 3D Appoints Urban Aura Interior Design LLC for Dubai South Project Interior Design

DUBAI, UAE - April 8, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce the appointment of Urban Aura Interiors as the interior design partner for its prestigious Dubai South project. This collaboration aims to create innovative and sustainable interior spaces that reflect the project’s cutting-edge vision.

 Urban Aura Interiors is renowned for its comprehensive interior fit-out solutions in Dubai. With a portfolio exceeding 30 offices, 12 restaurants, clinics, salons, gyms, and retail outlets, the firm has demonstrated expertise across diverse sectors. Their services encompass the entire project lifecycle, from conceptualization and design approvals to flawless execution. The team comprises experienced project engineers, skilled carpenters, painters, plumbers, and electrical contractors, ensuring seamless realization of complex designs.

 Notable projects by Urban Aura Interiors include:

 * Burzee Restaurant JLT (2023): A sophisticated Restaurant in JLT, blending modern aesthetics with functional design.
* Modern Office space (2023): A dynamic office environment tailored for a leading sports brand, emphasizing ergonomics and brand identity.
* Magisha Lounge Dubai Marina (2024): A luxurious restaurant showcasing bespoke interiors that reflect the esthetic touch.
* Tessa Restaurant Dubai Marina (2024): High-end Restaurant interiors that combine elegance with comfort, catering to discerning residents.

 Mohammedsaif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell 3D, stated that “we are excited to collaborate with Urban Aura Interiors for our Dubai South project. Their proven track record in delivering exceptional interior designs aligns perfectly with our vision of creating spaces that are both innovative and functional. We are confident that this partnership will result in interiors that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients and stakeholders.”

 This strategic alliance underscores Blackwell 3D’s commitment to excellence and innovation in its developments, ensuring that the Dubai South project sets new benchmarks in design and functionality.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov. Additional information regarding the project in Dubai South will be released as it becomes available.

 About Blackwell 3D Construction Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
