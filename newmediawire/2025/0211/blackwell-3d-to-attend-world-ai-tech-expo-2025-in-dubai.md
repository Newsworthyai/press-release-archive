# Blackwell 3D to Attend World AI Tech Expo 2025 in Dubai

DUBAI, UAE - February 11, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce its attendance at the World AI Tech Expo 2025, taking place in Dubai in May 2025.

 As AI continues to revolutionize industries worldwide, BDCC is eager to continue exploring the latest advancements in artificial intelligence and their applications in construction, automation, and design. The Company’s leadership sees this event as a valuable opportunity to engage with global innovators, forge strategic partnerships, and gain insights into emerging AI-driven solutions.

 While BDCC will be attending the expo as a guest this year, the company aspires to be in a position to actively participate in future editions of the event, showcasing its advancements in AI-integrated 3D construction technologies.

 “We are excited to be part of the conversation at the World AI Tech Expo 2025,” stated Mohammedsaif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell 3D. “AI is transforming the way we approach construction, and we look forward to learning from industry pioneers while positioning BDCC for a more prominent role in upcoming expos.”

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
