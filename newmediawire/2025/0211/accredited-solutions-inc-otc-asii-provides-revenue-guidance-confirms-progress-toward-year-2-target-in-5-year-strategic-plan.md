# Accredited Solutions, Inc. (OTC: ASII) Provides Revenue Guidance, Confirms Progress Toward Year 2 Target in 5-Year Strategic Plan

NEW YORK - February 11, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Accredited Solutions, Inc. (OTC: ASII) ("Accredited Solutions" or the "Company"), a diversified holding company focused on fintech and technology-driven businesses, today provided revenue guidance and reaffirmed its confidence in meeting its Year 2 revenue target as outlined in its five-year strategic plan filed with the SEC on 8/19/2024.

 As part of its growth strategy, the Company continues to scale its subsidiaries, and although no concrete agreements are in place, the Company is actively to pursuing its acquisition strategy, carrying on conversations with various suitors at the moment. Strong revenue momentum, strategic partnerships, and operational efficiencies position ASII to achieve its projected revenue growth in 2025.

 “We remain confident in our ability to meet our Year 2 revenue target as outlined in our five-year plan,” said Eduardo Brito, CEO of Accredited Solutions, Inc. “Our execution remains on track, and we continue to build on the foundation necessary for long-term growth. As we expand our fintech ecosystem and capitalize on high-demand digital transaction solutions, we are optimistic about sustaining this trajectory.”

 The Company’s five-year plan, filed with the SEC and available for public review here, details ASII’s roadmap for revenue expansion, strategic acquisitions, and continued innovation in the fintech space. The Company remains committed to transparent communication with shareholders and will provide further updates as milestones are achieved.
