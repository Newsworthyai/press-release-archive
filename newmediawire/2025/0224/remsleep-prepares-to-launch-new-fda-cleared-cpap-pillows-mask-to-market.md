# REMSleep Prepares to Launch New FDA Cleared CPAP Pillows Mask to Market

CLEARWATER, FL - February 24, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - REMSleep Holdings, Inc. (OTC: RMSL) announces the launch of new updated website dedicated for the launch of the new FDA cleared Deltawave nasal pillows CPAP mask.  The Deltawave is available to the market through the website.  We project first orders can be processed and available for shipment in approximately three weeks.  We are setting up an assembly, packaging, and shipping center dedicated to REMSleep products.

 We have filed for the REMSleep Medicare number and expect to have REMSleep Medicare number in approximately 30 days. REMSleep has sent samples of The Deltawave mask to large distributors for their review.  We obviously cannot disclose these distributors at this time, but we are corresponding with these distributors and hope to have more information in the near future.  We can say we have had a very positive response from these distributors.  Deltawave offers a new level of treatment for sleep apnea that has not been available.  Distributors and health care professionals will love and appreciate the therapeutic value and benefits of Deltawave for their sleep apnea patients.  We will soon focus on bringing the second-generation Longevity CPAP mask to market.  REMSleep CPAP masks will bring unprecedented compliance to CPAP treatment.

 About REMSleep Holdings, Inc.

 REMSleep Holdings, Inc. is a medical device manufacturer dedicated to forever changing the level of treatment provided to obstructive sleep apnea patients. Our focus is primarily designing and manufacturing devices and products for the treatment of sleep apnea and other respiratory conditions. With over 30 years of collective experience in CPAP therapy, the REMSleep team has extensive knowledge and understanding of CPAP and the challenges of patient compliance. We diligently strive for our products to make a difference and improve the condition of those suffering from sleep apnea.
