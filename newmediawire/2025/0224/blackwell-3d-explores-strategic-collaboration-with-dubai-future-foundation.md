# Blackwell 3D Explores Strategic Collaboration With Dubai Future Foundation

DUBAI, UAE - February 24, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce that it has submitted an official inquiry seeking to join the Dubai Future Foundation (DFF). This initiative aligns with BDCC’s commitment to innovation and its mission to drive the future of sustainable and efficient construction.

 The Dubai Future Foundation, renowned for its dedication to fostering innovation and technological advancements, serves as a dynamic hub for forward-thinking enterprises. By engaging with DFF, BDCC aims to explore opportunities for collaboration in cutting-edge construction methodologies, smart city development, and sustainable infrastructure solutions.

 “As a company seeking to be at the forefront of 3D construction technology, BDCC is excited about the prospect of working alongside Dubai Future Foundation to shape the future of the built environment,” stated Mohammedsaif Zaveri, CEO of BDCC. “Dubai’s commitment to technological advancement and sustainability makes it a prime destination for pioneering construction solutions. We look forward to exploring how BDCC can contribute to and benefit from this visionary ecosystem.”

 BDCC’s inquiry into joining DFF is part of its broader strategy to expand its global presence and leverage strategic partnerships that drive innovation. By aligning with DFF’s initiatives, BDCC seeks to enhance its research and development efforts, engage in high-impact collaborations, and contribute to transformative projects that redefine the construction industry.

 BDCC remains committed to pioneering solutions that revolutionize the construction landscape. As discussions with DFF progress, the company will provide updates.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
