# Vstock Transfer to Serve as Bronze Sponsor of Spartan Capital Securities' Second Annual Investor Conference

Premier Event Showcases Growth Companies and Fosters High-Level Investor Engagement

 NEW YORK, NY - October 24, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Vstock Transfer today announced that it will serve as a Bronze sponsor of the Spartan Capital Securities' Second Annual Investor Conference, taking place on November 3, 2025, at the Marriott Marquis Hotel in New York City.

 By sponsoring this year’s event, Vstock Transfer is reinforcing its commitment to supporting innovative companies and fostering meaningful engagement between issuers and the investment community. The conference is recognized as one of the premier gatherings for growth companies, institutional investors, and industry leaders.

 Vstock Transfer is a trusted stock transfer agent that supports public and private companies through every stage of the capital markets process with accuracy, speed, and personalized service.

 “Sponsoring the Spartan Capital Conference reflects our commitment to the ecosystem of CEOs and investors driving the next generation of IPOs and SPACs,” said Seth Farbman, Co-Founder of Vstock Transfer. “Our role is to support companies through every stage of going public with precision, transparency, and a deep understanding of what success in the capital markets truly requires.”

 "We are proud to have Vstock Transfer join us as a Bronze sponsor for our second annual investor conference," said John D. Lowry, Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Spartan Capital Securities, LLC. "Their support underscores the caliber of this event and our shared commitment to fostering meaningful connections in the investment community. Vstock’s participation will enrich the experience for all attendees."

 Organized in partnership with B2i Digital, the conference will feature presentations from more than 60 carefully selected companies across technology, healthcare, consumer, mining, precious metals, and other high-opportunity sectors. Over 500 institutional and high-net-worth investors are expected to attend.

 "We are grateful to welcome Vstock Transfer as a sponsor of this important event," said David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital. "Sponsors play a critical role in making conferences like this possible, and we sincerely value their support. Vstock Transfer’s involvement not only strengthens our ability to create meaningful connections between investors and presenting companies but also helps us advance B2i Digital’s core mission - combining the reach of digital marketing with the impact of face-to-face engagement."

 The program will include main-stage presentations, panel discussions, curated one-on-one meetings, and extensive networking sessions designed to maximize interactions among investors, presenting companies, and sponsors.
