# NeuralBase Announces the Launch of Its Enterprise AI Chatbot

LAS VEGAS, NV - August 26, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI and recognized as a fast-growing innovator in enterprise artificial intelligence, is proud to announce its innovative and forward-looking context‑aware AI chatbot widget that empowers businesses to integrate intelligent, adaptive conversations directly into their websites, with the goal of minimal hassle and designed for fast deployment.

 “The launch of our BMP AI chatbot widget is a defining milestone for NeuralBase,” stated Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of NeuralBase AI Ltd. “It reflects our commitment to delivering enterprise-ready AI that is not only powerful and context-aware, but also simple enough for any business to deploy in minutes. This achievement positions us to accelerate adoption and to demonstrate the real-world value of AI in transforming customer engagement.”

 The BMP AI platform offers enterprises several important differentiators designed to simplify adoption and improve outcomes:

 * Rapid integration: Designed for quick deployment with simple HTML/JavaScript embedding, requiring minimal setup.
* Context awareness: Built to recognize which page a visitor is on and adapt responses accordingly.
* Customization options: Supports developer‑defined prompts and welcome messages to fine‑tune tone, behavior, and flow.

 Some key features of the newly launched AI chatbot widget include the following:

 * With just two minutes of implementation, businesses can embed BMP AI’s AI widget via simple HTML and JavaScript. No backend infrastructure, no complex configuration - just install and deploy.
* BMP AI's chatbot is designed to go beyond responding - by contextualizing content on your site. Through context scanning, the AI identifies which page a visitor is on and tailors responses accordingly - without manual tagging or pre-training.
* Prefer a tighter control? BMP AI supports manual system prompts and custom welcome messages - letting developers fine-tune tone, behavior, and flow for different sections of the site.

 For enterprises, BMP AI matters because it provides a practical balance of efficiency, ease of use, and customer engagement. Its benefits include:

 * Faster deployment: Provides speed to market compared with traditional enterprise chatbot builds.
* Simple operations: Eliminates the need for training data, infrastructure, or ongoing model maintenance.
* Enhanced customer experience: Context sensitivity delivers more relevant, page‑specific assistance.
* Balanced solution: Lightweight, front‑end tool designed to complement broader enterprise AI strategies.

 For more information about our Enterprise AI ChatBot, please visit www.bmpaitech.com. Additional information about the Company is available at www.neuralbase.ai or by visiting www.sec.gov.
