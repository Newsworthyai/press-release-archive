# NeuralBase Provides Development Update on HeartEase App for Android and iOS Platforms

LAS VEGAS, NV - August 1, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI, today announced that the Android version of HeartEase is now complete and undergoing final testing. The public release of the Android app is expected in the coming weeks, and the iOS version is scheduled to enter testing shortly thereafter.

 HeartEase is designed to provide AI-powered cardiovascular and emotional health insights through intelligent monitoring, real-time feedback, and personalized wellness recommendations. The app leverages NeuralBase AI’s proprietary AI platform, behavioral insights, and biometric data analysis to create an adaptive user experience that supports proactive wellness management.

 “Achieving this development milestone marks an important step toward bringing HeartEase to the market,” said Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of NeuralBase AI Ltd. “We are pleased with the progress of the Android version and are preparing for the iOS testing phase, which will ensure feature parity and performance consistency across platforms.”

 The readiness of the Android version moves HeartEase closer to real-world deployment. Launching the app will:

 * Validate the platform’s technology stack in real-world conditions.
* Provide essential user feedback to guide further refinements and enhancements.
* Lay the foundation for the iOS release and ensure platform consistency.
* Support potential strategic partnerships with healthcare providers, wellness platforms, and wearable technology companies.
* Position NeuralBase AI to scale its AI-powered digital health ecosystem as part of its broader mission to support enterprise wellness and preventive health diagnostics.

 The Company will share further updates regarding HeartEase’s release schedule, platform enhancements, and strategic initiatives as testing progresses and new milestones are achieved.

 The closing of the HeartEase transaction is expected by no later than September 15, 2025. NeuralBase will provide further updates if and when available, including filing all necessary disclosures with the Securities & Exchange Commission via EDGAR.

 Additional information about NeuralBase AI Ltd. is available at https://www.neuralbase.ai or by visiting https://www.sec.gov.
