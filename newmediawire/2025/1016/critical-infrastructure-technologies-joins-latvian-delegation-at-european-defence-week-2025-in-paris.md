# Critical Infrastructure Technologies Joins Latvian Delegation at European Defence Week 2025 in Paris

VANCOUVER, BC - October 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Critical Infrastructure Technologies Ltd. (CSE: CTTT) (OTC: CITLF) (FRA: X9V) (“CiTech” or the “Company”), a leading developer of autonomous, high-capacity mobile communications and security platforms, is pleased to announce it will form part of the Latvian delegation participating in European Defence Week 2025, from October 27 to 31, hosted by Bpifrance and EuroQuity in Paris. The event brings together leading defence innovators, policymakers, and investors from across Europe to strengthen cooperation, build resilience, and accelerate dual-use technology development.

 CiTech’s participation underscores its growing engagement within the European Defence and Security ecosystem, working alongside key industry and institutional partners to advance its Nexus 20 Rapid Deployment Platform incorporating multi-role communications, surveillance, and counter-UAS system, designed for rapid field deployment and operational flexibility.

 “We are proud to represent Latvia’s growing contribution to European defence innovation,” said Brenton Scott, CEO of CiTech. “Being part of the Latvian delegation provides a unique opportunity to collaborate with European and NATO partners on the development of technologies that enhance resilience, connectivity, and frontline capability.”

 Latvia’s Strategic Role in Europe’s Defence Landscape

 Latvia’s geopolitical position at the frontline of NATO and the European Union, its leadership in the European Drone Coalition, and its enduring partnership with Ukraine, built on deep cultural and historical ties, make its engagement in the European defence and security ecosystem both timely and essential.

 The Latvian delegation’s participation highlights the nation’s commitment to advancing collective security, technological sovereignty, and regional defence cooperation across the European continent, of which CiTech is proud to be part of.
