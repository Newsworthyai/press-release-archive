# Beeline Holdings (BLNE) Expands Warehouse Lending Capacity to $25M to Support Rapid Growth

LOS ANGELES, CA - October 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), a fast-growing digital mortgage platform transforming the homeownership process, expanded its warehouse lending capacity from $5 million to $25 million to accelerate revenue growth and loan origination volume. The Company’s existing $5 million line with First Funding increased to $15 million, with new $5 million facilities added from Customers Bank and Northpointe Bank. With warehouse lines turning about three times per month, Beeline can now originate up to $75 million in monthly loans amid rising demand ahead of anticipated interest rate cuts.
