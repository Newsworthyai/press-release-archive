# NeuralBase AI Announces Strategic Beta Testing Collaboration With Vibes Multientertainment for BMP AI Chatbot Platform

LAS VEGAS, NV - June 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI and an emerging leader in enterprise artificial intelligence solutions, has entered into a strategic collaboration with Vibes Multientertainment (“VM Entertainment”), a premier multimedia and entertainment company based in Mumbai. Under the terms of this initial collaboration, VM Entertainment will become one of the first companies to participate in the beta testing of NeuralBase’s BMP AI Chatbot.

 Currently in its final development phase, the BMP AI Chatbot is a compliance-aware, intelligent virtual assistant designed to streamline document processing and client engagement, particularly in regulated industries. As part of the collaboration, VM Entertainment will gain early access to the beta version of the chatbot and provide essential real-world feedback to help refine and optimize its performance.

 “We are thrilled to welcome Vibes as an early partner in our beta testing program,” said Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase AI Ltd. “Their diverse work across event management, brand marketing, and artist representation makes them an ideal collaborator to evaluate how our AI performs in high-impact, creative environments.”

 VM Entertainment plans to integrate the BMP AI Chatbot into its operations to:

 - Enhance workflow efficiency and communication- Automate support and ticketing systems- Deliver AI-enhanced search and content support for client campaigns

 “We’re excited to be part of this next wave of innovation,” said Pujan Jobalia, Director at VM Entertainment. “Our partnership with NeuralBase perfectly aligns with our mission to harness transformative technology to elevate both client experiences and internal operations.”

 The agreement also includes provisions for potential co-branded pilots and a roadmap to finalize a broader commercial partnership by September 2025.

 More information about the company is available at www.neuralbase.ai or by visiting www.sec.gov.

 NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI) is an AI company developing secure, scalable, and context-aware conversational agents and workflow automation systems.
