# MWG Holdings' Director of State and Local Licensing Chelsea Haskins to Speak at Morro Bay Chamber of Commerce Event 'The Art of SLO Living' on June 18

Flagship Retail Brand Perfect Union to Sponsor Breakfast on the Bay Seminar in Morro Bay, California

 SACRAMENTO, CALIFORNIA - June 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - MWG Holdings Group, Inc. (“MWG Holdings”), a leading purpose-driven, locally-focused cannabis company with award-winning roots in the California medical and recreational markets, announced today that Chelsea Haskins, Director of State and Local Licensing for Perfect Union, will speak at Breakfast on the Bay, a seminar hosted by the Morro Bay Chamber of Commerce, on June 18 at the Morro Bay Community Center, 1001 Kennedy Way, Morro Bay, California.

 The event, “The Art of SLO Living: Embracing the Central Coast Lifestyle,” brings together local businesses and community leaders to explore how the Central Coast can grow sustainably while staying true to what makes it unique. Haskins will present ‘Strengthening Community Through Local Commitment’ and discuss how Perfect Union cultivates lasting community ties through compassion, collaboration, and pride in the Central Coast; themes that reflect MWG’s commitment to being an engaged and contributing partner in the region.

 Haskins brings a strong legal and regulatory background to her work in cannabis licensing and compliance. With a degree in criminal justice from CSU Chico and experience in both local government and the legal system, she specializes in navigating complex state and local cannabis regulations. Since joining Perfect Union in 2020, Haskins has led licensing efforts across multiple states and now serves as Chair of the National Cannabis Industry Association’s Cannabis Cultivation Committee, contributing her expertise to policy discussions at the national level.

 “It’s an honor to speak alongside others who care deeply about the Central Coast,” said Chelsea Haskins, Director of State and Local Licensing at Perfect Union. “Strong community relationships are essential to our work in cannabis. Events like this give us a chance to share ideas, listen, and continue building partnerships that reflect the values of the communities we serve.”

 Perfect Union continues to strengthen its community involvement across California through ongoing outreach, donations, and local sponsorships. Since its founding in 2011, MWG Holdings has made community benefit a core pillar of its business model, fostering trust, building long-term partnerships, and reinforcing customer loyalty in every region it serves.

 “Supporting the communities where we operate has been central to our mission since day one,” said Tom Sheridan, CEO of MWG Holdings. “We’re proud to see Chelsea share her perspective at Breakfast on the Bay, an event that aligns perfectly with our commitment to public engagement, local collaboration, and thoughtful industry participation.”

 About MWG Holdings

 MWG Holdings Group, Inc. is a California-based cannabis holding company with a portfolio spanning award-winning consumer brands and retail operations, including Perfect Union, one of the state’s top five retail chains.

 Founded in 2017, MWG owns and operates nine retail locations, including eight Perfect Union stores and one Wild Seed Wellness store, all of which achieved record-setting sales in 2024. The company's flagship Perfect Union store is based in Sacramento, California, where MWG began as Magnolia Wellness 15 years ago.

 MWG's retail stores are consistently recognized for their award-winning brands and community-driven approach. Its management team brings over a decade of experience building profitable cannabis businesses, having raised millions in capital, and bringing the company to cash flow positivity.

 MWG’s diverse and growing portfolio, featuring top brands like Blazy, 5G Pop, Cap City, 530 Grower, and Shake That, solidifies its standing as an industry leader.
