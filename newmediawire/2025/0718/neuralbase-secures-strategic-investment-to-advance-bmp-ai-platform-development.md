# NeuralBase Secures Strategic Investment to Advance BMP AI Platform Development

LAS VEGAS, NV - July 18, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI and recognized as a growing innovator in enterprise artificial intelligence, today announced a strategic investment financing agreement with Fifth Wall Building Contracting LLC, an infrastructure firm based in the United Arab Emirates. Subject to the terms of the agreement, Fifth Wall intends to invest up to USD $2 million in NeuralBase to support the continued advancement and prospective commercialization of its BMP AI platform.

 Fifth Wall, recognized for its track record in large-scale infrastructure projects across the Middle East, is expected to leverage this investment as a potential first step into emerging technology sectors, with an emphasis on long-term global impact. The proposed financing is anticipated to facilitate mutual innovation, particularly in areas where AI may intersect with smart infrastructure, predictive maintenance, and operational efficiency.

 “This prospective investment represents a significant endorsement of our long-term vision for BMP AI,” stated Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of NeuralBase. “We believe artificial intelligence is fast becoming a foundational layer of enterprise infrastructure. With the support of Fifth Wall, we may be better positioned to pursue our mission of delivering scalable, compliant AI solutions that address real-world enterprise needs.”

 Benefits of the Investment:

 * Innovation Synergies: Potential AI integrations in smart infrastructure, building automation, and operational efficiency align with Fifth Wall’s long-term development goals.
* First-Mover Advantage: This early-stage investment into BMP AI Technologies provides the company with influence in a rapidly scaling tech firm.
* Sustainable Growth: AI solutions offer scalable and sustainable tools that can enhance both internal operations and external service offerings.

 The investment will be disbursed in phases, aligned with BMP AI’s roadmap milestones, and will support product development, hiring, and go-to-market efforts.

 The BMP AI platform, currently in beta testing, is being designed as a secure, document-grounded, explainable AI system with enterprise-grade compliance capabilities. It is intended to serve regulated industries such as finance, legal, healthcare, and government.

 Additional information about NeuralBase is available at www.neuralbase.ai or by visiting www.sec.gov.

 About NEURALBASE AI LTD.

 NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI) is a publicly traded AI company focused on secure, scalable, and context-aware conversational systems and workflow automation. Through its BMP AI platform, currently in beta testing, the Company enables enterprises to streamline internal operations, enhance team collaboration, and improve productivity while maintaining compliance and data security.
