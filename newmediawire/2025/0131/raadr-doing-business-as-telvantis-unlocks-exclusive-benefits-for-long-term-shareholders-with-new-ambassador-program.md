# Raadr (Doing Business as Telvantis) Unlocks Exclusive Benefits for Long-Term Shareholders With New Ambassador Program

NEW YORK - January 31, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Raadr Inc., now doing business as Telvantis (OTC PINK: RDAR), a rapidly expanding US-based telecommunications company poised for significant growth in the U.S. and international markets, today announced the launch of its exclusive Shareholder Ambassador Program. Designed to engage loyal followers and investors, this program offers a unique opportunity for shareholders who share Telvantis's vision for long-term growth and innovation in the dynamic telecom sector.

 Program Highlights:

 • Exclusive networking opportunities: gain first-hand access to a network of like-minded, long-term Telvantis investors. Build valuable connections, share insights, and engage in strategic discussions about the company’s future.

 • Recognition and appreciation: receive formal recognition as a valued Telvantis Ambassador, including a personalized certificate of dedication and the opportunity to be featured in select company communications.

 • Unique rewards: enjoy exclusive perks such as Telvantis-branded merchandise, priority invitations to investor events and updates, and curated insights on industry trends and innovations—all shared in full compliance with regulatory guidelines.

 • Community impact: join a select group of shareholders committed to shaping a positive narrative and having a voice in the company’s long-term growth. Become a member of a distinguished group of shareholders dedicated to fostering a positive narrative and contributing to the company's sustained growth. Ambassadors will play a key role in strengthening trust and confidence within the Telvantis community.

 How to Join:

 Become a Telvantis Ambassador today! Space in this exclusive program is limited. Sign up now at https://telvantis.com/investors/ambassador-program/ to secure your spot. Upon enrollment, you'll receive a welcome email with details on accessing the private Ambassador community forum, hosted on platforms such as Facebook and Discord.

 CEO Vision:

 “The Telvantis Ambassador Program is about more than just rewards; it's about building a powerful community of investors who are as passionate about our future as we are," said Daniel Contreras, CEO of Telvantis. "This program gives our most dedicated shareholders a stronger voice and a unique platform to engage with us and contribute to our growth story. While we remain fully committed to timely and transparent public disclosures, this program provides a unique avenue for deeper engagement and shared success."

 About Telvantis Inc.

 Telvantis Inc. is a dynamic, U.S.-based telecommunications company rapidly expanding its footprint through innovative solutions and strategic acquisitions. Following the successful integration of Mexedia's U.S. and Ireland operations, Telvantis now offers a comprehensive suite of cutting-edge voice, data, and cloud-based services, positioning the company for significant growth in the global telecom market.
