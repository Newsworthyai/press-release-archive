# Skkynet Reports Record Sales Growth and Profitability for Fiscal Year 2024

Strong Demand for Secure Industrial Data Solutions Drives Revenue Growth and Fuels Optimism for 2025

 MISSISSAUGA, ON - February 4, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Skkynet Cloud Systems, Inc. (“Skkynet” or “the Company”) (OTCQB: SKKY), a global leader in real-time data communications technology for industrial systems, today announced record-breaking financial results for the fiscal year ending October 31, 2024. The Company achieved $2.56 million in annual revenue, reflecting an 8% year-over-year increase, alongside a profitable year with net income of $99,090—strengthening its financial position as it looks ahead to continued growth in 2025.

 “Skkynet’s momentum continues to build,” said Andrew Thomas, CEO. “Despite economic challenges, we delivered solid results and maintained a strong cash position, readying us for expansion in 2025 and beyond. The demand for secure remote access to industrial systems is surging—driven in part by security demands and AI applications—and we anticipate even greater growth as the industry rebounds.”

 Aarif Nakhooha, Senior Advisor to Skkynet, commented on the company’s performance and its focus on innovation. “Skkynet’s ability to adapt to market trends and integrate cutting-edge technologies like AI has been pivotal in driving its success,” Nakhooha said. “As industries increasingly rely on automation and real-time data solutions, Skkynet is well-positioned to capitalize on these opportunities. I’m confident that the company’s investments in AI-driven projects will further enhance its offerings and fuel sustained growth in the coming years.”

 2024 Milestones and Strategic Initiatives:

 * Launch of Cogent DataHub® V11 – featuring new, cutting edge security enhancements.
* Expansion of the sales team to accelerate market penetration.
* Engagement in AI-related projects set to unfold in 2025.
* Strengthened intellectual property portfolio, with 13 issued U.S. patents covering secure remote access and data management technologies.

 “2024 was a transformative year for Skkynet,” said Paul Thomas, President. “Customer adoption has surged, with both longtime clients upgrading their systems and new industries discovering our technology. The overwhelmingly positive response to our latest innovations confirms our position as a trusted leader in secure industrial connectivity.”

 Looking Ahead

 Skkynet remains at the forefront of digital transformation, empowering industrial enterprises to securely connect, monitor, and control their systems in real time—without exposing sensitive data to external networks. As businesses increasingly embrace AI-driven automation, Skkynet’s solutions are more relevant than ever, ensuring seamless, secure, and scalable industrial data communications.

 For more information, visit skkynet.com.

 About Skkynet

 Skkynet Cloud Systems, Inc. (OTCQB: SKKY) provides real-time, secure, and reliable communication technologies for industrial automation, AI-driven operations, and Industry 4.0. Skkynet’s solutions empower organizations to connect, monitor, and control their systems in real time while maintaining robust security and data privacy standards.
