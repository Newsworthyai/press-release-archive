# Blackwell 3D Engages ARCS Group to Develop AI-Powered App for Instant Home Design

DUBAI, UAE - February 4, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (BDCC), a leader in innovative 3D printing solutions for the construction sector, is pleased to announce the development of an AI-driven application in collaboration with ARCS Group (www.arcscorp.com). This newly developed technology is anticipated to empower users to create 3D models of homes or villas simply by using voice commands, text descriptions, or image inputs - eliminating the traditionally time-consuming and expensive architectural design process.

 By leveraging AI, the app is expected to allow customers to customize every aspect of their property instantly, from layout to interior design, without incurring additional costs. This innovation places the entire architectural and development process at the customer’s fingertips, significantly reducing the need for prolonged engagements with architects and contractors.

 “Our goal is to disrupt the industry by making property design as simple as speaking or uploading a photo,” said Blackwell 3D CEO Mohammedsaif Zaveri. “This app is not only expected to enhance customer experience but also to open a new revenue stream for us through licensing agreements on a larger scale.”

 In addition to direct consumer use, Blackwell 3D expects the technology to be integrated into a recently signed land development project, demonstrating its real-world application and effectiveness in streamlining the planning and construction process.

 “Once fully developed and launched, this AI-powered tool is expected to set a new standard in real estate design, making it faster, more affordable, and highly customizable,” concluded Zaveri.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit our corporate website at www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
