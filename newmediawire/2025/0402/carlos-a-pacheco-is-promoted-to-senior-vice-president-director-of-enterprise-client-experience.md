# Carlos A. Pacheco is Promoted to Senior Vice President, Director of Enterprise Client Experience

BEDMINSTER, NJ - April 2, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation (NASDAQ Global Select Market: PGC) and Peapack Private Bank & Trust are pleased to announce the promotion of Carlos A. Pacheco to Senior Vice President, Director of Enterprise Client Experience. In this new role, Carlos will develop and implement strategies across the organization that ensure every client interaction is positive and impactful.

 Carlos is now responsible for leading the Bank's client relationship surveys to drive improvements, implementing transformative changes across the organization’s client contact center, driving the adoption of artificial intelligence initiatives with the goal of enhancing client interactions, spearheading bank-wide process improvements and executing experience management strategies to continuously improve client satisfaction. He is committed to fostering a collaborative environment to drive organizational success and ensure that the Bank’s strategies align with client needs and expectations.

 Carlos has been with Peapack Private since 2018, successfully managing the deposit operations and electronic banking divisions, where he placed a strong emphasis on process automation and the organization’s digital banking strategy. A highly qualified and professional banker, with 25 years of extensive experience in banking and operations, he joined Peapack Private from New Jersey Community Bank (NJCB) where, as the head of operations, he managed a team of branch locations, overseeing retail sales, administration, and customer service. Prior to NJCB, Carlos was the retail operations manager at 1st Constitution Bank. He also worked for National City Bank, Orlando, Florida and Independence Community Bank in Staten Island, NY.

 Carlos attended The College of Staten Island (CUNY), has completed course work with the Center for Financial Training (CFT) and has completed Community Bankers Financial Education (CBFE) training programs including Bank Secrecy Act, Bank Security and Retail Banking training.

 About the Company

 Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation is a New Jersey bank holding company with total assets of $7.0 billion and assets under management and/or administration of $11.9 billion as of December 31, 2024. Founded in 1921, Peapack Private Bank & Trust is a commercial bank that offers a client-centric approach to banking, providing high-quality products along with customized and innovative wealth management, investment banking, commercial and personal banking solutions. The Bank’s wealth management division offers comprehensive financial, tax, fiduciary and investment advice and solutions to individuals, families, privately held businesses, family offices and not-for-profit organizations, which help them to establish, maintain and expand their legacy. Peapack Private Bank & Trust offers an unparalleled commitment to client service. Visit www.peapackprivate.com for more information.
