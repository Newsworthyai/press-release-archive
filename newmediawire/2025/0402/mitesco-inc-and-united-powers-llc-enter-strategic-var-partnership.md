# Mitesco, Inc. and United Powers, LLC Enter Strategic VAR Partnership

VERO BEACH, FLORIDA - April 2, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Mitesco, Inc. (OTC: MITI, www.mitescoinc.com ), a leading provider of cutting-edge data center solutions through its business unit Centcore, LLC, is pleased to announce a new strategic partnership with United Powers, LLC. This collaboration will allow United Powers to become an official value-added reseller (VAR) of Centcore’s products and services, significantly enhancing the company's reach and service offerings.

 The partnership will see United Powers market and resell Centcore's innovative cloud computing, data management and infrastructure solutions, driving growth in key markets.

 “We are excited to partner with United Powers, LLC to expand Centcore’s reach and provide more businesses with access to our state-of-the-art data center solutions,” said Brian Valania, General Manager of Centcore. He continued,  “United Powers has a proven track record in the industry and we’re confident that this collaboration will create long-term value for both parties. The value-added reseller agreement marks a significant step toward Centcore’s growth and reinforces our commitment to delivering scalable and efficient cloud-based infrastructure.”

 Centcore Data Center, a division of Mitesco, Inc., is rapidly establishing itself as a top choice for businesses looking for reliable, secure and high-performance cloud services. The strategic collaboration with United Powers will enable Centcore to expand its customer base and continue delivering high-quality, scalable solutions that meet the needs of modern businesses.

 “We believe Centcore offers an exceptional suite of products that will transform the way businesses handle their cloud infrastructure and data management needs,” said Jerry Powers, Managing Partner of United Powers, LLC. “This partnership offers significant opportunities for our clients, and we are eager to start working closely with Centcore to provide best-in-class solutions.”

 For more information about Centcore and its services, visit www.centcoreusa.com.
