# MWG Holdings CEO Tom Sheridan, Esq., to Speak on High Finance at Hall of Flowers Ventura, March 19-20

Award-winning Company to Showcase smish. Magazine, Flagship Cannabis and Lifestyle Publication

 SACRAMENTO, CA - March 13, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - MWG Holdings Group, Inc. (“MWG”), a leading purpose-driven, locally-focused cannabis company with award-winning roots in the California medical and recreational markets, is set to make a strong impact at Hall of Flowers Ventura, March 19-20. CEO Tom Sheridan, Esq., will take the stage for an insightful panel discussion titled “High Finance.” Additionally, MWG will debut the spring edition of its flagship magazine, smish., at Hall of Flowers, with copies available at prominent locations throughout the venue.

 On March 19 at 1 p.m. PST, Sheridan will join Kristi Kem and Kyle Sherman to discuss how cannabis businesses can effectively secure and deploy capital in an unpredictable, rapidly evolving industry. The conversation will address key challenges such as navigating regulatory shifts, accessing funding, and scaling operations in a turbulent financial landscape.

 “As cannabis continues to mature as an industry, strategic capital deployment is more critical than ever,” said Sheridan. “I look forward to sharing real-world solutions that help businesses thrive despite constant market fluctuations.”

 Sheridan brings a wealth of experience in strategic capital deployment, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance. His legal expertise and business acumen make him a key voice in shaping the future of cannabis finance.

 Hall of Flowers attendees can connect with key leaders from MWG’s C-suite team to explore strategic partnerships, learn about innovative retail marketing strategies, and inquire about distribution solutions.

 “Hall of Flowers is a cornerstone event for the California cannabis market, creating a vital space for retailers and top brands to connect, collaborate, and drive the industry forward,” said Shawn Samery, VP of Retail at Perfect Union, MWG’s leading dispensary chain in Northern California. “This show allows us to engage directly with partners, showcase our innovative retail strategies, and continue shaping the future of cannabis retail in California.”

 Hall of Flowers, the world’s premier cannabis trade show and expo, brings together top brands, retailers, industry professionals, and cannabis connoisseurs to explore new trends, products and partnerships in the industry.

 Learn more about MWG Holdings, Inc.’s Perfect Union and Wild Seed Wellness stores at https://www.perfect-union.com/ and wildseedwellness.com.

 About MWG Holdings

 MWG Holdings Group, Inc. is California's largest wholly-owned, vertically-integrated cannabis company. Founded in 2017, MWG owns and operates ten retail locations, including nine Perfect Union stores and one Wild Seed Wellness store, all of which achieved record-setting sales in 2024. The company's flagship Perfect Union store and its Fireworx Farms indoor grow are based in Sacramento, California, where MWG began as Magnolia Wellness 15 years ago. MWG's retail stores are consistently recognized for their award-winning brands and community-driven approach.

 MWG’s management team leverages over a decade of experience building profitable cannabis businesses, having raised millions in capital and bringing the company to cash flow positivity. MWG’s diverse and growing portfolio, featuring top brands like Blazy, 5G Pop, Cap City, 530 Grower, and Shake That solidifies its standing as an industry leader.

 MWG’s print magazine, smish., is available at all its retail store locations. Read smish. online at www.thesmishspot.com. Committed to quality, innovation, and local community engagement, MWG continues to set new standards in the cannabis sector. Learn more at www.perfect-union.com.
