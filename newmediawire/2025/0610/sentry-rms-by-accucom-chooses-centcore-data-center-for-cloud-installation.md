# Sentry RMS by Accucom Chooses Centcore Data Center for Cloud Installation

VERO BEACH, FLORIDA - June 10, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Centcore, (www.centcoreusa.com ), a division of Mitesco, Inc. (OTC:MITI, www.mitescoinc.com ), is proud to announce that Sentry RMS, a leading provider of public safety software solutions by Accucom, Inc. (www.accucomci.com ), has selected Centcore Data Center for the installation of its cloud infrastructure.Sentry RMS is known for its innovative solutions that support law enforcement, fire departments, and emergency medical services with robust records management, reporting, and analytics tools. After an extensive evaluation of cloud infrastructure providers, Sentry RMS chose Centcore due to its cutting-edge technology, strong commitment to security, and seamless scalability.Centcore Data Center, which operates as a division of Mitesco, Inc. (MITI), is rapidly establishing itself as a top choice for businesses looking for reliable, secure, and high-performance cloud services. Centcore’s state-of-the-art facility is certified with SOC 2, Tier 3+, and CGIS certifications, ensuring that Sentry RMS’ sensitive data is handled with the highest level of security, privacy, and compliance. These certifications guarantee superior uptime, redundancy, and the secure management of criminal justice data, making Centcore the ideal provider for Sentry RMS’ public safety solutions.“We are thrilled to partner with Sentry RMS and Accucom in providing them with a world-class cloud infrastructure that can scale with their evolving needs,” said Mack Leath, CEO at Mitesco, Inc. “Our team is excited to support their mission of enhancing public safety and helping them deliver more efficient and effective services to communities nationwide.”Sentry RMS will leverage Centcore's high-performance cloud environment to ensure reliable, secure, and accessible service for its clients. This collaboration will enable Sentry RMS to scale more efficiently, optimize their operational capabilities, and enhance their cloud-based offerings.For more information about Centcore and its services, visit www.centcoreusa.com.About Centcore Data CenterThe Centcore Data Center is a state-of-the-art cloud services provider and a division of Mitesco, Inc. (OTC:MITI). Located in Melbourne, Florida, Centcore offers secure, reliable, and scalable cloud infrastructure solutions designed to meet the needs of modern businesses. As a trusted provider for industries ranging from technology to public safety, Centcore is dedicated to delivering innovative solutions for clients seeking to enhance their cloud capabilities.
