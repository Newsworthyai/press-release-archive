# Blackwell 3D Places Order for Advanced 3D Printing Machine, Marking a Major Milestone in Construction Innovation

DUBAI, UAE - February 14, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is thrilled to announce that it has officially placed an order for a cutting-edge 3D printing machine, marking a significant step forward in its mission to revolutionize the construction industry. To confirm this strategic investment, the Company has made an initial advance payment, reinforcing its commitment to leveraging next-generation technology for faster, more efficient, and sustainable building solutions.

 A Major Milestone in the Future of Construction

 This acquisition represents a pivotal moment for Blackwell 3D as it prepares to introduce large-scale 3D-printed structures to the Dubai real estate market. The new technology will enable the company to streamline construction processes, reduce material waste, and unlock unprecedented architectural possibilities.

 Mohammad Saif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell 3D, highlighted the impact of this investment:

 “This marks a new era for construction in the region. With this 3D printing technology, we are setting the foundation for a more efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly approach to building. Our goal is to redefine how structures are designed and delivered, making high-quality, sustainable housing and commercial spaces more accessible.”

 “We have been working for months to design the best 3D printer for use with our AI technology and we are proud to announce our first order of this new printer that we are terming ‘Blackwell One’ has been ordered. We expect that our cutting-edge printer will take us to the next level.”

 Key Benefits of the 3D Printing Machine

 • Accelerated Construction – Reduces project timelines dramatically, allowing buildings to be completed in a fraction of the time compared to traditional methods.

 • Cost Savings – Lowers labor costs and optimizes material usage, making construction more affordable.

 • Eco-Friendly – Reduces construction waste and carbon footprint, supporting global sustainability goals.

 • Advanced Design Capabilities – Enables the creation of intricate, customizable structures with high precision.

 Mitesh Rasaikar, CTO of Blackwell 3D, stated:

 “This is a game-changing moment for us. The capabilities of this 3D printing machine will allow us to bring innovative, sustainable, and structurally sound designs to life at an unprecedented scale. We are not just building structures; we are shaping the future of construction technology, making it smarter, faster, and more eco-friendly. We chose this manufacturer as they will be able to meet our demands of future printers as we ramp up operations to meet the anticipated demand in the sector.”

 What’s Next?

 With the 3D printing machine set to arrive in the coming months, Blackwell 3D is preparing to launch its first set of pilot projects, demonstrating the full potential of this cutting-edge technology. The company is committed to leading the transformation of Dubai’s real estate and construction sectors, setting new benchmarks for innovation and sustainability.

 “Concurrently with this milestone, we are in advanced discussions with a major real estate development company in Dubai, exploring a partnership that will shape the future of our upcoming projects,” added Zaveri. “We expect that these discussions will continue through the weekend after the Holiday in the region today.”

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov.

 About Blackwell 3D Construction Corp.

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
