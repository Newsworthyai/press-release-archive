# NeuralBase Appoints Keval Shah as Chief Operating Officer

LAS VEGAS, NV - August 14, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI, is pleased to announce the appointment of Keval Shah as Chief Operating Officer, effective immediately. Mr. Shah brings a wealth of experience in technology strategy and innovation, which will be invaluable as the company accelerates its expansion in enterprise-grade AI solutions.

 Keval Shah is a seasoned professional whose leadership and strategic vision have made significant impacts across the AI and technology sectors. With broad experience ranging from corporate development to enterprise AI deployments, Mr. Shah has a proven track record of guiding technology companies through transformative growth phases. His diverse background will complement BMP AI Technologies’ mission to redefine conversational AI in regulated industries.

 “Keval Shah’s appointment aligns perfectly with our strategic priorities,” stated Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase AI. “As we continue to forge ahead with next-generation enterprise AI platforms, his insight and leadership will help us optimize our governance, expand market reach, and prioritize customer-centric innovation.”

 Key areas where Mr. Shah’s expertise will contribute:

 - Enhanced Enterprise Strategy & Partnerships -- With BMP AI targeting regulated sectors such as healthcare, finance, legal, and government, Mr. Shah’s experience will be instrumental in crafting industry-aligned go-to-market strategies and forging key strategic alliances.

 - Elevated Platform Governance & Compliance - BMP AI is evolving with features for explainability, auditability, and compliance (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2). Mr. Shah’s perspective will strengthen the company’s positioning as a trusted, enterprise-ready platform.

 - Accelerated Product Innovation -- As BMP AI integrates advanced generative AI technologies, Mr. Shah’s leadership will help guide responsible deployment, ensuring solutions are useful, scalable, and ethically aligned.

 - Guiding Corporate Evolution -- The company’s ongoing rebranding to BMP AI Technologies Inc., highlighting a shift toward bold, modern, predictive AI innovation, is a significant transformation. Mr. Shah’s deep understanding of corporate strategy and tech growth trajectories will be vital to navigating this evolution.

 About NEURALBASE AI LTD.

 NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI) is a publicly traded AI company focused on secure, scalable, and context-aware conversational systems and workflow automation. Through its BMP AI platform, currently in beta testing, the Company enables enterprises to streamline internal operations, enhance team collaboration, and improve productivity while maintaining compliance and data security.
