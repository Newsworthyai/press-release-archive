# Benzinga Announces Free Virtual Event: Navigating Market Volatility with Direxion's Leveraged and Inverse ETFs - 2025 Outlook

By Johnny Rice

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - April 9, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Benzinga, a leading financial media and data provider headquartered in Detroit, Michigan, is excited to announce its latest free virtual event: “Navigating Market Volatility with Direxion’s Leveraged and Inverse ETFs – 2025 Outlook.” The event will take place Thursday, April 10th, online and is open to traders and investors of all experience levels.

 Join Direxion’s experts as we explore the world of leveraged and inverse ETFs, designed to help traders and investors capitalize on market volatility. As 2025 brings new economic challenges and market fluctuations, these powerful tools could help provide amplified returns or hedge against downturns.

 Event Highlights Include: * Understanding Leveraged & Inverse ETFs: A comprehensive breakdown of how these funds work, their key benefits, and the associated risks.
* How to Use Them in Volatile Markets: Learn how leveraged and inverse ETFs can help navigate the ups and downs of unpredictable markets, focusing on practical strategies to optimize your portfolio during heightened volatility.
* 2025 Market Insights: An in-depth look at what the 2025 market landscape might hold and how you can position yourself using these unique financial instruments.

 Whether you’re an experienced trader or just starting to explore the world of leveraged and inverse ETFs, this webinar will provide valuable insights to help you make informed decisions in today’s volatile market.

 Featured Speakers Include: * Eliott Wellenbach, Senior Vice President – Institutional ETF Strategy, Direxion
