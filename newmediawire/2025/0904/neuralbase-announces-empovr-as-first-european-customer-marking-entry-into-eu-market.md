# NeuralBase Announces EmpoVR as First European Customer, Marking Entry Into EU Market

LAS VEGAS, NV - September 4, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI and recognized as a fast-growing innovator in enterprise artificial intelligence, today announced that EmpoVR, a Barcelona-based immersive technology company, has successfully integrated the BMP AI platform into its operations. This milestone makes EmpoVR NeuralBase’s first European customer and marks the Company’s official entry into the European Union market.

 With this integration, EmpoVR will leverage NeuralBase’s next-generation enterprise AI framework to deploy highly intelligent, customizable virtual assistants that support customer experience, immersive engagement, and back-office automation. The adaptability of the BMP AI platform enables seamless deployment across diverse departments, delivering tangible improvements in efficiency, productivity, and innovation.

 Benefits for EmpoVR and the European Market

 * AI-Powered Customer Support - Multilingual AI assistants provide 24/7 support to a global client base.
* Scalable Enterprise Framework - Integration across operations, sales, and support functions.
* Innovation Synergy - Combining immersive VR/AR with enterprise AI to deliver next-generation user experiences in Europe.
* Future-Proofing Growth - Ensuring adaptability as AI standards and regulations evolve in the EU.

 “We are thrilled to welcome EmpoVR as our first European customer,” said Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase AI Ltd. “This partnership validates the global scalability of our platform and marks the beginning of a strong presence in Europe. EmpoVR’s forward-thinking adoption of BMP AI underscores the value we bring in enhancing enterprise workflows, boosting efficiency, and shaping the future of human-AI collaboration.”

 About EmpoVR

 EmpoVR is a Barcelona-based company specializing in immersive virtual reality and augmented reality solutions for enterprises, education, and consumer experiences. With a focus on innovation, EmpoVR combines cutting-edge technology with user-centric design to deliver next-generation immersive solutions.

 About NEURALBASE AI LTD.

 NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI) is an AI company developing secure, scalable, and context-aware conversational agents and workflow automation systems. Through its BMP AI platform - now in beta testing - the Company enables organizations to streamline internal operations, enhance team collaboration, and increase productivity while maintaining strict compliance and data integrity.
