# Soulpower Acquisition Corporation Announces the Separate Trading of Its Class A Ordinary Shares and Rights, Commencing May 23, 2025

NEW YORK, NY - May 22, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Soulpower Acquisition Corporation (NYSE: SOULU) (the “Company”) announced today that, commencing May 23, 2025, holders of the units sold in the Company’s initial public offering may elect to separately trade the Company’s Class A ordinary shares and rights included in the units. The Class A ordinary shares and rights that are separated will trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbols “SOUL” and “SOULR,” respectively. Those units not separated will continue to trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol “SOULU.”

 Soulpower Acquisition Corporation

 Soulpower Acquisition Corporation is a special purpose acquisition company incorporated under the laws of Cayman Islands for the purpose of effecting a merger, amalgamation, share exchange, asset acquisition, share purchase, reorganization or similar business combination with one or more businesses. The Company may pursue an acquisition opportunity in any business or industry or at any stage of its corporate evolution but will focus on insurance services, retirement savings and other related financial services.
