# Peter Clemente Joins Peapack Private Bank & Trust

BEDMINSTER, NJ - May 22, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation (NASDAQ Global Select Market: PGC) and Peapack Private Bank & Trust announce that Peter Clemente has joined the Bank as a Senior Managing Director and Group Director with the Bank’s Commercial Banking division. He joins several teams that will be based out of the Bank’s newest location in Garden City, New York.

 Peter is a senior leader with extensive experience in all segments of commercial and retail banking. Throughout his career he has focused on driving consistently strong results in sales, client satisfaction, employee engagement, compliance and brand awareness. He joins Peapack Private from Flagstar Bank, formerly Signature Bank, where, as a senior vice president, group director, he was responsible for driving revenue growth by developing and implementing comprehensive sales strategies for high-profile clients and organizations. In years prior at IDB Bank, Peter was a senior vice president, group head, in Corporate Banking, responsible for establishing and developing the Long Island middle market segment in support of the bank’s growth plan. He held similar high-level positions at Flushing Bank, Capital One Bank, Legacy Banks, and HSBC Bank USA, N.A. His early career included roles in business banking at Citibank, N.A., Chemical Bank and E.F. Hutton & Co., Inc.

 Peter has a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting from St. John’s University and a master’s in business administration in Finance from Fordham University where he was a selected member of Beta Gamma Sigma, a national business honor society.

 About the Company

 Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation is a New Jersey bank holding company with total assets of $7.1 billion and assets under management and/or administration of $11.8 billion as of March 31, 2025. Founded in 1921, Peapack Private Bank & Trust is a commercial bank that offers a client-centric approach to banking, providing high-quality products along with customized and innovative wealth management, investment banking, commercial and personal banking solutions. The Bank’s wealth management division offers comprehensive financial, tax, fiduciary and investment advice and solutions to individuals, families, privately held businesses, family offices and not-for-profit organizations, which help them to establish, maintain and expand their legacy. Peapack Private Bank & Trust offers an unparalleled commitment to client service. Visit www.peapackprivate.com for more information.
