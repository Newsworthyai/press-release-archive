# PickleJar Announces Co-Hosts for PickleJar Up All Night Ahead of August 18 Launch

Katie Cook and Scott Gaines to Co-Host Syndicated Overnight Country Radio Show from Nashville

 HOUSTON, TX - August 12, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - PickleJar Entertainment Group (OTCMKTS: PKLE) today announced the co-hosts for its nationally syndicated overnight country music radio show, PickleJar Up All Night, relaunching on Monday, August 18 from its new flagship station, 106.7 WNFN-FM in Nashville.

 The show will be co-hosted by Katie Cook, a 20-year veteran of CMT, and Scott Gaines, a seasoned radio personality with major-market experience. Together, they bring a powerful blend of authenticity, industry expertise, and fan-first energy to the airwaves, broadcasting Monday through Friday from 12:00 AM to 5:00 AM CT, and available for syndication across the country.

 Katie Cook, widely recognized for her long-standing work with Country Music Television (CMT), brings deep experience as an interviewer, anchor, and champion of artists. Over two decades at CMT, she built trusted relationships with country’s biggest names, hosted red carpets and artist specials, and provided insightful coverage that resonated with fans. Cook has also hosted events for the Academy of Country Music (ACM Awards) and continues to serve as an influential voice for female artists and rising talent.

 “Pairing Katie’s iconic television presence with Scott’s deep radio background gives PickleJar Up All Night a powerful blend of storytelling and industry insight,” said Kristian Barowsky, President and Co-Founder of PickleJar. “They’re both passionate about the music and the people behind it, and I can’t wait for fans to hear what they’ve built together.”

 Joining her as co-host is Scott Gaines, whose impressive radio career includes stops in Detroit, Dallas, and national platforms like Westwood One, iHeartMedia, and Cumulus Media. Known for his deep understanding of audience engagement and radio programming, Gaines brings seasoned insight and a passion for curating meaningful on-air experiences.

 “Getting back behind the mic feels like coming home,” said Katie Cook. “I’m excited to reconnect with fans, share great stories, and celebrate the music I’ve loved my whole life.”

 Airing from Nashville - the heart of country music - PickleJar Up All Night will feature exclusive interviews, fan-driven contests, live performances, and spotlight segments for iconic, emerging and unsigned artists. The show will stream live and integrate with the PickleJar Live app, where fans can interact with talent, enter giveaways, and support their favorite performers directly.

 “We’re thrilled to bring PickleJar Up All Night back on the air with an aggressive affiliate strategy and talent-forward approach,” added Barowsky. “This show isn’t just a return to radio - it’s a platform for launching new music, connecting fans with artists, and building unforgettable experiences through content and contests powered by the PickleJar Live app.”

 All affiliate inquiries should be directed to Kristian Barowsky at kristian@picklejar.com or Gator Michaels at gator@picklejar.com.
