# tZERO Welcomes Back Vanessa Savino as EVP, Chief Legal Officer, and Board Secretary

NEW YORK, NY - November 13, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - tZERO Group, Inc., a leading innovator in blockchain-powered multi-asset infrastructure, today announced the appointment of Vanessa Savino as Executive Vice President, Chief Legal Officer, and Board Secretary. Savino will lead tZERO’s legal, compliance and policy functions globally, driving strategic initiatives that advance the company’s leadership in regulated digital asset markets.

 Savino returns to tZERO after six-and-a-half years with the company, where she most recently served as Deputy General Counsel and Chief of Legal and Compliance Staff. During her prior tenure, she played a central role in shaping tZERO’s regulatory framework, overseeing complex transactions, and building the company’s governance and compliance infrastructure as it pioneered new models for digital securities and tokenized assets.

 “We’re thrilled to welcome Vanessa back to tZERO,” said Alan Konevsky, Chief Executive Officer of tZERO. “She brings not only exceptional legal expertise across financial regulation and digital assets, but also a deep understanding of our mission and infrastructure. Her leadership will be instrumental as we continue to strengthen our regulatory foundation and deliver trusted, compliant digital market solutions globally.”

 “I am honored to return as tZERO’s Chief Legal Officer at such a pivotal moment in both the company’s trajectory and the broader evolution of regulated digital asset infrastructure," said Savino. “I’m genuinely enthusiastic about tZERO’s future under Alan’s leadership and will continue to foster the company’s strong legal, compliance and regulatory framework to support its next phase of growth.”

 tZERO Group Inc. is a leading innovator in blockchain-powered multi-asset infrastructure for financial markets. Through its regulated subsidiaries, tZERO offers an end-to-end platform for the issuance, trading, and settlement of tokenized securities and other digital assets. With a mission to bridge traditional finance and decentralized markets, tZERO delivers institutional-grade solutions that bring efficiency, transparency, and accessibility to global investors, redefining how capital is raised, traded, and owned in the Web3 era.

 About tZERO

 tZERO Group, Inc. (tZERO) and its broker-dealer subsidiaries provide an innovative liquidity platform for private companies and assets. We offer institutional-grade solutions for issuers looking to digitize their capital table through blockchain technology, and make such equity available for trading on an alternative trading system. tZERO, through its broker-dealer subsidiaries, democratizes access to private assets by providing a simple, automated, and efficient trading venue to broker-dealers, institutions, and investors. All technology services are offered through tZERO Technologies, LLC. For more information, please visit our website.
