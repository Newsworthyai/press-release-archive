# NeuralBase Announces Early Implementation of Its Fintech Prototype Workflow With Dubai-Based Forex Brokerage

LAS VEGAS, NV - July 11, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), operating under its enterprise brand BMP AI and a growing innovator in enterprise artificial intelligence, is pleased to announce that its cutting-edge fintech prototype workflow is now being implemented by Loyalty Liquidity, a leading forex brokerage company headquartered in Dubai.

 This milestone marks an important step in NeuralBase’s mission to transform financial services through AI-driven automation and intelligent process management. The prototype, though still in its iterative phase, is designed to streamline critical forex brokerage operations such as real-time trade monitoring, client onboarding, compliance checks, and automated reporting.

 By adopting NeuralBase’s prototype at this stage, Loyalty Liquidity stands to benefit from:

 - Operational Efficiency: Automation of routine workflows, freeing up human resources to focus on strategy and client relations.

 - Enhanced Compliance: Real-time AI monitoring helps reduce risk and ensure adherence to evolving regulatory requirements.

 - Faster Client Service: Improved onboarding and transaction processing speeds enhance customer experience.

 - Data-Driven Insights: AI-powered analytics provide actionable insights to support better decision-making.

 “We’re excited to see our prototype enter practical use with an innovative brokerage like Loyalty Liquidity,” said Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase. “This partnership reflects our shared vision of leveraging AI to bring agility and transparency to the financial trading sector.”

 NeuralBase emphasizes that this is not yet the final product. The workflow prototype will be continuously refined based on real-world feedback, with further updates planned in upcoming iterations to ensure it meets the dynamic needs of the forex market.
