# Isiah Thomas and One World Products Announce Q2 10-Q Filing and Position Company for Next Stage of Growth

Filing Completion Positions Company to Scale Eco Bio Plastics Production, Expand Partnerships, and Target Break-even by Year-end

 DETROIT, MI - September 22, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - One World Products, Inc. (OTCQB: OWPC), soon to be renamed Isiah Enterprises, today announced it has successfully filed its Form 10-Q for the quarter ended June 30, 2025. With this filing, the Company is now fully current on its reporting obligations and positioned to execute the next stage of its growth strategy.

 Key Highlights

 * Compliance Restored: The Q2 10-Q filing completes all outstanding reports, reinforcing the Company’s commitment to transparency and strong governance.
* On Track for Break-Even: Management reaffirmed guidance to achieve operational break-even by December 2025, supported by production scaling at Eco Bio Plastics Michigan.
* Patented Pelletization Process: The Company’s proprietary technology to pulverize and micronize biomass, rubber, cellulose, and other renewable feedstocks into advanced composites remains a key differentiator in the trillion-dollar global packaging and automotive industries.
* Expanding Revenue Streams: In addition to sustainable automotive materials, the Company is advancing development of food-safe packaging, specialty biopolymers, and new product lines to broaden future revenue opportunities.
* Partnership Momentum: Collaborations with Stellantis, Flex-N-Gate, ORBIS Corporation, and West Michigan Compounding validate industry demand, with additional partnerships under exploration.
* Exploring Expansion: Management is evaluating opportunities to increase production capacity and geographic reach to meet rising demand for sustainable materials.

 “Filing our 10-Q is more than checking a box - it signals that we are ready to execute at a higher level,” said Isiah Thomas, Chairman & CEO of One World Products (soon to be Isiah Enterprises). “I’ve led and played on championship-winning teams, and I’ve turned around and built businesses others thought couldn’t succeed. With Isiah Enterprises, we carry that same champion’s mindset: discipline, resilience, and teamwork. With our patented pelletization process scaling, partnerships with Stellantis, Flex-N-Gate, ORBIS, and West Michigan Compounding expanding, and a clear plan to reach break-even by December, we are positioned not just to participate but to emerge as one of the leaders in this space. We are building a champion for our shareholders, our customers, and our communities.”

 One World Products, soon to be renamed Isiah Enterprises, is the only Black-controlled publicly traded company pioneering renewable bioplastics and sustainable packaging solutions. In an industry dominated by multinationals, the Company brings diverse leadership, innovation, and agility to a trillion-dollar global market.

 About One World Products, Inc.

 One World Products, Inc. (OTCQB: OWPC), soon to be renamed Isiah Enterprises, is a Nevada-based public company specializing in renewable and sustainable materials. Through its Eco Bio Plastics division, the company processes rubber, cellulose, hemp, and a wide range of bio-feedstocks into advanced composites for automotive, industrial, and food packaging. Led by Chairman and CEO Isiah Thomas, the company is building a circular economy that delivers profitability, sustainability, and long-term value across a $1 trillion global packaging industry.
