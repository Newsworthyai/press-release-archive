# Beeline Holdings (BLNE) to Present at Centurion One Capital Bahamas Summit

LOS ANGELES, CA - September 22, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), a digital mortgage platform, announced it will present at the Centurion One Capital 3rd Annual Bahamas Summit on Oct. 28-29, 2025, at the Rosewood Baha Mar Hotel in Nassau. CEO Nick Liuzza will deliver the company presentation and attend investor meetings, while COO Jess Kennedy and CFO Christopher Moe will join a panel discussion.
