# Miranda Water Technologies Deploys Second Miracell® Essential System at Major Gold Mine in Turkiye

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA - July 31, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Rainmaker Worldwide Inc. (OTC: RAKR) announced today that its subsidiary, Miranda Water Technologies, has successfully deployed a second Miracell® Essential wastewater treatment system in at Gubretas Mining, a subsidiary of Turkiye Tarim Kredi Kooperatifleri. This latest installation follows the successful commissioning of the first system at the same site in 2022.

 The new system was installed to support one of Turkiye’s largest gold mining operations and is engineered to meet the wastewater treatment demands of over 500 day workers. Designed for scalability and rapid deployment, the Miracell® Essential is ideally suited for rugged environments such as mine sites, where reliability, compact footprint, and minimal maintenance are essential.

 The Miracell® is a robust, energy-efficient Rotating Biological Contactor (RBC) housed in a compact containerized format. Its design is especially beneficial in the mining sector where environmental compliance, operational uptime, and cost containment are critical. Key advantages for mining operations include:- Low power consumption- No daily operator required- Quick mobilization and setup- Reliable performance in remote locations- Adaptability to variable flow rates and influent characteristics

 Miranda’s Miracell® systems have been deployed in over 1,200 installations across more than 40 countries. In nearly every country where Miranda operates, a Miracell® has been commissioned for use at a mine or oil and gas refinery, reflecting the product’s trusted reputation in high-demand industrial applications.

 “This project reinforces our commitment to delivering scalable, high-performance wastewater systems for industrial clients,” said Michael O’Connor, Chairman and CEO of Rainmaker Worldwide Inc. “The continued trust from our client and the consistent performance of the original system made the decision to move forward with a second installation clear.”

 This second deployment highlights Rainmaker’s strategy of delivering modular infrastructure solutions that address the practical realities of rapid development, environmental compliance, and decentralized service delivery in industrial sectors.
