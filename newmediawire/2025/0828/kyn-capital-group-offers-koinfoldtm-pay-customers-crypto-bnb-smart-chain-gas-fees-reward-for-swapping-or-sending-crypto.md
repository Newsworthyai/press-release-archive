# KYN Capital Group Offers KOINFOLD™ Pay Customers Crypto BNB Smart Chain Gas fees Reward for Swapping or Sending Crypto

RANCHO CORDOVA, CA - August 28, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - KYN Capital Group, Inc. (OTC: KYNC) announces a $5.00 reward in BNB Smart Chain from August 29, 2025 through midnight September 5, 2025 when purchasing $100.00 in cryptocurrency or more to use for swapping fees or transfer fees.

 Reward will be credited to each customer after their receive address for BNB Smart Chain from the users KOINFOLD WALLET is sent to KoinfoldPay@KYNCap.com and purchase is validated within 72 hours.

 This initiative is designed to enhance the user experience by reducing the cost of transactions on the BNB Smart Chain, empowering KOINFOLD™ Pay users to seamlessly swap tokens and manage their digital assets with greater efficiency. The reward can be applied to gas fees, making it easier for users to engage with the vibrant ecosystem of decentralized finance (DeFi).

 “We are committed to providing our users with innovative and cost-effective solutions to navigate the crypto space,” said Rick Wilson, CEO of KYN Capital Group. “This BNB Smart Chain gas fee reward is just the beginning of incentives for our users and a step toward making cryptocurrency transactions more accessible and rewarding for our KOINFOLD™ Pay community.”

 KOINFOLD™ Pay 2.0, a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet, continues to lead the industry with its user-centric design and robust functionality. The platform supports a wide range of tokens and integrates seamlessly with high-performance blockchains, ensuring fast and secure transactions. This promotional offer further solidifies KYN Capital Group’s mission to revolutionize digital finance through innovative blockchain solutions.

 To take advantage of this offer, users can download KOINFOLD™ Pay 2.0 from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and complete a qualifying cryptocurrency purchase within the promotional period. For more information about KOINFOLD™ Pay 2.0 and this offer, visit KOINFOLD.com.
