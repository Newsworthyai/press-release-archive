# Shelly Group: Invitation to Earnings Webcast/Call on 13 November 2025 on Unaudited 9M 2025 Results

SOFIA, Bulgaria and MUNICH - November 10, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Shelly Group SE (Ticker SLYG / ISIN: BG1100003166) ("Shelly Group"), a provider of IoT and smart building solutions based in Sofia, Bulgaria, invites investors and analysts to an earnings webcast/call with the Management Board members on 13 November 2025, 09.00 CET (10.00 EET). The unaudited 9M 2025 consolidated results will be announced on 12 November 2025 after the close of trading.Earnings Call:

 Co-CEOs Dimitar Dimitrov and Wolfgang Kirsch will comment on the unaudited consolidated 9M 2025 results by means of a webcast presentation. The webcast/call will be held in English.
