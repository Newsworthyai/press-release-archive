# Blackwell 3D Inks Deal to Expand Into Dubai's Real Estate Sector With a New Development in Dubai South

DUBAI, UAE - March 4, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, is pleased to announce a major step in its Middle East expansion as its Dubai subsidiary has executed a binding Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”) for the development of a residential project in Dubai South.

 This move strengthens BDCC’s footprint in real estate development in the United Arab Emirates, aligning with its long-term strategy of leveraging innovative construction methodologies and establishing itself as a key player in Dubai’s rapidly growing property market.

 Following the signing of this MOU, BDCC is set to play a role in the development of a state-of-the-art residential project in Dubai South- one of the most sought-after real estate destinations in the UAE.

 "This agreement represents a significant milestone for BDCC as we expand our presence into Dubai’s high-growth real estate sector," stated Mohammed Saif Zaveri, CEO & Founder of BDCC. "Dubai South is an evolving, high-potential area, and we are excited to be part of its transformation. This project marks an important step in BDCC’s journey as we continue to establish our presence in key international markets."

 With BDCC’s strong foundation in modern construction methodologies, the Company remains focused on integrating efficient, sustainable, and high-quality development solutions.

 "This expansion is not just about entering a new market- it’s about bringing forward-thinking construction strategies that optimize cost, efficiency, and sustainability," declared Mitesh Ashok Rasaikar, Co-Founder & CTO of BDCC. "As BDCC continues to grow, our focus remains on delivering high-value developments that align with the future of urban living."

 BDCC’s Dubai subsidiary is now in the process of completing all necessary paperwork, official agreements and any governmental permits required, after which project execution will begin. The residential project in Dubai South is expected to contribute to the area’s continued growth as a leading hub for real estate investment and sustainable urban planning.

 Dubai South is widely recognized for its strategic importance, benefiting from its proximity to Al Maktoum International Airport, major logistics and trade hubs, and key infrastructure developments. With Dubai’s real estate sector seeing record-breaking investment growth, BDCC views this project as a catalyst for further expansion in the region.

 "We are committed to long-term success in Dubai’s real estate sector," added Zaveri. "This project is just the beginning, and we look forward to leveraging our expertise to drive innovation and deliver exceptional developments."

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov. Additional information regarding the details of this project in Dubai South will be made available soon.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
