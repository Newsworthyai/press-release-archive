# Sundial Collective Expands Across Sacramento Region With New Dispensary in Woodland, California

Leading Cannabis Retailer Celebrates With Grand Opening on Aug. 22

 

 REDDING, CA - August 18, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Sundial Collective (“Sundial”), a California-based cannabis dispensary group committed to education, quality service, and community connection, announced today the opening of its newest California location in Woodland, expanding its reach with a new retail storefront in the county seat of Yolo County. Sundial Collective is located at 3 Court St., Woodland, CA 95695, and will operate from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m.

 Sundial Collective invites the Woodland community to celebrate the grand opening Friday, Aug. 22 from 12 p.m. to 2 p.m. PST with exclusive promotions, local vendor features, and a first look at their curated product offerings.

 “Our goal has always been to provide the best value in cannabis while creating an environment where customers feel informed, supported, and respected,” said Laythen Martines, Co-Founder of Sundial Collective. “Opening in Woodland marks another exciting chapter for us, and we’re proud to bring our commitment to quality, service, and community to Yolo County and the greater Sacramento area.”

 Known for its unbeatable prices, wide product selection, and a relentless commitment to top-tier customer service, Sundial Collective has quickly become a trusted name in the California cannabis industry. Sundial’s team is dedicated to delivering a consistent and welcoming experience to every customer who walks through its doors.

 “We’re not just another dispensary. We’re a family-owned and operated business that has built everything from the ground up,” said Ashley Papazian, Co-Founder of Sundial Collective. “Some of our team members have been with us since the beginning, and their loyalty and dedication have shaped who we are. Opening a new location in Woodland is more than just growth. It’s a chance to welcome a new community into what we’ve built together.”

 For more information, visit www.sundialcollective.com or follow Sundial Collective on Instagram at sundial_socal.

 About Sundial Collective

 Sundial Collective is a network of recreational cannabis dispensaries operating across California, with five locations and more on the way. We take pride in providing unparalleled customer service, an exceptional selection of premium California-sourced cannabis products, and competitive pricing to fit every budget. Each of our dispensaries is designed to be a welcoming space where customers can explore a wide range of strains, concentrates, edibles, topicals, and more, with the guidance of our knowledgeable staff. As one of the first cannabis retailers in several of the communities we serve, Sundial is committed to giving back and ensuring our presence remains one that uplifts and enhances the lives of those around us.

 To learn more about Sundial and our community-driven approach, visit https://www.sundialcollective.com.
