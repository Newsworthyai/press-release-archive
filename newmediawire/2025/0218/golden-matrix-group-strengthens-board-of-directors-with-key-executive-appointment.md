# Golden Matrix Group Strengthens Board of Directors With Key Executive Appointment

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA - February 18, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Golden Matrix Group (NASDAQ: GMGI) (“Golden Matrix” or the “Company”), a leading provider of innovative gaming and technology solutions, today announced the appointment of Ms. Snezana Bozovic to its Board of Directors, reinforcing its strong market position and corporate governance structure.

 Ms. Bozovic brings proven expertise in financial planning, forecasting and corporate governance with over two decades of experience in financial strategy and operational management at Meridianbet, a subsidiary of Golden Matrix Group and a leading provider of innovative betting, gaming and technology solutions across 19 markets worldwide.

 Ms. Bozovic is currently Chief Operating Officer of Meridianbet, a position she has held since 2022. Prior to this role, she had successful tenures as CFO (2018-2022) and General Director (2008-2018). Her appointment reinforces the Company’s commitment to financial prudence and operational excellence during a period of significant growth and strategic developments.

 Brian Goodman, CEO of Golden Matrix Group, commented, "Ms. Bozovic's appointment to our Board of Directors brings valuable operational and financial expertise at a crucial time in our company's evolution. Her deep understanding of our industry and proven track record in financial management will be instrumental as we continue to expand our global footprint and innovate across our leading portfolio of gaming platforms."

 Zoran Milosevic, CEO of Meridianbet, added, “We are delighted to see Ms. Bozovic join Golden Matrix Group's Board of Directors. Having worked closely with her over many years, I can attest to her exceptional leadership abilities and deep understanding of our industry's complexities. Her proven track record in driving operational excellence and financial growth at Meridianbet makes her an invaluable addition to the board."

 About Golden Matrix

 Golden Matrix Group, based in Las Vegas, NV, is a leading B2B and B2C gaming technology company utilizing proprietary technology and operating globally across multiple international markets. The B2B division of Golden Matrix develops and licenses branded gaming platforms for its extensive list of clients, and RKings, its B2C division, operates a high-volume eCommerce site enabling end users to enter paid-for competitions on its proprietary platform in authorized markets. The Company also owns and operates MEXPLAY, a regulated online casino in Mexico. In 2024, Golden Matrix completed the acquisition of MeridianBet, a well-established B2B and B2C sports betting and gaming platform operating and regulated in multiple markets in Europe, Africa and LatAm, providing the combined entity with additional international operations.

 About Meridianbet

 Founded in 2001, Meridianbet Group is a well-established online sports betting and gaming group, licensed and currently operating in 17 jurisdictions across Europe, Africa and South America. Meridianbet’s successful business model utilizes proprietary technology and scalable systems, thus allowing it to operate in multiple countries and currencies and with an omni-channel approach to markets, including retail, desktop online and mobile.
