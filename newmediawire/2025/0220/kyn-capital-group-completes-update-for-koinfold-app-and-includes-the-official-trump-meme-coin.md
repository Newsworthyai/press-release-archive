# KYN Capital Group Completes Update for Koinfold App and Includes the Official Trump Meme Coin

RANCHO CORDOVA, CA - February 20, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - KYN Capital Group (OTC: KYNC) announced a significant update to its KOINFOLD cryptocurrency app. This update centers around the integration of the Official Trump Meme Coin, and positioning the company to capitalize on the growing meme coin trend in the cryptocurrency market.

 Key Features of the Update

 1. Official Trump Meme Coin Integration: • The addition of this high-profile meme coin is likely to attract attention from both Trump supporters and meme coin enthusiasts. • This move aligns with the predicted dominance of meme coins in the 2025 crypto market. 2. AI-Powered Tools: • The update includes AI-powered tools. • These tools are likely aimed at enhancing user experience and potentially offering advanced trading or analysis features. 3. Solana Integration for Meme Coin Purchases: • Users can now buy meme coins using Solana, a fast and efficient blockchain platform. • This integration could potentially reduce transaction costs and increase speed for meme coin acquisitions. 4. CoinMarketCap Listing: • KOINFOLD is now listed on the CoinMarketCap mobile app, available on both iOS and Android platforms. • This listing provides increased visibility and credibility for KOINFOLD in the broader crypto market. Download the CoinMarketCap app and Follow KOINFOLD for the latest as we start development of KOINFOLD Telegram group and Discord.

 “We will be adding MEME coins weekly to expand brand awareness leading to transactions and revenue to the KOINFOLD wallet. By being listed on CoinMarketCap, we are now able to start a verified group of users," stated Rick Wilson, CEO.
