# Benzinga Virtual Events Presents: Exploring Current Volatility And Market Trends

Join Kristin Boyd of Cboe Global Markets for an Exclusive Discussion on Trading Volume Trends, Volatility Products, and Diversity in Finance

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - March 27, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Benzinga is thrilled to announce an exclusive virtual event, Exploring Current Volatility and Market Trends, taking place on Thursday, March 27th, at 11 AM ET. This insightful session will feature Kristin Boyd, Senior Vice President, Global Head of Derivatives Sales and Distribution, of Cboe Global Markets, who will provide an expert analysis of today’s evolving market landscape and the role of volatility products in navigating uncertainty.

 Attendees will gain key insights into:

 * Market Volatility & Trading Trends: Understanding how uncertainty in market direction is influencing trading behaviors and volumes.
* Cboe’s Comprehensive Product Suite: Exploring the VIX suite and other volatility products as essential tools for traders looking to capture market shifts in implied volatility.
* Opportunities in Volatility Products: Examining the rise in volatility trading activity and how investors can use these instruments to express their market outlook.
* Empowering Diversity in Finance: Discussing Cboe’s initiatives to increase women’s representation in the financial markets and its commitment to trader education for professionals of all backgrounds.

 This event is free and open to traders, investors, and financial professionals seeking a deeper understanding of today’s market dynamics.
