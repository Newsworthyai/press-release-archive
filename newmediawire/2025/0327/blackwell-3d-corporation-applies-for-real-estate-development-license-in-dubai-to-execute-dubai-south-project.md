# Blackwell 3D Corporation Applies for Real Estate Development License in Dubai to Execute Dubai South Project

DUBAI, UAE - March 27, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. (OTC: BDCC) (“Blackwell 3D” or the “Company”), an innovative 3D house printing technology company, has officially applied for a real estate development license in Dubai, marking a significant step in its expansion into the Middle East. This move aligns with the company’s strategic vision to contribute to the dynamic growth of Dubai’s real estate sector, particularly through its involvement in the highly anticipated Dubai South Project.

 Dubai South, a master-planned urban development designed to be a hub for business, logistics, and lifestyle innovation, is set to redefine the city’s real estate landscape. With this initiative, Blackwell 3D aims to bring its expertise in smart infrastructure, sustainable architecture, and advanced 3D construction technologies to the region, ensuring world-class standards in development.

 Commenting on the application, Mohammedsaif Zaveri, CEO of Blackwell 3D, stated that “we are excited about the opportunity to be part of Dubai’s visionary real estate landscape. Our expertise in cutting-edge construction methodologies and sustainable urban planning will enable us to contribute meaningfully to the Dubai South Project. This step marks the beginning of a long-term commitment to innovation and excellence in the UAE’s real estate sector.”

 Blackwell 3D’s entry into Dubai aligns with the emirate’s broader 2040 Urban Master Plan, which focuses on sustainable, people-centric urban growth. The company is poised to introduce groundbreaking 3D-printed construction solutions, AI-driven smart city integrations, and eco-friendly development strategies to ensure efficiency, speed, and sustainability in project execution.

 Pending regulatory approvals, Blackwell 3D is set to commence operations and begin its role in shaping the future of Dubai South’s commercial, residential, and mixed-use developments.

 For further information about Blackwell 3D, please visit www.blackwell3d.com or refer to filings available at www.otcmarkets.com and www.sec.gov. Additional information regarding the details of this project in Dubai South will be made available soon.

 About Blackwell 3D Corporation

 Blackwell 3D Construction Corp. is focused on developing state-of-the-art 3D technologies for use in the construction industry. Our goal is to be able to use our technology to design 3D printed residential structures and that meet our clients' specific requirements and preferences. In addition, it is our objective to use a large-scale printer and a specialized concrete mixture to print the structural components of these residential structures. Our printing process is expected to be highly automated, allowing us to create intricate designs and details with precision.
