# BENZINGA VIRTUAL EVENTS: Unveiling The Cboe® Magnificent 10 Index - A New Way To Trade Tech Titans

Event Date & Time: Thursday, November 13, 2025Registration (Free): Click here to sign up

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - November 12, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Join Cboe® and Benzinga for an exclusive pre-launch webinar introducing the Cboe Magnificent 10 Index (MGTN) - a bold new benchmark capturing the pulse of U.S. large-cap technology and growth.

 This session will give market participants an early look at Cboe’s latest innovation: a thematic, equal-weighted index featuring the influential “Magnificent 7” stocks (Apple, Amazon, Alphabet, Meta, Microsoft, Nvidia and Tesla) plus AMD, Broadcom and Palantir.

 Join Industry Experts For An Inside Look

 Hosted by Henry Schwartz, Vice President of Derivatives Market Intelligence at Cboe, and Gianni Di Poce, Analyst and Contributor at Benzinga, this free live session will provide traders, investors and market enthusiasts with an early look at one of the year’s most anticipated index launches.

 In this one-hour event, attendees will gain insights into:

 * The design and methodology behind the MGTN Index
* How the MGTN product suite enables efficient exposure to today’s growth leaders
* What to expect from the upcoming launch of options and futures on MGTN, trading nearly 24x5

 Whether you’re a professional trader, portfolio manager or simply fascinated by the evolution of index-based trading tools, this event offers a front-row seat to the future of market innovation.
