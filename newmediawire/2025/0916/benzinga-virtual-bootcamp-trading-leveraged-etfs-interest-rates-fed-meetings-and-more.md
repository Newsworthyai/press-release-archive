# Benzinga Virtual Bootcamp: Trading Leveraged ETFs: Interest Rates, Fed Meetings, and More

Live Event: Thursday, September 18 | 11am EST

 DETROIT, MICHIGAN - September 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Benzinga, a leading financial media outlet, is excited to announce a free live virtual bootcamp: "Trading Leveraged ETFs: Interest Rates, Fed Meetings, and More" happening Thursday, September 18.

 Join analysts from Direxion - one of the leading providers of leveraged ETFs - for a high-impact virtual bootcamp focused on trading strategies during interest rate announcements. Whether it’s a Fed meeting, CPI data, or unexpected policy shifts, interest rate news can cause swift market reactions. This bootcamp dives deep into how traders can position themselves using leveraged and inverse ETFs before, during, and after key interest rate events.

 Through expert-led sessions and real-time case studies, you’ll gain insight into market behavior around macroeconomic catalysts and how to use leveraged ETFs to amplify your trading strategy. Audience participation is encouraged, so come ready with questions and get the answers you need in real time.

 Designed for active traders and market enthusiasts, this high-impact session dives into strategies for navigating market volatility during key macroeconomic events - including Federal Reserve meetings, CPI data releases, and unexpected interest rate shifts.
