# Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc. Announces Name and Symbol Change

HICKSVILLE, NY - September 16, 2025 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc. (OTC Pink Limited: NASC) (the “Company”) announced today that the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority has completed the processing of the change of the Company’s name for trading purposes from Can B Corp. to Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc. and the change of its trading symbol from “CANB” to “NASC.” These corporate actions become effective on September 16, 2025.

 Effective today, the Company’s common stock will trade under the symbol “NASC” and the new CUSIP number for its common stock is 63108T105.

 The name and symbol change follow the holding company reorganization completed in October 2024 pursuant to which Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc. became the parent of Can B Corp.

 About Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc.: Nascent Pharma Holdings, Inc., a Florida corporation, is a forward-thinking pharmaceutical Intellectual Property (IP) company, focused on maximizing the value of its cannabis patent portfolios. It owns two issued patents registered with the US Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) covering cannabis extracts and methods of preparation, emphasizing both composition and use.
