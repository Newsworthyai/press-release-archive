# Suncoast Venture Studio Launches RecruitTune.ai: AI-Powered Assessment Platform Helps Hiring Teams Cut Through Candidate Volume and Surface the Right Finalists - Before the First Interview

SARASOTA, FL - June 8, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Suncoast Venture Studio (SVS) today announced the full commercial launch of RecruitTune.ai, an AI-powered hiring assessment platform that makes the top of the recruiting funnel dramatically more efficient. Following a market beta with more than 50 organizations, RecruitTune.ai is now available to HR leaders, talent acquisition professionals and hiring managers nationwide - arriving at a moment when the demand for talent has never been higher.

 According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics’ April 2026 Job Openings and Labor Turnover Survey, U.S. job openings surged to 7.6 million - the highest level in nearly two years. Yet hiring rates slipped in the same period, underscoring a persistent challenge: employers are posting more roles but struggling to efficiently identify which candidates are worth bringing to the interview stage. As Noah Yosif, chief economist at the American Staffing Association, told CNN Business: “Miscalculating on the wrong worker can be costly for employers, and so employers are really taking their time to make sure they are filling jobs with the right candidates.” That friction is exactly what RecruitTune.ai is designed to eliminate.

 The cost of an inefficient hiring funnel is significant. The average cost per hire in the United States now stands at $4,700 to $4,800, according to SHRM’s 2026 Human Capital Benchmarking Report, with the average time to fill a position at 44 days. A bad hire compounds that cost dramatically - the U.S. Department of Labor and SHRM estimate a single mis-hire costs 30% to 50% of that employee’s first-year salary.5 Most of that waste originates at the top of the funnel, where hiring managers are forced to evaluate high volumes of candidates with little more than a resume before committing time to interviews.

 RecruitTune.ai solves the top-of-funnel problem with a structured, science-backed three-signal assessment that filters and ranks candidates before a single interview is scheduled. Hiring managers upload a job description, and the platform instantly generates a custom behavioral profile aligned to that specific role. Candidates complete a 20-minute assessment evaluated across three dimensions: Must-Have Criteria for the role; an Audio-based Role Scenario that surfaces how candidates respond to real workplace situations; and an IPIP Big Five Behavioral Profile mapped to O*NET occupational standards. The result is a Job Fit Score - a defensible, data-driven ranking that tells hiring teams exactly who deserves a deeper look, so interviews are reserved for candidates who are genuinely qualified.

 Unlike legacy assessment tools that require lengthy setup calls or enterprise implementation cycles, RecruitTune.ai was built for speed and accessibility. Organizations can create their first role assessment and begin screening candidates within minutes.

 “I had tried several other assessment tools, but they all required a setup call before I could see anything. RecruitTune let me get in immediately, paste in a job description, and have an assessment ready to send the same day. What really stood out was how the results framed lower scores as areas to improve rather than failures - that’s exactly the mindset we want to bring into our hiring conversations.”

 - Danelle Stumbo, talent acquisition, Centrica Business Solutions

 “We built RecruitTune.ai to solve a problem every hiring manager knows intimately: too many candidates, not enough signal,” said Daniel Alpert, chief executive officer of RecruitTune.ai. “When your funnel is clogged with unqualified applicants, your best candidates get lost in the noise and your team burns time on interviews that should never have happened. RecruitTune puts the right people in front of decision-makers faster, so organizations can move confidently from assessment to offer - and stop making expensive guesses.”

 The platform’s launch comes as the broader HR technology market accelerates investment in AI-powered screening and assessment tools. With 7.6 million open jobs and a hiring environment defined by heightened caution around candidate quality, RecruitTune.ai delivers the objective behavioral intelligence teams need to hire with confidence - at a price point accessible to companies of every size.
