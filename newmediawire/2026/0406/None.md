# Avolta Publishes Invitation to Its Annual General Meeting 2026

BASEL, SWITZERLAND - April 6, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Avolta today published the invitation to its Annual General Meeting 2026. The event will take place on May 06, 2026 at 14:30 CEST, at Rhypark, Muhlhauserstrasse 17, 4056 Basel, Switzerland. Doors open at 14:00 CEST.

The Annual General Meeting invitation and agenda, as well as the Letter of Avolta’s Chairman to Shareholders, can be accessed here: Annual General Meeting 2026.
