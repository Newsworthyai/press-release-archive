# Masters of Trivia Announces Global Tournaments: Year of the Horse - A Lunar New Year Trivia Tournament With Token Rewards on Solana

BOERNE, TEXAS - February 18, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - (MOT39052-USD) (SOL-USD) Masters of Trivia, a global knowledge-gaming platform, today announced Global Tournaments: Year of the Horse, a Lunar New Year–themed trivia event designed for web and mobile web players worldwide.

 Tournament format and timing: The tournament runs March 1, 2026, from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time (17:00 GMT). Players will answer 30 timed, multiple-choice questions focused on Year of the Horse traditions, history, and symbolism. Registration and full rules are available at our tournament headquarters.

 Prize purse and awards: The event includes a 1,000 MOT token prize purse for top performers, plus three in-kind prizes: handcrafted horse sculptures carved from cedar and agar wood by a 7th-generation artist based in Hoi An, Vietnam.

 “Lunar New Year inspires curiosity about culture, stories, and tradition,” said Dom Einhorn, founder of Masters of Trivia. “Year of the Horse turns that curiosity into a global competition - learn, compete, and see how much you really know.”

 Built for broad audiences: classrooms, casual players, and competitive trivia fans

 Global Tournaments are structured as seasonal, tournament-style experiences intended to serve multiple audiences:

 * Education & learning: culturally inspired questions that encourage discovery
* Lifestyle & culture: a timely Lunar New Year theme with zodiac storytelling and shareable results
* Technology & mobile gaming: cross-platform access, timed play, and global leaderboards
* Growth & community: creator partnerships and performance-based affiliates supporting tournament participation

 MOT on Solana

 The Masters of Trivia rewards ecosystem is powered by the MOT token (MOT39052-USD), built on the Solana blockchain (SOL-USD). The project’s primary liquidity is concentrated in the MOT/SOL trading pair, which supports token access and on-chain market activity for participants in the Masters of Trivia ecosystem.
