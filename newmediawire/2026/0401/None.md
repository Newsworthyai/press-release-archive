# Leonteq Publishes Results of Its Annual General Meeting 2026

Leonteq AG shareholders agreed to all the proposals put forward by the Board of Directors at today’s Annual General Meeting.

 ZURICH, SWITZERLAND - April 1, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Today’s general meeting in Zurich was attended by 102 shareholders representing 453,102 shares. The independent proxy represented 1,152 shareholders with 11,252,476 shares. The total attendance was approximately 64% of the issued shares of Leonteq.

 All five members of the Board of Directors proposed for re-election were elected for a further term of office of one year. In addition, Christopher Chambers was re-elected as Chairman of the Board of Directors. Shareholders also elected Barbara Heller and Juerg Steiger as new independent members of the Board of Directors.

 Additional proposals by the Board of Directors that were approved by shareholders include:

 * The Management Report, the Consolidated Financial Statements, and the Financial Statements for the financial year 2025
* The Sustainability Report 2025 and the Compensation Report 2025 (in advisory votes)
* The Allocation and appropriation of retained earnings and appropriation of reserves from capital contributions
* The re-election of the members of the Nomination and Remuneration Committee
* The re-election of the statutory auditors as well as the independent proxy
* The changes to the Articles of Association
* The compensation of the Board of Directors and the Executive Committee

 LEONTEQ

 Leonteq is a Swiss fintech company with a leading marketplace for structured investment solutions. Based on proprietary modern technology, the company offers derivative investment products and services and predominantly covers the capital protection, yield enhancement and participation product classes. Leonteq acts as both a direct issuer of its own products and as a partner to other financial institutions. Leonteq further enables life insurance companies and banks to produce capital-efficient, unit-linked pension products with guarantees. The company has offices and subsidiaries in 12 countries across Europe, the Middle East and Asia. Leonteq AG has a BBB-/stable credit rating by Fitch Ratings, was assigned with an AAA ESG rating by MSCI and is listed on the SIX Swiss Exchange (SIX: LEON). www.leonteq.com
