# EzaLife StartEngine Campaign Closing April 6

COLUMBUS, OH - April 6, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Ezalife has entered the final days of its equity crowdfunding campaign on StartEngine, with the raise set to close on April 6.

 The company, which developed the FDA-registered Button Huggie® device, is addressing a critical issue impacting over 1 million families annually who rely on feeding tubes.

 Founded by a pediatric surgeon who witnessed firsthand the limitations of traditional securement methods, Ezalife has built a solution focused on simplicity, safety, and reliability.

 Key highlights include:

 * Clinically demonstrated 77% reduction in feeding tube dislodgement
* Early traction with $8K in pre-orders and $11K in first-month sales
* Backing from leading institutions, including Children’s Hospital Colorado and CU Innovations
* Granted patents with global protection and future pipeline potential

 With momentum building across both clinical and consumer channels, Ezalife is now focused on scaling distribution and expanding access to patients and providers.

 “This is a pivotal moment for the company,” leadership noted. “We’ve built the foundation, now we’re focused on growth.”

 Investors have a limited window to participate before the campaign closes.
