# Ezalife Gains Early Revenue and Investor Momentum Ahead of StartEngine Campaign Close

COLUMBUS, OH - March 27, 2026 (NEWMEDIAWIRE) - Ezalife, a medical device company focused on improving outcomes for patients with feeding tubes, is gaining early traction as it approaches the final days of its equity crowdfunding campaign on StartEngine, closing April 6.

 The company recently reported $8,000 in pre-orders followed by $11,000 in its first 30 days of sales, signaling strong early demand for its flagship product, the Button Huggie®.

 Developed to replace the outdated tape-and-gauze method commonly used to secure gastrostomy buttons (G-buttons), the Button Huggie® is an FDA-registered device that improves stability, comfort, and safety for patients requiring enteral feeding.

 Ezalife has also secured $1.6 million in investment from leading institutions, including Children’s Hospital Colorado and the University of Colorado, supporting product development, manufacturing, and supply chain execution.

 “We’re seeing validation not only from clinical environments but now from real customers,” said Ezalife leadership. “This is a critical step as we scale into broader distribution channels.”

 The company is now focused on expanding into healthcare provider adoption, insurance reimbursement pathways, and hospital-to-home distribution, positioning itself for continued growth within the global feeding tube market.

 With its StartEngine campaign nearing close, Ezalife is inviting investors to participate in this next phase of expansion.
