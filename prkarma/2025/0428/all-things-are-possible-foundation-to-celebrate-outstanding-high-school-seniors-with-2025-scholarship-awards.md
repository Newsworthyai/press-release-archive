# All Things Are Possible Foundation to Celebrate Outstanding High School Seniors with 2025 Scholarship Awards

BURLINGTON, NJ / PR Karma / April 28, 2025 9:30

 Willingboro, NJ – April 28, 2025 – All Things Are Possible Foundation (ATAP) is proud to announce the recipients of its 2025 Scholarship Awards, recognizing exceptional high school seniors who are making a positive impact in their communities and demonstrating a commitment to excellence beyond high school. This year, ATAP will award a total of $8,500 in scholarships to five outstanding students at its annual Scholarship Award Lunch on Saturday, May 17, 2025, at 11 AM at The Boro Epicenter, 621 Beverly Rancocas Rd., Willingboro, NJ 08046.

 Since its founding, ATAP Foundation has been dedicated to empowering youth across Burlington County through academic enrichment, athletics, the arts, and community awareness. By investing in education and character development, ATAP affirms that all things truly are possible for the next generation of leaders, creators, and change-makers.

 This year’s scholarship honorees include:

 * Miicah Ice, Rancocas Valley Regional High School – Making a Difference Scholarship ($1,500)
* Derek Tindall, Florence Township Memorial High School – Scholar Athlete Scholarship ($1,500)
* David A. Watson, Jr., BCIT Westech – Vocational Excellence Scholarship ($1,500)
* Maya Morton, Lenape High School – Yes We Can! Scholarship ($1,500)
* Aiden J. Smiley, Doane Academy – Austin Conrad McIntosh Scholarship ($2,500)

 Each scholar has exemplified the spirit of leadership, service, and perseverance, reflecting the mission and vision of ATAP. Whether advancing their education at a university or joining the workforce,these young leaders are poised to make meaningful contributions to their communities and beyond.

 ATAP remains committed to expanding access to opportunities for youth who show promise, purpose, and passion.

 The community is invited to join ATAP Foundation and the NAACP Willingboro & Vicinity Branch in celebrating these scholars at the 2025 Scholarship Award Lunch. Tickets are just $10 and can be purchased online at https://atapfoundation.org/event/scholarshiplunch2025/.

 Come be inspired by the bright futures of tomorrow’s leaders and support the continued success of youth making a difference today!

 About All Things Are Possible Foundation (ATAP): ATAP is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization serving Burlington County, NJ, providing life-changing developmental programs that emphasize academics, athletics, awareness, and arts. Serving over 500 children annually, ATAP offers a variety of out-of-school-time (OST) programs, including before and after school care, full-day programs, STEAM workshops, and academic and cultural enrichment activities for K-8 and teens.
