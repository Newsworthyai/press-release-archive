# Ikea Damone Celebrates New Single and Award Nomination

NEW YORK, NY / PR Karma / December 23, 2025 11:00

Argo Vibes officially announces “Release,” the new single from Contemporary Soul and Alternative R&B and Jazz artist Ikea Damone. The track became available worldwide on Friday, December 19, 2025, which can be streamed and downloaded across major digital platforms. This new song signifies Ikea Damone’s highly anticipated return to the music scene after a period of creative hiatus. With “Release,” Damone introduces a fresh chapter in her artistry, highlighted by intimate, soul-driven storytelling and harmonious melodies.

 Just days after its debut, “Release” earned a nomination for Best R&B/Soul Song at the 37th Annual Wammie Music Awards. This single marked an exciting new chapter for Ikea Damone. Since its launch, “Release” has gained early momentum, including placement on Spotify’s Release Radar. Announced on December 21, 2025, the Wammie nomination highlights her return to the music scene and recognizes the impact of her latest artistic journey.

 “Release arrived in a moment of instinct. It was effortless and unplanned. Once it was out in the world, it became something bigger than me. That’s the beauty of music: once you let it go, it belongs to whoever needs it,” stated singer Ikea Damone.

 At its core, “Release” captures the comforting moment of unwinding with someone you trust. The lyrics and melodies reflect the experience of talking through the day and allowing emotions to settle. The song’s subtle musical arrangement further underscores this sense of honesty and vulnerability, making “Release” a heartfelt invitation to connect and find solace.

 Listeners can stream, download, and purchase “Release” through all digital entertainment platforms. You can also follow her on Instagram and TikTok.

 ABOUT IKEA DAMONE

 Ikea Damone is a Contemporary Soul and Alternative R&B/Jazz artist blending emotional depth with a modern, atmospheric style. Drawing from her family's musical heritage, her grandmother performed with The Shirelles, and her great-grandfather sang with The Rays, Damone continues the tradition of musical storytelling. Influenced by artists like Sade, Billie Holiday, Nina Simone, Amel Larrieux, and Queen Latifah, she offers a fresh yet classic sound. Her release, “Release,” signals a new creative chapter after years of growth.
