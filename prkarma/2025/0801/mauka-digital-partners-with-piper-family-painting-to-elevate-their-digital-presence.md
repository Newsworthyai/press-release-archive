# Mauka Digital Partners with Piper Family Painting to Elevate Their Digital Presence

PHOENIX, AZ / PR Karma / August 1, 2025 11:00

Mauka Digital, your premier full-service Digital Marketing and Technology Agency, is proud to announce a strategic partnership with the highly respected Piper Family Painting. This collaboration is poised to enhance Piper Family Painting's online visibility, connecting them with Southern Arizona homeowners and businesses in need of high-quality painting services.

 For over 30 years, Piper Family Painting has been a trusted name in the community, known for their skilled craftsmanship, customer-first approach, and deep ties to the region. Their unwavering commitment to quality and offering tailored services, such as interior and exterior painting, cabinetry refinishing, and restoration, has made them a standout provider. Piper Family Painting also exemplifies community values by offering discounts to veterans and families with special needs, further reflecting their dedication to giving back.

 With this partnership, Mauka Digital brings its advanced digital marketing expertise to the table. The agency will implement a comprehensive strategy to broaden Piper Family Painting’s reach and help them connect with a larger audience. Key initiatives include boosting their website’s search engine rankings through targeted SEO tactics, producing engaging podcast content that shares their story and mission, rebuilding their website for a seamless customer experience, and crafting a compelling social media presence for their podcast initiative.

 “We are excited to partner with Piper Family Painting, a company that shares our values of quality and community focus,” said  Jamie, CEO  at Mauka Digital. “Our goal is to help them reach new heights by leveraging the power of digital marketing to showcase their dedication to their customers.”

 This partnership marks a significant step forward for both companies, combining Mauka Digital's marketing and technology expertise with Piper Family Painting's outstanding craftsmanship to create a winning formula for success.
