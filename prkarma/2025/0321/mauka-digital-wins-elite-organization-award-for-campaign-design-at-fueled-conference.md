# Mauka Digital Wins Elite Organization Award for Campaign Design at Fueled Conference

PHOENIX, AZ / PR Karma / March 21, 2025 11:30

Mauka Digital, an award-winning full service digital marketing and technology agency, proudly announces its latest accolade, the Fueled Conference Elite Organization Award for Campaign Design. The award honors Mauka Digital’s innovative approach to leveraging data-driven insights and exemplary creativity in marketing campaigns, further cementing its position as a leader in the digital marketing industry.

 The Fueled Conference, a premier three-day global event, celebrates the intersection of marketing and technology. Attended by digital marketing and technology industry leaders, the event features cutting-edge technology demos, immersive sessions, and keynotes from leading voices shaping the future of marketing. The conference provides attendees with valuable tools, strategies, and networking opportunities to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

 This year, Mauka Digital was recognized for its outstanding ability to deliver custom solutions that redefine marketing norms. The Elite Organization Award was presented to Sofi Gutierrez of Mauka Digital for her exceptional campaign design work.

 Sofi Gutierrez stated, “At Mauka Digital, creativity and innovation are at the core of everything we do. This recognition reflects our relentless pursuit of excellence and unwavering commitment to empowering brands through custom, unconventional approaches.”

 Mauka Digital is known for its expertise across brand development, social media marketing, search engine optimization, paid search marketing, and technology-driven solutions. Its creative team specializes in delivering 100% custom approaches designed to amplify brand potential and drive measurable outcomes, all while maintaining a strong emphasis on quality and innovation.
