# HSR Zero Waste Returns as Sponsor and Participant at CWMA 2025 Annual Conference

VICTORIA, BC / PR Karma / October 10, 2025 17:00

HSR Zero Waste proudly returned as a sponsor and participant at the CWMA 2025 Annual Conference, joining sustainability leaders from across Canada to advance waste reduction and circular economy solutions.

 Marking its second consecutive year of involvement, HSR Zero Waste proudly continued its support for collaboration and innovation within BC’s waste management community. The conference brought together professionals from municipalities, businesses, and environmental organizations to share knowledge, build partnerships, and explore new pathways toward a cleaner, more sustainable future.

 HSR Zero Waste was represented both in person by company President Jamie Kaminski, and through its virtual booth, connecting with attendees to discuss waste reduction strategies, training programs, and zero waste transformation initiatives.

 “CWMA continues to be one of the most valuable platforms for collaboration in BC’s waste reduction community,” said Jamie Kaminski, President of HSR Zero Waste. “We’re proud to support this event again and to contribute to conversations that drive real, measurable impact.”

 As an ongoing supporter of CWMA, HSR Zero Waste remains committed to helping organizations reduce waste, improve diversion, and build circular systems that benefit both the environment and local communities.

 Learn more about HSR Zero Waste and its sustainability initiatives at www.hsrzerowaste.com.

 About HSR Zero Waste

 HSR Zero Waste is a leading environmental consulting firm specializing in Zero Waste strategies, waste diversion, and sustainability education.
