# Honoring Those Who Make “All Things Possible” — 2025 ATAP Community Service Awards & Kwanzaa Gala

BURLINGTON, NJ / PR Karma / October 15, 2025 5:50

All Things Are Possible Foundation (ATAP) proudly announces its 5th Annual ATAP Community Service Awards, a signature celebration recognizing the outstanding contributions of Burlington County residents and business leaders who uplift our youth, strengthen families, and empower the community.

 This year’s event will honor a remarkable group of individuals whose dedication to service, leadership, and excellence reflects the true spirit of community.

 The 2025 ATAP Kwanzaa Community Service Award Honorees are:

 * Building for the Future: Crystal Charley, Melanin and Maternal Wellness
* Courageous Leadership Award: LaChia L. Bradshaw, Burlington County Prosecutor
* Faith & Trust: Wilfrans Joseph, Christ Baptist Church
* Hope & Inspiration: Joyce C. Payne, Willingboro/Burlington, New Jersey Orators
* Humility & Strength: Chief Ian Bucs, Willingboro Township Police Department
* Peace & Unity: Algernon Hall, M.B.A., Jubilee Children's Entertainment, LLC / Algernon Hall Inspires
* Perseverance: Dr. Chekesha Ellis, KayosToClarity, LLC
* Understanding & Cooperation: Christina M. Teel, M.Ed., South Jersey Gas
* ATAP Scholar: Naasia King

 Nominations were reviewed by the ATAP Awards Committee, which selected this year’s honorees for their unwavering commitment to creating positive change and advancing ATAP’s mission of empowering the next generation.

 “As we gather to celebrate these incredible honorees, we also celebrate the power of community,” said Dr. Carlos Worthy, ATAP Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer. “Each of these leaders embodies what it means to make a lasting impact. This event is not just a recognition of their work—it’s an invitation for all of us to be inspired and to find our own ways to make a difference,” said Dr. Tiffani Worthy, ATAP Co-Founder and CEO.

 Join us for an unforgettable evening of recognition, culture, and connection at the 2025 ATAP Community Service Awards & Kwanzaa Gala on Friday, December 5, 2025, at The Merion, located at 1301 Route 130, Cinnaminson, NJ 08077. Check out highlights from 2024!

 Tickets are on sale now at 2025 ATAP Community Service Awards and Kwanzaa Gala. Be part of this inspiring celebration as we honor those who make ‘All Things Possible’ in Burlington County.

 About All Things Are Possible Foundation: For nearly 18 years, ATAP, a 501 (c) (3) nonprofit organization, has offered life-changing, developmental programs that uplift and empower students to achieve greatness. Our mission is to empower youth and to enhance the community through academics, athletics, arts, and awareness in response to critical youth and community needs. ATAP Co-founder and CEO Dr. Tiffani Worthy is a Willingboro resident, and business and community leader. She is a West Point graduate and decorated combat Veteran who earned a doctorate in education. As a working mother, she was disappointed in the limited choices for academic enrichment and afterschool programs and developed ATAP’s fun learning curriculum to help youth and teens discover what’s possible. Discover more at www.atapfoundation.org.
