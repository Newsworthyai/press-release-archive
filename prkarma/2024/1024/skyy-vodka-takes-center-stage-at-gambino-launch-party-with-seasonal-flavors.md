# Skyy Vodka Takes Center Stage at Gambino Launch Party with Seasonal Flavors

SAN DIEGO, CA / PR Karma / October 24, 2024 11:00

San Diego, CA – Skyy Vodka will be the star of the show at this year’s Gambino Launch Party, hosted by Bay City Brewing and Counterflow Coffee. Scheduled for Wednesday, October 30th, from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM at Bay City Brewing’s downtown East Village location (627 Eighth Ave, San Diego), the event will feature signature espresso martinis highlighting the smooth and crisp taste of Skyy Vodka.

 Guests will enjoy two special espresso martini creations:

 * Pumpkin Spice Espresso Martini, featuring Skyy Vodka for a seasonal favorite.
* Hazelnut Espresso Martini, with a hint of Frangelico to complement the rich coffee notes.

 "bar Ella [in PB] serves the best espresso martini, and it's only $6!" said Caroline Cardozo (@caroolzinhacv). You can view her reel here.

 Counterflow Coffee, known for its high-quality beans, is already a staple in some of San Diego’s favorite bars and restaurants, including Bar Ella, Local, OB Noodlehouse, Mavericks, Tavern, Wonderland, and The Holding Company. Now, the local favorite is partnering with Skyy Vodka to create an unforgettable cocktail experience.

 The evening will also celebrate the return of the Gambino Coffee Stout, with $5 drafts available alongside new seasonal flavors like Coconut Macadamia, Peanut Butter, and Maple Donut. Guests can also enjoy delicious bites from ELE Burgers, rounding out an evening of craft cocktails, beer, and great food.

 Event Details: What: Gambino Coffee Stout Launch Party featuring Skyy Vodka Espresso Martinis When: October 30th, 2024, 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM Where: Bay City Brewing Downtown, 627 Eighth Ave, San Diego, CA Cost: Free to the public

 For more information about Skyy Vodka and the Gambino Launch Party, visit Skyy Vodka, Counterflow Coffee, or Bay City Brewing.

 About Skyy Vodka Skyy Vodka is known for its crisp, refined flavor, making it a go-to choice for premium cocktails. As the featured vodka for the Gambino Launch Party, Skyy Vodka will take center stage in the evening’s signature espresso martinis, crafted to perfection.
