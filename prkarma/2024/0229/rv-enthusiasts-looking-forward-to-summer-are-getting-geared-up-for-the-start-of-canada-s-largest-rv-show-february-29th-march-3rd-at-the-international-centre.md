# RV enthusiasts looking forward to summer are getting geared up for the start of Canada's largest RV Show February 29th - March 3rd at The International Centre

MISSISSAUGA, ON / PR Karma / February 29, 2024 9:55

As Canadians continue to embrace outdoor adventures following the COVID-19 pandemic, the excitement is growing for the return of the 2024 Toronto Spring Camping and RV Show, scheduled from February 29th to March 3rd, 2024, at The International Centre in Mississauga.

 Proudly presented by the Canadian Recreational Vehicle Association (“CRVA”), the event stands as Canada's largest RV show and a testament to the growing interest in the RV and Camping lifestyle.

 The pandemic era witnessed a significant shift as more Canadians turned to RVing and camping, exploring new ways to safely enjoy travel and leisure and interest has not waned.

 The popularity of the Toronto Spring Camping and RV Show reflects this passion, offering over 450 RVs of every type, model and size from top Ontario retailers making it the ideal venue and occasion for both seasoned RV enthusiasts and first-time explorers eager to embark and learn aout their RV journey.

 Event Highlights:

 * A vast display of RV models, including high end Motor Homes, Travel Trailers, Fifth Wheels, Toy Haulers, Truck Campers and Folding Trailers, tailored for every taste and budget.
* The15,000 sq ft “Camper Marketplace” featuring 1000 Islands RV, offering a range of parts & accessories, other consumer products alongside direct access to campground operators.
* Insightful daily seminars by industry professionals covering essential RVing tips including maintenance, solar power, Tire Safety, Chemical Treatments, and a beginner's guide to RVing by the Canadian RV Association.
* Live entertainment in the RV Lifestyle Village on “The Big Stage.” and relaxing around a campfire consuming your favourite beverage
* Enter to Win exciting prize draws, including a Dream 7-Day Motor Home Vacation, including Coleman Camping Gear .and a $250 Visa Gas Card plus much more.
* Family-friendly activities in the Future Camper Zone, including face painting, contests, and interactive games

 Event Details:

 * Dates & Times: February 29th (10 a.m. – 7 p.m.), March 1st (10 a.m. – 7 p.m.), March 2nd (10 a.m. – 7 p.m.) and March 3rd (10 a.m. – 5 p.m.)
* Location: The International Centre, 6900 Airport Rd, Mississauga, ON L4V. Complimentary free parking available.
* Entrances are into Hall 1, 5 and 3

 As the winter gets ready to close,  the Toronto Spring Camping and RV Show is not just any event but the "Event of the Year" that you don't want to miss!

 Join us in this unique experience and get ready for a summer of unforgettable adventures. For more information, please visit www.torontospringcampingrvshow.com.
