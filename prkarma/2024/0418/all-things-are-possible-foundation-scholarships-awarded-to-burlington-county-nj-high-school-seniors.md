 ALL THINGS ARE POSSIBLE (ATAP) FOUNDATION SCHOLARSHIPS AWARDED TO BURLINGTON COUNTY, NJ HIGH SCHOOL SENIORS

 The annual All Things Are Possible (ATAP) Foundation Scholarships are awarded to high school seniors who have demonstrated a positive impact in their community and a commitment to further their impact through post-second education, in the workforce, and/or in the military. This year the scholarships total $7500 and will be awarded to some amazing scholars:

 Jasmyn Allen, BCIT Westampton Campus, Making a Difference Scholarship, $1250

 Soleil Casseus, BCIT Medford Campus, Scholar Athlete Scholarship, $1250

 Karreem Antonio Kamara, Willingboro High School, Vocational Excellence Scholarship, $1250

 Kaiya Johnson, Girard College,  Yes We Can! Scholarship, $1250

 Drew Green, Willingboro High School, Austin Conrad McIntosh Scholarship, $2500

 

 Join ATAP Foundation and the NAACP Willingboro & Vicinity Branch as we host the 2024 Scholarship Award Lunch on Saturday, May 18, 2024, at 11 AM to celebrate these scholars at The Boro Epicenter at 621 Beverly Rancocas Rd., Willingboro, NJ 08046. Tickets are only $10 and can be purchased at: https://atapfoundation.org/event/scholarshiplunch/

 

 All Things Are Possible Foundation (ATAP) serves Burlington County, New Jersey, families with academic enrichment programs with a differentiated and highly valuable offering: life-changing, developmental programs with an emphasis on academics, athletics, awareness, and arts.

 ATAP serves over 500 children annually with out-of-school-time (OST) programs, including before and after school care, full-day fall, spring, and summer programs, STEAM workshops, and other academic and cultural enrichment programs for K-8 and teens.

 ATAP is a non-profit 501(c)3 organization.
