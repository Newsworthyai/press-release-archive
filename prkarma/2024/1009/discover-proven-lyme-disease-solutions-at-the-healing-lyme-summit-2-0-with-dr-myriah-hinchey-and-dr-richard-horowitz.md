# Discover Proven Lyme Disease Solutions at the Healing Lyme Summit 2.0 with Dr. Myriah Hinchey and Dr. Richard Horowitz

CARLSBAD, CA / PR Karma / October 9, 2024 9:55

 DrTalks, a platform that brings health knowledge to your fingertips, is proud to present the Healing Lyme Summit 2.0, happening from April 15 to 21, 2025. This free, online event is open to everyone and is designed to raise awareness and provide practical tools for diagnosing, treating, and healing from tick-borne illnesses.

 The Healing Lyme Summit 2.0 will be hosted by two of the most trusted experts in this field: Dr. Myriah Hinchey and Dr. Richard Horowitz.

 * Dr. Hinchey has spent almost two decades transforming the way we approach tick-borne diseases. Combining the precision of conventional medicine with the healing power of natural protocols, she helps her patients reduce inflammation and enhance their immune defenses. Her use of herbal medicine helps create an internal environment that makes it difficult for infections to thrive, paving the way for optimal health.
* Dr. Horowitz has been revolutionizing the understanding and treatment of tick-borne diseases for almost three decades. His work has unveiled the complex nature of these illnesses, helping countless patients find answers. In addition to being a renowned physician, he is the bestselling author of Why Can’t I Get Better? Solving the Mystery of Lyme and Chronic Disease, a definitive guide for those seeking solutions.

 The Healing Lyme Summit 2.0 offers an unparalleled opportunity to learn straight from top experts. Attendees will gain insights into holistic healing practices, advanced treatment protocols, and the latest research on tick-borne diseases.

 Whether you’re a patient, caregiver, or healthcare professional, this summit will provide you with the tools and resources necessary to combat this debilitating disease and thrive.

 For more information about this summit and to register for free, please visit https://drtalks.com/lyme-summit/.
