# Anton Burnette Named Alignable's Business Person of the Year for Cleveland, TN

Webive CEO Honored Again for Leadership and Community Impact in Cleveland, TN

 Cleveland, United States - August 4, 2025 / Webive Digital Marketing and SEO /

 Anton Burnette, CEO and founder of Webive Digital Marketing, has been honored as Alignable's "Local Business Person of the Year" for Cleveland, TN, for the second consecutive year. This recognition underscores Burnette's unwavering commitment to empowering local businesses through innovative digital marketing strategies and community engagement.

Alignable, the largest online networking platform for small business owners, annually recognizes outstanding entrepreneurs who demonstrate exceptional leadership and peer support. Burnette's consecutive wins reflect his dedication to fostering growth and collaboration within the local business community.

Under Burnette's leadership, Webive Digital Marketing has become a trusted partner for small and mid-sized businesses seeking tailored solutions in SEO, web development, branding, and social media strategy. His efforts have significantly enhanced the online presence and growth of numerous local enterprises.

Beyond his digital marketing expertise, Burnette serves as an Alignable Ambassador for the Greater Chattanooga area. In this role, he hosts monthly in-person networking events at Webive's Cleveland office, fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing among entrepreneurs and professionals in the region.

"Being recognized by my peers for a second year is incredibly humbling," said Burnette. "This award is a testament to the strength of our local business community and the power of supporting one another."
