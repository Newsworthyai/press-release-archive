Dental implants are a popular and effective solution for individuals who have lost teeth due to injury, decay, or other dental issues. Unlike traditional dentures, dental implants provide a permanent solution that looks and functions like natural teeth. The process involves placing a titanium post into the jawbone, which serves as a sturdy foundation for a replacement tooth. This innovative approach not only restores the appearance of a smile but also enhances the ability to chew and speak comfortably.

The journey to a new smile begins with a comprehensive evaluation. During this initial visit, the oral surgeon will assess the patient's dental health, discuss their goals, and determine if dental implants are the right option. This thorough examination includes X-rays and possibly 3D imaging to create a detailed plan for the implant procedure. The dental implant procedure typically involves several stages. After the initial consultation, the first step is the surgical placement of the titanium post into the jawbone. This procedure is performed under local anesthesia or sedation, depending on the patient's preference and comfort level. Once the post is securely in place, a healing period follows, allowing the bone to integrate with the implant. This process can take several months, but it is crucial for the long-term success of the implant.

After the healing period, patients return to Babston Oral Surgery for the placement of the abutment, which connects the implant to the crown. The final step is the creation and placement of the custom-made crown, designed to match the patient's natural teeth. The result is a seamless and beautiful smile that enhances both appearance and function.
