# Join Good Golly Garage Doors in Supporting The Shade Tree This November

Good Golly Garage Doors Launches Charity Drive for Local Families

 Las Vegas, United States - November 9, 2025 / Good Golly Garage Doors /

 Good Golly Garage Doors Teams Up with The Shade Tree to Assist Families in Need This November, Las Vegas, NVGood Golly Garage Doors, a reputable provider of garage door repair, maintenance, installation, and opener services with locations in Las Vegas, NV, is excited to announce its collaboration with The Shade Tree, the leading domestic violence shelter and resource center in Southern Nevada. Together, they are launching a month-long community donation initiative focused on assisting women, teens, and children facing crises in the Las Vegas region. Join Us in Making a Difference - November 1-30, 2025Throughout the month of November, Good Golly Garage Doors will be gathering essential items to support the residents and outreach programs of The Shade Tree.Items Needed:* Sweaters and jackets for women, teens, and children
* Clothing for teens
* Non-perishable food items
* Diapers and baby formula

As a token of appreciation to the community, Good Golly Garage Doors is providing a FREE Garage Door Tune-Up with every donation.How It WorksCommunity members can reach out by calling [702-213-7418]() to arrange a convenient pick-up of their donated items. A technician from Good Golly Garage Doors will arrive in a company vehicle to collect the donations and offer a complimentary garage door tune-up as a gesture of gratitude for participating. This initiative aims to provide vital items that foster comfort and stability for local families in need while motivating community members to contribute in a practical and impactful manner.For over thirty years, The Shade Tree has served as a beacon of hope and safety for families in Southern Nevada, providing shelter, counseling, and transformative resources for survivors of domestic violence and human trafficking.How to Get InvolvedTo arrange for your donation pick-up and receive your free garage door tune-up, call [702-213-7418]().For additional information about Good Golly Garage Doors, please visit [www.goodgollygaragedoors.com](http://www.goodgollygaragedoors.com/).
