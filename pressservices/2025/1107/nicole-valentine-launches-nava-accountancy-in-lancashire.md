# Nicole Valentine Launches Nava Accountancy in Lancashire

Nava Accountancy Launches in Lancashire with a Fresh, Person-Centered Approach to Accounting

 Haslingden, United Kingdom - November 7, 2025 / Nava Accountancy Ltd /

 After 15 years of dedicated experience supporting small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) across the North West, seasoned accountant [Nicole Valentine is proud to announce the launch of her new venture, Nava Accountancy](https://navaaccountancy.co.uk/introducing-nava-accountancy-a-fresh-approach-to-accounting-in-lancashire/). Based in Rossendale, the firm is set to redefine the accounting experience for business owners with a modern, personal, and empowering approach.Nava Accountancy is founded on a clear vision: to move beyond traditional number-crunching and build close, supportive relationships with clients. The firm’s ethos, “More Than Just Numbers,” reflects a commitment to helping business owners genuinely understand their finances and make confident, informed decisions about their future.“I’ve seen too many business owners feel overwhelmed by their finances rather than empowered by them,” says Nicole Valentine, founder of Nava Accountancy. “I wanted to create a firm that feels approachable, where you feel listened to and supported. Accounting should empower people, not overwhelm them.”Nava Accountancy offers a range of services tailored to the needs of startups, growing businesses, and established SMEs. From providing clear and practical financial guidance to simplifying financial management and supporting sustainable growth, the firm is equipped to handle the diverse needs of today’s entrepreneurs.With a strong commitment to the local community, Nava Accountancy plans to establish a permanent office in Rossendale and recruit local talent that mirrors the region's ambition and entrepreneurial spirit. This local focus, combined with a modern approach, positions Nava Accountancy as a key partner for businesses in Lancashire and beyond.About Nava Accountancy:[Nava Accountancy](https://navaaccountancy.co.uk/) is a modern accounting firm based in Rossendale, Lancashire, founded by Nicole Valentine. The firm provides clear, practical, and personalized financial guidance to small and medium-sized businesses across the UK. With a focus on empowering business owners, Nava Accountancy simplifies financial management and supports sustainable business growth.
