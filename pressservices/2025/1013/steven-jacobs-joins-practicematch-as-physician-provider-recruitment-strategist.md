# Steven Jacobs Joins PracticeMatch as Physician &amp; Provider Recruitment Strategist

PracticeMatch Expands Team with Steven Jacobs, Driving Physician Recruitment Success

 St. Louis, United States - October 13, 2025 / PracticeMatch /

 [PracticeMatch](http://practicematch.com/), a leading provider of physician and advanced practice provider recruitment solutions, is proud to announce the addition of Steven Jacobs to its growing team as the company's new Physician & Provider Recruitment Strategist.In this role, Jacobs will partner directly with healthcare organizations across the country to strengthen recruitment strategies, maximize the effectiveness of recruitment tools, and ensure clients achieve the best possible results in attracting top physician and provider talent.With extensive experience in healthcare recruitment and a proven track record of building strong client relationships, Jacobs brings valuable expertise to [PracticeMatch's](http://practicematch.com/) client-focused mission. He will provide consultative support to help organizations implement best practices and optimize hiring outcomes in an increasingly competitive recruitment landscape."We are excited to welcome Steven to the PracticeMatch family," said Clint Rosser, CEO of PracticeMatch. "His knowledge and client-focused approach will be a tremendous asset as we continue to support healthcare organizations in meeting their recruitment needs."Jacobs' appointment reflects PracticeMatchâ€™s continued investment in providing expert resources, innovative technology, and data-driven solutions to help healthcare employers address ongoing physician and provider shortages.

About PracticeMatch:PracticeMatch is a trusted leader in healthcare recruitment solutions, providing hospitals, medical groups, and healthcare organizations nationwide with access to the most comprehensive database of physicians and advanced practice providers. With a focus on innovation, compliance, and client success, PracticeMatch delivers technology, consultative expertise, and data-driven tools that empower recruiters to connect with the right candidates quickly and effectively.
