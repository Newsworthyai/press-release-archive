# Whole Health Everyday Celebrates 15 Years of Exceptional Private Chef Services

Whole Health Everyday: Your Go-To for Personal Chef Meal Prep

 Newport Beach, United States - December 4, 2025 / Whole Health Everyday /

 Whole Health Everyday is celebrating 15 years of elevating the private chef industry with personalized, wellness-driven meal prep services. Since 2011, the company has set a new standard for chef professionalism, culinary excellence, and customized nutrition tailored to each client’s lifestyle and health goals. What began as a mission to make healthy eating accessible, sustainable, and enjoyable has grown into a trusted brand known for consistency, longevity, and exceptional client care.

At the core of Whole Health Everyday’s success is a deep commitment to health and wellness as a culinary foundation. Every chef on the team is trained in health-conscious cooking and stays current with evolving dietary research and trends. This expertise allows the company to craft meals for a wide range of dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegan, low-carb, anti-inflammatory, performance-focused, and medically guided nutrition plans. Clients receive meals that are both gourmet and tailored to their goals.

Whole Health Everyday’s team includes 20 highly skilled chefs, with an average chef tenure of more than eight years. This long-term stability fosters collaboration, shared learning, and consistently high-quality service. Every chef undergoes a rigorous vetting and training process, ensuring only the most qualified professionals represent the brand. This standard of excellence is one of the reasons clients rely on Whole Health Everyday year after year.

The company is also elevated by the influence of Michelin-starred Chef Laurent from France, whose culinary experience enriches the creativity, technique, and flavor profiles infused into the company’s wellness-driven menus. His expertise contributes to Whole Health Everyday’s commitment to delivering meals that are both nutritionally sound and restaurant quality.

Guiding the company’s growth are Founder Rebecca and long-time executive partner John, whose leadership has shaped the evolution, culture, and strategic direction of Whole Health Everyday. The company has also benefited from long-term strategic consulting support, strengthening internal systems, optimizing operations, and supporting sustainable growth. Combined, these efforts position Whole Health Everyday as a well-run, forward-thinking organization ready for continued expansion.

With teams currently serving clients in three states and demand steadily increasing, the company is preparing to expand its chef network, enhance its nutrition-focused services, and introduce innovative offerings that make gourmet, wellness-centered meal prep even more accessible.

As Whole Health Everyday looks to the future, it remains dedicated to its mission of promoting health and wellness through exceptional meal prep services. With a team of experienced chefs, including the renowned Chef Laurent, and a commitment to staying current with dietary trends, Whole Health Everyday is poised to continue its legacy of excellence in the personal chef industry. Clients can expect nothing less than the best as they embark on their culinary journey with Whole Health Everyday, where healthy eating meets gourmet cooking.
