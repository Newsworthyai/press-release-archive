# Search Atlas Website Studio: AI-Driven Landing Pages for High-Converting PPC

Search Atlas Website Studio Unveiled on Day 2 of Search Atlas Christmas Livestreams

 New York City, United States - December 16, 2025 / Search Atlas /

 Search Atlas Introduces Website Studio: AI-Driven Landing Page Creation for High-Converting PPC CampaignsSearch Atlas announced the launch of Website Studio, a new platform designed to create high-quality landing pages in minutes.

Website Studio brings enterprise-level page creation to teams of any size by combining conversion-optimized PPC pages, instant website cloning, and a flexible free-mode builder into one unified system.

“Campaigns now move faster than development cycles which leaves most dev teams with shifting priorities,” said Manick Bhan, CEO and CTO of Search Atlas. “Website Studio fixes that issue by giving marketers pages that are ready to convert and ready to launch at the pace their campaigns move.”

How Website Studio Works1. Create Conversion-Focused PPC Pages. Website Studio analyzes ad copy, campaign intent, and target audiences to automatically generate pages engineered to convert.

2. Clone Competitor or Brand Pages Instantly. Users enter a URL, and the platform rebuilds its structure so teams can iterate faster, model successful designs, or refresh outdated layouts with ease.

3. Design Freely with Creative Control. The free-mode builder supports custom sections, layouts, and branding elements, enabling marketers to craft unique experiences without relying on developers.

These capabilities remove the bottlenecks that slow campaign deployment and make landing page execution accessible to anyone responsible for driving performance.

Website Studio Sets a New Standard for Landing Page DesignWebsite Studio gives marketing teams a faster, more flexible way to create landing pages without relying on developers, rigid templates, or long production cycles. PPC specialists, digital marketers, agencies, and in-house teams use the platform to launch new pages, test variations, and adapt layouts in minutes while maintaining the design quality expected from enterprise teams.

“Landing pages have to evolve with the pace of modern campaigns. Website Studio makes creation as fast and flexible as advertising itself, and that shift is what the future of landing page design looks like,” says Manick Bhan.

About Search AtlasSearch Atlas empowers businesses to strengthen search visibility through advanced SEO, PPC, and AI-powered marketing solutions. Trusted by startups and Fortune 500 companies, Search Atlas delivers automation that reduces operational complexity and drives measurable growth. Learn more at searchatlas.com.
