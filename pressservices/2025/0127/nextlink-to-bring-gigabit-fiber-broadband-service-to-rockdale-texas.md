# Nextlink to Bring Gigabit Fiber Broadband Service to Rockdale, Texas

Nextlink Fiber internet coming to over 3,300 homes and businesses in Rockdale

 Hudson Oaks, United States - January 27, 2025 / Tent Apps /

 Nextlink Fiber internet coming to over 3,300 homes and businesses in Rockdale

HUDSON OAKS, Texas – Nextlink Internet announced today that construction has begun on a new state-of-the-art Fiber Internet Network in Rockdale, Texas.

When construction is completed, Nextlink will have deployed fiber broadband at data speeds up to symmetrical 2 gigabits per second (Gbps) to over 3,300 homes and businesses in Rockdale, Texas.

Bill Baker, Founder and CEO of Nextlink Internet, said, “As a rural-focused provider, we are excited to be bringing broadband to areas that have gone without adequate services for far too long. Our fiber infrastructure in Rockdale will help to bridge the digital divide and provide homes and businesses in the city with world-class fiber connectivity.”

A variety of high-speed fiber plans will be made available to homes and businesses in Rockdale. Residents can view available plans and pre-register for service here: https://fiber.nextlinkinternet.com/

About Nextlink InternetNextlink Internet is a rural-focused provider of high-speed internet and phone services, investing over $1 billion in unserved and underserved communities with its fiber and wireless infrastructure. As an active participant in public-private partnerships to close the digital divide, Nextlink is working to rapidly connect millions of homes, businesses, and anchor institutions in rural areas and small communities across 12 states. For more information visit: nextlinkinternet.com.
