# SOUTH FLORIDA LUXURY BOOM CONTINUES

The Ritz-Carlton Residences Set a New Benchmark for Waterfront Luxury in West Palm Beach

 West Palm Beach, United States - August 18, 2025 / The Ritz Carlton West Palm Beach /

 The Ritz-Carlton Residences, West Palm Beach: Redefining Waterfront LuxuryWest Palm Beach, FL – The Ritz-Carlton Residences, West Palm Beach, introduce an unprecedented level of elegance, service, and design to one of South Florida’s most coveted waterfront locations. This exclusive collection of bespoke homes offers discerning buyers a rare opportunity to experience The Ritz-Carlton lifestyle in a private residential setting—without the transient presence of a hotel.

Bespoke Waterfront LivingSituated on prestigious Flagler Drive, these residences capture sweeping views of Palm Beach Island, the Atlantic Ocean, and the Intracoastal Waterway. Each home features floor-to-ceiling glass, expansive private terraces, and custom Italian cabinetry by Rockwell Group. Kitchens are appointed with Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances, while spa-inspired bathrooms and smart home technology elevate comfort and convenience.

Legendary Ritz-Carlton ServiceResidents enjoy the personalized attention and five-star service synonymous with The Ritz-Carlton name. A dedicated residential team anticipates every need, complemented by 24-hour concierge, valet, and security. A curated list of à la carte services—including private chefs, in-home spa treatments, and luxury travel arrangements—ensures every detail is managed with precision.

Curated Amenities for Wellness and LeisureFrom the resort-style pool deck with cabanas to the exclusive Cove Club beach membership, every amenity is designed to enrich daily life. Residents can enjoy a world-class spa, state-of-the-art fitness center, VR golf simulator, sculpture garden, and private guest suites—creating a community where wellness, leisure, and sophistication meet.

A Prime West Palm Beach AddressLocated just minutes from Worth Avenue shopping, MICHELIN-starred dining, and world-class marinas, The Ritz-Carlton Residences place owners at the heart of Palm Beach’s cultural and lifestyle offerings. Championship golf courses, art museums, and pristine beaches are all within easy reach, blending coastal tranquility with cosmopolitan energy.

A Visionary Development TeamThe project is brought to life by Related Group and BH Group, with architecture by Arquitectonica and interiors by Rockwell Group. This powerhouse collaboration ensures a standard of design, craftsmanship, and execution that is unmatched in the South Florida real estate market.

Own Your Place in Palm Beach’s FutureWith limited availability, The Ritz-Carlton Residences, West Palm Beach, present a once-in-a-generation opportunity to own a piece of waterfront elegance in one of America’s most sought-after destinations.
