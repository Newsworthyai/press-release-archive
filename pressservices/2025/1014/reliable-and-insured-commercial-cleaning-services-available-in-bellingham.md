# Reliable and Insured Commercial Cleaning Services Available in Bellingham

Licensed, Bonded, and Insured Janitorial and Office Cleaning Services in Whatcom County

 Bellingham, United States - October 14, 2025 / RSK Cleaning Services /

 RSK Cleaning Services Expands Commercial Cleaning in Bellingham — Now Serving All of Whatcom County with Licensed, Bonded & Insured Coverage Up to $1,000,000Professional janitorial, office, construction cleanup and commercial carpet services tailored for local businesses.

Bellingham, WA — RSK Cleaning Services today announced the availability of its full suite of commercial cleaning solutions across Bellingham and Whatcom County. The locally owned company offers customized janitorial programs and one-time specialty services backed by licensed, bonded operations and commercial liability insurance up to $1,000,000 — providing businesses added peace of mind and professional protection.

“A clean workplace is more than appearance — it’s employee health, productivity and your company’s first impression,” said Roy Knaak, Owner of RSK Cleaning Services. “We built RSK to deliver reliable, professional cleaning with transparent pricing and the insurance and credentials businesses require. Our teams are trained to protect your people and your property.”

RSK Cleaning Services provides flexible programs to fit businesses of every size and industry, including:

* Recurring janitorial services (daily, nightly, weekly or custom schedules)
* Professional office cleaning and touch-point disinfection
* Construction site clean-up and post-construction detailing
* Commercial carpet cleaning using industry-standard methods to remove dirt, allergens and stains
* Specialty services: interior glass, upholstery care, floor maintenance, and emergency/after-hours response

Business managers and property owners in Bellingham, Ferndale, Lynden, Birch Bay and surrounding communities can request a free on-site walkthrough and customized quote. RSK’s licensing, bonding, and $1,000,000 commercial liability coverage make it an ideal partner for businesses that require both dependable service and professional protection.

For more information or to schedule a free walkthrough and estimate, visit:https://rskcleaningservices.com/services/commercial-cleaning-services/
