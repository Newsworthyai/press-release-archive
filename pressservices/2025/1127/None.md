# Sureway Comfort Supports Community Through Charity Campaign

Sureway Comfort Joins Salvation Army for Holiday Charity Drive

 Bridgeville, United States - November 27, 2025 / Sureway Heating Cooling Plumbing /

 Bridgeville, PA - Sureway Comfort, a reputable HVAC and plumbing service located in Bridgeville, is excited to announce its involvement in the Salvation Army's Red Kettle Campaign, an essential initiative that addresses the needs of local families and individuals during the festive season.Though Sureway Comfort consistently supports various charitable organizations throughout the year, the team believes that the Red Kettle Campaign represents the most urgent and impactful opportunity this season. Renowned for its distinctive red kettles and volunteers ringing bells outside retail locations, the campaign generates vital funds to offer food, shelter, and emergency aid to those who require assistance.For John Wilcox, the owner of Sureway Comfort, this campaign holds a special significance. "I have been engaged with the Salvation Army for six or seven years now," Wilcox shares. "It is more than just a cause for me-it is personal. I have witnessed how these contributions transform lives, and it is something I genuinely support." Over the years, Wilcox has not only dedicated his time and resources but has also documented and shared moving images that highlight the importance of giving back. With strong ties to the Bridgeville community, Sureway Comfort is devoted to doing more than merely ensuring homes are warm and that water flows smoothly. The company considers community involvement a crucial aspect of its mission."We urge others in the community-whether individuals or businesses-to give in any way they can," Wilcox added. "Every dollar placed into those kettles truly makes a difference."To learn more about Sureway Comfort and their community initiatives, visit www.surewaycomfort.com.
