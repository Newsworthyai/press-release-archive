What Distinguishes 5G From 4G LTE?

5G employs broader channels and more sophisticated technologies that facilitate:

* Accelerated download and upload speeds
* Reduced latency
* Enhanced capacity for numerous devices
* More reliable performance in densely populated areas

Performance can vary based on the frequency band utilized:

* Low-band 5G - excellent coverage with moderate speeds
* Mid-band (such as C-Band) - a balance between speed and range
* mmWave - the highest speeds but limited indoor coverage

Is Special Hardware Required for 5G?

Indeed. To access genuine 5G performance, a 5G-compatible router, modem, hotspot, or outdoor unit is essential. Older LTE routers are unable to connect to 5G networks or utilize newer frequency bands.

How Can I Enhance My 5G Speeds If My Signal Is Weak?

Many customers see performance improvements by utilizing:

* High-gain antennas
* Directional antennas aimed at towers
* Outdoor installation for unobstructed line-of-sight
