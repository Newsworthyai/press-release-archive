# Three-Surgeon Team Covers Four SD Surgical Sites

Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Coordinates Referrals and Insurance Across Four SD Locations

 Sioux Falls, United States - May 1, 2026 / Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery /

 Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery has announced a formal expansion of its surgical services across four South Dakota locations, broadening its reach to serve more patients throughout the Sioux Falls region with specialized oral and maxillofacial procedures. The expansion reflects deliberate growth in both clinical capacity and technology infrastructure, making specialized surgical care more accessible to residents across the area.

Four Locations Now Serving Oral Surgery Patients Across the Region

The practice now operates across four regional locations, each staffed by board-trained surgeons and equipped to handle a comprehensive range of oral and maxillofacial procedures. Services available include dental implant placement, wisdom teeth removal, corrective jaw surgery, and treatment for facial injuries - all delivered within a unified network of facilities that patients across the region can access.

The multi-location model is structured to reduce travel burden for patients in surrounding communities while maintaining consistent clinical standards at each site. Surgical preparation, patient monitoring, and post-operative care follow the same protocols across all locations, ensuring uniform outcomes regardless of where a procedure takes place.

Surgical Team Brings Board-Trained Expertise to Every Procedure

Three surgeons lead clinical operations across the four locations: Denis Miller, DDS, MBA, Louis George, DMD, and Jason Leet, DDS. Each holds specialized training in oral and maxillofacial surgery, enabling the practice to manage both routine outpatient cases and complex reconstructive procedures within a single organizational structure.

"With three board-trained surgeons now operating across four locations, we have the capacity to see more patients without compromising the surgical precision and safety standards that define this practice," said Denis Miller, DDS, MBA, Surgeon at Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery. "Our goal was to make expert oral surgery Sioux Falls SD patients rely on available to more people in surrounding communities, and this expansion does exactly that."

The surgical team handles cases ranging from simple tooth extractions to full-arch implant restorations and facial trauma repair. Having multiple surgeons across locations also supports more flexible scheduling - an advantage for patients who require time-sensitive procedures.

3D Imaging and Outpatient Anesthesia Support Precise, Safe Outcomes

A central element of the practice's clinical model is its use of 3D cone beam imaging technology, which delivers detailed anatomical mapping prior to surgical intervention. This allows surgeons to identify critical structures - including nerves, sinus cavities, and bone density variations - before performing procedures such as implant placement or bone grafting.

The practice also administers outpatient anesthesia on-site, enabling patients to undergo procedures in a controlled, monitored environment without requiring hospital admission. This model supports patient comfort and clinical efficiency, reducing procedure times and allowing for faster recovery in most outpatient cases.

Patient safety protocols are integrated throughout the surgical process, from pre-operative assessments through post-procedure follow-up. Strict standards for sterilization, equipment maintenance, and anesthesia monitoring are maintained across all locations in alignment with current guidelines for ambulatory surgical settings.

Expanded Access to Specialized Care Across South Dakota

The four-location network extends the geographic reach of Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery across the region. Patients from communities outside Sioux Falls now have access to a nearby facility for oral surgery in Sioux Falls SD and surrounding areas, without requiring referrals to out-of-state practices for procedures that were previously harder to access locally.

The practice accepts a range of insurance plans and coordinates directly with referring general dentists and primary care providers to manage care for patients with complex needs. This referral structure supports continuity of care, particularly for those undergoing multi-stage treatments such as full-mouth reconstruction or implant-supported prosthetics.

About Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

Siouxland Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery is a multi-location oral and maxillofacial surgery practice operating across four sites in South Dakota. The practice is led by three board-trained surgeons - Denis Miller, DDS, MBA, Louis George, DMD, and Jason Leet, DDS - and provides a full range of surgical services including dental implants, wisdom teeth removal, corrective jaw procedures, and facial injury treatment. The practice uses 3D imaging technology and on-site outpatient anesthesia to support precise surgical outcomes and patient safety.
