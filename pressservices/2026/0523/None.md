# No Shave Hair Transplant Now Available in Ankara

No Shave, No Signal: gethair.co.uk Brings Discreet Hair Transplants to Ankara

 London, United States - May 22, 2026 / GetHair /

 gethair.co.uk has formally announced the availability of its no shave hair transplant service at its Ankara clinic, giving patients a way to address hair loss without the shaved head that has long kept many people from pursuing treatment. The announcement marks a deliberate step by the clinic to serve international patients who have delayed seeking help not because of cost or logistics, but because of how they would look in the days and weeks immediately following a procedure.

A Barrier That Has Stopped Many People From ActingFor a significant number of people experiencing hair loss, the prospect of sitting across from colleagues, friends, or family with a freshly shaved head has been enough to put the idea of treatment aside entirely. The concern is understandable. A standard hair transplant procedure typically requires the donor area -- and often the full scalp -- to be shaved before grafts are extracted and placed. That visible change can feel exposing at work, socially, or simply in the mirror each morning.

gethair.co.uk's no shave hair transplant approach removes that requirement. Patients keep their existing hair at its current length throughout the procedure. The transplanted hair is placed to blend with surrounding hair from the moment treatment is complete, meaning there is no stark visual signal that anything has happened. For someone who has been quietly managing hair loss for years and is not ready to make that struggle public, this matters considerably.

Dr. Tayfun Oguzoglu and the Ankara ClinicThe service is led by Dr. Tayfun Oguzoglu, who brings nearly 30 years of specialist hair transplant experience to each procedure. He is a Fellow of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (FISHRS) and a Diplomate of the American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery (ABHRS) — credentials held by only a small number of surgeons in Turkey. That depth of experience and recognition means the work is carried out with the kind of precision that discreet hair restoration demands — individual grafts placed with care so that the final result looks natural rather than transplanted.

Turkey is well established as a global destination for hair transplant expertise , attracting patients from across Europe and beyond who are looking for skilled practitioners and high clinical standards. gethair.co.uk has built its practice specifically around serving international patients, with processes designed to make the experience manageable for those travelling from abroad.

"For many of our patients, the no shave option is the reason they finally made the call," said Dr. Tayfun Oguzoglu, lead surgeon of gethair.co.uk. "They had been thinking about treatment for a long time but could not face the idea of returning home or going into work looking like they had just had surgery. Keeping their hair at its existing length means the change is gradual and entirely on their terms."

Discreet Hair Restoration Designed Around Real LifeThe practical effect of the no shave approach is that patients can return to their daily routines without the procedure being visible to others. There is no recovery period spent avoiding mirrors or declining social invitations. The transplanted hair integrates naturally with the hair already present, and the process of new growth unfolds in a way that those around the patient may simply never notice.

gethair.co.uk has positioned hair transplant in Ankara as a credible and accessible option for people who previously assumed that effective treatment and a discreet experience could not coexist. The clinic's announcement is aimed directly at those who have been sitting with that assumption -- and waiting.

About gethair.co.ukgethair.co.uk is a UK-based hair transplant provider with its clinic in Ankara, Turkey, specialising in procedures for international patients. The clinic is led by Dr. Tayfun Oguzoglu, who has been performing hair transplant surgery since 1996 and is one of only a handful of surgeons in Turkey accredited by both the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS) and the American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery (ABHRS). gethair.co.uk offers a range of hair restoration services, including a no-shave hair transplant option designed to allow patients to maintain their appearance throughout treatment and recovery.
