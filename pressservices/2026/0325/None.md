# PowerSteering: Bridging the Gap in Financial Tracking

Accurate Financial Insights for Continuous Improvement

 Austin, United States - March 22, 2026 / Upland Software, Inc. /

 Upland PowerSteering Tracks Financial Impact with an Enterprise Project Portfolio Management ToolAUSTIN, TEXAS - Continuous improvement initiatives, including Lean Six Sigma and Agile transformations, play a vital role in achieving operational excellence. Nevertheless, organizations often face challenges in accurately assessing the financial outcomes of these methodologies. When transformation offices manage their cost-saving projects through disconnected spreadsheets, it becomes an administrative challenge to verify the "hard savings" and effectively communicate that return on investment (ROI) to the finance department, leading to potential inaccuracies.

To effectively capture the financial advantages of these programs, enterprise leaders need dedicated tracking solutions. PowerSteering, a business transformation platform, seamlessly incorporates financial tracking throughout the project lifecycle. By leveraging specialized Project Portfolio Management Software, continuous improvement experts can establish baselines, monitor ongoing expenses, and confirm actual savings against their initial projections. This financial discipline guarantees that each Lean Six Sigma project is accountable for its expected ROI.

Creating a credible connection between operational enhancements and the company's financial performance enhances the significance of the transformation office. When organizations implement an Enterprise project portfolio management tool, they furnish the CFO with a transparent, verifiable record of enterprise savings. By integrating PowerSteering into these workflows, businesses can confidently expand their continuous improvement initiatives, assured that each optimized process is positively impacting overall profitability.

About Upland PowerSteering

PowerSteering is an advanced Project Portfolio Management Software designed to assist executives in driving strategy, managing enterprise transformation, and tracking continuous improvement efforts. As a comprehensive Enterprise project portfolio management tool, PowerSteering offers top-down visibility into programs, projects, and financial performance across the organization. By dismantling departmental barriers and providing real-time analytics, PowerSteering empowers PMOs and business leaders to prioritize investments, optimize resource distribution, and accurately measure the financial ROI of their strategic initiatives.
