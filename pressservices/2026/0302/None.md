# SearchAtlas Unveils First Programmable Omnichannel Marketing Platform

Introducing the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP by SearchAtlas

 Hutto, United States - March 1, 2026 / Search Atlas /

 SearchAtlas has officially launched the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP, marking a significant advancement in the realm of digital marketing. This innovative platform is the first of its kind, built on the Model Context Protocol (MCP), and it offers a comprehensive suite of 112 AI-driven marketing tools. These tools span across 17 product namespaces, providing agencies and brands with a robust solution for their marketing needs.

The Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP is designed to revolutionize how businesses approach various aspects of digital marketing, including search engine optimization (SEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), Google Ads management, local SEO, Google My Business (GMB) optimization, AI search visibility, content generation, digital public relations (PR), cloud stacks, and website creation. By integrating these diverse functionalities into a single platform, SearchAtlas aims to streamline marketing efforts and enhance overall efficiency.

One of the standout features of the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP is its compatibility with leading AI agents, including Claude, GPT, and Gemini. This compatibility allows users to leverage the power of advanced AI technologies to optimize their marketing strategies. The platform is designed to work seamlessly with any MCP-compatible AI agent, ensuring that users can take full advantage of the latest advancements in artificial intelligence.

Among the many tools available within the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP, OTTO SEO automation stands out as a game-changer for SEO professionals. This feature automates various SEO tasks, allowing marketers to focus on strategy and creativity rather than getting bogged down in repetitive tasks. Additionally, the LLM Visibility tool enhances GEO optimization, ensuring that businesses can effectively reach their target audiences in specific geographic locations.

The platform also offers full Google Ads campaign management, enabling users to create, manage, and optimize their advertising campaigns with ease. This comprehensive management capability ensures that businesses can maximize their return on investment (ROI) from their advertising efforts. Furthermore, the Content Genius AI content generation tool empowers marketers to produce high-quality, engaging content quickly and efficiently, reducing the time and effort typically required for content creation.

Digital PR outreach is another critical component of the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP. The platform provides tools that facilitate effective outreach to media outlets and influencers, helping brands to build their online presence and enhance their reputation. This feature is particularly valuable in today's digital landscape, where a strong online presence is essential for success.

In addition to these features, the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP includes Cloud Stack authority backlinks across more than 14 providers. This functionality allows users to build high-quality backlinks that can significantly improve their website's search engine rankings. The LinkLab guest post marketplace further enhances this capability, providing users with access to a network of opportunities for guest posting and collaboration.

The launch of the Agentic Omnichannel Marketing MCP represents a significant milestone for SearchAtlas and the digital marketing industry as a whole. By combining advanced AI technologies with a comprehensive suite of marketing tools, the platform empowers businesses to take control of their marketing strategies and achieve their goals more effectively.
