# Russell Bus Yard Hosts CCSD Staff Event with Live Radio Broadcast

97.1 The Point Broadcast Live as CCSD Transportation Staff Were Honored at Russell Bus Yard

 Las Vegas, United States - June 17, 2026 / Richard Harris Law Firm /

 LAS VEGAS, NV - The daily safety and academic access of students depends on a broad network of community support, yet the contributions of school transportation professionals frequently go unrecognized.

Richard Harris Law Firm, a personal injury practice that has served Nevada for more than 40 years, joined forces with the Clark County School District (CCSD) to host a Bus Driver Appreciation Event. Held on Tuesday, April 28, 2026, the event recognized the transportation staff members who work each day to move students safely throughout the Las Vegas Valley.

The community gathering was designed to spotlight the important role that school bus drivers and logistics personnel play in supporting student well-being, consistent school attendance, and dependable regional infrastructure across Southern Nevada.

Community Collaboration to Honor Essential StaffThe appreciation event ran from 9:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. at the Russell Bus Yard, located at 1700 Galleria Drive in Las Vegas, directly across from White Middle School. Approximately 325 CCSD transportation employees attended the morning event.

Richard Harris Law Firm provided refreshments for all attendees as a gesture of appreciation. The event also received support from local community partners, including Pinkbox Doughnuts, Siegel Cares, and Lotus Broadcasting, with 97.1 The Point radio station broadcasting live on-site throughout the morning.

"Bus drivers and transportation staff are essential to the daily success and safety of our students," said Richard Harris, Founder of the Richard Harris Law Firm. "This event is a small way to recognize their hard work and dedication to our community."

A Longstanding Commitment to Southern NevadaA dedication to public safety and community well-being sits at the core of the firm's approach. While the legal team regularly assists individuals with complex insurance disputes, medical damages, and personal injury claims following serious accidents, the practice also maintains a consistent focus on regional investment.

Through support for education, first responders, and local nonprofits, the firm works to strengthen the Las Vegas community beyond its legal practice. The partnership with CCSD reflected the firm's ongoing commitment to recognizing the frontline workers who help keep local families safe each day.

Event Recap and Media CoverageThe appreciation event drew strong attendance from CCSD transportation staff and regional community partners. On-site coverage included interviews with event organizers, legal team representatives, and CCSD participants.
