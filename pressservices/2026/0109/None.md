# TG Orthodontics Named a Monmouth Magazine 2026 Top Dentist

TG Orthodontics Honored as a 2026 Top Dentist by Monmouth Magazine

 Marlboro Township, United States - January 8, 2026 / TG Orthodontics by Dr. Tara Gostovich, DMD /

 TG Orthodontics is proud to announce that the practice has once again been selected as a Monmouth Magazine 2026 Top Dentist, a prestigious recognition reserved for dental professionals who demonstrate exceptional clinical excellence and high professional regard within the Monmouth County community.

This honor is based on a peer-review study conducted by Professional Research Services (PRS) of Troy, Michigan. As part of the study, hundreds of dentists across Monmouth County were surveyed and asked to vote for peers they consider the best across seven dental specialties. TG Orthodontics was chosen based on the professional trust and respect of fellow dental providers throughout the region.

The complete list of 2026 Top Dentists will be published in the February 2026 issue of Monmouth Magazine.

“This recognition is truly meaningful because it comes from the dental community,” said Dr. Tara Gostovich, DMD, founder of TG Orthodontics. “Being selected again is a tremendous honor, and it reflects our commitment to delivering high-quality orthodontic care, personalized service, and a positive experience for every patient who entrusts us with their smile.”

TG Orthodontics is known for providing modern orthodontic care for children, teens, and adults, including treatment options designed for comfort, efficiency, and long-term results. The practice remains dedicated to helping patients achieve healthy, confident smiles through customized treatment planning and compassionate care.

For more information about TG Orthodontics or to schedule a consultation, please contact the office directly.

About TG OrthodonticsTG Orthodontics is a Marlboro, New Jersey orthodontic practice led by Dr. Tara Gostovich, DMD. The office offers expert orthodontic care with a focus on advanced treatment options, personalized attention, and exceptional patient experiences for all ages.

About Monmouth Magazine’s Top DentistsMonmouth Magazine’s annual Top Dentists recognition honors outstanding dental professionals in Monmouth County through a peer-review survey conducted by Professional Research Services (PRS). Dentists across the county are invited to vote for colleagues they believe represent the highest levels of excellence across multiple specialties.
