# HVAC Leader Partners with Local Livestock Show

Temperature Control Services Announces Support for CPJLS

 Creedmoor, United States - March 8, 2026 / Temperature Control Services Inc /

 Temperature Control Services Inc., a prominent provider of HVAC and comfort solutions in the area, is excited to announce its official sponsorship of the upcoming Central Piedmont Junior Livestock Show & Sale (CPJLS). This collaboration highlights the company's dedication to supporting local youth, agricultural education, and the diligent families of the Central Piedmont region.

The CPJLS stands out as a significant regional event that allows young exhibitors to demonstrate their commitment, skills, and livestock. Through their involvement in this program, local youth gain essential insights into responsibility, sportsmanship, and the vital role of the agricultural sector.

"We are proud to back the young leaders of our community through the Central Piedmont Junior Livestock Show & Sale," stated a representative from Temperature Control Services Inc. "These students dedicate numerous hours to their efforts, and we are committed to investing in the future of agricultural professionals."

The event showcases various livestock categories and culminates in a live auction, where the community unites to celebrate the hard work of these junior exhibitors. Supporters and those interested can access the complete schedule of events and registration information through the Showman app.

About Temperature Control Services Inc. Temperature Control Services Inc. delivers exceptional heating, cooling, and indoor air quality solutions. Renowned for their technical proficiency and community-oriented approach, they remain a key supporter of local initiatives across the region.

About the Central Piedmont Junior Livestock Show & Sale The Central Piedmont Junior Livestock Show & Sale is an annual event focused on fostering youth development through livestock exhibitions. It serves as a venue for education, competition, and community involvement.
