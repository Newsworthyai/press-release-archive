# White-Label SEO Platform From Search Atlas Hides Vendor From Clients

Search Atlas White-Label SEO Platform Lets 2,000+ Agencies Deliver Services Under Their Own Brand

 New York City, United States - May 7, 2026 / Search Atlas /

 Search Atlas has released a full-suite SEO platform built exclusively for agencies and brands, introducing OTTO AI -- an autonomous SEO engine that handles technical fixes, content optimization, and authority building without manual intervention. The release represents a deliberate effort by Search Atlas to replace the fragmented tool stacks most agencies depend on with a single, purpose-built system designed to operate at agency scale.

OTTO AI: Autonomous SEO at the Core

At the center of the platform sits OTTO AI SEO, an automation engine that operates without waiting for instructions. It identifies technical SEO issues, deploys fixes, generates optimized content, and builds site authority -- all without requiring an analyst to manage each task individually. This is not a recommendation engine. OTTO AI executes.

The platform addresses a specific operational challenge: agencies managing dozens or hundreds of client accounts cannot afford to treat each site as a manual project. OTTO AI SEO removes that bottleneck by executing across accounts simultaneously, allowing agency teams to concentrate on strategy and client relationships rather than repetitive task management.

"OTTO AI has processed technical and content optimizations across more than 2,000 agency accounts worldwide, and the platform now consolidates what used to require five or six separate tools into a single dashboard," said [Executive Name], [Title] of Search Atlas. "We built this for agencies that need to scale without adding headcount."

White-Label Infrastructure Built for Client Delivery

The white-label SEO platform component allows agencies to deliver the full Search Atlas toolset under their own brand identity. Clients interact with the agency's interface, logo, and reporting -- while Search Atlas functions as the underlying engine.

For agency founders who have established client trust around their own brand, this structure matters. The white-label SEO platform does not require agencies to disclose the underlying software vendor, which preserves positioning and pricing flexibility. More than 2,000 agencies currently rely on this infrastructure to deliver SEO services at scale.

The white-label layer extends across the entire platform -- reports, dashboards, and client-facing deliverables all reflect the agency's branding.

One Platform, Every SEO Function

Search Atlas consolidates what the SEO software for agencies market has historically distributed across multiple vendors. The platform includes:

- Real-time site auditing that surfaces technical issues as they appear, rather than on a weekly crawl schedule

- Keyword research tools with competitive gap analysis built in

- Backlink analysis covering link profile health, toxic link identification, and prospecting

- Rank tracking across locations, devices, and search engines

- SEO Content Assistant that uses AI to produce and optimize content aligned with specific keyword targets

Each module connects to the others. Audit findings inform content priorities. Keyword data shapes backlink strategy. Rank movement triggers automated responses through OTTO AI. The platform functions as a closed loop rather than a collection of standalone features.

Purpose-Built, Not Repurposed

The distinction Search Atlas draws is architectural. Most SEO software for agencies originated as tools for individual marketers or in-house teams and incorporated agency features as secondary additions -- multi-seat access, client folders, basic reporting. Search Atlas constructed the platform from the ground up with agency workflows as the foundational assumption.

That approach translates into bulk operations across client accounts, scalable reporting, and automation logic designed for managing dozens of sites with varying goals, industries, and competitive landscapes. The platform accounts for the operational difference between an agency managing 50 clients and a solo consultant handling a handful of projects.

Operators such as hoyts.com.au represent the type of brand-scale accounts the platform also serves, where SEO execution must match the pace of a high-traffic, multi-category digital property. Search Atlas positions its toolset as capable of handling enterprise brand accounts alongside agency client rosters.
