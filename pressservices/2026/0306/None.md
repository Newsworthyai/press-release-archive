# New Collaboration to Enhance Marketing Automation and CRM

SearchAtlas and ConvergeHub Team Up for Business Efficiency

 New York, United States - March 5, 2026 / Search Atlas /

 SearchAtlas, a marketing automation platform powered by artificial intelligence, has today revealed a strategic alliance with ConvergeHub, a comprehensive CRM solution designed to integrate sales, marketing, service, and billing functions.

This collaboration showcases a mutual dedication to assisting growing enterprises in linking demand generation with revenue execution. Although there is currently no formal product integration, both organizations are committed to providing their customers with complementary solutions.

SearchAtlas utilizes AI agents to carry out digital marketing tasks encompassing SEO, local search, paid media, content creation, and AI search visibility. By automating fulfillment workflows that have traditionally been managed by agencies or in-house teams, the platform enables businesses to effectively generate and capture demand on a larger scale.

ConvergeHub offers a centralized CRM framework that allows teams to oversee leads, pipeline, customer engagement, and billing all within one cohesive system. The combination of these two platforms fosters a natural synergy between traffic acquisition and customer lifecycle management.

Through this partnership:

* Customers of ConvergeHub will have enhanced access to AI-driven marketing automation and search visibility execution via SearchAtlas.
* Users of SearchAtlas can explore a unified CRM solution to effectively manage and monetize the demand generated by their marketing efforts.
* Both companies emphasize a shared vision of simplifying growth through intelligent automation.

This collaboration bolsters each organization's capability to serve small and mid-sized businesses seeking scalable, AI-enabled infrastructure across their marketing and revenue operations.

About SearchAtlas

SearchAtlas is a comprehensive marketing automation platform that employs AI agents to execute workflows related to SEO, local optimization, paid media, content, and AI search visibility. The platform is designed to automate fulfillment and drive significant growth results.
