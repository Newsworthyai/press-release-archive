# The Elm Group Brokered by REAL Ranks #5 Nationally for Large Team Units in Q4

Elm Group Brokered by REAL receives 3 awards

 Fargo, United States - March 9, 2026 / The Elm Group brokered by REAL /

 FARGO, ND – The Elm Group brokered by REAL is proud to announce its recognition as one of the top-performing real estate entities in the United States. According to the recently released Q4 REAL Trends report, The Elm Group has achieved an elite national standing, ranking #5 in the country for Units sold among large teams.

In addition to its top-five finish in transaction volume, the team secured the #14 spot for Gross Commission Income (GCI) and #18 for Total Sales Volume nationally. These rankings solidify The Elm Group as a powerhouse in the Upper Midwest, specifically serving the Fargo-Moorhead Metro and Grand Forks, ND areas.

"Our success is a direct reflection of our unwavering commitment to the client experience," said Tyler Lindell, CEO of The Elm Group. "In a shifting market, elite negotiating skills and deep local expertise are what move the needle. Ranking #5 in the nation for units proves that our process works for the families and investors we represent every day."

National Rankings at a Glance:

* #5 National Ranking: Total Units Sold (Large Teams)
* #14 National Ranking: Gross Commission Income (GCI)
* #18 National Ranking: Total Sales Volume

The Elm Group’s dual-office presence in Fargo and Grand Forks allows them to provide hyper-local market insights backed by the national resources of REAL. This "high-touch, high-tech" approach has made them the go-to choice for buyers and sellers seeking a competitive edge in the North Dakota and Minnesota markets.

For more information on The Elm Group’s award-winning services or to browse current listings, please visit www.realelmgroup.com or contact the team directly at 701446 7249.

About The Elm Group brokered by REAL The Elm Group is a premier real estate team specializing in residential and investment properties across the Fargo and Grand Forks metropolitan areas. Led by CEO Tyler Lindell, the team is dedicated to setting new standards for professional excellence and negotiation in the real estate industry.
