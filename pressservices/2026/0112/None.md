# New AI Automation Platform Launches to Boost Small Business Growth

Empowering Local Businesses with AI Automation Solutions

 Butte, United States - January 11, 2026 / Americas Best Shops /

 Americas Best Shops, a pioneering Montana AI company, has officially launched an innovative platform aimed at empowering small businesses, particularly auto repair shops, to harness the power of AI automation. Founded by Brad Killgore, the company is dedicated to providing tailored business growth solutions that enable local enterprises to thrive in an increasingly competitive market.

In today's fast-paced business environment, small businesses often face challenges that can hinder their growth and sustainability. Recognizing this, Americas Best Shops has developed a comprehensive suite of AI automation tools designed specifically for the unique needs of local businesses. The platform focuses on practical implementation, ensuring that even those with limited technical expertise can effectively utilize AI to enhance their operations.

Brad Killgore, the visionary behind Americas Best Shops, understands the struggles faced by small business owners. With years of experience in the automotive industry, he has witnessed firsthand the impact that effective marketing and operational efficiency can have on a business's success. His goal is to empower local auto repair shops in Butte, Montana, and beyond by providing them with the resources they need to compete with larger corporations.

One of the standout features of the Americas Best Shops platform is its FREE AI Readiness Assessment. This assessment allows business owners to evaluate their current operations and identify areas where AI automation can make a significant impact. By understanding their readiness for AI implementation, businesses can make informed decisions about how to integrate these advanced technologies into their workflows.

The platform offers a range of AI-driven marketing solutions that help businesses improve their online presence and attract more customers. With a focus on search engine optimization (SEO) and targeted marketing strategies, Americas Best Shops equips local businesses with the tools they need to enhance their visibility in a crowded marketplace. This is particularly crucial for auto repair shops, where local search results can significantly influence customer decisions.

In addition to marketing solutions, Americas Best Shops provides operational automation tools that streamline everyday tasks. By automating routine processes, businesses can save time and reduce the risk of human error. This not only improves efficiency but also allows business owners to focus on what they do best-serving their customers and growing their enterprises.

The launch of Americas Best Shops comes at a time when many small businesses are seeking innovative ways to adapt to changing market conditions. The COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the need for digital transformation, and AI automation is at the forefront of this shift. By leveraging AI, small businesses can not only survive but thrive in a post-pandemic world.

Killgore's vision extends beyond just providing tools; he aims to create a supportive community for local business owners. Through workshops, webinars, and one-on-one consultations, Americas Best Shops will offer ongoing education and support to help businesses navigate the complexities of AI technology. This commitment to empowerment is at the heart of the company's mission.

As a Montana AI company, Americas Best Shops is proud to contribute to the local economy by helping small businesses succeed. The company believes that when local businesses thrive, the entire community benefits. By investing in the growth of auto repair shops and other small enterprises, Americas Best Shops is playing a vital role in fostering economic resilience in Butte and surrounding areas.

The platform's user-friendly interface ensures that even those who may not be tech-savvy can easily access and utilize its features. This accessibility is crucial for small business owners who may feel overwhelmed by the prospect of adopting new technologies. Americas Best Shops is committed to demystifying AI automation and making it an attainable goal for all local businesses.

In addition to its core offerings, Americas Best Shops is continuously exploring new ways to enhance its platform. The company is dedicated to staying at the forefront of AI technology and regularly updates its tools to reflect the latest advancements. This commitment to innovation ensures that clients receive the most effective and relevant solutions for their needs.

As the launch of Americas Best Shops unfolds, the company is excited to engage with local business owners and demonstrate the transformative potential of AI automation. By providing practical, actionable strategies, Americas Best Shops aims to inspire confidence in small business owners, encouraging them to embrace the future of technology.

In conclusion, Americas Best Shops is more than just an AI automation platform; it is a partner in the journey of local businesses toward growth and success. With Brad Killgore's vision and dedication, the company is poised to make a significant impact on the small business landscape in Montana. By offering tailored business growth solutions, a FREE AI Readiness Assessment, and ongoing support, Americas Best Shops is empowering local auto repair shops and other small enterprises to thrive in an ever-evolving marketplace. The future of small business in Butte and beyond looks brighter with the innovative solutions provided by Americas Best Shops.
