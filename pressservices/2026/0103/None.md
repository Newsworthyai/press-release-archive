# Capture Your Leadership with Executive Portraits in NYC

Editorial Executive Portraits in NYC | Match Production

 New York, United States - January 2, 2026 / Match Production /

 

NEW YORK, NY — Match Production, a New York City–based portrait and production studio, today announced its expanded focus on editorial-style executive portraiture and custom corporate photography built specifically for leadership teams and ambitious startups. Match designs shoots the way a newsroom or magazine set would—concept first, a calm on-camera flow, and deliverables that work everywhere from investor decks and press releases to team pages and internal systems.

Unlike high-volume “assembly line” headshot studios, Match operates with a production mindset: structured scheduling, repeatable lighting, and a crewed workflow (photographer + support team) that keeps senior leaders comfortable while protecting quality and consistency. Sessions run in the Times Square studio and on-location across Manhattan and beyond, with remote options for distributed teams.

Built for high-stakes timelines (without the chaos)For communications, people ops, and founders, the problem is rarely “getting a headshot.” It’s getting the right images—fast—while matching brand standards, navigating tight calendars, and keeping a leadership team looking cohesive over time.

Match’s approach is designed for exactly that: proofs typically land same day or within one business day, and final retouched selects can be turned quickly depending on scope—so press moments don’t slip while you’re waiting on imagery.

Recent work that reflects the Match differencePress-day startup coverage, end-to-end. For a venture-backed AI startup preparing for a funding announcement, Match delivered a press-kit-ready library spanning team headshots, leadership portraits with editorial polish, and authentic workplace lifestyle imagery—plus a signature founders image captured on-location in lower Manhattan for unmistakable NYC context.

Executive portraits under a deadline. When a Manhattan fintech firm needed leadership headshots for a global press announcement on a 48-hour clock, Match converted an on-site conference room into a mobile studio and delivered final retouched portraits within 36 hours—supporting press and investor materials without interrupting the executives’ schedules.

Brand-accurate leadership consistency at scale. For a Fortune 500 financial services client, Match produced 47 executive portraits aligned to a precise brand background (Pantone 282 C), maintaining matching color temperature and backdrop positioning across multiple days—built for press materials, investor presentations, and LinkedIn updates.

Editorial roots, corporate reliabilityMatch Production is led by photographers with deep editorial experience—work published across major titles including Forbes, Esquire, Inc., GQ, and others—paired with a corporate delivery system that keeps projects predictable for internal stakeholders.

“Executives don’t need more time on their calendar—they need a process that works,” said Lisa, Head of Production at Match Production. “We build a calm, guided set, keep the day moving, and deliver portraits that look premium and deploy cleanly across every channel a company touches.”

Services and availabilityMatch Production offers executive portraits, leadership headshots, corporate team sessions, and custom image libraries for internal communications and external visibility—available in-studio near Times Square and on-site across NYC. The studio is located at 545 8th Ave, New York, NY 10018, with hours listed as every day, 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM.

About Match ProductionMatch Production is a New York City–based portrait and production studio serving executives, leadership teams, and high-growth companies with editorial-quality portraits and corporate photography. Match’s team has delivered work for brands including Google, VanEck, Bilt Rewards, Visa US, Nova Credit, Blue Owl Capital, Ramp, and more.
