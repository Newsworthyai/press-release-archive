# PA IT Provider Joins Greater West Chester Chamber Quest for the Chamber Cup

Trading Server Racks for Golf Carts: Connecting on the Green with the Greater West Chester Chamber

 Kennett Square, United States - June 18, 2026 / Pegasus Technologies /

 Pegasus Technologies Participates in Greater West Chester Chamber Annual Quest for the Chamber Cup![](https://storage.googleapis.com/signal-genesys-press-releases-prod/images/DBZuwm8lLPbPFgdjocXRgRoHNR0CqpsF9aTnJTla.png)

WEST CHESTER, Pa., June 15, 2026 — Pegasus Technologies, a regional Managed Service Provider (MSP) offering business computer support and network management, recently hit the green at the Greater West Chester Chamber of Commerce annual golf tournament. The event, known as the "Quest for the Chamber Cup," was held at the White Manor Country Club in Malvern, Pennsylvania, serving as a hub for local business networking and community engagement.

The annual outing gathers business executives, operational directors, and community leaders from across Chester County. Representing the firm on the course this year were team members Frank Heckman, Mindy Gallagher, Matthew Tucker, and Erik Gudmundson.

For Pegasus Technologies, participating in the tournament highlights the organization’s ongoing commitment to stepping out from behind technical infrastructure to actively support the regional business ecosystem.

"Participating in the annual golf outing isn't about setting course records; it's about connecting with and celebrating our vibrant business neighbors," said Matthew Tucker, CEO of Pegasus Technologies. "Our engineering team spends the vast majority of their time monitoring corporate data networks and hardening cloud security boundaries. Stepping onto the green with the Greater West Chester Chamber allows us to build stronger local relationships face-to-face, which remains a core element of how we deliver localized support."

The regional technology firm maintains deep roots in the area through active participation in regional business associations, community foundations, and economic development councils. By maintaining dedicated offices in Wayne, Bethlehem, Media, and Kennett Square, the company focuses on pairing its advanced technical capabilities with highly accessible, localized client care.

A summary of the event highlights and team commentary can be found on the official company post: Chasing the Chamber Cup: Pegasus Technologies Hits the Green with the Greater West Chester Chamber.

About Pegasus TechnologiesPegasus Technologies is a Managed Service Provider (MSP) delivering proactive IT support, data backup and disaster recovery solutions, and strategic cybersecurity consulting for small and mid-sized businesses. Operating physical offices throughout Southeastern Pennsylvania, the company helps organizations minimize operational downtime, safeguard digital assets, and transition to secure hybrid working environments.
