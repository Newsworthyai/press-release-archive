# Austin Clinic Offers Stem Cell Exosome and Micro FUE Treatments

Springs Rejuvenation Brings Stem 3 Protocol and Micro FUE Hair Restoration to Austin, TX

 Aventura, United States - July 14, 2026 / Springs Rejuvenation /

 Springs Rejuvenation has officially expanded its regenerative medicine services to Austin, Texas, bringing a full suite of non-surgical treatments - including the proprietary Stem 3 Protocol, stem cell exosome therapy, micro FUE hair restoration, and joint pain relief - to central Texas patients who previously had to travel out of state or overseas to access comparable care.

The Austin location marks the fifth clinic in the Springs Rejuvenation network, which also operates in New York City, Los Angeles, Miami, and Atlanta. The opening positions Austin as a direct access point for patients across central Texas seeking stem cell exosome therapy Austin providers have not historically offered at this level of clinical depth.

The Stem 3 Protocol and Exosome-Based Treatments

At the center of the Austin clinic's offerings is the Stem 3 Protocol, a structured regenerative approach that combines stem cell and exosome-based therapies into a coordinated treatment plan. Exosomes are extracellular vesicles that carry proteins, lipids, and genetic material between cells and have been studied for their role in tissue signaling and repair. The protocol is designed to be administered without surgery, reducing recovery time and procedural risk for patients.

Stem cell exosome therapy Austin patients can now access this protocol locally, removing the logistical burden of traveling to major coastal cities for treatment. The clinic serves patients with a range of concerns, from skin and cellular rejuvenation to more targeted musculoskeletal applications.

Minimally Invasive Hair Restoration With Micro FUE

Springs Rejuvenation's Austin clinic also offers micro FUE hair transplantation, a follicular unit extraction method that uses small-gauge instruments to harvest and implant individual hair follicles with minimal disruption to surrounding tissue. The technique leaves no linear scar and is associated with shorter recovery periods compared to traditional strip-harvest methods.

Minimally invasive hair restoration Austin TX patients are finding this approach increasingly relevant as demand grows for outpatient hair procedures that do not require extended downtime. The advanced FUE Austin service at Springs Rejuvenation integrates exosome application as part of the post-procedure protocol, supporting the scalp environment during the recovery phase.

The clinic's hair restoration team works with patients across varying stages of hair loss, tailoring the follicle harvesting and placement plan to each individual's scalp density and restoration goals.

Joint Pain Treatments for Knees, Hips, Shoulders, and Back

For patients dealing with chronic joint discomfort, Springs Rejuvenation's Austin clinic offers joint pain stem cell Austin protocols targeting the knees, hips, shoulders, and back. These treatments use stem cell and exosome preparations delivered directly to the affected joint or surrounding tissue, with the aim of supporting the body's natural repair processes without the need for surgical intervention.

Orthopedic surgery carries meaningful procedural risk and extended rehabilitation timelines. The stem cell joint therapy approach at the Austin clinic is structured as an outpatient treatment, meaning patients are typically able to return to normal activities within a shorter window than surgical recovery would allow.

Joint pain stem cell Austin patients presenting with degenerative joint conditions, sports-related wear, or age-associated stiffness are evaluated individually to determine whether regenerative protocols are clinically appropriate for their situation.
