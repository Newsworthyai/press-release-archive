# White House Ballroom Project Selects Baco Enterprises as Fastener Supplier

White House Ballroom Renovation Taps Baco Enterprises for American-Made Construction Fasteners

 Bronx, United States - July 11, 2026 / Baco Enterprises Inc. /

 Baco Enterprises, a family-owned U.S. manufacturer and distributor of construction fasteners, has been named a supplier for the new White House Ballroom project. The company is providing American-made fasteners for the project, supported by full Buy America compliance documentation and same-day delivery capabilities.

A Recognized Role in a High Profile RenovationThe White House Ballroom project represents a significant undertaking in terms of both scale and public visibility. Baco Enterprises was selected to supply construction fasteners for the work, with the company's ability to furnish complete compliance documentation cited as a key factor in its participation. Buy America requirements apply to federally connected projects and impose rigorous material sourcing standards - standards that Baco Enterprises meets through its domestic manufacturing and distribution operations.

The company's same-day delivery capabilities also address the logistical demands associated with high-priority construction timelines. Projects of this nature require suppliers that can respond quickly without compromising on documentation or product integrity, and Baco Enterprises maintains that operational model across its engagements.

Infrastructure Expertise Behind the AppointmentBaco Enterprises has established its standing within the heavy construction and infrastructure sectors, where fastener specifications, load ratings, and material compliance carry direct consequences for structural performance. The company manufactures and distributes a range of fasteners used in large-scale civil and commercial construction, including applications tied to bridges, transit infrastructure, and public facilities.

That depth of experience positions Baco Enterprises as a supplier that understands not only the physical requirements of a fastener, but also the documentation chain, traceability standards, and compliance frameworks that federal and institutional projects demand. The White House Ballroom appointment reflects that track record applied to one of the country's most recognized addresses.

"As a family-owned manufacturer, we've spent decades building the compliance documentation and delivery capabilities that federal projects demand," said David Cohen, Vice President, Baco Enterprises. "Being trusted to supply American-made fasteners for a project of this visibility reflects the standard we hold ourselves to on every job."

American Manufacturing and Supply Chain ReliabilityAs both manufacturer and distributor, Baco Enterprises maintains direct oversight of product quality at every stage of the supply chain - an advantage for contractors on federally connected projects who require a single domestic source for compliance documentation and fast turnaround. The White House Ballroom engagement extends that model into institutional restoration, a sector where material traceability is non-negotiable.

About Baco EnterprisesBACO Enterprises Inc. is a fastener distributor located in the Bronx, NY. The company distributes A325 and A490 structural bolts serving the Northeast, maintaining inventory across a broad range of sizes with the capability to manufacture special sizes and lengths with fast delivery. Next-day delivery coverage includes New York City, Long Island, Lower NY State, all of New Jersey, Eastern Pennsylvania, most of Connecticut, and parts of Massachusetts.
