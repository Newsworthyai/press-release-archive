# Cinderix Builds End-to-End Revenue Infrastructure for Local Trades and SMBs

How Cinderix Replaces Marketing Guesswork with a Systematic Revenue Infrastructure for Small Businesses

 Aylmer, Canada - June 10, 2026 / Cinderix /

 Cinderix, the fractional growth department built specifically for local trades and small-to-medium businesses, has formally launched its Revenue Infrastructure framework - a done-for-you growth model that replaces traditional marketing guesswork with a structured, data-backed system for generating consistent, qualified leads each month.

Founded by Frank Wiebe, Cinderix enters a market where many local trades businesses and SMBs rely on inconsistent referrals, ad spend with unclear returns, or disconnected marketing tools that never function as a unified system. The Revenue Infrastructure model is designed to address that gap directly by combining AI-driven SEO, automated sales funnels, and speed-to-lead engagement into a single, managed growth operation.

An Engineering Approach to Revenue Generation

The central premise behind Cinderix is that revenue growth should function less like a creative exercise and more like an engineered process. Wiebe developed the framework with the view that marketing outcomes become predictable when the underlying systems are built correctly - not when more budget is spent or more channels are tested.

Rather than advising clients on strategy and leaving implementation to them, Cinderix operates as an embedded fractional growth department. That means the company builds, manages, and continuously optimizes the full revenue stack on behalf of each client - from initial search visibility through to lead capture and follow-up engagement.

"Most small businesses are not losing because of bad products or weak demand - they are losing because their growth systems are broken or missing entirely," said Frank Wiebe, Founder of Cinderix. "What we build is the infrastructure that turns interest into revenue in a way that is repeatable, measurable, and not dependent on guesswork."

What the Revenue Infrastructure Model Covers

The Cinderix framework operates across three interconnected layers. The first is AI-assisted SEO, which positions client businesses in front of search traffic that reflects actual buying intent rather than general interest. The second is automated sales funnel architecture - structured sequences that move a prospect from first contact through to a qualified conversation without requiring manual follow-up at every stage.

The third layer is speed-to-lead engagement, which Wiebe describes as one of the most overlooked variables in local business growth. Research consistently shows that the probability of converting an inbound lead drops significantly within the first few minutes of inquiry. Cinderix builds response systems that act on new leads immediately, reducing drop-off and increasing the percentage of inquiries that become real sales conversations.

Together, these layers form what Cinderix describes as a complete revenue infrastructure - not a collection of separate tools or campaigns, but a functioning system designed to produce qualified leads on a consistent monthly basis.

Built for Local Trades and Small-to-Medium Businesses

The Cinderix model is specifically positioned for local trades operators and SMBs - businesses that typically lack an in-house marketing team but still need a reliable pipeline of new clients to sustain and grow revenue. The done-for-you structure is central to that positioning. Clients are not expected to manage campaigns, interpret analytics dashboards, or coordinate between multiple vendors. Cinderix handles the operational side in full.

Wiebe designed the company around the recognition that this segment of the market is frequently underserved by traditional agencies, which often apply broad strategies that do not account for the specific sales cycles, service areas, and customer behavior patterns of trades and local service businesses.

The fractional model also means clients access a dedicated growth function without the overhead of hiring a full-time team - a practical consideration for businesses operating with lean cost structures.

About Cinderix

Cinderix is a done-for-you growth company serving local trades businesses and small-to-medium businesses. Founded by Frank Wiebe, Cinderix builds and manages end-to-end Revenue Infrastructure systems that combine AI SEO, automated sales funnels, and speed-to-lead engagement to deliver consistent, qualified leads each month. The company operates as a fractional growth department, handling the full growth function on behalf of each client without requiring them to manage marketing operations internally.
