# East Texas Pest Control Partnership Covers Nacogdoches and Lufkin

New Pest Control Partnership Expands Service Reach Across Nacogdoches and Lufkin

 Lufkin, United States - April 24, 2026 / Spot On Pest Control, LLC /

 Spot On Pest Control has announced a new partnership with Carra's Pest & Termite, bringing together two woman-owned pest control companies with a shared dedication to protecting homes and businesses across East Texas.

Carra's Pest & Termite has been a recognized name in Nacogdoches and surrounding communities for more than 20 years, building a reputation for dependable service and strong local roots. Through this partnership, Spot On Pest Control and Carra's Pest & Termite will combine decades of field experience with modern pest control methods and a continued focus on customer-centered service.

The two companies aim to strengthen the standard for pest control across Nacogdoches, Lufkin, and the broader East Texas region. Customers can expect thorough solutions covering termite treatments, rodent control, and general pest management, delivered with accuracy, integrity, and individualized attention.

"This partnership represents a powerful alignment of values and vision," said a spokesperson for Spot On Pest Control. "By joining forces, we're able to offer more resources, faster response times, and an even higher level of service to the communities we care about."

As woman-owned businesses, both companies draw on a commitment to service and a close connection to the communities they operate in. The collaboration reflects a shared mission to deliver reliable, effective pest control while building lasting relationships with local customers.

With expanded resources and a unified operational approach, Spot On Pest Control and Carra's Pest & Termite are positioned for continued development within the East Texas market.

About Spot On Pest Control

Spot On Pest Control is a locally trusted provider of residential and commercial pest management services throughout East Texas, delivering fast, reliable solutions designed to protect both property and peace of mind. Headquartered in Lufkin and serving surrounding communities like Nacogdoches, the company specializes in comprehensive pest control - from termites and rodents to ants, bed bugs, mosquitoes, and more - using customized treatment plans tailored to each home or business.

About Carra's Pest & Termite

Carra's Pest & Termite has proudly served Nacogdoches and surrounding areas for over two decades, offering trusted pest control services rooted in experience, reliability, and community commitment.
