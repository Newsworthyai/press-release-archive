# Certified XPEL Installer Reaches 5,000 GTA Clients

Window Film Installer Hits 5,000 GTA Clients with Security, Solar, and Privacy Solutions

 Mississauga, Canada - June 7, 2026 / Film Bros /

 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

MISSISSAUGA, ON - Film Bros, a certified XPEL window film installer headquartered at 2251 Burnhamthorpe Rd W, Mississauga, is broadening its professional window and surface protection film services to address increasing demand throughout the Greater Toronto Area. The company delivers residential and commercial clients high-performance solutions designed to reduce solar heat gain, block harmful UV radiation, reinforce glass security, and improve privacy - all while preserving natural light and the existing appearance of windows.

Window film has become one of the most practical and cost-effective upgrades available to property owners across the GTA. As energy costs climb and concerns around home security and UV damage increase, more residents across Mississauga, Toronto, Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Vaughan, Markham, Brampton, and Kitchener are turning to professional film installation as a lasting solution.

"Our goal has always been to give homeowners and businesses a protective layer they can rely on every day," said a spokesperson for Film Bros. "Window film works quietly in the background - blocking heat, protecting interiors from fading, and reinforcing glass - without changing the look and feel of the space. That's why demand has continued to grow across the GTA."

Film Bros currently serves residential clients and commercial organizations throughout Southern Ontario, with a service area covering Mississauga, Toronto, Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Vaughan, Markham, Brampton, Richmond Hill, Kitchener, Waterloo, Ancaster, and surrounding communities.

Core services offered by Film Bros include:

Security Window Film: Film Bros installs XPEL Vision security film, a multi-layer reinforced product that holds shattered glass together following impact, break-in attempts, storms, or accidents. The film is a widely chosen option for ground-floor residential windows, retail storefronts, office buildings, and schools across the GTA.

Solar Control and UV Protection Film: Film Bros' solar control and UV protection films block up to 99% of harmful ultraviolet radiation while significantly reducing solar heat gain. These films protect furniture, hardwood floors, artwork, and interior decor from sun damage, while helping to lower cooling costs during Ontario's warmer months.

Privacy and Decorative Film: From one-way daytime privacy films to frosted matte finishes, Film Bros offers a full range of privacy and decorative window films for both residential and commercial properties. These solutions provide visibility control and design flexibility without sacrificing natural light - making them well-suited for bathrooms, sidelights, office partitions, boardrooms, and retail spaces.

Surface Protection Film: Beyond windows, Film Bros installs countertop and surface protection film for high-value interior surfaces, guarding against scratches, stains, and everyday wear in kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial environments.

Film Bros is a certified XPEL installer and a member of the International Window Film Association (IWFA). Every installation is supported by a written warranty that includes both the XPEL manufacturer's product warranty and Film Bros' own workmanship guarantee. The company has served more than 5,000 clients across the GTA and has completed installations for major organizations including IKEA, Tim Hortons, and KUKA.

Consultations are available at no charge and with no obligation. Film Bros provides a written quote with full pricing, film specifications, and installation timeline before any work begins. Residential projects are typically completed within one to two days, while commercial installations are scheduled according to project scope.

For homeowners and businesses who prefer to manage installation independently, Film Bros also operates an online DIY store at filmbros.ca, offering professional-grade window film rolls with pre-cut options available for delivery across Ontario.
