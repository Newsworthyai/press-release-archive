# Triad Residents Gain Non-Denominational Church in Winston-Salem

New Winston-Salem Church Grand Opening Expands Options for Walkertown and Kernersville Families

 Winston-Salem, United States - July 12, 2026 / Passion Church /

 Passion Church, a non-denominational Christian church grounded in the mission of reaching people far from God, has opened a new permanent location in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, marking a notable expansion for the congregation as it broadens its presence across the Triad region. The new church location positions Passion Church to serve a wider community of residents across Winston-Salem, Walkertown, Kernersville, and neighboring areas who are looking for a welcoming, Bible-believing church for Sunday worship and family-centered faith life.

A New Home for the Triad CommunityThe church grand opening represents more than a physical address change. For a congregation built around accessibility and genuine community, establishing a new permanent church building reflects a commitment to long-term presence in the region. Passion Church Winston-Salem now offers residents throughout the Triad - including those searching for churches in Walkertown and churches in Kernersville - a consistent, stable home for weekly gatherings, discipleship, and neighborhood outreach.

Passion Church operates as a family church with a culture described as welcoming, authentic, and compassionate. Its theological foundation is rooted in Scripture, making it a destination for those seeking a Bible-believing environment where both longtime Christians and those new to faith can find grounding. The congregation's stated mission - to reach people far from God - shapes everything from its Sunday services to the way it engages with surrounding neighborhoods.

Serving Winston-Salem, Walkertown, and KernersvilleThe new church location places Passion Church in close proximity to several growing communities in the greater Winston-Salem area. Residents of Walkertown and Kernersville, who have historically had fewer nearby options for Sunday worship, now have access to a Triad church that prioritizes both doctrinal clarity and an open-door atmosphere.

Passion Church Walkertown and Passion Church Kernersville area residents are among those the new site is specifically designed to serve, reducing travel barriers and making it easier for families to engage consistently with a local congregation. For anyone actively searching for a church near me in the Triad, the Winston-Salem location offers both proximity and a clearly defined mission.

What to Expect at Passion ChurchPassion Church is structured as a welcoming church that does not require newcomers to have an existing faith background or prior church experience. Its approach is deliberately accessible - services are designed to engage families, individuals at various stages of spiritual life, and those exploring Christianity for the first time.

As a Christian church in the non-denominational tradition, Passion Church NC does not operate under a denominational hierarchy, allowing the congregation to direct its resources and culture toward its core mission rather than institutional structures. Sunday worship gatherings serve as the central touchpoint for the community, supported by additional programming for families and individuals throughout the week.

The church opening comes at a time when Triad-area residents have increasingly sought community-oriented, faith-based spaces that balance theological substance with an approachable culture. Passion Church positions itself to meet that need by combining clear biblical teaching with a congregation-wide culture of hospitality and genuine engagement.

About Passion ChurchPassion Church is a non-denominational church located in Winston-Salem, NC, serving residents across the Triad including Walkertown, Kernersville, and surrounding communities. Guided by its mission to reach people far from God, Passion Church offers Bible-based Sunday worship in a family-friendly, welcoming environment open to all.
