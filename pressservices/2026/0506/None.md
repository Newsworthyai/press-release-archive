# Systems Integrations is Proud to Announce its Certification as DOBE

Systems Integrations Achieves The Disability-Owned Business Enterprise (DOBE) Certification via Disability:IN!

 Mullica Hill, United States - May 5, 2026 / Systems Integrations /

 

Mullica Hill, NJ, 04/21/2026 – Systems Integrations is pleased to announce it is now a certified disability-owned business enterprise (DOBE), as part of Disability:IN’s Supplier Inclusion program.

"We are thrilled to officially be recognized as a certified DOBE," said David Schulze, President of Systems Integrations. "Our clients already know the dedication, expertise, and innovative thinking we bring to every project. This certification provides yet another avenue for us to seamlessly align with their supplier diversity goals while continuing to deliver top-tier results."

The Disability:IN Supplier Inclusion program certifies disability-owned business enterprises, including service-disabled and veteran disability-owned businesses, and links them to organizations seeking to gain a competitive edge in their supply chains. A disability-owned business enterprise is a for-profit business that is at least 51% owned, managed and controlled by a person with a disability. On average, DOBEs employ people with disabilities at 6-7 times the rate of their non-DOBE peers, and DOBEs are recognized nationally by The Billion Dollar Roundtable.

About Systems Integrations Headquartered in Mullica Hill, NJ, Systems Integrations is a premier security integration firm specializing in commercial-grade, cloud-based physical security solutions. Operating with a strictly "Cybercentric" approach, we design, deploy, and manage advanced video surveillance and access control platforms for high-security environments. We believe that physical security and cybersecurity are inextricably linked, ensuring every physical electronic system we install is rigorously hardened as a network endpoint.

About Disability:IN Disability:IN is the leading nonprofit resource for business disability inclusion worldwide. Disability:IN partners with leading companies and drives progress through initiatives, tools, and expertise that deliver long-term business impact. Join us athttps://disabilityin.org/. #AreYouIN
