# Your Hotel's Online Presence Without High Commissions

Why Hotels Should Move Beyond OTAs for Online Visibility

 Wellingborough, United Kingdom - December 1, 2024 / Bee Social Digital Marketing /

 The hospitality industry is a competitive market, and hotels are increasingly recognising the importance of establishing a strong online presence. Many hotels have traditionally relied on Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) to fill their rooms, but this dependence can come at a significant cost. High commission fees charged by OTAs can eat into a hotel’s profits, making it essential for hotel owners to explore alternative strategies for online visibility. Bee Social Digital Marketing, a leader in hospitality digital marketing, is dedicated to helping hotels thrive in the digital space through effective hotel SEO and targeted paid advertising strategies.

The reliance on OTAs has become a common practice among hotels, especially smaller establishments that may lack the resources to invest in their own marketing efforts. While OTAs can provide a steady stream of bookings, the commissions they charge can range from 15% to 20% or even higher. This can significantly impact a hotel’s bottom line, leaving little room for profit. As the hospitality industry continues to evolve, it is crucial for hotels to seek out more sustainable and cost-effective methods for attracting guests.

One of the most effective ways for hotels to enhance their online visibility is through hotel SEO. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) involves optimising a hotel’s website and online content to improve its ranking on search engine results pages. By implementing a robust hotel SEO strategy, hotels can increase their chances of being discovered by potential guests who are searching for accommodations in their area. This not only helps to drive direct bookings but also reduces reliance on OTAs, ultimately leading to higher profit margins.

In addition to hotel SEO, paid ads for hotels can also play a crucial role in boosting online visibility. Paid advertising allows hotels to reach a targeted audience quickly and effectively. With platforms like Google Ads and social media advertising, hotels can create tailored campaigns that showcase their unique offerings and promotions. This targeted approach not only increases brand awareness but also drives traffic to the hotel’s website, leading to more direct bookings.

The combination of hotel SEO and paid ads for hotels creates a powerful marketing strategy that can significantly enhance a hotel’s online presence. By investing in these digital marketing efforts, hotels can reduce their dependence on OTAs and retain more of their revenue. Bee Social Digital Marketing works closely with hotels to develop customised marketing plans that align with their specific goals and budget, ensuring that each hotel can maximise its return on investment.

Moreover, the importance of having a user-friendly website cannot be overstated. A well-designed website that is easy to navigate can greatly enhance the user experience, encouraging potential guests to book directly. Bee Social Digital Marketing emphasises the need for hotels to have a responsive website that is optimised for both desktop and mobile users. With more travellers using their smartphones to search for accommodations, having a mobile-friendly website is essential for capturing this audience.

In addition to website optimisation, content marketing is another vital component of a successful hotel SEO strategy. By creating engaging and informative content, hotels can establish themselves as authorities in their local area. This can include blog posts about local attractions, travel tips, and unique experiences that guests can enjoy during their stay. By providing valuable information, hotels can attract more visitors to their website and encourage them to book directly.
