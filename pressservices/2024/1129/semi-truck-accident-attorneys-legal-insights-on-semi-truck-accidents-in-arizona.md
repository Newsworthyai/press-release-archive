Liability in Semi-Truck AccidentsDetermining liability in semi-truck accidents can be particularly complicated due to the involvement of multiple parties. Unlike standard car accidents, where one or two drivers are typically responsible, a semi-truck accident may involve the truck driver, the trucking company, vehicle manufacturers, or even third-party contractors. Each party may share some degree of liability for the accident.

For example, if the accident was caused by driver fatigue due to violating hours-of-service regulations, both the driver and the trucking company could be held accountable. Similarly, faulty truck parts might lead to a claim against the vehicle manufacturer. A Phoenix truck accident attorney at Avrek Law Firm can conduct a thorough investigation to determine all responsible parties involved in the accident and ensure that they are held liable for their actions.
