NAP Consistency for Improved Local Search RankingsConsistency in the Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) of a business across all online platforms is crucial for improving local SEO performance. eTegrity Digital’s local SEO services focus on auditing and correcting NAP details across directories, social media profiles, and websites to ensure uniformity. Consistent NAP ensures that potential customers can easily find and contact the business, enhancing its credibility in the local market.
