# Footography Emerges as Lucrative Art Form and Income Opportunity in Digital Age

A new visual art trend called footography is reshaping photography markets and creating accessible income streams for digital creators. This niche focuses on artistic depictions of feet and footwear, blending aesthetic appeal with commercial potential.

- Fashion brands showcasing footwear
