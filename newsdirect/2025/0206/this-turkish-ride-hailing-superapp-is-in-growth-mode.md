# This Turkish Ride-Hailing SuperApp Is In Growth Mode

By Johnny Rice Benzinga

![](https://public.newsdirect.com/952020959/Nz27IRcm.png)

Oguz Alper Oktem, CEO of Marti Technologies, Inc. (AMEX: MRT), was recently interviewed by Benzinga.

Marti fashions itself as Türkiye’s mobility super app, offering multiple transportation services to its riders. Marti operates a ride-hailing service that matches riders with car and motorcycle drivers and operates a large fleet of rental e-mopeds, e-bikes and e-scooters. All of Marti’s offerings are serviced by proprietary software systems and IoT infrastructure.
