Unusual Machines (NYSE: UMAC) CEO Allan Evans: ”Pure Play on Growth of US Drone Market”

Unusual Machines, Inc. (NYSE: UMAC) announced it has signed a lease for a 17,000-square-foot drone motor production facility in Orlando, Florida. The factory will significantly expand the company's domestic manufacturing capabilities. "This factory is a major milestone in our strategy to rapidly onshore drone component manufacturing," said Allan Evans, CEO of Unusual Machines. Motor deliveries from this facility are scheduled to begin in September 2025.The facility is designed to support the production of high-performance brushless motors for First-Person View (FPV) and commercial drones.
