# Activate Brings Premier Gaming Facility to Coquitlam, Offers Free Admission from Jan. 25 to 30

Get ready to experience a new way to play and join the movement that has already captivated millions worldwide! Activate, the world’s first active gaming facility and viral social media sensation, is set to revolutionize Coquitlam’s entertainment scene with the launch of its first location in British Columbia, opening on Friday, Jan. 17!

Activate Coquitlam spans 9,756 square feet and offers 10 game rooms, including social media sensation Mega Grid, Strike, and Laser. Designed to blend the thrill of digital gaming with real-world action, this groundbreaking venue offers a dynamic team-based experience that tests both mental and physical skills. Guests can choose cooperative or competitive modes, progress through levels, and earn rewards using Activate’s high-tech RFID wristbands.

“Coquitlam, get ready for a mind-blowing experience like no other" said Adam Schmidt, CEO of Activate. “This is more than gaming—it’s an electrifying fusion of advanced technology and physical adventure, designed to create unforgettable moments for families, friends, and thrill-seekers. We’re beyond excited to see Coquitlam dive into this one-of-a-kind, high-octane experience!”

Founded in Winnipeg, Canada, Activate has become the fastest-growing entertainment company in the world, with more than 30 locations across North America and new sites in Dubai and the UK. Additionally, Activate continues to expand its global footprint, with upcoming locations announced in Germany, Austria, Switzerland and Scandinavia.

ABOUT ACTIVATE

Activate is the world’s first active-gaming experience where players #EnterTheGame. Activate offers a unique blend of physical activity and gaming that promotes a healthy lifestyle. Each Activate location provides fun and interactive rooms for players to compete, earn stars and track achievements. Headquartered in Winnipeg, Canada, Activate has expanded to nearly 40 locations across Canada, the U.S., and the UK, with upcoming sites in Scandinavia and other international destinations.
