Ripple Coin Regains Bullish Momentum Amidst Price CorrectionEarlier in the week, Ripple (XRP), like most top altcoins, lost a great deal of their value to a broader market correction. Sui Token Trades Above Crucial Support Zone Just like Ripple, Sui (SUI) token also had its fair share of the effect of the recent price correction in the broader crypto market, registering approximately 10% decline in the past week and over 30% in the past month.
