Ethereum on a Rollercoaster Ride: Price SwingsOver the previous several days, Ethereum's price has displayed an erratic tendency. Currently, Ethereum is undergoing a consolidation phase, hovering around the $3,842 mark which is still an impressive 16% increase in the last 30 days.
