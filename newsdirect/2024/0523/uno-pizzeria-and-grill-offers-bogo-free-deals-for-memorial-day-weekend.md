# Uno Pizzeria and Grill offers BOGO Free deals for Memorial Day weekend

While Memorial Day itself honors Americas military members who lost their lives in service to our country, the weekend has become synonymous with travel, cookouts and time with family and friends. Uno Pizzeria and Grill is offering a series of BOGO free deals over the weekend to help bring family and friends together. The deals will run Friday, Saturday and Sunday and feature a different BOGO free deal each day. This BOGO deal encourages pairs or groups to come dine together and save.

On Friday, May 24th, Uno Pizzeria and Grill will offer a BOGO Free Pizza deal valid on all deep dish and thin crust pizzas. Buy one pizza and get a second of equal or lesser value for free. Valid for Dine-in and also take-out. Must use code BOGOFREE when ordering on-line.

On Saturday, May 25th, Uno Pizzeria and Grill will offer a BOGO Free Entree deal. Buy any Entrée and get a second Entrée of equal or lesser value for free. Valid for Dine-in only.

On Sunday, May 26th, Uno Pizzeria and Grill will offer a BOGO Free Pasta dish deal. Buy any Pasta dish and get a second Pasta dish of equal or lesser value for free. Valid for Dine-in only.

The Memorial Day Holiday is on Monday May 27th. Uno Pizzeria and Grill honors those US Military members who have passed and thanks all service members for their service and commitment. All restaurants will be open on the Monday.

Participation may vary by location.

About UNO Pizzeria & Grill

Based in Boston, Massachusetts, Uno Restaurant Holdings Corporation includes approximately 80 company-owned and franchised UNO Pizzeria & Grill restaurants located in 18 states, and the District of Columbia, India, and Saudi Arabia. UNO is all about connecting people over pizza – from its famous Chicago Deep Dish, which UNO invented in 1943, to its Chicago Thin Crust, to its gluten-free and vegan pizzas. The Company also operates Uno Foods, a consumer packaged-foods business which supplies supermarkets, airlines, movie theaters, hotels, airports, travel plazas, and schools, with both frozen and refrigerated UNO branded products. For more information, visit www.unos.com.
