# Ocean Power Technologies Secures Contract for WAM-V to Offshore Contractor in Sub-Saharan Africa

Ocean Power Technologies CEO Philipp Stratmann joined Steve Darling from Proactive to announce that the company has secured a contract to deliver a WAM-V (Wave Adaptive Modular Vessel) to an Offshore Construction Contractor. The contract entails providing a spare vehicle system that can be assembled onsite. This WAM-V will be utilized to provide survey services for an offshore dredging project in Sub-Saharan Africa.
