# CEO Of Cboe Sits Down With Benzinga To Talk State Of The Markets

By Johnny Rice, Benzinga

![](https://public.newsdirect.com/829727541/M0PGR20A.png)

Fred Tomczyk, CEO of Cboe Global Markets Inc. (Cboe: CBOE), was interviewed by Benzinga at the HOOD Summit, presented by Robinhood (NASDAQ: HOOD).

Cboe Global Markets is the world’s leading derivatives and securities exchange network – operating in 27 markets globally – and facilitates trading in options, futures, equities, FX and more for all investors. The company has long been an innovator in financial products.

A year into Mr. Tomczyk’s tenure as CEO, the firm appears to be firing on all cylinders. The CEO spoke about his firm's incredible growth and what he sees as the dominant trends in the upcoming year.
