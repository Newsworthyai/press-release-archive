To mark this historic occasion and empower investors to seize market opportunities, HTX rolls out a Bitcoin Halving Special Edition for Futures "Trade to Earn" event, which allows users to trade BTC futures at zero cost while splitting a daily prize pool of 100,000 USDT.

According to HTX's official announcement, this special edition of the "Trade to Earn" event is scheduled to run from 12:00:00 (UTC) on April 19, 2024, until 11:59:59 (UTC) on April 24, 2024. Users will have the opportunity to earn rewards in $HTX by trading BTCUSDT Perpetual (including copy trading).The more trades, the greater the rewards, with a maximum of 5,000 USDT daily per user.
