# Sustainable Resource Corp creates “Sustainable Shrimps” brand to cater for consultancy services and technology sales

Sustainable Resource Corp are pleased to announce the launch of its dedicated land-based shrimp farming brand Sustainable Shrimps. A consultancy and equipment supplier for both indoor and outdoor Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS).

The site show cases our complete end-to-end technology solution for indoor shrimp production. The innovative technology is based on an advanced water circulation system that enables recycling of water and minimum liquid discharge (MLD). This ensures that high-water quality is maintained and creates optimal growing conditions that are nine times more profitable than traditional shrimp farming.

To cater for aquaculturists who favor outdoor grow out conditions, Sustainable Shrimps (www.sustainableshrimps.com) also shares our tried and tested pond specification’s. Clients can receive delivery of all equipment (indoor and outdoor) with construction services also provided. Dedicated onsite training is also included, covering feeding & growth analysis, risk management, and water quality tracking procedures.

The global aquaculture industry continues to show year on year growth, although with stagnant funding, working collaboratively with industry professionals and entrepreneurs is essential for industry progression. “Supporting aquaculture enthusiasts, collaborating on optimal growing techniques and technological progressions is the backbone of the industry. The launch of Sustainable Shrimps is very much in support of the industry itself. Adding to its value chain.” Said Andy Farley SEO of Sustainable Resource Corp.

Global Shrimp Market

Valued at USD 50bn today, the global shrimp market has been the most internationally traded seafood product since the 1970’s and is second to salmon as the most valuable aquaculture species globally today.

Over the next 8 years the sector is expected to grow at CAGR of 9.5% reaching a USD 103bn valuation by 2032.

About Sustainable Resource Corp

Sustainable Resource Corp are a pioneering private equity firm focused specifically on the aquaculture industry. Operating privately of over 30 years, our team combines generations of aquaculture expertise, with the latest, most advanced RAS (Recirculating Aquaculture Systems) technology, to create sustainable and ecological solutions to local fish and seafood production.
