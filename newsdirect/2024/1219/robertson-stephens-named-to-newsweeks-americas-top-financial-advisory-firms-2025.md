# Robertson Stephens Named to Newsweek’s America’s Top Financial Advisory Firms 2025

Robertson Stephens Wealth Management, LLC ("Robertson Stephens"), a $7 billion registered investment advisor, announced today that it has been named one of America’s Top Financial Advisory Firms 2025 by Newsweek and Plant-A Insights Group, which developed its list by conducting a large-scale study based on an analysis of more than 15,000 SEC-registered financial advisories.

"We are honored to be recognized by Newsweek as one of America’s Top Financial Advisory firms in 2025," said Raj Bhattacharyya, Chief Executive Officer, Robertson Stephens. "This recognition is a validation of our commitment to providing our clients with differentiated advice alongside comprehensive wealth plans and tailored investment portfolios that help address their needs, goals and legacies."

“In today’s financial landscape, Americans want more than just advice—they want a trusted partner to help them achieve their financial goals. Newsweek and market-data research firm Plant-A Insights are proud to introduce America’s Top Financial Advisory Firms 2025, highlighting companies that exemplify what it means to be a financial leader in today’s market.” – Nancy Cooper – Global Editor in Chief – Newsweek.

Robertson Stephens paid no fee to obtain recognition but has paid a licensing fee to Plant-A Insights Group to reference its appearance on the rankings list.
