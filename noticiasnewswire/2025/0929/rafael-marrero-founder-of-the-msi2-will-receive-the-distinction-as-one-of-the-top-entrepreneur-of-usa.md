# Rafael Marrero, Founder of The MSI2, Will Receive The Distinction as One of The ‘Top Entrepreneur of USA’

MIAMI, FL — September 29, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Negocios and Ejecutiva Magazine, leaders in information and interviews with executives in southern Florida, will carry out on October 9, 2025, within the framework of the month of Hispanity, the awards ceremony “Top Entrepreneur of USA Awards 2025”, at the EB Hotel in Miami.

 One of the winners is Dr. Rafael Marrero, founder of the Miami Strategic Intelligence Institute (MSI²) who will receive the distinction as one of the Top Entrepreneur of USA, within the framework of the celebration for the month of Hispanicity.

 “Receiving the ‘Top Entrepreneur of USA 2025 ’prize is an honor that I share with the entire MSI² team,” said Dr. Marrero. He added: “This recognition not only reflects our effort to consolidate a reference Think tank in geopolitics, national security and strategic analysis, but also the value of Hispanic talent that integrates our institution.”

 “In a short time, we have shown that vision, dedication and excellence can turn an idea into an influential platform, capable of projecting the Hispanic voice and offering concrete solutions to the strategic challenges of Latin America and the hemisphere,” said economist Marrero.

 Top Entrepreneur of USA

 The “Top Entrepreneur of USA” awards are a great prestige recognition dedicated to honoring businessmen and business leaders who have demonstrated exceptional performance and leadership in their respective fields. These awards highlight the dedication, innovation and positive impact that these entrepreneurs have generated in their communities and beyond. Being recognized as one of the best entrepreneurs not only celebrates professional success but also inspires others who aspire to excel on their own paths.

 The cocktail and dinner will be held at the EB hotel in Miami on October 9, 2025. The night will have a cocktail followed by a formal dinner, creating an ideal environment to do networking and celebrating the achievements. During the event, entrepreneurs will officially receive their awards, recognizing their contributions and their commitment to excellence in their industries.

 Event attendees will have the opportunity to connect with other entrepreneurs, community leaders in a cozy environment. The “Top Entrepreneur of USA” ceremony is not only a platform to honor success, but also an opportunity to promote new alliances and collaborations. This event promises to be a memorable celebration of achievements, innovation and leadership in the United States entrepreneurial community.

 The “Top Entrepreneurs of USA Awards” are held every year, where women and businessmen who stand out in their businesses or activities they develop are distinguished. The event is organized by business and executive magazines.

 Negocios and Ejecutiva, Business Leaders in South Florida

 The journalist Eduardo Hapke joined forces with lawyer Karen Blanco and in 2013 they created Negocios Magazine and Ejecutiva Magazine, the only business magazines in Spanish in southern Florida focused on entrepreneurs that are published both on paper and digital format. Every month magazines are published in southern Florida. The magazine also has its website www.negociosmagazine.com , which has daily news updates and a large distribution of social networks content.

 The Ejecutiva Magazine also has its website www.ejecutivamagazine.com , which has daily news updates and a large distribution of social networks content.

 Magazine business is also distributed through Pressreader, a digital platform that has more than 7,000 publications worldwide. Hotels, companies, airplanes and cruises have access to this news platform.

 Negocios and Ejecutiva magazines are also published on the ISSUU.com platform, where the complete online publication can be visualized.

 Negocios Magazine is part of the media group Diurna Urbis Group Corp. based in Miami, Florida, which also publishes Ejecutiva Magazine, Welcome to America, Salud & Buen Vivir Florida, Hispanic Time and Hispanic Time: Electric Cars Plus.

 You can follow the interviews and news published in Negocios Magazine through their social networks on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and X.
