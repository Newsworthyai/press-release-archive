# The Eighth Season of En Positivo® Kicks Off with an Exclusive You Can’t Miss!

![](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/logo1.jpg)

 MIAMI, FL — February 25, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Audio Dice Network, digital audio partner of En Positivo®, podcast in collaboration with Award-winning journalist Lourdes Del Rio, proudly announces that En Positivo® is back with a brand-new season of her hit podcast En Positivo®, a space dedicated to sharing stories that inspire and transform.

 And she’s starting off with a bang! The season premiere features an exclusive interview with the legendary Cristina Saralegui, one of the most influential figures in Hispanic television—who rarely gives interviews. This is a huge milestone for the podcast and a rare opportunity to hear Cristina like never before.

 “Having Cristina on this season is truly an honor. Her journey, resilience, and authenticity make her a one-of-a-kind inspiration. This is a conversation you don’t want to miss,” says Lourdes.

 A star-studded season with Borja Voces, Alejandra Espinoza & more

 Following this exciting premiere, En Positivo® will continue to bring high-profile guests to the show, including Borja Voces, renowned journalist and TV host, and Alejandra Espinoza, actress and television personality. And that’s just the beginning—expect even more surprises and powerful, inspiring stories throughout the season.

 More than a podcast—a movement that’s making an impact

 Since launching in 2020, En Positivo® has grown into a movement that encourages people to see life through a positive lens. With the support of its dedicated community, the “Tribu Positiva”, the podcast has earned multiple Latin Podcast Awards, including the prestigious Podcast of the Year honor.

 “Get ready for an unforgettable season filled with deep conversations, inspiring moments, and an abundance of positive energy,” Lourdes adds.
