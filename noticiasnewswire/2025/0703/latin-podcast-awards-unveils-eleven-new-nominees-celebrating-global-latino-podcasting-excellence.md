# Latin Podcast Awards Unveils Eleven New Nominees, Celebrating Global Latino Podcasting Excellence

DALLAS, TX — July 3, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — The Latin Podcast Awards (LPA), the world’s only competition honoring Latino/Hispanic and non-Latino podcast content creators, proudly announces eleven new nominees for its 2025 edition. Presented by Felix A. Montelara, founder of Audio Dice Network (ADN) Media Group, the ninth annual awards continue to showcase the best in bilingual podcasting from the U.S. and around the globe.

 A distinguished panel of international podcast content producers is currently evaluating entries to select winners across various categories and countries, including the highly anticipated Latin Podcast Award of the Year for 2025. Winners will receive the coveted “Microphone Statue” trophy.

 Newly Announced Nominees Include:

 * Sexo sin tabú (United States) – Health & Fitness by Francis Mojica
* Sanguchito de salmón (Argentina) – Religion & Spirituality by Sergio Badaloni
* Pura Vida Perspectives (Costa Rica) – Leisure by Mauricio Salazar, Sarah Elena, and Jairo Cardenas
* Hablemos de Plata (Costa Rica) – Education by Mauricio Salazar, Gus Saenz, and Choché Romano
* El Podcast de Yiyo y Choché (Costa Rica) – Comedy by Mauricio Salazar, Yiyo y Choché
* Los Niños del Narco (México) – True Crime by Sebastián Arrechedera
* Susurros Podcast (Costa Rica) – Society & Culture by Bobby Chavarría, Waldin Badilla, and Adrian Morales
* Murmullos Radiantes / Radiant Whispers (United Kingdom) – Society & Culture by Gabriel Porras
* Estímate (Dominican Republic) – Health & Fitness by Jeymi Febles
* Un libro para vivir (Dominican Republic) – Education by Jeymi Febles
* Campamento Los Detectives (Dominican Republic) – Kids & Family by Nicolás Cruz, Steve Cruz, and Jeymi Febles

 For more information, visit LatinPodcast.org.
