# The ‘Top Entrepreneur of USA Awards’ Will be Presented on October 9th as Part of Hispanic Heritage Month

MIAMI, FL — August 1, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Negocios and Ejecutiva Magazine, leaders in news and interviews with executives in South Florida, will hold the “Top Entrepreneur of USA Awards 2025” on October 9, 2025, as part of Hispanic Heritage Month, at the EB Hotel in Miami.

 The “Top Entrepreneur of USA” awards are a prestigious recognition dedicated to honoring entrepreneurs and business leaders who have demonstrated exceptional performance and leadership in their respective fields. These awards highlight the dedication, innovation, and positive impact these entrepreneurs have had in their communities and beyond. Being recognized as one of the best entrepreneurs not only celebrates professional success but also inspires others who aspire to excel in their own paths.

 The cocktail reception and dinner will be held at the EB Hotel in Miami on October 9, 2025. The evening will feature a cocktail reception followed by a formal dinner, creating an ideal setting for networking and celebrating achievements. During the event, outstanding entrepreneurs will be officially presented with their awards, recognizing their contributions and commitment to excellence in their industries.

 The full list of honorees will be announced shortly.

 Event attendees will have the opportunity to connect with other entrepreneurs and community leaders in a welcoming environment. The “Top Entrepreneurs of USA” ceremony is not only a platform to honor success but also an opportunity to foster new partnerships and collaborations. This event promises to be a memorable celebration of achievements, innovation, and leadership in the U.S. entrepreneurial community.

 The “Top Entrepreneurs of USA Awards” are held annually, honoring men and women who excel in their businesses or activities. The event is organized by Negocios and Ejecutiva magazines.

 Negocios and Ejecutiva, business leaders in South Florida

 Journalist Eduardo Hapke joined forces with attorney Karen Blanco and in 2013 created Negocios Magazine and Ejecutiva Magazine, the only Spanish-language business magazines in South Florida focused on entrepreneurs, published in both print and digital formats. The magazines are published monthly in South Florida. The magazine also has its website www.negociosmagazine.com, which features daily news updates and extensive content distribution on social media.

 Ejecutiva Magazine also has its website www.ejecutivamagazine.com, which features daily news updates and extensive content distribution on social media.

 Negocios Magazine is also distributed through PressReader, a digital platform with more than 7,000 publications worldwide. Hotels, corporations, airlines, and cruise ships all have access to this news platform.

 Negocios and Ejecutiva magazines are also published on issuu.com, where you can view the full publication online.

 Negocios Magazine is part of the Miami, Florida-based media group Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp., which also publishes Ejecutiva, Welcome to America, Salud & Buen Vivir Florida, Hispanic Time, and Hispanic Time: Electric Cars Plus.

 You can follow interviews and news published in Negocios Magazine through its social media channels on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X.
