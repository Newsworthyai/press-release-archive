# Super Patch Presents: 2 Live and Die in L.A. 2025 – A Cultural Celebration Like No Other

![](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/logo1-2.jpg)

 ![](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/logo2.jpg)

 LOS ANGELES, CA — July 18, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Get ready for the most exhilarating cultural event of the year—Super Patch Presents: 2 Live and Die in L.A. 2025! Founded by the visionary Frankie Orozco, this year’s festival promises to be bigger, bolder, and more impactful than ever before. Mark your calendars for a day that will celebrate the heartbeat of Los Angeles through a vibrant fusion of art, music, food, and community. Curated by the legendary Estevan Oriol, the gallery portion of the event will present never-before-seen art and photography from some of L.A.’s most talented artists. Estevan, a cultural icon known for his powerful visual storytelling, brings his unparalleled perspective on the city’s complex and dynamic culture. This collection will showcase the true essence of L.A., offering a deep dive into its vibrant communities and untold stories.

 But that’s just the beginning. The festival will feature an electrifying lineup of live performances spanning multiple genres—punk, hip-hop, and more. Headlined by the one and only WC, a true West Coast legend, the event will also feature 13 other rising stars including Zoe Osama, Desmadre, Conejo, Righteous Soldiers, and many others. It’s an unmissable day for music lovers, with each performance embodying the raw energy and soul of L.A.

 As if that wasn’t enough, 2 Live and Die in L.A. will also shine a spotlight on emerging local filmmakers. Expect select short films from L.A.’s rising cinematic talents, showcasing the city’s rich storytelling tradition in a whole new light.

 Hosted by: Cece Valencta & Fabian Alomar

 Featuring Artists: Estevan Oriol, Robert Vargas, Defer, Merrick Morton, and many more!

 Supporting Local Entrepreneurs and the Community at the core of 2 Live and Die in L.A. is a commitment to the entrepreneurial spirit that drives Los Angeles. In a city where the cost of living continues to rise, many young creatives are carving out their own paths by starting businesses and brands. This festival provides a platform for these entrepreneurs to showcase their work and engage with the community, giving them a chance to grow and thrive. One of the featured vendors is Raised by Latinos, an international brand and South Bay Vendors. Their presence at the event highlights the festival’s dedication to elevating local businesses and connecting them with a global audience.

 Super Patch is redefining health and wellness with a product that’s as simple to use as it is effective. Driven by a mission to help people achieve their highest potential, Super Patch continues to empower individuals to take control of their health—naturally and without reliance on traditional medicine. www.superpatch.com.

 “I’m here to build something generational that will transform the world. Anything else is a waste of time.” Jay Dhaliwal, Founder & CEO. Perfect fit as a Sponsor for this event.

 A Day of Art, Music, and Culture 2 Live and Die in L.A. is more than just a festival—it’s a movement that embodies the spirit of Los Angeles. Whether you’re an artist, a music lover, or a fan of the local community, this event will bring people together from all walks of life to celebrate everything that makes L.A. unique.

 Event Details:

 Date: July 19, 2025.  Location: American Graffiti Supply, 1100 E 5th St, Los Angeles, CA. Time: 4 pm to 11 PM.  Admission: Ticket.
