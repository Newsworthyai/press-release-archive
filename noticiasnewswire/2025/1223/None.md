# Dentistry of Oxnard Expands Access to Advanced Digital Implant Dentistry and Comprehensive Dental Care in Oxnard, California

Oxnard, CA (December 23, 2025) / Noticias Newswire/ — Dentistry of Oxnard, led by Dr. Iraj Movahhedi, continues to set a high standard for comprehensive dental care in Ventura County by offering advanced digital implant dentistry, orthodontics, and a wide range of general and cosmetic dental services—all performed in-house with no referrals required.

 Conveniently located at 4225 Saviers Rd #9, Oxnard, CA 93033 (across from the DMV), Dentistry of Oxnard has proudly served the community since 1995, providing honest, compassionate, and affordable dental care to patients of all ages. The practice welcomes patients from Oxnard, El Rio, Port Hueneme, Saticoy, Camarillo, and surrounding communities.

 Dentistry of Oxnard specializes in computer-guided digital implant dentistry, including correction surgeries, bone grafting, and full-arch restorations. Additional services include orthodontics and braces, IV sedation dentistry, wisdom teeth extractions, root canal therapy performed under a dental microscope, laser dentistry with little to no injections, cosmetic dentistry, dentures, and more.

 A key differentiator of the practice is its commitment to accessibility and continuity of care. Dentistry of Oxnard accepts most major insurance plans, including Denti-Cal and Medicare, and offers flexible payment plans to ensure patients can receive the care they need without unnecessary financial barriers.

 Unlike many practices, there are no associates or outside specialist referrals. Dr. Movahhedi personally performs all procedures, allowing patients to receive consistent, customized treatment in one location from start to finish.

 “We are dedicated to delivering the highest quality dental care at the lowest possible fees,” said [Dr. Movahhedi](https://dentistryoxnard.com/iraj-movahhedi/). “We use the best materials and dental laboratories available, and we treat every patient with honesty, compassion, and respect.”![Personal Finance Expert, Consumer Advocate – Iraj Movahhedi DDS](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/iraj-movahhedi-dds-150-150-img.jpg)
