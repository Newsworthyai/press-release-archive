# Antonio Uribe, President of Terrabank, on The Cover of Negocios Magazine

MIAMI, FL — November 17, 20225 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Antonio Uribe, president and CEO of Terrabank, embodies modern leadership in the South Florida financial sector. With a strategic vision focused on innovation, institutional strength, and personalized service, Uribe has guided Terrabank toward a stage of sustained growth and expansion in key markets. His management combines deep experience in international banking with a firm commitment to the local business community, positioning Terrabank as an agile, reliable entity prepared for the challenges of the new global financial landscape.

 Antonio Uribe is featured in the new edition of Negocios Magazine, appearing on the cover of South Florida’s leading business magazine. Antonio Uribe assumed leadership of Terrabank in 2006 and transformed the bank through a strategy focused on technological innovation and the creation of customer-oriented solutions, positioning Terrabank as a solid competitor in an increasingly demanding market.

 Since his arrival at Terrabank, Uribe has formed a new management team with the goal of redefining the bank’s strategy. His focus is on developing digital solutions that respond to the current needs of customers, combining his international experience with a deep understanding of the local market.

 “Terrabank has proudly served the Miami community for 40 years and has established a solid foundation in both commercial lending and retail banking. Our evolution has combined personalized, community-focused service with modern banking tools. This has allowed us to grow without losing the close relationship that characterizes us. From the beginning, our mission has been to understand and meet the needs of our multicultural community, not only from a business perspective, but also through shared experiences,” Antonio Uribe told Negocios magazine in an exclusive interview.

 Negocios Magazine, a leading business publication in South Florida

 Journalist Eduardo Hapke joined forces with attorney Karen Blanco in 2013 to create Negocios Magazine, the only Spanish-language business magazine in South Florida focused on entrepreneurs, published in both print and digital formats. The magazine is published and distributed bimonthly throughout South Florida. It also has its own website, www.negociosmagazine.com, which features daily news updates and extensive social media content distribution.

 Negocios Magazine is also distributed through PressReader, a digital platform with over 7,000 publications worldwide. Hotels, businesses, airlines, and cruise lines have access to this news platform.

 Negocios Magazine is also published on issuu.com, where the complete publication can be viewed online. Negocios Magazine is part of the Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp. media group, based in Miami, Florida, which also publishes Ejecutiva magazine, Welcome a América, Salud & Buen Vivir Florida, Hispanic Time, and Hispanic Time: Electric Cars Plus.
