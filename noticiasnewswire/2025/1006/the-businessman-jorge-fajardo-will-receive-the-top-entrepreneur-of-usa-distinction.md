# The Businessman Jorge Fajardo Will Receive The ‘Top Entrepreneur of USA’ Distinction

MIAMI, FL — October 6, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — The businessman Jorge Fajardo, founder and president of KPI Capital Partners, will be recognized next Thursday, October 9 in Miami with the prestigious “Top Entrepreneur of USA” award, an award that highlights visionary leaders who have promoted economic growth, innovation and business development in the country. This recognition celebrates its trajectory as a promoter of strategic investment projects and creator of opportunities in various sectors, as well as its commitment to strengthening the Latin business ecosystem in the United States. His leadership, global vision and ability to transform ideas into successful businesses consolidate it as one of the most influential figures in the current corporate world.

 Negocios and Ejecutiva Magazine, leaders in information and interviews with executives in southern Florida, will carry out on October 9, 2025, within the framework of the month of Hispanity, the awards ceremony “Top Entrepreneur of USA Awards 2025”, at the EB Hotel in Miami.

 In addition to being a visionary entrepreneur, Jorge is a passionate mentor and published author. Through its educational programs, it has trained aspiring investors in real estate, sharing their vast experience and knowledge so that others can follow their way to success.

 In addition to his business success, Jorge is distinguished by his commitment to the Latin community. As a Colombian immigrant who grew up in the American education system, he has combined his personal experience with his professional knowledge to empower others.

 The “Top Entrepreneur of USA” awards are a great prestige recognition dedicated to honoring businessmen and business leaders who have demonstrated exceptional performance and leadership in their respective fields. These awards highlight the dedication, innovation and positive impact that these entrepreneurs have generated in their communities and beyond. Being recognized as one of the best entrepreneurs not only celebrates professional success but also inspires others who aspire to excel on their own paths.

 The cocktail and dinner will be held at the EB hotel in Miami on October 9, 2025. The night will have a cocktail followed by a formal dinner, creating an ideal environment to do networking and celebrating the achievements. During the event, entrepreneurs will be officially distinguished with their awards, recognizing their contributions and commitment to excellence in their industries.

 The master of ceremonies of the cocktail and dinner will be the journalist and businesswoman Sabina Covo, founder of Covo Communications.

 The complete list of entrepreneurs who will receive the distinction is as follows: Claudia Marquez (Coo of Hyundai Motor America); German Pugliese-Bassi (CEO of Persea Capital Ventures); Rafael Marrero (CEO of Rafael Marrero & Company); Robert Thorne (CEO of Urban Network Capital Group (UNCG); Calixto Garcia Velez (CEO of Banesco USA), Dr Anna Chacon (Founder of Miami Derm); Jorge Fajardo (Founder and Ceo of KPI Capital Partners); Willy Riveron (President of EAG); Jorge Raad,  (President of Las Mercedes Medical Centers);  Ammer Cabrera  (CEO of Las Madrinas);  Miguel Priore  (Co-Founder of Pelleriti Priore);  Tony Delgado  (Founder of Latino Wall Street);  Gabriela Berrospi  (Founder of Latino Wall Street);  Jerry Brodsky  (Founder of Jerry Brodsky Legal);  Gisela Rojas  (Founder & Ceo Caissa Realty);  Elena Solis Gonzalez  (Founder of Renaissance Dental Clinic);  Erwin Mosqueda  (CEO of Top Gun M.O.T Design Services) ; Fabiola Malka  (Founder of New Concept PR); Fernando Socol  (Founder of Fernando M.  The “Top Entrepreneur of USA” ceremony is not only a platform to honor success, but also an opportunity to promote new alliances and collaborations. This event promises to be a memorable celebration of achievements, innovation and leadership in the United States entrepreneurial community.
