# Gabriela Berrospi, Founder of Latino Wall Street, on The Cover of Ejecutiva Magazine

MIAMI, FL — October 16, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Ejecutiva Magazine launched its new issue with a cover and interview with Gabriela Berrospi, founder of Latino Wall Street, a pioneering educational platform dedicated to democratizing access to financial knowledge in Spanish.

 One of Gaby Berrospi’s (IG @gabywallstreet) missions is to empower the Latino community through training programs, seminars, and mentorships that teach professional investing in the stock market and other markets. With an international presence and thousands of students in more than 20 countries, Latino Wall Street combines financial education, personal development, and leadership, promoting a growth mindset and financial freedom within the Hispanic community.

 Gabriela Berrospi continues to set milestones in the financial world with a pioneering partnership with FinTech TV, from the iconic floor of the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE). This collaboration, launched in 2025, represents an unprecedented advance in offering bilingual financial education in Spanish and English, bringing firsthand knowledge directly from the heart of global markets to the Latino community.

 “Our goal is to empower the Latino community and ensure they have access to key information to build wealth and fully participate in the financial markets,” says Berrospi. With this partnership, Gabriela not only reaffirms her leadership in the field of financial education but also positions Latinos in a historic position on Wall Street, demonstrating that financial knowledge has no language or nationality barriers.

The magazine is published every two months and distributed in South Florida. The magazine also has a website: www.ejecutivamagazine.com, with daily news updates and extensive content distribution on social media.

 Ejecutiva Magazine is also distributed through Pressreader, a digital platform with 7,000 publications worldwide. Hotels and businesses have access to this news platform.

 Ejecutiva Magazine is also published on the platform www.issuu.com, where you can view the full issue online.

 Ejecutiva Magazine is part of the Miami, Florida-based media group Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp., which also publishes Negocios Magazine, Welcome to America, Salud & Buen Vivir Florida, Hispanic Time, and Hispanic Time: Electric Cars Plus.
