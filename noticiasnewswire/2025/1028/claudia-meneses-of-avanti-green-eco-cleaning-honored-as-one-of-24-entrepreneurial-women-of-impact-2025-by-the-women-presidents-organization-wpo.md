# Claudia Meneses of Avanti Green Eco Cleaning Honored as One of 24 Entrepreneurial Women of Impact 2025 by The Women Presidents Organization (WPO)

![](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/logo1-1.jpg)

 LAS VEGAS, NV — October 28, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Avanti Green Eco Cleaning is proud to announce that Claudia Meneses, Co-Founder & CEO of the company, has been named among the 2025 “Entrepreneurial Women of Impact” by the Women Presidents Organization (WPO), in partnership with Women Elevating Women (W.E.W.). The awards, sponsored by J.P. Morgan Commercial Banking, recognize 24 outstanding women entrepreneurs who combine business success with meaningful community impact.  women-presidents.com+2women-presidents.com+2

 Claudia Meneses founded Avanti Green Eco Cleaning in 2011 and has led the company’s growth in the competitive Las Vegas and Henderson markets by promoting residential and commercial eco-friendly cleaning solutions, customized service offerings (including carpet cleaning, window cleaning, pressure washing, and floor care), and a strong emphasis on team training, quality assurance, and sustainability. Under her leadership, the company has distinguished itself for its personalized customer experience, green branding and innovative digital marketing efforts.

 “I am deeply honored to be recognized by the WPO alongside such accomplished women,” said Claudia Meneses. “This award reflects the dedication of our entire Avanti Green team and underscores our commitment to delivering exceptional, eco-conscious cleaning services while making a positive impact in our community.”

 Earlier this year, Avanti Green Eco Cleaning won first prize and $35,000 USD at the Amazon Web Services (AWS) AI Pitch Competition , enabling the company to develop its AI-powered scheduling and route optimization system , designed to provide real-time availability, create upselling opportunities and increase operational efficiency. The innovation caught the attention of AWS, which continues to support its product development. Additionally, Avanti Green’s idea was selected among the top 10 startups in Nevada’s Startup World Cup , showcasing its growing leadership in sustainability-driven innovation and technology for the home services industry. The company was also the 2024 Small Business Development Center’s Business of the Year.

 About WPO The Women Presidents Organization (WPO) is a global non-profit peer advisory community for women business leaders of privately-held, multi-million-dollar companies. WPO’s mission is to accelerate business growth, enhance competitiveness and promote economic security for women entrepreneurs by providing access to peer networks, executive education and confidential round-table chapters.
