# AmigosMax Announces Call for Submissions for 2025 Latino Mother’s Day Gift Guide

MIAMI, FL — March 28, 2025 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — AmigosMax.com, the premier network for Latino professionals, is excited to announce its Call for Submissions for the 2025 Latino Mother’s Day Gift Guide—a curated showcase of gifts created by Latino entrepreneurs to celebrate and honor madres everywhere.

 Each year, this special edition gift guide highlights digital and physical products from Latino-owned businesses, offering thoughtful, culturally inspired gifts that speak directly to the Latino community. Past guides have featured a diverse range of offerings—from handmade goods and beauty products to digital downloads, subscriptions, and personalized experiences.

 Submissions are 100% FREE and open now to eligible businesses.

 “Mother’s Day is one of the most celebrated holidays in Latino culture, and what better way to honor that than by spotlighting amazing products from our comunidad,” said Danay Escanaverino, founder of AmigosMax. “This is more than a gift guide—it’s a movement to elevate Latino small businesses and bring visibility to the unique vibrance and sabor we as entrepreneurs bring to the table.”

 How to apply:

 Latino-owned or co-owned businesses can submit a product or service for consideration by completing the online application form.

 Deadline to submit: April 25, 2025 Apply now: https://amigosmax.com/mom25

 Requirements:

 * Business must be Latino-owned or co-owned
* Must have a website or online sales platform (e.g., Etsy, Shopify, Instagram, TikTok)
* Must be able to accept online payments
* Complete application required (entries missing images or key information will not be considered)

 Free promotion:

 Selected products will be featured in the official guide, supported by press releases, paid social media campaigns, newsletters, and the full AmigosMax.com ecosystem, reaching millions of consumers looking to support Latino-owned businesses.

 About AmigosMax: Founded in 2020, AmigosMax is the leading professional community for Latino professionals in the U.S., connecting over 45,000 members with resources, networking, and opportunities for growth. Founder and Latina entrepreneur Danay Escanaverino created AmigosMax.com to celebrate Latino excellence in business and beyond—through social audio, digital content, and strategic brand collaborations.
