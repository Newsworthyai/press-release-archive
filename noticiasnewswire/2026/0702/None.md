# Summer Starts with Canela Verano on Canela.TV

NEW YORK, NY — July 2, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — An unforgettable summer starts now! Canela Verano arrives on Canela.TV, the free streaming platform with a fully integrated rewards program, Club Canela. The seasonal celebration features an exciting lineup of original series, must-watch telenovelas, upbeat music shows, and family-friendly programming. Adding to the excitement, registered users in the U.S. can enjoy summer-themed prizes designed to make the season even more memorable.

 Canela.TV is bringing even more ways to enjoy summer with a growing collection of English-language movies, featuring titles for younger, bilingual audiences. Alongside its extensive Spanish-language library, these additions make it easier than ever to find something to watch, whether in English, Spanish, or both. This Canela Verano season celebrates great entertainment for today’s multicultural audiences, all in one place.

 The temperature rises with Secretos de Parejas, Canela.TV’s original reality series, packed with drama, romance, and unexpected revelations. The show takes viewers on an unfiltered journey to the idyllic island of Bali, Indonesia, alongside four celebrity couples: Bárbara de Regil and Fernando Schoenwald, Julián Gil (El Quinto Partido, Mi Vida) and Valeria Marín, Poncho de Nigris and Marcela Mistral, and Bernardo Flores and Sury Sadai, providing the perfect setting for romance and drama, as the couples open their hearts, reveal secrets, explore the beautiful island, and navigate between playful and tense moments.

 For telenovela fans, Cautiva por Amor proves that betrayal, secrets, and resilience never go out of style. Starring Litzy (Doble Traición), Osvaldo de León, and Daniela Castro, the story follows a quest for truth that sparks passion, unexpected revelations, and life-changing conflicts. Determined to uncover the truth, the characters discover a series of shocking secrets that ignite passions and tear families apart.

 The excitement continues with Safir, a fan-favorite Turkish drama set against the breathtaking landscapes of Cappadocia, Turkey. The story follows Feraye as she faces forbidden love, family secrets, and decisions that will forever alter her destiny.

 Music lovers also have a place in this exciting summer lineup thanks to Lo Que Suena, an original Canela Music video podcast, in which Cesár García and Ana Sof Rojas discuss trends, debates, artists, viral moments, and the cultural impact of Latin urban, pop, and regional music.

 Adding even more excitement to the summer, Café con Canela, Canela.TV’s daily lifestyle show, presents its special edition, Un Verano en Nueva York. In this episode, Luz Blanchet, Natalia Valenzuela, and Suly Castillo travel to the Big Apple to experience the city through friendship, fashion, memorable conversations, and its vibrant energy. Throughout the special, they explore iconic locations from New York’s fashion scene, take in stunning skyline views, and discover exclusive dining and fashion destinations, while also visiting key gathering spots within the Latino community in the United States, all as part of a journey filled with style, fun, and unforgettable moments.

 Canela.TV also celebrates the Golden Age of Mexican Cinema with unforgettable films: Amor se dice Cantando starring Miguel Aceves and Julia Sandoval; Allá en el Rancho Grande starring Jorge Negrete and Lilia del Valle; Los Hijos de María Morales starring Pedro Infante, Antonio Badú and Carmelita González and Solo Veracruz es Bello starring Esther Fernández and Antonio Badú. A must-see journey through the romance, music, humor, and traditions that shaped some of the most beloved Cine de Oro classics.

 For those seeking a captivating story set against a tropical paradise, Flor del Caribe, delivers a compelling melodrama set amid Brazil’s breathtaking landscapes. Mystery and drama intertwine masterfully in Kuma, a compelling Turkish series that will keep audiences on the edge of their seats. The story follows a young woman who is wrongly accused of murder and is forced to make an extreme decision.

 This summer, Canela.TV is expanding its English-language programming with curated collections designed for younger bilingual audiences. These collections feature buzzworthy coming-of-age, romance, comedy, drama, and action films. Additional themed collections include a futuristic, AI-inspired lineup featuring films such as Neo, Dragon Hunter, Bones of Yew, Sci-Fi, and Drama, giving viewers even more ways to discover fresh stories throughout the season.

 With Canela Music, the musical celebration continues with Ídolos: Peter Manjarrés, a look back at the career of the acclaimed Colombian vallenato singer. In addition, Canela Music Talks: Flavia Laos features an intimate conversation with the talented Peruvian artist about her career and upcoming projects.The rhythm continues with El Top 10: Días de Sol and the Verano Peligroso playlist, two must-watch collections featuring hits that will define the season.

 This summer, Canela Kids brings entertainment for the whole family with Bajo Terra, an underwater adventure about friendship and teamwork. Captain Tsubasa continues to inspire new generations with values such as discipline and perseverance. Creativity takes center stage with Cielitos, a musical series that sparks imagination, while Boonie Bears: Verano Maravilloso delivers new adventures filled with humor, friendship, and a love for nature.

 All of this entertainment comes with exclusive rewards through Club Canela, the free loyalty program available in the United States and Mexico, where the more you watch, the more you win.

 Whether you’re enjoying a movie under the stars, hosting a backyard gathering, or spending a summer evening with friends and family, Canela.TV registered users in the United States can start watching now to earn points, and beginning July 10, redeem them for sweepstakes entries for a chance to win prizes such as speakers, grills, and much more.

 To participate, download the Canela.TV app, create an account, and join Club Canela in the app, completely free. Club Canela is available in the United States and Mexico.
