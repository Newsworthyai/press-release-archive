# Happiness has found a home!

![](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/logo-1.jpg)

 SARASOTA, FL — APRIL 20, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Legendary radio personality Osvaldo Vega brings his show, Alegre Despertar to Solmart Media’s La Nueva Zeta 910 AM – 102.1 FM, airing Mondays through Fridays from 6:00 AM to 11:00 AM, and reaching the key Latino demographic markets of Tampa, Orlando, Lakeland, and the I-4 corridor.

 “We are proud to welcome Osvaldo Vega into our radio family. His humor, wit, and deep understanding of our market positions him as a true pioneer whose talent enhances our listeners’ radio experience,” said Tomás Martínez, CEO of Solmart Media.

 Known for decades as one of the most astute, comedic and playful personalities on U.S. Spanish-language airwaves, Osvaldo is already winning over new listeners with his signature style—authentic, dynamic, and entertaining.

 “Radio is my home, my soul, and my heart. The purpose of Alegre Despertar is to uplift and inspire our audience to find the beauty in life and to enjoy it through the power of music and humor,” emphasized the celebrated broadcaster.

 So, turn up the volume, let the rhythm move you, and join the morning party that is Alegre Despertar! Discover new urban and tropical genres and take a trip down memory lane with the classics that have captivated generations. Alegre Despertar will rock your world.

 About Solmart Media LLC Solmart Media LLC is an independently owned Media company based in Southwest Florida. It was founded by seasoned radio executive Tomás Martínez and his wife, recognized international Spanish language broadcast journalist and television anchor, Mercedes Soler.
