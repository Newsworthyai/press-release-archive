# Gevorg Shahbazyan, CEO of Starlife Group on The Cover of Negocios Magazine

MIAMI, FL — May 26, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Negocios magazine launched its new edition with a cover by Gevorg Shahbazyan, CEO of Starlife Group. Shahbazyan positions itself as an actor that supports a comprehensive vision of real estate development, combining strategic planning, technical execution and a business structure designed to respond to the current demands of the sector.

 Based in Miami, one of the most dynamic and observed markets in the world, the company has built its identity on a vertical integration model that allows it to cover all stages of the real estate cycle. From land acquisition to construction, marketing, financing and asset management, Starlife Group has consolidated a platform that seeks to reduce the traditional fragmentation of the industry.

 Over the years, the company has expanded its portfolio beyond residential projects, incorporating multifamily developments, commercial properties, luxury housing and mixed-use projects. Its presence extends from South Florida to Europe, reflecting an international vocation that dialogues with the global nature of contemporary real estate capital. This diversification not only expands its reach but also allows it to adapt to different economic and urban contexts, maintaining a coherent vision focused on long-term value creation.

 “My vision was simple but ambitious: to create a single company that controls all stages of the real estate life cycle: development, construction, financing, mortgages, brokerage, insurance, property management and asset protection, all under one roof. From the investor’s perspective, this means working with a single responsible operator that controls the entire value chain. There are no mutual accusations between suppliers. There are no information gaps between departments. There is one team, one standard, one result,” said Gevorg Shahbazyan, CEO from Starlife Group to Negocios magazine.
