# Vme Kids Welcomes Teletubbies, The Global Icon of Preschool Entertainment

MIAMI, FL — June 15, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Vme Kids, the network dedicated to preschool audiences, is proud to announce the arrival of Teletubbies to its lineup for the very first time. One of the most beloved and recognizable children’s franchises in the world, Teletubbies joins the network with both the original series that captivated millions of families and the new animated production, Teletubbies Let’s Go, offering parents and children the opportunity to share an experience that has transcended generations.

 Since its original debut, Teletubbies has become a global cultural phenomenon, specifically designed to support early childhood development through color, repetition, music, and imaginative play. Its addition to Vme Kids represents a strategic expansion of the network’s programming lineup, bringing a globally recognized brand with a strong emotional connection to parents and caregivers.

 “Bringing Teletubbies to Vme Kids is a significant moment for our audience,” said Doris Vogelmann, VP of Programming and Operations. “We are adding a franchise that has been part of the lives of millions of families around the world and continues to resonate with new generations. It is the perfect combination of nostalgia for parents and discovery for young children.”

 The arrival of Teletubbies reinforces Vme Kids’ commitment to providing Hispanic families with safe, educational, and entertaining content that encourages early learning and family connection.

 With this addition, Vme Kids continues to strengthen its position as a trusted destination for preschool content, offering programming designed to foster curiosity, language development, and early interaction in a safe and age-appropriate environment.

 Teletubbies premieres on Vme Kids on Monday, June 15, airing Monday through Sunday at 8:00 a.m. and 8:15 a.m., available through Comcast Xfinity and U-verse.

 For more information, visit www.vmekids.com or follow Vme Kids on social media.

 About Vme Kids

 Vme Kids, America’s first 24-hour, Spanish-language network broadcasting educational preschool programming from Vme Media, Inc.
