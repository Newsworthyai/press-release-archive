# Total Wireless Becomes First U.S. Carrier to Include Western Union Money Transfers in Select Phone Plans

NEW YORK, NY and DENVER, CO — June 25, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Total Wireless, a fast-growing, no-contract wireless service provider, powered by Verizon's 5G network, today announced an official partnership with Western Union to offer, for the first time in the industry, a money transfer benefit for its mobile customers. Starting today, customers with Total Wireless MAX 5G BYO, MAX 5G, and ALL ACCESS plans will receive a free monthly money transfer. With the ability to send funds to over 200 countries and territories through Western Union's global network, customers can stay connected in more ways than one.

This partnership sets a milestone in the industry. Total Wireless is now the only wireless provider in the United States offering a monthly money transfer as part of its plans. The benefit covers the transaction fee, providing real value to the customer base of both brands. It is also a new chapter for telecommunications, built around the financial realities of communities that have historically been underserved.

Designed for the way customers stay connectedMillions of people in the United States send money abroad each year, averaging more than 12 transactions annually. With Total Wireless, families save on transfer fees so every dollar goes further.

For millions of families, sending money home is one of the most profound ways to care for loved ones. Total Wireless was created to offer top-tier connectivity with benefits that reflect how its customers live and support each other.

“Total Wireless was built on the belief that a good wireless plan should do more for its customers,” said David Kim, president of Verizon Value. “Incorporating a Western Union money transfer into our plans reflects something we deeply believe: no one should have to choose between staying connected and caring for their loved ones. We are proud to be the first to build with that in mind.”

How it worksFor customers with Total Wireless MAX 5G BYO, MAX 5G, and ALL ACCESS plans, the ability to send money home is directly integrated into their plan. These plans range from $25 per month (MAX 5G BYO for customers who bring their own device) to $60 per month (ALL ACCESS) with auto-pay. Each includes top-tier features like unlimited 5G data, unlimited hotspot, and unlimited international calling and texting to over 200 countries. All backed by a five-year price guarantee that includes taxes and fees.

The Western Union benefit offers one more reason to choose a plan designed to stay connected with the people who matter most:

* One free money transfer per month, no fee: no transaction fee, seamlessly integrated as part of select premium plans.
* Easy access: available online at westernunion.com or through the Western Union app.
* Global reach: send money to family and friends in over 200 countries and territories.
* Trusted network: over 360,000 Western Union payout locations, with delivery to bank accounts, digital wallets, and cards, ensuring fast and reliable delivery to recipients worldwide.

“We know how much it means to send money home. We designed these plans to ensure that, at least once a month, it's on us,” said Kim.

Partnership with Western UnionThe partnership goes beyond the plan benefit, uniting two brands that serve communities relying on affordable connectivity with loved ones across borders. Western Union will co-promote Total Wireless through its own customer communication channels, including the Western Union app, email communications, social media, and in-store digital displays.

“For generations, Western Union has helped people move money across borders because we understand what each transfer represents: not just dollars, but our mission to make financial services accessible to everyone,” said Jesse Mory, senior vice president of Strategic Partnerships at Western Union. “By partnering with Total Wireless, we make it easier for customers to support those who depend on them, turning an everyday wireless plan into a more meaningful way to stay connected, care for loved ones, and move money with confidence.”

With a mission to raise the standard in prepaid wireless, Total Wireless offers more value than any other no-contract provider, with plans that include unlimited data and access to the Verizon 5G Ultra-Wideband network, prices guaranteed for five years (taxes and fees included), select free 5G phones with qualified plan purchases, and more.
