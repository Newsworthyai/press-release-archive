# Hand and Upper Extremity Care Specialist Dr. Alejandro Badia to Host Exclusive Consultation Meet &amp; Greet Event in Lima, Peru

[Lima, Peru – March 2026 /Noticias Newswire/] — Patients in Peru will have a unique opportunity to access world-class orthopedic expertise as Dr. Alejandro Badia, a renowned hand and upper extremity specialist, visits Lima to host an exclusive in-person consultation event.

 Titled “Exclusive Meet & Greet with Dr. Badia in Lima, Peru: Consult About the Health of Your Hands and Upper Extremity,” this event is designed for individuals experiencing pain, injuries, or chronic conditions affecting the hand, wrist, elbow, or shoulder, and who are seeking expert guidance on diagnosis and treatment options.

 Based in Miami, Florida, Dr. Badia is internationally recognized for his advanced techniques and minimally invasive approaches to treating conditions such as carpal tunnel syndrome, sports injuries, tendonitis, arthritis, and chronic joint pain.

 This exclusive event offers attendees the opportunity to:

 * Receive personalized guidance from a globally recognized specialist
* Discuss potential diagnoses and treatment options
* Learn about both surgical and non-surgical solutions
* Explore care options in the United States, if appropriate

 Event Details:

 * Date: Thursday, March 26, 2026
* Time: 4:00 – 6:00 PM
* Location: Lobby of the Swissôtel, Lima, Peru
* Schedule Your Appointment: +1 (305) 227-4263

 Dr. Badia emphasizes the importance of early evaluation:

 “Many hand and upper extremity conditions can be effectively treated when diagnosed early. These types of events allow us to guide patients and help prevent long-term complications.”![Dr Badia, Internationally renowned hand surgeon](https://www.noticiasnewswire.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/dr-badia-noticias-newswere.jpg)

 [Dr. Alejandro Badia](https://drbadia.com/alejandro-badia/) [Internationally renowned hand surgeon](https://drbadia.com/alejandro-badia/) Space is limited, and early scheduling is strongly recommended.

 For more information about the event, visit: https://drbadia.com/events/exclusive-meeting-in-lima-consult-dr-alejandro-badia-about-your-upper-extremity-health/

 About [Dr. Alejandro Badia](https://drbadia.com/): Is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in hand and upper extremity care. Based in Miami, Florida, he is the founder of Badia Hand to Shoulder Center and is internationally recognized for his innovative and minimally invasive treatment techniques.
