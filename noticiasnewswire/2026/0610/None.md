# Launch of ‘Miami es Mundial’: A New Platform Highlighting The Economic and Business Impact of Global Events in Miami

MIAMI, FL — June 10, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — With the world’s eyes on Miami as a key host city for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp. announces the official launch of Miami es Mundial. This new digital platform is designed to highlight the opportunities in business, investment, innovation, technology, luxury tourism, and corporate leadership that will accompany the arrival of millions of visitors to the region for the major global events taking place in Miami and South Florida.

 Available at www.miamiesmundial.com, the new portal launches with the mission of informing and connecting entrepreneurs, executives, investors, thought leaders, international figures, and organizations that will leverage the World Cup as a platform to build strategic relationships and commercial opportunities.

 “Miami will not merely be a stage for soccer matches; it will be a meeting point for business leaders, global investors, multinational companies, innovators, and decision-makers from around the world. Miami es Mundial seeks to tell that story and become a go-to resource for anyone wanting to stay informed about everything surrounding this economic and social phenomenon,” said Eduardo Hapke, co-founder and president of Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp.

 The platform will offer special coverage on:

 * Investments and economic development linked to the World Cup.
* International entrepreneurs and executives visiting Miami.
* Corporate events, conferences, and networking gatherings.
* Technology, artificial intelligence, and innovation.
* Luxury tourism and exclusive experiences.
* Real estate and investment opportunities.
* Prominent figures from sports, business, and politics.
* The economic impact of global events on Miami and South Florida. In addition to its website, Miami es Mundial will maintain an active presence on social media—specifically Instagram and Facebook—sharing news, exclusive interviews, videos, special reports, and real-time content regarding key events related to the World Cup and its regional impact.

 The 2026 FIFA World Cup represents a historic opportunity for Miami and South Florida. The event is expected to draw hundreds of thousands of international visitors, corporate executives, investors, sponsors, and media representatives from around the globe, cementing Miami’s status as a premier international hub for business, innovation, and tourism.

 About Miami es Mundial

 Miami es Mundial is a digital platform specializing in news, business, investment, innovation, technology, tourism, and lifestyle. It was created to report on the economic, business, and social impact of the 2026 FIFA World Cup on Miami and South Florida.

 The platform is part of Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp., a Florida-based publishing and media group that owns various publications and digital platforms catering to the business, executive, and international communities. Its portfolio includes brands such as Negocios Magazine, Ejecutiva Magazine, Hispanic Time, Welcome to America, Salud Florida Magazine, and Electric Cars Plus, alongside other specialized communication and event projects.

 Through its print and digital media, social channels, and corporate events, Acta Diurna Urbis Group Corp. connects entrepreneurs, investors, executives, community leaders, and decision-makers, fostering business opportunities and strengthening ties between the United States, Latin America, and international markets.
