# Vme TV Renews Longstanding Partnership with Maryland Public Television to Continue Bringing MotorWeek to Hispanic Audiences

MIAMI, FL — July, 9 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Vme TV announced today the renewal of its longstanding agreement with Maryland Public Television (MPT) to continue airing MotorWeek, the award-winning automotive series that has been part of the network’s programming since its launch nearly twenty years ago.

 The renewed partnership ensures that Spanish-speaking audiences across the United States will continue to enjoy one of television’s most trusted automotive programs, featuring expert vehicle reviews, industry news, consumer buying advice, and coverage of the latest developments shaping the future of transportation.

 “MotorWeek has been part of the Vme TV family since day one,” said Michael Fernandez, Vice President of Marketing & People at Vme Media. “As we prepare to celebrate Vme TV’s 20th anniversary in 2027, we’re proud to continue our partnership with Maryland Public Television and bring our viewers a program that has earned the trust of generations of drivers, car enthusiasts and consumers alike. This renewal reflects our continued commitment to delivering informative, high-quality programming that serves the interests and everyday lives of Hispanic audiences.”

 John Davis, Creator and Host of MotorWeek added:MotorWeek’s nearly two-decade relationship with Vme TV fulfills a long-held personal desire of mine to serve Hispanic audiences—a desire that dates back to when the series began in 1981. Vme allows MotorWeek a far broader reach than would ever have been possible on our own. Through Vme TV, MotorWeek is able to deliver money-saving automotive advice to the fastest-growing consumer group in the economy. As Vme TV approaches its 20th anniversary, we congratulate everyone at the network and are thrilled that MotorWeek is still along for the ride.”

 Since premiering in 1981, MotorWeek has become one of the most respected and enduring automotive programs on American television, offering in-depth vehicle road tests, technology updates and consumer guidance that keep pace with a rapidly evolving industry. Its continued place on Vme TV complements the network’s broader mission of delivering educational, informative and lifestyle-focused content to Hispanic audiences nationwide.

 MotorWeek airs nationwide on Vme TV. For local listings and additional programming information, visit www.vmetv.com.

 About VME Television Vme TV is a premiere national Spanish language television network that provides a quality alternative to Latino families by selecting programming that is engaging, empowering, educational and entertaining. Vme is available in millions of households in the United States, distributed via Charter Spectrum, DIRECTV, & AT&T U-verse. The 24-hour digital broadcast service is dedicated to entertaining, educating and inspiring families in Spanish with a contemporary mix of original productions, exclusive premieres, acquisitions, and popular television programs specially adapted for Hispanics.
