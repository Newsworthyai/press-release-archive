# Primo TV Powers up 2026 with 500+ Hours of New Shows

MIAMI, FL — January 27, 2026 — (NOTICIAS NEWSWIRE) — Primo TV, the network dedicated to kids and tweens ages 6–16, is kicking off 2026 with a bold refresh — unveiling a new on-air look, an updated programming grid, and hundreds of hours of brand-new programming across animation, live action, anime and STEM-based series, designed to entertain, educate, and inspire.

 In January, Primo TV’s refreshed screen will feature a dynamic lineup of new animated programming, including fan-favorite franchises and high-energy adventure series. The network will further strengthen its anime offering with key titles such as Bakugan, reinforcing Primo TV’s commitment to delivering exciting, age-appropriate content for kids and tweens.

 Continuing its promise to offer fun, inspirational, and educational edutainment, Primo TV expands its slate of nature- and STEM-focused programming with titles such as Brain Buzz and Earth Science Investigators. These series are designed to spark curiosity, encourage discovery, and support learning through engaging storytelling.

 Educational curiosity takes center stage with engaging series like Wow! That Is Amazing, while humor and imagination shine through titles such as Kaeloo and You’re Called What? — reinforcing Primo TV’s commitment to learning through entertainment.

 Our 2026 programming refresh brings together the best of animation, live action, anime, and STEM-based content,” said Doris Vogelmann, Vice President of Programming and Operations at Vme Media. “We’re proud to continue offering programming that entertains, inspires curiosity, and gives families content they can trust.”

 With its refreshed visual identity and expanded lineup, Primo TV continues to strengthen its position as a trusted destination for edutainment, offering content that balances excitement, creativity, and discovery — all in a safe, friendly environment.

 Primo TV is currently available on the Comcast Xfinity platform.

 About Primo TV Primo TV is the first English language network targeting U.S. bicultural Hispanic Gen Z viewers (6-16) and their families with inspirational and educational programming. Owned and operated by V-me Media, Inc., Primo TV offers culturally relevant programming in English, appealing to parents as a way to keep their kids culturally engaged with their Latino roots. Primo TV is currently available nationally on Comcast Xfinity. For more information please visit www.primotv.com or follow us on social media via www.facebook.com/primotelevision/ or on Instagram @primotv.
