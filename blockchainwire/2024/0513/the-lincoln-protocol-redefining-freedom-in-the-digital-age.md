The Lincoln Protocol, a newly launched ERC20 token, emerges as a beacon of hope and liberation in the realm of decentralized finance. Inspired by the visionary ideals of Abraham Lincoln, this groundbreaking project seeks to revolutionize the way business contracts, settlements, and litigation are constructed, paving the way for a future where power and freedom belong to the people.

The Early Vision and Emerging Momentum:

The vision for The Lincoln Protocol was first introduced nearly two years ago through a Medium post and has been carefully honed since. Unveiling The Lincoln Protocol:

Rooted in the ethos of emancipation and equality, The Lincoln Protocol embodies the spirit of Abraham Lincoln, championing fairness, transparency, and justice for all.
