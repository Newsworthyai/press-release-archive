Ethereum ETF Approval Is Soon: What Now?The current price of Ethereum (ETH) stands at approximately $3,537.89, reflecting a remarkable 15% increase since yesterday. Following the approval of spot Bitcoin ETFs by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), attention has turned to applications for spot Ethereum ETFs. This week marks a crucial period for spot Ethereum ETF decisions. The SEC, which has consistently postponed evaluations of spot ETH ETF applications, is expected to announce decisions on two applications this week. VanEck's application will be addressed on May 23, while ARK Invest's application will be decided on May 24. While these two companies await decisions, numerous other firms, including BlackRock and Grayscale, have submitted spot ETH ETF applications. Although the SEC's decisions this week are limited to two applications, they are anticipated to provide insight into future rulings. However, experts in the cryptocurrency and ETF fields are less optimistic about spot Ethereum ETFs than spot Bitcoin ETFs. SEC Chairman Gary Gensler's cautious stance towards cryptocurrencies and Ethereum being perceived more as security than a commodity strengthens the possibility of rejecting these applications. Additionally, Ethereum lacks the same level of political support as Bitcoin, further bolstering this notion.
