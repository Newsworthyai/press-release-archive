# Tokenize! Austin Conference Caps Off Consensus Week with 24 Company Presentations and Key Speakers in AI, Real World Assets (RWA), Tokenized Finance and Gaming

What: Tokenize! Austin takes place across two days at the Capital Factory, walking distance from the Austin Convention Center. The conference gathers leaders in the bitcoin, crypto and blockchain investing sectors to hear keynotes and panels by industry leaders, plus company presentations, parties and meals.

When: BitAngels company presentations, Friday, May 31, 1:30 - 7 p.m. Tokenize! Austin, Saturday, June 1, 8:30 am - 6 pm, followed by Legendary BBQ, 7 - 10 pm.

Where: All events at Capital Factory, 701 Brazos St., other than Legendary BBQ at a private estate.

Cost: BitAngels presentation passes are free for accredited investors. Full-conference passes begin at $1,499 for both days, including all meals and parties. For more information, please visit https://tokenizecon.com
