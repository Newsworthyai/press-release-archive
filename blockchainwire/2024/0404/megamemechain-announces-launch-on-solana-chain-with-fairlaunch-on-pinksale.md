# MegaMemeChain Announces Launch on Solana Chain with FairLaunch on PinkSale

To ensure the utmost security for our community, MegaMemeChain introduces five badges: KYC, Doxxed, Audit, SAFU, and AMA tags. These badges signify our commitment to transparency, accountability, and the safety of our investors' funds.Incubated by BrandB.org, a renowned name in the blockchain industry, MegaMemeChain benefits from the expertise and support of a trusted partner.
