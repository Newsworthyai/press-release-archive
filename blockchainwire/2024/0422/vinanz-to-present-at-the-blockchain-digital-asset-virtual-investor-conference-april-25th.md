# Vinanz to Present at the Blockchain & Digital Asset Virtual Investor Conference April 25th

Vinanz Limited (OTCQB: VINZF and AQSE: BTC), the London listed Bitcoin mining company focusing on decentralised deployment of Bitcoin mining clusters in multiple data facilities throughout the US and Canada, today announced that the Company’s Executive Chairman, Mr. David Lenigas (“Chairman”), will present live at the Blockchain & Digital Asset Virtual Investor Conference hosted by Alpha Transform Holdings and VirtualInvestorConferences.com, on April 25th , 2024.DATE: April 25thTIME: 10am ETLINK: [https://bit.ly/3JaIRj3](https://bit.ly/3JaIRj3)Available for 1x1 meetings: April 25thThis will be a live, interactive online event where investors are invited to ask the Company questions inreal-time.
