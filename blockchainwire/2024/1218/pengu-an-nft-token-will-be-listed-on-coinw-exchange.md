# PENGU, an NFT Token, Will Be Listed on CoinW Exchange

CoinW, the world's pioneering cryptocurrency trading platform, will list PENGU, an NFT token, on Meme Zone, Solana Zone and NFT Zone. For all CoinW users, the PENGU/USDT will be officially available for trading on December 17th 2024, at 14:00 (UTC). To celebrate the listing of PENGU, we are launching the “ PENGU Bounty Program" event with a reward pool of 10,000 USDT.
