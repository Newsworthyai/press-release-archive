# BlockyPro unveils groundbreaking innovations at Global Tech Conference and initiates global expansion

Recently, at the Global Tech Conference held in Silicon Valley, BlockyPro, a leading global digital asset trading platform, showcased its latest technological achievements to the world and announced the launch of its global expansion plan. These developments not only highlight BlockyPro's innovative strength in the fintech field but also demonstrate its ambition in the global market.

During the conference, BlockyPro unveiled its newly developed cryptocurrency trading system, which utilizes advanced distributed architecture and microservices technology, achieving outstanding performance by processing over 3 million transactions per second. This achievement signifies a significant breakthrough for BlockyPro in handling high-frequency and large-scale transaction data.
