What is a Roadmap in Crypto?

A roadmap serves as the backbone of any cryptocurrency project; The document delineates a strategic, step-by-step blueprint encompassing the objectives, milestones, goals, and anticipated outcomes of a cryptocurrency project. Serving as a structured checklist, roadmaps furnish detailed insights into a project's aspirations and the timeline set to achieve them. Frequently accompanying decentralised finance (DeFi), non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and various other crypto ventures, roadmaps offer stakeholders visibility into the project's trajectory and direction.

Additionally, the document serves as a mechanism to ensure accountability within the project team, providing a framework against which progress can be measured. While roadmaps offer a structured plan, they remain adaptable, allowing teams the flexibility to revise strategies as needed to better align with project objectives.
