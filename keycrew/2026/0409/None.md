# Why the Mississippi Gulf Coast Is One of America’s Best-Kept Real Estate Secrets

For years, real estate investors and homebuyers have overlooked the Mississippi Gulf Coast in favor of flashier markets. Jon Lester, Head of Growth and Operations at Home Buyer Mississippi, is here to change that narrative. After years of sharpening his skills in one of the country’s most demanding real estate markets in New Orleans, Jon turned his attention to coastal Mississippi, and what he found surprised even him.

 “The American dream is still alive and well there,” Jon says. “You can buy a three-bed, two-bath house within blocks of the coast for under $200,000. That is nearly impossible to find anywhere else right now.”

 While much of the country stalled under rising interest rates and post-pandemic uncertainty, the Mississippi Gulf Coast kept moving. Inventory stayed tight at around two and a half months, compared to nine months of active inventory in New Orleans at the same time. Buyers were still buying. Sellers were still selling. The market had a momentum that was hard to ignore.

 Jon credits a combination of affordability and lifestyle as the driving force. Coastal Mississippi offers what many sun-seekers are looking for, namely fresh seafood, a laid-back pace, and beach access, at a fraction of the price of comparable coastal markets in Florida or Alabama. The region also benefits from what Jon describes as a proximity advantage. It sits just over an hour from New Orleans, close enough to draw buyers who love the culture and energy of the city but want an easier, more affordable life.

 What Jon also discovered on the ground was that the Gulf Coast is not one single market. Each town has its own personality and its own investment profile. Bay St. Louis carries the most New Orleans influence and is the entry point from the west. Pass Christian attracts an older, more established buyer. Long Beach, where Jon purchased and renovated his first Mississippi property, offers a sweet spot of tradition and accessibility. “I was able to buy, renovate, and bring a house in Long Beach back to market at $149,000,” Jon says. “You just don’t see that in many parts of the country right now. When you can deliver a finished product at that price point near the coast, you know it’s going to move. Ours went under contract in six days.” Gulfport, the largest city on the coast, has lower price points and higher distress inventory, with a major downtown revitalization project underway. Biloxi brings the energy, the casinos, and a younger crowd.

 Understanding these distinctions matters. Jon did not learn them from a spreadsheet. He learned them by showing up, driving through neighborhoods, talking to locals, and asking questions. That boots-on-the-ground knowledge now informs every deal Home Buyer Mississippi pursues.

 For homeowners along the coast who are sitting on a property they are unsure what to do with, Jon and the team at Home Buyer Mississippi offer a straightforward, pressure-free process. Whether the property needs work, has legal complications, or has been sitting vacant for years, Home Buyer Mississippi has the experience and the local network to find a solution.

 The Mississippi Gulf Coast is no longer a secret to those paying attention. Home Buyer Mississippi is ready to help sellers and buyers make the most of it.

 About Home Buyer Mississippi Home Buyer Mississippi helps homeowners across MS sell properties that are difficult, unwanted, or simply no longer make sense to keep. From inherited homes and vacant properties to houses in need of major repairs, the team specializes in situations where a traditional sale can be slow, uncertain, or overwhelming. The company purchases homes directly, in as-is condition, with no repairs, no commissions, and no pressure.

 Home Buyer Mississippi is led locally by Jon Lester, who is actively on the ground across markets like Gulfport, Biloxi, and Bay St. Louis. His hands-on approach and understanding of each submarket allow the team to assess properties accurately and create solutions that reflect real market conditions, not generic formulas.

 The company is built on systems and experience developed through Home Buyer Louisiana, where founder Stephen Keighery and his team have completed hundreds of transactions since 2019, including many involving complex title, probate, and distressed property scenarios. That operational foundation now supports Home Buyer Mississippi’s growth.
