# Yitzchak Pierson Named a RealTrends Verified Agent for 2025 Production

National, independently verified ranking recognizes New Braunfels broker’s closed transaction volume

 NEW BRAUNFELS, TX – July, 2026 – Yitzchak Pierson, Broker Associate with eXp Realty, has been named a RealTrends Verified agent based on his 2025 production, a national, independently verified ranking of real estate professionals by closed transaction volume.

 For 2025, Pierson closed 63 transactions totaling more than $13.23 million in sales volume, ranking him #55 in Texas and #1,180 nationally for transaction sides.

 Unlike locally voted recognitions, RealTrends Verified rankings are based on independently confirmed closed-sale data, making the distinction a widely referenced benchmark within the real estate industry for measuring production at the individual agent level.

 Pierson, who serves buyers, sellers, and investors along the I-35 corridor between Austin and San Antonio with a focus on New Braunfels, Canyon Lake, San Marcos, and Seguin, said the recognition reflects the volume of transactions he closed for clients navigating the submarket’s shifting resale and new construction conditions over the past year.

 The RealTrends Verified recognition adds to a list of honors Pierson has received in 2026, including being voted Best Real Estate Agent in New Braunfels for a third consecutive year, named a 2026 PT500 REALTOR® by Platinum Top 500 San Antonio, a Top 250 eXp Realty agent nationally, a second-consecutive eXp Realty ICON Agent, and #33 on the San Antonio Business Journal’s Top 100 Residential Real Estate Agents list.

 About Yitzchak Pierson: Yitzchak Pierson Real Estate is a licensed real estate broker associate with eXp Realty (License #751573) serving buyers, sellers, and investors along the I-35 corridor between Austin and San Antonio, with a focus on New Braunfels, Canyon Lake, San Marcos, and Seguin. He serves on the Board of Directors for the Four Rivers Association of REALTORS® and the Texas REALTORS® Professional Standards Committee.

 About RealTrends Verified: RealTrends Verified is a national ranking system that independently verifies closed transaction data submitted by real estate professionals, providing a standardized measure of production used across the residential real estate industry.
