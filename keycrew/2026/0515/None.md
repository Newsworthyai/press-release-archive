# KeyCrew Media Names Daryl Judy as Verified Expert in DC Luxury Real Estate

Washington, DC, May 14, 2026 – KeyCrew Media, a real estate analytics and media network, has selected Daryl Judy, Associate Broker at Washington Fine Properties, as a KeyCrew Verified Expert. Judy will contribute exclusive market intelligence and expert analysis on luxury residential real estate across Washington, DC and the broader DMV region, including McLean, Arlington, Alexandria, Chevy Chase, Bethesda, and Middleburg, VA.

 KeyCrew Verified Experts are carefully selected as prolific market trend authorities who demonstrate exceptional insight and expertise in their fields. These distinguished professionals regularly contribute market insights, expert perspectives, and forward-looking analysis to help audiences navigate complex industry landscapes.

 Daryl Judy brings rare, boots-on-the-ground intelligence to the KeyCrew platform. Based on MLS data, Judy is on track to rank among the top individual agents in Washington, DC by sales volume, a distinction he has built through deep market knowledge, an extensive off-market network, and a reputation for representing the region’s most exceptional properties. His listings include the penthouse at 1711 14th Street NW, described as one of the most architecturally significant and exclusive residences currently available in the District, which was featured as a story of the day in the Washington Business Journal.

 Judy’s coverage area spans the full DMV luxury market, from Georgetown and Logan Circle to McLean, where record sale after record sale is reshaping the $5M–$9M price tier, and he brings the kind of real-time transactional intelligence that does not appear in public data or industry reports. He is also recognised for his expertise in the nuances of DC’s historic preservation landscape, off-market trading at the top of the market, and the lifestyle factors that drive high-net-worth buyers to specific neighbourhoods and communities.

 “The luxury market in DC and the DMV is moving fast, and the buyers and sellers I work with deserve to have someone at the table who really knows what’s happening, not just what’s showing up in the headlines,” said Daryl Judy. “I’m glad to have a platform to share what I’m seeing on the ground and help people make better decisions at the top of the market.”

 Judy’s areas of expertise include:

 * DC Luxury Residential Market: Exclusive transaction intelligence across Washington, DC’s most competitive luxury neighbourhoods, including Georgetown, Logan Circle, Dupont Circle, and Capitol Hill
* McLean & Northern Virginia Luxury: Deep knowledge of the DMV’s hottest sub-market, where new construction demand, strong school systems, and Virginia tax advantages are driving record prices in the $5M–$9M+ range
* Off-Market Transactions & Access: Extensive relationships that provide buyers and sellers access to properties that never reach the open market, a critical advantage at the ultra-luxury tier
* Luxury Pricing Strategy & Buyer Psychology: Insight into the herd mentality dynamics, ‘PLU’ (people like us) neighbourhood selection, and friction-reduction priorities that define how high-net-worth buyers actually make decisions
* Historic Preservation & DC Architecture: Specialist knowledge of DC’s strict preservation rules and what they mean for buyers and sellers in one of America’s most architecturally significant cities
