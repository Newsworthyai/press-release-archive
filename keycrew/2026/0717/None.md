# KeyCrew Media Names Golden Hays Group’s Rhoni Golden as Verified Expert in Inner-Loop Dallas Residential Real Estate

DALLAS, TX, July 15, 2026. KeyCrew Media, a real estate analytics and media network, has selected Rhoni Golden, Co-Founder of Golden Hays Group at Dave Perry Miller Real Estate, as a KeyCrew Verified Expert. Golden will contribute market intelligence and expert analysis on inner-loop Dallas residential real estate, neighborhood selection, and the buyer experience across Lakewood, East Dallas, Lower Greenville, Preston Hollow, and the Park Cities.

 KeyCrew Verified Experts are carefully selected as prolific market trend authorities who demonstrate exceptional insight and expertise in their fields. These distinguished professionals regularly contribute market insights, expert perspectives, and forward-looking analysis to help audiences navigate complex industry landscapes.

 Rhoni Golden brings deep local knowledge and a hands-on perspective on the inner-loop Dallas market to the KeyCrew platform. As Co-Founder of Golden Hays Group, an all-women luxury residential team at Dave Perry Miller Real Estate, she works the neighborhoods in the 635 loop daily, advising buyers and sellers on the block-by-block distinctions that shape value in one of Dallas’s most sought-after markets.

 Golden’s work is defined by a focus on neighborhood nuance. Rather than treating inner-loop Dallas as a single market, she guides clients through the micro-neighborhoods that make each area distinct, from the historic architecture and winding streets of deep Lakewood to the walkable energy of Lower Greenville and the school-anchored demand of the Park Cities. This approach is especially valuable for buyers relocating from other cities, who often arrive with assumptions built elsewhere and need local context to choose where to land.

 “So much of what I do comes down to helping people find the right neighborhood, not just the right house,” said Rhoni Golden. “Inside the loop, a quarter mile in any direction can mean a different school, a different feel, a different way of life. Buyers coming from out of town often can’t see that from a map or a price-per-square-foot number. My job is to help them understand it before they commit.”

 Golden’s areas of expertise include:

 * Inner-Loop Dallas Neighborhood Expertise: Street-level knowledge of Lakewood, East Dallas, Lower Greenville, Preston Hollow, and the Park Cities, including the micro-neighborhood distinctions that shape value in the 635 loop.
* Out-of-State and Coastal Buyer Guidance: Helping buyers relocating from New York, California, and the Northeast understand how inner-loop Dallas differs from the markets they know.
* Neighborhood Matching: Aligning buyers with the right neighborhood for their lifestyle, budget, and stage of life, from first homes to luxury moves.
* School Access and Pricing Dynamics: How school rankings and reputation function as a pricing anchor and a driver of demand throughout Dallas.
* Green Space and Lifestyle Value: The role of lot sizes, walkability, and access to green space in shaping demand and pricing across inner-loop Dallas.
