# Brera Holdings (NASDAQ: BREA) Showcases Grassroots Football Spirit with FENIX Trophy Documentary

Brera Holdings (NASDAQ: BREA) announced the screening of The Amazing Adventures of Mr. Fenix (in the Lands of European Non-Professional Football) at the OFFSIDE FOOTBALL FILM FESTIVAL 2024 in Milan. The documentary, which celebrates the FENIX Trophy’s mission to honor non-professional football clubs with cultural and community significance, was followed by a discussion featuring co-founder Leonardo Aleotti and director Gigi Mammana. Highlighting the competition’s focus on grassroots football, the film explores how the FENIX Trophy provides an alternative to commercialized tournaments, showcasing smaller clubs’ dedication to social impact, fair play, and local pride, with the official endorsement of UEFA.
