# InvestorNewsBreaks – Fathom Nickel Inc. (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) to Present Project Developments in Upcoming Live Webinar with Red Cloud Financial Services

Fathom Nickel (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) will host a live virtual corporate update with Red Cloud Financial Services on Nov. 6, 2024, at 2 p.m. ET. CEO and VP of Exploration will provide insights on the company’s advancements at the Gochager Lake and Albert Lake projects, focusing on nickel, copper, cobalt, and platinum group elements (PGEs). The presentation will include a live Q&A session, and a replay will be available to all registrants via email and on Red Cloud’s website.

 To view the full press release, visit https://ibn.fm/iSd0m

 About Fathom Nickel Inc.

 Fathom is a junior exploration company focused on exploring for and developing world class nickel camps in the province of Saskatchewan.
