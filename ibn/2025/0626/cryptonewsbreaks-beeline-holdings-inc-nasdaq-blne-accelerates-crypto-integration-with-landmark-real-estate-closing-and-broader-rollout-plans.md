# CryptoNewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Accelerates Crypto Integration with Landmark Real Estate Closing and Broader Rollout Plans

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE) has successfully executed a residential real estate transaction using a crypto token backed by real estate—via its subsidiary Beeline Title Holdings, Inc.—marking a major milestone in the convergence of blockchain and mortgage finance. With deeper rollout plans in place, including a crypto-driven fractional equity product set to launch in August 2025, Beeline is building a future-ready infrastructure for lenders seeking secure, compliant pathways into the emerging world of crypto-funded real estate.

 About Beeline Financial Holdings, Inc.

 Beeline Financial Holdings, Inc. is a trailblazing mortgage fintech transforming the way people access property financing. Headquartered in Providence, Rhode Island, Beeline is reshaping mortgage origination with speed, simplicity, and transparency at its core.
