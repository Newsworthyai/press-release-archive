# RockBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) Identifies Dual Gold Trends at Globex’s Duquesne West Property

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH), in partnership with Globex Mining Enterprises Inc. (TSX: GMX), reported a revised geological interpretation at the 50% Globex-owned Duquesne West Gold Property in Quebec’s Abitibi region. By compiling historical and recent drill results, Emperor has identified two plunge directions of high-grade gold mineralization—one trending easterly, supported by multiple drill holes, and another trending westerly. These structural insights will inform a new resource calculation expected in the coming weeks. The analysis and release were led by Globex CEO Jack Stoch, P.Geo., as a Qualified Person under NI 43-101 and approved by Emperor Metals.
