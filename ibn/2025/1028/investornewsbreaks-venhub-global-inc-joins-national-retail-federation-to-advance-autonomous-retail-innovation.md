# InvestorNewsBreaks – VenHub Global, Inc. Joins National Retail Federation to Advance Autonomous Retail Innovation

VenHub Global, a developer of fully autonomous Smart Store technology, today announced it has joined the National Retail Federation (NRF) as an Industry Partner Member. As part of the world’s largest retail trade association, VenHub gains access to NRF research, councils, and industry forums to share its expertise in automation and data-driven merchandising. “Joining NRF marks an exciting step for VenHub,” said Shahan Ohanessian, CEO. “As part of the retail community’s largest network, we can exchange ideas, showcase our autonomous store platform, and learn from industry leaders.” VenHub’s Smart Stores integrate robotics, automation, and mobile-first technology for a fully contactless, 24/7 shopping experience. The company plans to participate in NRF events, including Retail’s Big Show, to demonstrate its latest innovations in autonomous retail.

 About VenHub

 VenHub designs and builds autonomous Smart Stores that operate 24/7 without on-site staff. Each store combines robotic automation, real-time inventory tracking, and mobile-based checkout to provide secure, convenient retail access. The company is headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada.
