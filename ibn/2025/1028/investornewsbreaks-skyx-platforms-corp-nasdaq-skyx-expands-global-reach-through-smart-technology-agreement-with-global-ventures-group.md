# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Expands Global Reach Through Smart Technology Agreement With Global Ventures Group

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX), doing business as SKYX Technologies, announced an agreement with Chicago-based Global Ventures Group to deploy its advanced smart home and building technologies across residential, commercial, and hotel projects in the Middle East, including developments in Saudi Arabia and Egypt. The collaboration supports SKYX’s global expansion strategy and mission to make homes and buildings smarter, safer, and more connected. SKYX expects to supply hundreds of thousands of units of its plug & play smart products, including ceiling lighting, fans, recessed and wall lights, EXIT and emergency lights, and LED mirrors. Global Ventures Group, led by Founder and CEO Randall Langer, will integrate SKYX’s technologies to enhance safety, convenience, and design standards throughout the region.
