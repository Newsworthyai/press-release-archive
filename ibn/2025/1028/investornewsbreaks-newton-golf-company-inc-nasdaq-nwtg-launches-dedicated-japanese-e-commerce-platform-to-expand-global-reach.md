# InvestorNewsBreaks – Newton Golf Company Inc. (NASDAQ: NWTG) Launches Dedicated Japanese E-Commerce Platform to Expand Global Reach

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG), a leading innovator in performance-driven golf equipment, today announced the launch of its dedicated Japanese e-commerce site, www.newtongolf.jp. The new platform enables golfers in Japan to purchase Newton’s performance-engineered products directly, marking a key milestone in the company’s global expansion. With golf participation rising across Japan, the site offers localized access to Newton’s advanced technologies, including its proprietary DOT System(TM) within Newton Motion Shafts and soon-to-launch Gravity Putters, designed to enhance precision, balance, and performance for golfers at every level.
