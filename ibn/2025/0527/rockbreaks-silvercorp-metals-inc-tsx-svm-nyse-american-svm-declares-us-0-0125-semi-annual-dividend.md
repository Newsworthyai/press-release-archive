# RockBreaks – Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) Declares US$0.0125 Semi-Annual Dividend

Silvercorp Metals (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM)  announced its Board of Directors has declared a semi-annual dividend of US$0.0125 per share, payable on or before June 26 to shareholders of record as of June 6. The dividend is designated as an eligible dividend for Canadian tax purposes. The company noted that future dividends will be determined at the Board’s discretion based on market conditions, financial performance, and other relevant factors.

 About Silvercorp

 Silvercorp is a Canadian mining company producing silver, gold, lead, and zinc with a long history of profitability and growth potential. The Company’s strategy is to create shareholder value by 1) focusing on generating free cash flow from long life mines; 2) organic growth through extensive drilling for discovery; 3) ongoing merger and acquisition efforts to unlock value; and 4) long term commitment to responsible mining and ESG.
