# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) to Join Russell 2000 and 3000 Indexes Following 2025 Reconstitution

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX) announced it will be added to the Russell 2000® and Russell 3000® indexes following the 2025 annual reconstitution, with inclusion effective after the market opens on June 27. The company, which holds more than 97 issued and pending patents worldwide and operates over 60 lighting and home décor websites, sees the move as a key milestone in raising institutional visibility. Co-CEO Lenny Sokolow stated the inclusion reflects SKYX’s strategic progress and will support broader investor awareness as it advances its smart and safe home technology mission.
