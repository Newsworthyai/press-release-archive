# MiningNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Advances Toward Production With Delivery of Gravity Separation Circuit

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) announced the successful delivery of Humphrey spiral concentrators to its Montauban site in Quebec, completing the full gravity separation circuit essential for near-term production. The system will process up to 1,000 tonnes per day of historic tailings into a marketable mica concentrate while also recovering gold and silver. CEO Paul Mastantuono described the milestone as a tangible step toward ESGold’s transition to producer status, with construction progressing and first production targeted by year-end 2025. An updated preliminary economic assessment is expected soon.
