# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Reports Record Q3 Revenue and Expands Global Smart-Building Deployments

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX), reported record Q3 2025 revenue of $24 million, an 8 percent sequential gross profit increase to $8 million, 32 percent gross margin, $13 million in cash and receivables, and additional $5 million financing from lead shareholders as the Company advances toward cash flow positivity exiting 2025, extending $11 million in notes to 2030 while accelerating large-scale deployments of its advanced and smart plug and play technologies through new agreements with Global Ventures Group for tens of thousands of Middle East units, a $3 billion Miami smart city project requiring more than 500,000 units, a 278-unit Texas development, and retail and pro-segment collaborations with Home Depot, Wayfair and Parrot Uncle, supported by a U.S. manufacturing partnership with Profab Electronics and continued adoption from Marriott-affiliated investors; SKYX’s safety code standardization efforts continue gaining regulatory momentum, its AI-driven e-commerce upgrade targets a 30 percent conversion lift, and management highlights a U.S. TAM of roughly $500 billion across 4.2 billion ceiling applications.
