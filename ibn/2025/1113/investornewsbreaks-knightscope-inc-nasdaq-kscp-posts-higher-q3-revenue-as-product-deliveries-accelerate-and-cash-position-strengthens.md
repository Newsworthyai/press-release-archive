# InvestorNewsBreaks – Knightscope, Inc. (NASDAQ: KSCP) Posts Higher Q3 Revenue as Product Deliveries Accelerate and Cash Position Strengthens

Knightscope (NASDAQ: KSCP) reported Q3 2025 revenue of $3.1 million, up from $2.5 million last year, driven by stronger product deliveries after prior-quarter material shortages, while gross loss widened to $1.6 million due to a $0.6 million non-cash inventory write-off tied to its transition to a new Sunnyvale headquarters and manufacturing facility; operating expenses rose 10 percent to $7.9 million with continued investment in next-generation autonomous systems, net loss improved to $9.5 million from $10.9 million, and cash reached $20.4 million, a $15.2 million year-over-year increase.
