# InvestorNewsBreaks – EDERRA Launches Crowdfunding Campaign to Scale Functional Wellness Movement

EDERRA, the wellness brand behind EMPWR+—a first-of-its-kind supplement combining broccoli microgreens and Lion’s Mane mushroom—has launched a $250,000 crowdfunding campaign on Wefunder. The campaign invites wellness advocates and early-stage investors to join the company’s mission to disrupt the $150 billion supplement market with a transparent, farm-to-body approach. In less than a year, EDERRA has secured distribution with top California retailers, partnered with chefs and spas, and achieved 65% repeat revenue. Funds will support national expansion, production scaling, and further innovation in functional nutrition and wellness gifting. Investors can join with as little as $2,500 under a 10% discount SAFE, with potential perks ranging from product bundles to immersive wellness experiences.

 About EDERRA

 Founded in San Diego, EDERRA is the first farm-to-body supplement brand focused on purity, science and performance. Its flagship product, EMPWR+, combines fresh broccoli microgreens and Lion’s Mane mushrooms to support cognitive health, inflammation reduction, and longevity. EDERRA is committed to radical transparency, sustainable sourcing, and building a future where food is both functional and deeply nourishing.
