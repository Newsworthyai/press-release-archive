# TechMediaBreaks – ONAR Holding Corporation (OTCQB: ONAR) to Attend Cannes Lions 2025, Sponsor UNLISTED Industry Event

ONAR (OTCQB: ONAR), a global marketing technology company and agency network, announced its participation in the Cannes Lions 2025 Festival, where CEO Claude Zdanow and Board Member Jon Bond will engage with industry leaders to drive new partnerships and brand visibility. As part of its presence, ONAR is sponsoring UNLISTED, a Day Zero event hosted by experiential agency Super Great Fantastic, bringing together marketers, creatives, and brand teams. With a team of nearly 50 professionals across five continents, ONAR continues to expand its AI-driven, integrated marketing services for the middle market, fueling its growth and acquisition strategy.
