# InvestorNewsBreaks – ECGI Holdings Inc. (OTC: ECGI) Secures Multi-Million-Dollar, Five-Year Facility Services Contract in Los Angeles

ECGI Holdings (OTC: ECGI) announced it has signed a multi-million-dollar, five-year contract to provide facility services at a Los Angeles commercial property, marking a key milestone in its shift from restructuring to recurring revenue generation. The agreement establishes a stable income stream, strengthens the company’s balance sheet, and supports scalable, technology-integrated growth as ECGI oversees maintenance, repairs, and inspections while advancing additional initiatives aimed at sustainable, long-term expansion.

 About ECGI Holdings

 ECGI Holdings, Inc. (OTC: ECGI) is a technology-driven investment and development company focused on building innovative, technology-enabled businesses with sustainable, long-term revenue models.  Through active partnerships, strategic investments, and disciplined development, ECGI leverages emerging technologies to unlock new revenue opportunities and position its portfolio for accelerated growth and broader market visibility.
