# TinyGemsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Initiates CUSIP Change to Bolster Shareholder Protections Amid Rebrand

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), formerly Lottery.com, announced a CUSIP number change as part of its corporate rebrand and ongoing efforts to combat illicit trading activity. The move aims to enhance market transparency and counter suspected naked short selling that has impacted trading. Now trading under the SEGG ticker, the company is working with counsel at Paul Hastings LLP to pursue regulatory action while advancing its global expansion in sports, entertainment, and gaming.
