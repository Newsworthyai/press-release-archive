# InvestorNewsBreaks – AI Maverick Intel, Inc. (OTCID: BINP) Engages IBN to Lead Corporate Communications Strategy

AI Maverick Intel (OTC PINK: BINP), a technology-forward company focused on transforming business customer acquisition through artificial intelligence, selected IBN to manage its corporate communications efforts. AI Maverick Intel’s proprietary platform delivers human-like, AI-powered prospecting with adaptive engagement across sectors including healthcare, biotech, insurance and transportation. Following its May 2025 rebrand and platform acquisition, the company launched an enhanced version in July designed to handle transactional and consultative sales engagements with context-aware messaging and autonomous dialogues. The platform aims to shorten deal cycles and reduce acquisition costs while positioning AI Maverick to capture growth in the AI-in-marketing sector. IBN will leverage its distribution network of over 5,000 outlets, social channels and wire services to expand AI Maverick Intel’s visibility among investors and the public.

 About AI Maverick Intel, Inc.

 AI Maverick Intel, Inc. is a growth-focused artificial intelligence company acquiring and optimizing revenue-generating businesses through its proprietary AI Maverick platform. The Company enables intelligent, two-way engagement across healthcare, biotech, insurance, transportation and other verticals, delivering long-term value through innovation, efficiency, and strategic partnerships.
