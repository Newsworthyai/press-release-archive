# InvestorNewsBreaks – IBN Tapped as Media Partner for DealFlow Discovery Day on Nuclear Energy

IBN (InvestorBrandNetwork), a multifaceted communications organization committed to connecting companies with the investment community through its dynamic portfolio of 60+ brands, today announced its engagement as the official media partner for DealFlow Events’ inaugural DealFlow Discovery Day on Nuclear Energy. The event is slated to take place on May 22, 2025, at the conveniently located Jay Suites in New York City.

 “DealFlow produces industry-flagship events for investors and financial market participants. The upcoming conference will offer highly specialized educational and networking opportunities in the rapidly emerging nuclear energy space,” said IBN’s Director of Global Operations Randy Clark. “Our team is excited to be part of this new event series organized by DealFlow Events. We look forward to working closely with their team to drive maximum visibility among investor audiences.”
