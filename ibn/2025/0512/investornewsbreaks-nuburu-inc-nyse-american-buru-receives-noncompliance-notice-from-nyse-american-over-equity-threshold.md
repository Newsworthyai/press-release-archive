# InvestorNewsBreaks – NUBURU, Inc. (NYSE American: BURU) Receives Noncompliance Notice from NYSE American Over Equity Threshold

NUBURU (NYSE American: BURU) has received a Notice of Noncompliance from NYSE Regulation for failing to meet the $2 million stockholders’ equity requirement under Section 1003(a)(i) of the NYSE American Company Guide, following continued operating losses and a going concern warning in its latest 10-K. The notice does not immediately impact BURU’s trading status, though its ticker now carries a “.BC” designation. The company has until May 29 to submit a plan to regain compliance and until Oct. 29, 2026 to meet the listing standards. Management cited recent strategic initiatives as potential paths to compliance, though their success is not guaranteed.
