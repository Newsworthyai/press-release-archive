# Getting a Second Opinion After Breast Cancer Diagnosis Doesn’t Delay Treatment Commencement

It is very natural to feel immense anxiety and fear after receiving a breast cancer diagnosis. Most people don’t consider seeking second opinions and would rather begin treatment right away. After all, breast cancer is one of the deadliest types of cancer in women, second only to lung cancer in terms of fatalities. The Breast Cancer Research Foundation estimates that slightly over 42,000 women will die from the condition in 2025, making it one of the most prolific killers in the USA.
