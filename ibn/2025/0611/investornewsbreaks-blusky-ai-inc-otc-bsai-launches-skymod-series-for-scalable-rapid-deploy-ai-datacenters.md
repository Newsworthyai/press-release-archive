# InvestorNewsBreaks – BluSky AI Inc. (OTC: BSAI) Launches SkyMod Series for Scalable, Rapid-Deploy AI Datacenters

BluSky AI (OTC: BSAI) announced the completion of its modular AI datacenter infrastructure, unveiling the SkyMod series designed for fast deployment in both indoor and outdoor environments. The flagship SkyMod One delivers 1 MW of compute power in a 1,400-square-foot footprint, while the larger SkyMod XL offers 1.7 MW across 3,000 square feet. Each unit integrates NVIDIA GPUs and is fully preassembled, tested, and shipped for plug-and-play activation at client or company sites. Targeted at AI-heavy workloads such as generative AI and large language models, the SkyMod line supports real-time inference and GPU-as-a-service. BluSky AI emphasized sustainability in its design, featuring efficient cooling and power systems to reduce environmental impact and support ESG goals.
