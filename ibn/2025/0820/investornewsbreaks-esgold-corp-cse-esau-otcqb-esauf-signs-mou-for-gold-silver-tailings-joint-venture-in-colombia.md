# InvestorNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Signs MOU for Gold-Silver Tailings Joint Venture in Colombia

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) said it signed a binding memorandum of understanding with Planta Magdalena S.A.S. to form a joint venture to develop and reprocess fully permitted gold- and silver-bearing tailings in Bolívar, Colombia. ESGold will invest C$1.5 million for a 50% interest and hold a right of first refusal to acquire the balance within 12 months. The company said the venture expands its tailings-to-cash-flow model beyond Québec, with the potential to add a second high-margin operation by 2026 while delivering environmental remediation benefits in one of South America’s top gold-producing regions.
