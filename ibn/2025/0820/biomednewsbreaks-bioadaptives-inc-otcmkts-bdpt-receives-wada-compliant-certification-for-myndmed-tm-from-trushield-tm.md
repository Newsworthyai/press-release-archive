# BioMedNewsBreaks — BioAdaptives, Inc. (OTCMKTS: BDPT) Receives WADA-Compliant Certification for MyndMed(TM) From TruShield(TM)

BioAdaptives (OTCMKTS: BDPT), a developer of natural health and performance supplements, said its nootropic formula MyndMed(TM) was certified clean by TruShield(TM) Laboratory Services following testing for World Anti-Doping Agency compliance. TruShield, whose protocols are trusted by professional sports organizations including the UFC(R), confirmed MyndMed contains no banned substances and meets safety standards for competitive athletes. BioAdaptives said the clearance supports integration of MyndMed into the UFC’s approved supplement pool, providing fighters with cognitive performance, focus and reaction-time support without risk of anti-doping violations.
