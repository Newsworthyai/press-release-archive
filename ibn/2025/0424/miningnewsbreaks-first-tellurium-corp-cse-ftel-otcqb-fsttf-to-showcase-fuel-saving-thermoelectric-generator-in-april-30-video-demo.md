# MiningNewsBreaks – First Tellurium Corp. (CSE: FTEL) (OTCQB: FSTTF) to Showcase Fuel-Saving Thermoelectric Generator in April 30 Video Demo

First Tellurium (CSE: FTEL) (OTCQB: FSTTF) announced that its majority-owned subsidiary, PyroDelta Energy Inc., will unveil its thermoelectric generator/radiator on April 30 via a video demonstration. The tellurium-based device, developed over eight years, recovers waste heat from combustion engines, delivering fuel savings of at least 6% while eliminating the need for traditional alternators and radiators. Designed for easy installation in new or existing vehicles, the device may also serve industrial sectors such as AI and crypto mining. CEO Tyrone Docherty also announced a shareholder luncheon in mid-May and the issuance of 1.5 million stock options at $0.15 per share.
