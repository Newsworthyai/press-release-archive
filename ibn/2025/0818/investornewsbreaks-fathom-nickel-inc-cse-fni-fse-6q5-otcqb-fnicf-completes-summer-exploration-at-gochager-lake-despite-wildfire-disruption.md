# InvestorNewsBreaks – Fathom Nickel Inc. (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) Completes Summer Exploration at Gochager Lake Despite Wildfire Disruption

Fathom Nickel (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) has completed its summer field exploration program at the Gochager Lake Project. Crews collected 2,926 B-horizon soil samples across 3,183 planned sites and gathered 337 chip and grab rock samples from outcrops. All rock samples were analyzed on-site via pXRF for nickel, copper, cobalt, sulphur, and mafic-ultramafic pathfinder elements. Several mineralized samples have been sent for multi-element assay and whole rock geochemistry. Though a lightning-caused wildfire forced early evacuation and cut the program short by three days, approximately 90% of the targeted soil sampling was achieved.
