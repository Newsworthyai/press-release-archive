# InvestorNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Featured in NetworkNewsWire Editorial on Gold’s Record Surge and Shift Toward Cash-Generating Producers

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) announced its inclusion in a NetworkNewsWire (“NNW”) editorial titled “Gold Breaks $4,000 as Capital Shifts Toward Cash-Generating Producers.” The piece explores investors’ growing movement from inflated growth sectors toward tangible assets such as precious metals, with both gold and silver reaching record highs alongside strong equity markets. ESGold exemplifies this shift through its Montauban Gold-Silver Project in Quebec, now under construction and targeting 2026 production, and its Planta Magdalena joint venture in Colombia, where historical data and early sampling indicate large-scale potential.

 About ESGold Corp.

 ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) is a fully permitted, preproduction resource company at the forefront of clean mining and exploration innovation. With proven expertise in Quebec, the company is advancing its projects toward production and feasibility while delivering long-term value through sustainable resource recovery and exploration. ESGold’s flagship Montauban property, located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, serves as a model for responsible mining practices, combining near-term production with district-scale discovery potential.
