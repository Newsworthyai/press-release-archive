# InvestorNewsBreaks – Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) Partners with Ninja Digital to License 30,000-Title Entertainment Library for AI Training

Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) has signed an exclusive representation agreement with Ninja Digital Holdings LLC, a data labeling and AI consultancy, to market its extensive entertainment library of more than 30,000 movies, TV shows and animated series to the global AI marketplace. The $11 million catalog acquisition, totaling over 30 terabytes of digital content, will be licensed for AI model training, machine learning and synthetic data generation through KLED.ai, a premier data marketplace serving clients including Google, Meta and Tesla. “Our entertainment library will take on a powerful new life within the AI ecosystem,” said Chris Giordano, President and Chairman of Authentic Holdings. “This partnership opens a major new revenue stream by applying our Goliath Motion Pictures catalogue to AI data labeling and training.”
