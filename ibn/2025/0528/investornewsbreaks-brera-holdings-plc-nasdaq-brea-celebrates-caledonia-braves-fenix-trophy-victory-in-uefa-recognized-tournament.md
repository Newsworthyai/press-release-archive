# InvestorNewsBreaks – Brera Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: BREA) Celebrates Caledonia Braves’ Fenix Trophy Victory in UEFA-Recognized Tournament

Brera Holdings (NASDAQ: BREA) announced that its affiliated Scottish club, Caledonia Braves, claimed the 2025 Fenix Trophy title with a 3-1 win over two-time champions FC United of Manchester. The final took place earlier this month at Lake Iseo, Italy, during the fourth edition of the tournament, which has earned recognition from UEFA and is dubbed the “Champions League for amateurs” by BBC Sport. Organized by Brera’s Milan-based club, Brera FC, the FENIX Trophy unites 16 non-professional clubs from across Europe in a celebration of sporting culture, community impact, and innovative football models.
