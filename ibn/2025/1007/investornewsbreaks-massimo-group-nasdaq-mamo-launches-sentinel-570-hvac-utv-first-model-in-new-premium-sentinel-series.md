# InvestorNewsBreaks – Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO) Launches Sentinel 570 HVAC UTV, First Model in New Premium Sentinel Series

Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO), a leading manufacturer and distributor of powersports vehicles, announced the nationwide availability of its all-new Sentinel 570 HVAC UTV, the debut model in its premium Sentinel Series. Designed for year-round comfort and performance, the 500-class utility vehicle features a fully enclosed, climate-controlled cabin, touchscreen GPS display, on-demand 4WD, and independent suspension. CEO David Shan said the Sentinel 570 “delivers a complete package of power, comfort, and advanced technology,” reinforcing the company’s commitment to innovation across work and recreational applications. Additional Sentinel models, including the 770, 900, and 1500, are slated for 2026 release.
