# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Reports Strong Audience Engagement as Sports.com Streams Opening Weekend of Super League Kerala

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), the global sports, entertainment, and gaming conglomerate, announced a highly successful opening weekend for the Super League Kerala streamed live on its Sports.com platform. The event featured three competitive matches drawing more than 517,000 digital viewers with an average watch time of 60 minutes, alongside strong stadium attendance led by Calicut FC’s 2-1 victory over Forca Kochi FC. Backed by sponsors Sports.com and Amul, with Sony handling linear broadcasts, the SLK launch marked a milestone in Sports.com’s expansion across India and MENA, reinforcing its strategy to drive fan engagement, sponsorship revenue, and global football streaming growth.
