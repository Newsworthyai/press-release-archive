# NetworkNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Featured in NetworkNewsWire Editorial on Gold’s Rise Amid Dollar Weakness

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) announced its inclusion in a NetworkNewsWire editorial titled “When the Dollar Blinks, Real Assets Take Center Stage,” which explores how a weakening U.S. dollar and record-high gold prices are driving renewed investor interest in real assets. With gold recently trading near $3,700 per ounce and silver reaching 14-year highs, the article highlights ESGold’s strong positioning in this environment, citing its fully funded, permit-backed business plan and pathway to production in 2026 as offering near-term profit potential and significant leverage to the ongoing gold cycle.

 About ESGold Corp.

 ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) is a fully permitted, preproduction resource company at the forefront of clean mining and exploration innovation. With proven expertise in Quebec, the company is advancing its projects toward production and feasibility while delivering long-term value through sustainable resource recovery and exploration. ESGold’s flagship Montauban property, located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, serves as a model for responsible mining practices, combining near-term production with district-scale discovery potential.
