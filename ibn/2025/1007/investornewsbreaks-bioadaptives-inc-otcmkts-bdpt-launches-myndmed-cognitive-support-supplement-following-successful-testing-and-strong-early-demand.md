# InvestorNewsBreaks – BioAdaptives, Inc. (OTCMKTS: BDPT) Launches MyndMed Cognitive-Support Supplement Following Successful Testing and Strong Early Demand

BioAdaptives (OTCMKTS: BDPT) announced the commercial launch of MyndMed(TM), a next-generation dietary supplement designed to enhance memory, focus, and overall brain performance. Following extensive testing with highly positive user feedback, the company is scaling production and expanding distribution to meet anticipated high demand beginning in November. Formulated to support cognitive function across short-, mid-, and long-term pathways, MyndMed(TM) delivers what the company calls “Mental Armor for Modern Minds(TM)” through clinically studied ingredients that promote alertness, neuroplasticity, and cellular energy balance. Manufactured in the U.S. in cGMP-certified facilities and third-party tested for purity, MyndMed(TM) is available nationwide at www.MyndMed.com.
