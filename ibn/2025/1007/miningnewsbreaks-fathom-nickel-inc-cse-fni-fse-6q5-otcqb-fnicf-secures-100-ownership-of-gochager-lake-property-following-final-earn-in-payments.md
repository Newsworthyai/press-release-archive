# MiningNewsBreaks – Fathom Nickel Inc. (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) Secures 100% Ownership of Gochager Lake Property Following Final Earn-In Payments

Fathom Nickel (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) announced it has completed the final cash payment and share issuance required under the Gochager Lake Earn-In Agreement, granting the Company full ownership of the Gochager Lake Property in Saskatchewan, subject to a 2% NSR royalty. Since entering the agreement in 2022, Fathom has expanded the property from 4,696 to over 34,000 hectares, advanced its geological model, and extended the mineralized corridor to more than 1.4 kilometers. CEO Ian Fraser said the milestone marks significant progress toward establishing a district-scale nickel camp through continued science-driven exploration.
