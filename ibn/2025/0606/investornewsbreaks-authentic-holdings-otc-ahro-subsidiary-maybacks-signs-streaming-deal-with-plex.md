# InvestorNewsBreaks – Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) Subsidiary Maybacks Signs Streaming Deal With Plex

Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) announced that its wholly owned subsidiary, Maybacks Global Entertainment, LLC, has entered into a licensing and distribution agreement with Plex GmbH to bring its iDreamCTV network brands—Toro TV, Comfy TV, Winnie’s World, and iCowboy—to a global streaming audience. The partnership leverages Plex’s AVOD and FAST channel infrastructure to monetize and distribute Maybacks’ content across its 25 million-user platform. Following its acquisition of Goliath Motion Picture Promotions, Maybacks is pivoting from traditional film sales to building proprietary media brands with scalable digital reach.
