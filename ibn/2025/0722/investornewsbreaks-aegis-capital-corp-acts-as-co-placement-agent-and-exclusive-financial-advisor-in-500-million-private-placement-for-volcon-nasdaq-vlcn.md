# InvestorNewsBreaks – Aegis Capital Corp. Acts as Co-Placement Agent and Exclusive Financial Advisor in $500 Million Private Placement for Volcon (NASDAQ: VLCN)

Aegis Capital announced its role as co-placement agent and exclusive financial advisor in the successful $500 million private placement for Volcon, Inc. (NASDAQ: VLCN), an emerging digital asset and power sports company soon to rebrand as Empery Digital, Inc. The offering, which featured strong participation from leading crypto venture firms and institutional investors, marks the launch of the company’s bitcoin treasury strategy. Volcon intends to allocate over 95% of the gross proceeds toward acquiring BTC and has partnered with Gemini for institutional-grade execution and custody services. The transaction highlights Aegis’ continued leadership in transformative digital finance deals.
