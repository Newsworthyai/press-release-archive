# InvestorNewsBreaks – Roth Capital Partners, LLC Announces Closing of Silver Pegasus Acquisition Corp. (NASDAQ: SPEGU) $115 Million IPO Including Full Over-Allotment Exercise

Roth Capital Partners acting as sole manager, today announced the closing of the $115 million initial public offering for Silver Pegasus Acquisition Corp. (NASDAQ: SPEGU), a special purpose acquisition company. The offering, which closed July 16, 2025, included the full exercise of the underwriters’ over-allotment option, totaling 11,500,000 units at $10.00 per unit. Each unit consists of one Class A ordinary share and one right to receive one-tenth of a Class A share upon the consummation of a business combination. The units began trading on Nasdaq on July 15 under the symbol “SPEGU.”

 The offering was made only by means of a prospectus, available through Roth Capital Partners or the SEC’s website at www.sec.gov. A registration statement was declared effective by the SEC on July 14, 2025.
