# CryptoNewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Adds Crypto-Enabled Equity Access to Mortgage Tech Platform

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE)  is expanding its digital mortgage suite with an equity conversion product powered by blockchain-backed stablecoins. Teaming with RealCo, which will acquire up to 49% interest in high-value homes, the program lets homeowners receive immediate funds—without loans or interest—by selling a portion of their equity. RealCo will mint stablecoins tied to the property’s recorded fractional ownership, offering flexibility in either cash or token form. The innovation positions Beeline to meet surging demand from non-traditional borrowers while generating consistent, interest-rate-agnostic revenue starting in Q4 2025.
