# TechMediaBreaks – Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) Launches D.C.-Based Division to Protect Diplomats from Escalating Cyber Threats

Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) has launched a Government and Diplomatic Corps division in Washington, D.C., responding to a sharp rise in cyberattacks targeting officials amid growing geopolitical tensions. Sekur’s Swiss-hosted encrypted communications suite offers secure email, messaging, and VPN services tailored to high-value targets such as diplomats and government leaders. With recent breaches exposing thousands of D.C. staffers and top U.S. officials, Sekur’s proprietary platform aims to counter threats like phishing, AI hacks, and chat compromises. The company is marketing the service through The Washington Diplomat and expects rapid adoption as demand for secure communications surges globally.
