# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Climbs More Than 15% in Active Morning Trade

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) rose more than 15% to trade above $5.08 Monday morning, extending recent momentum as volume neared 60,000 shares against a 92,000-share daily average. The move lifted the online lottery and gaming platform to its highest intraday level since mid-September, continuing a rebound from a 52-week low of $2.20. Gains come amid renewed retail activity and a broader uptick across select microcap and gaming-related stocks.

 About SEGG Media Corporation

 SEGG Media (Nasdaq: SEGG, LTRYW) is a global sports, entertainment and gaming group operating a portfolio of digital assets including Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com. Focused on immersive fan engagement, ethical gaming and AI-driven live experiences, SEGG Media is redefining how global audiences interact with the content they love.
