# AINewsBreaks – Micropolis Holding Co. (NYSE American: MCRP) Partners with Swedish Firms to Develop Autonomous ‘Box Cleaner’ Robot

Micropolis (NYSE American: MCRP), a pioneer in unmanned ground vehicles and AI-driven security solutions, announced an agreement with Helsingborgs Hamn AB and MCS Robotics AB to co-develop and test the “Box Cleaner,” an autonomous robotic cleaning system for port and industrial environments. Built on the Micropolis M2 platform, the Box Cleaner leverages proprietary AI-driven navigation and edge-computing architecture to perform precision cleaning while minimizing water, energy, and labor costs. Initial trials at Helsingborgs Hamn—one of Scandinavia’s most technologically advanced ports—will provide operational data to optimize performance ahead of planned commercial rollouts across Europe and the Middle East.
