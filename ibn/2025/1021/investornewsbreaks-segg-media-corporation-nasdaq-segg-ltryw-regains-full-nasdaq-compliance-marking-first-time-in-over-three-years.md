# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Regains Full Nasdaq Compliance, Marking First Time in Over Three Years

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) announced it has regained full compliance with Nasdaq’s continued listing requirements, as confirmed in a notice received Oct. 16, 2025. The resolution closes the shareholder approval deficiency under Listing Rule 5635(c) and marks the first time in more than three years the Company will be removed from Nasdaq’s Non-Compliant list. Chairman and CEO Matthew McGahan said the milestone reflects SEGG Media’s successful turnaround since rebranding from Lottery.com and positions the Company for continued growth across its core domains—Sports.com, Concerts.com, and Lottery.com.
