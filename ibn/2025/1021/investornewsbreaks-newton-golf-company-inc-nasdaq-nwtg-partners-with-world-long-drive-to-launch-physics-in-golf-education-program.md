# InvestorNewsBreaks – Newton Golf Company Inc. (NASDAQ: NWTG) Partners with World Long Drive to Launch Physics-in-Golf Education Program

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG), a technology-driven golf brand advancing performance through physics-based engineering, announced its participation in an educational program with World Long Drive (WLD) to engage students in the science of golf. Held alongside the 2025 World Long Drive Championships, the initiative combines live competition, classroom learning, and demonstrations illustrating physics principles behind elite performance. CTO Akinobu Yorihiro said the program helps students connect concepts like velocity, torque, and energy transfer to real-world drives exceeding 400 yards.
