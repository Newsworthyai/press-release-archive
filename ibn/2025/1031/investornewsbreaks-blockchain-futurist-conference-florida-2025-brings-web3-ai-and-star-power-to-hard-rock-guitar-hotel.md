# InvestorNewsBreaks – Blockchain Futurist Conference Florida 2025 Brings Web3, AI, and Star Power to Hard Rock Guitar Hotel

The Blockchain Futurist Conference Florida 2025, taking place Nov. 5–6 at the Hard Rock Guitar Hotel, will debut the first U.S. edition of the renowned Futurist event. A major gathering for leaders in Web3, crypto, and AI, the conference will feature celebrity appearances by Iggy Azalea and NBA champion Tristan Thompson, blending star power with cutting-edge innovation. Over 200 speakers, including figures from Coinbase, SingularityNET, and Impact Theory, will address the evolution of blockchain, AI, and digital systems. Highlights include the AI Futurist Conference, VIP Cabana networking areas, and engaging exhibits featuring the latest trends. With more than 60 sponsors and immersive side experiences, Futurist Florida 2025 is set to shine the spotlight on how Web3 and AI will shape the next era.
