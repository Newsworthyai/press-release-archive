# MissionIRNewsBreaks – Datavault AI Inc. (NASDAQ: DVLT) Condemns Wolfpack Research Short Report and Announces Legal Action

Datavault AI (NASDAQ: DVLT), a leader in data tokenization and management, today issued a formal response to what it calls a false and defamatory short report published by Wolfpack Research. The Company stated that the report contains misleading claims intended to manipulate DVLT stock for short sellers’ financial gain. Datavault AI has retained Paul Hastings LLP and Dickinson Wright PLLC to pursue legal remedies, with litigation counsel Jacob Frenkel noting the Company’s intent to hold Wolfpack accountable for “abusive, fraudulent, and manipulative practices.” CEO Nathaniel T. Bradley reaffirmed Datavault AI’s strong intellectual property foundation—now exceeding 70 patents in AI-driven data valuation, blockchain tokenization, and acoustic data systems—and highlighted ongoing partnerships, acquisitions, and media initiatives that underscore the Company’s growth and innovation strategy.
