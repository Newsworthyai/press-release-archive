# InvestorNewsBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) Engages Norda Stelo for Gap Analysis at Lac Pelletier, Eyes Near-Term Production

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA:9NH) has engaged Norda Stelo to conduct a Gap Analysis Study for its Lac Pelletier Gold Project in Quebec, marking a critical step toward the potential launch of its exploitation phase. The mandate includes a full review of environmental permitting and identification of regulatory requirements ahead of mining activity. CEO John Florek noted that this milestone positions Emperor as a potential near-term cash-flow producer, with the project already featuring extensive underground development, a mining permit for 1,000 tons per day, and over 100,000 meters of historical drilling. Emperor aims to define a one-million-ounce resource in 2025, supported by rising gold prices and the site’s proximity to infrastructure and adjacent Agnico Eagle assets.
