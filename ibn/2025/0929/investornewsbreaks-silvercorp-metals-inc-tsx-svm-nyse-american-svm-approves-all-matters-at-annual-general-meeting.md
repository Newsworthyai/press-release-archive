# InvestorNewsBreaks – Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) Approves All Matters at Annual General Meeting

Silvercorp Metals (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) announced that all matters set out in its Aug. 11, 2025 Notice of Meeting and Information Circular were approved by the requisite majority of votes at the company’s annual general meeting held today, with 108,216,233 common shares—representing 49.50% of outstanding shares as of the record date—represented at the meeting.

 About Silvercorp

 Silvercorp is a Canadian mining company producing silver, gold, lead, and zinc with a long history of profitability and growth potential. The Company’s strategy is to create shareholder value by 1) focusing on generating free cash flow from long life mines; 2) organic growth through extensive drilling for discovery; 3) ongoing merger and acquisition efforts to unlock value; and 4) long term commitment to responsible mining and ESG.
