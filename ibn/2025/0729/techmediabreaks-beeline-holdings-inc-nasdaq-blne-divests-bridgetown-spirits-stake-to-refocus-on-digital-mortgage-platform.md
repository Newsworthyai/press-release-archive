# TechMediaBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Divests Bridgetown Spirits Stake to Refocus on Digital Mortgage Platform

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE),  a digital mortgage platform, announced it has disposed of its majority ownership in Bridgetown Spirits Corp. for $367,404 under a Debt Satisfaction Agreement, removing Spirits as a subsidiary and completing the separation of non-core assets acquired through its October 2024 forward merger. The transaction included a $75,000 senior secured term loan to Spirits and a $100,000 promissory note in return. CFO Chris Moe said the move strengthens Beeline’s balance sheet and positions the company to focus exclusively on proptech, mortgage, and SaaS revenue as the market prepares for a 2026 recovery.
