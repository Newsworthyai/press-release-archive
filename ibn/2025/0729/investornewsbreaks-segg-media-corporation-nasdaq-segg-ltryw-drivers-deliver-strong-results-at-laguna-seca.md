# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Drivers Deliver Strong Results at Laguna Seca

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a technology company at the intersection of sports, entertainment and gaming, showcased its Sports.com and Lottery.com brands during the Java House Grand Prix of Monterey at WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca. Callum Ilott surged from P24 to finish P6, marking PREMA Racing’s best-ever NTT INDYCAR Series result. Louis Foster qualified P7, maintaining his streak of top-10 starts, while Sebastian Murray rebounded from an early setback with a career-best fifth-place finish in INDY NXT by Firestone, earning top honors for the Andretti fleet. SEGG Media Chairman and CEO Matthew McGahan said the exposure underscored the value promised to shareholders and partners.
