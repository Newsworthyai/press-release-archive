# RockBreaks – Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) Prevails in Final Legal Challenge for El Domo Environmental License

Silvercorp Metals (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) announced that Ecuador’s Constitutional Court unanimously rejected a final legal challenge against the environmental license for the El Domo mining project, confirming its validity. El Domo, now under construction, is the first mining project in Ecuador to complete an environmental consultation aligned with the Escazú Agreement’s standards. The license was backed by 98% of the local population and has been upheld by multiple judicial levels, including the Constitutional Court. Despite continued obstruction efforts by anti-mining groups, project development remains on track.
