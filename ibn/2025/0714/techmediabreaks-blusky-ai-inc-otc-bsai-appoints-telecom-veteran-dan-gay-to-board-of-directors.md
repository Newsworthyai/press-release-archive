# TechMediaBreaks – BluSky AI Inc. (OTC: BSAI) Appoints Telecom Veteran Dan Gay to Board of Directors

BluSky AI (OTC: BSAI), a developer of modular AI data center infrastructure, announced the appointment of Dan Gay to its Board of Directors. Gay, a veteran of companies including MCI, Qwest, and RackScale, brings over 30 years of experience in data center and enterprise IT strategy. He previously served as BluSky AI’s COO, playing a key role in advancing the company’s scalable, energy-efficient infrastructure strategy. The move supports BluSky’s mission to rapidly deploy modular AI data centers amid surging demand for compute capacity.
