# TinyGemsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Boosts Brand Visibility at Iowa INDYCAR Weekend Ahead of Toronto Showcase

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a tech leader at the crossroads of sports, entertainment, and gaming, reported strong brand momentum following an impactful weekend at Iowa Speedway. Flagship platforms Sports.com and Lottery.com gained visibility through high-profile INDYCAR exposure, including on-car branding and broadcast coverage. Rookie Louis Foster maintained his lead in the Rookie of the Year standings, delivering consistent performances, while Callum Ilott and Robert Shwartzman showcased speed and resilience. SEGG Media now turns to the Toronto street circuit, leveraging the growing media presence and fan engagement to expand its motorsport marketing footprint across North America.
