# Policy Recommendations the US Can Leverage to Source Copper from Brazil

As the strategic importance of copper continues to grow around the world, it is imperative that the U.S. “friendshores” its international supply chains. Brazil has immense copper resources and the U.S. can benefit from nurturing favorable relationships with the South American country in order to tap this resource in a mutually beneficial way. The Center for Strategic & International Studies (CSIS) offers a number of policy recommendations through which copper supplies can be obtained from Brazil for decades to come.
