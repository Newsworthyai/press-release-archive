# How Patients Can Boost Dietary Nutrient Intake During Cancer Treatment

Chemotherapy, radiation, and other cancer treatments can turn favorite foods into unpalatable options while simultaneously increasing the body’s nutritional demands, putting cancer patients in a difficult position.
