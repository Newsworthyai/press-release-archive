# InvestorNewsBreaks – New Silver Launches Small-Balance Commercial Real Estate Loan Program

New Silver, a lending platform for real estate investors, announced the launch of its small-balance commercial real estate loan program, providing experienced investors with short-term financing options across multifamily, mixed-use, retail, and industrial properties. The program offers loans ranging from $2 million to $15 million with terms of 12 to 24 months, up to 70% LTARV, and up to 80% LTC, targeting purchases, purchases with rehab, and refinances. Designed for investors with at least two years of experience and five completed projects, the initiative expands New Silver’s offerings beyond residential real estate, positioning the company as a nationwide capital partner for seasoned investors.
