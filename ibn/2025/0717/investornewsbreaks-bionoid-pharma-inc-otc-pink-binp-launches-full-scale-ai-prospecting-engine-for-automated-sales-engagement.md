# InvestorNewsBreaks – Bionoid Pharma Inc. (OTC PINK: BINP) Launches Full-Scale AI Prospecting Engine for Automated Sales Engagement

Bionoid Pharma (OTC PINK: BINP) has announced the full rollout of its next-generation AI prospecting engine, designed to automate the research, engagement, and qualification of sales prospects without increasing headcount. The platform unifies millions of data points to deliver real-time insights and context-aware communication, supporting both transactional and consultative sales. CEO Wayne Cockburn emphasized that the system now handles discovery, objections, and scheduling—key functions typically performed by human reps. The platform, which enables companies to streamline complex sales processes through AI-driven conversations, is available immediately with same-day deployment.
