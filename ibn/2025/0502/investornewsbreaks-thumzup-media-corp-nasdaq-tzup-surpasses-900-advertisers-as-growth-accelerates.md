# InvestorNewsBreaks – Thumzup Media Corp. (NASDAQ: TZUP) Surpasses 900 Advertisers as Growth Accelerates

Thumzup (NASDAQ: TZUP) announced it has surpassed 900 advertisers, achieving a 220% compound annual growth rate as it continues expanding its AdTech platform across major U.S. markets. The company expects to reach 1,000 advertisers by mid-Q2 and 5,000 by year-end 2025. Thumzup’s platform integrates with Instagram Reels and X, reaching over 535 million monthly users and offering enhanced engagement tools for brands. CEO Robert Steele highlighted the milestone as validation of the platform’s growing demand and long-term market potential.

 About Thumzup®

 Thumzup Media Corporation is democratizing the multi-billion dollar social media branding and marketing industry. Its flagship product, the Thumzup platform, utilizes a robust programmatic advertiser dashboard coupled with a consumer-facing App to enable individuals to get paid cash for posting about participating advertisers on major social media outlets through the Thumzup App. The easy-to-use dashboard allows advertisers to programmatically customize their campaigns. Cash payments are made to App users/creators through PayPal and other digital payment systems.

 Thumzup was featured on CBS Los Angeles and in KTLA.
