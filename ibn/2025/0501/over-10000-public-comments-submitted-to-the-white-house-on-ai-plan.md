# Over 10,000 Public Comments Submitted to the White House on AI Plan

The White House recently announced it had received over 10,000 public responses regarding its upcoming strategy on AI. The Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) released the collected responses in a massive 18,480-page document. This overwhelming feedback highlights the intense public and industry interest in AI as the global competition to lead in the field heats up.
