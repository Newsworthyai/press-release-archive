# TechMediaBreaks – Onar Holding Corporation (OTCQB: ONAR) Appoints Industry Veteran Jon Bond to Board of Directors

Onar (OTCQB: ONAR) announced the appointment of marketing pioneer Jon Bond to its board of directors. Bond, co-founder of Kirshenbaum & Bond and former CEO of Big Fuel, brings decades of experience in creative, digital, and programmatic media, having held leadership roles at top firms including Publicis and Inuvo (NYSE: INUV). His addition is expected to enhance ONAR’s M&A strategy, agency network growth, and brand credibility, reinforcing the company’s mission to redefine modern marketing through innovation and strategic expansion.
