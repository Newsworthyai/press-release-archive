# RockBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Appoints Mining Executive Peter Espig to Board as Montauban Project Nears Production

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) has appointed veteran mining executive and former global investment banker Peter Espig to its board of directors, as the company advances its fully permitted Montauban Gold-Silver Project in Quebec toward 2025 production. Espig brings decades of leadership across capital markets and mining operations, including a turnaround success as CEO of Nicola Mining. His appointment comes as ESGold focuses on production readiness, economic reassessment, 3D geological modeling and expanded exploration at Montauban.

 About ESGold Corp.

 ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) (FSE: Z7D) is a fully permitted, pre-production resource company at the forefront of clean mining and exploration innovation.
