# TechMediaBreaks – NextPlat Corp (NASDAQ: NXPL) Named Exclusive UK and EEA Distributor for ZOLEO Satellite Devices

NextPlat (NASDAQ: NXPL, NXPLW) announced that its subsidiary, Global Telesat Communications Ltd (GTC), has been appointed the exclusive distributor for ZOLEO Inc. across the UK and European Economic Area. ZOLEO’s flagship global satellite communicator extends smartphone messaging coverage via the Iridium(R) satellite network and has earned accolades including Outdoor Retailer’s Innovation Award “Product of the Year.” The partnership adds to GTC’s expanding e-commerce and Amazon storefront portfolio of satellite connectivity solutions for remote workers, outdoor enthusiasts, and global travelers.
