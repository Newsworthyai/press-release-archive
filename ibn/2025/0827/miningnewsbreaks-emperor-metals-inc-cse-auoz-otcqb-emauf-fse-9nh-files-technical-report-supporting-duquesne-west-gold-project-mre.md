# MiningNewsBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FSE: 9NH) Files Technical Report Supporting Duquesne West Gold Project MRE

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FSE: 9NH) announced the filing of a National Instrument 43-101 technical report supporting its maiden Mineral Resource Estimate (“2025 Duquesne West MRE”) for the Duquesne West Gold Project in Quebec. The estimate outlines an Inferred Mineral Resource of 26.9 million tonnes, containing 1.46 million ounces of gold at an average grade of 1.69 g/t Au. CEO John Florek said the report underscores the project’s compelling potential, noting robust values across multiple cut-off grades and significant growth opportunities within the deposit’s initial footprint.
