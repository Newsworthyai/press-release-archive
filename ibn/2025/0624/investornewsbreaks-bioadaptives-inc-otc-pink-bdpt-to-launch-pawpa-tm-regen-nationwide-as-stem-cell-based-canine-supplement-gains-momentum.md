# InvestorNewsBreaks – BioAdaptives, Inc. (OTC Pink: BDPT) to Launch Pawpa(TM) Regen Nationwide as Stem Cell-Based Canine Supplement Gains Momentum

BioAdaptives (OTC Pink: BDPT), a leader in nutraceutical and wellness innovation, will launch its proprietary canine supplement Pawpa(TM) Regen nationwide by the end of July 2025. Designed to support natural stem cell activation in aging dogs, the supplement has shown promising results in early trials, including improved energy, mobility, coat quality, and temperament. CEO James Keener emphasized the product’s unique regenerative approach using safe, naturally sourced ingredients. With strong early feedback and growing demand for holistic pet care solutions, BioAdaptives is expanding production ahead of the broader e-commerce rollout.
