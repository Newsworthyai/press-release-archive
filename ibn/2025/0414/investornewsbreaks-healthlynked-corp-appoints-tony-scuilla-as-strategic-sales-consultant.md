# InvestorNewsBreaks – HealthLynked Corp. Appoints Tony Scuilla as Strategic Sales Consultant

HealthLynked (OTCQB: HLYK), a healthcare technology company, announced the appointment of Anthony “Tony” Scuilla as a strategic sales consultant to drive partnerships with major insurance providers. Scuilla brings more than 35 years of leadership experience in healthcare IT sales, having held key roles at AGS Healthcare Group, Alteer Corp., Eclipsys Corp., and Shared Medical Systems. Known for generating over $100 million in product sales and raising more than $20 million in venture capital, Scuilla will support HealthLynked’s efforts to expand its healthcare network and enhance patient-provider connectivity through strategic insurance collaborations.
