# InvestorNewsBreaks – IBN NetworkNewsWire Extended by IgnitionX for Precision Editorial Reach

IBN (InvestorBrandNetwork) NetworkNewsWire delivers financial news and content distribution through editorial syndication to more than 5,000 outlets along with social media channels across the IBN brand family. With IgnitionX integrated, editorial coverage moves beyond traditional distribution as stories are programmatically targeted to investor segments on Google, Meta, YouTube, LinkedIn and Reddit, ensuring placement within curated high value audiences.

 The combined approach gives clients trusted editorial coverage enhanced by data driven ad targeting, pushing stories directly into investor feeds. This creates expanded visibility, measurable engagement across digital platforms, and clearer attribution of how investors interact with specific content.
