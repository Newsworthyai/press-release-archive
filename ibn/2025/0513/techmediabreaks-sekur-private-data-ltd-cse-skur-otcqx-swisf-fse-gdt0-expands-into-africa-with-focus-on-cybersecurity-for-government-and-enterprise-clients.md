# TechMediaBreaks – Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) Expands Into Africa with Focus on Cybersecurity for Government and Enterprise Clients

Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0), a Swiss-hosted cybersecurity and private communications platform, has initiated discussions with multiple referral partners to launch its privacy-focused solutions across several African countries. The move targets enterprise and government clients, especially in high-risk sectors such as education, telecom, and public administration, in response to the continent’s growing exposure to cyber threats. Sekur’s communications suite, which includes encrypted email, messaging, and VPN services, will soon feature multi-tiered authorization for added enterprise security. All services are hosted in Switzerland under strict federal data protection laws, supporting Sekur’s commitment to global privacy standards.
