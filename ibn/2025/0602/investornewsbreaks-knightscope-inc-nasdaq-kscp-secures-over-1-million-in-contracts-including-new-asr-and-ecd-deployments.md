# InvestorNewsBreaks – Knightscope, Inc. (NASDAQ: KSCP) Secures Over $1 Million in Contracts Including New ASR and ECD Deployments

Knightscope (NASDAQ: KSCP) has announced more than $1 million in new contracts spanning higher education, government, and commercial clients across the U.S. The agreements include orders for 525 Emergency Communication Devices (ECDs) and commitments for seven Autonomous Security Robots (ASRs). Highlights include 118 ECDs for college campuses, a K5 ASR deployment by a major real estate firm, and expanded public sector coverage with extended maintenance contracts. Thirteen ASR subscriptions were also renewed across multiple industries, reinforcing Knightscope’s growing recurring revenue model and its expanding footprint in AI-powered security solutions.
