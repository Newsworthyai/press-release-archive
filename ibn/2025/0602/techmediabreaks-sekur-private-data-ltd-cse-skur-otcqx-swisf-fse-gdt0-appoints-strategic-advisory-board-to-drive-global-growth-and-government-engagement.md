# TechMediaBreaks – Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) Appoints Strategic Advisory Board to Drive Global Growth and Government Engagement

Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) has announced the formation of its Strategic Advisory Board, naming three high-profile members—Jill Kelley, James M. Connelly, and Nikolas Perrault—to support global expansion, strategic partnerships, and government engagement. Kelley, a globally recognized diplomatic advisor and privacy advocate, will focus on elevating Sekur’s brand among government and enterprise stakeholders. Connelly brings over three decades of Washington, D.C.–based diplomatic and corporate relations experience, positioning Sekur to pursue contracts with U.S. and international institutions requiring secure communications. Perrault, a seasoned finance executive and M&A consultant, will support Sekur’s growth strategy through global partnerships and expansion efforts, particularly in Europe and Africa. CEO Alain Ghiai noted the board has already contributed to active discussions across multiple regions, reinforcing Sekur’s mission to deliver privacy-first cybersecurity solutions amid rising digital threats.

 About Sekur Private Data Ltd.

 Sekur Private Data is a cybersecurity and internet privacy provider of Swiss-hosted solutions for secure communications and secure data management. The company distributes a suite of encrypted emails, secure messengers and secure communication tools. It sells its products through its own website, approved distributors and telecommunication companies worldwide. Sekur Private Data serves consumers, businesses and governments worldwide.
