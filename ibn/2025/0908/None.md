# CryptoNewsBreaks – Forward Industries, Inc. (NASDAQ: FORD) Secures $1.65 Billion PIPE to Launch Solana-Focused Digital Asset Treasury Strategy

Forward Industries (NASDAQ: FORD) announced $1.65 billion in cash and stablecoin commitments for a private investment in public equity offering led by Galaxy Digital (NASDAQ: GLXY), Jump Crypto, and Multicoin Capital to initiate a Solana-focused digital asset treasury strategy. C/M Capital Partners, LP, one of the Company’s largest shareholders, also participated. The sponsors will provide both capital and strategic support to position Forward Industries as the leading publicly traded institutional participant in the Solana ecosystem, leveraging Galaxy’s institutional platform, Jump Crypto’s infrastructure expertise, and Multicoin’s deep investment history in Solana projects.
