# AINewsBreaks – Safe Pro Group Inc. (NASDAQ: SPAI) Signs MOUs with Ondas (NASDAQ: ONDS) and Unusual Machines (NYSE: UMAC) to Advance AI-Powered Drone Solutions

Safe Pro Group (NASDAQ: SPAI), a leader in AI-powered defense and security solutions, announced it has entered into Memoranda of Understanding with Ondas Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: ONDS) and Unusual Machines Inc. (NYSE: UMAC), both leaders in the U.S. drone industry. The collaboration aims to integrate Safe Pro’s patented drone imagery analysis and computer vision technologies, including its Safe Pro Object Threat Detection (SPOTD) system, into their hardware and software offerings. Designed to detect more than 150 types of threats such as landmines, cluster munitions and unexploded ordnance, SPOTD has already been deployed in Ukraine, where it has identified over 31,600 threats across nearly 20,000 acres. The partnerships are expected to enhance Ondas’ autonomous commercial and defense platforms and Unusual Machines’ FPV drone solutions, supporting applications for the U.S. military, NATO allies, public safety, and global humanitarian operations.

 About Safe Pro Group Inc.

 Safe Pro Group Inc. is a mission-driven technology company delivering AI-enabled security and defense solutions. Through cutting-edge platforms like SPOTD, Safe Pro provides advanced situational awareness tools for defense, humanitarian, and homeland security applications globally. It is a leading provider of artificial intelligence (AI) solutions specializing in drone imagery processing leveraging commercially available “off-the-shelf” drones with its proprietary machine learning and computer vision technology to enable rapid identification of explosives threats, providing a much safer and more efficient alternative to traditional human-based analysis methods. Built on a cloud-based ecosystem and powered by Amazon Web Services (AWS), Safe Pro Group’s scalable platform is targeting multiple markets that include commercial, government, law enforcement and humanitarian sectors where its Safe Pro AI software, Safe-Pro USA protective gear and Airborne Response drone-based services can work in synergy to deliver safety and operational efficiency.
