# TechMediaBreaks – Vision Marine Technologies Inc. (NASDAQ: VMAR) Reaffirms E-Motion(TM) as World’s Fastest Electric Boat Technology

Vision Marine Technologies (NASDAQ: VMAR), a leader in high-voltage marine propulsion and multi-brand boat retail, announced that its E-Motion(TM) system remains the fastest electric boat technology globally, holding firm nearly two years after setting a 116-mph electric speed record at the Lake of the Ozarks Shootout with Shaun Torrente Racing. Since then, Vision Marine has advanced the E-Motion(TM) 180E from record-setting demo to commercial use, integrating the 180-horsepower high-voltage outboard into 25 boats across diverse recreational platforms, including pontoons, bowriders, dual consoles, center consoles, and catamarans.
