# TechMediaBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Outlines Milestones Achieved and Strategic Initiatives Driving 2026 Outlook

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE),  following the divestiture tied to its forward merger with Eastside Distilling, updated shareholders on progress toward its 2026 goals. Highlights include launching BeelineEquity, a token-backed liquidity option for homeowners; eliminating more than $7 million in debt by Sept. 5, months ahead of schedule; and reaffirming its target to reach cash-flow positive by January 2026. Additional milestones include Nasdaq uplisting, stronger-than-expected Q2 EPS, AI-driven revenue of more than $170,000, enhanced cybersecurity, $6.2 million in cash with $50 million in equity, and expansion of SaaS and AI platforms such as BlinkQC and MagicBlocks. CEO Nick Liuzza emphasized his $16 million personal investment and minimal salary as part of a leadership team aligned with shareholders in pursuing long-term value creation.
