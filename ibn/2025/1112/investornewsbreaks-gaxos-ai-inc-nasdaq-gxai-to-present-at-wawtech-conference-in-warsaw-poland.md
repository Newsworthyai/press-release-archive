# InvestorNewsBreaks – Gaxos.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: GXAI) to Present at WAWTech Conference in Warsaw, Poland

Gaxos.ai (NASDAQ: GXAI), a company developing artificial intelligence applications across high-growth sectors, today announced its participation in the upcoming WAWTech Conference, to be held Dec. 16–17, 2025, in Warsaw, Poland. “We’re thrilled to take part in this year’s WAWTech Conference,” said Vadim Mats, CEO of Gaxos. “This event offers a tremendous opportunity to engage with technology leaders and innovators across Europe, exchange ideas, and build partnerships that accelerate the growth and adoption of Gaxos AI offerings.”
