# TechMediaBreaks – Gaxos.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: GXAI) Launches UnGPT.ai to Transform AI-Generated Text into Humanlike Content

Gaxos.ai (NASDAQ: GXAI), a company developing artificial intelligence applications across various sectors, announced the official launch of UnGPT.ai, a real-time rewriting tool that converts machine-generated text into natural, human-quality content without compromising meaning. UnGPT leverages a proprietary multi-pass transformation model to deliver undetectable, high-quality output, outperforming leading AI detection tools. The platform includes features such as an adaptive text engine for tone tuning, contextual synonym intelligence, seamless browser integration, and recursive text refinement. CEO Vadim Mats said UnGPT “builds a bridge between raw LLM output and real-world usability.”
