# MiningNewsBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) Advances Duquesne West Exploration with High-Grade Gold Zones

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) announced progress on its exploration program at the Duquesne West property in the Abitibi Greenstone Belt, revealing a clearer geological model and expanded gold potential. The two-year drilling campaign identified high-grade plunge trends and broad low-grade zones, enhancing both scale and grade ahead of a forthcoming Maiden Mineral Resource Estimate. CEO John Florek highlighted newly defined dual-plunge orientations, with easterly and westerly trends opening multiple high-priority drill targets, including intercepts such as 21.7 m of 35.2 g/t Au in hole DQ24-12.
