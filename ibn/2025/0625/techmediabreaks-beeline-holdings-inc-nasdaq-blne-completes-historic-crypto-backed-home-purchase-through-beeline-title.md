# TechMediaBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Completes Historic Crypto-Backed Home Purchase Through Beeline Title

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE),  a fast-growing digital mortgage platform, announced that its subsidiary, Beeline Title Holdings, Inc., has completed what it believes to be one of the first residential real estate transactions funded by a cryptocurrency token backed by real property. The milestone represents a major step forward in bridging decentralized finance with traditional title and escrow services. With broader adoption expected, Beeline is preparing to launch a crypto-powered fractional equity sale product in August 2025, with Beeline Title providing compliant closing services to lenders nationwide.
