# InvestorNewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Subsidiary Closes Crypto-Backed Real Estate Deal

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), a digital mortgage platform, announced that its subsidiary Beeline Title Holdings, Inc. has completed what it believes to be one of the first residential real estate closings funded via a cryptocurrency token backed by real property. The milestone marks a significant step in merging blockchain finance with traditional title and escrow services. Beeline aims to expand its crypto-enabled platform to all lenders, offering infrastructure for compliant transaction disbursement and reconciliation. The company plans to launch a fractional equity sale product in August 2025 through Beeline Loans, Inc., with Beeline Title handling closings.
