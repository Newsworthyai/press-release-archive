# TechMediaBreaks – Knightscope, Inc. (NASDAQ: KSCP) Secures Over $1.3M in New Contracts Amid Surge in Emergency Device and Robot Demand

Knightscope (NASDAQ: KSCP) announced more than $1.3 million in new contracts driven by strong nationwide demand for its emergency communication devices (ECDs) and autonomous security robots (ASRs). The company booked 834 new ECDs across sectors including aviation, healthcare, higher education, and government, while securing multiple K5 robot deals in commercial real estate and gaming. Renewals and expanded service agreements signal growing public sector adoption and sustained client confidence in Knightscope’s security technologies.
