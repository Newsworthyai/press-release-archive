# TechMediaBreaks – Thumzup Media Corp. (NASDAQ: TZUP) Enters $6.5 Million Direct Offering to Support Growth and Crypto Strategy

Thumzup (NASDAQ: TZUP), a digital asset accumulator and advertising industry disruptor, has signed a definitive agreement with investors to raise approximately $6.5 million through a registered direct offering. The deal includes up to 1,083,333 shares of common stock at $6.00 per share, including common stock issuable upon conversion of 179,500 shares of Series C Convertible Preferred Stock. The Series C shares are non-voting and subject to 4.99% and 9.99% beneficial ownership caps. Net proceeds will support general corporate purposes and potential cryptocurrency accumulation. Dominari Securities LLC is the sole placement agent.

 About Thumzup(R)

 Thumzup Media Corporation is democratizing the multi-billion dollar social media branding and marketing industry. Its flagship product, the Thumzup platform, utilizes a robust programmatic advertiser dashboard coupled with a consumer-facing App to enable individuals to get paid cash for posting about participating advertisers on major social media outlets through the Thumzup App. The easy-to-use dashboard allows advertisers to programmatically customize their campaigns. Cash payments are made to App users/creators through PayPal and other digital payment systems.

 Thumzup was featured on CBS Los Angeles and in KTLA.
