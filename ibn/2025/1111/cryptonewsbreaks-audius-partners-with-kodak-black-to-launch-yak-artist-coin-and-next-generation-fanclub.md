# CryptoNewsBreaks – Audius Partners with Kodak Black to Launch $YAK Artist Coin and Next-Generation Fanclub

Audius, the world’s largest decentralized music community and marketplace for artists and fans, today announced a partnership with rap superstar Kodak Black to launch his own Artist Coin, $YAK, powering a next-generation fanclub experience. $YAK holders will gain exclusive access to unreleased music, track stems, and behind-the-scenes content that Kodak Black will release regularly on Audius. The rapper will distribute $YAK coins to fans and residents during his annual Kodak Day celebration on Nov. 11 at the Golden Acres housing project in Pompano Beach, Florida, and debut an unreleased $YAK-gated track on the platform. “The future of music is ownership, and $YAK puts that power in the fans’ hands,” said Kodak’s manager, Killshot. Audius Co-Founder and CEO Roneil Rumburg added, “He’s taking his pioneering spirit to create an entirely new way to engage and reward his most passionate fans.”
