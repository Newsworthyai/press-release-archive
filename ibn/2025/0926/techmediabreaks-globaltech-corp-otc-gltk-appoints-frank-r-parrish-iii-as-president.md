# TechMediaBreaks – GlobalTech Corp. (OTC: GLTK) Appoints Frank R. Parrish, III as President

GlobalTech (OTC: GLTK), a U.S.-headquartered technology holding company specializing in AI, big data, and emerging technologies, announced the appointment of Frank R. Parrish, III as president. Parrish brings more than 25 years of assurance and advisory experience across industries including telecom, biotech, pharma, oil & gas, construction, manufacturing, and software, with expertise in SEC reporting, IPOs, compliance, and risk management. A CPA and Certified Internal Auditor with multiple additional certifications, he previously held senior leadership roles at Big Four and regional accounting firms and holds degrees from the University of Texas at San Antonio and Wayland Baptist University. CEO Dan Green said Parrish’s appointment strengthens GlobalTech’s U.S. management presence and will support the company’s continued market evolution.
