# TechMediaBreaks – ONAR Holding Corporation (OTCQB: ONAR) Appoints JUICE Co-Founder Troy Osinoff as Chief Growth Officer

ONAR (OTCQB: ONAR), a fast-growing marketing technology company and global network of performance-driven agencies, announced the appointment of JUICE Co-Founder Troy Osinoff as Chief Growth Officer following its recent JUICE acquisition via subsidiary Storia. Osinoff, who scaled JUICE to #105 on the Inc. 5000 and previously led customer acquisition at BuzzFeed, will drive ONAR’s platform-wide growth strategy by expanding enterprise client acquisition, deepening partnerships, advancing new technology, and leveraging JUICE’s roster of brands—including L’Oréal, Barstool Sports, Paris Hilton, Ramp, CBDistillery, and Diesel—while strengthening channel relationships with Meta, Google, TikTok, Shopify, and Klaviyo.
