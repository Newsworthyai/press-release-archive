# InvestorNewsBreaks – IBN Launches IgnitionX to Drive Precision Investor Marketing

IBN (InvestorBrandNetwork), has unveiled IgnitionX, a precision-driven marketing solution designed to help publicly traded companies responsibly harness big data and accelerate fair market valuation. By integrating programmatic advertising platforms with advanced analytics, IgnitionX ensures content is delivered to the right investors across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit and more.

 The platform offers four distinct campaign models — Awareness, News Amplification, Awareness & News Amplification, and Lead Generation — with deliverables ranging from multimedia content and influencer support to tailored landing pages. Each campaign is engineered for measurable results, with performance benchmarked against industry standards and detailed monthly reports provided to clients.
