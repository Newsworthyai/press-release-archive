# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Expands Global Reach With Launch of SEGG Digital and SEGG Productions

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a sports, entertainment and gaming conglomerate that owns Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com, announced the launch of two new divisions, SEGG Digital and SEGG Productions, to accelerate international growth, audience engagement and monetization. SEGG Digital, led by Charlie Davis of Jet House and supported by influencer executive Ray Harris, will serve as the company’s marketing arm, deploying data-driven digital and social strategies to scale its flagship brands. SEGG Productions, led by Tamer Hassan, will focus on premium storytelling across music, lifestyle and entertainment, with its first major project, “SAS Unscripted,” hosted by Ant Middleton, already in production. Both ventures will be headquartered in Dubai, leveraging SEGG Media’s pending acquisition of Nook to strengthen global content delivery and shareholder value.
