# InvestorNewsBreaks – G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) Secures Environmental Permit for Oko West Gold Project in Guyana

G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) announced it received the Environmental Permit from Guyana’s Environmental Protection Agency for the Oko West Gold Project, authorizing construction and operation through July 2030. The permit follows nearly two years of environmental and social studies and a multi-stakeholder review process. Oko West is expected to create an average of 1,270 direct permanent jobs during its lifetime, supporting a construction decision later this year. The milestone strengthens G Mining Ventures’ partnership with the Government of Guyana, which was recently ranked a top 10 global mining jurisdiction by the Fraser Institute.
