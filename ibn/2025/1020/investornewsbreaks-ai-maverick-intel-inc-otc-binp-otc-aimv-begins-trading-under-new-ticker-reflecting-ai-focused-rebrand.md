# InvestorNewsBreaks – AI Maverick Intel Inc. (OTC: BINP OTC: AIMV) Begins Trading Under New Ticker Reflecting AI-Focused Rebrand

AI Maverick Intel (OTC: BINP OTC: AIMV), a technology innovator redefining how businesses acquire and engage customers through artificial intelligence, announced that its common shares will begin trading on the OTC Market under the new ticker AIMV effective Oct. 20, 2025. The change follows the company’s rebranding from Bionoid Pharma to AI Maverick Intel, aligning its market identity with its mission to drive customer acquisition and revenue growth through its AI Maverick platform. CEO Wayne Cockburn said the new symbol reflects the company’s evolution into an AI-powered platform focused on partner-led deal flow and intelligent growth.
