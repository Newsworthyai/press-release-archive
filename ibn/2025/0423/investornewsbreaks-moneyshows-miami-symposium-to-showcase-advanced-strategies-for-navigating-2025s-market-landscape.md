# InvestorNewsBreaks – MoneyShow’s Miami Symposium to Showcase Advanced Strategies for Navigating 2025’s Market Landscape

MoneyShow, a leading producer of financial conferences for affluent investors, active traders and financial advisors, has announced the second annual MoneyShow Masters Symposium, set to take place at the Hyatt Regency Miami. With a theme of “Your 2025 Portfolio: Preparing for Maximum Profit,” the event will shine the spotlight on strategies for navigating the current environment and key elements that are likely to impact holdings and drive volatile markets.

 “Amid tariff-driven uncertainty, rising geopolitical tensions, and increased market volatility, our Miami Symposium couldn’t be better timed,” said MoneyShow Editor-in-Chief Mike Larson. “Opportunities are created in times like this because astute investors can deploy advanced market strategies to take advantage of volatility. At this year’s MoneyShow Masters Symposium, invited experts will share invaluable perspectives on building a robust portfolio that can maximize profits in 2025 and beyond.”
