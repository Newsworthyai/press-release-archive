# Texas Republicans Could Delay Trump’s Infrastructure Plans for AI

President Donald Trump recently launched a big plan to help the United States become a leader in artificial intelligence (AI). The project, called Stargate, involves major companies like OpenAI, SoftBank, Oracle, and MGX, an investor backed by the United Arab Emirates. Together, they have promised to invest up to $500 billion to build 20 new data centers across the country. These data centers are important because they provide the computing power needed to train and run AI systems.
