# NetworkNewsBreaks – Newton Golf Company Inc. (NASDAQ: NWTG) to Present at ArcStone-Kingswood Growth Summit 2025 in Toronto

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG), a technology-forward golf company delivering performance-driven innovations through physics-based engineering, announced its management team will present at the ArcStone-Kingswood Growth Summit 2025 on Sept. 18 at The St. Regis Toronto. Chairman and CEO Greg Campbell and CFO Jeff Clayborne will highlight Newton’s strong year-to-date financial results, accelerating adoption of its Motion and Fast Motion shafts on Tour, and long-term growth outlook during the Digital Transformation & Frontier Tech track.

 About Newton Golf Company

 Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG) is a pioneering golf technology company redefining performance through physics-based engineering, precision design, and U.S.-based innovation. Its flagship Newton Motion and Fast Motion shafts are trusted by Tour professionals worldwide, delivering measurable improvements in stability, control, and consistency. Newton’s mission is to empower golfers of all levels with advanced equipment that is engineered for results.
