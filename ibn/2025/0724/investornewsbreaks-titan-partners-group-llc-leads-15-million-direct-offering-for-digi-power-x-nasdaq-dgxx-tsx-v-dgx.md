# InvestorNewsBreaks – Titan Partners Group LLC Leads $15 Million Direct Offering for Digi Power X (NASDAQ: DGXX / TSX.V: DGX)

Titan Partners, a division of American Capital Partners, is acting as sole placement agent for a $15 million registered direct offering by Digi Power X Inc. (NASDAQ: DGXX / TSX.V: DGX), a vertically integrated AI and digital infrastructure company. The offering includes 4,807,693 shares of common stock (or pre-funded warrants) at $3.12 per share, sold to a single new fundamental institutional investor.

 Proceeds will support the global expansion of DGXX’s NeoCloud GPU-as-a-Service platform, manufacturing and distribution of ARMS 200 Tier 3 modular data center pods, and strategic energy-backed deployments. Following the close, DGXX expects to hold over $30 million in cash with no long-term debt. The offering is expected to close on or about July 22, 2025, pending TSX Venture Exchange approval and customary conditions.
