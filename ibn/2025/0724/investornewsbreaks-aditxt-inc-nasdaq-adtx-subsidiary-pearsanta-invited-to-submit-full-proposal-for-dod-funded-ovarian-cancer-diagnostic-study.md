# InvestorNewsBreaks – Aditxt Inc. (NASDAQ: ADTX) Subsidiary Pearsanta Invited to Submit Full Proposal for DoD-Funded Ovarian Cancer Diagnostic Study

Aditxt (NASDAQ: ADTX), a social innovation platform advancing health technologies, announced that its subsidiary Pearsanta, Inc. has been invited to submit a full proposal for the U.S. Department of Defense’s OCRP Pilot Award. The proposal supports Pearsanta’s Mitomic(R) Ovarian Test (MOT(TM)), a novel blood-based diagnostic aimed at early ovarian cancer detection. Pearsanta’s pre-proposal was favorably reviewed for scientific merit and innovation. If awarded, the grant would provide up to $350,000 over two years. The company plans to validate the test in a multi-center clinical study and prepare for commercial launch as a laboratory-developed test.
