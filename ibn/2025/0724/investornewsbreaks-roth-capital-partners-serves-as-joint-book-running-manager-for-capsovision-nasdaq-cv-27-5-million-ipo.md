# InvestorNewsBreaks – Roth Capital Partners Serves as Joint Book-Running Manager for CapsoVision (NASDAQ: CV) $27.5 Million IPO

Roth Capital Partners acted as joint book-running manager for the initial public offering of CapsoVision, Inc. (NASDAQ: CV), a commercial-stage medical technology company focused on AI-powered capsule endoscopy solutions. CapsoVision offered 5,500,000 shares of common stock at $5.00 per share, with gross proceeds totaling approximately $27.5 million before deductions. The shares began trading on the Nasdaq Capital Market on July 2, 2025, under the ticker symbol “CV.” The company also granted underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 825,000 shares at the IPO price.
