# MiningNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Closes C$3.45M Financing to Advance Montauban Gold-Silver Project

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) has closed its previously announced non-brokered private placement for gross proceeds of C$3,453,940.38, issuing over 16 million common shares and warrants. Proceeds will fund mill circuit construction and final mobilization at the Company’s fully permitted Montauban Gold-Silver Project in Quebec. Management and insider participation totaled 1.25 million units, underscoring internal confidence in ESGold’s strategy to achieve near-term production and long-term exploration success.

 About ESGold Corp.

 ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) (FSE: Z7D) is a fully permitted, pre-production resource company at the forefront of clean mining and exploration innovation. With proven expertise in Quebec, the Company is advancing its projects toward production and feasibility while delivering long-term value through sustainable resource recovery and exploration. ESGold’s flagship Montauban property, located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, serves as a model for responsible mining practices, combining near-term production with district-scale discovery potential.
