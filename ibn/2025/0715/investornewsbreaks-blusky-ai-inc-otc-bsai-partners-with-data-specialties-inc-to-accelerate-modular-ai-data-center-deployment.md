# InvestorNewsBreaks – BluSky AI Inc. (OTC: BSAI) Partners With Data Specialties Inc. to Accelerate Modular AI Data Center Deployment

BluSky AI (OTC: BSAI), a leader in modular AI data infrastructure, announced a strategic partnership with Data Specialties Inc. (DSI) to supply pre-manufactured modular data centers for its SkyMod platform. DSI, based in Buena Park, California, brings over 30 years of mission-critical data center expertise, enabling BluSky to rapidly deploy its SkyMod One (1.0 MW) and SkyMod XL (1.7 MW) units nationwide. The collaboration will support BluSky AI’s goal of exceeding 100 MW of operational capacity within 24 months, delivering scalable, high-performance compute environments for AI developers, enterprises and research institutions.
