# TechMediaBreaks – Nightfood Holdings Inc. (OTCQB: NGTF) to Launch AI-Integrated Culinary School With Majority Stake in Modern Culinary Systems

Nightfood Holdings (OTCQB: NGTF), through subsidiary Future Hospitality Ventures Holdings Inc. (FHVH), announced a binding LOI with Stratford Education Group, dba Los Angeles Cooking School, to form Modern Culinary Systems Inc. FHVH will hold a 51% stake and invest up to $150,000 over 18 months to drive AI automation and growth. The new venture aims to become the first U.S. culinary school to integrate AI into its curriculum, targeting a $32.5 billion market. FHVH will oversee operations and hold exclusive rights to acquire 100% of Stratford and LACS within 24 months at a 30% discount.
