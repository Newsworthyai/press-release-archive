# InvestorNewsBreaks – HealthLynked Corp. (OTCQB: HLYK) Engages Meridian Group to Drive Strategic Partnerships and Nasdaq Uplisting Goals

HealthLynked (OTCQB: HLYK), a healthcare technology and patient engagement company, has retained Meridian Group to identify strategic partnerships aimed at accelerating adoption of its platform, boosting revenue, and positioning the company for a potential uplisting to the Nasdaq Capital Market. Meridian will support HealthLynked’s expansion through alliances with insurers, healthcare systems, tech providers, and enterprise partners, and assist in establishing key banking relationships. Meridian’s David Chua will lead the initiative and serve as a strategic advisor. CEO Dr. Michael Dent called the engagement a pivotal step toward scaling HealthLynked’s ecosystem and increasing market visibility.
