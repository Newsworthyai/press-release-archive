# TechMediaBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Names Kristin Miller to Lead AI Mortgage QC Platform BlinkQC

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE),  a digital mortgage platform, announced Kristin Miller will head BlinkQC, its AI-driven mortgage quality control solution. BlinkQC automates the mandatory 10% pre-closing audits required on conventional mortgage files, replacing costly and time-consuming third-party reviews or in-house staffing increases. The platform applies over 800 rules to dramatically cut audit time from hours to minutes and helps prevent costly mortgage buybacks. Already in production at Beeline and undergoing external testing, BlinkQC is set to generate revenue in August through Beeline Labs, the company’s SAAS division. CEO Nick Liuzza praised Miller’s leadership and history with the company, while Miller highlighted the product’s speed, accuracy and value. BlinkQC will be sold on a seat-based pricing model.
