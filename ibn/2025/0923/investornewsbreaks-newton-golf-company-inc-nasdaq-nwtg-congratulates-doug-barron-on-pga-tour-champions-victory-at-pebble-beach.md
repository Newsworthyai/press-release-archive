# InvestorNewsBreaks – Newton Golf Company Inc. (NASDAQ: NWTG) Congratulates Doug Barron on PGA TOUR Champions Victory at Pebble Beach

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG), a technology-forward golf company delivering performance-driven innovations through physics-based engineering, congratulates sponsored athlete Doug Barron on his dramatic win at the PGA TOUR Champions’ Pure Insurance Championship in Monterey, California. Barron, 56, birdied the final three holes, including a decisive putt on Pebble Beach’s par-5 18th, to secure his fourth career PGA TOUR Champions title and first win of 2025. Closing with a 6-under-par 66 for a 12-under total, Barron edged Vijay Singh by one stroke, dedicating the victory to his late father. Ranking in the Top 10 in driving accuracy at 78.6%, Barron showcased the stability of Newton shafts. CEO Greg Campbell praised Barron’s resilience and precision, calling him a true ambassador for the Newton brand. The $2.4 million event concluded with one of the season’s most thrilling finishes, highlighted by Barron’s clutch bunker escape and back-nine charge.
