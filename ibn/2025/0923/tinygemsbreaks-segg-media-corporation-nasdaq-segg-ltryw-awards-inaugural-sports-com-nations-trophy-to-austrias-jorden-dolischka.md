# TinyGemsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Awards Inaugural Sports.com Nations Trophy to Austria’s Jorden Dolischka

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a global sports, entertainment, and gaming conglomerate, celebrated a landmark weekend at Donington Park with Austria’s Jorden Dolischka winning the first Sports.com Nations Trophy. Eight drivers from seven nations competed across qualifying, sprint races, and an endurance finale, with Dolischka earning 40 points to secure the trophy and a funded drive at the Radical Gulf Cup opener in Abu Dhabi on Nov. 1–2, 2025. Sports.com branding was featured throughout the event, highlighting SEGG Media’s commitment to expanding global motorsport visibility. Directors Marc Bircham and Tamer Hassan participated in the weekend, with Hassan presenting the prizes and drawing media attention to the Racing Women initiative.
