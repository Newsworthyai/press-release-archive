# InvestorNewsBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) CEO Appointed to McEwen Inc. Board

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA: 9NH) announced that Chief Executive Officer John Florek has been appointed to the Board of Directors of McEwen Inc. (NYSE: MUX) (TSX: MUX), a leading metals producer operating across the Americas. Florek, who brings more than 30 years of geological and executive leadership experience, will retain his role as CEO of Emperor Metals while serving on McEwen’s Board. The Company emphasized its continued focus on advancing the Duquesne West Gold Project and Lac Pelletier in Quebec. Emperor Metals views the appointment as a reflection of its leadership strength and a strategic opportunity to deepen industry relationships.
