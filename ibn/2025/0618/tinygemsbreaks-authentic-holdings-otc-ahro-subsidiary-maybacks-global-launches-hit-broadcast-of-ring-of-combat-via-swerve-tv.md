# TinyGemsBreaks – Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) Subsidiary Maybacks Global Launches Hit Broadcast of Ring of Combat via Swerve TV

Authentic Holdings (OTC: AHRO) announced a major licensing agreement between its subsidiary Maybacks Global Entertainment, LLC and Swerve TV, marked by a successful debut broadcast of “Ring of Combat” from the Tropicana in Atlantic City on June 13. The event exceeded expectations, drawing substantial viewership and social media traction across Swerve TV’s extensive platform, which reaches 80% of U.S. and Canadian households and over 5 million combat sports fans. The collaboration showcased the legacy of “Ring of Combat,” a promotion known for launching 165 fighters into the UFC, including champions like Matt Serra and Merab Dvalishvili. The broadcast success has laid the groundwork for future events and will be available on demand through the upcoming iDreamCTV app.
