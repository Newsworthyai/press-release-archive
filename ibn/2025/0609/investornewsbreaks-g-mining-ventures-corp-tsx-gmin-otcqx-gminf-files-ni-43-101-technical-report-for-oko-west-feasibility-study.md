# InvestorNewsBreaks – G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) Files NI 43-101 Technical Report for Oko West Feasibility Study

G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) has filed a National Instrument 43-101 technical report for the feasibility study of its wholly owned Oko West gold project in Guyana. The report, effective April 28 and issued June 6, 2025, was prepared by independent Qualified Persons covering key areas including capital and operating costs, metallurgy, geology, mine planning, and environmental permitting.

 G Mining Ventures Corp. is a mining company engaged in the acquisition, exploration and development of precious metal projects to capitalize on the value uplift from successful mine development.
