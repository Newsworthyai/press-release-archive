# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Secures $4 Million Strategic Investment as Part of $15 Million Funding Round

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX), a smart home platform technology company with over 97 issued and pending patents, announced it has raised approximately $4 million from strategic investors through preferred stock priced at $2.00 per share. The investment is part of a broader $15 million financing round led by The Shaner Group, with additional participation from SKYX insiders including President Steve Schmidt and Co-CEOs Lenny Sokolow and John Campi. The funding will support general working capital and corporate initiatives as the company advances its home innovation platform and e-commerce portfolio.
