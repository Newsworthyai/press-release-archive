# CannabisNewsBreaks – Software Effective Solutions Corp. (d/b/a MedCana) (OTC: SFWJ) Expands Into Australia With Acquisition of Cannabis Import and Distribution Licenses

Software Effective Solutions (d/b/a MedCana) (OTC: SFWJ) doing business as Medcana, announced it is acquiring licenses to import and distribute pharmaceutical cannabis in Australia, marking a major step in the Company’s global expansion strategy. The move strengthens Medcana’s international presence and enables it to supply high-quality, research-based medical cannabis products to meet rising demand in the region. With this addition, Medcana now extends operations across Latin America and the Asia-Pacific, reinforcing its position in the global pharmaceutical cannabis supply chain.
