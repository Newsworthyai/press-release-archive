# TechMediaBreaks – Nightfood Holdings Inc. (NGTF) Leading Hospitality Tech Revolution with Innovative Solutions

Nightfood Holdings (OTCQB: NGTF) is modernizing the hospitality industry with its AI-driven robotics solutions and innovative Robots-as-a-Service (“RaaS”) model, offered through subsidiaries Skytech and Future Hospitality Ventures. Addressing the ongoing labor crisis, NGTF enables hotel operators to integrate cutting-edge robotics without significant upfront costs. This model boosts operational efficiency and guest satisfaction while also creating customer-retention value and recurring revenue streams. With successful deployments in its first properties from InterContinental Hotels Group and Hilton Worldwide Holdings, as well as a rapidly expanding robotics portfolio, NGTF is positioned at the forefront of the service robotics market, which is projected to reach $107.75 billion by 2030 (ibn.fm/k7Skz).
