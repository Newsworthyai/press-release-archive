# MiningNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Begins On-Site Construction at Montauban Gold-Silver Project

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) has commenced on-site construction and mill assembly at its fully permitted Montauban Project in Quebec, marking the final phase before production. Backed by its recent C$3.45 million financing, the Company is mobilizing heavy equipment, expanding the mill building, and installing core infrastructure, including power and monitoring systems. ESGold is targeting initial gold and silver production by early Q4 2025, with additional catalysts ahead including an updated economic assessment and ANT survey results aimed at unlocking deeper mineralization potential.

 About ESGold Corp.

 ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) (FSE: Z7D) is a fully permitted, pre-production resource company at the forefront of clean mining and exploration innovation. With proven expertise in Quebec, the Company is advancing its projects toward production and feasibility while delivering long-term value through sustainable resource recovery and exploration. ESGold’s flagship Montauban property, located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, serves as a model for responsible mining practices, combining near-term production with district-scale discovery potential.
