# TechMediaBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Reports Strong Momentum with Expanding Deployments, Strategic Partnerships, and Path to Profitability

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX), a platform technology company with more than 100 pending and issued patents and over 60 lighting and home décor websites, issued a corporate update highlighting continued financial and operational progress. The company reported $15.7 million in cash as of June 30, 2025, followed by a $3.25 million raise from an existing lead investor in September. SKYX showcased its plug-and-play smart technologies during a Marriott Hotel renovation and secured major deployments, including more than 10,000 units for a 278-apartment project in Austin, Texas, and over 500,000 units for Miami’s $3 billion Urban Smart Home City. The company also announced a U.S. manufacturing partnership with Profab Electronics to strengthen its domestic supply chain, supplementing global production in Vietnam, Taiwan, China, and Cambodia. SKYX’s six consecutive quarters of revenue growth and 54% reduction in operating cash use reflect significant progress toward its goal of achieving cash-flow positivity by the end of 2025, with Titan Partners Group serving as a key strategic advisor in financing initiatives and market expansion.
