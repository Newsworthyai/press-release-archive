# InvestorNewsBreaks – FAVO Capital (OTC: FAVO) President Shaun Quin Joins Forbes Business Council

FAVO Capital (OTC: FAVO), a diversified finance company providing alternative revenue-based funding solutions to small and medium-sized businesses across the United States along with strategic investments in income-producing real estate, announced that President Shaun Quin has been accepted into the Forbes Business Council, an invitation-only network of business leaders. Quin was selected based on his leadership record and more than two decades of experience in capital markets, alternative finance and organizational transformation. As a member, he will contribute thought leadership on Forbes.com and participate in panels and events. His first article, “Why Some Small Businesses Turn to Private Credit — And What Owners Should Consider,” was published Aug. 19 on Forbes.com.

 About FAVO Capital, Inc.

 FAVO Capital, Inc. (OTC: FAVO) is a diversified finance company. FAVO provides alternative revenue-based funding solutions to small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) across the United States, complemented by strategic investments in income-producing real estate. Since inception, FAVO has supported more than 10,000 businesses through the purchase of future receipts, while building a portfolio of stabilized, cash-flowing real estate assets that strengthen its balance sheet and expand its collateral base. Headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, FL, with operations in New York and the Dominican Republic, FAVO Capital is committed to financial transparency, sustainable growth, and creating long-term value for its shareholders.
