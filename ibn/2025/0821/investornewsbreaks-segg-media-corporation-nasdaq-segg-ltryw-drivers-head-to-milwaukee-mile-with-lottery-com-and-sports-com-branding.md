# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Drivers Head to Milwaukee Mile With Lottery.com and Sports.com Branding

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), through its backing of Louis Foster, Callum Ilott, and Seb Murray, will be represented at the Milwaukee Mile this weekend as the 2025 NTT INDYCAR and INDY NXT by Firestone seasons near their conclusion. Foster, who clinched the 2024 INDY NXT championship at Milwaukee, returns with Rahal Letterman Lanigan Racing leading the INDYCAR Rookie of the Year standings, carrying Sports.com branding across his car and helmet. Ilott, fresh off consecutive top-six finishes with PREMA Racing, features Lottery.com’s logo prominently on his helmet. Rising Scottish talent Murray will make his Indy NXT debut at Milwaukee with Andretti Cape, running Lottery.com on his rear wing and Sports.com on his front wings. With SEGG Media’s brands showcased across cars, helmets, and racewear, Lottery.com and Sports.com will gain global broadcast visibility throughout the weekend’s coverage on Fox, FS1, FS2, and Sky Sports F1.
