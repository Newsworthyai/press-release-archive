# AINewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Expands Stake in MagicBlocks With $225,000 Investment

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE),  a digital mortgage platform redefining the path to homeownership, announced a further strategic investment in MagicBlocks, committing an additional $225,000 in its current SAFE round. Beeline, which incubated MagicBlocks, now holds a 47.6% stake. MagicBlocks powers Beeline’s AI agent “Bob,” which has delivered 6x stronger lead conversion rates than humans and generated $162,000 in Q2 revenue during a limited release. Since launching in June, MagicBlocks has signed 18 clients—more than half outside the U.S.—to its customizable AI sales and service platform. Beeline said the partnership creates recurring SaaS revenue opportunities and broadens its reach beyond the U.S. while advancing its own AI infrastructure, including the BlinkQC quality-control product for mortgage compliance.
