# Scientists Turn Bacteria into Trojan Horses to Deliver Anti-Cancer Viruses into Tumors

Scientists at Columbia University have engineered a cancer treatment that leverages bacteria to smuggle cancer-killing viruses into tumors in a method that sidesteps the defenses of the immune system. This groundbreaking approach utilizes two vital weapons against the cancer; the bacteria are drawn to cancer cells, and the viruses inside the bacteria deliver the killer blow to the tumor cells after being delivered there by the bacteria.
