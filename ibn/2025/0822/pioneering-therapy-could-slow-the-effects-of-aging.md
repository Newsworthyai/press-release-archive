# Pioneering Therapy Could Slow the Effects of Aging

A new groundbreaking therapy could slow down or even reverse aging by recharging body cells with new “batteries” that provide new energy for bodily function. The new therapy utilizes mitochondria from the placenta of infants, which are then transplanted into older people or those suffering from diseases that are currently regarded as incurable.

 There is still plenty of work to do to refine and demonstrate the efficacy of this therapy in the wider population, but the findings so far hold great promise.
