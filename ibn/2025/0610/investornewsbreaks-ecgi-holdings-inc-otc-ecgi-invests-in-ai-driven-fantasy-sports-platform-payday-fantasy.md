# InvestorNewsBreaks – ECGI Holdings Inc. (OTC: ECGI) Invests in AI-Driven Fantasy Sports Platform Payday Fantasy

ECGI Holdings (OTC: ECGI) announced a new investment by its Uplist Ventures arm in Payday Fantasy, a next-gen fantasy sports platform that blends artificial intelligence with social engagement. Unlike traditional betting apps, Payday combines smart picks, real-time advice, and interactive features inspired by DraftKings, Discord, and Twitch. The move aligns with ECGI’s strategy to back early-stage companies with strong user traction and disruptive potential. Payday has already attracted over 15,000 users in a limited rollout and plans to launch Version 2 soon to boost retention and growth.
