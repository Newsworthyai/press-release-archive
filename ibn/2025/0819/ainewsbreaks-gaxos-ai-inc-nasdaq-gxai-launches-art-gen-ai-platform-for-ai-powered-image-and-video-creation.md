# AINewsBreaks – Gaxos.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: GXAI) Launches Art-Gen.AI Platform for AI-Powered Image and Video Creation

Gaxos.ai (NASDAQ: GXAI), a developer of artificial intelligence applications across high-growth sectors, today announced the official launch of Art-Gen.AI, an AI image and video creation platform designed to make professional-grade content effortless for anyone, anywhere. Art-Gen combines state-of-the-art AI models from Google, Stability AI, and PixVerse with Gaxos’ proprietary enhancements to deliver unmatched speed, detail, and flexibility. With simple text prompts or reference images, users can generate cinematic visuals, hyper-realistic imagery, or animated video content at a fraction of traditional production time and cost. Launching into a global generative AI market forecasted to reach hundreds of billions of dollars annually over the next decade, Art-Gen.AI is offered on a subscription basis, providing Gaxos with a scalable, high-margin, recurring-revenue model. “Art-Gen is not just a creative tool — it’s a scalable technology platform with massive commercial potential,” said Vadim Mats, CEO of Gaxos. “By combining best-in-class AI models with our seamless, intuitive interface, and a multitude of enhancements, we are empowering creators, brands, and enterprises to accelerate content production while driving long-term value for our shareholders.”
