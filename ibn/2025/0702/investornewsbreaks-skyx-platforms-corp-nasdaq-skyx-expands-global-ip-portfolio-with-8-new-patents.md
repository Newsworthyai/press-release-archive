# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Expands Global IP Portfolio With 8 New Patents

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX) a smart home platform technology company, announced the issuance of eight new patents across the U.S., India, Japan, U.K., France, Germany, Italy, and Spain, bringing its total to over 100 issued and pending patents, including 45 issued. The Company’s portfolio supports its AI-enabled plug-and-play smart home ecosystem, with applications across lighting, safety sensors, ceiling fans, and heaters. SKYX estimates its total addressable market exceeds $500 billion, and Executive Chairman Rani Kohen said the new patents reinforce SKYX’s global leadership in advanced smart and safe home technologies.
