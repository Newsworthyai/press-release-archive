# InvestorNewsBreaks – Lelantos Holdings, Inc. (OTC PINK: LNTO) Gains OTC Level 2 Quotation, Approved for Improved Disclosure Service

Lelantos Holdings (OTC PINK: LNTO) has qualified for OTC Level 2 quotations on the OTC Markets platform, enhancing real-time trading visibility and market transparency for investors. Level 2 access provides detailed bid and ask data across all market makers, offering deeper insight into stock activity. CEO Nathan Puente called the milestone a key step in the company’s capital markets strategy. Lelantos also received approval to join OTC Markets’ Improved Disclosure Service starting July 2025, a move COO Joshua Weaver said reflects the company’s commitment to transparency, regulatory alignment, and long-term investor trust.
