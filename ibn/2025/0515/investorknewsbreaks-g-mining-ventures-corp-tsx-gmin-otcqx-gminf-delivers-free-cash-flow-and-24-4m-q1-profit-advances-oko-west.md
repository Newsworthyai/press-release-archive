# InvestorkNewsBreaks – G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) Delivers Free Cash Flow and $24.4M Q1 Profit, Advances Oko West

G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) reported Q1 2025 gold production of 35,578 ounces at an all-in sustaining cost of $960/oz, generating $24.4 million in net income and $36.0 million in free cash flow. With $149 million in cash and no lost time or recordable incidents, the company remains on track to meet 2025 guidance. GMIN advanced early works at Oko West and released a feasibility study outlining a $2.2 billion NPV at $2,500 gold. Proven and probable reserves rose to 6.7 million ounces, and the company gained inclusion in three major mining indices, boosting institutional visibility.

 About G Mining Ventures Corp.

 G Mining Ventures Corp. is a mining company engaged in the acquisition, exploration and development of precious metal projects to capitalize on the value uplift from successful mine development. GMIN is well-positioned to grow into the next mid-tier precious metals producer by leveraging strong access to capital and proven development expertise. GMIN is currently anchored by the Tocantinzinho Gold Mine (” TZ “) in Brazil and Oko West Project in Guyana , both mining friendly and prospective jurisdictions.
