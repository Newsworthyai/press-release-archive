# MiningNewsBreaks – Fathom Nickel Inc. (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) Launches Summer Program at Gochager Lake

Fathom Nickel (CSE: FNI) (FSE: 6Q5) (OTCQB: FNICF) has mobilized its geological crew to the Gochager Lake Project in Saskatchewan, launching a three-week summer field program delayed by regional wildfires. Crews have completed a 3.5 km trail and a 100-meter grid to support future geophysical work. The program includes mapping and sampling northeast and southwest of the historic deposit, with 4,000 soil samples to be collected across areas showing strong multi-element anomalies identified in 2024, including nickel values up to 1,650 ppm. Fieldwork will also target potential outcrops and priority zones identified by a 2008 VTEM survey.
