# InvestorNewsBreaks – Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO) Expands Golf Cart Production to Vietnam to Boost Supply Chain and Meet Demand

Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO) announced it will begin receiving deliveries of its new six-seater golf carts from a manufacturing partner in Vietnam, complementing production at its Garland, Texas facility. The Vietnam expansion supports supply chain diversification, improved lead times, and cost efficiencies. The feature-rich MVR4X model offers a 60km range, 48V motor, and advanced safety features, positioning Massimo to meet growing U.S. demand while protecting long-term margins.

 About Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO)

 Massimo Group is a manufacturer and distributor of manufacturers of powersports products.
