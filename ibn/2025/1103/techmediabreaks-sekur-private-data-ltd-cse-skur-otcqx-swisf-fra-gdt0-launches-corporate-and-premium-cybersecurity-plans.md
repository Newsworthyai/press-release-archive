# TechMediaBreaks – Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FRA: GDT0) Launches Corporate and Premium Cybersecurity Plans

This article has been disseminated on behalf of Sekur Private Data and may include paid advertising.

 Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FRA: GDT0), a Swiss-hosted cybersecurity and private communications platform, announced the official launch of its new Corporate and Premium plans for secure email, messaging and data transmission. The Business plan is priced at US$90 per month or US$900 per year, while the Corporate and Premium plans start at US$150 per month or US$1,500 per year. Sekur also plans to introduce an AI intrusion-free voice and video conferencing suite in Q1 2026, priced between US$2,500 and US$3,500 per license annually. Targeting high-value clients such as executives, government officials and HNWIs, Sekur aims to address the rising cost of AI-driven business email compromise attacks, which average US$4.89 million per incident according to IBM.
