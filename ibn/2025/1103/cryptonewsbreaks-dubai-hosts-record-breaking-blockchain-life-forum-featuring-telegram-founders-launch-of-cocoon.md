# CryptoNewsBreaks – Dubai Hosts Record-Breaking Blockchain Life Forum, Featuring Telegram Founder’s Launch of ‘Cocoon’

Blockchain Life 2025, the 15th edition of the world-renowned crypto forum, broke records in Dubai with 16,730 attendees from 130 countries, solidifying its status as a premier global event. Over 160 speakers, including leaders and representatives from top companies such as Tether, Mastercard, Ripple, and Google Cloud, shared the latest trends in digital assets, Web3, and mining. A key highlight featured Telegram founder Pavel Durov, who took the stage to unveil Cocoon, a new network merging the TON blockchain with decentralized AI technologies — an announcement that quickly became a centerpiece of industry discussion. With 200 exhibiting companies, the World Series of Crypto Trading finals, and an exclusive afterparty headlined by Akon, the event blended high-level insights with immersive experiences and networking.
