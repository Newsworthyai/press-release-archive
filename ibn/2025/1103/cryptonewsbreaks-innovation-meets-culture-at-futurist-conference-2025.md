# CryptoNewsBreaks – Innovation Meets Culture at Futurist Conference 2025

The Blockchain Futurist Conference Florida 2025 is set to debut in Miami on Nov. 5–6, bringing together innovators, investors, and creators at the intersection of Web3, crypto, and AI. Hosted at the Hard Rock Guitar Resort’s DAER venue, the two-day event will feature multiple stages including Argentum AI, as well as keynote presentations on cutting-edge trends, tokenized economies, and global crypto adoption, as well as panels on DeFi, stablecoins, DAOs, NFTs, and decentralized governance. Startup showcases, hands-on workshops, live demos, fireside chats, and cultural activations will spark innovation and accelerate real-world application. Highlighting the fusion of emerging tech and cultural influence, Futurist 2025 emphasizes discovery, networking, and strategies shaping tomorrow’s digital world.
