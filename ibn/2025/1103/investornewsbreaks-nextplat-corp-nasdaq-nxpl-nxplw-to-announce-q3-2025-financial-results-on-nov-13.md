# InvestorNewsBreaks – NextPlat Corp. (NASDAQ: NXPL, NXPLW) to Announce Q3 2025 Financial Results on Nov. 13

NextPlat (NASDAQ: NXPL, NXPLW), a global consumer products and services company providing healthcare and technology solutions through e-commerce and retail channels, will release its financial results for the quarter ended Sept. 30, 2025, before market open on Nov. 13, 2025. CEO and President of Global Operations David Phipps, CFO Amanda Ferrio, and Vice President of Healthcare Operations Birute Norkute will host a conference call that day at 8:30 a.m. ET to discuss the results and other recent developments.

 To view the full press release, visit: https://ibn.fm/SNtRu

 About NextPlat Corp.

 Nextplat is a global consumer products and services company providing healthcare and technology solutions through e-Commerce and retail channels worldwide. Through acquisitions, joint ventures, and collaborations, the Company seeks to assist businesses in selling their goods online, domestically, and internationally, allowing customers and partners to optimize their e-Commerce presence and revenue. NextPlat currently operates an e-Commerce communications division offering voice, data, tracking, and IoT products and services worldwide as well as pharmacy and healthcare data management services in the United States through its subsidiary, Progressive Care.
