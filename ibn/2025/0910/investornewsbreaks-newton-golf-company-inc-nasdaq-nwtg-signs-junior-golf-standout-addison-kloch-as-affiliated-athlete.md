# InvestorNewsBreaks – Newton Golf Company Inc. (NASDAQ: NWTG) Signs Junior Golf Standout Addison Kloch as Affiliated Athlete

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG), a technology-forward golf company delivering performance-driven innovations through physics-based engineering, announced that Addison Kloch, one of Southern California’s top junior golfers, has joined the Newton Golf team as an affiliated athlete. A member of the Class of 2026 from Agoura Hills, California, Kloch recently announced her verbal commitment to play Division I golf at California State University, Fullerton. Her achievements include three-time Marmonte League First Team honors, a 2024 All-Ventura County First Team selection, recognition as California Junior Player of the Year in the Championship Girls Division, and an AJGA Top-300 national ranking. Kloch has credited Newton Motion shafts for adding stability and consistency to her game, further highlighting the company’s role in supporting her rapid ascent in the sport.
