# InvestorNewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Reports 35% Sequential Growth in Q3 Originations and 37% Revenue Increase, Projects Profitability in Early 2026

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), an emerging digital mortgage lender and SaaS platform, reported third-quarter 2025 originations of $69.8 million, up 35% from Q2, with net revenue rising 37% to $2.3 million. Operating expenses declined 8% to $5.2 million, and adjusted EBITDA improved to $(2.0) million from $(2.8) million in Q2. Beeline posted a Q3 net loss of $(4.0) million, including a $718,000 one-time disposition expense related to its spirits business. October originations reached $35.3 million—the company’s strongest month since the mortgage downturn—with Q4 originations expected to increase approximately 65% from Q3. Management projects operating profitability for Beeline Loans in November 2025 and for Beeline Holdings by early Q1 2026.
