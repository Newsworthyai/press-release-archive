# TechMediaBreaks – Micropolis Holding Co. (NYSE American: MCRP) to Showcase Autonomous Security and Mobility Solutions at DRIFTx in Abu Dhabi

Micropolis (NYSE American: MCRP), a pioneer in unmanned ground vehicles and AI-driven security systems, will participate in DRIFTx, Abu Dhabi’s leading exhibition for smart and autonomous mobility, taking place Nov. 10–12, 2025, at Yas Marina Circuit. The company will exhibit its advanced robotics lineup, including the Robotic Forestry Unit, M0-2 Border Unit, and Dubai Police M0-1 and M0-2 Patrol vehicles—each designed to enhance automation, safety, and operational efficiency across forestry, border, and urban security applications.

 About Micropolis Holding Co.

 Micropolis is a UAE-based company specializing in the design, development, and manufacturing of unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs), AI systems, and smart infrastructure for urban, security, and industrial applications. The Company’s vertically integrated capabilities cover everything from mechatronics and embedded systems to AI software and high-level autonomy.
