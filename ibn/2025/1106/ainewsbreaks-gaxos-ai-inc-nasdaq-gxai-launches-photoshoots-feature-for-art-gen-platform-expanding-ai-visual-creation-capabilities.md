# AINewsBreaks – Gaxos.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: GXAI) Launches ‘Photoshoots’ Feature for Art-Gen Platform, Expanding AI Visual Creation Capabilities

Gaxos.ai (NASDAQ: GXAI), a developer of artificial intelligence applications across high-growth sectors, has launched Photoshoots, a new feature for its Art-Gen AI image and video creation platform that enables users to generate consistent, cinematic, studio-quality visuals from any photo or concept in seconds. CEO Vadim Mats said the addition transforms Art-Gen into a full visual studio, enhancing creative control and animation while positioning Gaxos to lead in the AI-driven creator economy, projected to exceed $480 billion globally by 2027.
