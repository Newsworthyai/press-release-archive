# TechMediaBreaks – Parler Technologies, Inc. Launches $50 Million Private Placement Ahead of Planned Public Listing

Parler Technologies has initiated a Regulation D, Rule 506(c) private placement to raise up to $50 million, with the option to expand to $100 million, ahead of a planned Nasdaq or NYSE listing in late 2026 or early 2027. Managed by Digital Offering LLC, the raise will support Parler’s fully rebuilt, end-to-end technology ecosystem, which includes Triton Cloud, Edgecast CDN, Kyvo, PlayTV, Cartix, ParlerNews, and the Optio Blockchain. Chairman and CEO Yasser Elgebaly said the offering will accelerate Parler’s mission to build a self-sustaining, creator-driven economy where users maintain ownership of their data, earnings, and content.

 About Parler Technologies, Inc.

 Parler Technologies powers the Pulse ecosystem, which includes the Parler social network, PlayTV streaming service, Cartix marketplace, and Kyvo digital wallet. These products run on Triton Cloud and Edgecast CDN, giving creators speed, scalability, and independence to operate outside the constraints of traditional tech platforms.
