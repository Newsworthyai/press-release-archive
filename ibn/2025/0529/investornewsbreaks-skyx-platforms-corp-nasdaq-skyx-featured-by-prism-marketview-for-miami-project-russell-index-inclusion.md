# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Featured by PRISM MarketView for Miami Project, Russell Index Inclusion

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX) was spotlighted by PRISM MarketView after announcing it will deploy over 500,000 smart home devices in a $3 billion mixed-use development in Miami’s Little River District, where it will serve as the exclusive smart home infrastructure provider. The company also revealed its upcoming inclusion in the Russell 2000(R) and Russell 3000(R) Indexes, effective June 27, 2025, and recently reported record Q1 revenue of $20.1 million while reaffirming its outlook for second-half cash flow positivity.
