# InvestorNewsBreaks – Brera Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: BREA) Partners with Toronto Blizzard to Launch Global Youth Soccer Development Program

Brera Holdings (NASDAQ: BREA) announced a new advisory agreement with Toronto Blizzard Corp., Canada’s top soccer scholarship program, to launch an international grassroots development initiative for boys and girls. The program will integrate Blizzard’s academy system with Brera’s professional club network across Europe, Asia, and Africa—including Serie B’s SS Juve Stabia and North Macedonia’s Brera Strumica FC and Brera Tiverija FC—to create global pathways to the professional ranks for youth players. Soccer Hall of Famer and Blizzard owner Giuseppe “Joe” Parolini will serve as Brera’s Academy Sports Director, North America, as both organizations prepare for talent camps and international exchanges beginning in summer 2026.
