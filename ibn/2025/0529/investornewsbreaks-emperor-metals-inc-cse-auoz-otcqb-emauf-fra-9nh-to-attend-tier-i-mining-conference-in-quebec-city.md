# InvestorNewsBreaks – Emperor Metals Inc. (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA:9NH) to Attend Tier I Mining Conference in Québec City

Emperor Metals (CSE: AUOZ) (OTCQB: EMAUF) (FRA:9NH) will participate in THE Mining Investment Event of the North, Canada’s premier Tier I mining conference, held June 3–5 in Québec City. Company management will be available for one-on-one investor meetings and will showcase core samples from its 2024 exploration programs at the Coreshack display during evening networking events. Emperor is currently advancing its Duquesne West Gold Project and recently acquired Lac Pelletier Project, which offers near-term production potential.
