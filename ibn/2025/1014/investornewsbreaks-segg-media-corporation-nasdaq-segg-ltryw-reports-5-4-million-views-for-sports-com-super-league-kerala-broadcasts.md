# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Reports 5.4 Million Views for Sports.com Super League Kerala Broadcasts

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), the global sports, entertainment, and gaming group, announced that Sports.com surpassed 5.4 million total views across its live broadcasts of the Super League Kerala and related digital channels, marking a major milestone in its growth as a global streaming platform. The achievement reflects record engagement across India and international markets as Sports.com expands its high-impact content slate, including a new collaboration with Ant Middleton on SAS: Unscripted and the continued rollout of GOATS, its premium multi-genre sports series.
