# DefenseNewsBreaks – Datavault AI Inc. (NASDAQ: DVLT) Expands WiSA Portfolio with Falcon Transmitter Module, Connect App, and Certification Platform

Datavault AI (NASDAQ: DVLT), through its Acoustic Science Division’s Wireless Sound Association (WiSA), introduced three new solutions designed to advance interoperable, high-performance wireless audio: the WiSA E Falcon Transmitter Module, WiSA Connect Mobile Application, and WiSA Certification Application. The Falcon 5GHz module delivers uncompressed, low-latency multichannel audio optimized for soundbars, TVs, and home theater systems, outperforming traditional 2.4GHz Bluetooth with superior RF performance and AES 256-bit encryption. The WiSA Connect app enables users to control and customize WiSA E Certified devices through their home Wi-Fi network, while the WiSA Certification app streamlines product development and interoperability testing for manufacturers. CEO Nathaniel Bradley said the new suite positions Datavault AI to meet rising demand for seamless, premium sound experiences and extend its audio technologies into emerging areas such as robotics and Web3 applications.
