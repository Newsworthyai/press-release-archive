# TechMediaBreaks – Micropolis Holding Co. (NYSE American: MCRP) and Dubai Police Launch Autonomous Police Patrol at Dubai Global Village

Micropolis (NYSE American: MCRP), a pioneer in unmanned ground vehicles and AI-driven security solutions, announced the official deployment of the Autonomous Police Patrol at Dubai Global Village in collaboration with Dubai Police. The AI-powered patrol, designed and manufactured by Micropolis Robotics, enhances real-time surveillance, behavior analysis, and rapid response capabilities through advanced detection and fleet management software. The vehicle, named DPR-02, will enter operational service on Oct. 15, 2025, marking Dubai Police’s first official deployment of autonomous patrol technology and reinforcing the Emirate’s leadership in smart policing and AI-driven public safety innovation.
