# InvestorNewsBreaks – DealFlow Events to Host 2025 PIPEs Conference at Hard Rock Hotel in Hollywood, Florida

DealFlow Events will host The PIPEs Conference on Nov. 12–13, 2025, at the Hard Rock Hotel and Casino in Hollywood, Florida, bringing together public issuers, investors, and industry leaders for two days of expert panels, private meetings, and high-level networking. Building on the success of its 2024 relaunch, the event will feature sessions on regulatory developments, market trends, and transaction strategies, with insights from top firms including Loeb & Loeb, Sheppard Mullin, Maxim Group, and Roth Capital Partners. Attendees will engage in presentations and roundtables covering topics such as PIPEs in DeSPAC deals, crypto-related investments, valuation practices, and the evolving regulatory landscape.
