# TechMediaBreaks – Knightscope, Inc. (NASDAQ: KSCP) Surpasses $1 Million in New Contracts and Renewals, Extending Market Momentum

Knightscope (NASDAQ: KSCP), a leader in autonomous security robots and emergency communication devices, announced it has exceeded an additional $1 million in new contracts and renewals, marking continued growth following similar milestones in recent months. The gains were driven by strong demand for the company’s Emergency Communication Devices across healthcare, aviation, education, and government sectors, alongside new deployments of its K5 Autonomous Security Robots in housing, retail, and hospitality. Knightscope said the expansion reinforces nationwide adoption of its AI-driven safety solutions and supports its strategy of building predictable, subscription-based revenue.
