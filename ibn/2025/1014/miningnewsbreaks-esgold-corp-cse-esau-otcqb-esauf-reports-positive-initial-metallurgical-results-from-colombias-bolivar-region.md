# MiningNewsBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Reports Positive Initial Metallurgical Results from Colombia’s Bolívar Region

ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) announced encouraging initial metallurgical results from Deister table tests on bulk tailings samples collected in Colombia’s Bolívar region under its Planta Magdalena MOU. Bench-scale work supervised by Edmond St-Jean, Ing., confirmed the material’s amenability to simple gravity separation with mean feed grades of ~4.88 g/t gold and ~35.7 g/t silver and gold recoveries reaching up to 52% without chemical reagents. CEO Gordon Robb said the results validate ESGold’s clean, low-reagent process design and highlight strong potential economics as the company advances pilot testing and optimization toward scalable, low-impact gold and silver recovery.
