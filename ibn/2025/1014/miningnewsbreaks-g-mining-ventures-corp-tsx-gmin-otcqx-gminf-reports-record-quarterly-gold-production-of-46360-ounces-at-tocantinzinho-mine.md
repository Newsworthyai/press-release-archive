# MiningNewsBreaks – G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) Reports Record Quarterly Gold Production of 46,360 Ounces at Tocantinzinho Mine

G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) announced Q3 2025 production results from its Tocantinzinho Gold Mine in Pará, Brazil, reporting a record 46,360 ounces of gold produced—bringing year-to-date output to 124,525 ounces. The mill processed 1,094 kt of ore at an average grade of 1.43 g/t with recoveries of 92.3%, achieving 92% of nameplate capacity. President and CEO Louis-Pierre Gignac said the results mark the company’s strongest operational quarter to date, driven by continued improvements in both mining and milling performance.
