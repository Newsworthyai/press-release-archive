# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Launches Redesigned Corporate Website to Mark Turnaround and Growth Phase

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a sports, entertainment and gaming conglomerate that owns Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com, announced the launch of its newly redesigned corporate website, seggmediacorp.com, a digital hub signaling the completion of the Company’s turnaround and the start of its next phase of growth. The site highlights SEGG Media’s evolution into a content-first platform focused on fan engagement, younger audiences and immersive digital experiences, while centralizing access to Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com. The relaunch underscores SEGG Media’s unified corporate identity, investor transparency, fan engagement initiatives and ecosystem integration, positioning the Company as a forward-thinking media brand built for scale and sustainable shareholder value.
