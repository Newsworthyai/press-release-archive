# Researchers Develop AI Toolkit for Predicting Diseases Prior to Symptom Onset

A team of researchers from the University of Utah has developed an innovative software tool called RiskPath. The open-source platform uses explainable AI (XAI) to forecast the likelihood of people developing long-term and progressive health conditions years before any symptoms start to show. This development could mark a major shift in how preventive care is approached in the medical field.
