# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Backs High-Speed Action at INDYCAR Iowa Speedway Doubleheader

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) announced that its Lottery.com and Sports.com-sponsored drivers will compete in this weekend’s NTT INDYCAR Series and INDY NXT by Firestone doubleheader at Iowa Speedway. The 0.875-mile oval, dubbed the “Fastest Short Track on the Planet,” will test teams with its demanding layout. PREMA Racing’s Callum Ilott and Robert Shwartzman aim to capitalize on recent progress following June testing at the track. Andretti Global, riding momentum from its 300th win at Mid-Ohio, brings strong credentials to Iowa with a history of 10 wins and 26 podiums at the venue. In INDY NXT, Seb Murray prepares for his second career oval start, building on a promising debut and support from the experienced Andretti Cape team.
