# InvestorNewsBreaks – G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) Hits 42,587 oz Gold Production in Q2, Nears Nameplate Throughput at Tocantinzinho

G Mining Ventures Corp. (TSX: GMIN) (OTCQX: GMINF) reported second quarter 2025 gold production of 42,587 ounces at its Tocantinzinho Gold Mine in Pará, Brazil, marking a 20% increase over Q1. The processing plant averaged 12,890 tonnes per day over a 30-day stretch, with quarterly throughput averaging 11,100 tpd—86% of nameplate capacity. Recovery rates improved to 90.3%, up from 87.7% in Q1, while mining rates rose 18% to 47,900 tpd. CEO Louis-Pierre Gignac credited the turnaround to successful SAG mill liner upgrades and affirmed the company remains on track to meet 2025 production targets. No lost time or recordable incidents were reported during the quarter.
