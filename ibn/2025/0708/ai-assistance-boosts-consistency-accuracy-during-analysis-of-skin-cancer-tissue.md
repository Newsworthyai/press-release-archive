# AI Assistance Boosts Consistency, Accuracy During Analysis of Skin Cancer Tissue

A recent study led by researchers at Karolinska Institute, in partnership with Yale University, has shown that artificial intelligence can significantly enhance the work of pathologists when examining skin cancer tissue samples. The use of AI made the assessments more reliable and helped provide a clearer picture of patient outcomes.
