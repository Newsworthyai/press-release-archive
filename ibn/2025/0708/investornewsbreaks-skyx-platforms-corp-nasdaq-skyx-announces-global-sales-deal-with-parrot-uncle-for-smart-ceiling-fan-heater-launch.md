# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Announces Global Sales Deal with Parrot Uncle for Smart Ceiling Fan-Heater Launch

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX) a smart home technology innovator, announced a global sales and marketing collaboration with Parrot Uncle, a leading ceiling fan manufacturer, to promote SKYX’s patented all-in-one smart ceiling fan with a built-in heater. Targeting both residential and commercial markets, SKYX will launch two product versions in 6–8 color options beginning Q3 2025. With production underway, the company sees this four-season product as a key driver toward achieving cash-flow positive operations and expanding its presence in the multi-billion-dollar fan and heater market.
