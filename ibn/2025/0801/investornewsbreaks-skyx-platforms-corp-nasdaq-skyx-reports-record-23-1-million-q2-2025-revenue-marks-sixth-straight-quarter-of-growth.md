# InvestorNewsBreaks – SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) Reports Record $23.1 Million Q2 2025 Revenue, Marks Sixth Straight Quarter of Growth

SKYX Platforms (NASDAQ: SKYX), a smart home platform technology company with more than 97 issued and pending patents globally and a portfolio of over 60 lighting and home décor websites, announced record pre-audited revenues of $23.1 million for the second quarter ended June 30, 2025, up from $20.1 million in the first quarter. The Company has delivered six consecutive quarters of revenue growth, with results rising from $19 million in Q1 2024 to $23.1 million in Q2 2025. Rani Kohen, Founder/Inventor and Executive Chairman, said the record performance underscores SKYX’s ability to expand its retail and pro channels, leverage its e-commerce platform and advance its smart home technologies while driving recurring revenue opportunities.
