# TinyGemsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Debuts Quadrant Speedway Collection Following Veloce Investment

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), owner of Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com, announced the launch of the Quadrant Speedway Collection, the first major apparel drop since its strategic investment in Veloce Media Group and Veloce’s acquisition of Quadrant, the motorsport and lifestyle brand co-founded by Formula 1 driver Lando Norris. The collection, blending motorsport heritage with streetwear aesthetics, debuted with Norris in the F1 paddock ahead of his pole position and podium finish at the Belgian Grand Prix. Chairman and CEO Matthew McGahan said the launch reflects SEGG Media’s vision to power next-generation sports, fashion and fandom through strategic investments and the reach of Veloce’s platform, which drives more than 750 million monthly views and 55 million subscribers.
