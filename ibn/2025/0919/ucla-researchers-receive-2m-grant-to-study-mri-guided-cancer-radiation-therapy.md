# UCLA Researchers Receive $2M Grant to Study MRI-Guided Cancer Radiation Therapy

A grant of $2 million has been awarded to UCLA Health by a for-profit company, ViewRay Systems. The purpose of this grant is to enable the university to accelerate its research efforts in finding ways in which MRI-guided radiation therapy can be leveraged to provide targeted treatment to cancer patients.
