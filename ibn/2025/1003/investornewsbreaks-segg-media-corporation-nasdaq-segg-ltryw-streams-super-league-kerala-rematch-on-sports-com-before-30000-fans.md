# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Streams Super League Kerala Rematch on Sports.com Before 30,000 Fans

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), the global sports, entertainment, and gaming conglomerate, streamed the Super League Kerala match on Sports.com where defending champions Calicut defeated Kochi 2–1 at EMS Stadium before more than 30,000 fans. The decisive late goal sealed the rematch of last year’s final, with SEGG Media Director and Head of Sports.com Acquisitions Marc Bircham presenting the Man of the Match award on the pitch. The debut live broadcast drew strong engagement across India and worldwide, marking a key milestone in Sports.com’s strategy to expand its global footprint and deliver fan-first digital experiences.
