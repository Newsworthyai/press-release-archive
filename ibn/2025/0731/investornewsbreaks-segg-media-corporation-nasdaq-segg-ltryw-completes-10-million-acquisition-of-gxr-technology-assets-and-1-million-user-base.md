# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Completes $10 Million Acquisition of GXR Technology Assets and 1 Million-User Base

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), owner of Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com, has completed a $10 million acquisition of the technology assets and active user base of more than 1 million from GXR Racer Holdings Limited, marking its second acquisition in July. The integration of GXR’s platform with the Sports.com app will create the Sports.com Super App, an all-in-one digital ecosystem for sports fans featuring live streaming, community hubs, social stats, e-commerce, gaming and real-time translations. Paul Roy, GXR Founder and CEO, will join as a Director of the newly formed entity overseeing the Super App, which is slated for a phased global launch beginning Q3 2025. SEGG Media Chairman and CEO Matthew McGahan said the deal advances the company’s goal to deliver a unified, immersive sports and entertainment media platform, with the Sports.com Super App aiming to capture the full spectrum of fan engagement.
