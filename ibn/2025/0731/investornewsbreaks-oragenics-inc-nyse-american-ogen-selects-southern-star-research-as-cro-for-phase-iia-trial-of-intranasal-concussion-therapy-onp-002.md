# InvestorNewsBreaks – Oragenics Inc. (NYSE American: OGEN) Selects Southern Star Research as CRO for Phase IIa Trial of Intranasal Concussion Therapy ONP-002

Oragenics (NYSE American: OGEN), a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing intranasal drug delivery for neurological conditions, has chosen Southern Star Research as its Clinical Research Organization for the upcoming Phase IIa trial of ONP-002 in mild traumatic brain injury. The randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study will evaluate safety, tolerability, pharmacokinetics and early pharmacodynamic effects of the proprietary intranasal neurosteroid designed to enhance brain delivery while reducing systemic exposure. With Human Research Ethics Committee approval secured in Australia, Oragenics and Southern Star are finalizing site selection and preparing for patient enrollment. CEO Janet Huffman said the partnership marks a pivotal step in advancing ONP-002 to address the significant unmet need in concussion treatment.

 About Oragenics

 Oragenics is a development-stage biotechnology company focused on nasal delivery of pharmaceutical medications in neurology and fighting infectious diseases, including drug candidates for treating mild traumatic brain injury (“mTBI”), also known as concussion, and for treating Niemann Pick Disease Type C (“NPC”), as well as proprietary powder formulation and an intranasal delivery device.
