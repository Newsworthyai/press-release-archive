# MiningNewsBreaks – Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM) President Featured on Latest IBN MiningNewsWire Podcast

Silvercorp Metals (TSX: SVM) (NYSE American: SVM), a Canadian mining company producing silver, gold, lead and zinc, is featured in the newest episode of IBN’s MiningNewsWire Podcast. In the interview, President Lon Shaver discussed Silvercorp’s transition into a diversified, multi-asset operator, highlighting its recent acquisition in Ecuador and growth strategy. Shaver detailed funding plans for the El Domo project, including $175 million through a Wheaton stream arrangement and $75 million from cash on hand, and emphasized the company’s focus on disciplined M&A and delivering consistent shareholder value.

 To view the full press release, visit https://ibn.fm/TPalC

 About Silvercorp

 Silvercorp is a Canadian mining company producing silver, gold, lead, and zinc with a long history of profitability and growth potential. The Company’s strategy is to create shareholder value by 1) focusing on generating free cash flow from long life mines; 2) organic growth through extensive drilling for discovery; 3) ongoing merger and acquisition efforts to unlock value; and 4) long term commitment to responsible mining and ESG.
