# InvestorNewsBreaks – Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO) Expands Global Production with Vietnam Launch and New Lithium-Ion MVR Models

Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO), a manufacturer and distributor of powersports vehicles and products, has begun production of its 2026 MVR Golf Cart and MVR Cargo Max Electric Utility Cart in Vietnam. The new partnership enhances Massimo’s global supply chain, expanding flexibility and capacity for its growing electric vehicle lineup. “Our partnership in Vietnam represents an important milestone in Massimo’s continued evolution,” said David Shan, CEO of Massimo Group. The company also introduced upgraded 48V 105Ah Lithium-Ion battery options across its MVR Series, offering improved range, faster charging, and maintenance-free operation with more than 5,000 charge cycles, further positioning Massimo as a competitive force in the future of electric mobility.
