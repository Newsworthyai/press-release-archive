# InvestorNewsBreaks – MoneyShow Announces Virtual Fixed Income Forum Nov. 18-20, 2025

MoneyShow, a leading producer of live and online financial conferences for investors, traders, and financial advisors, announced its upcoming Fixed Income Forum, taking place virtually Nov. 18-20, 2025. The event will feature more than a dozen leading speakers offering strategies for optimizing returns across municipal, high-yield, corporate, and emerging market bonds. Attendees will gain insights into managing inflation risk, assessing credit quality, and identifying opportunities amid global uncertainty. “Fixed income products offer stability, protection, and diversification, but today’s environment demands a more selective approach,” said Mike Larson, Editor-in-Chief at MoneyShow. “We’re gathering top experts to deliver actionable strategies that help investors make informed decisions.” The summit will include presentations from economists, analysts, and money managers providing forward-looking perspectives on fixed income markets.
