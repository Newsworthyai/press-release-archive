# TechMediaBreaks – Safe Pro Group Inc. (NASDAQ: SPAI) AI Detects More Than 36,000 Landmines Across 22,500 Acres in Ukraine

Safe Pro Group (NASDAQ: SPAI), a leader in AI-powered security and situational awareness solutions, reported that its advanced algorithms have identified over 36,000 landmines and explosive objects after analyzing more than 2 million drone images spanning 22,500 acres of Ukrainian battlefields. Leveraging a crowd-sourced model that aggregates data from military, commercial, and humanitarian drone operators, Safe Pro’s AI transforms imagery into shareable 2D and 3D maps for real-time threat detection. The Company’s technology, which recently earned selection for the U.S. Army’s 2026 Concept Focused Warfighter Experiment, is also being integrated through partnerships with Red Cat Holdings (Nasdaq: RCAT), Ondas Holdings (Nasdaq: ONDS), and Unusual Machines (NYSE: UMAC) to expand its battlefield and disaster-zone applications.
