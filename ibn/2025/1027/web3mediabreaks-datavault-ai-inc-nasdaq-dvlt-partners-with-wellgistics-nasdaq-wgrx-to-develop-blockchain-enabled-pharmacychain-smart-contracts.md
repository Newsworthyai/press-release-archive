# Web3MediaBreaks – Datavault AI Inc. (NASDAQ: DVLT) Partners with Wellgistics (NASDAQ: WGRX) to Develop Blockchain-Enabled PharmacyChain Smart Contracts

Datavault AI (NASDAQ: DVLT), a leader in AI data experience, valuation, and monetization, has entered a SaaS agreement with Wellgistics Health Inc. (NASDAQ: WGRX) to co-develop PharmacyChain(TM), a blockchain-enabled smart contract system designed to optimize prescription distribution from manufacturers to patients. The collaboration leverages Datavault AI’s patented Information Data Exchange(R) platform to create secure, token-driven data transfers that enhance transparency and efficiency in pharmaceutical logistics. CEO Nathaniel Bradley said the partnership will build digital twins of logistics, delivery systems, and patients, supporting personalized health recommendations and new revenue streams in the $639 billion prescription drug market.
