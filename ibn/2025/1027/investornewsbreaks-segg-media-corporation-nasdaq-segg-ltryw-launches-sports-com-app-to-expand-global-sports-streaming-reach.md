# InvestorNewsBreaks – SEGG Media Corporation (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) Launches Sports.com App to Expand Global Sports Streaming Reach

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW), a global sports, entertainment, and gaming group, announced the official launch of its new Sports.com App, now available on the Apple App Store and other marketplaces. The platform integrates live and on-demand sports, highlights, and interactive features into a single hub aimed at driving fan engagement and monetization across emerging markets. The app debuts with live coverage of the Oct. 28 Super League Kerala match between Malappuram FC and Thiruvananthapuram Kombans FC, following record-breaking viewership that surpassed 14.5 million interactions last week. CEO Tim Scoffham said the launch marks a key step in unifying SEGG Media’s sports ecosystem worldwide.
