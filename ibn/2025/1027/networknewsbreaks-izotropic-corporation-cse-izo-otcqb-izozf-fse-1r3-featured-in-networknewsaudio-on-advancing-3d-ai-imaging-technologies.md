# NetworkNewsBreaks – Izotropic Corporation (CSE: IZO) (OTCQB: IZOZF) (FSE: 1R3) Featured in NetworkNewsAudio on Advancing 3D AI-Imaging Technologies

Izotropic (CSE: IZO) (OTCQB: IZOZF) (FSE: 1R3) is spotlighted in a NetworkNewsAudio release titled “Investors Eye Next-Gen 3D AI-Imaging Technologies in Growing $9 Billion Market,” highlighting its IzoView Breast CT Imaging System. Positioned between tomosynthesis and MRI, IzoView integrates high-resolution volumetric imaging with proprietary AI reconstruction for enhanced diagnostic precision. Built on over a decade of breast CT data and designed for potential CADx integration, the system underscores Izotropic’s leadership in AI-driven breast cancer detection.
