# InvestorNewsBreaks – Future of Health and Longevity to Take Center Stage at Octane’s Medical Innovation Forum

Octane, Southern California’s premier innovation accelerator, is set to host its 2025 Medical Innovation Forum on Oct. 28–29 at the Irvine Marriott. The two-day forum will feature more than 50 experts and leaders who will share insights on cutting-edge advancements in the latest medical trends, including foresights in longevity, wellness, and technology. Dr. Shaista Malik and Dr. Claudia Kawas will take the stage to deliver keynote presentations on longevity, including groundbreaking findings from a 90+ study and new insights in heart and brain health, emotional well-being, and resilience across a lifetime. Attendees will also experience an engaging fireside chat with Octane co-founder Jim Mazzo and Willow Laboratories founder Joe Kiani, the new Integrative Wellness Pavilion, as well as capital pitch presentations from 14 entrepreneurs.

 Register for the chance to engage with industry pioneers, discover groundbreaking longevity and wellness innovations, and explore investment opportunities that could shape the future of medicine.
