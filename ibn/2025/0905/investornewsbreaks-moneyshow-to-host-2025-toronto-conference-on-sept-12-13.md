# InvestorNewsBreaks – MoneyShow to Host 2025 Toronto Conference on Sept. 12-13

MoneyShow, a leading producer of financial conferences for affluent investors, active traders, and financial advisors, will host the 2025 MoneyShow Toronto – Discover New Stock and Market Opportunities on Sept. 12-13 at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre North. The event will feature dozens of sessions led by economists, market analysts, fund managers, and professional traders, along with interactive trading sessions, keynote addresses, panel discussions, book signings, and a signature networking reception. Attendees will explore timely topics such as Canada-U.S. relations, Ottawa politics, real estate, metals and mining, and Bank of Canada policy while connecting with industry experts. Among the notable speakers are Ardi Aaziznia of Ayandeh Investing Group, Larry Berman of ETF Capital Management, Maya Corbic of Wealthy Kids Investment Club, Braden Dennis of Fiscal.ai, Ryan Irvine of Keystone Financial, Eric Nuttall of Ninepoint Partners LP, and Benjamin Tal of CIBC Capital Markets. The agenda also includes Canadian MoneySaver Day and a Real Estate Investing Track, while a state-of-the-art Exhibit Hall will showcase products, deals, and direct access to C-suite executives. MoneyShow has partnered with IBN (InvestorBrandNetwork) as the official media sponsor to expand the conference’s digital reach.
