# InvestorNewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Insider Buying Led by COO’s 100,000-Share Purchase

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), a digital mortgage platform redefining the path to homeownership, today reported a wave of insider buying that underscores management’s confidence in the company’s trajectory. The activity was led by Chief Operating Officer Jessica Kennedy, who purchased 100,000 shares. Chief Financial Officer Chris Moe acquired 20,000 shares, while Chief Accounting Officer Tiffany Milton added 10,000 shares. Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder Nick Liuzza also increased his holdings by 3,000 shares, bringing his total personal investment in Beeline to more than $16 million. The executive team has consistently emphasized that they “sit with shareholders,” and these recent purchases highlight leadership’s conviction in Beeline’s growth strategy and alignment with long-term investors.
