# Renewable Energy Solutions Do More Beyond Generating Power, WEF Says

The World Economic Forum (WEF) says renewable energy solutions like wind, solar, and hydropower have the potential to do more than generate clean energy. In a blog that was recently published as part of its Annual Meeting of the New Champions, the non-governmental organization outlined how green energy solutions could be used to both generate renewable energy and protect air, water, and land quality.
