# TechMediaBreaks – Octane to Host Cardiovascular Tech Forum 2025 in Newport Beach

Octane, a leading organization fueling technology growth in Southern California, announced the Octane Cardiovascular Tech Forum 2025 (“CTF”) will take place Sept. 11-12, 2025, at VEA Newport Beach, A Marriott Resort & Spa. A cornerstone of Octane’s Innovation Event Series, CTF 2025 will unite entrepreneurs, investors, physicians, and industry leaders to highlight the latest advances in interventional cardiology, digital health, imaging, and medical devices. Program highlights include innovation panels, a physician and entrepreneur workshop, investor-focused presentations, and networking sessions. Keynote speaker Dr. John B. Simpson, a pioneering interventional cardiologist and inventor of the over-the-wire balloon angioplasty catheter, will headline the event. The forum’s committee, featuring leaders from Edwards Lifesciences, Cathworks, Knobbe Martens, UC Irvine, Hoag Hospital, and Nidus Biomedical, will guide the agenda. CEO Bill Carpou said the forum will accelerate solutions poised to redefine patient outcomes and strengthen the healthcare ecosystem.
