# InvestorNewsBreaks – Sustain Southern California to Host 10th Annual Water Solutions Event at UCI Beall Applied Innovation

Sustain SoCal will host the 10th Annual Water Solutions event (“WS10”) on Tuesday, Aug. 26, 2025, at UCI Beall Applied Innovation in Irvine, California. The event will convene thought leaders, policy experts, and innovators to explore pragmatic strategies for ensuring sufficient and affordable water supplies across Southern California and beyond. Sessions will cover reuse, recycling, desalination, PFAS mitigation, stormwater capture, hydrogen power, and cybersecurity, alongside investment insights, policy analysis, and AI-driven data applications. Sustain SoCal President and CEO Scott Kitcher said the forum has become essential in strategizing the region’s water resources, adding that WS10 will spotlight the latest innovations and collaborative approaches for sustainable water and economic development.

 About Sustain SoCal:

 Sustain SoCal, a non-profit organization, accelerates sustainability and economic growth through innovation, collaboration, and education in Southern California. The organization has a ten-year history in exploring and implementing pragmatic, real-world solutions to the challenges created by growth, change and inefficiency. It conducts conferences, workshops and networking events that lead to initiatives that positively impact our region’s economic progress and sustainability.
