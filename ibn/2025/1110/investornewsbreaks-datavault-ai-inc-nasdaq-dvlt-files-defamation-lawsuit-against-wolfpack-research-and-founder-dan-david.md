# InvestorNewsBreaks – Datavault AI Inc. (NASDAQ: DVLT) Files Defamation Lawsuit Against Wolfpack Research and Founder Dan David

Datavault AI (NASDAQ: DVLT), a leader in data tokenization and management, announced that it has filed a complaint against Wolfpack Research and its founder Dan David over a defamatory short report released on Oct. 31, 2025. Represented by Paul Hastings LLP, Datavault asserts that the report contained false and misleading claims, ignored verifiable facts, and omitted key context—causing direct harm to the Company and its shareholders. “We will pursue all options to hold Wolfpack and Dan David accountable in court,” said Nathaniel Bradley, CEO of Datavault AI, adding that the Company remains focused on generating revenue and driving shareholder value. A copy of the complaint is included as Exhibit 99.1 to Datavault’s Form 8-K filed with the SEC on Nov. 10, 2025.
