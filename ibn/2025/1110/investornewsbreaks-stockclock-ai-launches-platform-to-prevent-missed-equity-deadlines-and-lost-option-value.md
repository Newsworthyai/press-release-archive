# InvestorNewsBreaks – StockClock.ai Launches Platform to Prevent Missed Equity Deadlines and Lost Option Value

StockClock.ai today announced the launch of its equity deadline intelligence platform, designed to help founders, executives, investors, and employee shareholders capture the full value of their equity grants, stock options, restricted shares, and warrants by preventing missed expirations and unlock dates. The platform unifies all grants and securities in a single dashboard, delivering automated alerts by email or mobile notification and soon enabling escalation tools for advisors, attorneys, and brokers. Built to replace error-prone spreadsheets, StockClock.ai provides clear visibility into vesting, lockup, and maturity schedules—turning awareness and timing into measurable shareholder value.

 About StockClock.ai

 StockClock.ai helps companies and individuals safeguard equity value by tracking critical deadlines across all granted equity instruments.
