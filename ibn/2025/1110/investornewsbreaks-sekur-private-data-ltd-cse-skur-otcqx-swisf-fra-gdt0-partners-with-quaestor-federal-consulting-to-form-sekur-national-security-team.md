# InvestorNewsBreaks – Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FRA: GDT0) Partners with Quaestor Federal Consulting to Form Sekur National Security Team

This article has been disseminated on behalf of Sekur Private Data and may include paid advertising.

 Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FRA: GDT0), a Swiss-hosted cybersecurity and private communications platform, announced a partnership with Quaestor Federal Consulting to establish the Sekur National Security Team (“NST”). The collaboration aims to integrate and advance Sekur’s secure communications technology within the U.S. national security landscape. Quaestor, a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) composed of former military and intelligence professionals, brings more than 400 years of combined Defense and Intelligence Community experience. Quaestor President Phil Oakley said secure, rapid communication of unclassified digital media remains a critical challenge for U.S. agencies, adding that “Sekur offers an elegant solution to this problem.”
