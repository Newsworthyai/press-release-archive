# InvestorNewsBreaks – Planet Ventures Inc. (CSE: PXI) (OTC Pink: PNXPF) (FSE: P6U) Engages PRmediaNow and Appoints Strategic Advisor

This article has been disseminated on behalf of Planet Ventures Inc. and may include paid advertising.

 Planet Ventures (CSE: PXI) (OTC Pink: PNXPF) (FSE: P6U) announced it has engaged PRmediaNow Inc. to provide public relations and media outreach services under a six-month agreement valued at $6,000 per month, along with 150,000 stock options exercisable at $0.25 per share. The company also confirmed it has engaged Investor Brand Network and appointed Tansu Yegen as a strategic advisor, as Planet seeks to expand visibility through earned media coverage across mainstream outlets, trade publications and digital platforms.
