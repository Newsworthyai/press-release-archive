# InvestorNewsBreaks – BioAdaptives, Inc. (OTCMKTS: BDPT) Launches MyndSystem Platform and Website for Cognitive Performance and Recovery

BioAdaptives (OTCMKTS: BDPT) announced the launch of MyndSystem(TM), a dual-phase cognitive support platform featuring MyndMed(TM) for daytime performance and MyndRenew(TM) for nighttime recovery, alongside a dedicated website to support direct-to-consumer engagement. The system is designed to address brain function across both active and restorative cycles, targeting focus, clarity and stress resilience during the day while promoting sleep quality and neurological recovery at night, positioning the platform as a long-term growth driver for the Company.
