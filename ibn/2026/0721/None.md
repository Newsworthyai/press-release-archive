# ESGBreaks – ESGold Corp. (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) Advances Montauban Project With Equipment Procurement Milestone

This article has been disseminated on behalf of ESGold Corp. and may include paid advertising.

 ESGold (CSE: ESAU) (OTCQB: ESAUF) announced that its technical team has completed inspections of remaining mill equipment in China and approved the equipment for shipment after confirming it meets industry and company specifications. The remaining manufactured mill equipment is expected to arrive within 45 to 60 days, while the mobile water treatment system has already been delivered to the Montauban Gold-Silver Project in Québec. The company also said the Falcon Concentrator and thickeners have been purchased and fully paid for, with delivery expected during the second week of August.

 The company said the latest procurement milestones move the fully permitted Montauban Project closer to site installation and construction readiness. Alongside equipment deployment, ESGold continues to advance exploration planning across its district-scale property, including interpretation of a recently completed 70-square-kilometer ambient noise tomography survey, as it awaits permits to begin a structured drilling program targeting priority exploration areas.
