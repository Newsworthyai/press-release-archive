# Metal Prices Rattled as Chile Faces Stormy Weather, Iran Hostilities Flare Up

Metal prices retreated as the week ended amid an escalation in the hostilities between Iran and the U.S., while a huge storm bearing down on Chile rattled copper markets. However, damage to copper prices was tamped down due to supply concerns as adverse weather in Chile triggered concerns about disruptions to mine output.

 Stocks of companies that mine copper were hit harder than the metal itself. Shares of Freeport-McMoRan dropped by 4%, Ivanhoe Mines slipped 4.9%, Antofagasta dropped 4.1%, while BHP retreated by 2.3%.
