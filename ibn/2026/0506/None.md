# AINewsBreaks – Beeline Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: BLNE) Achieves Triple-Digit Growth, Positions for Scalable Expansion

Beeline Holdings (NASDAQ: BLNE), a fast-growing digital mortgage platform offering a quicker and easier path to homeownership, was featured in a recent article that discussed its strong Q4 2025 results, highlighted by 127% year-over-year revenue growth to $2.5 million and a 44% increase in mortgage originations to $84.7 million. The results were driven by the company’s expansion of its digital mortgage platform and improved efficiency metrics. Alongside faster processing times and higher conversion rates, reflecting growing leverage through AI-driven automation, Beeline increased average revenue per loan by 31% while reducing costs by 18%. The company also launched its blockchain-enabled platform, BeelineEquity, which empowers homeowners to access equity without refinancing. Having eliminated corporate debt and strengthened its balance sheet in 2025, management is focused on scaling its mortgage business, growing BeelineEquity, and expanding AI and SaaS capabilities in 2026.
