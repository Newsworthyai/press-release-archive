# InvestorNewsBreaks – Linkers Industries Limited (NASDAQ: LNKS) Prices $16 Million Best Efforts Public Offering

Linkers Industries Limited (NASDAQ: LNKS) announced the pricing of a best-efforts public offering expected to generate approximately $16 million in gross proceeds through the sale of 23,088,023 units priced at $0.693 per unit, each consisting of one Class A ordinary share (or pre-funded warrant) and accompanying Series A and Series B warrants. The company said proceeds will be used in part to support a potential increase in its equity stake in LPW Electronics Co. Ltd., where it currently holds 20%, with the balance allocated to capital expenditures, working capital and general corporate purposes, with closing expected on or about March 24, 2026.

 About Linkers Industries Limited

 Linkers Industries Limited is a manufacturer and a supplier of wire/cable harnesses with manufacturing operations in Malaysia and have more than 20 years’ experience in the wire/cable harnesses industry. The Company offers customized wire harness for different applications and electrics designs. Our customers are generally global brand name manufacturers and original equipment manufacturers in the home appliances, industrial products and automotive industries that are mainly based in the Asia Pacific Region.
