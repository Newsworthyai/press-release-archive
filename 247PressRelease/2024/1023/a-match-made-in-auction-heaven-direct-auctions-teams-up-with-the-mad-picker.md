# A Match Made in Auction Heaven: Direct Auctions Teams Up with The Mad Picker!

VANCOUVER, BC, October 23, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Direct Auctions, powered by Direct Liquidation and located at 2635 Lillooet Street, Vancouver, BC, V5M 4P7, is thrilled to announce an exciting new partnership with The Mad Picker, located at 27276 Fraser Hwy, Aldergrove, B.C., V4W 3P8. This collaboration brings a one-of-a-kind auction experience to buyers and collectors across Canada and the U.S. and promises an expanded offering of rare finds, high-quality assets, and unbeatable deals from various industries.  Jeff "The Liquidator," owner of Direct Auctions and Direct Liquidation, has amassed over 30 years of experience in the auction business. Jeff leads a dedicated team that works tirelessly to facilitate the best auction experience for both buyers and sellers. Direct Auctions runs approximately 5-8 auctions per month for the general public, conducted live online and set at multiple locations across Canada and the U.S., with assets marketed and shipped worldwide.  Wayne, known as The Mad Picker, brings 50 years of experience in antiques and collectibles, along with a rich background in sourcing unique items. As an auctioneer and appraiser, he sets a high standard in the industry, specializing in buying, selling, and liquidating single items or estates while providing invaluable expert advice on large collections.  "We are beyond excited to partner with Wayne and The Mad Picker team," said Jeff. "This partnership allows us to expand our reach and offer buyers even more incredible items while maintaining the same level of care and curation that our loyal customers expect."  Wayne echoed Jeff's sentiments, stating, "Teaming up with Direct Auctions is a natural fit. We're taking the auction experience to a whole new level, offering everything from rare collectibles to industrial tools. Our combined expertise ensures that buyers have access to a diverse range of high-quality goods, all backed by professional customer support."  With the partnership now live, upcoming auctions will showcase a diverse range of high-quality goods, from the rarest collectibles to everyday essentials. Both Direct Auctions and The Mad Picker will post auction announcements and previews on their respective platforms, ensuring that buyers never miss out on the latest and most exciting opportunities.
