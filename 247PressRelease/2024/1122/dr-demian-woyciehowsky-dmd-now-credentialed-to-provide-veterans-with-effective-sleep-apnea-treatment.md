# Dr. Demian Woyciehowsky, DMD, Now Credentialed to Provide Veterans with Effective Sleep Apnea Treatment

HOLLYWOOD, FL, November 22, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- apZme, a leader in dental sleep medicine, is proud to announce that Dr. Demian Woyciehowsky, DMD, in Camas, Washington, has officially been credentialed to provide oral appliance therapy to Veterans suffering from sleep apnea. This significant milestone enables Veterans to receive high-quality, non-invasive sleep solutions in the Camas, Washougal, Vancouver and greater Portland area.  Dr. Woyciehowsky will be providing these services at his practice located in Camas, through Millennium Sleep Lab and Sleep Impressions, both apZme companies that serve as National Community Care Providers for the VA.  Millennium Sleep Lab and Sleep Impressions bring over 20 years of experience in dental sleep medicine, dedicated to improving patient health and well-being through innovative treatment options. Dr. Woyciehowsky, who has expanded his dental services to include the treatment of sleep apnea, will now extend his reach as a trusted provider to Veterans in the Camas, Washougal, Vancouver, and greater Portland area.  "We are honored to serve Veterans in Southwest Washington and the greater Portland, Oregon area by providing treatment for sleep apnea," said Dr. Demian Woyciehowsky, DMD. "With the growing need for providers in our region, it's a privilege to offer Veterans a comfortable and effective alternative to CPAP therapy, helping them achieve better sleep and improved health."  With Dr. Woyciehowsky's addition to the Community Care Network, Veterans can now access oral appliance therapy as a covered benefit in the greater Camas area, offering a vital alternative for those who cannot tolerate CPAP devices. The partnership among apZme, Sleep Impressions, Millennium Sleep Lab, and the VA addresses the growing demand for alternative sleep apnea treatments, helping Veterans achieve better sleep health.  "We are proud to expand our presence in the Pacific Northwest by welcoming Dr. Woyciehowsky to our VA provider network," said Rani Ben-David, CEO and Founder of apZme. "As our list of exceptional clinicians continues to grow, we remain committed to lead the charge to ensure Veterans across the country have access to the highest quality sleep apnea care and innovative treatment options."  About apZme apZme partners with dental practices nationwide to integrate comprehensive sleep medicine programs, enhancing patient care and expanding treatment options for sleep-disordered breathing. With Sleep Impressions and Millennium Sleep Lab under its umbrella, apZme supports practices in delivering proven therapies like oral appliances, improving health outcomes and quality of life.  About Millennium Sleep Lab, an apZme company Millennium Sleep Lab, contracted with VACCN Tri-West, is a leading provider of home sleep testing and oral appliance therapy. Accredited by the Joint Commission, Millennium is 100% HIPAA compliant and accepts most private insurance, ensuring timely and reliable treatment pathways.  About Sleep Impressions, an apZme company Sleep Impressions, contracted with VACCN Optum, is a network of dentists trained in dental sleep medicine with proven processes and technology to effectively treat sleep apnea.
