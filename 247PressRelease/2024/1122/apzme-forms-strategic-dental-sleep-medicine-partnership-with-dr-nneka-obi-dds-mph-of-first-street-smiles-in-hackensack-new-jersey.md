# apZme Forms Strategic Dental Sleep Medicine Partnership with Dr. Nneka Obi, DDS, MPH, of First Street Smiles in Hackensack, New Jersey

HOLLYWOOD, FL, November 22, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Sleep Group Solutions and Sleep Impressions, subsidiaries of apZme, announced their partnership with Dr. Nneka Obi, DDS, MPH, of First Street Smiles, to provide sleep apnea treatment in Hackensack, New Jersey. This collaboration enables Dr. Obi to offer advanced dental sleep medicine services to patients suffering from snoring and Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA).   "We are excited to offer Oral Appliance Therapy, an effective and patient-friendly solution that improves sleep quality, health, and overall well-being for our community," said Dr. Obi. "With the support of Sleep Group Solutions and Sleep Impressions, we're equipped to make a significant impact on the lives of those affected by sleep disorders."  The apZme Dental Sleep Medicine Business Builder Program integrates SGS's proprietary EccoVision Airway Imaging Technology with Sleep Impressions' ongoing service partnership, to facilitate in-network insurance coverage for many patients seeking treatment for their sleep apnea. Dr. Obi and the team at First Street Smiles are well-positioned to address the growing need for sleep disorder management in Hackensack and the surrounding areas.  "Our mission at SGS is to partner with exceptional dentists across the nation to bring essential sleep medicine services to their communities," said John Nadeau, President of Sleep Group Solutions. "Dr. Obi and First Street Smiles are committed to providing top-tier care and helping patients find relief from sleep-related breathing disorders."  Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) affects nearly one in four adults in the United States, yet the majority remain undiagnosed. Common symptoms include loud snoring, excessive daytime sleepiness, and hypertension, which can lead to serious health complications such as heart disease and diabetes. Dental devices offer an effective alternative to traditional CPAP therapy, and are often covered by medical insurance and Medicare.  "In our field, there is no goal more noble than to broaden our reach to patients suffering with this largely unrecognized disease," said Dr. Kent Smith, DDS, D-ABDSM, CEO of Star Sleep & Wellness with 5 sleep medicine practices in the DFW area, and Co-Founder & President of apZme. "We are committed to partnering with practices that share our dedication to improving sleep health and transforming the standard of care for those suffering from sleep-related breathing disorders."  About apZme apZme partners with dental practices nationwide to integrate comprehensive sleep medicine programs, enhancing patient care and expanding treatment options for sleep-disordered breathing. With Sleep Impressions and Sleep Group Solutions under its umbrella, apZme supports practices in delivering proven therapies like oral appliances, improving health outcomes and quality of life.  About Sleep Group Solutions, an apZme Company Sleep Group Solutions (SGS) is a premier provider of dental sleep medicine education and training, offering turnkey business solutions for dentists across North America. Established in 2005 and headquartered in Hollywood, FL, SGS equips dental professionals with the tools, training, and support needed to successfully integrate sleep medicine into their practices.  Through innovative programs and proprietary technology, SGS empowers dentists to play a pivotal role in combating sleep disorders and improving patient outcomes. As part of the apZme family, SGS continues to expand its reach and impact, solidifying its position as a leader in the rapidly growing field of dental sleep medicine.  About Sleep Impressions, an apZme company Sleep Impressions is a network of dentists trained in dental sleep medicine with proven processes and technology to effectively treat sleep apnea. As a dental sleep practice billing medical insurance, Sleep Impressions offers patients a cost-effective treatment alternative.
