# Marquis Who's Who Listee Gene Saunders Spotlights Project Lifesaver's Rescue Technology on The Hampton Roads Show

WILKES-BARRE, PA, November 05, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Gene Saunders, founder and CEO of Project Lifesaver International, appeared on WAVY-TV 10's The Hampton Roads Show to discuss the nonprofit's mission and work. Project Lifesaver equips public safety agencies with radio tracking technology to locate individuals with cognitive conditions, achieving nearly 5,000 rescues over 25 years without a single failure.
