# NetCom Learning Named a 2024 Top 20 IT and Technical Training Company by Training Industry

NEW YORK, NY, November 05, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Training Industry has announced NetCom Learning as one of the 2024 Top 20 IT and Technical Training Companies, highlighting the company's return to this distinguished list after their previous consecutive recognitions from 2012 to 2017.  NetCom Learning was recognized for its extensive IT and technical training portfolio, innovative learning delivery methods, and strong industry partnerships with leading technology vendors. The company's selection was based on several key factors including the breadth and quality of IT and technical training programs offered, industry recognition and innovation, strength of clients and geographic reach, and company size and growth potential.   Training Industry, a leading voice in the world of corporate learning, selects its Top 20 based on a rigorous evaluation of each company's offerings, industry visibility, innovation, client impact, and business performance. This recognition emphasizes NetCom Learning's role in shaping IT education and supporting learners as they meet the evolving demands of technology-driven roles.  Comprehensive Training for the Modern Workforce   NetCom Learning provides a wide range of training programs across disciplines such as AI & Data, Cloud, Security, Networking, App Development, and Architecture & Design. These courses are designed to equip IT professionals with essential skills, supporting workforce readiness across industries. The company also offers flexible learning formats, including Online Instructor-led Training, eLearning, and blended learning, to meet varied learning preferences and needs.  Previous Awards by NetCom Learning   Recently, we were recognized by Microsoft Partners in 2022 and 2023 as "Microsoft Learning Partner of the year for Business Excellence" and "Microsoft US Learning Partner of the Year for Business Transformation" respectively. Training Industry has also named NetCom Learning as a Top 20 Learning Services Company 2024. We have been recognized in the past as well by Inc 5000.  Innovating for Client Success   The inclusion on this year's Top 20 list underscores NetCom Learning's dedication to client success. The company continually enhances its curriculum and delivery methods to stay aligned with industry trends, ensuring relevant and impactful learning experiences.  NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations in the workplace by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth. With more than 26 years of experience, NetCom Learning has been empowering innovative learning organizations to adapt and drive growth in this fast-paced world by closing critical skill gaps and ensuring smooth deployment, implementation, and consumption through authorized training delivered by Certified Trainers on vendors such as Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, AI Certs, EC-Council, CompTIA, and more.
