# Consensus 2024: FFTV+ Live Streaming Unveils New Voting Mechanism

AUSTIN, TX, July 17, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- At Consensus 2024, a gathering of renowned guests took place, including Robert F. Kennedy Jr., a16z crypto founder and managing partner Chris Dixon, ARK Invest CEO Cathie Wood, Draper Associates founder Tim Draper, U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission commissioner Hester M. Pierce, and Ripple CEO Brad Garlinghouse. These industry leaders, along with numerous attendees, discussed the latest developments and future prospects of technology.  At the Conference, George Kong, founder of FFTV+ Live Streaming, unveiled a new Voting Mechanism that drew widespread attention. Central to this mechanism is the FFTV+ Streaming Platform, which provides viewers, creators, and actors with more opportunities for interaction and rewards.  Viewers can use FFTV+ platform to vote for their favorite scripts and actors, and even participate in various aspects of content creation. George Kong stated that this mechanism not only enhances viewer engagement but also offers more opportunities for creators and actors to showcase their talents.  Additionally, FFTV+ has leveraged artificial intelligence technology to further improve the efficiency and quality of content creation. The platform integrates stable diffusion technology, which can automatically generate high-quality video content based on simple descriptions, lowering the barrier to entry and enabling more creators to participate in short drama production.  This new Voting Mechanism demonstrates the immense potential of artificial intelligence technologies in the short drama field, providing valuable experience and reference for other content creation platforms.  FFTV+ Live Streaming is a pioneering company at the intersection of media and technology, dedicated to revolutionizing content creation and viewer engagement.
