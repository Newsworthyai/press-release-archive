# Psychiatrist Jonathan Raines named a Castle Connolly Top Doctor for 12th time

GLADWYNE, PA, November 19, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Psychiatrist Jonathan M. Raines, MD, has been recognized as a 2024 Castle Connolly Top Doctor — the 12th year he has received the prestigious Top Doctor honor. Only about 7 percent of the nation's licensed physicians are selected annually as Castle Connolly Top Doctors in their regions for their specialties.  A skilled psychoanalyst and psychiatrist, Dr. Raines has special expertise in diagnosing and treating anxiety disorders and psychosomatic disorders.  He is an attending physician at Penn Medicine's Pennsylvania Hospital, as well as an adjunct professor and medical staff member at Main Line Health/Jefferson Health System's Lankenau Institute for Medical Research in Wynnewood, Penn.  Dr. Raines is certified in psychoanalysis by the American Psychoanalytic Association, The International Psychoanalytic Association, and the American Board of Psychoanalysis.  He is a Distinguished Life Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association and a Fellow of the International Psychoanalytic Association.  Dr. Raines earned his medical degree from the Lewis Katz School of Medicine at Temple University. He completed a three-year psychiatry residency at the Institute of the Pennsylvania Hospital and a seven-year fellowship at the Philadelphia Association for Psychoanalysis — now called the Psychoanalytic Center of Philadelphia (PCOP).  He serves as both a PCOP member and a board member of the Psychoanalytic Study Group of Philadelphia, an affiliate of the Psychoanalytic Association of New York.   Dr. Raines has been named one of Philadelphia Magazine's Top Doctors every year since 2013.  Top Doctors are nominated by peer physicians and selected by the physician-led Castle Connolly research team.  About Castle Connolly Castle Connolly publishes its Top Doctors list at castleconnolly.com and in a variety of print and online partner publications. Nominations are open to all board-certified MDs, DOs, and DPMs. The Castle Connolly team chooses honorees based on nominee criteria that includes their medical education, training, hospital appointments, and disciplinary histories.  Doctors do not and cannot pay to be selected as a Castle Connolly Top Doctor.
