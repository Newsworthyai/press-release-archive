# NetCom Learning, a trusted Cisco Platinum Learning Partner, is gearing up to showcase its extensive Cisco training expertise at Cisco Live 2024 in Las Vegas

NEW YORK, NY, May 25, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a leading provider of IT training and certifications, has been invited to be featured at the Cisco booth at the upcoming Cisco Live 2024 conference in Las Vegas from June 2-6. As a Cisco Platinum Learning Partner, NetCom Learning will be making a presence at the premier networking event.  Cisco Live 2024 brings together IT professionals, networking experts, and technology enthusiasts from around the world. With the theme of "Redefine Possible," this year's event promises to explore the cutting edge of networking innovations like AI and automation. Highlighting the excitement, legendary quarterback Tom Brady and rising F1 star Oscar Piastri are also set to make special appearances.  Amid the bright lights and energy of Las Vegas, Nevada at the iconic Mandalay Bay Convention Center, NetCom Learning will showcase its extensive portfolio of authorized Cisco training courses and certifications. Attendees can meet NetCom Learning to: • Learn about expert-led Cisco training across routing, switching, security, collaboration, and other technologies. • Explore options for onsite, virtual, and on-demand Cisco courses. • Get details on using Cisco Learning Credits for training. • Meet the NetCom Learning team of Cisco-certified instructors.
