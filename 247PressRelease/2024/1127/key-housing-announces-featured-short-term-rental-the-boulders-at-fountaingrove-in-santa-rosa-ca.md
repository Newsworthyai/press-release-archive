# Key Housing Announces Featured Short-Term Rental: The Boulders at Fountaingrove in Santa Rosa, CA

SANTA ROSA, CA, November 27, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Key Housing, the premier site for corporate and short-term housing in Northern California, proudly announces its Northern California featured listing for December, 2024, to be "The Boulders at Fountaingrove" located at 3680 Kelsey Knolls, Santa Rosa, California 95403. The property was chosen for its top-quality short term corporate rentals, including serviced or furnished rentals that make a "home away from home" for any business traveler coming to Santa Rosa and the Wine Country in December. A premier property, the complex blends luxury with sustainability, provides business travelers with the perfect combination of convenience, and yields comfort and eco-consciousness in the heart of California's picturesque Wine Country.  "The Boulders at Fountaingrove exemplifies our commitment to providing business travelers with exceptional short-term housing options," stated Bob Lee, President of Key Housing. "Its blend of luxury, sustainability, and prime location in Santa Rosa's prestigious Fountaingrove neighborhood offers our clients an unparalleled living experience in Sonoma County."  More information can be found at https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-rental-properties/the-boulders-at-fountaingrove/ and at https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-housing-city/santa-rosa/.
