# I'm On Duty – A Heartwarming Story of Loyalty, Service, and the Bond Between a Service Dog and His Human

SAN DIEGO, CA, November 20, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- I'm On Duty, the latest children's book by renowned dog trainer and behavior specialist Graham Bloem, is now available. Through the eyes of Alphie, a service dog on a mission, Bloem takes readers on an inspiring journey of bravery, love, and the unbreakable bond between humans and their four-legged companions. With his wealth of experience and dedication to improving the lives of dogs and their owners, Bloem offers an inside look at the life of a service dog and the vital role they play in their human's world.  Alphie isn't just any dog. He's a highly trained service dog with a big responsibility—helping his mom navigate daily tasks and providing comfort when life gets overwhelming. From alerting her to important tasks to staying focused in busy environments, Alphie is always "on duty" when his vest is on. With the engaging narrative and fun illustrations, I'm On Duty introduces young readers to the incredible work of service dogs and the essential role they play in their human's lives.  "I've had the privilege of working with many incredible dogs throughout my career, and I'm On Duty is a celebration of their courage, loyalty, and the profound difference they make in the lives of their owners," says Bloem. "Through Alphie's story, I hope children will better understand and appreciate the important work that service dogs do—and also learn how to respect them while they're on duty."  A Unique Look into the Life of a Service Dog In I'm On Duty, Alphie takes readers through his daily routine, explaining his duties and the importance of staying focused when he's working. The book also teaches valuable lessons about respect for service animals, particularly the need to give them space and allow them to do their job without distraction. Through Alphie's eyes, children learn about the powerful connection between service dogs and their humans and how these remarkable animals provide life-changing support.  The book is filled with charming illustrations that bring Alphie's adventures to life, showing the joys and challenges of being a service dog. Whether he's finding lost keys, providing comfort in a crowded store, or offering emotional support at night, Alphie is always ready to help his mom. His story is a touching reminder of the extraordinary work done by service dogs and the special bond they share with their owners.  About the Author Graham Bloem, who has over 24 years of experience in dog training and behavior modification, is well-known for his work with military veterans and rescue dogs. As the co-founder of Shelter to Soldier™, a nonprofit that trains rescue dogs for military personnel with PTSD, and the founder of Specialty Dog Training, Bloem has dedicated his life to improving the lives of both dogs and their human families.  In addition to training famous rescue dogs like Nubs and Lava, Bloem's contributions to dog welfare have earned him recognition in various media outlets, including NBC, CNN, and The Ellen Show. I'm On Duty marks his debut in children's literature, offering an accessible and heartwarming way for young readers to understand the vital role service dogs play in the world.
