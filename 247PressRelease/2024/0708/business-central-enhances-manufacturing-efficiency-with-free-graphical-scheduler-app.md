# Business Central Enhances Manufacturing Efficiency with Free Graphical Scheduler App

EDMONTON, AB, July 08, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Insight Works proudly celebrates the fourth anniversary of the Graphical Scheduler app on Microsoft AppSource this July 1st. A cornerstone for small to medium-sized manufacturing enterprises using Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, Graphical Scheduler has improved how businesses manage their production schedules.  The Graphical Scheduler app remains completely free with no locked features, embodying Insight Works' commitment to supporting the manufacturing sector with accessible, high-quality tools.  "Since launching the Graphical Scheduler in 2020, our vision has always been to provide apps that deliver real value, and in the case of Graphical Scheduler, making it accessible to all businesses without the barrier of cost," said Brian Neufeld, Director of Marketing at Insight Works. "We are thrilled to celebrate this milestone, reaffirming our promise that the Graphical Scheduler is free forever, with no need for any upgrades to unlock its capabilities."  Key features of the Graphical Scheduler include:  - Drag-and-Drop Interface: The Drag-and-Drop Interface offers users unparalleled flexibility for making real-time scheduling adjustments, helping ensure production lines are always optimized for efficiency. - Single-Click Optimization: Immediate access to crucial operation details like production order numbers, routing specifics, and descriptions enhances decision-making and operational visibility. - Operation Highlighting: Provides a panoramic view of operations, linking related tasks visually for better management and oversight. - Pinpoint Precision: Powerful built-in filters allow for rapid location of specific operations, turning potentially hours of searching into moments of clarity. - Customizable Color Coding: Users can craft visual narratives with vibrant, customizable colors to represent different operation statuses, making it easier to spot potential issues and make adjustments on the fly.  As Graphical Scheduler continues its journey, Insight Works remains dedicated to enhancing its functionalities, ensuring it always meets the evolving needs of modern manufacturers.  For more information about the Graphical Scheduler and other productivity-enhancing tools from Insight Works, please visit SchedulerForDynamics.com.  About Insight Works  Since 2008, Insight Works has been a prominent Independent Software Vendor (ISV), initially specializing in Microsoft NAV before transitioning to Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central in 2018. Headquartered in Canada with substantial operations in North America and the Netherlands, Insight Works actively supports European markets. Their extensive network of over 500 Microsoft Partners globally ensures top-tier support and service, facilitating seamless system integration and continuous application enhancement.
