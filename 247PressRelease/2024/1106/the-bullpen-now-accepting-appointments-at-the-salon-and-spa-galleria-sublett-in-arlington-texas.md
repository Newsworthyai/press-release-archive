# The Bullpen Now Accepting Appointments at the Salon and Spa Galleria Sublett in Arlington, Texas

ARLINGTON, TX, November 06, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Stylist Stephanie Aguilar proudly announces the opening of her new barbering salon, The Bullpen, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Sublett in Arlington.  After attending beauty school while still in high school, Aguilar now has five years of experience working in the beauty industry. She specializes in high fades, mullets and fast haircuts, and has a special knack for baby haircuts.  "I went into this business because I have lots of brothers, and I knew cutting hair for guys was going to be filled with jokes, sports, and no drama," Aguilar said. "I really love the line work, fades and diversity in cutting men's hair."  Aguilar prides herself on ensuring that her customers enjoy a unique and affordable experience while visiting her salon.  "I am a stylist meant for people with all sorts of budgets," Aguilar said. "I do not overcharge for more difficult haircuts."  The Bullpen is currently accepting new customers, and services are available by appointment from 10 a.m.-6 p.m. Tuesday through Thursday, 11 a.m.-6 p.m. Friday, and 11 a.m.-5 p.m. Saturday by appointment only. Schedule an appointment through Aguilar's booking site. New clients who show proof of having looked up her services online will receive a free five-minute neck massage.  Conveniently located close to numerous businesses, shopping centers, and several major highway interchanges, The Bullpen in the Salon and Spa Galleria Sublett is easily accessible from any direction. There is plenty of free parking for customers, and the building is handicap accessible, with wide hallways. For more information, contact Aguilar by text at (972) 515-3605.   About The Bullpen Located inside the Salon and Spa Galleria South Sublett, The Bullpen offers children's and men's hair services, including cuts, fades, shaves and waxing at the Salon and Spa Galleria Sublett in Arlington, Texas.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria Sublett, visit the salon chain's website.
