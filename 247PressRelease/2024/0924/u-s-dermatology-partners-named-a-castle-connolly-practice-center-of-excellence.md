# U.S. Dermatology Partners named a Castle Connolly Practice Center of Excellence

DALLAS, TX, September 24, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- U.S. Dermatology Partners, a leading multi-state network of dermatology practices, has been named Castle Connolly's newest Practice Center of Excellence.  Castle Connolly Practice Centers of Excellence are premier healthcare entities that employ multiple Castle Connolly Top Doctors. Only about 7 percent of the nation's licensed physicians are chosen annually as Castle Connolly Top Doctors in their regions for their specialties.  Founded in 2012, U.S. Dermatology Partners is among the nation's largest dermatology practices, caring for more than two million patients annually across eight states. Eighty-seven of U.S. Dermatology Partners' 200 dermatologists have been named Castle Connolly Top Doctors. Based on the number of doctors being honored, Castle Connolly named U.S. Dermatology Partners the top dermatology group in two states, Texas and Arizona, and third overall in the United States.   U.S. Dermatology Partners is known for delivering coordinated, patient-centered care; using advanced, proven treatments and technologies; and leading valuable clinical research in the field of dermatology. The organization is also well-regarded for its Outreach Program, through which it is expanding access to high-quality dermatological care and education in communities with little or no access to specialty healthcare. Since 2022, more than 30 new offices have been opened in rural areas. Overall, the practice comprises more than 100 locations in eight states: Arizona, Colorado, Kansas, Maryland, Missouri, Oklahoma, Texas, and Virginia.  Our physicians are a resource and partners to their primary care and specialty colleagues, collaborating on the care of patients with chronic skin conditions and skin lesions. To be recognized by one's peers for quality of care and professionalism is among the highest honors.   U.S. Dermatology Partners stands out for having a patient-centered approach, innovative technology and treatment methods, leadership in clinical research, and a coordinated care network, setting itself apart as a national leader in dermatology. Thanks to our extensive network of recognized national leaders in dermatology in areas such as clinical research, psoriasis, and Mohs surgery, and our numerous practice locations, U.S. Dermatology Partners patients have access to the latest and most advanced medical, surgical, and cosmetic skin treatments. As we continue to build the premier dermatology care community in the country, we are building on our legacy of reaching patients closer to home in the communities where they live and work.   "Being named Castle Connolly's newest Practice Center of Excellence is a true testament to the dedication and expertise of our remarkable team at U.S. Dermatology Partners," said Paul Singh, CEO of U.S. Dermatology Partners. "This prestigious recognition reflects our unwavering commitment to clinical excellence, innovative research, and delivering patient-centered care across the communities we serve. Being acknowledged as the third dermatology group in the nation and having nearly 90 of our physicians recognized is an incredible achievement that highlights the exceptional talent within our network and our dedication to providing the highest quality of care to our patients."  "U.S. Dermatology Partners physicians embody the ideals of Castle Connolly Top Doctors: deep expertise, empathetic care, demonstrated leadership, and so much more," said Steve Leibforth, Managing Director, Castle Connolly. "Being named a Practice Center of Excellence, and top three in the nation, is an incredible accomplishment that only underscores U.S. Dermatology Program's commitment to its patients, communities, and improving the overall field of dermatology."  About U.S. Dermatology Partners  U.S. Dermatology Partners is one of the premier dermatology practices in the country, caring for over two million patients each year. With over 100 locations across eight states that span large metroplexes and rural, underserved communities, they provide the benefits of private, personalized levels of care paired with a network of coordinated, specialized dermatology physicians. U.S. Dermatology Partners is fervently focused on providing the highest level of compassionate and comprehensive patient-first care, making it seamless for all people to connect with dermatologists and national leaders in areas such as clinical research, psoriasis, and Mohs surgery, and gain access to state-of-the-art dermatological treatments, including surgical and cosmetic dermatology. To learn more, visit usdermatologypartners.com.   About Castle Connolly Castle Connolly publishes its Top Doctors list at castleconnolly.com and in a variety of print and online partner publications. Nominations are open to all board-certified MDs, DOs, and DPMs. The Castle Connolly team chooses honorees based on nominee criteria that includes their medical education, training, hospital appointments, and disciplinary histories.  Doctors do not and cannot pay to be selected as a Castle Connolly Top Doctor.
