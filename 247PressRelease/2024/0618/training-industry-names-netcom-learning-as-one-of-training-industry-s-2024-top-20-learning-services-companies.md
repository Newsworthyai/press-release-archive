# Training Industry Names NetCom Learning as One of Training Industry's 2024 Top 20 Learning Services Companies

NEW YORK, NY, June 18, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Training Industry has recognized NetCom Learning as one of the 2024 Top 20 Learning Services Companies, marking the third consecutive year the company has received this prestigious recognition. This award recognizes the leading companies providing exceptional learning services including content development, learning administration, learning delivery, vendor management, measurement and analytics, learning strategy, and learning technology.  Training Industry, the leading research and information resource for corporate learning leaders, identified the top 20 companies leading in the learning services market through an intensive data-driven application evaluation process. NetCom Learning was recognized for its breadth and quality of learning services offered, innovation and impact in the learning services market, client representation across industries, and strong business performance and growth.  NetCom Learning offers comprehensive learning services including custom content development, learning administration through its NetCom365 portal, virtual and in-person instructor-led training delivery, training vendor consolidation, data-driven learning analytics, and strategic learning roadmaps tailored to client needs. The company serves over 36,000 client organizations across the globe spanning various industries.  To learn more about the 2024 Top 20 Learning Services Companies, check the full list of 2024 winners here. NetCom Learning was also recognized on the 2023 Top 20 Learning Services Companies list by Training Industry, see the full list of 2023 winners here.  "We are honored to be recognized by Training Industry as one of the top learning services companies for 2024, This back-to-back achievement underscores our team's relentless dedication to delivering transformative learning solutions that drive skills mastery and align with our clients' strategic business objectives. Our innovative learning technologies and managed learning services empower organizations to build a future-ready workforce." Russell Sarder CEO, Netcom Learning  About Netcom Learning NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations in the workplace by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth. With more than 23 years of experience, NetCom Learning has been empowering innovative learning organizations to adapt and drive growth in this fast-paced world by closing critical skill gaps and ensuring smooth deployment, implementation, and consumption through authorized training delivered by Certified Trainers on vendors such as Microsoft, AWS, EC-Council, CompTIA, and more.
