# Rottermond Jewelers Celebrates Grand Reopening in Brighton

BRIGHTON, MI, June 14, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Rottermond Jewelers is thrilled to announce the grand reopening of its Brighton location, an event marked by elegance and excitement. The newly designed store, a masterpiece by the renowned Leslie McGwire, who is celebrated for her award-winning expertise in jewelry store design, now offers an enhanced shopping experience.  Attendees were treated to an exclusive preview of the latest collections from the esteemed brands Michael M and Shinola. This special showcase highlighted the exquisite craftsmanship and innovative designs that Rottermond Jewelers proudly offers to its discerning clientele.  The celebration commenced with a ribbon-cutting ceremony, symbolizing a new chapter for Rottermond Jewelers in Brighton. Guests were then invited to enjoy a delightful selection of appetizers paired with bubbly, creating an atmosphere of festivity and sophistication. Adding to the excitement of the evening, Rottermond Jewelers held a drawing for a $1,000 gift card. The lucky winner, Christel M., was announced to the cheers and congratulations of fellow attendees.  The grand reopening event underscored Rottermond Jewelers' commitment to providing exceptional service and luxury jewelry to the communities they call home. The beautifully redesigned store promises to be a premier destination for fine jewelry, offering customers an unparalleled shopping experience. Contact either the Milford or Brighton locations of their Michigan jewelry stores for more information about their selection and services.  About Rottermond Jewelers  Since their establishment in 1984, Rottermond Jewelers has served as a trustworthy purveyor of all things fine jewelry and luxury watches for the people in Milford and Brighton, Michigan. Rottermond Jewelers is renowned for their unparalleled inventory, top-notch customer care, expert custom jewelry design, and other services. The retailer is an active member of their community and for more than 35 years has donated over $500,000 to local charities and non-profit organizations such as the Pink Fund, the Michigan chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and ALS of Michigan, Inc.
