# Marquis Who's Who Listee Laurie Urbancik Discusses Guilt-Free Desserts on Good Day Stateline

ROCKFORD, IL, December 03, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Laurie Urbancik, owner of No Sugar Momma, appeared on "Good Day Stateline" to discuss her line of healthy snacks and baked goods made with natural, nutrient-rich ingredients. A former Fortune 100 executive, Ms. Urbancik shared how her health challenges inspired her to create treats free from gluten, sugar, dairy and artificial additives. No Sugar Momma snacks are available in 25 Chicago-area locations, online at nosugarmomma.us and on Faire.com for nationwide shipping.  Watch the full segment here.
