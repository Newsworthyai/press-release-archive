# Marquis Who's Who Listee Mark Souza Discusses HVAC Excellence on Studio40 LIVE

SACRAMENTO, CA, December 03, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Mark Souza, owner of Souza's Heating and Air, appeared on "Studio40 LIVE" to highlight his company's commitment to exceptional customer service and transparent pricing in the heating, ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC) industry. Mr. Souza, a former U.S. Air Force member and published author, shared how his team of experienced professionals strives to set a new standard for HVAC services across the metropolitan area. With a focus on building trust, Mr. Souza discussed the importance of trade careers and his plans to grow his team while supporting initiatives like the mikeroweWORKS Foundation.
