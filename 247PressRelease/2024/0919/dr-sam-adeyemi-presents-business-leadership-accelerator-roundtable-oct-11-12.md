# Dr. Sam Adeyemi Presents Business & Leadership Accelerator Roundtable Oct. 11-12

ATLANTA, GA, September 19, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Dr. Sam Adeyemi, author and leadership coach to some of the most successful executives across the globe, will present his Business & Leadership Accelerator Roundtable Oct. 11-12 at the Atlanta Marriott Marquis.  The Business & Leadership Accelerator Roundtable is an exclusive for business leaders who are serious about scaling their results exponentially. Participants will experience:  •	Dinner With Dr. Sam Adeyemi: Kick off the event with an intimate dinner, meet fellow leaders, and start networking in a relaxed setting. •	Focused Learning: Participate in an intensive, hands-on workshop covering key areas: -	Growth: Learn strategies to drive your business forward. -	Systems: Discover how to create efficient and scalable systems. -	Scaling: Understand the principles of scaling your business rapidly. -	Leadership Capacity: Enhance your leadership skills to support sustained growth. •	Interactive Sessions: Engage directly with Dr. Sam Adeyemi and his team during a four-hour workshop and interactive Q&A session. •	Exclusive Spotlight Interview, Q&A, and Live Coaching Session with a leading industry business expert. •	Networking Opportunities: Connect with other high-caliber business leaders, fostering opportunities for collaboration and growth. •	Exclusive Perks: Enjoy a signed autographed book by Dr. Sam Adeyemi, a meet and greet dinner, and one-night accommodation in a king-size deluxe room. •	Receive a Certificate of Participation signed by Dr. Sam Adeyemi (hard copy included).  Experience a transformative journey that will empower you to achieve 10x your business results. Tickets are available here. Tickets are nonrefundable but can be credited toward a future Sam Adeyemi event with notice of cancellation. To attend as a group, team, or organization, email info@samadeyemiglc.com.  About Dr. Sam: Dr. Sam Adeyemi is CEO of Sam Adeyemi, GLC, Inc. and founder and executive director of Daystar Leadership Academy (DLA). More than 45,000 alumni have graduated from DLA programs, and more than 3 million CEOs and high performing individuals follow him on top social media sites. Dr. Sam's latest book is "Dear Leader: Your Flagship Guide to Successful Leadership." He holds a Doctorate in Strategic Leadership from Virginia's Regent University and is a member of the International Leadership Association. He and his wife, Nike live in Atlanta and have three children. Learn more at SamAdeyemi.com.
