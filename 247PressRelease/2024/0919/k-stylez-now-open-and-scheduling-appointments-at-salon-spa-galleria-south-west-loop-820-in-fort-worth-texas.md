# K.Stylez Now Open and Scheduling Appointments at Salon & Spa Galleria South West Loop 820 in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, September 19, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Cosmetologist Kimberly Sewell proudly announces the opening of her new salon, K.Stylez, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Hulen in Fort Worth, Texas.   "I grew up doing everyone's hair for special events like school photos, homecoming and prom," Sewell said. "Not only did I enjoy doing that, I also realized I had talent. That's why I decided to make it my career," she added.  Sewell has spent eight years working in the beauty industry, and sharpened her skills during her time at different shops.  K.Stylez is open from 10 a.m.-6 p.m. Monday-Tuesday and Thursday-Friday, and Saturday from 10 a.m.-5 p.m., and is now accepting new clients. Schedule your appointments now by texting or calling (682) 540-9314, or by using the booking site. Walk-ins are always welcome. Learn more about K.Stylez by visiting Sewell's Instagram page.  Conveniently located close to numerous businesses, shopping centers, and several major highway interchanges, K.Stylez in the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820 is easily accessible from any direction. There is plenty of free parking for customers, and the building is handicap accessible, with wide hallways.   About K.Stylez Located inside the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820, K.Stylez offers a variety of beauty and hair services, including cuts for men, women and children, plus beard and mustache trims; facials; massages; and ear, nose and brow waxing.  Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio suites. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820, visit the salon chain's website.
