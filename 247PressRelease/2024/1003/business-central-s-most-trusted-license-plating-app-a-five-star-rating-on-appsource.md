# Business Central's Most Trusted License Plating App, a Five-Star Rating on AppSource

EDMONTON, AB, October 03, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Insight Works, an innovative provider of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central apps for the manufacturing and distribution industries, is pleased to introduce upgraded functionalities and improved features of the License Plating app. In its seventh year of leading the market, the latest version of the app, License Plating v1.97, expands its capabilities even further.   The License Plating app effortlessly monitors mixed items across pallets, boxes, or containers with unique LPNs for unmatched tracking precision, which reduces manual scans, speeds up warehouse operations, and minimizes data entry errors. By reducing manual input and leveraging LPNs for precise tracking, the app strengthens inventory accuracy, ensuring more reliable inventory counts and management.   Manufacturers and distributors have varying inventory and warehouse management requirements that are beyond the one-size-fits-all solutions; therefore, the License Plating app offers seamless integration with Dynamics 365 Business Central and Insight Work's Warehouse Insight, a powerful warehouse management system.   Why Use License Plating?   - License Plate Creation: Enables users to track multiple items simultaneously by creating a unique license plate. Users can easily input a Location Code, optional Bin, and item details.   - Direct Barcode Printing: Print generated LPN barcodes directly from handheld devices, reducing wait times, accelerating transaction processes, and improving operational efficiency.   - Customizable Archiving Filters: Gain complete control over user control of the archiving process, filtering by Date, Status, Location Code, and more.  Brian Neufeld, Director of Marketing at Insight Works, stated, "We designed License Plating to enhance warehouse management by effortlessly monitoring mixed items with unique LPNs, improving tracking precision. This approach speeds up operations, reduces manual scans, and significantly enhances inventory accuracy. By integrating seamlessly with Business Central and the Warehouse Insight app, Insight Works meets the diverse and specific needs of manufacturers and distributors alike."   The License Plating app simplifies daily warehouse processes through advanced tracking, reduced scanning requirements, and better inventory data management. Whether dealing with mixed items or large volumes, the app integration ensures faster, more precise operations while catering to the unique requirements of the distribution and manufacturing industries.
