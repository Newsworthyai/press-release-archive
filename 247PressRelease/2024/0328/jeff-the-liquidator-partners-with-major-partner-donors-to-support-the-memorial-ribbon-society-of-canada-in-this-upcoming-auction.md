# Jeff, "The Liquidator" partners with Major Partner Donors to support the Memorial Ribbon Society of Canada in this upcoming Auction

VANCOUVER, BC, March 28, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Jeff the Liquidator, owner of Direct Liquidation and Direct Auctions has committed to auctioning off donated items throughout 2024 with proceeds of donations going to the Memorial Ribbon Society. The Memorial Ribbon Society helps the families of fallen Police and Peace Officers throughout Canada by providing future scholarships to the children and assistance to grieving families. ​ ​ Following the success of the donations from the first auction, this Saturday, March 30, 2024 will be the second auction of many to come with items donated by partners of Jeff and Direct Liquidation with proceeds going to the Memorial Ribbon Society.​ ​ The March 30 auction donated items include renowned auctioneer Roy from Storage Wars Canada, in partnership with Direct Liquidation Toronto and XO Auctions, presents an exclusive addition to our event: a 'Range Rover Sport SVR for Kids' generously donated by Roy himself LOT 9A, "RANGE ROVER SPORT SVR for Kids".  In a testament to his unwavering dedication to charitable causes, Jeff Schwarz, television personality and owner of Direct Liquidation, has magnanimously contributed a signed memorabilia featuring the esteemed Floyd Mayweather Jr. to the Ribbon Memorial Society LOT 9B, Signed Floyd Mayweather Jr. w/COA.  This lot is a must have for any ice hockey collector. It is a framed card collection of Edmonton Oilers star player, Connor McDavid #97 LOT 9C Collectors' Item donated by Richy Middleton.  Another lot is a crowd favourite from West Coast Wash, voted Best Car Wash, this auto detailing package will definitely upgrade your car to looking like brand new, LOT 9D, West Coast Wash 1 Comprehensive Mini Auto Detailing Package, Redeem this great donation throughout the year (1 of 5 mini auto detailing valued at $1500.00 by visiting 5318 Kingsway in Burnaby.  This lot is graciously donated by Coast North Vancouver for Tire Rotation, Wheel Alignment, and tune up on your vehicle LOT 9E, Ribbon Memorial Society Coast North Vancouver.  "Successful bidders of each item can not only enjoy their newfound treasure but can know that their purchase is going to support families and children of our fallen honourable law enforcement," Says Jeff Schwarz, AKA The Liquidator. "Plus, participating in online auctions is fun. Things can get pretty crazy, and I love engaging with bidders live online." ​ Participating in the auction is easy and registration is free. Simply sign up and start bidding on these lots. You can also place a max bid if you cannot attend the online live auction on Saturday March 30rd, 2024.​ ​ Throughout 2024 Direct Auctions will be adding donated lots to each auction continuing to raise money. Each auction will vary so be sure to sign up for email notifications to keep up to date on what amazing items will be going up for auction in support of the Memorial Ribbon Society. ​  To see all the auction items, visit: https://www.icollector.com/auction.aspx?as=97341  Auction Date & Time: Saturday, March 30, 2024  ​ Location: See items and register online  Details: Registration is free.
