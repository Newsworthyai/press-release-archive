# BZBGEAR Partners with Iris to Revolutionize Cloud-Based Camera Control

SACRAMENTO, CA, September 09, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- BZBGEAR®, a global leader in end-to-end pro AV and broadcasting solutions, is excited to announce a partnership with Iris, a pioneer in AI-powered cloud camera control solutions. This collaboration integrates Iris's advanced software into BZBGEAR's PTZ cameras, enabling users to control their devices through the cloud via Iris's platform, without needing additional hardware.  Cloud-Based Camera Control at Your Fingertips  This partnership brings a revolutionary approach to camera management, allowing BZBGEAR cameras to be fully operated from anywhere in the world through a cloud-based interface. Users can seamlessly adjust camera movement, fine-tune settings, and take advantage of AI-driven features like auto-tracking in real-time—all from a single, intuitive interface. This integration sets a new standard for video production, making it more flexible, scalable, and user-friendly for professionals in education, broadcasting, corporate settings, and live events.  "We're thrilled to collaborate with Iris and bring their advanced AI-powered platform to our camera lineup," said Eugene Bocharov, CEO and president, BZBGEAR. "This partnership empowers our customers to control their cameras with unprecedented ease—all without any additional hardware."  Redefining the Future of Video Production  The combination of Iris's cloud control technology with BZBGEAR's innovative camera designs provides an unprecedented solution for video professionals. This integration not only enhances accessibility but also simplifies workflows, giving users the freedom to manage their camera systems from virtually anywhere, whether they're in a broadcast studio, a classroom, or on the go.  "It's awesome partnering with BZBGEAR to bring Iris to a global audience of cameras and customers," said Noah Johnson, CEO, Iris. "Together, we're delivering a groundbreaking solution that will redefine how users interact with their cameras."  The Future of Cloud-Enabled Camera Control  This partnership with Iris aligns perfectly with BZBGEAR's mission to innovate and lead the way in delivering smart AV solutions. By integrating cloud control technology into their product line, BZBGEAR is offering a more accessible and powerful solution for camera control that redefines what is possible in video production.  About BZBGEAR  Established in 2019 and headquartered in Sacramento, California, BZBGEAR is a global manufacturer of end-to-end pro AV and broadcasting products. Their mission is to provide top-grade AV and broadcasting solutions focusing on design, user experience, and lifetime product support. Every solution is carefully designed by BZBGEAR hardware and software engineers to ensure unmatched quality and features. With award-winning products and industry-leading support, BZBGEAR is the number-one choice for any AV and broadcast application. Learn more at bzbgear.com.  About Iris  Iris is revolutionizing video production with a suite of intelligent production tools. Iris Studio, their flagship product, offers powerful cloud-based control features that integrate seamlessly with leading camera brands. Iris is dedicated to empowering professionals to achieve superior video production results with ease and efficiency.
