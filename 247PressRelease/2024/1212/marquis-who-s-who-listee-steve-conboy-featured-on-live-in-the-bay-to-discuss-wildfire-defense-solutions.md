# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Steve Conboy, Featured on LIVE! in the Bay to Discuss Wildfire Defense Solutions

SAN FRANCISCO, CA, December 12, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Steve Conboy, Chief Technologist of Mighty Fire Breaker, appeared on "LIVE! in the Bay" with host Jessica Wills to discuss innovative ways to protect homes from wildfires. Mr. Conboy introduced a groundbreaking chemistry solution, safe for children, pets and wildlife, that can be remotely activated to defend properties during evacuations without relying on water pressure. As an EPA Partner of the Year, Conboy emphasized the urgency of wildfire mitigation in California and the importance of using non-toxic solutions for long-term safety.
