# The Dairy Alliance and Grandbaby Cakes Debut Festive Holiday Recipes

ATLANTA, GA, December 20, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Dairy Alliance, a nonprofit funded by dairy farm families in the Southeast, and Grandbaby Cakes, led by Jocelyn Delk Adams, are releasing new recipes just in time to make your holidays more merry and bright. The latest installment of their dairy-inspired recipe series features a Brisket Mac and Cheese and Eggnog Bread Pudding, and of course, showcases another delicious way to incorporate dairy into your cooking.   Grandbaby Cake's Brisket Mac and Cheese is a creamy, cheese-filled pasta dish topped with tender, smoky brisket. Packed with high-quality protein, it's a hearty, crowd-pleasing holiday favorite. Fans can also try her Eggnog Bread Pudding, a rich and festive dessert with creamy eggnog and warm spices. Lactose-intolerant? Use lactose-free milk!   "Partnering with The Dairy Alliance has been such a wonderful opportunity to showcase how versatile and accessible dairy can be in creating recipes that truly bring people together," said Jocelyn Delk Adams, Founder of Grandbaby Cakes. "These dishes were developed with love, tradition, and the holidays in mind, and I'm excited to share them with families looking to elevate their holiday celebrations."   The Dairy Alliance teamed up with acclaimed author, television personality, and recipe blogger Jocelyn Delk Adams of Grandbaby Cakes earlier this season to share recipes featuring dairy. She is known for creative recipes that come from the heart and add a modern twist to traditional flavors. Adams is sharing exclusive holiday-inspired recipes with The Dairy Alliance that highlight the versatility of dairy milk and lactose-free dairy milk.   "Eggnog Bread Pudding and Brisket Mac and Cheese are quintessential holiday comfort foods, and we are thrilled to have Jocelyn put her creative spin on these festive dishes said Geri Berdak, Chief Executive Officer at The Dairy Alliance. "From providing high-quality protein to offering lactose-free options, dairy is a key ingredient that enhances both the flavor and nutritional profile of these recipes, meeting the needs of today's diverse households."   The Dairy Alliance is making it the season of winning with their holiday sweepstakes. Fifteen winners will receive a signed copy of Jocelyn Delk Adams' cookbook, Everyday Grand. Visit The Dairy Alliance's blog to explore Grandbaby Cakes' exclusive recipes, including the Brisket Mac and Cheese and Eggnog Bread Pudding, and enter the holiday sweepstakes for your chance to win. For more information about The Dairy Alliance, visit https://thedairyalliance.com or follow them on Facebook or Instagram @TheDairyAlliance.  About The Dairy Alliance  The Dairy Alliance is a nonprofit funded by dairy farm families of the Southeast. We work diligently with dairy farmers, schools, sports teams, health professionals, local organizations, state leaders, the media, and the public to promote dairy foods and knowledge about the dairy industry. Our efforts center in eight states: Alabama, Georgia, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Virginia.
