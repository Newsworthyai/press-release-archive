# FFTV+ Online Short Series Create a New Ecosystem, Disrupting Traditional Models

AUSTIN, TX, July 31, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- At Consensus 2024, a gathering of renowned guests took place, including Robert F. Kennedy Jr., a16z crypto founder and managing partner Chris Dixon, ARK Invest CEO Cathie Wood, Draper Associates founder Tim Draper, U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission commissioner Hester M. Pierce, and Ripple CEO Brad Garlinghouse. These industry leaders, along with numerous attendees, discussed the latest developments and future prospects of blockchain technology.  During the Conference, FFTV+ Online Short Series unveiled its groundbreaking new ecosystem, revolutionizing traditional content creation and consumption models. George Kong, founder of FFTV Live Streaming, showcased how the platform integrates a voting mechanism with artificial intelligence to create a novel short series creation ecosystem.  Through the voting mechanism, viewers can participate in selecting scripts and actors, and even take part in various stages of content creation. This interactive approach significantly enhances viewer engagement and satisfaction while providing more opportunities for creators and actors to showcase their talents.  The FFTV+ platform also leverages AI technology to improve the efficiency and quality of content creation. With stable diffusion technology, the platform can automatically generate high-quality video content based on simple descriptions, lowering the barrier to entry and enabling more creators to participate in short Series production.  The success of FFTV+ demonstrates the immense potential of blockchain and artificial intelligence technologies in the short series field, providing valuable experience and reference for other content creation platforms.  FFTV Live Streaming, founded by George Kong, is at the forefront of media innovation, combining AI to revolutionize content creation and viewer engagement. The FFTV+ platform allows viewers to vote on scripts and actors, participate in content creation, and uses AI to generate high-quality video content.  Related Link: https://fftv.ai
