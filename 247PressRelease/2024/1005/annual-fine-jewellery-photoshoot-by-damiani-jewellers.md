# Annual Fine Jewellery Photoshoot by Damiani Jewellers

TORONTO, ON, October 05, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Damiani Jewellers, located in the heart of Woodbridge, is excited to announce their much-anticipated annual editorial-style photoshoot, set to highlight their latest jewellery collection. Since 2018, the jeweller has collaborated with renowned Toronto photographer Isabella (@izzydigital), crafting visually stunning content for social media and advertising campaigns.  Each year, the creative vision of Michelle, Damiani's Director of Marketing and Communications, shines through in the photoshoot. Michelle meticulously curates mood boards that guide every detail—from hair and makeup to wardrobe and poses—ensuring a cohesive aesthetic that perfectly complements the hand-selected jewellery. These photoshoots have taken place in some iconic locations in Toronto, such as the Distillery District and Yorkville Village, providing a sophisticated backdrop for the elegant designs.  A hallmark of the event is the selection of models, ranging from familiar faces to local influencers, each chosen to enhance the artistry of the jewellery. With each piece thoughtfully matched to the models, every photoshoot aims to capture the timeless elegance and modern flair of Damiani's collection.  Fans of fine jewellery and photography alike are invited to follow Damiani Jewellers on social media for a first look at the stunning imagery created by this year's photoshoot. Stay tuned for the release of this extraordinary visual showcase! Contact their Woodbridge jewellery store for more information about their jewellery selection and services.  About Damiani Jewellers With over 60 years of experience serving clients with their fine jewellery expertise, Damiani Jewellers is the Greater Toronto area's premier jewellery retailer. For three generations, their family-owned business has provided engagement rings, wedding bands, loose diamonds, fine jewellery items, and luxury watches for their customers' most treasured moments. Damiani Jewellers is also proud to be an authorized dealer of authentic Rolex watches, and this accomplishment speaks to the unbeatable customer service that each shopper receives.
