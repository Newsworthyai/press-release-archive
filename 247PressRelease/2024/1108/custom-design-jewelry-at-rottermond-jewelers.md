# Custom Design Jewelry at Rottermond Jewelers

MILFORD AND BRIGHTON, MI, November 08, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Rottermond Jewelers is proud to have two skilled craftsmen on staff in their Milford and Brighton stores to create unique custom designed jewelry for their customers. Having these two jewelers with 40 years combined experience in-house makes the process of creating custom jewelry more memorable and guaranteed to create gorgeous results.  Custom jewelry is first created through CAD and hand carved wax castings to make a rendering of the ring, necklace, bracelet, or earrings you wish to be created. Redesigning heirlooms or using gemstones and diamonds from old jewelry to make something new is also one of Rottermond Jewelers custom design specialties.   Along with custom design services, Rottermond's in-house jewelers also make exceptional repairs to fine jewelry and watches. From repairing tips and prongs, giving rings a spa to get back in new condition, channel setting repairs, replacing missing or damaged stones, shank replacement, and more, their team is also proficient with the laser machine to work on platinum or to match handwriting for engravings. Rottermond Jewelers in-house watchmaker can change your luxury timepieces batteries, replace parts, and complete overhauls of your watches. All of their professional watch and jewelry services are conducted in a timely manner so that you're not parted from your gorgeous items for long or can plan out the perfect custom gift for someone you care about.   For 40 years Rottermond Jewelers has been providing the best selection of and care for fine jewelry and watches in the Milford and Brighton areas. Contact either of their Michigan jewelry stores to learn more about how to start a new custom design project.  About Rottermond Jewelers  Since their establishment in 1984, Rottermond Jewelers has served as a trustworthy purveyor of all things fine jewelry and luxury watches for the people in Milford and Brighton, Michigan. Rottermond Jewelers is renowned for their unparalleled inventory, top-notch customer care, expert custom jewelry design, and other services. The retailer is an active member of their community and for more than 35 years has donated over $500,000 to local charities and non-profit organizations such as the Pink Fund, the Michigan chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and ALS of Michigan, Inc. Get in touch with the jewelry retailer by sending an email to info@rottermond.com or by calling (248) 685-1150 for their Milford location and (810) 220-0015 for their Brighton location!
