# Ackerman & Co. and Trout Daniel & Associates Represent StorageMart in Acquisition of 865-Unit Self-Storage Facility in Canton, GA

ATLANTA, GA, September 12, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Ackerman & Co. and Trout Daniel & Associates (TD&A) have brokered the acquisition of a 95,430-square-foot self-storage facility in Canton, GA, on behalf of StorageMart. The newly built facility at 4843 Holly Springs Pkwy features 865 units.   David Paulson, Stephen Lapierre and Wyatt Whitaker of Ackerman Co. and Steven Cornblatt and Coleman Tirone of Trout Daniel & Associates represented StorageMart in the transaction. The seller of the property was SMV Hudson LLC.  The self-storage facility – StorageMart's fifth property in Georgia – features a mix of unit sizes, climate-controlled options and drive-up access.   "This acquisition provides StorageMart a newly built, state-of-the-art facility in the fast-growing city of Canton in north metro Atlanta in a location with a strong mix of residential communities, retailers and businesses. We're very pleased to assist StorageMart in this acquisition as part of its strategic expansion in key markets," said David Paulson, Senior Vice President of Ackerman & Co.  The Canton facility has enhanced security features including 24-hour video surveillance, on-site management and 24/7 customer support.  Also in Georgia, StorageMart has facilities in Athens at 3985 Atlanta Hwy and 251 Collins Industrial Blvd, Lawrenceville at 794 Scenic Hwy South, and in Milledgeville at 1891 North Columbia Street.  StorageMart StorageMart is a leading provider of self-storage solutions with a strong presence across the continental United States, Canada, and United Kingdom. Headquartered in Atlanta, Ackerman & Co. is a privately held, full-service commercial real estate firm focused on providing quality investment, brokerage, management and development services in the Southeast and Texas.
