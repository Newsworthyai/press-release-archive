# FFTV+ Explores Advanced AI Digital Human Technology in Short Series at Consensus 2024

AUSTIN, TX, August 08, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- At Consensus 2024, a gathering of renowned guests took place, including Robert F. Kennedy Jr., a16z crypto founder and managing partner Chris Dixon, ARK Invest CEO Cathie Wood, Draper Associates founder Tim Draper, U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission commissioner Hester M. Pierce, and Ripple CEO Brad Garlinghouse. These industry leaders, along with numerous attendees, discussed the latest developments and future prospects of blockchain technology.  At the conference, FFTV+ showcased its cutting-edge AI Digital Human Technology within the short series sector. George Kong, founder of FFTV+ Live Streaming, highlighted the platform's innovative use of AI technology to revolutionize viewer engagement and interaction in short series production.  The AI Digital Human Technology on the FFTV+ platform allows for the creation of digital personas that can play iconic characters from short dramas, enabling them to converse directly with viewers. These digital humans are equipped with sophisticated long-term memory capabilities, which ensure that interactions are consistently personalized and contextually relevant. Each digital persona retains unique conversational memories with individual users, allowing for highly tailored and meaningful engagements that feel authentic and personal.  In addition to their memory capabilities, FFTV+'s digital humans leverage advanced technology to accurately replicate voice and tone, providing an immersive communication experience that closely mimics human interaction. The platform's real-time video rendering technology enables seamless and lifelike live conversations, making it possible for digital humans to engage with audiences in real time.  With these capabilities, FFTV+ can recreate classic characters from short dramas, bringing movie characters to life with vivid detail. These digital humans interact with audiences in real time, offering companionship and engagement by embodying the personas of beloved characters, providing emotional support, and fostering a deeper connection with viewers.  The success of the FFTV+ platform underscores the transformative potential of AI Digital Human Technology in the short series industry, offering valuable insights and references for other content creation platforms seeking to leverage AI for innovative storytelling and audience engagement.  FFTV Live Streaming, is a leader in media innovation, specializing in AI-driven solutions that transform content creation and audience interaction. The platform's AI Digital Human Technology empowers creators to bring iconic characters to life, allowing them to engage with audiences through realistic and personalized interactions.
