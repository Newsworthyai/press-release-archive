# Key Housing Announces Featured SoCal Listing for Short Term Rentals for August 2024: The Residences at Bella Terra

This exceptional apartment complex offers luxury accommodations ideally suited for corporate travelers seeking short term housing in Huntington Beach and nearby communities.  "Selecting 'The Residences at Bella Terra' as our featured listing for August 2024 underscores our commitment to providing best-in-class short term housing options for business travelers," said Bob Lee, spokesperson for Key Housing. "With its prime location and outstanding amenities, this property exemplifies the quality and comfort we aim to offer our clients seeking corporate housing that makes a home away from home possible."  Located near the bustling Bella Terra entertainment and shopping center (https://www.bellaterra-hb.com/), The Residences at Bella Terra (https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-rental-properties/residences-at-bella-terra/) provide unparalleled access to a variety of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. These amenities create a top-rated atmosphere for the busy corporate traveler seeking short term housing in Huntington Beach, California.
