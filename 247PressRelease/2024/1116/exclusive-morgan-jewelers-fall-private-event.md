# Exclusive Morgan Jewelers Fall Private Event

SALT LAKE CITY, UT, November 16, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- This fall, Morgan Jewelers is hosting its highly anticipated Semi-Annual Private Event, a one-day celebration of luxury, style, and exclusive savings. This elegant gathering invites guests to explore their exquisite collections and enjoy unparalleled offers and giveaways.  First 100 guests who RSVP will receive a free gift: the first 100 to sign up will receive a pair of Three Stone Cubic Zirconia Earrings (valued at $30). This beautiful gift adds a touch of elegance to any collection and is available for one day only.  During the event, clients can take advantage of exceptional one-day pricing, including a Three Stone Necklace for just $29.99 (valued at $65) and special promotions on select fine jewelry, with discounts ranging from $25 to $750 based on purchase value. Attendees can also receive a $750 Gift Card*, available for use on this special day.  In addition, guests will be entered into our Grand Prize Drawing, where one lucky woman will win a 14K Gold Diamond Pendant and Earrings set by Shy Creation (valued at $1,000) and one man will receive a 14K Yellow Gold Rolo Chain Bracelet (valued at $1,700).  Discover remarkable deals on Flashpoint Created Diamond Studs, featuring 1 Carat studs for $500 (originally $999) and 2 Carat studs for $1,000 (originally $1,999).  With over a century in business, Morgan Jewelers is grateful to their customers for the success their family-owned and operated jewelry stores maintain. Contact one of Morgan Jewelers' seventeen locations in six states of the Rocky Mountain Region, Utah, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, Oregon, and Washington, for more information about their jewelry selection and about the event.  About Morgan Jewelers  Established in 1914, Morgan Jewelers has grown substantially throughout the years, yet holds true to the traditions and work ethic that helped build their business. As a third-generation family-owned business, they understand that important moments deserve the best. They are dedicated to providing the utmost customer care and incredible selection of designer jewelry to their clientele that stretches across seventeen locations in six states along the Rocky Mountain Region. Contact any of the Morgan Jewelers locations in Utah, Nevada, Idaho, Oregon, Washington, and Montana for more information about their selection and professional services.
