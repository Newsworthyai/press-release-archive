# Hobby Shop Spaces Available at Mouser Self Storage

ALVARADO, TX, March 19, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- RDS Real Estate, a leader in commercial space for lease in the greater Fort Worth area, is pleased to announce the availability of small shop space for rent in the newly constructed Mouser Self Storage facility located at 4595 J D Mouser Pkwy in Alvarado, Texas.  Designed for the hobbyist in search of an alternative to working from home, the unique units come with electricity and lights and are located on the ground floor for easy drive-up access. Rates are affordable and rental can be month-to-month or for a predetermined length of time.  The newly available hobby shop units range in size from 12' x 30' to 24' x 40.' The shops are ideal for people that do woodworking, furniture restoration, creative painting or other artwork, auto repair/rebuilding, or any other endeavor that requires electricity and enough room to work and store supplies and equipment. They also suit entrepreneurs who make and sell their own products and writers in need of a quiet space to create.  In order to help keep property safe and secure, Mouser Self Storage has gated access, 24/7 video surveillance, and NokeONE Smart Entry (a Bluetooth electronic lock system which can be accessed by a smart device) for each individual unit. With easy access to I-35 and Highway 287, the location is convenient from a number of towns in the Fort Worth area.  For more information about Mouser Self Storage, contact Manager Ashley Oliphant at (682) 800-3949 or visit mouserselfstorage.com.
