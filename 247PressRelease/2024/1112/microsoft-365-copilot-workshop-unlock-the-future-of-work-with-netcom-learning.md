# Microsoft 365 Copilot Workshop: Unlock the Future of Work with NetCom Learning!

NEW YORK, NY, November 12, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, an award-winning Microsoft Training Services Partner, is excited to announce the upcoming Microsoft 365 Copilot Use Cases Workshop on December 10, 2024. This engaging live virtual event is designed for a diverse audience, including business leaders, decision-makers, and professionals eager to upskill in the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence and productivity tools.   In this comprehensive three-hour workshop, participants will learn key concepts from the MS-4004 course, focusing on how to use Microsoft 365 Copilot in practical ways. They will learn how to streamline workflows and drive innovation using copilot in departments such as IT, Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR, and Operations. The sessions will be led by Jason Kellington, a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) and industry expert known for his engaging teaching style.   In addition to expert training, participants will have the opportunity to network with over 200+ industry experts attending the M365 Copilot Workshop. They will also receive digital achievement trophies and badges from Microsoft, recognizing their newly acquired skills. Furthermore, attendees will gain exclusive access to the NetCom 365 portal, a digital learning platform that provides additional resources and tools for tracking their progress.   This workshop presents a unique opportunity for individuals and organizations to prepare for the future of work in an AI-driven environment. By participating in this live virtual event, attendees will be better equipped to navigate challenges and seize opportunities presented by emerging technologies.   For those looking to stay ahead in their careers and enhance their organizational capabilities, this workshop is not to be missed. To learn more about the event and secure a spot, please visit Microsoft 365 Copilot Use Cases Workshop.  Join NetCom Learning on December 10th to explore innovative solutions and unlock your potential in the AI era!  NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations in the workplace by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth.
