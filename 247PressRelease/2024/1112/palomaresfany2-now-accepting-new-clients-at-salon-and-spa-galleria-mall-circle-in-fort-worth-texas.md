# Palomaresfany2 Now Accepting New Clients at Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, November 12, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Beautician Maria Estefani Cano Palomares proudly announces the opening of Palomaresfany2, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth, Texas.  With four years of experience in the industry, Palomares learned her skills in beauty school and honed them while working in salons.  "I chose this business because I really enjoy working with people," Palomares said. "I like offering my customers the best care possible."  In addition to hair care, Palomaresfany2 offers other services, including lash lift and false lash application; microblading; and lip blush tattoos. Palomares is delighted to be setting up shop in her new location, and is looking forward to serving new customers, along with her current clientele.  As a special promotion, new clients will receive a 10-percent discount on highlight, color or balayage services.  Palomaresfany2 is open from 10 a.m.-6 p.m. Tuesday-Friday by appointment only. Schedule your appointments through the booking site, or by calling or texting Palomares at (817) 706-7493.  In close proximity to several large businesses and shopping areas, Palomaresfany2 at the Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth is a convenient location for clients. Serving the Ridgemar and Ridglea areas of Fort Worth, Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle is close to several major interchanges and roadways, allowing easy access from any direction.  About Palomaresfany2 Located inside the Salon and Spa Galleria, 2400 Mall Circle #100, Suite 301, Palomaresfany2 offers cuts, wash/blowdry, color, highlights, balayage, permanents, keratin treatments, and more.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth, visit the salon chain's website.
