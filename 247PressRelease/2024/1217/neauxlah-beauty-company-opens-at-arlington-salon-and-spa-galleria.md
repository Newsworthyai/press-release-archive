# NeauxLah Beauty Company Opens at Arlington Salon and Spa Galleria

ARLINGTON, TX, December 17, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Salon owner Dajane "Daee" Landers is pleased to announce the opening of her new shop, NeauxLah (NOLA) Beauty Company, located in Suite #121 of the Salon & Spa Galleria at 700 Six Flags Drive in Arlington, Texas. By choosing this location, Ms. Landers has joined a group of experienced health and beauty professionals who are each able to offer specialized services in their own private and luxurious suite.  With 18 years in the beauty industry and comprehensive training from the well-respected Ogle School for Hair Skin and Nails, Daee has the knowledge and expertise to provide exceptional customer service which keeps clients coming back. In the new salon, she has incorporated New Orleans Style and Rhythm in the d√©cor to promote a "homey creole feeling" while at the same time ensuring that clients receive the very best care.  Services at NeauxLah Beauty Company include traditional and custom cuts for men, women and children, lash extensions and lash/brow tints, single virgin and creative color techniques, hair extension consultation and application (stitch, bond, clip ins or tape ins), deep conditioning with silk press or blow out, and ponytail installations with hair, retwist and detox. Salon specialties include signature lash extensions, professional unit maintenance and certified natural hair regimens. A selection of high-quality products (including professional brands and the salon's own product line) is available as well.  The NeauxLah Beauty Company is open Tuesday through Friday from 8:00am to 4:30pm and Saturday from 9:30am to 3:30pm. Clients can easily book services by texting the salon at (682) 500-1907 or by making an appointment on-line at dallasbeautybarandacademy.com/book-online. Walk-ins are also welcome to accommodate everyone's needs.  For more information about the salon and to view samples of the services provided, check out the salon's activity on Facebook at DaeeLashedMe and on Instagram at NeauxLahBeautyCo and DaeeTheBeautician. Daee also offers training for those looking to enter the industry or enhance their skills. All classes focus on hands-on experience and personalized attention to ensure that participants gain practical skills that can be directly applied in their own work. More information can be found at the Daee Lashed Me website.  When asked why she chose the Arlington Salon and Spa Galleria for her new location, DaJane replied "I have always been passionate about beauty and self-care and wanted to create a space where clients can feel confident and pampered, offering them personalized experiences that enhance their natural beauty." Additionally, "I love our salon's location because it's easily accessible and situated in a vibrant neighborhood that attracts a diverse clientele."  About NeauxLah Beauty Company NeauxLah Beauty Company is in Suite #121 of the Salon and Spa Galleria located at 700 Six Flags Drive in Arlington, Texas 76001 and serves the Mansfield, Kennedale, Crowley, Burleson, Everman and Forest Hill areas. The salon is open Tuesday to Friday 8:00am to 4:30pm and Saturday 9:30 am to 3:30pm. To make an appointment, text or call (682) 500-1907 or  book online. Walk-ins are also welcome. Says owner Dajane Landers, "It's important to know that we prioritize client satisfaction and continually seek to improve our services based on feedback. Google Reviews Get Discounts!"
