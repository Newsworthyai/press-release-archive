# Summer Vacation by Huntington Fine Jewelers

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, May 17, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Huntington Fine Jewelers announces an enticing summer promotion, transforming your shopping experience into an extraordinary journey. From May 20th to May 25th, customers who make a purchase of $3999 or more will receive a FREE getaway of their choice!  This exclusive offer grants patrons a 7-night resort getaway with numerous destinations available. Whether you envision a romantic retreat with your loved one, or aspire to propose with a gorgeous engagement ring in a captivating locale, this promotion promises to fulfill your desires. With a variety of destinations ranging from tropical paradises to exotic international locales, the options are boundless.  Furthermore, Huntington Fine Jewelers offers flexible financing options to suit your financial needs. Take advantage of 12-month 0% financing or special 60-month financing to make your desired purchase more attainable. Explore their gorgeous selection of bridal and fine jewelry for that special someone.   Embark on the summer journey of a lifetime by visiting Huntington Fine Jewelers Oklahoma jewelry stores during May 20th-25th. Complete details are available in-store at their Oklahoma City, Yukon, Stillwater, Midwest City, Norman, and Shawnee showrooms.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers   For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services.
