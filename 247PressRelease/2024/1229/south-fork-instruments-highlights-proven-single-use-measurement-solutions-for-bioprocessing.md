# South Fork Instruments Highlights Proven Single-Use Measurement Solutions for Bioprocessing

AUBURN, CA, December 29, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- South Fork Instruments, a leading supplier of measurement instrumentation, announces the availability of Kemtrak disposable optical measurement cells designed to eliminate cross-contamination risks in bioprocessing applications. These single-use cells address critical measurement needs in chromatographic separation, filtration, and protein concentration monitoring.  The measurement cells feature zero dead volume design and optical path lengths from 0.5mm to 10mm, enabling precise measurements across a range of 4.5 to 90 OD. Manufactured from medical-grade poly-phenylsulfone (PPSU), the cells meet USP Class VI and FDA requirements for bioprocessing applications.  "The bioprocessing industry increasingly demands flexible, contamination-free measurement solutions," says John Daly, Technical Director at South Fork Instruments. "These disposable cells deliver exceptional measurement precision while eliminating cleaning validation requirements and cross-contamination risks."  The cells accommodate line sizes from 1/8" to 1" with ready-to-attach hose barb connections, supporting applications from small-scale discovery to full production. A quick-release mechanism ensures precise positioning in the optical path for reliable measurements.  For detailed specifications or to learn more about Kemtrak measurement solutions, contact South Fork Instruments at 925-461-5059 or visit www.southforkinstruments.com.  About South Fork Instruments: South Fork Instruments, based in Auburn, Calif., is an established supplier of measurement instrumentation, analyzers, and control products serving the Americas. The company's cross-industry experience translates into a unique ability to identify, develop, and implement innovative measurement solutions. The state-of-the-art instrumentation products provide reliable, low maintenance, high-value results for plant control and quality improvement for a wide range of industry segments, including chemical plants, breweries, refineries, mining, power generation, food processing, offshore/onshore oil production, and pipelines.
