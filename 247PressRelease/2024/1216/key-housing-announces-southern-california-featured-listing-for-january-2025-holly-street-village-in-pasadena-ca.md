# Key Housing Announces Southern California Featured Listing for January 2025: Holly Street Village in Pasadena, CA

PASADENA, CA, December 16, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Key Housing, a leading provider of corporate and short-term housing solutions in California, proudly announces Holly Street Village as its featured Southern California property for January 2025. Located in the heart of Old Town Pasadena at 151 E Holly St, Pasadena, CA 91103, this luxurious corporate rental opportunity offers a unique space for business travelers to experience comfort, convenience, and vibrant culture.  Bob Lee, President of Key Housing, emphasized the appeal of this exceptional property: "Holly Street Village exemplifies the perfect balance of luxury and practicality for business travelers. Its location in Old Town Pasadena makes it the ideal choice for those seeking short term corporate rentals in Southern California. We selected it for January for these reasons, plus the incredible weather Pasadena offers wintertime travelers."  Persons who want to learn more can visit https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-rental-properties/holly-street-village/, while those wanting to browse all Pasadena properties can visit https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-housing-city/pasadena/. Those who want a more hands-on approach are encouraged to reach out to a rental consultant at the company.
