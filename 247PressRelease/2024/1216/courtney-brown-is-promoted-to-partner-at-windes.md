# Courtney Brown Is Promoted to Partner at Windes

LONG BEACH, CA, December 16, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Courtney Brown joined Windes in 2010 and has since been an integral part of the Tax practice. She specializes in tax planning and consulting for large, midsize, and small businesses, as well as tax compliance for corporations, partnerships, and high-net-worth individuals. Courtney's expertise spans a wide range of industries, including real estate, professional services, and the unique sectors of oil, gas, and renewable energy, allowing her to deliver exceptional results to her diverse client base.  Courtney holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of California, Riverside, and a Master of Science in Accounting with an emphasis in Taxation from San Diego State University. She is a Certified Public Accountant and an active member of both the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the California Society of Certified Public Accountants (CalCPA).  "This is a well-earned promotion for Courtney," says Managing Partner Sean McFerson. "Her commitment to client service and her technical expertise have made her an invaluable resource to the firm and our clients. I am confident that Courtney's leadership will strengthen the Tax department and contribute to the continued success of our clients and our firm."  About Windes  Windes is a leading advisory, audit, and tax firm for growth-oriented small and mid-sized privately held companies, nonprofit organizations, and high-net-worth individuals. Our approach uses tailored expertise to proactively inform decision-making to maximize our clients' business potential. For more information on how we can be your trusted advisor, visit us at windes.com.
