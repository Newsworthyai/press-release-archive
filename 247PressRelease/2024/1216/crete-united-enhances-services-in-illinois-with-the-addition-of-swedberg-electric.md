# Crete United Enhances Services in Illinois with the Addition of Swedberg Electric

CHARLOTTE, NC, December 16, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Crete United ("Crete"), a leading provider of energy efficiency and HVAC, electrical, and other mechanical ("MEP") services, has added Swedberg Electric ("Swedberg') to the Crete United partner network.  Swedberg is a commercial electrical contractor based in Sycamore, I.L. and serving Northern I.L., including the Chicagoland area for 20 years.  "Our Chicago partner network now sets the standard for what we are cultivating at Crete United with the addition of Swedberg Electric," said Mike Cox, CEO of Crete United. "As we look ahead, we plan to have all key trades in focus markets."  Swedberg is known for their comprehensive industrial and commercial electrical services, from the pre-design phase through construction and delivery. They have formed strong alliances with property owners, architects, engineers, and other local partners to improve the energy efficiency and sustainability of the customers and communities they serve.  "We're proud of the company we have built, and we are excited to continue driving the growth and development of our business," said Jim Swedberg, President of Swedberg Electric. "What we saw in Crete United was a special opportunity to form a strategic partnership based on shared values and a common vision for Swedberg Electric."  About Crete United Crete United is a nationwide multi-service building solutions provider with a vast network of local partners who are united in improving the communities they serve and the built environments within. The company provides energy efficiency-focused building services through its partners across non-residential HVAC, electrical, plumbing, building automation, controls, and other sustainable energy solutions. Crete United provides the resources and reach of a large organization while allowing its partners to maintain the freedom to continue operating their successful businesses.  About Swedberg Electric  Swedberg Electric is a commercial electrical contractor serving Northern Illinois since 2004. Our electricians and staff are dedicated to customer satisfaction, quality and safety.
