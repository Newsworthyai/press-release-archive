# Christ Today with Father Ray" Returns in 2025 with All-New Episodes

EVANSVILLE, IN, December 25, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Father Raymond Brenner is thrilled to announce that his acclaimed podumentary series, Christ Today with Father Ray, will resume its second season in early 2025 after a two-year hiatus. The upcoming episodes will delve into Father Ray's personal journey through recent health challenges and offer new perspectives on faith, resilience, and navigating a rapidly changing world.  To welcome audiences back, Father Ray invites viewers to revisit A Look Back, the most recent episode released during Christmas 2022. This special holiday installment features highlights from earlier episodes, focusing on charity, social justice, religious liberty, and other universal themes.  "This is a perfect way to reconnect with our audience," Father Ray shares. "'A Look Back' offers a glimpse into the heart of the show and reminds us of the meaningful stories and lessons we've shared. As we step into a new chapter, I'm excited to build on that foundation."  With its return in 2025, Christ Today aims to inspire and guide viewers in these challenging times, continuing its mission of providing thought-provoking, faith-based content.  Catch A Look Back now on Father Ray's official website and prepare for an exciting new season of Christ Today with Father Ray!  For updates and information, visit Father Raymond Brenner's official website.
