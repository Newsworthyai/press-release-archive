# NetCom Learning Introduces AI Learning Roadmap to Help Professionals Stay Ahead in the Evolving Job Market

NEW YORK, NY, December 18, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning is excited to introduce the AI CERTs™ Learning Roadmap, a comprehensive, role-based training guide aimed at equipping professionals with the skills needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence (AI). The roadmap is designed for individuals seeking to build or enhance their expertise in AI, helping them stay competitive in an increasingly AI-driven job market.   As AI continues to shape industries, professionals across all sectors must adapt to its growing influence. Gartner's recent report notes that the rise of generative AI is creating new roles and driving significant changes in existing job functions. In response to this trend, NetCom Learning has created the AI CERTs™ Learning Roadmap, a series of structured learning paths that will guide professionals through the essential skills required to stay relevant and excel in their fields.   AI Learning Roadmap Overview   This AI Learning Roadmap offers a range of certifications across different proficiency levels and job roles, including:   - AI Essentials: Entry-level courses for individuals starting their AI journey. - AI Data & Robotics: Intermediate and advanced training in data science, robotics, and machine learning. - IT Professionals: Specialized programs in AI development, AI security, AI cloud, and AI architecture. - AI for Business: Tailored learning paths for professionals in marketing, project management, sales, and more, to help them integrate AI into their business functions.   It empowers professionals to develop the AI skills they need to remain at the forefront of their respective industries. Explore the complete Roadmap here.   Mastering AI Skills for 2025 and Beyond   In addition to the roadmap, NetCom Learning is offering a free webinar titled "Mastering the Future: AI Skills and Certifications to Thrive in 2025 and Beyond." The 40-minute session, set for January 16, 2025, will cover the latest AI trends, the impact of generative AI, and actionable strategies for integrating AI tools into business workflows.   Key takeaways from the webinar include:   - An overview of AI developments expected in 2025. - Insights into the growing role of generative AI across industries. - Strategies for leveraging AI tools to enhance productivity. - A guide to emerging AI certifications and their relevance in today's job market.   To register for the Mastering the Future: AI Skills and Certifications webinar, visit here.   Interactive Learning and Certification   NetCom Learning's AI CERTs™ Learning Roadmap provides virtual instructor-led training (VILT) and certification programs, designed to align with professionals' specific roles and career aspirations. The roadmap ensures that individuals receive targeted and effective training to advance their skills in AI.
