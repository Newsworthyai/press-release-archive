# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Sean Lafferty, Featured on PA Live! to Discuss Leadership and Innovation in Real Estate

LANCASTER, PA, December 18, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Sean Lafferty, owner and president of Premier Settlements Inc., appeared on PA Live! to discuss his company's role in transforming real estate services across Pennsylvania. With over 30 years of success, Premier Settlements offers expertise in home purchases, commercial real estate and land acquisitions. Mr. Lafferty shared how his leadership philosophy, rooted in teamwork and empowering employees, has driven the company's growth. He also highlighted plans to expand Premier Settlements' offerings, including real estate brokerage and renters insurance, while maintaining a commitment to core values.
