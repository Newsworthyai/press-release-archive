# Easy Dynamics Receives NIST MEIS MEP Connect Support Task Award

MCLEAN, VA, November 21, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) has awarded Easy Dynamics a task order under the GSA 8(a) STARS III contract to provide support for the Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) Enterprise Information System (MEIS). This latest win expands the scope of Easy Dynamics' existing services for NIST.   As part of this award, Easy Dynamics will offer comprehensive DevSecOps support services for NIST MEP's client facing mission apps including the MEIS & Connect platforms, critical resources for small and medium sized manufacturers across the country. Highlights of this project include continued enhancements to the MEIS system, migration to the MEIS Next platform, and continued support and enhancements to the MEP Connect collaboration portal. The goal is to support the MEP national network in empowering small manufacturers by providing access to expert insights, digital tools, SME support, and shared experiences, and to give them a level playing field against large and international firms in an increasingly complex and competitive landscape.   "We are excited to continue our relationship with NIST," Greg Gordon, Easy Dynamics Chief Delivery Officer, said. "This award will enhance the MEP MEIS platform further and enable small manufacturers to benefit from cutting-edge technology and services that drive innovation and business growth."   This expansion of services aligns with Easy Dynamics' commitment to supporting the CHIPS and Science Act, assisting in the growth and resilience of small manufacturers through strategic and innovative IT solutions. The project will play a critical role in enhancing the MEP system's operational efficiency and user experience, enabling more manufacturers to benefit from MEP 's technical resources.  About Easy Dynamics  Easy Dynamics Corporation is a leading technology services provider with a core focus in Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Information Sharing. We are builders, problem solvers, and trusted advisors who bring well-architected solutions and management consulting to our clients to align them with the best practices their missions demand. As industry leaders, we are committed to delivering unparalleled quality and service in all aspects of our organization and providing our customers with outstanding technical excellence and the business acumen to advise them on both tactical and strategic initiatives.
