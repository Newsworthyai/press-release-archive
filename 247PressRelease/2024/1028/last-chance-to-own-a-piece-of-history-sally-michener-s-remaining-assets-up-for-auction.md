# Last Chance to Own a Piece of History: Sally Michener's Remaining Assets Up for Auction!

VANCOUVER, BC, October 28, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Art lovers and treasure seekers are invited to the Sally Michener Art, Jewelry & Estate Auction, a dazzling event that honours the remarkable legacy of a pioneering artist. Hosted by Direct Auctions, powered by Direct Liquidation and led by the charismatic Jeff "The Liquidator" Schwartz, this exciting auction will take place on Saturday, November 2, 2024, at 10 AM (PDT).  Sally Michener (1935 - 2023) was an American Postwar & Contemporary artist renowned for her innovative ceramic creations and profound influence on the art community. As a longtime faculty member and Dean at Emily Carr University of Art and Design, she inspired generations of artists and left an indelible mark on Canadian art. Throughout her illustrious career, Sally exhibited in numerous esteemed galleries and museums, including the Seymour Art Gallery, showcasing her exceptional talent and commitment to her craft.  Sally's work transcended traditional boundaries, exploring the interplay of form, texture, and the human experience. Her unique ceramic pieces reflect a deep understanding of the natural world and a passionate engagement with her medium. This auction presents a rare opportunity to acquire stunning works from an artist who not only shaped contemporary ceramics but also nurtured the next wave of artistic talent.  Auction Highlights The auction features the remaining assets and estate of Sally Michener, including a curated selection of her exquisite ceramic pieces that exemplify her artistic vision. Each piece offers a glimpse into Sally's creative process, inviting collectors to experience her artistic journey firsthand.  Additionally, the auction will showcase a limited edition framed print by the legendary Salvador Dalí, titled "The Milky Way." With only 150 copies in existence, this rare print adds a touch of surrealism and sophistication to the collection, perfectly complementing Sally's work.
