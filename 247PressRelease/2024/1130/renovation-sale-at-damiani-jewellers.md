# Renovation Sale at Damiani Jewellers

WOODBRIDGE, ON, November 30, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Before Damiani Jewellers begins renovations to their Woodbridge jewellery store in mid-January 2025, they are offering incredible savings of up to 50% off select fine jewellery, watches, and luxury gift merchandise in-stock in their showroom from December 2, 2024 to January 11, 2025.   Help them make room for new additions while saving on gorgeous pieces of fine jewellery or timepieces that may be on sale. Get someone on your shopping list something beautiful for the holidays while maintaining your budget with Damiani Jewellers' Renovation Sale.   Explore the great prices during this Renovation Sale on select gold and diamond jewellery, timepieces, and luxury gifts, like Montblanc pens and leather goods, William Henry pocket knives, or watch winders at their Woodbridge showroom December 2, 2024 until January 11, 2025.   As a source of fine jewellery, timepieces, and luxury accessories in the Greater Toronto Area for over 60 years, Damiani Jewellers continues to grow and aspire to the best their jewellery store can achieve. Contact or visit their Woodbridge showroom for more details about the Renovation Sale, their selection, or their professional jeweller services.  About Damiani Jewellers With over 60 years of experience serving clients with their fine jewellery expertise, Damiani Jewellers is the Greater Toronto area's premier jewellery retailer. For three generations, their family-owned business has provided engagement rings, wedding bands, loose diamonds, fine jewellery items, and luxury watches for their customers' most treasured moments. Damiani Jewellers is also proud to be an authorized dealer of authentic Rolex watches, and this accomplishment speaks to the unbeatable customer service that each shopper receives. To learn more about the products and services offered at their showroom in Woodbridge, Ontario visit their website, call (905) 850-4653, email info@damianijewellers.com, or stop by their store in person.
