# Marco Bicego Makes a Special Appearance at Adlers Jewelers

WESTFIELD, NJ, April 30, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Adlers Jewelers is thrilled to announce an exclusive personal appearance by the globally celebrated jewelry designer, Marco Bicego. Set to take place on May 10, 2024, this event will provide attendees with a unique opportunity to witness the craftsmanship and beauty of these creations along with meeting the man behind the brand.   Guests can expect to explore a stunning array of one-of-a-kind pieces, new elevated designs, and the latest collections curated by Marco himself. Alongside the showcase, Marco will share insights into his design process, offer personalized styling advice, and personally sign each item purchased during the event.  Customers are cordially invited to join Adlers Jewelers for an evening of elegance and sophistication as they celebrate the timeless artistry of Marco Bicego's jewelry designs. Attendees will have the chance to indulge in Marco's favorite wine and enjoy light treats while immersing themselves in the world of luxury jewelry.  The event will be held at Adlers Jewelers, located at 217 North Ave W, Westfield, New Jersey from 3-6pm, followed by an invitation-only session from 6-8pm. Space is limited, so interested individuals are encouraged to RSVP at their earliest convenience by contacting sales@adlersjewelers.com or visiting the website for appointment sign-up.  About Adlers Jewelers With over 100 years of experience and customer service excellence in Westfield, New Jersey, Adlers Jewelers is still the premier jeweler in the area, represented by current owners, brothers Andrew and Jeffrey Arkin, both certified gemologists. Adlers proudly carries the top bridal and fashion jewelry brands and offers repair services for jewelry and watches. The store is fully equipped and available to custom design any item. Most of all, everyone at Adlers is trained to follow the thoughtful tradition of Mama Adler always offering coffee when Maurice Adler first sold jewelry from their parlor. Come by to experience the same level of friendliness and caring. For more information on their products and services, call (908) 233-6900 or email their staff at sales@adlersjewelers.com.
