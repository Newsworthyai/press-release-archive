# Cisco Awarded NetCom Learning's US Trainer, Richard Govinda, with Cisco 2024 Distinguished Instructor Award

NEW YORK, NY, May 28, 2024 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a globally renowned provider of IT and business training solutions, proudly announced that Richard Govinda, its esteemed Core Trainer in the US, has been awarded the prestigious Cisco 2024 Distinguished Instructor Award.   The 2024 Distinguished Instructor award recognizes influential Cisco Certified Systems Instructors (CCSI's) who provide the highest quality training experience and demonstrate the best overall instructor performance across multiple Cisco technologies, as measured by the customer satisfaction survey throughout year 2023.   Richard Govinda is an experienced IT Trainer with over 10 years of expertise in teaching Cisco, CompTIA, and Microsoft courses. He is highly skilled in various technologies, including Cisco Routing, Switching, Security, and Wireless domains. His passion for teaching and the IT industry is evident in every classroom experience, whether instructing veterans or computer engineers.   NetCom Learning provides Cisco authorized training solutions to business and government agencies. Being a Cisco Platinum Learning Partner, NetCom Learning offers top-tier Cisco training solutions aimed at empowering organizations to excel in a hybrid, multi-cloud, and application-centric landscape. With a team of Cisco Certified Systems Instructors (CCSIs), authorized courseware and labs, and a versatile blend of learning methods, NetCom learning provides an exceptional training experience. Additionally, they proudly accept Cisco Learning Credits, allowing individuals to leverage them for complimentary access to various training programs.   To learn more about Cisco training programs, visit NetCom Learning's website.  About NetCom Learning NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations in the workplace by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth. With more than 23 years of experience, NetCom Learning has been empowering innovative learning organizations to adapt and drive growth in this fast-paced world by closing critical skill gaps and ensuring smooth deployment, implementation, and consumption through authorized training delivered by Certified Trainers on vendors such as Microsoft, AWS, EC-Council, CompTIA, and more.
