# Grace's Hair & Beyond Now Accepting Appointments at Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, February 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Cosmetologist Grace Vargas proudly announces the opening of her new salon, Gracie's Hair & Beyond, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard in Fort Worth, Texas.  Vargas has had a fascination with doing hair since she was young, and quickly became captivated by the beauty industry.  "My mom was in beauty school and I was enchanted as I watched her and my sister create stunning hairstyles and flawless makeup looks," Vargas said. "It was like being in a magical world of color and creativity, and I couldn't get enough of it," she added.  Vargas said her journey started as a little girl who recognized the opportunity to help those struggling with their hair and makeup.  "I always offered my services selflessly, fueled by my love for this craft," she said. "My goal has always been to uplift those in need."  That's why it was only natural for Vargas to pursue a full-time career in the beauty industry. She knew that with the right education and training, she'd be able to polish her skills. Vargas did just that, attending the Ogle Beauty School of Hair, Skin and Nails, where she immersed herself in the art of beauty and wellness. And now, driven by a deep passion for the beauty industry, Vargas said her story embodies that spirit.   "I wholeheartedly believe everyone deserves to look and feel beautiful," Vargas said. Vargas provides a wide variety of professional services, ranging from special event hairstyles to color and perms.   "I offer precision haircuts tailored to your individual style, ensuring a flattering and manageable look," Vargas said. "From elegant updos for special occasions to casual styles for everyday wear, I specialize in creating looks that suit your personality and event." Vargas also provides special event hair, makeup and nails for weddings or proms, helping clients achieve stunning looks to complement their outfits and enhance their natural beauty.  At Gracie's Hair & Beyond a variety of color services are available, from subtle lowlights to vivid colors. You can keep your color looking fresh with root touch-up services, which seamlessly blend your natural color with your existing dye. If you're looking to enhance your natural hair color, Vargas offers expertly applied highlights or lowlights, giving you a subtle glow or bold contrasts.  "Partial highlights can add dimension without a full color change," Vargas said. They're perfect for creating a sun-kissed look," she added.  To celebrate the grand opening of Gracie's Hair & Beyond at its new location, Vargas is offering several specials: $20 off when you spend $200, $15 off when you spend $150, and $10 off when you spend $100.   Gracie's Hair & Beyond is accepting clients from 9 a.m.-5 p.m. Appointments are recommended, but Vargas will accept walk-ins if possible. To schedule an appointment, visit Vargas's booking site. Learn more about Gracie's Hair & Beyond by visiting Vargas's Instagram page or her Facebook page; you can also text or call her for more information at (832) 716-8135.  Conveniently situated near several major roadways, Gracie's Hair & Beyond in the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard is an easy drive from any direction.
