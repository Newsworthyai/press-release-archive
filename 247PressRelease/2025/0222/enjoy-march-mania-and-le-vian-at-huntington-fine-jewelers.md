# Enjoy March Mania and Le Vian at Huntington Fine Jewelers

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, February 22, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Celebrate the annual March Mania Sale at Huntington Fine Jewelers with certain items up to 50% off at all of their Oklahoma jewelry store locations. Thursday, March 6th through Saturday, March 8th, find incredible prices on beautiful fine jewelry in the showrooms.   Explore stunning jewelry like diamond necklaces and earrings during March Mania and feel the savings as you add stylish new jewelry to your collection. Enter to win a shopping spree on Huntington, learn more details from the retailer when you visit. Financing is available at all of our Huntington Fine Jewelers locations to make your jewelry dreams a reality without affecting your budget.   Throughout March, Le Vian will be holding Pop-Up Trunk Show Events at Huntington Fine Jewelers' Oklahoma jewelry stores for, mostly, one day sales and specials in each location; March 4th in Norman, March 5th in Yukon, March 6th in Midwest City, March 10th in Stillwater, March 11th in Shawnee, and then March 12th and 13th in Oklahoma City. Revel in the vibrant and well-crafted gemstone jewelry from Le Vian as you pick out your next piece of luxury at an amazing price. Save 20% when you RSVP to the Le Vian Pop-Up Trunk Show near you.   Over 35 years of experience guides Huntington Fine Jewelers, and they are honored to continue to serve the communities that have become their homes. Contact any of their Oklahoma jewelry stores to learn more details about the March events, their fine jewelry selection, and professional jeweler services.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers   For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services. For more information visit their website at www.huntingtonfinejewelers.com, send an email to info@huntingtonfinejewelers.com or visit their showrooms in person.
