# Marquis Who's Who Listee Holly Kleiman Featured on Innovator's Journey for Wellness Vision

CHICAGO, IL, June 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Marquis Who's Who listee Holly Kleiman appeared on Innovator's Journey to discuss Sapphire + Salt, her wellness spa rooted in holistic healing and longevity. Based in Southwest Michigan, the center uses advanced therapies to detoxify, reduce inflammation and restore cellular balance. Ms. Kleiman draws on 26 years in health care consulting to challenge the U.S. health care system and promote natural, preventive wellness. She urges aspiring entrepreneurs to embrace purpose over fear and announced plans to expand Sapphire + Salt nationally, starting with a second location in Chicago.  Watch the segment here.
