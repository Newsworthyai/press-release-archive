# Fortis Military Defense: Advocate for the Protectors of Our Nation

ATLANTA, GA, December 30, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Fortis Military Defense, LLC stands as a trusted defender for servicemembers across all branches of the U.S. Armed Forces. Founded by former Army JAG Corps attorney Sean C. H. Flood, the firm is dedicated exclusively to military law, representing Soldiers, Marines, Sailors, Airmen, and Guardians facing investigations, administrative hearings, military criminal charges, and other legal challenges that can impact their careers. Fortis Military Defense Defense also assists Veterans in applying to Boards for the Correction of Military Records and Discharge Review Boards.  "At Fortis Military Defense, the firm understands what's at stake," said founder Sean Flood, Esq. "When a servicemember's honor, career, or freedom is on the line, they deserve a defender who knows the system inside and out and will fight with integrity and precision to protect them."  Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, and representing clients globally, Fortis Military Defense offers deep experience in the complex world of military justice. Attorney Flood served nearly a decade in the U.S. Army JAG Corps, including deployments to Afghanistan and Syria, where he advised commanders on the Law of Armed Conflict and Rules of Engagement. His service in the Army also gave him extensive experience in courts-martial, separations and separations board, and other administrative practice to include advising on and rebutting military investigations. His combat-zone, courtroom, and board room tested judgment and legal acumen make him uniquely qualified to handle cases other attorneys often turn down. Prior to starting Fortis Military Defense, Attorney Flood worked as a civilian military attorney for over five years handling courts-martial, boards, administrative rebuttals and appeals, and petitions to review boards.  Fortis Military Defense provides skilled representation in a full range of military matters, including:  Military Law Enforcement Investigations: Protecting your rights and defending against investigations that can threaten your freedom and career.  Sexual Harassment & EO Cases: Representation during the investigation and in drafting responses and appeals to adverse findings.  Administrative Investigations: Strategic advocacy and responses to non-law enforcement investigations.  Separation Boards: Representation to protect careers against involuntary discharge.  Boards for Correction of Military Records: Build a case for the removal of adverse information in your military records for errors and injustice.  Article 15 & Non-Judicial Punishment: Challenging unjust allegations and preserving servicemember's records.  Discharge Review Boards: Prepare matters for upgrading unjust discharges for Veteran benefits and potential reenlistment.  Administrative Hearings: Comprehensive representation in military administrative proceedings.  With over 15 years of military law experience and multiple commendations—including the Bronze Star Medal (Meritorious), Meritorious Service Medal, and three Army Commendation Medals—Sean Flood brings both combat-zone-tested leadership and courtroom expertise to every case.  Whether a client is stationed stateside or overseas, Fortis Military Defense provides confidential consultations and steadfast representation from start to finish. The firm's approach is guided by one unwavering principle: do the right thing and fight with everything you've got.  About Fortis Military Defense, LLC Fortis Military Defense, LLC is a military defense law firm based in Atlanta, Georgia, representing servicemembers worldwide. Founded by Sean C. H. Flood, Esq., a former Army JAG Corps attorney, the firm focuses exclusively on military law, including investigations, separation boards, Article 15s, and administrative hearings. With a decade of military service and global case experience, Fortis Military Defense is dedicated to protecting the careers, reputations, and futures of those who serve.
