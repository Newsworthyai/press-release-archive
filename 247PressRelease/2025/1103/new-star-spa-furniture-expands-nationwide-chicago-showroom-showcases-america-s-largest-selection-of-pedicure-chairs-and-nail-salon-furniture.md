# New Star Spa &amp; Furniture Expands Nationwide: Chicago Showroom Showcases America's Largest Selection of Pedicure Chairs and Nail Salon Furniture

CHICAGO, IL, November 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- New Star Spa & Furniture Corp., America's trusted manufacturer of luxury pedicure chairs and custom nail salon furniture, continues to raise the bar for quality, innovation, and customer experience in the professional beauty industry.  With more than 12 years of excellence and six expansive showrooms nationwide—in Illinois, Pennsylvania, New York, Georgia, Virginia, and Texas—New Star has become known as the "IKEA of the nail industry." The company offers a complete one-stop solution for salon owners seeking stylish, durable, and functional furniture that enhances both client comfort and salon efficiency.  At the heart of New Star's success is its commitment to American craftsmanship and customer-first service. Every pedicure chair and furniture piece is assembled in the U.S.A. and ETL-certified, meeting the highest standards of safety, performance, and design.  A Destination for Salon Professionals Nationwide  The company's Chicago showroom, located at 4928 N Broadway St, Chicago, IL 60640, is one of the largest nail salon furniture destinations in the Midwest. With over 50 premium pedicure chair models, 10 distinct furniture collections, and 60 technician and client seating options, the showroom gives beauty professionals the ability to compare, customize, and visualize their ideal salon setup before purchase.  Whether salon owners are furnishing a brand-new business or upgrading an existing space, New Star's expert team offers personalized consultation, custom 3D design renderings, and flexible financing options to help clients make confident, informed decisions.  Comprehensive Product Line  New Star's product lineup is designed to balance luxury, functionality, and durability, including:  Pedicure Spa Chairs: Available in the Supreme, Royal, Luxury, Deluxe, Premium, and Essential collections — featuring state-of-the-art massage systems and ergonomic design.  Custom Salon Furniture: Stylish manicure tables, reception desks, display cabinets, and seating options, crafted to complement modern salon aesthetics.  3D Design Services: Advanced 3D modeling allows salon owners to preview their full layout and furniture selections before installation.  Financing Programs: Making premium-quality furniture attainable for both new and established salon owners.  Commitment to Quality & Customer Care  "We don't compete on price — we compete on quality," said Tinh Nguyen, spokesperson for New Star Spa & Furniture Corp. "Every detail, from the stitching of our chairs to the finish of our furniture, reflects our promise to deliver lasting beauty, comfort, and confidence to every salon we serve."  Every showroom is staffed by a team of experienced professionals who guide clients from design consultation through delivery and installation. The company also provides after-sales support, maintenance resources, and educational materials to help salon owners extend the life and performance of their furniture.  New Star's dedication to innovation and service has made it a top choice for nail salon owners, spa designers, and entrepreneurs across the United States. Its products combine modern design, technical precision, and affordability, ensuring each client receives a tailor-made solution that fits both budget and brand identity.
