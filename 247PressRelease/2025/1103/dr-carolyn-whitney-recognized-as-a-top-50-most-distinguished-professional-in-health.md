# Dr. Carolyn Whitney Recognized as a Top 50 Most Distinguished Professional in Health

MEMPHIS, TN, November 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- With an unwavering passion for transforming lives and a relentless dedication to delivering exceptional care, Dr. Carolyn Whitney, a cherished member of the Preferred Professionals Network, has been honored as one of Preferred Health Magazine's TOP 50 Most Distinguished Professionals in Health, shining brightly in the field of Pediatrics. Her commitment to nurturing young patients and inspiring hope in families exemplifies the heart of healthcare excellence.  This is Dr. Whitney's second time earning this national distinction, solidifying her reputation as one of the most trusted and accomplished pediatricians in the country. She currently treats patients at Regional One Health in Memphis, Tennessee, where she has served for over 30 years.  Dr. Whitney's impressive career is marked by numerous honors, including the "Empowered Women in Medicine" recognition (2023), a previous Top 50 Most Distinguished Professionals in Medicine award (2022), and the "Exemplary Award" in Pediatrics from the Preferred Professionals Network (2021). She was also featured in the Best in Medicine Edition of Preferred Health Magazine (2019).  "I have always loved children," says Dr. Whitney. "My mother was the neighborhood babysitter, and I would help her out constantly—so really, I've had 50 years of experience helping kids grow and prosper."  Expert Pediatric Care, Rooted in Compassion  Trained to meet the unique needs of children at every stage of development, Dr. Whitney provides a full spectrum of pediatric services—from routine wellness visits and immunizations to advanced treatments for ADD, Autism, Pneumonia, and Hernias. Her patient-first philosophy sets her apart in today's healthcare environment.  "I'm still old-fashioned," she explains. "I believe we should always focus on the patient and how we care for them—not the numbers."  A Lifelong Commitment to Excellence  A graduate of Meharry Medical College in Nashville, Tennessee (1984), Dr. Whitney completed her post-graduate training at Wayne State University School of Medicine in Detroit, Michigan. She is Board Certified in Pediatrics by the American Board of Pediatrics, an active member of the Tennessee Medical Association, the American Academy of Pediatrics, and the Bluff City Medical Society.  Beyond her clinical work, Dr. Whitney serves as an Instructor of Nursing at the Primary/Public Health Department at the University of Tennessee Health Science Center, sharing her expertise with the next generation of healthcare professionals.  For more information about Top Industry Expert in Pediatrics, Dr. Carolyn Whitney, please visit: www.CarolynWhitneyMD.com and check out her TOP 50 Most Distinguished Professionals in Medicine profile at www.PreferredHealthMagazine.com/TOP50.  Preferred Health Magazine is a premier publication dedicated to empowering readers with the latest insights, trends, and innovations in health and wellness. Committed to delivering evidence-based information, the magazine covers a wide range of topics, including physical and mental well-being, nutrition, fitness, medical advancements, and holistic care. With contributions from leading healthcare professionals, researchers, and wellness experts, Preferred Health Magazine serves as a trusted resource for television and broadcast news sources, and individuals seeking to make informed decisions about their health. Available in print and digital formats, the magazine inspires readers to lead healthier, more balanced lives through engaging content and actionable advice.
