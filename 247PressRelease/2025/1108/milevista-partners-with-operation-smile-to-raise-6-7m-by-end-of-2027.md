# Milevista Partners with Operation Smile to Raise $6.7M by End of 2027

CULVER CITY, CA, November 08, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Milevista, the Culver City–based experiential marketing agency, today announced a new partnership with global medical nonprofit Operation Smile to support surgical care, comprehensive cleft treatment and family-focused programs — with an ambitious fundraising goal of $6.7 million by December 31, 2027. Milevista+1  Milevista will deploy its team-based, high-impact in-person activations, live pop-ups to increase awareness, raise funds in markets across the U.S. These efforts will include a series of live events and experiential activations designed to educate communities about the work that we do and generate sustained donor support.  "As an agency built around real human connections, we believe powerful experiences can do more than build brands — they can change lives," said Milevista's leadership. "Partnering with Operation Smile lets us bring our on-the-ground expertise to bear on a deeply meaningful mission: helping children and families access life-changing surgery and care. Our goal is ambitious, but with the right partners and persistent community engagement, it's entirely achievable." Milevista  Operation Smile — an international nonprofit with decades of experience delivering cleft lip and palate surgeries and long-term comprehensive care — will work closely with Milevista to raise funds to support surgical programs.   A short film documenting the early stages of this initiative and patient impact is available here: 100 Kids Smile For The First Time. youtube.com  Why this partnership matters Operation Smile's model pairs safe surgery with multidisciplinary care and community education; Milevista brings measurable, in-person reach and real-time campaign optimization. Together, the organizations aim to accelerate donor response and increase the number of children who receive corrective surgery and follow-up services over the next two years. Operation Smile+1  About Milevista Milevista is a Culver City–based experiential marketing and field activation agency that creates immersive, results-driven campaigns for brands, nonprofit partners, and purpose-driven organizations. The company specializes in pop-ups, brand ambassador programs and live activations that convert attention into measurable outcomes.  About Operation Smile Operation Smile is an international nonprofit dedicated to increasing access to safe surgery and comprehensive cleft care for children worldwide. For more than four decades the organization has partnered with hospitals, medical volunteers, and communities to deliver surgery, treatment and long-term support to patients born with cleft lip and/or palate.
