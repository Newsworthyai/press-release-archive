# Cymatics Digital Announces Renowned Producer Yang Qian to Lead Distinguished Short Film My Life in LA

LOS ANGELES, CA, November 08, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Cymatics Digital LLC proudly announces that acclaimed producer Yang Qian has officially joined as Lead Producer for My Life in LA, the company's highly anticipated short film project. This production marks the sixth installment in Cymatics Digital's distinguished slate of projects and underscores the company's commitment to collaborating with world-class talent in the film industry.  My Life in LA is an ambitious short film (15â€“20 minutes) within the coming-of-age genre, scheduled to begin filming on September 21, 2027, with completion targeted for April 15, 2028. The film explores the world of Charlie, an 11-year-old boy in Los Angeles navigating neglect, childhood fears, and resilience amidst emotional and environmental instability. The narrative highlights universal themes of survival and belonging, positioning the project for broad appeal within the international festival circuit.  Yang Qian's Leading Role As Lead Producer, Yang Qian holds primary creative, managerial, and financial authority over the production. Her role is central not only to the development and execution of the film but also to its artistic identity and ultimate success. Responsibilities include:  - Overseeing script development and ensuring compelling storytelling aligned with international standards.  - Assembling and managing the film's celebrated roster of collaborators, including award-winning cinematographer Siwei Li, production designer Yun Gu, and ACE Eddie Award-winning editor Guanqing Lin.  - Supervising production management, ensuring compliance with child performer protections and rigorous safety protocols for earthquake simulation scenes.  - Leading all financing, sponsorship, and grant negotiations to secure the production's structure and viability.  - Directing post-production workflow, from editorial to sound and color, with a focus on elevating the narrative's emotional authenticity.  - Creating the festival submission and global distribution strategy to secure recognition for the film at distinguished international showcases.  Yang Qian's leadership ensures that My Life in LA not only reaches completion but also achieves the artistic and professional benchmarks necessary to establish it as a distinguished production positioned for international recognition.  Distinguished Creative Team The production brings together a notable team of globally-recognized collaborators:  - Director: Florian "Yifeng" Ling (IMDb), known for his acclaimed short Pay It, who remarked: "This story is close to my heart, and having Yang Qian as the lead producer gives me confidence that the film will reach its full potential. Their vision and leadership are essential to realizing My Life in LA."  - Cinematographer: Siwei Li (IMDb), recipient of AbelCine's Fresh Perspectives in Cinematography Grant.  - Production Designer: Yun Gu (IMDb), an emerging designer celebrated for cross-disciplinary creative work.  - Editor: Guanqing Lin (IMDb), a recognized talent at A24 and a recipient of the American Cinema Editors Eddie Award.  By uniting this distinguished creative team under Yang Qian's leadership, the project reflects industry recognition and prestige, affirming My Life in LA as a professional and artistic benchmark in short-form cinema.  About Cymatics Digial Cymatics Digital LLC is a premier media and production company committed to the creation of distinguished, impactful, and globally resonant visual stories. With an emphasis on originality and cross-cultural narratives, Cymatics Digital continues to develop projects that stand out for artistic merit and professional excellence.
