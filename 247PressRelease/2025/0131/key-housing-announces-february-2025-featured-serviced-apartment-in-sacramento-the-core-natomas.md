# Key Housing Announces February 2025 Featured Serviced Apartment in Sacramento: The Core Natomas

SACRAMENTO, CA, January 31, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Key Housing, a leader in corporate housing solutions across California, is excited to announce "The Core Natomas" as its featured serviced apartment property for February 2025. Located in Sacramento at 2745 Orchard Lane, this premier property offers business travelers from Europe and the UK a luxurious and comfortable "home away from home" during their visit to the state capital. It features short-term rentals and serviced apartments, making it easy for someone to arrive from Europe and instantly find a "home away from home" that is ready for occupancy.  "The Core Natomas is the perfect choice for corporate travelers looking for serviced apartments that combine luxury, practicality, and comfort," said Bob Lee, President of Key Housing. "Its proximity to downtown Sacramento and top-tier amenities make it an unparalleled option for European and British visitors, especially those seeking a more homelike experience while conducting business in Northern California."  Interested persons can learn more at https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-rental-properties/the-core-natomas/ or visit the Sacramento landing page at https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-housing-city/sacramento/. In all cases, they are encouraged to reach out for a free consultation with Key Housing on their rental needs. The company specializes in finding hard-to-find short term rentals throughout California, including the popular state capital.  Here is background on this announcement. For European and British travelers seeking to escape winter's chill, Sacramento's mild early spring climate provides the perfect backdrop for mixing business and pleasure. Sacramento's strategic location also provides easy access to both Northern California's urban centers and its picturesque landscapes. Whether attending meetings downtown or venturing into nearby wine country, visitors will find the convenience and charm of this vibrant city hard to resist.   THE CORE NATOMAS: WHERE COMFORT MEETS CONVENIENCE  Nestled in the desirable Natomas neighborhood (https://www.visitsacramento.com/plan/neighborhoods/natomas/), The Core Natomas redefines short-term living with its thoughtfully designed serviced apartments. This property offers one-, two-, and three-bedroom options, each featuring luxurious vinyl plank flooring, soft-close cabinetry, quartz countertops, and gourmet kitchens with stainless steel appliances. In-unit washers and dryers, spacious closets, and private patios or balconies ensure maximum convenience for guests staying for extended periods.  Designed with corporate travelers in mind, the complex boasts a range of community amenities that elevate the living experience. Guests can stay productive with access to conference rooms and a Wi-Fi-enabled clubhouse while enjoying state-of-the-art fitness facilities, a resort-style pool with private cabanas, and outdoor BBQ and kitchen areas for relaxing evenings. From a WELLBEATS virtual fitness studio to library lounges and media rooms, the property offers everything needed for a productive yet enjoyable stay.
