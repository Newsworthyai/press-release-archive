# Woodbridge Pacific Group Delivers High Value New Home Opportunities At University Park in Palm Desert

PALM DESERT, CA, January 31, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- For home shoppers considering the California desert, Nova at University Park is a highly desirable choice for diverse buyers. "We love living here," said empty nester Rose Arguin, who moved from San Diego with her husband Dan. "We love the house, the area and our neighbors, and we will never move."  Attainable—University Park homes by Woodbridge Pacific Group (WPG) feature distinctive architecture with enriched detailing, upscale interiors and builder-included extras. Esprit and Nova offer move-in ready homes, and Nova features casita and ADU designs.   Nova—Prices are from the low $700,000s. Plan sizes range from 2,328 to 2,751 square feet and provide 4 to 5 bedrooms and 3.5 to 4.5 bathrooms. All homes include a 2-car garage. Two new casita designs highlight the model tour and increase choices for independent living capacity.   Casita and ADU Designs—Empty nesters, first-time buyers, seniors and multi-gen households appreciate Nova designs. Ideal as a permanent residence, these homes also offer the lock-and-leave ease of a winter home.  "Some homeowners want space for their parents to make an extended visit to Palm Desert in the winter," said Todd Cunningham, WPG President. "Some buyers want extra income to help with the mortgage, and the potential for higher resale value is attractive to all."  Self-contained layouts—Nova's casita and ADU layouts offer sleeping and living space, a bathroom and wardrobe, washer/dryer space and a compact kitchenette. A separate entry further privatizes these layouts, optimizing their income potential or as independent space for parents, live-in help or long-term guests.  Great room layouts, through-plan views, and private side courts deliver indoor/outdoor connectivity in every Nova home. Sleek kitchens are ideal for entertaining and everyday pleasure. Secluded primary suites with spa baths and walk-in wardrobes strengthen luxurious design quality.   Esprit—this elegant line of new WPG homes also offers varied choices. Priced from $848,190 to $1,155,000, Esprit's move-in ready homes provide 1,992 to 2,925 square feet, with 3 to 5 bedrooms and 2 to 3.5 baths. Spacious great rooms and island kitchens connect easily with outdoor lounges and courtyards, and luxurious owners' suites feature large, walk-in wardrobes and compartmented spa baths. Esprit shoppers will also discover such additional per plan delights as portico entry, alcove dining and overlook views from stairs.   Samsung appliances are included with every Esprit move-in ready home. Among featured upgrades in model homes are upgraded countertops, backsplashes, flooring, lighting, landscaping and window coverings.  Verve—this popular collection has just 2 homes available for sale. Both are model homes, priced from $809,900, and with 2,318 square feet of living space. The two-story plans include 2-car garages at rear, which open directly to kitchens; one plan has a handy drop zone. Hobby/flex space highlights one plan, and both homes offer a host of included upgrades.   Residents of all three WPG neighborhoods can enjoy their new home in a well-situated Palm Desert locale. University Park is near the 10 Freeway, with the intersection of Gerald Ford Drive and Portola Road offering easy access to WPG's neighborhoods.   Key Destinations—Eisenhower Health draws buyers who are employed here, and residents seeking ready access to renowned medical services. See www.eisenhowerhealth.org for more on the many out-patient and hospital services in Palm Desert, Palm Springs, North Palm Springs, Indio and other locales.   Strong schools—For family buyers, area schools are another welcome plus. The Desert Sands Unified School District and Palm Springs Unified serve University Park with quality schools for K-12.   Shopping and Leisure—Abundant retail and dining choices are convenient, and so are hiking trails and other recreational options. Signature destinations include El Paseo Shopping District and the new Acrisure Arena, which hosts sports and entertainment events.   Close To Home—Leisure fun is close at hand at The Grove, the master plan's amenity center. The Grove features a resort pool, a lap pool and a kiddie pool, plus gathering rooms, kitchen and an indoor fitness center. A shaded playground, pickleball court, a stage area, lawn areas for events and games, a firepit and a BBQ area add more enticing venues.   Residents of University Park will also find leisure time choices at nearby parks. These include a dog park and a park with covered play area, basketball court and lawn.
