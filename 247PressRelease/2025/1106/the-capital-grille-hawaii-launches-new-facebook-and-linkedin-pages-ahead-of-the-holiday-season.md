# The Capital Grille Hawaii Launches New Facebook and LinkedIn Pages Ahead of the Holiday Season

HONOLULU, HI, November 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Capital Grille Hawaii announces the launch of its official Facebook and LinkedIn pages, inviting guests, business leaders, and travelers to connect and discover all that the restaurant has to offer. These new platforms provide an inside look at The Capital Grille's renowned fine dining experience — from culinary events and featured wines to private dining and catering opportunities for the holiday season.  Located in the heart of Waikīkī, The Capital Grille Hawaii is celebrated for its dry-aged steaks, fresh seafood, and gracious service. Each dish is prepared with meticulous care — from hand-cut, 18- to 24-day dry-aged steaks to freshly prepared sides and handcrafted desserts — offering guests a refined dining experience that blends timeless luxury with island warmth — complete with complimentary valet parking for all who dine with us.  As the holidays approach, The Capital Grille Hawaii invites guests to celebrate Thanksgiving and Christmas Day in style. Whether dining with ʻohana or visiting from afar, guests can enjoy a festive meal surrounded by elegant ambiance, world-class wines, and the spirit of aloha.  Reservations are strongly encouraged as seating is limited.  The Capital Grille's award-winning wine list features more than 225 selections curated by expert sommeliers — from classic Bordeaux and Burgundy to global standouts — ensuring every pairing complements the season perfectly.  "Our goal has always been to create an environment where every guest feels truly valued and every moment feels exceptional. You no longer have to go to New York City or Washington DC to enjoy your favorite Capital Grille — now you have one here locally," said Jasmine Peterson, General Manager of The Capital Grille Hawaii. "Launching our Facebook and LinkedIn pages allows us to connect more deeply with Hawaii's vibrant community and visitors alike, sharing our passion for hospitality and celebration."  Follow The Capital Grille Hawaii on Facebook and LinkedIn for exclusive updates on events, featured menu items, and the latest holiday dining experiences.
