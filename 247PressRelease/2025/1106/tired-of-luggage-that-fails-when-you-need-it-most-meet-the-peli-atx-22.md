Built for those who move boldly, it brings protection and elegance together in one uncompromising design.  From Proven Protection to Premium Travel For over four decades, Peli has safeguarded what matters most, from field missions to film sets. When other luggage gives in at the first impact, the ATX 22 endures, protecting what you value most from start to destination.  Purposeful Design, Effortless Style Durability can look refined.
