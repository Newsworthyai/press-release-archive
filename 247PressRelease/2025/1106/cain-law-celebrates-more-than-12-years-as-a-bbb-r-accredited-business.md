# Cain Law Celebrates More Than 12 Years as a BBB® Accredited Business

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, November 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Cain Law is proud to celebrate more than 12 years as an Accredited Business with the Better Business Bureau® Serving Central Oklahoma (BBB®). This milestone highlights the firm's ongoing commitment to ethical practices, transparency, and client-centered representation across Oklahoma.  Since first earning BBB Accreditation, Cain Law has consistently upheld the organization's high standards of trust, integrity, and accountability in the legal profession. The recognition underscores Cain Law's dedication to serving clients with honesty and professionalism in every case, as Monty Cain, owner and managing attorney at Cain Law, said the following:  "Our firm's mission has always been to do what's right for our clients and our community. Maintaining our BBB Accreditation for more than a decade reflects the values that guide every decision we make."  The Better Business Bureau Serving Central Oklahoma, led by President & CEO Kitt Letcher, commended Cain Law for its leadership and ethical commitment to consumers.  About Cain Law  Cain Law is a personal injury law firm based in Oklahoma City, with offices in Edmond and Tulsa. The firm represents clients in cases involving traumatic brain injuries, trucking accidents, car accidents, and product liability. Cain Law has recovered more than $250 million in verdicts and settlements for clients across Oklahoma and Tulsa.  The firm is recognized for its role in shaping trucking litigation in Oklahoma and Tulsa, including appellate victories that expanded negligent-entrustment claims against unsafe commercial carriers. Cain Law remains committed to providing client-focused service while advancing legal strategies in complex personal injury cases.  For more information visit https://cainlaw-okc.com
