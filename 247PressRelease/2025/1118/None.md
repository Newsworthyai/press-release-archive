# Japan-America Society of Dallas/Fort Worth Announces Exclusive Perks for the 2026 Journey Japan Luxury Cruise

DALLAS-FORT WORTH, TX, November 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Japan-America Society of Dallas/Fort Worth (JASDFW) is pleased to announce a special partnership with Japanet America offering travelers an extraordinary opportunity to explore Japan through the 2026 Journey Japan Luxury Cruise — complete with exclusive benefits for those who book through JASDFW.  The 15-day cruise features two thoughtfully curated itinerary options: the All Japan Tour and the Western Japan Tour, each designed to blend iconic highlights with lesser- traveled regions for a deeply enriching experience. Both journeys include 10 nights aboard a luxury vessel, visits to multiple ports across Japan (with one stop in South Korea), and pre- and post-cruise hotel stays in Yokohama, Kamakura, Tokyo, and Hakone.  As part of this partnership, JASDFW is offering the following exclusive perks:  JASDFW Exclusive Traveler Benefits  5% Discount Code: JASDFW2026 Complimentary "Japanese for Travelers" Workshop (four sessions, USD $120 value) One-year JASDFW membership included with each booking  "We are excited to provide our community with a unique and immersive way to experience Japan," said Lawrence P. Howorth, Chair of the Board of Directors, Japan- America Society of Dallas/Fort Worth. "This cruise offers a perfect balance of comfort, cultural enrichment, and unforgettable exploration — and our exclusive perks add even more value to the journey."  The cruise itineraries showcase destinations ranging from major cities such as Tokyo and Yokohama to quieter, culturally rich regions including Sado Island, Amami Oshima, Matsuyama, and Hakodate. With limited passenger capacity, curated shore excursions, upscale dining, and 24-hour room service, travelers can explore Japan at a relaxed pace while enjoying world-class amenities.  The trip's combination of iconic landmarks and lesser-known locations provides a unique opportunity for travelers seeking a deep, meaningful experience of Japan beyond the typical fast-paced tours.  More information about the cruise and detailed itineraries can be found at the Japanet America website and in the official cruise brochure.  About the Japan-America Society of Dallas/Fort Worth The Japan-America Society of Dallas/Fort Worth (JASDFW) connects North Texas and Japan by providing opportunities for friendship, understanding, and meaningful exchange. Through cultural, business, and educational programming, JASDFW strengthens the partnership between Japan and Texas while cultivating mutual respect and appreciation. Learn more at www.jasdfw.org.
