# Marquis Who's Who Listee Don Herner Featured on FOX 5 San Diego to Discuss Disrupting the Auto Industry

SAN DIEGO, CA, April 15, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Don Herner, a renowned automotive designer and Marquis Who's Who listee, appeared on FOX 5 San Diego's The Localist SD to share his vision for the future of car design. With over 30 years in the industry, Mr. Herner is now launching a model car company that brings wildly imaginative, boundary-pushing concepts to collectors and enthusiasts. Drawing from his work on iconic vehicles like the Acura Integra and Honda Ridgeline, Mr. Herner aims to challenge design norms and revive automotive artistry through bold, nontraditional creations.
