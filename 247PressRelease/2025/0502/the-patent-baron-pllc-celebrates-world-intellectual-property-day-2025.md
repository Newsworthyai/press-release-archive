# The Patent Baron PLLC Celebrates World Intellectual Property Day 2025

DETROIT, MI, May 02, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Patent Baron PLLC, led by experienced intellectual property attorney J. Baron Lesperance, proudly joins the global community in celebrating World Intellectual Property Day 2025.
