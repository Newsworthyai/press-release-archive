# Windes Partners Recognized as Leaders of Influence: Minority CPAs

LONG BEACH, CA, April 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- This recognition highlights their professional excellence, leadership, and contributions to the accounting profession and their communities.  Tom Huey, Partner in Audit & Assurance, is a leader in the Nonprofit Organizations Group, specializing in federal grant compliance, GASB reporting, and Single Audits. His expertise has been instrumental in supporting nonprofit organizations nationwide. He is also a mentor within the firm and has served as Board Secretary of the Diamond Bar Community Foundation, volunteering in various community initiatives.  Guy Nicio, Partner in the Tax Department, serves on the Windes Board of Directors and leads the Long Beach office and Windes' growing Philippines team, ensuring seamless international operations and client service. His leadership has expanded the firm's global capabilities, integrating cross-border teams to enhance efficiency. A dedicated mentor, Guy fosters collaboration within the firm, guiding professionals in both technical and leadership development. Recognized as one of LA's Top 100 Accountants, he continues to make a lasting contribution to the industry.  Bella Wang, Partner in the Tax Department, leads the firm's International Tax Practice and State and Local Tax (SALT) team, advising businesses on complex tax strategies and compliance. She is a frequent speaker on tax legislation and credits, serves as Vice President of the Asian Pacific Certified Public Accountants Association, and actively mentors aspiring CPAs. She is also a Board member of Women Shelter of Long Beach, a nonprofit organization that supports survivors of domestic violence through education and personal empowerment.  "Tom, Guy, and Bella exemplify the expertise and commitment that define Windes," said Sean McFerson, Managing Partner at Windes. "Their leadership and dedication not only strengthen our firm but also make a meaningful impact on our clients and communities."  About Windes  Windes is a leading advisory, audit, and tax firm for growth-oriented small and mid-sized privately held companies, nonprofit organizations, and high-net-worth individuals. Our approach uses tailored expertise to proactively inform decision-making to maximize our clients' business potential.
