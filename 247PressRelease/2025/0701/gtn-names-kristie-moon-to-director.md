# GTN Names Kristie Moon to Director

MINNEAPOLIS, MN, July 01, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Global Tax Network (GTN), a leading mobility tax services firm, has announced the promotion of Kristie Moon to Director, effective June 30, 2025.   Since joining the firm in 2014, Moon has drawn on her years of experience to deliver exceptional support to clients navigating the complexities of mobility and relocation. Most recently serving as Senior Manager, she has built a reputation for providing practical, tailored guidance that helps clients confidently manage their global assignments.  In addition to her work directly supporting clients, Moon partners closely with GTN's technology development team, leveraging insights gained through her client interactions to enhance the firm's client-facing tools—improving the overall experience for mobile employees and HR teams alike.  "Kristie's dedication to understanding client needs and turning that knowledge into actionable improvements for both our mobility services and technology has made her an invaluable member of our team," said GTN President, Eric Loff. "As she moves into this new role, her leadership and deep expertise will continue to strengthen the support we provide to organizations and their mobile workforces."   Expressing her enthusiasm, Moon added, "I'm excited to step into this role and continue working with such a talented team to address our client's challenges, while helping shape the evolution of our technology to deliver impactful solutions. Helping clients feel confident and supported during relocation has always been a driving force in my career and I look forward to advancing that mission in this new capacity."  Moon earned her Bachelor of Science in Business, Accounting from the University of Minnesota – Carlson School of Management. She is also a Certified Public Accountant licensed in the state of Minnesota. Outside of the office, she enjoys spending quality time with her family and dog, exploring new destinations, attending live music performances, and expanding her knowledge through reading.  ABOUT GLOBAL TAX NETWORK (GTN) GTN is a mobility tax services firm focused on empowering people to live and work anywhere in the world. They help corporate mobility program managers and mobile employees navigate cross-border tax complexities and manage risks. Their scope includes providing support for expatriates, foreign nationals, business travelers (both international and domestic), remote workers, and permanent transfers. GTN is the recipient of several industry awards for excellence, including Accounting Today's "Best Accounting Firms to Work For", as well as the INSIDE Public Accounting "Best of the Best Firms". GTN is headquartered in Minneapolis, Minn., with employees based throughout the United States. For more information, please visit gtn.com.
