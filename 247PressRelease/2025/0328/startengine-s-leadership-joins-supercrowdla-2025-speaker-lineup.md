# StartEngine's Leadership Joins SuperCrowdLA 2025 Speaker Lineup

LOS ANGELES, CA, March 28, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- SuperCrowdLA, the leading conference for impact crowdfunding and social entrepreneurship, proudly announces the participation of Ron Miller, Chairman of StartEngine, and Johanna Cronin, Chief Marketing Officer of StartEngine, in its 2025 speaker lineup.
