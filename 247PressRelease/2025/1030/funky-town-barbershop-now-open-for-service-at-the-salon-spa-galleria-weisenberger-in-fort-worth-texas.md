# Funky Town Barbershop Now Open for Service at the Salon &amp; Spa Galleria Weisenberger in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, October 30, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Barber David Rivera, Jr. proudly announces the opening of Funky Town Barbershop, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Weisenberger in Fort Worth, Texas.  Rivera offers services that include traditional and new haircuts, specialty cuts, cuts for children and seniors, fades, beard trims, straight razor shaves, and hot towel shaves.  With 16 years of experience in the industry, Rivera is a proud third-generation barber. He grew up learning to love barbering by watching other professionals in his family, including his mother, who is a stylist, and his grandfather, who cut hair for 50 years. Rivera said he fell in love with the art of barbering, and the rest is history.  "It started out as a hobby for me," Rivera said. "Then it grew into a business, and now it's my passion."   Rivera has a simple goal: to simply go the extra mile to give each and every customer an experience to remember. And it's clear by his online reviews that he's been successful in doing just that.  "I want every person who walks into my shop to leave feeling like a million bucks," Rivera said.  Funky Town Barbershop is currently accepting new clients. Hours are 12 pm to 7 pm on Monday. 10 to 7 pm on Wednesday, Thursday and Friday and 8 am to 5 pm on Saturday. Appointments are recommended, but walk-ins are accepted; clients should call or text Rivera first at (817) 313-9107 to make sure a spot is available. To schedule an appointment, visit Rivera's booking site. Learn more about Funky Town Barbershop by visiting Rivera's Instagram page or his Facebook page.   To celebrate Funky Town Barbershop's new location, Rivera is offering a five-percent discount for all new clients.  Funky Town Barbershop in the Salon and Spa Galleria Weisenberger is conveniently located near Sundance Square, Camp Bowie, Westover Hills, University Park, and downtown Fort Worth. There is plenty of free parking for customers, and the building is handicap accessible, with wide hallways.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria Weisenberger, visit the salon chain's website.
