# Mayra's Hair Obsession Salon Accepting New Clients at Salon and Spa Galleria on Boat Club Road in Lake Worth, Texas

LAKE WORTH, TX, October 14, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Beautician Mayra Acuña proudly announces the grand opening of Mayra's Hair Obsession Salon, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Lake Worth on Boat Club Road in Lake Worth, Texas. Acuña offers a variety of makeup and hair services, including cuts; fades; hairstyling; updos; color; balayage; and makeovers.  "I love hairstyling and being creative," Acuña said. "And I especially love doing makeovers. It's a wonderful way to boost someone's confidence by enhancing their appearance."  Acuña attended beauty school in California, and has spent eight years working in the industry. Known for being a passionate hairstylist and colorist, Acuña proudly has mastered the art of updos. In addition to being a licensed beautician, she also is a barber.  "That versatility means I can provide services for the entire family, from makeup to balayage or updo on mom to a fade for dad and the kids," Acuña said. "It's so easy to just bring everyone in together."  To better serve her clients, Acuña offers Matrix and Amica products for sale in her salon.  Mayra's Hair Obsession Salon is currently accepting new clients by appointment only. Walk-ins are welcome on a first-come, first-served basis from 9 a.m.-2 p.m. on Thursdays and Fridays, and from 9 a.m.-5 p.m. on Saturdays. To schedule an appointment, send a text message to Acuña at (805) 878-2449.   To celebrate her shop's grand opening, Acuña is offering free hair treatment for new clients who book an appointment for any hair service for the first time. For more information about Mayra's Hair Obsession Salon, or to view samples of Acuña's work, visit her Instagram page, Facebook page, or TikTok page.  Acuña is especially delighted with the location of her new salon, as Boat Club Road is a very busy road, with many opportunities for advertising. In close proximity to several large businesses and shopping areas, Mayra's Hair Obsession Salon at the Salon and Spa Galleria in Lake Worth, Texas also is a convenient location for clients. The Salon and Spa Galleria on Boat Club Road in Lake Worth serves Lake Worth, Saginaw, Azle, Blue Mound, River Oaks, and White Settlement. Several major interchanges and roadways are nearby, allowing easy access from any direction.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria Lake Worth, visit the salon chain's website.
