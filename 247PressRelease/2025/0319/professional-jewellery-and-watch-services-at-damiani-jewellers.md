# Professional Jewellery and Watch Services at Damiani Jewellers

WOODBRIDGE, ON, March 19, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Damiani Jewellers continues to set the standard for excellence in fine jewellery and luxury watch services. With a team of highly skilled professionals, the Woodbridge-based jeweller offers expert repairs, precise appraisals, and beautiful custom design that you can be sure will be treated with the utmost care and craftsmanship.  Jewellery repairs at Damiani Jewellers are conducted on-site by some of the most experienced goldsmiths in the Greater Toronto Area. Using a combination of time-honoured jewellery techniques and modern technology, including laser welding, the jeweller restores and repairs pieces with precision in their Woodbridge jewellery store. Convenient and never shipped off-site, you know your jewellery and timepieces are being serviced locally.   Watch servicing is entrusted to two expert watchmakers who can repair most watch makes and models in-house at Damiani Jewellers showroom. From battery replacements to complete movement overhauls, each timepiece is handled with meticulous attention to detail to ensure optimal performance.  Appraisal services are conducted by two GIA Graduate Gemologists, available by appointment in Woodbridge for on-site evaluations. Whether assessing loose diamonds, fine jewellery, or Swiss-made timepieces, these experts utilize advanced tools to determine authenticity and value without needing third-party appraisers.  For those seeking a truly unique piece, Damiani Jewellers offers a seamless custom design experience. Through a collaborative three-step process of consultation, CAD rendering, and expert craftsmanship, clients can bring their visions to life with a creation designed to be cherished.  To learn more about the expert services available, visit or contact Damiani Jewellers' Woodbridge jewellery store to discuss how they can assist with fine jewellery and luxury watch needs.  About Damiani Jewellers With over 60 years of experience serving clients with their fine jewellery expertise, Damiani Jewellers is the Greater Toronto area's premier jewellery retailer. For three generations, their family-owned business has provided engagement rings, wedding bands, loose diamonds, fine jewellery items, and luxury watches for their customers' most treasured moments. Damiani Jewellers is also proud to be an authorized dealer of authentic Rolex watches, and this accomplishment speaks to the unbeatable customer service that each shopper receives. To learn more about the products and services offered at their showroom in Woodbridge, Ontario visit their website, call (905) 850-4653, email info@damianijewellers.com, or stop by their store in person.
