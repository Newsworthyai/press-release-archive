# In Search of Unique Wedding Venues? Have Your Wedding at DFW Car and Toy Museum in Fort Worth

FORT WORTH, TX, July 23, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- If you are part of a couple that loves cars or toys, you can get married in a private event space that goes far beyond a non-descript hotel ballroom. Instead, you can have your special day at Fort Worth's hottest new attraction, DFW Car and Toy Museum.   Able to accommodate from 50 to 500 guests, the museum is sure to dazzle with its collection of more than 200 exotic and antique cars, as well as the toy collection of more than 3000 rare and collectible toys.  With high ceilings, stunning lighting, and open floor plans, the museum creates an ideal atmosphere for your wedding, set against a multi-million-dollar backdrop of collectible cars and nostalgia-evoking treasures.  The museum is a place that sparks wonder, triggers memories and starts conversations. It's an event venue with a mixture of indoor and outdoor spaces, including a dedicated dining area, an outdoor veranda, and a full-service bar.  The museum is tops among DFW places to get married because you can use your choice of caterers and the finishing and warming kitchen onsite or even feed the guests on your special day from your choice of gourmet food trucks (the museum has a separate inside area for food trucks).   "Let us help you create an event that your guests will never forget at the most unique of unique wedding venues," says Jennifer Brook, Marketing Manager for DFW Car and Toy Museum.   To schedule a tour or find out more, call DFW Car and Toy Museum at (817) 834-3625, x1.  About the DFW Car and Toy Museum  DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980s collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings, etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum events website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
