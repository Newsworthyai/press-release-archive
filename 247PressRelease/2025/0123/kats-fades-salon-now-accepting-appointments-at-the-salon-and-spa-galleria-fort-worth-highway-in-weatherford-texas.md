# Kats Fades & Salon Now Accepting Appointments at the Salon and Spa Galleria Fort Worth Highway in Weatherford, Texas

WEATHERFORD, TX, January 23, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Cosmetologist Kathrine McGhghy proudly announces the opening of Kats Fades & Salon at the Salon and Spa Galleria Fort Worth Highway in Weatherford.  McGhghy offers services for men, women and children. Her services for men include cuts, fades, perms, beard color treatment, shaves, and ear, nose and neck waxing. For women, she offers extensions, cuts, perms, color and touchups, bleach, highlights, lowlights and waxing.  Currently accepting new clients, Kats Fades & Salon is open from 9 a.m.-3 p.m. Thursday and Friday, plus 9 a.m.-2 p.m. on Saturday. Special appointments available outside normal hours upon request.  Learn more about Kats Fades & Salon by visiting McGhghy's Facebook page. To schedule an appointment, call or text (469) 450-0384 or visit McGhghy's booking site.
