# NetCom Learning Slashes Prices on AWS Generative AI Training with Unprecedented 50% Discount

NEW YORK, NY, May 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a leader in IT training and workforce development, today announced a special limited-time discount on its cutting-edge Generative AI Essentials on AWS course. For a limited time, professionals seeking to build critical skills in artificial intelligence can access this highly sought-after training at 50% off.  The one-day intensive virtual instructor-led training comprehensively introduces generative AI technologies within the AWS ecosystem. Participants will gain essential knowledge in AI concepts, methods, and practical implementation strategies specifically designed for the AWS platform.  The course is ideal for business managers, IT professionals, and aspiring AI engineers who want to understand AI fundamentals, explore real-world use cases, and develop responsible AI strategies. In just eight hours, participants will learn to:  • Master generative AI concepts, methods, and strategies on AWS • Identify appropriate applications for generative AI solutions • Implement responsible and safe AI practices • Recognize specific use cases for various AI technologies • Develop implementation plans for organizational AI adoption  This special pricing offer reduces the course fee from $675 to just $337.50—a savings of 50%—but is only available until April 30, 2025.  Participants in the Generative AI Essentials on AWS course will receive expert instruction, comprehensive course materials, and preparation toward AWS Certified AI Practitioner certification.
