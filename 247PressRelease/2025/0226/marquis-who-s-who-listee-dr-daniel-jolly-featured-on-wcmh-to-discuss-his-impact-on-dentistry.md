# Marquis Who's Who Listee Dr. Daniel Jolly Featured on WCMH to Discuss His Impact on Dentistry

COLUMBUS, OH, February 26, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Dr. Daniel Jolly, a dentist, educator and forensic consultant, reflects on his 50-year career, driven by a deep commitment to service. From missionary work in Honduras to identifying 9/11 victims, he has dedicated his expertise to those in need. Passionate about caring for the elderly and individuals with special needs, he continues to advocate for improved Medicaid access in Ohio. As one of the nation's few forensic dental experts, Dr. Jolly's impact spans clinical, educational and humanitarian efforts.  For more on Dr. Jolly's work, visit www.danielejolly.com.
