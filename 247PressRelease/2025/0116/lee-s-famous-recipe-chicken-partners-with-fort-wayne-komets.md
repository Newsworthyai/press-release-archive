# LEE'S FAMOUS RECIPE CHICKEN PARTNERS WITH FORT WAYNE KOMETS

FORT WAYNE, IN, January 16, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Lee's Famous Recipe® Chicken, a name synonymous with delicious, home-style fried chicken for more than 55 years, is teaming up with the Fort Wayne Komets to introduce a new menu offering, the Komets Box! But here's the hat trick: with every purchase of a Komets Box, guests will score a voucher for $14 off 2 Upper Arena Tickets, while supplies last.   "We are excited to support the Komets and celebrate this partnership with a win-win offering that fans can truly enjoy during the season," said Ryan Weaver, CEO of Lee's Famous Recipe® Chicken." Our Komets Box is geared to elevate the fan experience, combining bold flavors with a great value that's just as exciting as the action on the ice."   From now until the end of the regular season, hockey fans can visit any Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken location in the greater Fort Wayne area to grab the Komets Box. This fan-favorite features four Jumbo Chicken Strips, a regular order of potato wedges, and two dipping sauces, along with the Komets ticket voucher for just $8.99. Choose from any of Lee's signature sauces – Bonsai, Chipotle Ranch, BBQ, Creamy Ranch, Buffalo, or Honey Mustard. Guests can add a regular-sized fountain drink for just $1.   Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken at 6316 Stellhorn Rd in Fort Wayne, IN, will be hosting radio remotes with 963 XKE from 2 to 3 pm on February 12, March 19, and April 9. Each event will feature KOMET Mascot ICY, play-by-play broadcaster Shane Albahrani, and two Komets players available for autographs. The fun doesn't end there though, there will be tickets given away at each remote that will be for the next home game.   The Komet's box is available in-store or online for pickup or delivery. Customers can download the Lee's mobile app or visit the Lee's website to place orders and start earning rewards. The app is available for free via Google Play and the App Store.   For more information about Lee's Famous Recipe® Chicken, visit www.leesfamousrecipe.com or follow them on Facebook or Instagram.   *Available while supplies last.   About Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken  For more than 55 years, Lee's Famous Recipe® Chicken, a quick service franchise founded in Lima, Ohio, has specialized in fresh, never frozen chicken. Lee's is recognized for two years in a row as a top 10 Fast Food Fried Chicken by USA Today's 10Best Readers' Choice Award. Today, there are more than 130 Lee's Famous Recipe® Chicken locations in 12 U.S. states and in Canada, most are individually owned and operated. For additional information, please visit LeesFamousRecipe.com.
