# WebCE Wins 2025 Silver Stevie® Award for Contact Center of the Year

DALLAS, TX, March 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- WebCE, a leading provider of online professional education and training, is proud to announce it has been honored with a Silver Stevie Award for Contact Center of the Year (Up to 100 Seats) in the 19th Stevie Awards for Sales & Customer Service. This marks the fourth year in a row that WebCE has received a Stevie Award, following back-to-back Bronze wins in 2022 and 2023 and now consecutive Silver wins in 2024 and 2025.   The Stevie Awards for Sales & Customer Service are the world's top honors for customer service, contact center, business development and sales professionals. The Stevie Awards organizes nine of the world's leading business awards programs, also including the prestigious American Business Awards® and International Business Awards®.   More than 2,100 nominations from organizations of all sizes and in virtually every industry were considered in this year's competition. Winners were determined by the average scores of 176 professionals worldwide on seven specialized judging committees.   "This award is more than a milestone—it's a reflection of the consistent excellence our support team brings to every interaction," said Jennifer Haworth, President of WebCE. "For four straight years, they've shown that personalized, attentive service can make all the difference. Their work sets the tone for our entire organization and continues to be a key reason professionals choose WebCE again and again."   The Stevie Awards judges praised WebCE's Support Services for their commitment to excellence and their unique role in shaping both customer satisfaction and company success.   "WebCE's Support Services team exemplifies exceptional customer service that goes beyond simply answering calls or resolving issues—they're integral to the entire customer experience and the company's strategic growth," the judges said. "Their proactive approach, which includes reviewing course completion surveys and reaching out to customers with support before they even contact the team, sets them apart from many other support departments in the industry."   A strong training foundation, growth opportunities, and an active voice within the company enable WebCE's Support Services team to keep raising the bar in how they support customers and contribute to company success.   "I'm incredibly proud of our team for earning this outstanding recognition once again," said Jordan Smith, Director of Support Services at WebCE. "Their commitment to delivering outstanding service, day in and day out, is what makes this achievement possible. It's an honor to see their hard work celebrated on this level, and I'm grateful to lead such a dedicated and talented group of professionals."   Winners will be celebrated during a gala event attended by more than 400 professionals from around the world at the Marriott Marquis Hotel in New York City on April 10.   Stevie Awards president Maggie Miller stated, "The outstanding scores awarded to this year's Stevie winners reflect the exceptional levels of achievement they demonstrate. We proudly join the judges and the entire Stevie Awards community in congratulating and celebrating the winners on their accomplishments."  About WebCE®  Recognized as a Newsweek Top Online Learning Provider for 2025 and a four-time Stevie® Award Winner for Customer Service, WebCE is a trusted leader in online professional education. From insurance and financial services to real estate, tax, and more, our courses help individuals meet compliance requirements while building lasting career momentum. Whether you're just starting out or looking to advance, WebCE is here to support your goals at every stage. Learn more at www.WebCE.com.   About The Stevie® Awards Stevie Awards are conferred in nine programs: the Asia-Pacific Stevie Awards, the German Stevie Awards, the Middle East & North Africa Stevie Awards, The American Business Awards®, The International Business Awards®, the Stevie Awards for Great Employers, the Stevie Awards for Sales & Customer Service, the Stevie Awards for Technology Excellence, and the Stevie Awards for Women in Business. Stevie Awards competitions receive more than 12,000 entries each year from organizations in more than 70 nations and territories. More than 1,000 professionals around the world participate in the Stevie Awards judging process each year. Honoring organizations of all types and sizes and the people behind them, the Stevies recognize outstanding performances in the workplace worldwide. Learn more about the Stevie Awards at www.StevieAwards.com.
