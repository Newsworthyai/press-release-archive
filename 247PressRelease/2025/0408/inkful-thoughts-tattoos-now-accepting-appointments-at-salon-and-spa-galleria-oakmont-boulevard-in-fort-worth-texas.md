# Inkful Thoughts Tattoos Now Accepting Appointments at Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, April 08, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Tattoo Artist Malachi Gary proudly announces the opening of his new studio, Inkful Thoughts Tattoos, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard in Fort Worth, Texas.  Gary has been fascinated with art since he was young, and it became a passion for him by the time he was about seven years old. He put his artistic talents to work, learning to apply tattoos by becoming a self-taught tattoo artist.   "I found my love for art very early in life," Gary said. "Growing up in Chicago, it was one of those things that kept me out of trouble."  After working in the industry for more than 30 years, Gary certainly has made a name for himself, both locally and nationally. His work has been featured on Spike TV and in multiple magazines, including Urban Ink and Echo Magazine.  Gary loves his work, and prides himself on being on time, prompt, and patient. He enjoys offering his clients top-quality tattoos, affordable prices and excellent customer service, all while teaching them about the art of tattooing.  "I truly care about what I'm doing," he said. "It's important for me to be patient and communicate clearly with my clients before, during, and after their sessions." Gary also provides aftercare cards for all clients, explaining how to properly care for their tattoos.  To celebrate the grand opening of Inkful Thoughts Tattoos at its new location, Gary is offering a 20 percent discount for new clients when they spend $100 or more.  Inkful Thoughts Tattoos is accepting clients daily from 10 a.m.-10 p.m. by appointment only. To book services, visit Gary's Instagram page or text him at (512) 815-2522. Learn more about Inkful Thoughts Tattoos by visiting Gary's Facebook page.  Conveniently situated near several major roadways, Inkful Thoughts Tattoos in the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard is an easy drive from any direction.
