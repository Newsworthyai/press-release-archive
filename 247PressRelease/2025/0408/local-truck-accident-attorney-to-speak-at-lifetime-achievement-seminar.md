# Local Truck Accident Attorney to Speak at Lifetime Achievement Seminar

INDIANAPOLIS, IN, April 08, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Alex Craig, partner at personal injury law firm Craig, Kelley & Faultless, will once again speak at Indiana Trial Lawyer Association's Lifetime Achievement Seminar.   The 37th Annual Lifetime Achievement Seminar will be held May 7 and 8, 2025, in Indianapolis. Alex spoke at last year's seminar and, at the 2023 seminar, was presented with the prestigious Max Goodwin Young Lawyer of the Year award.   As a truck wreck lawyer with trial experience, Alex will present "First Steps in a Semi-Truck Wreck Case" on May 7. He is one of dozens of Indiana attorneys invited to speak at the seminar.   Alex is an attorney with Craig, Kelley & Faultless, a personal injury law firm headquartered in Indianapolis. He and the firm's nine other lawyers represent the victims of serious commercial motor vehicle collisions throughout the country.   Since becoming a lawyer in 2013, Alex has successfully handled several jury trials and pro-bono civil rights cases. He has been recognized in The National Trial Lawyers Top 40 Under 40 yearly since 2015; has been a Super Lawyers Rising Star Honoree since 2021; and was selected by his peers for inclusion in the 2025 edition of Best Lawyers in AmericaÂ®.  He is a lifelong Hoosier who grew up in Lawrence, Indiana. He attended Indiana University Kelley School of Business and Robert H. McKinney School of Law for bachelor's in finance and Doctor of Law degrees, respectively. He graduated law school Magna Cum Laude. Alex also holds a master's degree in business administration from St. Louis University John Cook School of Business.   As a member of Indiana Trial Lawyers Association, Alex is honored to join the organization's Lifetime Achievement Seminar and educate other attorneys on the complexities of personal injury cases involving semi tractor-trailers, dump trucks, flatbed trucks and other commercial motor vehicles.   Craig, Kelley & Faultless has battled negligent motor carriers and insurance companies since 1999. Its attorneys are licensed to practice in several states, including Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Ohio, Kentucky, Iowa and Tennessee. They are nationally recognized for their success representing injured victims of heavy truck wrecks.   Indiana Trial Lawyers Association, founded in 1956, brings attorneys together to protect the rights of injured individuals and advocate for laws that benefit Hoosier consumers, workers and families.  About Craig, Kelley & Faultless  Craig, Kelley & Faultless is headquartered in Indianapolis and has offices in Indiana and an office in St. Louis, Missouri. The firm handles cases throughout the country and has attorneys licensed in Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Kentucky, Ohio and Iowa.
