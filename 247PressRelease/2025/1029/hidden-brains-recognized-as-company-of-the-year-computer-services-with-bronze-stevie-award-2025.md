# Hidden Brains Recognized as Company of the Year - Computer Services with Bronze Stevie Award 2025

LISBON, PORTUGAL, October 29, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Hidden Brains takes home the Stevie Bronze Award. With over 22 years of experience in the field, they have been recognized for their innovation and excellence, receiving multiple international business awards. Steve Annual International Business Award honoured them with 'Company of the Year - Computer Services', adding the 19th award to their arsenal.  The "Computer Services" category defines the organization's scope, and the team at Hidden Brains has made remarkable strides in delivering exceptional computer-related services to clients. This umbrella term is used for various services, including consulting, support, integration, operations management, and technology-enabled solutions. As an AI app development company, Hidden Brains relentlessly pursues excellence, cementing its position as a global leader in innovation. Lastly, it has been in the media buzz as the 'best software development company' by GESIA and 'Emerging AI product of the year' for datums.  Stevie undertakes numerous factors and follows a rigorous process to pick a winning player. After over two months of judging by more than 250 professionals worldwide, the award ceremony took place in Portugal on Friday, 10 October.  The selection process for the International Stevie Awards combines an expert jury and public voting. Organizations submit nominations detailing their achievements, which are reviewed and scored by panels of industry professionals based on innovation, impact, service excellence, and measurable outcomes. Entries that exceed a specific score threshold qualify for Gold, Silver, or Bronze awards.  "Innovation isn't just discovery; it comes in a package of value it drives and impact it delivers," said Chirag Patel, VP President at Hidden Brains. "The award is an honor for us and our service, where we, as a partner, stood with clients in their journey of excellence, innovation, and technology."   "At Hidden Brains, we follow a framework that's not just about growth or success; it's about collective aspects that meet a benchmark. Whether it's implementation, strategy, or giving a better direction to your goals with optimization, we cut all the odds for organizations of all levels, ensuring they win with results, not debts."   Hidden Brains' vision is strong and deeply optimistic. With a dedicated team of over 700 developers, strategists, and innovators, they are the creators and drivers of new technology. Their recent trips to the UAE, USA, UK, and other parts of the world have not only solved countless client challenges but have also opened new doors of opportunity, transforming dreams into reality. Their client base is not just startups or Fortune 500s, but all those visionaries who want to dive into the era of technology and make the most of it.
