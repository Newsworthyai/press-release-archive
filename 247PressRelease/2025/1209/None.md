# 4 Your Wellness to Be Featured on The Balancing Act This December

HOLLYWOOD, FL, December 09, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- 4 Your Wellness (4YWN) announced today that the company will be featured on an upcoming segment of The Balancing Act, airing December 13 and 14 on WSFL–INDEPENDENT. The episode highlights 4YWN's bestselling products and the brand's mission to provide accessible, high-quality wellness solutions for everyday health.   Broadcast Schedule   · Saturday, December 13, 2025 — 11:00 AM ET on WSFL – INDEPENDENT  · Sunday, December 14, 2025 — 8:30 AM ET on WSFL – INDEPENDENT   Channel numbers may vary by cable provider. Viewers are encouraged to check their local listings for the exact channel.   A Spotlight on Modern Wellness  The Balancing Act episode features a personal testimonial from a 4YWN customer who shares improvements in endurance, longer walking capacity, and general cardiovascular wellness after incorporating the brand's Nitric Oxide Capsules into their routine.   The Balancing Act also highlights the brand's focus on quality ingredients and its mission to support individuals throughout their health journey.   During the segment, Michelle Barda, Vice President of Sales at 4 Your Wellness, shares insight into the brand's philosophy:   "I believe that staying healthy is a mindset. It's a choice. So that first step is something that needs to happen, and it has to come from you," said Barda. "Our products are designed with top-quality ingredients because we care deeply about supporting every part of your wellness journey. When you take care of the inside, everything else begins to follow."  About 4 Your Wellness  4 Your Wellness (4YWN) is a South Florida–based wellness brand offering simple, effective supplements designed to support cardiovascular health, vitality, energy, immunity, and overall well-being. Known for its Nitric Oxide Capsules and growing collection of wellness products, 4YWN is committed to delivering trustworthy, high-quality formulas backed by thoughtful ingredient sourcing.   About The Balancing Act   The Balancing Act is a long-running morning show dedicated to sharing lifestyle tips, health insights, and product spotlights that help viewers live well-balanced, empowered lives.
