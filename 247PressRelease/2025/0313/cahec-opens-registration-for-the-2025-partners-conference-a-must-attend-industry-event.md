# CAHEC Opens Registration for the 2025 Partners Conference: A Must-Attend Industry Event!

RALEIGH, NC, March 13, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- CAHEC is excited to announce that online registration is now open for the highly anticipated 2025 Partners Conference! Scheduled for June 3-4, 2025, this event offers an unparalleled opportunity to network, learn, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of tax credits and community development.  Designed for developers, investors, community organizations, and industry professionals, the conference will provide expert insights into key economic, legislative, and regulatory trends shaping our field. Meanwhile, those working at the site level will benefit from specialized sessions focused on tax credit compliance and LIHTC management, giving them essential knowledge to excel in their roles.  Attendees can look forward to a dynamic lineup of engaging training sessions, interactive educational programs, and valuable networking opportunities designed to empower them with the skills and insights needed to navigate the affordable housing and community development sector.  Amy Cross, Director, Communications, shared her excitement: "This year's conference is set to be one of our best yet! With an incredible lineup of speakers, sessions, and networking events, attendees will gain invaluable knowledge while making meaningful connections. Plus, our totally tubular 80s-themed welcome reception is sure to be a hit!"
