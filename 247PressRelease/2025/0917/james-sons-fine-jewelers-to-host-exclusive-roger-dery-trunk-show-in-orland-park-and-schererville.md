# James &amp; Sons Fine Jewelers to Host Exclusive Roger Dery Trunk Show in Orland Park and Schererville

ORLAND PARK, IL, September 17, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- James & Sons Fine Jewelers invites clients to experience the brilliance of the world's finest colored gemstones at an exclusive Roger Dery Trunk Show, taking place at two showroom locations this September. The Orland Park event will be held on Thursday, September 18th, from 11:00 am to 5:00 pm, followed by the Schererville showcase on Friday, September 19th, from 11:00 am to 5:00 pm.  This trunk show presents a rare opportunity to explore the extraordinary Roger Dery Gem Collection, which represents the top 1% of colored gemstones worldwide. Known for precision faceting, exceptional quality, and ethical sourcing, each Roger Dery gemstone reflects a blend of artistry and integrity.  Guests will have the chance to view and acquire breathtaking gemstones, including sapphires, tourmalines, garnets, and spinels, each hand-selected and cut with unparalleled attention to detail. Whether clients are seeking a loose gemstone for a custom jewelry design or looking to add a remarkable piece to their collection, this event is designed to inspire.  Throughout the day, James & Sons' expert jewelry consultants will be joined by the Roger Dery team to provide personalized guidance and insight into each stone's unique story. With their combined expertise, guests can feel confident in selecting a gemstone that reflects both beauty and meaning.  As this is an exclusive event, appointments are required to ensure individual attention and the highest level of service. Contact James & Sons Fine Jewelers for more information about the event and their selection.  About James & Sons In 1964, James F. Sunderland Sr. opened the doors to the original James & Sons Fine Jewelers in Calumet City, IL. Since those humble beginnings, three generations of the Sunderland family have been the trusted jeweler for the Chicagoland area. By offering the very best price, quality, and selection, the James & Sons Family is committed to making every customer have a 5 Star experience. With over 60 years of expertise and service, James & Sons has built a legacy of integrity and trust with their customers, who have in turn become like family. Contact one of their James & Sons jewelry store locations in Chicago, Naperville, Orland Park, and Schererville to learn more about their incredible fine jewelry and timepiece selection along with their professional jewelry services.
