# Huntington Fine Jewelers' 2025 Getaway for the Holidays

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, November 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- From Monday, November 10th through Saturday, November 15th, customers who spend $2,999 or more at any Oklahoma Huntington Fine Jewelers location will receive a complimentary 7-night resort getaway. This exclusive offer allows shoppers to select a destination of their choice from a wide-selection of trips, and there are all-inclusive options available for an even more luxurious experience.  This limited-time promotion is the perfect opportunity to find a stunning holiday gift, a memorable engagement ring, or a special piece of fine jewelry while simultaneously securing a much-deserved vacation. Imagine celebrating the new year in New York City or spending Christmas relaxing on a tropical beach; the customer gets to choose what's best for them!  Huntington Fine Jewelers encourages customers to RSVP for the event, so you don't miss the chance to score the perfect piece of jewelry and the trip of a lifetime simultaneously.  Visit any of Huntington Fine Jewelers' Oklahoma jewelry stores for complete details.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers  For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services. For more information visit their website at www.huntingtonfinejewelers.com, send an email to info@huntingtonfinejewelers.com or visit their showrooms in person.
