# Bhavik Sarkhedi and Sahil Gandhi Release eBook "Become Someone from No One" on Building Personal Branding and Professional Identity

NEW YORK, NY, November 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Ohh My Brand co-founders Bhavik Sarkhedi and Sahil Gandhi have released a new eBook titled "Become Someone from No One." The publication presents a structured approach to personal branding, digital visibility, and self-positioning for individuals seeking to strengthen their professional presence.  The eBook focuses on the growing need for personal branding across diverse sectors. It outlines methods for building professional identity in an era where digital profiles often serve as first impressions.   The authors address the process of transforming individual skills and experiences into a consistent narrative that communicates expertise and credibility.  Purpose and Scope of the eBook "Become Someone from No One" aims to guide readers through the stages of creating and maintaining a personal brand. It emphasizes clarity, authenticity, and consistency as the core elements of long-term professional visibility. The content is structured to assist individuals in identifying what defines them, articulating their value, and presenting it across digital and professional spaces.  The book includes chapters that explore: Understanding the fundamentals of personal branding Translating personal experience into professional identity Building trust and credibility through consistent communication Managing public perception and digital presentation Sustaining visibility through strategic engagement  The eBook provides practical models, templates, and reflective exercises that allow readers to apply the ideas directly to their work or career path.  Relevance for Modern Professionals The publication is designed for a broad professional audience. It addresses the challenges faced by: Students, who are preparing to enter competitive industries and need to establish their digital identity early Entrepreneurs, who aim to align personal visibility with business goals Consultants and coaches, who depend on credibility and personal presentation to attract clients Human resources professionals, who shape internal and external perception of talent and culture Freelancers and creators, who seek to differentiate their skills in an open market   "Personal branding has become an essential part of professional growth," said Bhavik Sarkhedi, Co-founder of Ohh My Brand. "This eBook explains how individuals can create structured visibility through defined steps instead of following trends."  "The goal was to build a process that applies to every professional, regardless of role or industry," added Sahil Gandhi, also known as Brand Professor. "Anyone who wants to communicate their value clearly can use this framework to do so."  About the Authors Bhavik Sarkhedi is an author and communication strategist who has written multiple books on branding, writing, and professional development. Sahil Gandhi, known as Brand Professor, specializes in personal brand management and visibility consulting.   Together, they lead Ohh My Brand, a content and brand strategy firm that works with professionals and organizations on storytelling, communication, and positioning.  Ohh My Brand is a global personal branding agency and content strategy firm that helps individuals and organizations establish clear, consistent, and credible digital identities. The company works across sectors to develop communication frameworks, brand storytelling, and positioning strategies. Founded by Bhavik Sarkhedi and Sahil Gandhi, Ohh My Brand focuses on structured visibility and narrative-driven brand development.
