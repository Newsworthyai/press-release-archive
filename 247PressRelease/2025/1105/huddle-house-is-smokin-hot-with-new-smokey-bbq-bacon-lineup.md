# Huddle House Is Smokin' Hot with NEW Smokey BBQ Bacon Lineup

ATLANTA, GA, November 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Huddle House, the neighborhood gathering place that brings friends and family together over delicious food served from the heart, brings the sauce and smoke with a NEW Smokey BBQ Bacon lineup featuring bold Southern flavors. Guests can dig into saucy, smoky creations that bring cozy comfort and indulgent taste to every bite for a limited time only.  "As the weather cools and the holiday season begins, guests are craving hearty, flavorful dishes. That's why we're highlighting our new Smokey BBQ Bacon lineup this season," said Stephanie Mattingly, VP of Marketing for Huddle House. "Huddle House is here to deliver that comforting, made-from-the-heart experience at every meal, whether it's breakfast, lunch, or dinner with friends and family."  New! Smokey BBQ Bacon Lineup  Smokey BBQ Bacon Smashed Burger: Two juicy smashed all-beef patties, Applewood smoked bacon, crispy onion tanglers, American cheese, pickle chips, and rich Smokey BBQ sauce on a toasted potato bun.  Smokey BBQ Bacon Grilled Chicken Sandwich: Grilled chicken breast topped with Applewood smoked bacon, American cheese, pickle chips, and rich Smokey BBQ sauce on a toasted potato bun.  Smokey BBQ Bacon Fried Chicken Sandwich: Crispy Southern-fried chicken filet topped with Applewood-smoked bacon, American cheese, pickle chips, and rich Smokey BBQ sauce on a toasted potato bun.  Smokey BBQ Fried Chicken Salad / Smokey BBQ Grilled Chicken Salad: Mixed greens, carrots, red cabbage, tomatoes, cheddar cheese, Applewood smoked bacon, croutons, and crispy onion tanglers, drizzled with Smokey BBQ sauce and creamy ranch dressing.
