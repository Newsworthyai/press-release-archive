The PrestaShop Mobile App Builder: A Game-Changer in Mobile Commerce  As mobile shopping takes the lead in the global eCommerce movement, having a dedicated mobile app is now essential for customer engagement and retention. Knowband's Mobile App Builder for eCommerce platforms, such as WooCommerce and OpenCart, makes this transformation easy. The module helps the PrestaShop store owners to create an Android and iOS app without any coding, with just a few clicks.  Key features of the module include:   The module provides a real-time connection with the PrestaShop store backend, and the module automatically updates products, orders, and inventory. Moreover, merchants can easily personalise the home screen, which would suit the brand themes and seasonal sales offers.  Also, they will be able to send push notifications to reconnect consumers with new offers, updates, and abandoned carts. The module supports all shipping and payment options on their site. Hence, this gives the website an easy checkout system.  Also, it works with several languages and currencies, thus it can be used by foreign businesses. Embedded WhatsApp customer service will allow customers to interact with the company and receive assistance in the app.  Moreover, admins can add ishlist and coupon services to attract users and keep them engaged, thereby retaining them. The module has instant order tracking facilities, and the product and category screens are user-friendly, which makes shopping easy.  The GDPR compatibility adds compliance convenience by offering options for data consent and personal data removal to users. Furthermore, with the auto banner slider and the numerous layouts, store owners can feature promotions and campaigns with image sections or a video section.  Thousands of merchants have praised this module because it is easy to use, stable in performance, and has great support.
