# KAGED® Ignites Category Innovation with Pre-Workout MAX

COSTA MESA, CA, November 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- KAGED®, the performance-nutrition brand known for clean, clinical, and cutting-edge formulations, has unleashed its latest breakthrough — Pre-Workout MAX, a formula redefining what serious athletes expect from a pre-workout. First launched exclusively at The Vitamin Shoppe®, KAGED Pre-Workout MAX will roll out nationally across all major retail channels in 2026, marking one of the most anticipated category expansions of the year.  Available in two bold flavors: Blue Raspberry and Watermelon. Every serving is engineered for smoother absorption, cleaner energy, and measurable performance.  This isn't just another pre-workout — it's a redefinition of what clean, effective performance feels like.
