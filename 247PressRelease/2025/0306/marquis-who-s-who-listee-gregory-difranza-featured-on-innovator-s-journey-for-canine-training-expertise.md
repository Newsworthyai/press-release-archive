# Marquis Who's Who Listee Gregory DiFranza Featured on Innovator's Journey for Canine Training Expertise

JACKSONVILLE BEACH, FL, March 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Gregory DiFranza, founder of Kamp K9 Jax Bch, was featured on Innovator's Journey to discuss his groundbreaking approach to dog training. With a background in tactical operations training, Mr. DiFranza discovered that most canine behavior challenges stem from human miscommunication. His method focuses on instinctual communication, leadership and trust rather than traditional commands. In his book "Dog Stories," he shares real-life cases illustrating the need for a personalized approach. Kamp K9 offers tailored training to strengthen the bond between dogs and their owners.
