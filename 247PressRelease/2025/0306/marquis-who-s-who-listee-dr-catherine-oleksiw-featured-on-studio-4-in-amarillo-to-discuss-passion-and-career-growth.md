# Marquis Who's Who Listee Dr. Catherine Oleksiw Featured on Studio 4 in Amarillo to Discuss Passion and Career Growth

AMARILLO, TX, March 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Psychologist and professional coach Dr. Catherine Oleksiw shared with Studio 4 her insights on embracing a nonlinear career path and discovering personal passions. In her consulting and coaching business, Measured Transitions LLC, as well as in the classroom, Catherine encourages individuals to recognize their unique gifts and use them to make meaningful contributions. As an academic advisor, she helps graduate students navigate academic challenges, particularly in completing their dissertation. Dr. Oleksiw is writing a book on overcoming psychological barriers to finishing a PhD dissertation, emphasizing resilience, grit and self-forgiveness. In addition to her role as Business Psychology Department Chair for The Chicago School College of Graduate and Professional Studies, she provides executive coaching for federal employees.
