# Jason McDonald Consulting Announces Over 225 Reviews for Popular Social Media Marketing Book on Amazon

Seeing reviews from beginners to pros saying they've used it to grow their businesses—that's why I do this. The book also helps me stay current with the industry, thus feeding into my expert witness work in social media ads and organic content."
