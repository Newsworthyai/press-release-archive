# Donate to the Associated Press and Subscribe to Reuters

WASHINGTON, DC, May 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Associated Press and Reuters are the best wire services in journalism today. They give the world the best information out there when it comes to the best investigative stories or the days events from the White House or congress.   The public is vulnerable to fall for political propaganda where there is no access to reliable news outlets or their local newspaper is on a paywall due to their financial concerns. We need to ensure that the Associated Press is not on a paywall and everybody gets the best news content from the United States and beyond. Also that people get access to the best international and investigative content from Reuters.   When making a donation with the Associated Press you can trust that your contribution is going to a great cause and making a positive impact such as fighting for the First Amendment in the White House.
