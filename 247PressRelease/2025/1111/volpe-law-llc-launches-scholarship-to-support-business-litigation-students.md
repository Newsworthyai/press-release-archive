# Volpe Law LLC Launches Scholarship To Support Business Litigation Students

DENVER, CO, November 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Denver, CO commercial contract lawyer team at Volpe Law LLC have announced The Volpe Law LLC Future Litigators Scholarship. This financial award of $1,000 will go to a student who demonstrates academic excellence, commitment to putting people first, and a student who has experience in the business world.  The scholarship application is open to students enrolled in an accredited program in the United States. This includes community colleges as well as high school seniors who have their GEDs and have been accepted into an accredited program. Applicants must be in good academic standing and must demonstrate a commitment to business in their essay; essays must address three questions provided by the firm to showcase the applicant's experience in this area of law and how it pertains to the community.  The Volpe Law LLC Future Litigators Scholarship aims to financially support students as they work through school by providing $1,000 to be used for course materials, supplies, and more. The firm hopes to promote their mission of doing what's right, even if it is not always easy by providing students with this financial assistance.   Complete application instructions are provided on the law firm's website. For additional information about this scholarship, please contact the firm using this email: scholarships@volpelawllc.com  About Volpe Law LLC:  Volpe Law LLC was founded in 2020 in Denver, CO by Ben Volpe. The firm was created based on the premise that people come first in legal cases, and the mission of the firm is to do what's right, even if it is not always easy. They work with 7 and 8 figure companies focused on commercial litigation, real estate, and general counsel for businesses. With a 10.0 rating on Justia and over 65 five star ratings on Google, the firm has demonstrated their commitment to their clients and their mission of putting the clients first, repeatedly.
