# High Rise Financial LLC Expands with New Philadelphia Office

PHILADELPHIA, PA, November 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- High Rise Financial LLC, a trusted leader in pre-settlement funding, proudly announces the opening of its newest office in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. This strategic expansion marks a key milestone in the company's mission to provide accessible, transparent financial support to plaintiffs across the country.   The new office, located at 1635 Market Street, Suite 1620, Philadelphia, PA 19103, will offer in-person resources and regional support to both individual plaintiffs and partnering law firms. With this new location, High Rise Financial enhances its ability to serve the Mid-Atlantic region while continuing to meet the growing national demand for reliable legal funding services. This office joins the company's established presence in Newark, further strengthening its geographic footprint and commitment to local service.   "We're excited to officially join the Philadelphia community," said Mark Berookim, co-founder of High Rise Financial LLC. "Philadelphia's vibrant legal market and the growing need for pre-settlement funding make it an ideal location for us. Our mission has always been to provide ethical, transparent, and timely financial support to clients during difficult times, and this new office allows us to fulfill that mission more effectively."  With more personal injury and civil litigation cases progressing through the courts, many plaintiffs are left in financial limbo while waiting for a resolution. High Rise Financial helps bridge that gap by offering fast, non-recourse cash advances that allow clients to cover essential living expenses and maintain financial stability throughout the legal process.  About High Rise Financial LLC  High Rise Financial LLC is a premier pre-settlement funding firm that helps plaintiffs in personal injury, wrongful death, medical malpractice, and other civil lawsuits access the financial assistance they need while awaiting case resolution.   The company's funding solutions are designed to alleviate the pressure of mounting bills and lost income during litigation, offering peace of mind when clients need it most. With a steadfast commitment to ethical practices, clear terms, and fast approvals, High Rise Financial continues to lead the industry in client-first service.
