# Opus Virtual Offices Helps Business Coaches Launch and Scale Client Success

BOCA RATON, FL, May 28, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Business coaches across the country are turning to Opus Virtual Offices to help their clients grow faster and smarter. With over 650 locations nationwide, Opus makes it easy for coaches to guide entrepreneurs toward building credibility, reaching new markets, and running a professional operation without the high costs of a physical office.  One Miami-area injury attorney, with the help of a business coach, expanded his home-based law firm to Orlando after he noticed the demand pick up in that region. Using an Opus Virtual Office, he built a local presence in Central Florida so that he could attract new clients while remaining at home.  "Our mission has always been to empower entrepreneurs," said Opus Virtual Offices CEO Yori Galel. "It's a privilege to be the trusted resource business coaches rely on to help their clients succeed and scale."  Opus Virtual Offices offers an additional tool for Business Coaches to add to their portfolio to better assist in the growth of their clients. Whether someone is launching their first business or expanding into a new market, Opus makes it easy to establish a professional presence and build confidence from day one. It's a simple, reliable tool that adds real value to the coaching process.  About Opus Virtual Offices  Opus Virtual Offices has become the fastest-growing company in the alternative workspace industry, with more than 650 locations throughout the U.S. and Canada. Their successful business model is built on providing complete office services for a single, low monthly rate of $99. Exclusive features of an Opus Virtual Office include a live receptionist answering and transferring calls, a prestigious corporate mailing address, a business phone, fax, and voicemail.
