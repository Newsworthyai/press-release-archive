# New York Immigration Law Firm Bardazzi Law PLLC Expands Investor Visa Services With Deep International Experience

NEW YORK, NY, December 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Bardazzi Law PLLC, operating through its well-known platform E-2VisaWorld, announces the continued growth of its U.S. business-immigration practice supporting entrepreneurs, investors, and professionals seeking to establish their presence in the United States. From its office at 40 Wall Street in New York (see Google Maps), the firm provides multilingual legal guidance in Italian, English, and Spanish for clients pursuing E-2 visas, O-1 visas, EB-1 green cards, family-based immigration, EB-2 and EB-2 NIW petitions.  Bardazzi Law PLLC offers an integrated approach to these matters by assisting with U.S. company formation, business-plan review, eligibility analysis, and the preparation of comprehensive visa petitions. The firm additionally supports Italian clients with matters involving Italian citizenship pathways and elective residency visas, as well as select business licensing issues including NYC liquor licensing.  "Every client who comes to us shares a common goal: the desire to build a better life and pursue the American Dream. With our international background and focused experience in investor and professional visa categories, we are committed to providing the precise, attentive, and personalized legal guidance they deserve."   Mr. Emanuele Bardazzi brings a uniquely global foundation to his practice. A member of both the New York State Bar Association and the Italian Bar Association, he practiced law in Italy for more than 12 years before relocating to the United States. He holds a J.D. from the University of Pisa, an LL.M. from the Benjamin Cardozo School of Law, and additional Master's degrees in Corporate Law and Alternative Dispute Resolution. His experience counseling European companies and individuals in U.S. business and immigration matters forms the cornerstone of the firm's mission.  Bardazzi Law PLLC, operating through E-2VisaWorld, is a New York-based immigration law firm founded in 2018 and dedicated to investor-visa matters, extraordinary-ability visas, and employment- and family-based green card processes. Led by attorney Emanuele Bardazzi, the firm offers multilingual services and a personalized, detail-driven approach for international entrepreneurs and professionals relocating to the United States.
