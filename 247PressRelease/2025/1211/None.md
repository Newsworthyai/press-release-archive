# Topsail Steamer Serves Up Sea-sational Family Feasts for the Holidays

SURF CITY, NC, December 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Move over turkey and ham, because this holiday season, Topsail Steamer is bringing coastal cheer to homes nationwide with its unique, make-at-home seafood steam pots. The family-run company is making holiday hosting easier and tastier than ever with their sea-sational Bay Buckets® built for feeding a crowd. Each pot is stocked with all the fixings you need. Just add your liquid of choice, steam, and serve. It's truly as easy as 1-2-3.  A woman-owned business featured on Shark Tank and handpicked by Oprah for The O List: Summer Fun, Topsail Steamer continues to make waves by offering a flavorful, fuss-free alternative to traditional holiday meals.  "The holidays are the perfect time to bring loved ones together over great-tasting food and experiences," said Danielle Mahon, CEO and Founder of Topsail Steamer. "Our seafood steam pots help families create warm, coastal moments no matter where they celebrate, and we're delighted to share our seaside feasts with communities far and wide."  As an added holiday treat, customers who purchase a $100 gift card will receive a $50 bonus card, which is perfect for gifting or keeping for yourself. Offer valid on Wednesday December 17, 2025. Both traditional and eGift Cards are available on the Topsail Steamer website for use in-store. Offer not applicable to Goldbelly gift cards.   Where To Scoop Up a Bay Bucket® Reeling in a holiday feast is easy with Topsail Steamer's flexible ordering options. Customers can visit any of the brand's 11 locations across Alabama, Delaware, Florida, New Jersey, and North Carolina, or place an order by phone for pickup or delivery, where available. Topsail Steamer also offers nationwide shipping via Goldbelly.  Topsail Steamer stands out with its distinctive take-home model. At the Topsail Steamer locations, customers can choose from a menu of shrimp-boil style "Bay Buckets" featuring various fresh seafood combinations, which can also be customized to suit individual preferences. All pots begin with a base of sweet corn on the cob, Red Bliss potatoes, and a proprietary blend of seasonings, along with cocktail sauce and butter for dipping. Customers are then presented with their fresh, single-use steam pot filled to brim with fresh seafood options including NC shrimp, little neck clams, crab, Scallops, and lobster tail and choice of sausage along with simple-to-follow instructions to prepare their beach-inspired meal from the comfort of their own homes.  To learn more about Topsail Steamer or place an order, please visit topsailsteamer.com.  About Topsail Steamer  Topsail Steamer was founded on North Carolina's Topsail Island in 2017. The company currently operates 11 stores across five states and ships products nationwide. The family-owned business was started by Danielle Mahon, who grew up in an entrepreneurial family but had no direct experience until a mid-career switch out of corporate America. In early 2024, the company announced a partnership with Elite Franchise Capital to help develop 40+ new stores nationwide.
