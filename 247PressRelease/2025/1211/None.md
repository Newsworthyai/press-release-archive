# Kelso Law Celebrates Attorney Alma Chavez's Outstanding Texas Bar Exam Achievement

DALLAS, TX, December 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- We are proud to announce that Alma Chavez, one of our exceptionally talented and compassionate attorneys, has recently passed the Texas Bar Exam, scoring in the top 1-2% across the nation! As a Dallas native and a dedicated civil litigator, Chavez joined Kelso Law in 2022. Since then, she has excelled in her law school classes, consistently ranking in the top 10-15%, and has proven to be an outstanding asset to our firm. While at the University of North Texas at Dallas College of Law, Chavez was a Dean's Scholar for five semesters, and that dedication to her legal education has continued since she graduated in May of 2025.   As a part of the Kelso Law team, Attorney Chavez has worked hard to make a name for herself in the field of law, focusing her talents on personal injury, medical malpractice, and immigration law. She speaks fluent Spanish, and her translation services have enabled us to reach more clients in need of legal guidance. From the beginning of her internship with us, she has shown a commitment to our clients and our team that makes us proud to call her one of our own. As our firm grows, we look forward to seeing Attorney Chavez grow with us.  About Kelso Law   Kelso Law offers trusted legal advocacy in personal injury, medical malpractice, and immigration law. Our team has been helping Texas clients win cases for over a decade. In that time, we've secured over $20 million in compensation, enabling our clients to obtain financial assistance to cover medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering. Whether you've been the victim of an accident, experienced medical malpractice, or been denied a Visa or citizenship, we're here to support you. If you require legal support, contact Kelso Law today.
