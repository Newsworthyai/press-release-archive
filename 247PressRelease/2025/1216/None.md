# THE CAPITAL GRILLE HAWAII INTRODUCES CAPITAL HOURS - AN ELEVATED AFTERNOON EXPERIENCE IN WAIKIKI

HONOLULU, HI, December 16, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Capital Grille Hawaii, Waikiki's newest destination for refined steakhouse dining, proudly announces the launch of Capital Hours, a curated afternoon-to-early-evening experience featuring signature cocktails, exceptional wines, and chef-crafted small plates served in the restaurant's sophisticated lounge.  Capital Hours brings a refined twist to the traditional happy hour, offering guests an opportunity to unwind in the luxurious atmosphere The Capital Grille is known for. Capital Hours is available from 4-6PM daily.  Capital Hour Highlights:  • Hand-Crafted Cocktails & Classics – Expertly mixed beverages showcasing premium spirits and classic flavors. • Distinctive Wines by the Glass – A rotating selection from The Capital Grille's acclaimed wine list, designed to be both approachable and memorable. • Shareable Lounge Plates – Elevated bites and signature small plates, perfect for savoring and sharing.  "We are excited to introduce Capital Hours to the Honolulu community," said Jasmine Peterson, General Manager for The Capital Grille. "This new offering provides residents and visitors a relaxed yet elegant way to experience The Capital Grille - whether gathering with friends, unwinding after work, or enjoying a memorable moment before dinner."  RESERVATIONS & DETAILS To learn more about Capital Hour or to book reservations, visit www.thecapitalgrillehawaii.com or call (808) 762-0020.  MEDIA CONTACT: Margaret McKoin Email: Margaret.McKoin@unionmak.com Phone: 817-403-0866  ABOUT THE CAPITAL GRILLE HAWAII Located at 2380 Kūhiō Avenue at Lilia Waikiki, The Capital Grille Hawaii offers a premier dining experience featuring dry-aged steaks, fresh seafood, and an award-winning wine selection. Guests are enjoying unmatched service and refined culinary craftsmanship in an atmosphere designed for unforgettable dining. Complementary three-hour valet parking is provided.
