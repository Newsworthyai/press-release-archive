# Huntington Fine Jewelers Hosts Annual April Trunk Show

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, April 12, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Throughout the month of April, Huntington Fine Jewelers is inviting clients to experience its highly anticipated Annual Trunk Show event. Known for showcasing extraordinary pieces from around the world, this exclusive event offers a rare opportunity to explore a curated selection of fine jewelry—each piece handpicked by Huntington's dedicated global buyer.  This expert travels internationally to uncover exquisite, one-of-a-kind designs, acquiring them at close-out prices from top-tier sources. These treasures are then offered to Huntington Fine Jewelers' valued clientele at an extraordinary 50% off for discerning jewelry lovers and collectors alike.  The Trunk Show will travel across Huntington's Oklahoma locations:  - Yukon: April 8–9  - Midwest City: April 10–11  - Norman: April 15  - Shawnee: April 16  - Stillwater: April 22  - Oklahoma City: April 23–25   With new selections arriving daily, each stop on the tour promises a fresh and unique experience. Whether searching for a statement piece or a refined addition to a personal collection, clients are encouraged to RSVP promptly to take advantage of these exceptional offerings.  For details on attending the Trunk Show or to reserve your spot, please contact your nearest Huntington Fine Jewelers location. Let their expert teams in their Oklahoma jewelry stores assist you in discovering a remarkable piece at an equally remarkable value.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers   For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services. For more information visit their website at www.huntingtonfinejewelers.com, send an email to info@huntingtonfinejewelers.com or visit their showrooms in person.
