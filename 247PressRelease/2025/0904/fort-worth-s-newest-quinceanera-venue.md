# Fort Worth's Newest Quinceanera Venue

FORT WORTH, TX, September 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- If your daughter is coming up on her Quinceanera and you want to make sure her day is special, we must introduce you to one of the unique event venues near me if you are in North Fort Worth: DFW Car and Toy Museum  The museum isn't just the attraction that Trip Advisor rated the #1 fun thing to do in Fort Worth. It's also available to host private events, including your daughter's special Quinceanera.  Unlike some other places to throw a party, the museum adds to the experience of your event. Your guests are sure to enjoy the collection of more than 200 special autos and more than 3,000 rare toys.   A pristine new venue with more than 150,000 square feet of indoor space, the museum can host events for between 50 and 300 guests with flexible layouts to make sure your daughter's special day is memorable and fun for your guests.   DFW Car and Toy Museum is conveniently located at 2550 McMillan Parkway (at Meacham and I35w) in Fort Worth. Among Tarrant County's premier party venues, the museum has plenty of free parking for hosting a perfect quinceanera. It even has one acre of manicured outdoor space if you wish to have outdoor activities for your guests.   The museum has become the most popular of all local quinceanera venues near me because of its simple, flat rate pricing. "Our prices include many of the extras that other event centers charge separately for, such as tables, chairs, sound equipment and use of our onsite warming and finishing kitchen," said museum Marketing Manager Jennifer Brook.   Event rental clients can keep costs down by using the caterer of their choice, doing the cooking themselves and using our finishing kitchen or even bringing in gourmet food trucks," said Brook. "In addition, the museum has a VIP room for the birthday girl, her court of damas and her chamberlains to get ready for photographs or just relax during the quinceanera festivities," said Brook.  The museum has opened bookings for 2025 and beyond. To reserve the museum for your special quince, or to arrange a tour, call (817) 834-3625, x1.  About the DFW Car and Toy Museum  DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980s collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings, etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. The museum was rated the #1 Fun thing to do in Fort Worth by Trip Advisor (out of 328 things to do), and the museum has 600 five-star reviews on Google. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum's website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
