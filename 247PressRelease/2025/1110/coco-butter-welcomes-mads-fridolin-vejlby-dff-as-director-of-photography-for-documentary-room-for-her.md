# Coco Butter Welcomes Mads Fridolin Vejlby, DFF as Director of Photography for Documentary "Room For Her"

LOS ANGELES, CA, November 10, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Coco Butter proudly announces that celebrated cinematographer Mads Fridolin Vejlby, DFF will take on the role of Director of Photography for the documentary feature "Room For Her," directed by Katie Ginge Cox and produced by Ian McClellan.  "Room For Her" tells the compelling story of Alyson Fouse, one of Hollywood's trailblazing black women in television comedy. Raised in Compton, California, Alyson's journey embodies resilience, determination, and creative triumph. The film spotlights her path from her family home—where storytelling began with comics and church minutes—to her first professional steps in radio, and onto the writers' rooms of hit Hollywood comedies. With notable collaborations featuring Chris Rock and Wanda Sykes, Alyson's story demonstrates the power of breaking barriers and forging a new legacy in entertainment.  Director of Photography Mads Fridolin Vejlby will work side-by-side with producer Ian McClellan and director Katie Ginge Cox to craft a signature documentary style. The film will blend observational camerawork and reflective interviews, capturing Alyson's daily life and pivotal moments with nuance and intimacy. Vejlby will oversee all aspects of the cinematography—from developing visual concepts and assembling crew members, to guiding lighting choices, and ensuring every frame elevates Alyson's story.  Through collaborative planning, look books, and detailed shotlists, Vejlby's vision will support the heart of the film: revealing not only Alyson's achievements, but also the journey of change in Hollywood over recent decades. The project will unfold in phases, starting with pre-production work and culminating in principal photography, pick-up shoots, and post-production collaboration—all with Vejlby's expert guidance ensuring a cohesive and powerful visual narrative.  For press inquiries, interview requests, or further information, please contact: Coco Butter Ian McClellan, Co-founder & Producer  https://cocobutter.family/  About Coco Butter: Coco Butter is a creative media studio dedicated to amplifying visionary voices through innovative documentaries, films, and media, committed to reflecting authentic stories from diverse perspectives.
