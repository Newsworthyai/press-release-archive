# Atlanta Community Food Bank Confirms Tyler Perry Donation......Applauds and welcomes all support

ATLANTA, GA, November 10, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Atlanta Community Food Bank is honored by the generous support of filmmaker and philanthropist Tyler Perry. His contribution will have an immediate impact, helping ensure families across our community have access to the nutritious food they need.  "Every donation matters in our mission to help those who are food insecure. The donation by Mr. Perry during this critical time, when need is at an all-time high, WILL change lives. We thank him and all those who are able to donate, from one dollar to the incredibly generous half-million-dollar gift made by Mr. Perry." - Kyle Waide, President & CEO, Atlanta Community Food Bank.  The Food Bank encourages others in the community who are able to give to join in supporting neighbors across metro Atlanta and North Georgia. Donations of any size help provide nutritious food to families who are struggling to keep meals on the table.  To support the Food Bank's mission or learn more about how to get involved, visit ACFB.org.  About Atlanta Community Food Bank  The Atlanta Community Food Bank works to end hunger with the food, people, and big ideas needed to ensure our neighbors have the nourishment to lead healthy and productive lives. Far too many people in our own community experience hunger every day. Through more than 700 community-based, nonprofit partners in 29 counties, we help thousands of families, children, and seniors get healthy food every year. Our goal is that all hungry people across metro Atlanta and north Georgia will have access to the nutritious meals they need when they need them. It takes the power of our whole community to make that possible. In 2023, the Atlanta Community Food Bank distributed its one billionth meal. Join us at ACFB.org.
