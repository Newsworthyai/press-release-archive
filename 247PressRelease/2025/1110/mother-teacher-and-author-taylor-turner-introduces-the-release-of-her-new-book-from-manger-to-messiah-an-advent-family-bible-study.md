# Mother, Teacher, and Author Taylor Turner Introduces the Release of Her New Book, "From Manger to Messiah: An Advent Family Bible Study"

MAGNOLIA, TX, November 10, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Taylor Turner is a Christian mom and science teacher from West Texas with a heart for Jesus and a passion for helping families grow in their faith. With years of experience in education, she believes children should come to know Christ early and is committed to equipping parents to lead with confidence.   Taylor is devoted to bringing the hope of the gospel into everyday life and encouraging families to walk closely with God— together. She believes family Bible study combines faith and fun in a family-friendly experience. "From Manger to Messiah" is a Christmas family Advent Bible study that guides readers through the book of Luke, helping families grow in faith together. Jesus is the greatest gift of all—and what better way to celebrate His birth than by learning about His life and mission?  Designed for families of all ages, this study includes daily prayers, simple guided discussions, and thoughtful questions to help parents confidently lead their children, even if they're still growing in their own faith. With festive recipes, hands-on activities, and a focus on meaningful connection, "From Manger to Messiah" offers more than Bible study—it's a chance to draw closer to God and each other this Christmas season.  A valuable asset that has already received glowing reviews from readers across the US, Taylor's book will educate, support and inspire. "From Manger to Messiah" is a companion book written to bring families closer together and closer to loving Jesus. As His Word fills readers' hearts, their family's prayer life will be transformed!
