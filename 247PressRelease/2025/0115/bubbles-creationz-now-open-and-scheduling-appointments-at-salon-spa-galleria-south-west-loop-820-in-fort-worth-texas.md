# Bubbles Creationz Now Open and Scheduling Appointments at Salon & Spa Galleria South West Loop 820 in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, January 15, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Cosmetologist Sarah Sibley proudly announces the opening of her new salon, Bubbles Creationz, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820 in Fort Worth, Texas.  While she offers a variety of services, Sibley said her specialties are braids and locs, which she learned as a young girl. Opening her own salon is like a dream come true, she added.  "As a child, I was inspired by my sister," Sibley said. "I started out braiding when I was 12 years old. After that, I just took off with it."  Sibley attended Remington College of Cosmetology, and is continuing her education at the Ogle School of Beauty and Cosmetology. She prides herself on understanding the various needs of her clients, and strives to build their confidence while providing a better experience with every visit.  Bubbles Creationz is open from 9 a.m.-6:30 p.m. Tues.-Thurs., 9 a.m.-8 p.m. Friday, and from 9 a.m.-4 p.m. Saturday. Appointments are preferred. Schedule your appointments now by texting or calling (318) 762-5952, or by using Sibley's booking app. Learn more about Bubbles Creationz by visiting Sibley's Instagram page or her TikTok page. To celebrate the shop's grand opening, Sibley is offering a $20 discount off any service to the first five people who book for the new year.  Conveniently located close to numerous businesses, shopping centers, and several major highway interchanges, Bubbles Creationz in the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820 is easily accessible from any direction. There is plenty of free parking for customers, and the building is handicap accessible, with wide hallways.   About Bubbles Creationz Located inside the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820, Bubbles Creationz offers a variety of beauty and hair services, including box braids, kids' braids with beads, weaves, locs, knotless, silk press, and sew-ins.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria South West Loop 820, visit the salon chain's website.
