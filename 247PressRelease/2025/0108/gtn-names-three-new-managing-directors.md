# GTN Names Three New Managing Directors

MINNEAPOLIS, MN, January 08, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Global Tax Network (GTN), a leading mobility tax services firm, has announced the promotions of Raj Azad, Richard Leach, and Tracy Novotny to Managing Director, effective January 1, 2025.   These well-deserved promotions reflect their exceptional contributions to GTN and the impact they've made in delivering outstanding service to our clients. Each of them brings a unique blend of experience, leadership, and commitment to our firm's continued success.  Raj Azad  Azad has been with GTN for over 21 years playing a pivotal role in the firm's expansion. Known for his strong client relationships, he takes pride in delivering customized, responsive service to clients across various industries. His leadership has been instrumental in building trust and long-term partnerships.  "Raj has been a vital member of our team throughout the years," said GTN President, Eric Loff. "His proven leadership and ability to foster meaningful client relationships has been invaluable. I'm eager to see the continued impact he'll make as Managing Director."  Azad expressed his gratitude for the promotion, stating, "I am honored by this promotion and excited for the future of GTN. I look forward to continuing to work alongside our talented team to provide exceptional service to our clients."  Earning his undergraduate degree with honors in accounting from Hansraj College, Delhi University, India, Azad also holds a Master of Business Taxation from the University of Minnesota, Carlson School of Management. He is a Certified Public Accountant. Away from the office, he values time with his wife and three daughters and is an avid tennis player.  Richard Leach  With over two decades of international tax management experience, Leach brings a wealth of expertise to his new role as Managing Director. Since joining GTN, Leach has played a significant role in expanding the private client services and high net-worth practices, while also strengthening the firm's core mobility service offerings.  "Richard has a 'can-do' attitude and ability to focus on what is possible," Loff said. "As he steps into this new role, Richard's leadership will be integral to driving future success."  Expressing his enthusiasm, Leach said "This is an incredible opportunity to contribute in new ways, and I'm eager to help shape the future of the firm alongside my dedicated colleagues."  Leach holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Classics from Bristol University and is an IRS Enrolled Agent. Outside of the office, he enjoys quality time with his family and maintains an active lifestyle, engaging in activities such as distance running, tennis, and skiing.  Tracy Novotny  Novotny brings 18 years of experience in the global mobility tax field, specializing in advisory and compliance services for both corporate and individual clients. She is passionate about identifying continuous process improvements to enhance the customer experience and increase efficiencies.   Loff shared his excitement on Novotny's achievement, stating "Tracy has consistently delivered exceptional results, and this promotion reflects her commitment to excellence and growth. Her strategic insights and leadership have made a lasting impact on our firm, and we look forward to her continued success in this new role."   Reflecting on this milestone, Novotny said, "The opportunity to lead our team through new challenges and help explore new avenues for growth is something I truly look forward to."   Active in the global mobility community, Novotny frequently contributes as a writer and speaker on international assignment topics at local and national events. She earned her undergraduate degree from Metropolitan State University and is a Certified Public Accountant. Away from the office, Novotny enjoys spending time outdoors with her family, embracing seasonal activities throughout the year. She also enjoys reading and learning about history through her travels.  GTN is confident that Azad, Leach, and Novotny will continue to lead with the same dedication, vision, and commitment to excellence that have been key to their success. As Managing Directors, they will play a crucial role in guiding the firm through its next phase of growth and in ensuring GTN remains a trusted partner for clients navigating complex global mobility tax challenges.  ABOUT GLOBAL TAX NETWORK (GTN) GTN is a mobility tax services firm focused on empowering people to live and work anywhere in the world. They help corporate mobility program managers and mobile employees navigate cross-border tax complexities and manage risks. Their scope includes providing support for expatriates, foreign nationals, business travelers (both international and domestic), remote workers, and permanent transfers. GTN is the recipient of several industry awards for excellence, including Accounting Today's "Best Accounting Firms to Work For" and INSIDE Public Accounting's "Best of the Best Firms". GTN is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN, with employees based throughout the United States. For more information, please visit gtn.com.
