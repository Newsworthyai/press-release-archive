Clean and Versatile  From sleek, modern designs for contemporary homes to more ornate patterns suitable for traditional businesses, aluminum gates are incredibly versatile. Sturdy and Durable  One of the most significant advantages of aluminum gates is their low-maintenance requirements. Unlike iron, which requires frequent treatments to prevent rusting, or wood, which rots without frequent painting and staining, aluminum maintains its pristine beauty with occasional hose-downs and wipes. It will not warp, rot, or rust, making it ideal for Vancouver's mild coastal climate.   When to Reconsider Aluminum Gates in Vancouver  While aluminum gates offer many benefits, they may not be suitable for every scenario. Reconsider aluminum gates in the following cases:  Extreme Weight Requirements: Some establishments require exceptionally heavy-duty gates, and may want to opt for materials denser than aluminum. For example, if the purpose is a crash barrier, wrought iron or steel may be a better choice.  Historical Authenticity: In restoration projects where material authenticity is crucial, traditional metals like iron may align more closely with the desired outcomes.  Budget Constraints: Aluminum gates tend to be cost-effective in the long run; however, the initial investment can be higher than for other materials.
