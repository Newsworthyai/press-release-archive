# Suzy Chase Presents 12 Days of Genres, an Audio Advent Calendar from The Groove Radio Podcast

NEW YORK, NY, December 19, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Suzy Chase, a podcasting pioneer and New York City DJ, is releasing a new limited holiday series titled 12 Days of Genres: An Audio Advent Calendar from her long-running music podcast, The Groove Radio.  The series delivers one genre per day across twelve episodes, each anchored by Chase's signature approach to music curation, storytelling, and immersive listening. Rather than relying on algorithms or playlists, the project invites listeners to rediscover music through genre, memory, and mood.  12 Days of Genres continues Chase's commitment to human-led music discovery, a defining element of The Groove Radio since its debut in 2005, and follows her recent anniversary project celebrating two decades of the podcast.
