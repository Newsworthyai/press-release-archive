# Veritas Global Protection Expands Luxury and Exotic Vehicle Protection Plans Across the U.S. and Canada

PHOENIX, AZ, June 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Veritas Global Protection, a recognized leader in the vehicle service contract industry, proudly announces the expansion of its luxury and exotic car protection plans across the United States and Canada. Tailored to meet the unique demands of high-end vehicle owners, the newly enhanced offerings provide superior coverage for some of the world's most prestigious automobile brands. With this move, Veritas strengthens its commitment to delivering trusted, high-quality service in the luxury vehicle protection market.  The new exotic car service contracts from Veritas Global Protection are designed specifically for owners of vehicles that demand the highest level of performance and care. These service plans cover a wide range of major mechanical and electrical components, providing protection against unexpected repair costs that can often reach tens of thousands of dollars for luxury and exotic vehicles. As the costs associated with maintaining premium vehicles continue to rise, Veritas Global Protection ensures that car owners have an affordable and reliable option to safeguard their investment.  Whether driving a performance supercar, a custom luxury SUV, or a collector-grade exotic vehicle, Veritas' clients can choose from multiple levels of coverage, allowing them to select the service contract that best matches their needs and vehicle usage. Each plan comes standard with features such as 24-hour emergency roadside assistance, rental car coverage, and trip interruption benefits, further enhancing the ownership experience and ensuring uninterrupted peace of mind. All contracts are accepted at any ASE-certified mechanic or dealership, providing flexibility and convenience to drivers across North America.  Veritas Global Protection's reputation for excellence is supported by its backing from an A-rated insurer and accreditation with the Better Business Bureau. By expanding its suite of services to include a more extensive lineup of luxury and exotic vehicle protection plans, the company reinforces its leadership role in the high-end vehicle service market. The launch of these new offerings aims to attract luxury car dealerships seeking a trusted partner to protect their clients' investments, as well as affluent individual car owners looking for reliable and comprehensive protection.  As more consumers invest in rare, high-value vehicles, the need for specialized coverage has never been greater. Veritas Global Protection is meeting this demand by combining expertise, superior customer service, and innovative contract solutions that provide unmatched protection in the luxury automotive sector. This expansion further solidifies Veritas' mission to redefine how owners of luxury and exotic vehicles experience vehicle service contract solutions, offering unparalleled peace of mind with every mile.  About Veritas Global Protection: Veritas Global Protection is a leading provider of vehicle service contracts and finance and insurance (F&I) products. Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona, Veritas serves customers worldwide, including the United States, Canada, Latin America, South Africa, and Europe. With a reputation for customer-centric service, flexible protection plans, and a strong foundation backed by an A-rated insurer, Veritas continues to be a trusted name in luxury and exotic vehicle protection. To learn more, visit www.veritasprotection.com.
