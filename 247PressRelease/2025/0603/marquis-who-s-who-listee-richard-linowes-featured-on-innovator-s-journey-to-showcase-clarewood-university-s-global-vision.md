# Marquis Who's Who Listee Richard Linowes Featured on Innovator's Journey to Showcase Clarewood University's Global Vision

RESTON, VA, June 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Richard Linowes, president and founding professor of Clarewood University, was featured on Innovator's Journey, where he shared insights on his transition from investment banking to academia and the innovations he has introduced in business education. From his experiences in Japan and on Wall Street, Mr. Linowes emphasized the value of experiential learning and the necessity of cultivating integrity in future business leaders. Highlighting Clarewood's international student body, Mr. Linowes stressed the power of cross-cultural exchange in preparing students for the global workforce.   Watch the segment here.
