# Marquis Who's Who Listee Lacette Cross Explores Shame, Faith, and Leadership on Innovator's Journey

RICHMOND, VA, June 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Reverend Dr. Lacette Cross, founder of Liberation Lounge and executive director of Diversity Richmond, was featured on Innovator's Journey to discuss how shame impacts self-worth, relationships and leadership. During the segment, Rev. Cross shared how her work helps individuals connect faith, sexuality and leadership to foster wholeness and authenticity. She emphasized that healing begins with the self and extends outward, shaping how we lead and relate to others. "When we live from a place of authenticity, we liberate ourselves and others," Rev.
