# Summer Estate Trunk Show at Huntington Fine Jewelers

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, June 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Oklahoma's premier jeweler, Huntington Fine Jewelers, invites the public to experience the elegance and history of estate jewelry during its highly anticipated Summer Estate Jewelry Trunk Show, taking place throughout June at locations across the state.  The show begins June 3–4 in Shawnee, followed by June 5–6 and June 12–13 in Oklahoma City. The event continues with a single-day showcase June 10 in Yukon, then moves to Norman on June 11, Stillwater on June 17, and concludes with a three-day event June 18–20 in Midwest City.  This exclusive event will feature an exceptionally curated collection of one-of-a-kind estate jewelry that contains timeless pieces that blend vintage charm with unmatched craftsmanship. Guests can explore heirloom-quality rings, necklaces, bracelets, and more at significant savings of up to 50% off. All trunk show items are buy one, get one FREE!   Whether you're a seasoned collector or simply searching for show-stopping jewelry, this trunk show promises a unique shopping experience that cannot be missed. With limited availability and remarkable values, guests are strongly encouraged to RSVP early to ensure access to these extraordinary treasures.  For event details, directions, or to schedule a personal consultation, contact your nearest Huntington Fine Jewelers location in Oklahoma. Their knowledgeable staff is ready to assist with any questions regarding the Summer Estate Trunk Show, professional jewelry services, or their extensive selection of fine jewelry.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers   For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services. For more information visit their website at www.huntingtonfinejewelers.com, send an email to info@huntingtonfinejewelers.com or visit their showrooms in person.
