# Perinatologist Dr. Lama L. Tolaymat named a 2025 Castle Connolly Top Doctor

ORLANDO, FL, June 03, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Lama L. Tolaymat, MD, MPH, has been recognized as a 2025 Castle Connolly Top Doctor â€” the second year she has received the prestigious Top Doctor honor. Only about 7 percent of the nation's licensed physicians are selected annually as Castle Connolly Top Doctors in their regions for their specialties.   Dr. Tolaymat is an obstetrician and gynecologist with advanced fellowship training in Maternal-Fetal Medicine. In 2014, she established Sunshine Perinatology, becoming the first female perinatologist to establish her own private, independent practice in Central Florida.  Dr. Tolaymat's journey was undoubtedly challenging, requiring resilience, determination, and unwavering dedication. The leadership of Aitor Goyarrola was instrumental in the initiatives that have expanded the clinics' services and enhanced the reputation. Goyarrola's business acumen combined with Dr. Tolaymat's expertise created a powerful catalyst for the success of Sunshine Perinatology.   A Fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists, Dr. Tolaymat is dual board-certified in obstetrics & gynecology and Maternal-Fetal Medicine by the American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology.   Dr. Tolaymat specializes in Maternal-Fetal Medicine with a focus on high-risk pregnancies.   The first Syrian woman certified in this field, she has over 20,000 deliveries and consultations across her 28 years of medical expertise.   Dr. Tolaymat is committed to providing personalized, compassionate care. "Every interaction is imbued with warmth and empathy, creating a nurturing environment that fosters trust and healing," she says.   This ethos extends to her dedicated team of professionals, whose unwavering commitment to excellence mirrors Dr. Tolaymat's values.   She is also committed to serving diverse, underserved communities, including embracing telehealth appointments and conducting targeted community outreach initiatives to reach those with geographical constraints.   "Diversity is celebrated as a cornerstone of our identity, reflected in our vibrant and multicultural staff. Our diverse team collaborates seamlessly to deliver personalized care that transcends linguistic and cultural barriers, ensuring that every patient feels valued, respected, and understood," Dr. Tolaymat says.  Dr. Tolaymat serves hospital appointments with AdventHealth Orlando, AdventHealth Tampa, Winnie Palmer Hospital for Women & Babies, and St. Joseph's Hospital.   Dr. Tolaymat earned her medical degree at the University of Florida, Gainesville. She completed a residency at the University of Florida at Sacred Heart Hospital and a fellowship in Maternal-Fetal Medicine at Jackson Memorial Hospital. She also earned a Master of Public Health at the University of Miami.   She has published articles in peer-reviewed journals, contributed to the books Handbook of Obstetric and Gynecologic Emergencies and Conn's Current Therapy, and delivered national and international lectures, earning awards for her presentations. Her accolades include being the inaugural recipient of the Mini-Sabbatical Grant from the Pregnancy Foundation and the Society of Maternal-Fetal Medicine Foundation.   Dr. Tolaymat is a revered educator, earning awards for her teaching prowess and contributing to academic literature used in OB/GYN curricula nationwide. She is a sought-after lecturer, sharing her insights at medical conferences worldwide.  Top Doctors are nominated by peer physicians and selected by the physician-led Castle Connolly research team.   "Being nominated as a Connolly Top Doctor by my peers is an incredible honor that fills me with gratitude and humility," Dr. Tolaymat says. "I believe this recognition reflects not only my dedication to providing the highest standard of care but also my commitment to creating a compassionate and supportive environment for both patients and colleagues. My approach has always centered on personalized patient care, meticulous attention to detail, and keeping up with the latest advancements in Maternal-Fetal Medicine.   "I am deeply appreciative of my peers for acknowledging my efforts and contributions to the field. This recognition motivates me to continue striving for excellence and innovation in patient care. I would not have been able to grow my career without the support of my fellow colleagues and the mentorship of Dr. Rachel Humphery. Receiving this also emphasizes the importance of collaboration and mutual respect within the medical community, as we work together to advance healthcare and improve outcomes for all patients. Thank you for this meaningful acknowledgment as it truly inspires me to keep pushing the boundaries of what we can achieve in medicine."  About Castle Connolly Castle Connolly publishes its Top Doctors list at castleconnolly.com and in a variety of print and online partner publications. Nominations are open to all board-certified MDs, DOs, and DPMs. The Castle Connolly team chooses honorees based on nominee criteria that includes their medical education, training, hospital appointments, and disciplinary histories.  Doctors do not and cannot pay to be selected as a Castle Connolly Top Doctor.
