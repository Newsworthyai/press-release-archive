# EMPOWERED ESSENCE GRAND OPENING AT HILL COUNTRY SALON SUITES

GEORGETOWN, TX, February 19, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Kristinia, the visionary behind Empowered Essence, has dedicated 15 years to mastering the art of hairstyling, with a special focus on all hair textures. Born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana, she spent 17 years in East Texas before making Austin her home for the past three years. Her journey reflects a deep commitment to enhancing natural beauty and empowering clients through personalized care.  The vision: Empowered Essence is not just a hair salon; it's a sanctuary where clients of all ages enjoy personalized services that cater to their individual needs. With Kristinia's mastery as a colorist and her diverse range of services, every client leaves feeling both confident and refreshed.  Each treatment is performed with precise attention to detail, showcasing Kristinia's passion for her work and her commitment to client satisfaction. Empowered Essence is about more than just beauty—it's about nurturing a sense of empowerment and self-assurance in every person who enters.  "We are excited to welcome Kristinia and Empowered Essence to Hill Country Salon Suites. We think of all our tenants as family at Hill Country. Our mission is to support our tenants and help them to prosper", states Melanie.
