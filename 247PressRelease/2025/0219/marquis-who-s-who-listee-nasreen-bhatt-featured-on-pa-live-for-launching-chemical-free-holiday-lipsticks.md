# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Nasreen Bhatt Featured on PA Live! for Launching Chemical-Free Holiday Lipsticks

ALLENTOWN, PA, February 19, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Nasreen Bhatt, visionary founder and CEO of InnerSoul, recently shared the inspiration behind her groundbreaking, Vegan Award-Winning holiday lipstick collection on "PA Live!" This labor of love, nearly a year in the making, is a testament to Ms. Bhatt's unwavering commitment to empowering individuals to embrace their authentic beauty.  The collection's twelve carefully crafted shades are infused with a proprietary, chemical-free formula enriched with nourishing macadamia oil. This intentional blend not only provides a healthy, radiant sheen but also honors the intricate connection between self-care and natural beauty.  As a passionate advocate for holistic well-being, Ms. Bhatt designed InnerSoul's holiday collection to transcend mere aesthetics. Each lipstick serves as a reminder to prioritize self-love, self-acceptance, and mindfulness. By celebrating individuality and promoting a deeper understanding of one's inner beauty, InnerSoul's collection invites wearers to embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery.  "I believe that true beauty radiates from within," says Ms. Bhatt. "Our holiday collection is more than just a range of lipsticks – it's an invitation to reconnect with your inner self, to love and accept yourself exactly as you are."
