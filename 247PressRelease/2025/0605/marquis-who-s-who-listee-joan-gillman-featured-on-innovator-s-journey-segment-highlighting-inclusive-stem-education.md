# Marquis Who's Who Listee Joan Gillman Featured on Innovator's Journey Segment Highlighting Inclusive STEM Education

NEW YORK, NY, June 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Joan Gillman, veteran educator and science teacher at The Browning School, was featured on the Innovator's Journey segment, where she shared insights from her 43-year career. Ms. Gillman reflected on her early inspirations in community service and discussed how she now integrates equity, environmental justice and cultural relevance into a dynamic, project-based science curriculum. Known for fostering creativity and inclusion, she highlighted student-led initiatives like sustainability challenges and multimedia science presentations. A proponent of lifelong learning, Ms. Gillman continues to innovate in the classroom and inspire the next generation of educators and scientists.  Watch the segment here.
