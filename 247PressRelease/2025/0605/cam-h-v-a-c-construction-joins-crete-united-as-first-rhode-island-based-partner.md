# CAM H.V.A.C. &amp; Construction Joins Crete United as First Rhode Island-Based Partner

CHARLOTTE, NC, June 05, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Crete United ("Crete"), a leading provider of energy efficiency and HVAC, electrical, and other mechanical ("MEP") services, has added CAM H.V.A.C. & Construction ("CAM") to the Crete United partner network.  Since their founding in 1974, CAM has offered installation and repair services for public infrastructure projects, school systems, commercial buildings and more, across Southern New England.  "Welcoming CAM into the Crete United network marks a meaningful step in our continued expansion across the Northeast," said Mike Cox, CEO of Crete United. "As our fourth partner in the New England region, and our first in Rhode Island, CAM brings nearly 50 years of experience and a reputation for dependable, high-quality mechanical services. Their in-house sheet metal fabrication capabilities enhance our technical depth, and their values-driven approach strengthens our ability to deliver integrated, high-performance MEP solutions across the region."  With a longstanding reputation for excellence in mechanical construction, CAM is well-positioned to collaborate with Crete United partners, improving project delivery and expanding the company's regional footprint.  "This partnership marks a valuable new chapter for our company and our customers," said Mike Monti, President of CAM H.V.A.C. & Construction. "Joining Crete United allows us to bring our high standards and proven expertise to a broader network, while staying true to our roots in quality service and community impact."  Griffin Financial Group served as financial advisor to CAM H.V.A.C. & Construction.  About Crete United Crete United is a nationwide multi-service building solutions provider with a vast network of local partners who are united in improving the communities they serve and the built environments within. The company provides energy efficiency-focused building services through its partners across non-residential HVAC, electrical, plumbing, building automation, controls, and other sustainable energy solutions. Crete United provides the resources and reach of a large organization while allowing its partners to maintain the freedom to continue operating their successful businesses.
