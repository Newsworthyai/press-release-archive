# BZBGEAR Joins 8K Association at NAB Show 2025 to Demonstrate 8K Ecosystem Advancements

LAS VEGAS, NV, April 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- BZBGEAR®, a leading manufacturer of professional AV and broadcasting solutions, is participating at NAB Show 2025 in Las Vegas, Nevada, from April 6–9 at the Las Vegas Convention Center. BZBGEAR is exhibiting together with the 8K Association (8KA) at West Hall, Booth W3942, to spotlight the future of 8K in pro AV applications.  The 8K Association is a global organization dedicated to promoting the advancement and adoption of 8K technology. And as proud members of the 8K Association, BZBGEAR is spearheading the 8K movement with 8KA. BZBGEAR is excited to embark on their ongoing mission in advancing 8K tech and raising awareness of the rapidly evolving 8K ecosystem.  At NAB Show 2025, BZBGEAR will be showcasing its full complement of 8K Ultra HD gear from award-winning matrix switchers and testers to splitters, extenders, and cables. These solutions are engineered for high-performance installations in environments requiring uncompromising video clarity, bandwidth, and reliability—ideal for control rooms, high-end conference spaces, digital signage networks, and esports arenas providing the ultimate gaming experience for enthusiasts and pro gamers alike.  "8K is no longer a future concept—it is here now and it is transforming how we experience and deliver content," said Eugene Bocharov, CEO and President, BZBGEAR. "Collaborating with the 8KA at this year's NAB Show allows us to demonstrate our latest 8K gear and our commitment to leading the industry into the next generation of pro AV and broadcasting."  A Broad Range of 8K Association Activities  The 8KA will be leading a high-profile presence at NAB Show 2025 with both educational and experiential initiatives.  They will be moderating a panel Discussion titled 8K Technology and its Emerging Role in the Creative Process on April 7 from 10:40 a.m. – 11:10 a.m. at the Creator Lab, Theater A (South Hall – SL9823). Panelists from Warner Bros. Discovery and IMAX will explore 8K's role in film restoration and creative production.  Additionally, at the main booth (W3942) in West Hall, the 8KA will host an Interactive Demonstration Area featuring key contributors BZBGEAR (Pro AV 8K switching and distribution gear), Samsung (8K television displays), AMD (8K 120Hz gaming over HDMI), 4BY4 (AI-powered upscaling and optimization), and WyreStorm (HDMI-certified 8K cables).  "From image capture to distribution, the 8K ecosystem is advancing and providing truly dynamic and immersive viewing experiences," said Juan Reyes, Executive Director, 8K Association. "BZBGEAR and their currently available products are a testament to the high-performance infrastructure vital for tapping into the full potential of 8K in professional environments."   Make sure to visit BZBGEAR and the 8K Association at Booth W3942 in the West Hall. Use guest code NS8345 when registering for free passes. Get ready to discover how BZBGEAR can help make your pro AV systems future-ready today!  About BZBGEAR  Established in 2019 and headquartered in Sacramento, California, BZBGEAR is a leading manufacturer of end-to-end pro AV and broadcasting products. Their mission is to provide top-grade AV and broadcasting solutions focusing on design, user experience, and lifetime product support. Every solution is carefully designed by BZBGEAR hardware and software engineers to ensure unmatched quality and features. With award-winning products and industry-leading support, BZBGEAR is the number one choice for any AV and broadcasting application. Learn more at bzbgear.com.  About The 8K Association  The 8K Association (8KA) has been organized to educate, promote and evangelize on the many aspects of the rapidly evolving 8K ecosystem. From content creation and production to distribution and consumer consumption, their mission is to ensure the 8K ecosystem thrives as they help to accelerate industry-wide adoption of 8K content, products, and services.  The 8KA is composed of key technology companies in the consumer and professional 8K ecosystem. To accomplish their mission, the 8KA has established Work Groups to help provide information about the state of the 8K ecosystem to consumers, organizations, and standards development groups. Learn more at 8kassociation.com.
