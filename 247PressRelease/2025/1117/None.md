# ByAlexdavid Reveals The Secret Business Of AI Influencers

MAKATI, PHILIPPINES, November 17, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- ByAlexdavid, a gaming and tech website that also covers online income ideas, has released a new guide explaining how AI influencers really make money. The article gives readers a clear look at how AI generated characters attract followers, work with brands, and generate income in today's creator economy.  Titled The Secret Business of AI Influencers, the guide explains what AI influencers are, how they are created, and how they differ from human creators. It outlines the typical stages of building an AI character, from design and personality to content planning, and then breaks down how brands decide which virtual profiles to work with. The article also walks through common income paths, including sponsored posts, brand partnerships, and paid communities, so readers can see how money actually flows behind the scenes.  The guide places a strong focus on realistic expectations. It notes that AI tools can speed up content creation, but they do not replace the need for audience research, consistent publishing, and basic planning. Readers are encouraged to view AI influencers as one possible side hustle model, not a guaranteed shortcut to fast income.  "AI tools make it easier to build digital characters that feel real, but that does not guarantee quick results," said Alex David, founder of ByAlexdavid. "The aim of this guide is to cut through vague claims and show what is really happening behind the AI influencer trend, so people can make informed choices before they start."  The guide is aimed at creators, freelancers, and curious readers who see social media content about AI money making strategies and want a grounded explanation before they launch a new project. It also connects to the wider Side Hustle section on ByAlexdavid, where readers can explore other detailed guides on online income ideas.  ByAlexdavid is a gaming and tech site that covers coding projects, creator tools, side hustles, and everyday money topics. The site focuses on clear, practical guides that help readers understand digital tools, online platforms, and realistic ways to earn and manage money.
