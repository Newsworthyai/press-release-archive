# Glatt Kosher NYC Restaurant, Talia's Steakhouse, Will Serve Rosh Hashanah 2025 Meals

NEW YORK, NY, September 02, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- For over two decades, Talia's Steakhouse & Bar, the premier glatt kosher restaruant on the Upper West Side in NYC, has been a cherished venue for families to gather and celebrate Rosh Hashanah.  Rosh Hashanah 2025 begins the evening of September 22nd, and concludes the evening of September 24th.  Talia's traditional, prix fixe Rosh Hashanah feast is a unique experience that features all the symbolic dishes integral to the holiday, including challah, apple & honey, pomegranates and dates. Each table will be provided with the Hebrew and English text of the Rosh Hashanah kiddush and blessings along with easy-to-follow English instructions.  Celebrate this special occasion with your loved ones in a warm inviting atmorsphere, where the energy and ambiance complement the delicious Glatt Kosher cuisine, making Talia's the perfect destination for your holiday celebration.  Talia's follows all strictly Orthodox Jewish law, so no money is exchanged on the holiday, and all reservations must be prepaid.  Talia's also offers delivery, takeout, and full-service catering options. Talia's fast-food sister restaurant, Shawarma Shabazi, also offers catering options. For more information feel free to visit Talia's website: http://taliassteakhouse.com/  Talia's Steakhouse & Bar 668 Amsterdam Ave. New York, NY 10025  For over 23 years, Talia's Steakhouse has continuously reinvented the strictly Glatt kosher restaurant as a hip, modern place, where everyone will feel welcome: from Chabad and Satmer Chasidim to Modern Orthodox, secular Jews, non-Jewish customers and all other ethnic groups.  Talia's is a great Manhattan kosher restaurant to take your wife or girlfriend out to dinner. Enjoy the romantic atmosphere, dimly-lit, candles and fresh roses on each table, mellow live music and a wonderful blend of American and Middle-Eastern cuisines. She will be talking about her special evening for weeks to come. In addition, Talia's offers prepaid Shabbat dinner every week, as well as all major Jewish holiday meals.
