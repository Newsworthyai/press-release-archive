# McClung-Logan Expands with New Full-Service Branch in Aberdeen, MD

BALTIMORE, MD, November 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- McClung-Logan Equipment Company, a leading distributor of heavy and compact construction equipment, is proud to announce the opening of its newest full-service branch in Aberdeen, Maryland. This marks the company's 12th branch further strengthening its presence in the Mid-Atlantic region.  Strategically positioned in Harford County, the Aberdeen branch enhances McClung-Logan's ability to serve its growing customer base throughout northern Maryland. The facility is conveniently located approximately one hour from the company's headquarters in Baltimore, creating a strong regional network to better support customers across the eastern portion of the state.  The 9,360-square-foot facility, set on 3 acres, is located at 629 S. Philadelphia Blvd, Aberdeen, MD 21001 and features: • 4 service bays plus 1 bay designated for PDIs. • Full climate-control throughout the facility. • Fully fenced equipment yard. • Dedicated mobile equipment ramp with adjustable height for easy loading and unloading. • 5,100-square-foot addition expected within six months, featuring:  o 4 additional service bays. o 7.5-ton overhead crane.  The Aberdeen team is led by: • Steven Miller, Territory Manager (Compact) • Jamie Reid, Territory Manager • John Perry, Service Manager • Ron Whittington, Parts Manager • Highly skilled service technicians supporting customers both in the field and on-site  "Our decision to expand into Aberdeen reflects the continued growth and stability of our business, as well as the confidence our customers and manufacturing partners place in us," said Darrin Brown, President of McClung-Logan Equipment Company. "This milestone is driven by the hard work and dedication of our entire team. As we build our presence in the area, our focus remains on providing exceptional service, reliable equipment solutions, and the strong support our customers deserve."  McClung-Logan's Aberdeen location provides sales, rentals, parts, and service for industry-leading manufacturers, including Volvo Construction Equipment, Takeuchi, Mecalac, Gradall, Shuttlewagon, Avant, Prinoth, as well as McClung-Logan's full product line represented in Maryland.  For more information, contact the Aberdeen branch at (410) 803-6750 or visit www.mcclung-logan.com.  Founded in 1939, McClung-Logan Equipment Company is a trusted distributor of heavy and compact construction equipment throughout the Mid-Atlantic region. Across its 11 full-service locations, the company provides sales, rentals, parts, and service support for a comprehensive lineup of industry- leading equipment brands. McClung-Logan is committed to delivering powerful, reliable solutions that drive productivity and performance for its customers.
