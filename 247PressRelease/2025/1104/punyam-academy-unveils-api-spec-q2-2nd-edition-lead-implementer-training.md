# Punyam Academy Unveils API Spec. Q2 (2nd Edition) Lead Implementer Training

LOS ANGELES, CA, November 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Punyam Academy, a leading global e-learning provider specializing in ISO and management systems training, today announced the launch of its new API Spec. Q2 (2nd Edition) Lead Implementer Training course. This comprehensive online program is designed for quality and management professionals in the oil and gas service sector who need to implement the API Specification Q2 Quality Management System (QMS) requirements. The course provides participants with the knowledge and tools to build and manage a robust QMS aligned with API standards. It includes practical resources such as sample quality manuals, process flowcharts, risk assessment worksheets and other templates to facilitate hands-on learning.  API Specification Q2 (2nd Edition) defines stringent QMS requirements for service-supply organizations in the upstream petroleum and natural gas industry. The updated standard emphasizes safety, environmental protection and risk management â€“ critical concerns in drilling, well services, inspection and other oilfield operations. Punyam Academy's new Lead Implementer course covers all key elements of the specification, guiding learners through the plan-do-check-act cycle of system implementation. Topics include the standard's overview, clause-by-clause requirements, key definitions, documentation best practices, and methods for data analysis, risk assessment, service delivery and validation. By the end of the course, participants will understand how to develop the documentation and controls needed for effective API Spec Q2 implementation.  The course provides comprehensive module coverage of all required topics and certification requirements, and is supported by rich downloadable learning materials (detailed handouts and practical templates including sample documents). It is delivered via a flexible, self-paced online format and culminates in an officially recognized certification upon successful completion.  "Quality management is mission-critical in the oil and gas service industry, and the updated API Q2 standard reflects the highest levels of safety and performance," said Devang Jhaveri, Director of Punyam Academy. "Our new Lead Implementer course equips industry professionals to implement API Spec Q2 effectively, reinforcing Punyam Academy's commitment to accredited education and helping organizations achieve compliance and operational excellence under the latest standards."  The API Spec. Q2 Lead Implementer Training adds to Punyam Academy's extensive portfolio of management system programs. The company already offers certified training courses for ISO and API standards, including lead auditor, internal auditor and awareness courses. All Punyam courses are accredited by respected international bodies (such as Exemplar Global and CPD) and meet rigorous quality benchmarks. With a presence in over 100 countries, the academy's content is designed by expert trainers to be practical, accessible and aligned with its own ISO-certified quality processes.
