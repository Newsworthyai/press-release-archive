# Associate Attorney Aiden Lorentzen Joins Nicolet Law

MINNEAPOLIS, MN, November 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Nicolet Law Accident & Injury Lawyers is proud to welcome Associate Attorney Aiden Lorentzen to its personal injury practice. Lorentzen is a recent graduate of the Mitchell Hamline School of Law, where he served on the Mitchell Hamline Law Review.  He was admitted to the Minnesota Bar this October, and his passion for serving his home state is clear.  "I've lived in Minnesota all my life, so I know what it means to be a Minnesotan," Lorentzen says. "I went to high school, undergrad, and law school in different parts of the state, giving me insight into all parts of my home state. To me, this makes representing my fellow Minnesotans all the more rewarding."  While Lorentzen will be working on "a little bit of everything," he anticipates working primarily on car accident cases. He is a welcome addition to Nicolet Law's growing practice.  About Nicolet Law Accident & Injury Lawyers:  Nicolet Law is a respected personal injury law firm. Committed to standing up for the rights of injured individuals, Nicolet Law has built a reputation for fierce advocacy and personalized client care.
