# Simon &amp; Simon Launches "English for Impact" Open Workshop Series to Empower Global Professionals

LONDON, UK, November 30, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Simon & Simon International, a leading provider of bespoke language and communication training, successfully hosted the first session in its new Open Workshop Series, titled English for Impact.   This live online event brought together professionals from diverse sectors to strengthen their ability to communicate with clarity, confidence, and influence in global business settings. The 75-minute interactive workshop focused on practical strategies for:  - Expressing ideas persuasively in meetings and presentations.  - Adapting tone and style for different audiences. - Building confidence when collaborating across cultures.  "Strong communication skills are no longer optional, they're essential for success in today's global workplace," said Corinne Taylor, Head of Corporate Clients at Simon & Simon. "Our Open Workshop Series is designed to give professionals actionable tools they can apply immediately, whether they're leading meetings, negotiating deals, or managing international teams."  This inaugural session marks the beginning of a series aimed at helping businesses and individuals elevate their English communication skills. Future workshops will cover topics such as English for Meetings, Cross-Cultural Communication, and Impactful Writing for Business.  Why It Matters:  With English as the lingua franca of global business, professionals who can communicate effectively gain a competitive edge. Simon & Simon's workshops provide a cost-effective, high-impact solution for organisations seeking to improve team performance and confidence.  Upcoming Sessions:  The next workshop in the series will take place in February 2026. Details and registration will be available soon at www.simonandsimon.co.uk.  About Simon & Simon International  Simon & Simon International has been delivering tailored language and communication training for over 25 years. With a network of 4,500+ expert trainers and innovative tools like AI-powered language assessments, the company helps global teams achieve measurable results in clarity, confidence, and cultural competence.
