# Springer School and Center Adds Parent Coaching to Their Services

BELLINGHAM, WA, May 09, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Parent Coaching Institute (PCI) enthusiastically announces that PCI Certified Parent Coach®, Emily Schweppe will offer parent coaching services at the Springer School and Center. Located in Cincinnati, Ohio, Springer's mission is to empower students with learning disabilities to lead successful lives. Their specialized curriculum and personalized learning strategies help students with learning disabilities build the educational, emotional, and social skills to succeed in the classroom—and everywhere else.  "I'm so thrilled to be partnering with Springer School and Center as a Parent Coach," states Mrs. Schweppe. "With over 16 years of experience in child development, education, and family support, I'm passionate about partnering with caregivers as a compassionate thinking partner—offering encouragement, insight, and support to help make the parenting journey become a little less stressful and a lot more joyful."  Gloria DeGaetano, Founder and CEO of the Parent Coaching Institute where Emily attended and received her Parent Coach Certification® couldn't be more thrilled as well. "Whenever any PCI graduates are selected by a school or organization to offer parent coaching services, I know everyone benefits. We train family support professionals in a strength-based coaching approach that is highly effective, even life changing. PCI's coaching model fits well with Springer's mission."  Carmen Mendoza, Springer's Director of Growth and Enrollment adds, "Emily's presence as a Parent Coach offers our community a meaningful new layer of support—one that honors parents' wisdom while equipping them with tools to navigate challenges with confidence and connection."  Mrs. Schweppe explains, "What makes the role of parent coach at Springer especially meaningful to me is their clear and consistent commitment to supporting the whole child and the whole family. From specialized academic programs and diagnostic evaluations to referral services and parent support offerings, the school provides a wide range of meaningful resources designed to help children thrive and ensure families feel connected, supported, and empowered."  In addition to offering parent coaching services, Mrs. Schweppe will provide parent talks and workshops as well as contribute blog posts to their website. Her most recent post, What You Focus on Grows, gives practical suggestions to free parents from negative feedback loops when correcting their children.  "I highly respect Emily's expertise and professionalism," explains Ms. DeGaetano. "At PCI we understand the necessity of and power in diversity. We believe that parents who embrace their unique strengths will naturally discover more joy and ease in their parenting. Emily's approach meets parents where they are at. And this is exactly what the PCI coaching process is all about. When we help parents grow and apply their distinctive talents and abilities, they experience more energy within themselves and many more life-affirming moments with their kids."  "And, of course, as Emily says, 'When parents feel supported and connected, kids thrive—because they feel supported and connected, as well.'"   Recognized by Life Coach Magazine as the overall front runner for parent coach training, PCI attracts highly skilled professionals to its year-long Parent Coach Certification® program. Offered in affiliation with Southern New Hampshire University, PCI's acclaimed distance-learning, graduate-level training program, enrolls family support professionals through an application process. Applications are now being accepted for the 2025 Summer Term, to begin June 18. Application deadline is May 20. For more information or to download an application form, please visit www.ThePCI.org.  About The Parent Coaching Institute The Parent Coaching Institute (The PCI) offers Parent Coach Certification® for family professionals, coaching services for moms and dads, and specialized programs for companies and organizations. Founded in 2000 by internationally acclaimed educator, Gloria DeGaetano, PCI originated the parent coaching industry. Today it continues to set the highest standards for Parent Coach Certification® and for parent coaching services worldwide. The PCI is headquartered in Bellingham, Washington. For more information, please visit www.ThePCI.org or call 425-449-8877.
