# Windes Launches Human Capital Consulting Services

LONG BEACH, CA, October 01, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Windes, a premier advisory and accounting firm, has introduced a dedicated Human Capital Consulting practice to support organizations in managing their most valuable resource: their people. The offering provides strategic and operational HR guidance for businesses aiming to strengthen hiring practices, navigate compliance requirements, and build engaged, high-performing teams.  Windes Human Capital Consulting delivers hands-on support across areas such as recruitment strategy, employee handbook development, training programs, compensation planning, regulatory compliance, and organizational development. The new practice is designed for companies seeking flexible, expert HR support without the overhead of a full-time department.  "HR challenges can affect every part of a business, from compliance risk to employee morale," said Managing Partner Sean McFerson. "Our Human Capital Consulting practice offers tailored support to help clients overcome those challenges and build a people strategy that supports long-term growth."  Leading the new offering is Eileen K. Harris, Esq., Chief Human Resources Officer and HR Consultant at Windes. "Whether a company is scaling quickly or reassessing its workplace policies, we provide practical, experience-driven solutions," said Harris. "From executive searches to compliance audits and employee engagement initiatives, we help organizations strengthen their foundation and operate with confidence."  To learn more about how Human Capital Consulting can help support your workforce strategy, visit windes.com/advisory/human-resources-consulting-placement/ or contact us at eharris@windes.com.  About Windes  Windes is a leading advisory, audit, and tax firm for growth-oriented small and mid-sized privately held companies, nonprofit organizations, and high-net-worth individuals. Our approach uses tailored expertise to proactively inform decision-making to maximize our clients' business potential. For more information on how we can be your trusted advisor, visit us at windes.com.
