# ROONYX Strengthens BNPL Industry Ties at LatBAN Annual Event

ST. PETERSBURG, FL, May 16, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- ROONYX Inc. is pleased to announce the participation of its Founder and CEO, Vladimir Rogan, in the annual Latvian Business Angels Network (LatBAN) event, honoring Latvia's "Investor of the Year." Bringing together over 300 participants, this high-profile gathering in Riga attracts a focused audience of BNPL innovators, digital commerce leaders, and early-stage tech investors from across the Baltic and wider European region.  As a company deeply embedded in the Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) sector, ROONYX sees the LatBAN event as a pivotal moment to strengthen ties with investment partners and industry peers who are actively shaping the future of deferred payment technologies and intelligent consumer financing. "ROONYX is here not only to connect, but to contribute to the broader BNPL conversation unfolding across Europe."  At the heart of ROONYX's offering is a proprietary BNPL platform designed to provide flexible, secure, and transparent purchase experiences for merchants and financial institutions. As the company scales operations across key European markets, participation in investor-focused events such as LatBAN is central to forging meaningful partnerships that support rapid growth and innovation.  With its strong focus on next-generation commerce models, ROONYX remains committed to advancing the BNPL landscape through collaborative engagement and real-world, user-centric solutions.
