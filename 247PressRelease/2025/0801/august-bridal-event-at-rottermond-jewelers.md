# August Bridal Event at Rottermond Jewelers

MILFORD and BRIGHTON, MI, August 01, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- This August, discover the perfect symbol of your love during Rottermond Jewelers' exclusive bridal event featuring the Michael M Bridal Trunk Show at their Milford and Brighton jewelry stores.  Explore a stunning array of engagement rings and wedding bands, including designs from Michael M, a celebrated designer known for sleek modern lines, handcrafted details, and timeless elegance.  Whether you're looking for a classic solitaire or a bold, contemporary design, this event offers an unmatched opportunity to find a ring that reflects your unique style and story.  Enjoy limited-time event savings: • Save $100 on purchases of $1,000 • Save $350 on purchases of $3,000 • Save $700 on purchases of $5,000 • Save $1,000 on purchases of $7,500 or more  To make the day even more memorable, the first ten guests who schedule and attend an appointment will receive a $50 gift card to either Caio Amici or Pettibone to toast the moment with a romantic dinner.  This one-of-a-kind trunk show brings together luxury, design, and personal service in an intimate setting designed to help you find the ring.  Appointments are strongly recommended to receive individualized attention and exclusive access to Michael M styles available only during this special event.  Visit Rottermond Jewelers in Milford or Brighton to book your appointment and begin the next chapter of your love story.  About Rottermond Jewelers  Since their establishment in 1984, Rottermond Jewelers has served as a trustworthy purveyor of all things fine jewelry and luxury watches for the people in Milford and Brighton, Michigan. Rottermond Jewelers is renowned for their unparalleled inventory, top-notch customer care, expert custom jewelry design, and other services. The retailer is an active member of their community and for more than 35 years has donated over $500,000 to local charities and non-profit organizations such as the Pink Fund, the Michigan chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and ALS of Michigan, Inc. Get in touch with the jewelry retailer by sending an email to info@rottermond.com or by calling (248) 685-1150 for their Milford location and (810) 220-0015 for their Brighton location!
