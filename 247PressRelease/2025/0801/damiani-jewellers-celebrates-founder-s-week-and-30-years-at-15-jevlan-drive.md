# Damiani Jewellers Celebrates Founder's Week and 30 Years at 15 Jevlan Drive

WOODBRIDGE, ON, August 01, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- From Saturday, August 9 through Saturday, August 16, Damiani Jewellers will be celebrating 30 years in their 15 Jevlan Drive showroom and 68 overall years bringing incredible fine jewellery to the Woodbridge community. Join the jewellers throughout the week for giveaways, specially priced items, event sales, and ice cream on one of the days.  It is an honour for the family-owned and operated jewellery store to continue to serve the Woodbridge community after over half a century in business. With 30 of those years spent in their beautiful showroom, their third-generation family maintains the legacy of luxury they have developed and their clients have learned to expect.   The Founder's Day celebration week kicks off on Saturday, August 9, when they will give away free jewellery pouches with every purchase. Make sure to stop by throughout the week as there will be specially priced items and surprise sales of beautiful jewellery and accessories every day. To cap off the week, an ice cream truck will be parked out front of Damiani Jewellers' 15 Jevlan Drive all day August 16 handing out free ice cream! Follow along with Damiani Jewellers on Instagram at @damianijewellers to learn more details as they release them!  Help Damiani Jewellers celebrate their anniversaries while also saving on beautiful pieces of luxury for yourself or someone you care about. To learn more about their fine jewellery selection, professional jewellery services, or about the events happening during Founder's Week, contact their Woodbridge jewellery store, or give them a call at 905-850-4653, to speak with one of their Damiani professionals.  About Damiani Jewellers With over 60 years of experience serving clients with their fine jewellery expertise, Damiani Jewellers is the Greater Toronto area's premier jewellery retailer. For three generations, their family-owned business has provided engagement rings, wedding bands, loose diamonds, fine jewellery items, and luxury watches for their customers' most treasured moments. Damiani Jewellers is also proud to be an authorized dealer of authentic Rolex watches, and this accomplishment speaks to the unbeatable customer service that each shopper receives. To learn more about the products and services offered at their showroom in Woodbridge, Ontario visit their website, call (905) 850-4653, email info@damianijewellers.com, or stop by their store in person.
