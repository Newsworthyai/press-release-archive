# Monroe Criminal Defense and Family Law Firm Wosnik Law, LLC Brings 40 Years of Combined Experience to Georgia Clients

MONROE, GA, December 12, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Wosnik Law, LLC, a criminal defense and family law firm located in Monroe, Georgia (see Google Maps), is proud to announce its commitment to providing exceptional trial-level legal services to clients facing serious criminal charges and family law disputes. With 40 years of combined experience, the attorneys at Wosnik law have a proven track record of achieving favorable outcomes, including not guilty verdicts and dismissals of charges.  Wosnik Law focuses on criminal defense, particularly serious cases such as DUI, battery, drug possession, and homicide. The firm also offers legal services for family law cases including divorce, child custody, legitimation, and temporary protective orders. As an experienced trial lawyer, Peter J. Wosnik has built a reputation for aggressively defending his clients' rights and ensuring the best possible outcomes. His history includes numerous victories in jury trials, dismissals of charges, and favorable plea agreements, showcasing his exceptional skill in litigation and negotiations.  In 2025 alone, Wosnik Law secured three consecutive not guilty verdicts in jury trials, solidifying their reputation as a firm that never backs down in the face of challenging cases. "At Wosnik Law, we dedicate ourselves to helping clients who are facing some of the most critical challenges of their lives. Our focus is always on achieving the best possible result for each person we represent, whether that's through a not guilty verdict, plea reduction, or securing favorable terms in family law matters."  With a legal career spanning multiple successful outcomes in high‑stakes cases, Peter Wosnik is recognized as one of Georgia's rising legal stars. He was named to Super Lawyers' Rising Stars list 2023–2025 and earned a 10/10 AVVO rating for his criminal defense work.  Wosnik Law, LLC is a full-service criminal defense and family law firm based in Monroe, Georgia. The firm, led by attorney Peter J. Wosnik, offers 40 years of combined experience in defending clients against serious criminal charges and advocating for families in legal disputes such as divorce and child custody. With a focus on trial-level cases and a proven history of not guilty verdicts and favorable outcomes, Wosnik Law is committed to protecting the rights and futures of clients throughout Walton County and the surrounding areas.
