# NetCom Learning Unveils Multi‑Cloud Generative AI Training Bundles for Enterprise Teams

NEW YORK, NY, October 25, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a leading provider of IT and business training solutions, today announced new Multi‑Cloud Generative AI Bundles that package authorized trainings from AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, and AI CERTs into outcome‑driven learning paths. Designed for executive decision‑makers, technical builders, and everyday knowledge workers, the bundles help organizations upskill end‑to‑end—from AI strategy and governance to hands‑on building and day‑to‑day productivity—backed by NetCom Learning's multi‑modal delivery and advisory services. NetCom Learning offers free consultations and tailored Gen AI plans to align programs to each organization's goals.   What's included in the bundles:  1. AWS GenAI + Google Gemini Bundle: A strategy‑to‑productivity path pairing with foundational Gen AI training for business leaders with hands‑on Gemini enablement for day‑to‑day work.  2. Generative AI + AWS Cloud Practitioner Essentials: To build GenAI foundations on AWS while covering cloud core services and Cloud Practitioner exam readiness.  3. Multi‑Cloud Fundamentals (AI Cloud Mastery — Plus Any 2 Hyperscaler Tracks: AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud): A three‑course pack to establish cross‑cloud and AI literacy for broad teams.   Limited-Time Offer: All bundles are available at a discounted price until 31st December 2025.   Who should enroll  1. Executives & Business Leaders shaping AI strategy, governance, and value realization  2. Developers, Data & AI Practitioners building and deploying multi‑cloud Gen AI solutions  3. IT, Security & Compliance professionals establishing controls and responsible AI practices  4. Knowledge Workers & PMOs adopting everyday AI tools to improve productivity   Outcomes your teams can expect  1. Strategy to execution: connect leadership strategy to practical enablement and build cycles  2. Multi‑cloud fluency: upskill across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud to choose the right tool for the job  3. Responsible AI & governance: incorporate safety, security, and compliance practices from day one  4. Measurable impact: align learning to KPIs (velocity, quality, cost), with certification paths where applicable   Recent events, such as the October 2025 AWS outage that temporarily disrupted major websites and enterprise services have underscored the growing need for multi-cloud resilience. Organizations are realizing that relying on a single cloud provider can create operational risks—especially as AI workloads expand. NetCom Learning's Multi-Cloud Generative AI Bundles equip teams to design, deploy, and manage AI solutions seamlessly across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, ensuring continuity, agility, and smarter workload distribution in any scenario.  Delivery & learner experience  1. Live virtual (vILT) training to fit global teams and schedules.  2. Program management & portals to streamline enrollments, track progress, and report outcomes.   Availability & enrollment  The Multi‑Cloud Generative AI Bundles are available immediately with flexible scheduling, global delivery, and volume pricing.
