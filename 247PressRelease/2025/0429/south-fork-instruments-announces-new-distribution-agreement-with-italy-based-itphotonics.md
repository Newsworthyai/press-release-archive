# South Fork Instruments Announces New Distribution Agreement with Italy-based ITPhotonics

AUBURN, CA, April 29, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- South Fork Instruments, an established supplier of measurement instrumentation, analyzers, and control products serving the Americas, today announced a new distribution agreement with ITPhotonics, an Italian company specializing in spectrophotometric solutions, electronic design, and software development.  This strategic partnership will expand South Fork Instruments' product portfolio to include ITPhotonics' innovative Polispec line of multifunction spectrophotometers, bringing advanced analytical capabilities to industrial, agricultural, and chemical sectors across the Americas.  "This collaboration with ITPhotonics aligns perfectly with our mission to identify, develop, and implement innovative measurement solutions for our customers," said John Daly, Technical Director at South Fork Instruments. "Their expertise in spectrophotometry and applied electronics complements our existing offerings and allows us to provide even more comprehensive solutions to address complex measurement challenges."  Founded in 2012, ITPhotonics has built a reputation for versatile, multisectoral approaches to spectrophotometric applications. Their Polispec instruments feature industrial design that is compact, innovative, and designed for portable laboratory use or integration into industrial processes. The technology enables both quantitative and qualitative measurements through the interaction of light sources with molecules and chemical bonds.  The distribution agreement grants South Fork Instruments rights to distribute ITPhotonics products throughout the Americas, enhancing their existing portfolio of flow measurement solutions, process analytics, level measurement solutions, and foam measurement solutions. Key benefits to South Fork Instruments' customers include:  Immediate operational efficiency with ITPhotonics' pre-set calibration curves that require no further interventions  Access to customized applications and calibrations designed specifically for diverse industry needs in the Americas market  Support from specialized technical staff from both companies to create tailored solutions for specific customer requirements  Cutting-edge software systems from ITPhotonics optimized for both portable field use and industrial plant integration  The ITPhotonics product line is available now through South Fork Instruments.  About South Fork Instruments: South Fork Instruments, based in Auburn, Calif., is an established supplier of measurement instrumentation, analyzers, and control products serving the Americas. The company's cross-industry experience translates into a unique ability to identify, develop, and implement innovative measurement solutions. The state-of-the-art instrumentation products provide reliable, low maintenance, high-value results for plant control and quality improvement for a wide range of industry segments, including chemical plants, breweries, refineries, mining, power generation, food processing, offshore/onshore oil production, and pipelines.
