# Windes Welcomes Maddy Rozo (Sander) as Tax Partner

LONG BEACH, CA, January 27, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Windes is pleased to announce that Maddy Rozo (Sander) has joined the firm as a Partner in its Tax department, effective January 13.  "Joining Windes is an exciting opportunity to collaborate with an exceptional team," says Maddy. "I look forward to fostering strong relationships and contributing to the continued growth of the firm."  Maddy brings more than a decade of experience in tax compliance, planning, and consulting. She specializes in residential and commercial real estate and has extensive knowledge of the tax implications of real estate transactions, credit and deduction opportunities, including purchases and sales of land and/or buildings, ยง1031 Like-Kind Exchanges, partnership interest transfers and purchases, Opportunity Zone Credits, Low-Income Housing Credits, and cost segregation studies. Maddy also provides tax compliance and planning services to high-net-worth individuals, including real estate professionals.  Maddy holds a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration with a concentration in Accounting from California State University, Fullerton. She is a Certified Public Accountant and an active member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the California Society of Certified Public Accountants (CalCPA).  "Maddy's technical expertise and strategic leadership represent a significant addition to our team," says Managing Partner Sean McFerson. "Her skills will strengthen our ability to deliver exceptional tax services and drive innovation within our practice."  About Windes  Windes is a leading advisory, audit, and tax firm for growth-oriented small and mid-sized privately held companies, nonprofit organizations, and high-net-worth individuals. Our approach uses tailored expertise to proactively inform decision-making to maximize our clients' business potential. For more information on how we can be your trusted advisor, visit us at windes.com.
