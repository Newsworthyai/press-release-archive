# NetCom Learning to Highlight AI-Powered Enterprise Learning Solutions as Platinum Learning Partner at Cisco Live 2025

NEW YORK, NY, June 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a premier provider of IT training and certification solutions, today announced its participation as a Platinum Learning Partner at Cisco Live 2025, taking place June 8-12 in San Diego, California. The company will showcase its comprehensive suite of Cisco learning solutions and demonstrate how organizations can leverage AI-powered NetOps and strategic upskilling initiatives to drive digital transformation.   At Cisco Live 2025, NetCom Learning will highlight its expertise in delivering enterprise-grade training solutions that align with Cisco's unified approach to networking, security, observability, and collaboration. The company's thought leaders will engage with attendees in discussions on next-generation workforce development strategies and the critical role of continuous learning in today's rapidly evolving technology landscape.   "As organizations navigate an increasingly complex digital ecosystem, the need for skilled professionals who can harness the power of AI-driven network operations has never been greater," said Russell Sarder, CEO of NetCom Learning. "Our partnership with Cisco enables us to deliver cutting-edge training solutions that empower organizations to maximize their technology investments and accelerate their digital transformation journeys."   NetCom Learning will demonstrate several key solution offerings at the conference, including:   Cisco Learning Credits (CLC) Management: Comprehensive solutions for redeeming, purchasing, and managing CLCs to enhance workforce technical competencies and maximizing ROI   Cisco Continuing Education Programs: Structured pathways for professionals to gain new skills while maintaining their certifications  Expert-Led Cisco Training: Instructor-led courses spanning Security, DevNet, Enterprise, and other core technology tracks   Cisco U. Platform: Personalized learning paths with unlimited hands-on lab access and community-driven continuous growth opportunities   The NetCom Learning leadership team attending Cisco Live 2025 includes Mary Rose Feeney, VP of Vendor Relations and Product Management, and Georgia Liberatos, Chief Operating Officer. Both executives will be available for meetings with partners, customers, and industry professionals interested in exploring strategic learning and digital evolution initiatives.   NetCom Learning's participation in Cisco Live 2025 underscores the company's commitment to advancing the IT skills landscape and supporting organizations in their journey toward digital excellence. The company's comprehensive approach to learning combines technical expertise with business strategy, ensuring that training investments deliver measurable results.   Conference attendees are invited to connect with NetCom Learning representatives to discuss customized training solutions, certification pathways, and strategic learning initiatives. The company will also share trends and insights through various conference activities and networking opportunities.   Conference Registration: Organizations interested in connecting with NetCom Learning at Cisco Live 2025 can register here for the conference or contact the company directly to schedule meetings with the leadership team.  NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations in the workplace by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth. With more than 25 years of experience, NetCom Learning has been empowering innovative learning organizations to adapt and drive growth in this fast-paced world by closing critical skill gaps and ensuring smooth deployment, implementation, and consumption through authorized training delivered by Certified Trainers on vendors such as Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, AI Certs, EC-Council, CompTIA, and more.
