# Marquis Who's Who Listee Nanette Yabiku Featured on Studio 17 Live to Discuss Leadership in Education

BAKERSFIELD, CA, March 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Nanette Yabiku, an educator and leadership expert, joined Studio 17 Live to discuss how brain science enhances teaching effectiveness. A former teacher and academic coach, Ms. Yabiku's journey into leadership began when she sought to improve classroom influence. Through studying under John Maxwell and delving into neuroscience, she discovered how personal growth impacts leadership. Now, she coaches teachers and parents, helping them refine communication and leadership skills to foster positive learning environments. Ms. Yabiku continues her mission to serve educators, offering tailored training sessions to strengthen their classroom presence and reduce stress.  Watch the segment here.
