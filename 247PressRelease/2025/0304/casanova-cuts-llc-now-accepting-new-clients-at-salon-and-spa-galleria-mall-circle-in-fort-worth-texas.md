# Casanova Cuts LLC Now Accepting New Clients at Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth, Texas

FORT WORTH, TX, March 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Licensed Beautician Selena Casanova Bijarro proudly announces the opening of Casanova Cuts LLC, located in the Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth, Texas.  With more than eight years of experience in the industry, Bijarro picked up her skills while attending beauty school and honed them while working in salons.  "One day, I decided to go to cosmetology school, and the rest is history," Bijarro said. "And I have loved every moment behind the chair."  After attending the Texas College of Cosmetology in San Angelo, Texas, Bijarro earned her license, and has continued her education to keep her skills sharp. To make her clients feel at home, Bijarro said her goal is to make her clients feel at home. That's why she believes it's important to get to know them and learn what they want.  "I ask every client what they like about their hair and if there is anything they want to change," Bijarro said. "I ask about their hair goals, and we discuss our choices, so I can help them achieve their desired look.  Bijarro offers a wide selection of services for men, women and children, including shampoo and styling; cuts; perms; color; highlights; balayage; hair extensions and hair tinsel; deep condition treatments; and eyebrow waxing.  Casanova Cuts LLC is open from 9 a.m.-2:30 p.m. Monday-Friday, and Saturdays by appointment only. Schedule your appointments through the booking site, or by calling or texting Bijarro at (432) 312-9538.   In close proximity to several large businesses and shopping areas, Casanova Cuts LLC at the Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle in Fort Worth is a convenient location for clients. Serving the Ridgemar and Ridglea areas of Fort Worth, Salon and Spa Galleria Mall Circle is close to several major interchanges and roadways, allowing easy access from any direction.
