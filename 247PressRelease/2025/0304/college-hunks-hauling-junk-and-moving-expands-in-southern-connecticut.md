# COLLEGE HUNKS HAULING JUNK AND MOVING EXPANDS IN SOUTHERN CONNECTICUT

DANBURY, CT, March 04, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- College HUNKS Hauling Junk and Moving®, an award-winning company and industry leader in stress-free solutions for junk removal and moving needs, is expanding its services in Southern Connecticut. College HUNKS franchisees Joshua and Kylie Harrington are now offering the company's efficient and convenient moving, junk hauling, donation pickups and general labor assistance to Fairfield, Litchfield and New Haven Counties.   The local team provides tailored moving and junk hauling services to fit each client's needs whether it's a full-service relocation, trash pickup, furniture staging, vehicle loading, and more. All moving costs are provided upfront so there are no surprise fees. College HUNKS signature fast, friendly offerings are available for area residents and businesses alike.   "Kylie and I have worked hard to establish College HUNKS as a trusted resource in Western Connecticut, and have had the goal to expand our business into new areas since we started," said Joshua Harrington, franchise owner of the Danbury and Southern Connecticut locations. "We are excited to bring College HUNKS to new communities in Southern Connecticut and look forward to continuing to give back to our areas."   In addition to being franchise owners, Joshua works as an emergency room nurse and Kylie is a local real estate agent. Both have high-pressure jobs and understand how to handle challenges with efficiency and care, making them the perfect moving and junk hauling experts. As a real estate agent, Kylie also understands the ins and outs of transitioning to a new home, and operating as a College HUNKS franchisee is a natural extension of her job.   "Joshua and Kylie have been star franchisees for us growing a successful business in the Danbury area," said Nick Friedman, Co-Founder and Visionary of College HUNKS. "We were thrilled they wanted to expand their business into the Southern part of the state and know they are the right partners to do so."   College HUNKS is a purpose-driven company dedicated to being a part of the communities it serves. College HUNKS recently celebrated donating 5 million meals to U.S. Hunger by donating two meals for every moving or junk-hauling job completed. Locally, Joshua and Kylie have partnered with Off the Streets, an organization that helps the homeless, by providing furniture for individuals transitioning into permanent housing.   You can find the Danbury-area and Southern Connecticut College HUNKS at 3 Production Drive, Unit 2 in Brookfield, CT. Or for more information, visit https://www.collegehunkshaulingjunk.com/danbury/ or call 860.530.5178.  About College HUNKS Hauling Junk and Moving  College HUNKS Hauling Junk & Moving® was originally founded by two college buddies with a beat-up cargo van and now has more than 200 franchises providing full-service tech-enabled residential and commercial moving, junk removal, donation pickups, and labor services in the United States as well as Canada. H.U.N.K.S., which stands for Honest, Uniformed, Nice, Knowledgeable, Service, is a socially conscious, values-based organization and has a national partnership with U.S. Hunger, which strives to end childhood hunger. The brand was awarded the Community Hero award by U.S. Hunger in 2022. The company strives to become recognized as an iconic brand, renowned for its world-class company culture and service, while also providing a viable employment and franchise opportunity to pursue personal and professional fulfillment.   For more information, visit www.collegehunkshaulingjunk.com.
