# Award-Winning Documentary Where the Horses Heal the Soul Screens in Austin June 16 to Support Equine Assisted Services for Veterans, Kids &amp; Adults Facing Physical &amp; Emotional Challenges

AUSTIN, TX, June 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Directed by Twila LaBar, the film follows four people at ROCK: two military veterans navigating PTSD and combat injuries, a teenager with Down syndrome stepping into her confidence, and a young woman with cerebral palsy with a passion for advocacy. Through equine assisted services and their bond with horses, each discovers new strength, confidence, and purpose.  "This film is about people who refuse to be defined by diagnosis or labels," says LaBar. "Their stories show what healing looks like when hope is restored. Horses are an incredible catalyst in that process."  Where the Horses Heal the Soul has been recognized both nationally and internationally, including official selections at the 2025 Waco Independent Film Festival and Germany's Equinale Film Festival. The film has also earned top honors at several major festivals:  Best Inspirational Feature – Tennessee International Indie Film Festival Winner – Horse and Healing Best Documentary Over 60 Minutes and Best Director, Horse and Healing Documentary Over 60 Minutes – EQUUS Film & Arts Festival Best Documentary Feature – Crown Point International Film Festival (Best of Fest Awards) Grand Jury Award, Best Documentary Feature – New York International Film Festival Best Documentary Feature, Best Woman Filmmaker – Vegas Movie Awards  EVENT DETAILS What: Where the Horses Heal the Soul – Documentary Screening & Benefit for ROCK Ride On Center for Kids non-profit When: Monday, June 16, 2025 | 6:30 PM (Doors open at 6 PM) Where: AFS Cinema, 6259 Middle Fiskville Rd, Austin, TX Tickets: https://bit.ly/4jGVUca Proceeds Benefit: ROCK (Ride On Center for Kids) Presented by: Rotary Club of Austin University Area  Media Contact: Twila LaBar   Website: https://wherethehorseshealthesoul.com
