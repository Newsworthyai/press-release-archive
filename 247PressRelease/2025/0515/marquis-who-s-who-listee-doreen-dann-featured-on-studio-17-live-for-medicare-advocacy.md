# Marquis Who's Who Listee Doreen Dann Featured on Studio 17 Live for Medicare Advocacy

BAKERSFIELD, CA, May 15, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Doreen Dann, a Marquis Who's Who listee and owner of DLD Healthcare Insurance Services, appeared on Studio 17 Live to share expert tips on navigating Medicare and insurance choices. A longtime health care executive, Ms. Dann launched her business after encountering a Medicare enrollment error following her husband's death. Now a licensed agent, she helps clients avoid similar pitfalls. Ms. Dann's patient storytelling practice, created during her husband's critical care, was later adopted nationwide, reflecting her commitment to compassionate, informed care.
