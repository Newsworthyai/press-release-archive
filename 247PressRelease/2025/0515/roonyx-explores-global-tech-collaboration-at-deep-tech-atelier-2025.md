# ROONYX Explores Global Tech Collaboration at Deep Tech Atelier 2025

ST. PETERSBURG, FL, May 15, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Deep Tech Atelier is a leading platform uniting scientists, startups, policymakers, and investors to accelerate breakthroughs in fields such as AI, robotics, quantum computing, and biotechnology. The 2025 edition will bring together top minds from across Europe and beyond to explore how deep technologies can shape our collective future.  Vladimir Drogan's participation highlights ROONYX's continued commitment to pioneering advanced digital solutions and engaging with the global deep tech ecosystem. With a strong portfolio of innovations and a growing presence across international markets, ROONYX aims to forge new collaborations and stay at the forefront of technological transformation.  "Innovation thrives in collaboration. At Deep Tech Atelier, we're looking forward to exchanging ideas with other forward-thinkers and contributing to the conversations that will define the next decade of tech," said Vladimir Drogan, CEO of ROONYX.  ROONYX's team invites fellow participants and partners to connect during the event to discuss potential synergies in product development, digital strategy, and emerging technologies.  ROONYX is a leading fintech IT company dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology. With a focus on innovation, ROONYX develops groundbreaking solutions to meet the evolving needs of businesses and individuals across the globe.  Related Link: https://www.roonyx.tech
