# Classic Cool on Full Display: 1964 Chevrolet Corvair Convertible at DFW Car & Toy Museum

FORT WORTH, TX, April 28, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Get ready to cruise down memory lane! The DFW Car & Toy Museum is excited to showcase an automotive icon from the Ron Sturgeon Collection â€“ the 1964 Chevrolet Corvair Convertible. With its sleek design, smooth handling, and undeniable charm, this classic cruiser is a must-see for car enthusiasts and history buffs alike.  The 1964 Chevrolet Corvair Convertible is more than just a car; it's a symbol of style and innovation. Featuring a robust 6-cylinder engine, a 4-speed manual transmission, and a freshly updated look with new paint, a new top, and new brakes, this vintage beauty offers the perfect blend of power and elegance. Its well-preserved original interior adds an authentic touch that transports visitors straight back to the golden age of American automobiles.  "The Corvair is a beloved classic known for its unique engineering and timeless appeal," said Ron Sturgeon, owner of the DFW Car & Toy Museum. "We are thrilled to have this exceptional 1964 convertible on display. It runs and drives great, and we can't wait for our guests to experience its charm up close."  Visitors to the DFW Car & Toy Museum in Fort Worth can explore an impressive collection of classic cars, toys, and memorabilia that celebrate automotive history and nostalgia. The 1964 Chevrolet Corvair Convertible is a standout addition that promises to captivate audiences of all ages. Don't miss your chance to see this classic cruiser in person. For more information or to plan your visit, visit www.dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.  About DFW Car & Toy Museum DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980's collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum's website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
