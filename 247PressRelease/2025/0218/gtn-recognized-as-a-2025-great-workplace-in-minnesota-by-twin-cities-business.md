# GTN Recognized as a 2025 Great Workplace in Minnesota by Twin Cities Business

MINNEAPOLIS, MN, February 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Leading global mobility tax firm Global Tax Network (GTN) has been named one of the 2025 Great Workplaces in Minnesota by Twin Cities Business in the medium/large employer category.   This awards program aims to identify, recognize, and celebrate the most exceptional employers in Minnesotaâ€”organizations where employees are deeply engaged and motivated to contribute. The list is determined by the Best Companies Group which ranks companies based on a thorough and comprehensive evaluation process.   "We are incredibly honored to be recognized as a Great Workplace in Minnesota," noted Eric Loff, President of GTN. "This recognition is a result of the passion and commitment our team brings every day, not only to each other but to our clients. We believe that fostering an empowered and supportive environment translates into outstanding service and excellence for our clients. We look forward to the ongoing achievements and growth that our team will reach together."   To determine the recipients of the Great Workplaces in Minnesota honor, Best Companies Group conducted a comprehensive evaluation of each participating organization. Companies completed an extensive questionnaire, and their employees provided candid feedback through a company-wide survey. Best Companies Group then analyzed the results to rank each organization. Only those organizations that achieved the highest percentage of positive responses across a variety of workplace factors earned the distinction of being among the state's best. This recognition is solely based on real, data-driven feedback.  The Great Workplaces in Minnesota excel in creating quality workplaces for their employees, setting a standard others can follow. For more information on Twin Cities Business Great Workplaces in Minnesota program, visit https://tcbmag.com/2025-great-workplaces-in-minnesota/  ABOUT GLOBAL TAX NETWORK (GTN) GTN is a mobility tax services firm focused on empowering people to live and work anywhere in the world. They help corporate mobility program managers and mobile employees navigate cross-border tax complexities and manage risks. Their scope includes providing support for expatriates, foreign nationals, business travelers (both international and domestic), remote workers, and permanent transfers. GTN is the recipient of several industry awards for excellence, including Accounting Today's "Best Accounting Firms to Work For", as well as the INSIDE Public Accounting "Best of the Best Firms". GTN is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN, with employees based throughout the United States. For more information, please visit gtn.com.
