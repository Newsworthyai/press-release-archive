# Elliston Pediatrics named a Castle Connolly Practice Center of Excellence

NEW YORK, NY, February 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Elliston Pediatrics, a concierge practice serving Manhattan and Brooklyn families, has been named a Castle Connolly Practice Center of Excellence.  Castle Connolly Practice Centers of Excellence are premier healthcare entities that employ multiple Castle Connolly Top Doctors. Castle Connolly Top Doctors represent the top 7% of all U.S. practicing physicians. Elliston Pediatrics is the first and currently only Private Pediatrics Primary Care Practice that has received this prestigious designation.   Elliston Pediatrics is home to a skilled team of pediatricians, pediatric nurses, and support staff who deliver to members the complete spectrum of pediatric primary care services. Elliston Pediatrics covers families in Manhattan (below 96th Street), in Downtown Brooklyn, and in the Brooklyn neighborhoods DUMBO, Brooklyn Heights, Cobble Hill, Boerum Hill, and Williamsburg.  The team evaluates and treats concerns that range from colds, infections, and chronic conditions to prenatal support and developmental concerns. They also perform well-child exams, administer immunizations, provide lactation consulting, manage prescriptions, and coordinate care with specialists.  These house-call pediatricians deliver premier primary care to children from birth until they are 21 years old.  The experienced clinical team includes four American Board of Pediatrics-certified pediatricians, all Fellows of the American Academy of Pediatrics (FAAP):  Andrew Elliston, MD, co-founded the practice in 2019 with Heidi Meeker, BSN, RNC-OB, MNN, LRN, EFM, a registered nurse and his wife. A 2025 Castle Connolly Top Doctor, Dr. Elliston has built a reputation for providing exceptional, family-centered pediatric care that emphasizes preventive medicine, anticipatory guidance, and evidence-based, minimally invasive treatments. Known for his measured, non-alarmist approach, he empowers parents with the knowledge and confidence to navigate their child's health, focusing on proactive wellness rather than unnecessary interventions. Dr. Elliston's expertise in developmental and behavioral pediatrics ensures that families receive comprehensive support tailored to their child's unique needs, making Elliston Pediatrics a trusted leader in modern pediatric care.  Dr. Elliston earned his medical degree at Loma Linda University School of Medicine and completed residency training in pediatrics at Loma Linda University Children's Hospital.  Michelle L. Bennett, MD, has been practicing pediatrics since 2002. She has a special interest in supporting breastfeeding and in providing a holistic, individualized approach to each child's care. She is a 2025 Castle Connolly Top Doctor.  Dr. Bennett earned her medical degree — Alpha Omega Alpha, with honors in pediatrics — at the University of Kentucky College of Medicine. Dr. Bennett completed a residency in pediatrics at the University of South Carolina, Palmetto Richland Memorial Hospital, now known as Prisma Health Richland Hospital.  Cherilyn Davis, MD, has unique expertise in health communications, specifically patient-provider dialogue. She has been honored with the Susan L. Ivey Courage to Lead Award and the 2022 Inspire Award from the American Medical Women's Association.  Dr. Davis earned her medical degree, with honors in pediatrics, from Sidney Kimmel Medical College, where she is an inductee of the Hobart Armory Hare Medical Honor Society. She completed residency training in pediatrics at Children's National Hospital.  Danis Copenhaver, MD, has been serving Brooklyn families since 2014. She has special clinical interest in development, neurodiversity, and dermatology.  Dr. Copenhaver earned her medical degree from Weill Cornell Medical College and completed residency training in pediatrics at the Children's Hospital of New York/Columbia University. She is a 2025 Castle Connolly Top Doctor.  Laura Varghese, BSN, RNC-OB, LRN, MNN, IBCLC — Elliston Pediatrics' Clinical Nurse Coordinator — works closely with patients and the physicians to ensure a seamless follow through of care plans and specialist referrals. The nursing team provides parents with services like prenatal support, lactation and food allergy education, and critical advocacy to ensure parents feel supported and empowered every step of the way.  "We ensure that nothing slips through the cracks, empowering families to navigate parenthood with confidence," says Dr. Danis Copenhaver.  "Our physicians and nurses go above and beyond to provide best-in-class care in the comfort and safety of our patients' homes. This personalized approach ensures that every child receives the attention and expertise they deserve."  Investing in a membership with Elliston Pediatrics means prioritizing your child's health with the highest level of personalized, proactive, and accessible pediatric care," says Dr. Andrew Elliston. "Our model ensures longer visits, same-day sick appointments, and direct communication with your child's doctor — without the barriers of a traditional system."  The value of this investment extends far beyond traditional doctor visits. With expert pediatricians available when and where they are needed — whether at home or while traveling, through their advanced telemedicine offering — parents gain invaluable peace of mind. This approach helps prevent unnecessary emergency room visits, reduces anxiety around childhood illnesses, and ensures that every decision regarding a child's health is made with thoughtfulness and a deep understanding of their unique needs.  At Elliston Pediatrics, this commitment is more than just care — it is an investment in a child's long-term well-being and development.  Top Doctors are nominated by peer physicians and selected by the physician-led Castle Connolly research team.  "We are honored to have been designated as a Castle Connolly Practice Center for Excellence. This prestigious recognition reflects our unwavering dedication to delivering the highest standard of care," says Dr. Michelle Bennett. "The designation as a Center for Excellence and the recognition of our doctors as Castle Connolly Top Doctors underscores what makes Elliston Pediatrics unique," Heidi Meeker, RN, Cofounder/CEO, says. About Castle Connolly Castle Connolly publishes its Top Doctors list at castleconnolly.com and in a variety of print and online partner publications. Nominations are open to all board-certified MDs, DOs, and DPMs. The Castle Connolly team chooses honorees based on nominee criteria that includes their medical education, training, hospital appointments, and disciplinary histories.  Doctors do not and cannot pay to be selected as a Castle Connolly Top Doctor.
