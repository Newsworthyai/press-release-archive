# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Marshall Flam, Featured on Central Valley Today to Discuss His Book, Wordle Limericks

FRESNO, CA, January 10, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Marshall Flam, MD, a retired physician and former clinical professor of Medicine at UCSF, appeared on Central Valley Today to introduce his book, "Wordle Limericks." Inspired by the popular internet game Wordle, Dr. Flam crafts limericks incorporating the game's word of the day, blending humor, education and nostalgia. His first volume, covering 603 limericks across 33 categories like art, science and medicine, is now available on Amazon. With Wordle continuing until 2027, Dr. Flam anticipates publishing eight volumes. He hopes his creative works inspire readers to "laugh and learn."
