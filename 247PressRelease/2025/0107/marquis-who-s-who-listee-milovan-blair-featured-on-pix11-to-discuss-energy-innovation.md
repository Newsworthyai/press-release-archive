# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Milovan Blair, Featured on PIX11 to Discuss Energy Innovation

BROOKLYN, NY, January 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Milovan Blair, chairman of the board at Northeast Power Coordinating Council Inc. (NPCC) and senior vice president at Con Edison, was featured on PIX11 to share insights on addressing energy challenges. Mr. Blair highlighted critical areas, including enhancing grid security, responsibly integrating renewables and ensuring resilience against extreme weather. Emphasizing stakeholder engagement and robust standards, he stressed the importance of maintaining reliable power amidst evolving demands.  Mr. Blair views leadership as a service, striving to educate and empower communities while advancing sustainability and reliability in the energy sector. Learn more at npcc.org.
