# Joseph Smolarz Featured on Innovators' Journey to Discuss Liftoff Now Framework

UNIONDALE, NY, September 23, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Joseph Smolarz was featured on Innovators' Journey to discuss Liftoff Now Framework, which is a pathway for doctors to learn how to become savvy investors. Dr. Smolarz, who is an otolaryngologist, investor, inventor and entrepreneur, uses this framework to teach doctors how to have the right mindset on investing. The framework is strategic, as it challenges doctors to change the way they think. Dr. Smolarz currently serves as the chief executive officer of STOR Management, is a physician at VIENT and is also the founder of Smell Again.  Watch the segment here.
