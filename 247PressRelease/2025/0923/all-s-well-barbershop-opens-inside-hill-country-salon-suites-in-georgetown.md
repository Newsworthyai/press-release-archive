# All's Well Barbershop Opens Inside Hill Country Salon Suites in Georgetown

GEORGETOWN, TX, September 23, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Hill Country Salon Suites is proud to announce the opening of All's Well Barbershop, operated by talented barber Jared Shotwell, in Suite 118 of its Georgetown location.  At All's Well Barbershop, Jared has crafted a welcoming space where clients can relax, recharge, and enjoy personalized haircuts designed to fit their style and personality.  "For me, barbering is more than just a haircut," said Jared. "It's about creating an experience that helps clients look sharp and feel renewed."  The Georgetown community is invited to stop by, meet Jared, and book an appointment to experience the artistry and care that define All's Well Barbershop.  "We're thrilled to welcome Jared Shotwell and All's Well Barbershop to Hill Country Salon Suites," said Richard Spradley, co-owner of Hill Country Salon Suites. "Jared's creativity, professionalism, and commitment to client care make him a wonderful addition to our community of independent beauty and wellness professionals."
