# Newsweek Names WebCE a 2025 Top Online Learning Provider

DALLAS, TX, January 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- WebCE is proud to announce their inclusion as a five-star provider in Newsweek's list of America's Top Online Learning Providers 2025.   This prestigious award is presented in collaboration with Statista, the world-leading statistics portal and industry ranking provider. The awards list was announced on November 6th, 2024 within the November issue of Newsweek Magazine.   America's Top Online Learning Providers 2025 were identified through an independent survey which included over 10,000 participants. Institutions other than a university or college and platforms, which provide access to their own or third-party online courses were the participants.   Learning Providers were assessed by the following parameters: Overall Satisfaction, Overall Recommendation, Organization & Accessibility, Support & Service, Cost of Program, Organizational Reputation, Expected Success, and Practical Relevance of Contents.   WebCE's inclusion on this list is a strong testament to their commitment to delivering a high standard of education in a flexible and accessible way, enabling students to learn and succeed from anywhere.   "This recognition reflects the passion and expertise our team brings each and every day," WebCE President Jennifer Haworth said. "Their commitment to excellence has empowered thousands of students to achieve their goals through flexible and accessible education."   As a Top Online Learning Provider, WebCE will continue to empower learners with innovative education and remain committed to setting new standards in online education and professional development.
