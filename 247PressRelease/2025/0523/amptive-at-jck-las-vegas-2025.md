# Amptive at JCK Las Vegas 2025

LOS ANGELES, CA, May 23, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Amptive, the premier website design and marketing agency exclusively serving jewelry stores, watch retailers, and jewelry manufacturers, is proud to announce its return to the JCK Show at The Venetian Expo in Las Vegas. The agency will be attending the highly anticipated event from June 6 through June 9, 2025.  With a proven track record of helping jewelers elevate their online presence and digital marketing strategies, Amptive continues to make an impact across the jewelry industry. Their presence at JCK Las Vegas has become a staple for businesses looking to stand out in a competitive market. As the JCK Show 2025 dates approach, the Amptive team is excited to once again engage with brands seeking custom solutions tailored specifically to the jewelry sector.  For those attending JCK 2025, this is a valuable opportunity to connect directly with the experts behind some of the industry's most effective jewelry websites and jewelry ad campaigns. Whether you're a family-owned jewelry store, a luxury watch retailer, or a global-scale jewelry manufacturer attending JCK, Amptive's services are built to grow your digital reach and convert more customers.  The JCK Show is known for bringing together the best in fine jewelry, and Amptive stands ready to support those exhibitors and attendees looking to modernize their web platforms, increase conversions, and optimize their digital marketing.  Visit with Amptive during the JCK Luxury Show 2025 to explore how your business can thrive online. Can't make it to Las Vegas? Contact Amptive's Los Angeles headquarters to learn how their team can transform your website and marketing efforts into powerful tools for growth.  About Amptive  Based in Los Angeles, Amptive started in 2011 by two brothers passionate about helping jewelry businesses reach their full potential. From well-developed websites, to e-boutiques, and data management, Amptive is a full-service digital marketing agency that specializes in assisting jewelry stores and jewelry manufacturers gain more leads that can turn into larger profits. It is their pleasure to bring their clients dreams to life by brainstorming jewelry marketing ideas and instilling a marketing plan unique to their clients businesses. Contact their Los Angeles office to begin your digital marketing journey to enhance your jewelry business in extraordinary ways.
