# The Pillars Hotel &amp; Club Wins Fourth Consecutive Condé Nast Johansens Award for Excellence!

FORT LAUDERDALE, FL, November 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- We're delighted to share some wonderful news — for the fourth year in a row, The Pillars Hotel & Club has received a Condé Nast Johansens Award for Excellence, one of the most respected honors in luxury hospitality.  Each year, Condé Nast Johansens celebrates outstanding independent hotels around the world, and we're proud to announce that for 2026 The Pillars was recognized as the "Best Luxury Value Hotel in the Americas."  This recognition reflects what we value most — genuine hospitality, personal service, and meaningful experiences shared beneath the palm trees and along the Intracoastal.  We are deeply grateful to our members and guests for inspiring this achievement year after year. You make The Pillars what it is, and we look forward to celebrating with you soon — whether over a glass of wine by the water or during your next stay.  The Pillars Hotel & Club is a luxurious, boutique-style hotel offering a serene escape in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Renowned for its personalized service, beautiful accommodations, and exclusive amenities, The Pillars provides an unparalleled guest experience on the Intracoastal Waterway.  As a member of Small Luxury Hotels of the World, The Pillars Hotel & Club is part of a curated collection of over 500 boutique hotels across 80+ countries, recognized for their high standards and unique charm. Since opening as a luxury boutique property over 25 years ago, the hotel has consistently received multiple awards, reflecting its ongoing commitment to excellence.  For bookings and more information, visit www.pillarshotel.com or call (954) 467-9639.
