# Verdigris Selects EnergyHUB 360 as Channel Partner Manager to Drive Adoption and Growth in Energy Intelligence

PALO ALTO, CA, November 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- This partnership reflects Verdigris's recognition that achieving the full potential of AI-powered analytics requires more than advanced technology—it requires helping organizations successfully adopt and integrate it into how they operate. By partnering with EnergyHUB 360, Verdigris is reinforcing its commitment to delivering both technological innovation and the human intelligence needed for clients to realize measurable, long-term value.  As Channel Partner Manager, EnergyHUB 360 will oversee the recruitment, enablement, and success management of Verdigris resellers and integration partners, establishing the structure and systems to support successful deployment, adoption, and client outcomes.  Verdigris's patented hardware and AI-driven software deliver high-frequency electrical data capture (8 kHz), enabling advanced fault detection and diagnostics (FDD) that transform building operations from reactive to predictive. As global demand for this capability grows, Verdigris stands out as one of the few technology companies actively investing in the people and process side of adoption—helping clients move beyond insights to meaningful, sustained performance improvements.  Verdigris is focused first on AI data centers—the place where power-to-compute orchestration unlocks the biggest operational leverage. EnergyHUB 360 will scale Verdigris' data-center partnerships and restore high-touch coverage for its legacy commercial and industrial customers (hospitality, multifamily, manufacturing), while also bringing Verdigris capabilities into EnergyHUB 360's broader customer base.  "Technology alone doesn't drive transformation—people do," said Greg Head, CEO of EnergyHUB 360. "Our role is to help organizations adopt Verdigris's AI systems effectively, ensuring that clients moves from reactive operations to predictive, data-driven performance."  "This collaboration lets us keep AI data-centers as our north star while leveraging EnergyHUB 360's field expertise to deliver electrical intelligence across our legacy commercial accounts and their customer base," said Mark Chung, CEO & Co-Founder, Verdigris.  Together, Verdigris and EnergyHUB 360 ensure that clients across mission-critical facilities, manufacturing, and commercial real-estate portfolios gain not only the most advanced energy monitoring technology available, but also the consultative, change-management support required to embed intelligent analytics into daily operations—bridging the gap between AI innovation and human adoption to deliver enduring results.  About Verdigris Technologies   Verdigris is the global leader in AI-native electrical intelligence with more than 14 years of patented innovation. Driven by a mission to sustain and enrich human life with responsive energy monitoring and analytics, Verdigris's solutions deliver circuit-level visibility, predictive fault detection, and actionable insights that help mission-critical facilities, data centers, manufacturers, and commercial real-estate portfolios reduce costs, extend equipment life, and achieve sustainability goals.  About EnergyHUB 360  EnergyHUB 360 is a global channel partner manager and energy-solutions firm dedicated to helping organizations translate Verdigris technology into measurable business transformation. Through its 360° Partner Success Model and coaching-based enablement programs, EnergyHUB 360 equips resellers and clients alike to deploy, adopt, and sustain Verdigris systems successfully. By aligning people, process, and technology, EnergyHUB 360 ensures Verdigris clients maximize ROI, achieve sustained efficiency gains, and embed intelligent energy practices across their operations.
