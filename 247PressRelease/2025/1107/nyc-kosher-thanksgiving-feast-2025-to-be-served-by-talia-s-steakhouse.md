# NYC Kosher Thanksgiving Feast 2025 to be Served by Talia's Steakhouse

NEW YORK, NY, November 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The premier glatt kosher restaurant on the Upper West Side in NYC, Talia's Steakhouse & Bar, has been providing Thanksgiving feasts for over two decades.   Patrons can look forward to enjoying a four-course meal that includes roasted turkey, stuffing, sweet mashed potatoes and all the fixings once can expect from a kosher Thanksgiving meal, for the price of $114 per person. Talia's can also deliver the prix fixe menu at $104 per person. Even those who do not enjoy turkey may choose from grilled ribeye, Moroccan salmon, and other Talia's staples.
