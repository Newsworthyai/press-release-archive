# Calotren® Clarifies Health &amp; Safety Facts: 100% Stimulant-Free, Drug-Free Collagen Supplement

JACKSON, TN, November 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Top of the World Distributors, maker of Calotren®, has released its official Ingredient & Safety Fact Sheet, reaffirming the supplement's 25-year history as a 100% stimulant-free, drug-free collagen-based wellness product.  The new document was published to promote transparency and consumer education in the health and nutrition space. It details Calotren's simple, research-based formulation — collagen hydrolysate, aloe vera, and ProHydrolase™ — and confirms that the product has never contained caffeine, ephedra, or any stimulant ingredients.  "Calotren has always been designed around clean, safe, and effective collagen nutrition," said Elizabeth Miller, CEO. "Our Ingredient & Safety Fact Sheet makes it easy for customers, partners, and health professionals to see exactly what's in it — and what's not."  Calotren® supports overall wellness by promoting healthy metabolism, muscle recovery, and joint comfort through collagen-based nutrition — without drugs, stimulants, or harsh additives. The fact sheet also outlines the brand's Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) certification and FDA-inspected production standards.
