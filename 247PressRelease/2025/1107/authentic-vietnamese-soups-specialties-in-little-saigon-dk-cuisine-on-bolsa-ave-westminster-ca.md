# Authentic Vietnamese Soups &amp; Specialties in Little Saigon: DK Cuisine on Bolsa Ave, Westminster, CA

WESTMINSTER, CA, November 07, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- DK Cuisine is a Vietnamese restaurant in the heart of Little Saigon offering comforting soups, handcrafted noodles, charcoal-kissed specialties, and a full Vietnamese coffee & tea lineup. Located at 9856 Bolsa Ave, Westminster, CA 92683 and reachable at (714) 839-3788, we welcome dine-in guests, reservations, takeout, and delivery—seven days a week.
