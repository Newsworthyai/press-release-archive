# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Susan Schall, Featured on Daytime Columbus to Discuss Support for Small Manufacturers

COLUMBUS, OH, January 17, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Susan Schall, founder of SOS Consulting, appeared on Daytime Columbus to highlight how her company helps small and medium-sized manufacturers navigate challenges and find success. Drawing on over 30 years of manufacturing engineering experience, Dr. Schall emphasized the unique struggles these manufacturers face, including limited resources, regulatory hurdles and supply chain issues. Through SOS Consulting, Dr. Schall provides guidance on quality/process improvement approaches, leadership coaching, and organizational health often overlooked by smaller businesses.
