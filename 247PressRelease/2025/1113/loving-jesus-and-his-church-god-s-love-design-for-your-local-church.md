# Loving Jesus and His Church: God's Love Design for Your Local Church

NEW YORK, NY, November 13, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Pastor and author Karen E. Cedergren introduces her newest release, Loving Jesus and His Church: God's Love Design for Your Local Church - a powerful reminder of the local church's purpose and value in a believer's life.  Backed by over 45 years of ministry experience, Cedergren presents a heartfelt message for today's Christians: to grow in Christ means to grow with His Church.  "Loving Jesus means loving His Church." – Karen E. Cedergren  This book helps readers rediscover the local church as more than a place - it's a spiritual family, a community, and part of God's intended plan for personal and collective growth.  Who Is This Book For? This book is for pastors, ministry leaders, faithful church members, and anyone who has ever questioned their connection to the local church. Whether you're leading a congregation, recovering from church hurt, or simply seeking a deeper understanding of your role in the Body of Christ, Loving Jesus and His Church provides the insight, encouragement, and clarity you need.  About the Author: Karen E. Cedergren is an experienced pastor, missionary, and author with a lifelong calling to build strong, biblically sound church communities. She began her ministry journey at just 19 years old and has since founded a Bible school in the Philippines and planted a thriving international church in Zürich, Switzerland. A graduate of RHEMA Bible Training Center, Karen is known for her ability to teach deep biblical truths in ways that are simple, clear, and relatable.  Her passion for pastoring, mentoring, and writing continues to equip believers around the world.
