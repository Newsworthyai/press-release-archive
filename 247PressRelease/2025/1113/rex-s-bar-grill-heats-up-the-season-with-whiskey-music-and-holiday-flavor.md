# Rex's Bar &amp; Grill Heats Up the Season with Whiskey, Music, and Holiday Flavor

FORT WORTH, TX, November 13, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Rex's Bar & Grill, Fort Worth's favorite gathering spot for elevated comfort food, craft whiskey, and live local entertainment, is closing out the year with a calendar packed full of unforgettable experiences. From whiskey pairings and acoustic showcases to game-day watch parties and weekend brunch, Rex's is where Fort Worth comes to eat, drink, and celebrate.  Whiskey & War Stories with Horse Soldier Bourbon  Friday, November 14 | 7–9 PM Sip the spirit. Hear the story. Join Green Beret Will Summers, one of the heroes behind Horse Soldier Bourbon, for a powerful evening of courage, camaraderie, and craftsmanship. Guests will enjoy premium whiskey tastings paired with remarkable first-hand stories from the battlefield. This event is free, but seating is limited. Click to RSVP  TX Whiskey Dinner: A Four-Course Pairing Experience  Tuesday, November 18 | 7 PM Experience a night of chef-driven cuisine and award-winning whiskey during Rex's exclusive TX Whiskey Dinner. Each of the four courses will be expertly paired with a unique expression from the celebrated TX Whiskey line, showcasing how exceptional food and spirits come together in true Texas style.  Guests can expect an evening of warm hospitality, thoughtful storytelling, and local flavor — all set in the heart of Fort Worth. Click to purchase tickets  Thanksgiving Day at Rex's  Thursday, November 27 | 10 AM–12 AM Leave the cooking to Rex's this Thanksgiving and enjoy a chef-crafted traditional turkey dinner and all-day sports viewing.  The in-house traditional meal ($27.99) features Herb & Garlic Roasted Turkey, Yukon Gold Whipped Potatoes, Cornbread & Sausage Dressing, and all the classic sides — or order the Take-Out Meal for Four ($99.99) for a stress-free feast at home.  Whole desserts like Bourbon Chocolate Pecan Pie and Pumpkin Cheesecake with Caramel Pecan Sauce round out the perfect holiday meal. Place your preorders or reserve your table at rexsftw.com/thanksgiving.  The Last Knife Fighter: Acoustic Showcase  Wednesday, November 19 | 7 PM Get ready for a gritty, soulful night of live Texas music when The Last Knife Fighter takes the stage at Rex's for an acoustic showcase, joined by Trick Myers and Izzy Ryder. With raw vocals and authentic storytelling, it's a performance that captures the true spirit of Texas songwriting.  Weekly Happenings at Rex's  Karaoke Tuesdays | 7–10 PM – Grab the mic (and a half-priced bottle of wine).  Live Music Wednesdays – Featuring a lineup of Fort Worth's favorite local artists.  Weekend Brunch | Saturdays & Sundays, 9:30 AM – Brunch classics, breakfast cocktails, and laid-back patio vibes.  Sports Watch Parties – Catch every NFL, NBA, and college game across the big screens, inside and out.  About Rex's Bar & Grill  Located at 1501 South University Drive in Fort Worth, Rex's Bar & Grill blends the best of Texas hospitality, elevated bar fare, and a lively entertainment schedule. With a chef-inspired menu, expansive patio, and community-driven events, Rex's continues to be Fort Worth's favorite spot to unwind, celebrate, and connect.
