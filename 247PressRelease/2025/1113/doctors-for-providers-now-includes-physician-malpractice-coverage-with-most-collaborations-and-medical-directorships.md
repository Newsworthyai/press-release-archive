# Doctors For Providers Now Includes Physician Malpractice Coverage with Most Collaborations and Medical Directorships

FORT LAUDERDALE, FL, November 13, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Doctors For Providers (DFP), the nation's leading physician-led organization connecting healthcare professionals with board-certified collaborating physicians and medical directors, today announced a major enhancement to its service model: physician malpractice insurance is now included with most collaborations and medical directorships.  This first-of-its-kind offering gives nurse practitioners, physician assistants, and other healthcare providers an unprecedented level of protection—combining risk mitigation, compliance assurance, and cost efficiency in a single, streamlined solution.  "Our mission has always been to make collaboration simple, compliant, and secure," said Kyle Renkei, Chief Operating Officer of Doctors For Providers." By including malpractice coverage for both collaborative agreements and medical directorships, we're eliminating one of the biggest concerns for providers and setting a new national standard for safety and accountability."  A New Era of Protected, Compliant Partnerships  The enhanced program provides a powerful layer of assurance for healthcare providers and practices by helping them: • Reduce legal and financial risk in both collaborations and medical directorships • Avoid additional insurance expenses through inclusive physician malpractice coverage • Operate confidently and compliantly across high-growth sectors such as IV hydration, medical aesthetics, and medical weight loss, functional medicine, and telehealth  As the healthcare industry embraces collaborative practice models, DFP's move addresses a growing need for secure, affordable, and fully compliant collaborations and medical directorships.  "This change empowers providers to focus on patient care rather than paperwork or liability," added Renkei. "It's protection and partnership in one simple solution."  For more information or to explore a compliant collaboration, visit DoctorsForProviders.com.  About Doctors For Providers  Doctors For Providers (doctors4providers.com) connects nurse practitioners, physician assistants, nurses, and healthcare entrepreneurs nationwide with board-certified collaborating physicians and medical directors.  Founded and operated by physicians, DFP provides white-glove coordination, compliance oversight, and ongoing support to ensure every collaboration is safe, legal, and structured for long-term success.  DFP serves thousands of providers across the U.S. in specialties including IV hydration, medical aesthetics, medical weight loss, functional medicine, and telehealth—helping practices launch, scale, and thrive in today's evolving healthcare environment.
