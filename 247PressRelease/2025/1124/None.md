# FreightBroker911.com Launches FREE Freight Broker Training to Break Industry Barriers Under the Leadership of CEO Milton Collier

ATLANTA, GA, November 24, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- In an industry where education costs and lack of support often prevent newcomers from getting started, FreightBroker911.com is redefining accessibility. The company, led by CEO and President Milton Collier, has officially launched its FREE Freight Broker Training Program, designed to help aspiring brokers enter the logistics world with confidence—without the financial burden.  Milton Collier, a respected industry veteran and logistics entrepreneur, is on a mission to remove common barriers that keep talented individuals from becoming successful freight brokers. His leadership vision is simple yet powerful: everyone deserves an opportunity to grow, earn, and build a business in logistics, regardless of background or financial means.  A Training Program Designed for Real-World Success The FREE Freight Broker Training offered at: https://www.FreightBroker911.com provides a high-quality introduction to the freight brokerage profession, including:  • Understanding freight movement and brokerage fundamentals • Step-by-step guidance on starting a freight broker business • Carrier and shipper relationship strategies • Compliance, operations, and load management • Sales, negotiation, and scaling techniques  While many programs charge thousands of dollars for similar content, FreightBroker911.com's free model reflects Collier's commitment to expanding opportunities within the logistics community.  Milton Collier: Removing Barriers, Building Futures With decades of industry experience, Milton Collier understands the challenges new brokers face—especially those entering the field with limited resources. His goal is to empower motivated individuals with the tools and knowledge required to achieve long-term success.  "Our goal is to level the playing field," says Collier. "If someone has the desire to learn and the work ethic to build, money should not be the barrier that stops them from becoming a successful freight broker."  This philosophy has shaped FreightBroker911.com into not just a training portal but a supportive community for logistics entrepreneurs.  A Platform Built for Growth FreightBroker911.com continues to expand resources for students and professionals, including: • Ongoing educational updates • Industry tools and templates • Business development support • Freight dispatcher and logistics training resources • Mentoring opportunities  Explore more about the free training and additional logistics education at: https://www.freightbroker911.com/free-freight-broker-training  A New Standard in Logistics Education In an era where logistics is growing at record speed, demand for qualified freight brokers is higher than ever. By eliminating upfront training costs, FreightBroker911.com is helping thousands take their first step toward entrepreneurship—making the logistics industry more inclusive, diverse, and opportunity-driven.  About FreightBroker911.com FreightBroker911.com is an educational and resource platform dedicated to providing accessible logistics training to aspiring freight brokers, freight dispatchers, and transportation entrepreneurs. Through free and low-cost training options, the company empowers newcomers to start, operate, and grow successful freight broker businesses.  For more information, visit: https://www.FreightBroker911.com  Welcome to Freight Broker 911 — Your Gateway Into the Freight Brokerage Industry. Freight Broker 911 was created to solve one of the freight industry's biggest challenges: the impossible loop of "no experience, no job" that aspiring freight broker agents face.
