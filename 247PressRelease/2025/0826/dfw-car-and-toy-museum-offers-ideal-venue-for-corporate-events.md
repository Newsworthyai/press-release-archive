# DFW Car and Toy Museum Offers Ideal Venue for Corporate Events

FORT WORTH, TX, August 26, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- You could hold corporate team building activities in a sterile hotel ballroom or you could take over the venue that Trip Advisor rated the #1 fun thing to do in Fort Worth. The venue has more than 600 five-star Google reviews.  For businesses large and small, productive teams are a crucial part of profitability and growth, which is why team building events are so popular. In addition to scavenger hunts among the exhibits, the museum offers team building using exciting slot car racing.   Even for nonprofits, the team is the heart of the mission. Think about using the museum's nonprofit meeting space for a team building adventure.  Make your event uniquely fun and memorable by hosting it at the museum! DFW Car and Toy Museum, located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway (at Meacham and I35w) is currently accepting bookings for corporate events in 2025 and beyond.  "One way that we help keep corporate event costs down is by offering a variety of food and beverage options that include unrestricted access to professional caterers, access to trendy chef-inspired food trucks, and even options that allow our clients to prepare and serve the food themselves using our onsite finishing kitchen with warming area," said Brook.   "In terms of beverage service for company events, we offer options that include a hosted full bar, a cash bar and even the money-saving option of doing a fun BYOB event," said Brook.  Unlike many of the other local places to hold events, the museum does not have a F&B minimum, keeping you in control of the cost of your team building event or retreat.   The museum can host private events for between 50 and 500 people. To learn more or reserve for your event, call the museum at (817) 834-3625, x1 or visit our website.  About the DFW Car and Toy Museum  DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980s collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings, etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. The museum was rated the #1 Fun thing to do in Fort Worth by Trip Advisor (out of 328 things to do), and the museum has more than 600 five-star reviews on Google. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum's website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
