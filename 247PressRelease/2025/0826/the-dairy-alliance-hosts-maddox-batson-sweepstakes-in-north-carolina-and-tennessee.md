# The Dairy Alliance Hosts Maddox Batson Sweepstakes in North Carolina and Tennessee

ATLANTA, GA, August 26, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Dairy Alliance, a nonprofit funded by dairy farm families in the Southeast, brings fans closer to rising country artist Maddox Batson through two exclusive sweepstakes in North Carolina and Tennessee. Winners enjoy VIP experiences, including meet-and-greets, concert access, and behind-the-scenes opportunities, celebrating both the region's vibrant music scene and the hardworking dairy families who support the Alliance.   The Meet Maddox in North Carolina: VIP Experience Sweepstakes will award up to four winners, each receiving two tickets to Maddox Batson's I Need A Truck Tour and backstage access to meet the artist at the Fillmore in Charlotte, NC, on September 27, 2025. Each prize holds a value of $119, and winners may receive a maximum of one prize per person, making this a truly one-of-a-kind experience.   The Tennessee Turn Up: Win Floor Seats to Maddox Batson Sweepstakes gives fans the chance to win two floor seat tickets to Lainey Wilson's Whirlwind World Tour with support from Maddox Batson at Bridgestone Arena in Nashville on October 2, 2025, which holds a value of $1,369. Two additional winners receive autographed Maddox Batson posters.   "Maddox has actively championed The Dairy Alliance's mission, helping spread the word about dairy's role in sustainable nutrition across the Southeast," said Farrah Newberry, CEO of The Dairy Alliance. "These sweepstakes celebrate the strong connection between dairy, music, and community, and we're excited to bring fans together for unforgettable experiences while sharing the positive impact of dairy across the Southeast."   Entry is open to any resident of Alabama, Georgia, South Carolina, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, Tennessee, or Virginia. Participants must be at least 15 years of age and located in the United States or its territory at the time of entry*.   Fans can enter either sweepstakes via Instagram by liking, commenting, and submitting an entry form, or by mailing a completed postcard to The Dairy Alliance. Both sweepstakes remain open for entries through September 15, 2025 at 11:59 p.m. ET.   For full sweepstakes rules and entry details, visit https://thedairyalliance.com/official-sweepstakes-rules#maddox-NC-2025 and https://thedairyalliance.com/official-sweepstakes-rules#maddox-TN-2025.   For more information about The Dairy Alliance, visit https://thedairyalliance.com or follow them on Facebook or Instagram @TheDairyAlliance.   *Participants under 18 must have permission from a parent or legal guardian to enter. No necessary to enter, and each entry receives equal consideration.   About The Dairy Alliance  The Dairy Alliance is a nonprofit funded by dairy farm families of the Southeast. We work diligently with dairy farmers, schools, sports teams, health professionals, local organizations, state leaders, the media, and the public to promote dairy foods and knowledge about the dairy industry. Our efforts center in eight states: Alabama, Georgia, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Virginia.
