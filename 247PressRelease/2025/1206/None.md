# Entrepreneur and Investor Aaron Keay Featured in New Career Spotlight Article

VANCOUVER, BC, December 06, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Canadian entrepreneur and investor Aaron Keay is the subject of a new in-depth career spotlight article. The feature explores his journey from professional athlete to business founder, and his role in shaping Canada's venture and wellness industries.  The article highlights Keay's key ventures, including Klutch Financial, his venture-backed family office; Kommunity Fitness, his boutique gym brand; and his advisory role with RX3 Growth Partners, a consumer-focused private equity firm co-founded by NFL quarterback Aaron Rodgers.  "I've always been most interested in what happens behind the scenes," Keay shares in the interview. "You don't have to be loud to build something meaningful—you just need to be consistent, thoughtful, and ready to adapt."  The feature covers pivotal moments in Keay's career—from founding one of the first publicly listed cannabis companies in Canada (OrganiGram), to launching wellness concepts that merge fitness, design, and technology. It also highlights his ongoing commitment to philanthropy and youth mentorship.  "You don't need a billion-dollar valuation to be successful," Keay notes. "You just need to be doing something that reflects who you are and helps others win too."  Aaron Keay is a Vancouver-based entrepreneur, investor, and former professional soccer player. He is the founder of Klutch Financial and Kommunity Fitness, and an advisor to RX3 Growth Partners. His work focuses on supporting early-stage startups in consumer products, health, and wellness.
