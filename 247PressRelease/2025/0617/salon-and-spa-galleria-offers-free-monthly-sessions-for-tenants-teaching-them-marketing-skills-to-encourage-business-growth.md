# Salon and Spa Galleria Offers Free Monthly Sessions for Tenants, Teaching Them Marketing Skills to Encourage Business Growth

FORT WORTH, TX, June 17, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Salon and Spa Galleria wants its tenants to not only succeed, but prosper and grow. That's why the company is going the extra mile to help these business owners learn the best ways to market their salon services.  With its private suites specifically designed to meet the needs of independent beauty or spa professionals, Salon and Spa Galleria offers affordable salon leases. But it's not just about providing rental space. Salon and Spa Galleria knows that starting a business isn't always easy. There are many things to worry about, and that's why the company is invested in helping its tenants achieve success.  One of the most important facets of running a business is putting a solid marketing plan in place, according to Salon and Spa Galleria Marketing Manager Jennifer Brook. That's why the company holds a free monthly marketing workshop for its tenants on the last Thursday of each month.  "During these sessions, we choose one marketing topic that is specific to developing a small salon business, and then we break it down into easy-to-follow steps." Brook said. "We want to give our salon owners everything they need to succeed in business," she added.  The workshop provides an excellent opportunity for salon operators to interact and network with other tenants, according to Brook. Participants can bring questions to the sessions and get answers, along with helpful input and suggestions.   "We also provide pizza, so you definitely won't want to miss out on that," Brook added.  Along with the free monthly marketing sessions, all tenants get a web page in the online salon directory, in addition to receiving a complimentary marketing program. This includes a 26-step salon marketing plan and a personalized press release about their business, which is distributed via wire service to local and national media outlets. Salon and Spa Galleria is active on social media.  Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons in the country, with 23 locations. The company delivers top-of-the-line value by offering competitive rates and flexible leasing terms. Suites include amenities such as state-of-the-art equipment, free WiFi, smart TV with fiber, plus a washbowl or sink. Stations are equipped with a mat, chair, mirror and cabinet. The waiting area gives clients a chance to sit and relax while waiting for appointments. A break room is outfitted with a microwave, sink, refrigerator, and Keurig coffee maker.  Leasing options are tailored to specifically meet the needs of small or medium-sized operations. Single and double suites are available, and station rentals include water and electricity. Tankless water heaters deliver an unlimited supply of instant hot water, so there's never a wait for the water to get warm. Additionally, all salon tenants have access to the free onsite laundry room, and soap is provided.   Salon and Spa Galleria locations offer plenty of free parking. Buildings are handicap accessible and feature spacious, wide hallways. Salon access is readily available around the clock.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in any Salon and Spa Galleria location, visit the salon chain's website.
