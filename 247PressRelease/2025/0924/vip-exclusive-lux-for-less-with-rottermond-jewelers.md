# VIP Exclusive: Lux for Less with Rottermond Jewelers

BRIGHTON and MILFORD, MI, September 24, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- This October 2nd through the 4th at their Milford and Brighton jewelry stores, Rottermond Jewelers is happy to present their Lux for Less event for their valued VIP clients.   For three days only, guests will be treated to a curated showcase of fine jewelry pieces that have been brought in especially for this occasion. Each creation embodies the refinement and craftsmanship that Rottermond is known for, but with special event pricing reserved solely for their VIPs. It is a rare chance to acquire truly exceptional jewelry while enjoying exclusive savings.  Every detail of this event is designed to make luxury more accessible. From elegant necklaces and earrings to dazzling rings and bracelets, the selection on display has been carefully chosen to highlight timeless sophistication. Quantities are limited, and with these pieces available only during the event, promptness is essential for those wishing to secure their desired treasures. Rottermond Jewelers invites clients to mark their calendars and join in celebrating luxury at an extraordinary value. To learn more about the Lux for Less Event or to explore the jeweler's remarkable selection, guests are encouraged to reach out directly to the Milford or Brighton jewelry stores. A personal consultation with Rottermond's expert team ensures a memorable experience and the opportunity to bring home jewelry that will be cherished for a lifetime.  About Rottermond Jewelers  Since their establishment in 1984, Rottermond Jewelers has served as a trustworthy purveyor of all things fine jewelry and luxury watches for the people in Milford and Brighton, Michigan. Rottermond Jewelers is renowned for their unparalleled inventory, top-notch customer care, expert custom jewelry design, and other services. The retailer is an active member of their community and for more than 40 years has donated over $500,000 to local charities and non-profit organizations such as the Pink Fund, the Michigan chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and ALS of Michigan, Inc. Get in touch with the jewelry retailer by sending an email to info@rottermond.com or by calling (248) 685-1150 for their Milford location and (810) 220-0015 for their Brighton location!
