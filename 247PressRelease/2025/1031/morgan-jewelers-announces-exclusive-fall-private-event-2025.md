# Morgan Jewelers Announces Exclusive Fall Private Event 2025

SALT LAKE CITY, UT, October 31, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Morgan Jewelers invites clients across the Rocky Mountain region to experience its highly anticipated Fall Private Event on Thursday, November 20th, from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. with hours varying by location. This one-day celebration of luxury and craftsmanship offers some of the biggest deals of the year on exquisite fine jewelry and timepieces.  Guests will receive a special gift card valued up to $750 off, redeemable only during the event. The first 100 guests to RSVP will also receive a complimentary pair of cubic zirconia earrings as a token of appreciation. For November 20th, an $80 sterling silver necklace will be priced down to $39.99. Adding to the excitement, attendees will have the chance to enter a grand prize drawing to win a distinguished Bulova men's watch and a stunning women's necklace.  This exclusive event will feature tiered discounts, local giveaways, and savings of up to 60% off select merchandise. Featured among the luxurious offers, lab-grown diamond crawler earrings that were originally priced at $1,000 will be available for just $499.99 that day only.  Morgan Jewelers' Fall Private Event promises an unparalleled shopping experience where elegance, value, and personal service come together. Guests are encouraged to RSVP for the Fall Private Event now to secure their spot and receive their event-exclusive offers.  Celebrate the beauty of the season with Morgan Jewelers, where timeless craftsmanship and remarkable savings await during this one-day-only event. Contact any of their jewelry stores in Utah, Nevada, Idaho, Montana, Oregon, and Washington for more information about the event, their selection, and their professional jeweler services.  About Morgan Jewelers  Established in 1914, Morgan Jewelers has grown substantially throughout the years, yet holds true to the traditions and work ethic that helped build their business. As a third-generation family-owned business, they understand that important moments deserve the best. They are dedicated to providing the utmost customer care and incredible selection of designer jewelry to their clientele that stretches across seventeen locations in six states along the Rocky Mountain Region. Contact any of the Morgan Jewelers locations in Utah, Nevada, Idaho, Oregon, Washington, and Montana for more information about their selection and professional services.
