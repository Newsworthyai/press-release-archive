# BZBGEAR Brings Buzz to Barcelona, Goes Beyond 4K into 8K at ISE 2025

BARCELONA, SPAIN, January 28, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- BZBGEAR®, a global leader in pro AV and broadcasting solutions based in Sacramento, CA, is exhibiting at Integrated Systems Europe 2025 in Barcelona, Spain, from February 4–7 at Hall 5, Booth 5K680. ISE is the largest professional AV and systems integration show in the world and is being held at Fira de Barcelona - Gran Via.  BZBGEAR's main theme at ISE, as well as a company for 2025, is Beyond 4K into 8K. BZBGEAR is excited to be at the forefront of the 8K movement by being proud 8K Association members. 8KA is an organization that educates on the beauty and benefits of 8K and the future of the 8K ecosystem.  BZBGEAR already has a full complement of 8K Ultra HD gear from award-winning matrix switchers and testers to splitters and cables. They will be adding one of the first 8K AVoIP solutions over 1G switch in the BG-IPGEAR-ULTRA8K. This versatile AV over IP solution also does seamless matrix switching, video walls, multiviewing, and KVM control. More exciting, BZBGEAR will be debuting a first-ever 8K@60Hz HDMI 2.1 16x16 matrix switcher in the BG-8K-1616MA.  Make sure to drop by BZBGEAR's ISE 2025 booth to experience gaming in amazing 8K. They'll have three gaming stations exploring their 8K universe. The main attraction will be PS5 Pro's Gran Turismo 7 and their racing simulator cockpit connected to a stunning 8K UHD 75" TV. Hop into the driver's seat, grab hold of the racing wheel, and put pedal to metal in this fully immersive driving experience!  With all this 8K news, rest assured, BZBGEAR has certainly not forgotten about 4K. 4K is still king and BZBGEAR will be adding new, robust 16x16 models to their award-winning line of VP and VPPRO matrix switchers BG-4K-VP1616 and BG-4K-VP1616PRO, respectively. Dubbed the Swiss Army Knife of AV Distribution because they support matrix switching, video walls, multiviewing, and signal extension.  In the market for a more basic 4K matrix switcher? BZBGEAR will have a new line arriving in the BG-4K-MA series, available in 4x4, 8x8, and 16x16, which will all support independent audio matrixing. They're also debuting a new line of Full HD and Ultra HD IP streamers.   Certainly making an appearance is their flagship PTZ camera—eight-time award-winning Adamo series: Adamo 4K (UHD) and Adamo Jr (FHD). This AI auto-tracking camera line supports HDMI/SDI/USB 2.0, 3.0/IP along with NDI|HX 3 and Dante AV-H. Be sure to catch it at ISE 2025 and see why Adamo is the most awarded PTZ camera in the market today.  And introducing their latest conferencing solution: 4K PTZ video bar BG-CYCLOPS-PRO. AI-powered features like automatic framing and speaker tracking, along with the ability to add expansion microphones for larger rooms, will make Cyclops Pro the most exciting collaboration tool in 2025.  The BZBGEAR team is eager to meet you! Drop by and say hello at Hall 5, Booth 5K680. You can register with code PMPE2M7W for free exhibit passes.  About BZBGEAR  Established in 2019 and headquartered in Sacramento, California, BZBGEAR is a global manufacturer of end-to-end pro AV and broadcasting products. Their mission is to provide top-grade AV and broadcasting solutions focusing on design, user experience, and lifetime product support. Every solution is carefully designed by BZBGEAR hardware and software engineers to ensure unmatched quality and features. With award-winning products and industry-leading support, BZBGEAR is the number-one choice for any AV and broadcast application. Learn more at bzbgear.com.
