# Chaikin Trial Group Earns BBB® Accreditation

MELVILLE, NY, October 22, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Chaikin Trial Group Personal Injury Lawyers is proud to announce our achievement of Accreditation with the Better Business Bureau®.  Chaikin Trial Group is a New York–based personal injury law firm representing clients across New York City, the five boroughs, Long Island, and Westchester County. With over 25 years of combined legal experience, our attorneys are dedicated to providing compassionate representation and fighting for justice for those injured due to negligence.  BBB is a standards-based, nonprofit organization with a local Board of Directors. It works to advance business trust. BBB believes businesses and nonprofits that embrace trust as a core principle in their organization will see growth in employees, customers, community, and their bottom line.  "At Chaikin Trial Group, we believe trust and transparency are the foundation of every client relationship," said Ian Chaikin, Founding Attorney. "Earning BBB Accreditation reflects our ongoing commitment to integrity—both in how we practice law and how we serve our community."  Businesses and charities are invited to apply for Accreditation and work collaboratively with local BBB staff to provide transparent information for consumers who are looking for businesses they can trust. BBB Accreditation is approved by the board of directors, and Chaikin Trial Group Personal Injury Lawyers is proud to join businesses across North American and BBB in an effort to advance marketplace trust through standards for ethics and integrity.  About Chaikin Trial Group  One of Long Island's premier personal injury law firms, Chaikin Trial Group is built on a foundation of trust and transparency. The law firm has achieved impressive results, with a total amount of $100 million recovered for clients.  With 50 years of combined experience, the team brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to every case. Since its founding in 2020, the firm has established a strong reputation for its commitment to securing favorable outcomes for clients.  Founder Ian Chaikin is known for his aggressive approach and proven track record in securing results for clients with various injuries. Whether handling cases involving motor vehicle collisions or other catastrophic events, the firm's attorneys bring unmatched skill and energy to every case.
