# Key Housing Announces May 2025 Featured Bay Area Listing: Artisan Crossing in Belmont, CA

BELMONT, CA, May 30, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Key Housing, California's top short-term and corporate housing provider at https://www.keyhousing.com/, is proud to announce Artisan Crossing as its featured Bay Area listing for June 2025. Its central location helped it garner the coveted designation for June, as business and pleasure travelers flock to the Bay Area for its booming economy, weather, and world-class things to do.  Located in Belmont, CA, this modern, pet-friendly community offers short-term housing solutions throughout the Peninsula for business travelers. The central location puts it in good driving distance to San Francisco, Oakland, Palo Alto, and even San Jose. The weather is picture-perfect and often rated the best in the United States.  "Artisan Crossing exemplifies the ideal blend of location, luxury, and lifestyle for today's discerning traveler," said President of Key Housing, said Bob Lee. "Its central location in Belmont provides unparalleled access to Bay Area cities, making it a strategic choice for a busy business traveler who wants to combine business and pleasure."  Central Location for Optimal Connectivity  Belmont's strategic location on the Mid-Peninsula offers residents easy commute times to Bay Area cities. Whether attending meetings in downtown San Francisco, collaborating with tech innovators in Palo Alto, or exploring cultural sites in Oakland, Artisan Crossing is the perfect home base. As a short-term apartment option, it is more "homey" than a drab hotel. It can be the "home away from home" for any man or woman who wants to bring the family on a business trip to the San Francisco Bay Area. Proximity to major highways and public transportation, including Caltrain (https://www.caltrain.com/), ensures that going to and from business meetings is efficient and stress-free.  Luxury Living with Modern Amenities  Artisan Crossing (https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-rental-properties/artisan-crossing/) redefines short-term living with its thoughtfully designed studio, one, and two-bedroom apartments. Those who want to browse listings in Belmont, California, can also see the city-specific page at https://www.keyhousing.com/corporate-housing-city/belmont/. Each unit features open layouts, energy-efficient stainless-steel appliances, quartz countertops, and in-unit washers and dryers. Residents can unwind on private balconies or patios, enjoying Belmont's beautiful views and weather.
