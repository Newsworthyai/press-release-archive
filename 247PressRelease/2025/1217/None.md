# Item by Item Announces the Launch of Copilot for D365 Supply Chain Training

DALLAS, TX, December 17, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Item by Item (IBI), a leading provider of Microsoft Dynamics 365 training, is excited to announce the release of Copilot for D365 Supply Chain Training, the latest addition to its growing library of AI-focused content. This module supports Microsoft's vision of embedding AI directly into the enterprise application experience, enhancing how supply chain professionals work within D365.  Copilot in D365 Supply Chain brings a growing set of intelligent capabilities designed to improve productivity and decision making across the supply chain. The IBI training covers: • Copilot Sidecar – A conversational assistant that sits alongside D365 Supply Chain and enables users to interact with the system through natural language, helping them complete tasks quickly without navigating away from their workflow • Embedded Summarizations – Contextual insights built into supply chain modules.  • AI-Driven Agents – Smart tools that automate repetitive tasks, interpret data, and support business processes.  • AI-Powered Pages and Workspaces – Purpose-built screens that organize and summarize supply chain data for quick review and action.   "This training will help users take full advantage of the embedded AI features that are now part of D365 Supply Chain," said Elif Item, CEO of Item by Item. "Copilot isn't just a tool, it's a new way of working. Our training ensures users can confidently navigate and apply these AI capabilities in real life supply chain scenarios."  This release marks the final addition to the IBI platform in 2025, a year that has seen rapid growth in content and training offerings across D365 products. As organizations continue to embrace AI, IBI is committed to delivering practical, role-based training that drives adoption and impact.  Item by Item (IBI) offers the largest user-focused online training platform for Microsoft Dynamics 365. Thousands of users across industries have trained on the IBI platform, gaining practical, role-based knowledge. In addition to self-serve online training, IBI provides custom course development and hybrid training solutions tailored to each organization's needs. The platform includes over 1,300 micro-courses, 90+ learning paths, and 220+ assessments across Finance, Supply Chain, Commerce, Sales, Customer Service, Field Service, and Change Management.
