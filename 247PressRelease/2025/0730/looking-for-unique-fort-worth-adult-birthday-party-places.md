# Looking for Unique Fort Worth Adult Birthday Party Places?

FORT WORTH, TX, July 30, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- If you live anywhere in the DFW Metroplex and want to have a high-octane birthday party for your significant other, you need to know DFW Car and Toy Museum, the newest of adult birthday party places.   DFW Car and Toy Museum is at 2550 McMillan Parkway, at Mecham and I35w and has an abundance of free parking for birthday party guests. Tops among local places to throw a party, the museum can host adult birthday parties with as many as 500 guests.  The museum is sure to delight the guest of honor and all of the birthday party guests because it has scores of five-star Google reviews and was voted Fort Worth's #1 thing to do by Trip Advisor.  "We are the right venue for an adult birthday because we offer a full range of food & beverage options to people who want to throw an unforgettable party," said museum Marketing Manager Jennifer Brook.  "As one of DFW's premier birthday party places, the unique event venue offers unfettered access to pro caterers, access to trendy gourmet food trucks, and even options for you to prepare the food yourself using our onsite finishing kitchen with warming area," said Brook.   "In terms of beverage service for special events, we offer options that include a hosted full bar, a cash bar and even the alternative of doing a BYOB event," said Brook.  The event venue offers transparent pricing so that those planning events know the total facility rental cost before signing any booking agreement.   To learn more or reserve for your personal event, call Jennifer at (817) 834-3625, x1.  About the DFW Car and Toy Museum  DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980s collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum's website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
