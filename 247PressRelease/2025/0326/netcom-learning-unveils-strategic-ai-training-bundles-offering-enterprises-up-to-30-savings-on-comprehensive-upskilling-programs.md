# NetCom Learning Unveils Strategic AI Training Bundles, Offering Enterprises Up to 30% Savings on Comprehensive Upskilling Programs

NEW YORK, NY, March 26, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a leading IT and business training provider, today announced the launch of its new AI CERTs™ bundled training packages, designed to help organizations strategically upskill their workforce while realizing significant cost savings. These carefully curated bundles offer up to 30% discount compared to individual course purchases, providing businesses with a streamlined approach to building AI capabilities across their organization.  The new AI CERTs™ bundles are strategically organized into four primary categories:  - AI for IT Teams ($17,978, reduced from $23,970): Six comprehensive courses including AI+ Developer™, Cloud™, Security™, Network™ and Architect™ certifications. - AI for Business Teams ($3,732, reduced from $4,975): Five specialized courses covering AI applications in Marketing, Customer Service, Finance, Human Resources, and Sales. - AI for Leaders and Decision Makers ($5,982, reduced from $7,975): Five executive-focused courses, including AI+ Executive™, Ethics™, Prompt Engineer™, Security™ Compliance, and Project Manager™. - AI for Innovation and Automation ($10,482, reduced from $13,975): Five forward-looking courses covering AI+ Robotics™, Design™, advanced Prompt Engineering, Research, and Quantum applications.  Additionally, a "Comprehensive AI Mastery" bundle ($11,228, reduced from $14,970) provides an all-in-one solution combining six essential courses across disciplines.  All bundles feature NetCom Learning's Guaranteed to Run (GTR) assurance, with monthly classes scheduled to provide flexibility and certainty for enterprise training plans. Each bundle combines instructor-led training delivered by industry experts with hands-on practical applications, ensuring participants not only understand AI concepts but can implement them effectively in real-world scenarios.
