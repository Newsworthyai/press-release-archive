# Nail Vibes with Bebe Now Open and Accepting New Clients at Salon and Spa Galleria in Lake Worth, Texas

LAKE WORTH, TX, September 16, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- After spending her youth immersed in the nail industry, Bebe has turned her passion for nails into a full-time career.  "My family owned a salon when I was young," Bebe said. "I grew up watching and learning from the experts." Bebe decided she wanted to learn more and eventually open her own business, so she attended nail school in Missouri. And now, with more than 18 years in the industry, she has accomplished her lifelong dream of operating her own nail salon.  Bebe prides herself on being professional, reliable and responsible. She thoroughly enjoys expressing her creativity through her nail art. If a client sees something they like on internet, for example, they can bring a picture to Bebe, and she will replicate the look. She offers a wide variety of nail services, acrylic, dip, builder gel, gel X, overlays, designs and more.   Nail Vibes with Bebe is currently accepting new clients. Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended, and can be made through Bebe's booking site, or by calling or texting her at (314) 691-1052. Hours for Nail Vibes with Bebe are 9 a.m.-5 p.m. Mon.-Fri. Saturday appointment only.   To celebrate her studio's grand opening, Bebe is offering 20 percent off any nail service for new clients. For more information about Nail Vibes with Bebe, or to view samples of her work, visit Bebe's Instagram page, Facebook page, or Tiktok page.  In close proximity to several large businesses and shopping areas, Nail Vibes with Bebe at the Salon and Spa Galleria located at 4300 Boat Club Road, Suite 202, Lake Worth Texas 76135 is a convenient location for clients. Several major interchanges and roadways are nearby, allowing easy access from any direction.  About Salon and Spa Galleria Salon and Spa Galleria offers salon suites designed for independent beauty and wellness professionals. With 23 locations in the United States, Salon and Spa Galleria is one of the fastest-growing, privately held salon studio rental salons. To inquire about space for rent in the Salon and Spa Galleria Lake Worth, visit the salon chain's website.
