# Item by Item (IBI) Expands Its D365 Training with the Launch of Rebate Management Curriculum

DALLAS, TX, February 25, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Item by Item (IBI) is pleased to announce the release of its latest D365 Rebate Management training curriculum, designed to empower organizations with the knowledge needed to effectively manage rebates, enhance financial visibility, and drive profitability.  The new curriculum includes 14 micro-courses, one new learning paths, and two new assessment tests, covering the core functionalities of Microsoft D365 Rebate Management. This module enables companies to create, track, and process rebates efficiently, ensuring compliance with financial agreements and improving cash flow management.  "As Microsoft continues to expand D365 capabilities, we remain committed to keeping pace with these advancements by providing timely and relevant training," said Elif Item, Founder and CEO of Item by Item. "The addition of Rebate Management training reflects our dedication to equipping businesses with the knowledge they need to maximize the value of their D365 investment."  The D365 Rebate Management curriculum is available immediately on the IBI Learning Platform for both existing and new customers.  Item by Item (IBI) Learning Platform is one of the world's only self-serve online training platforms for Microsoft Dynamics 365. The platform contains more than 1,300 micro-courses, 90 learning paths, and 220 assessments across multiple D365 products, including Finance, Supply Chain, Commerce, Sales, Customer Service, and Field Service.
