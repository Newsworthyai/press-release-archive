# Marquis Who's Who Listee, Olga Morales, Featured on The LOCAList SD to Discuss Empowering Foster Youth

SAN DIEGO, CA, February 28, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Olga Morales, founder of VIBRANT FC, appeared on "The LOCAList" to share how her organization empowers foster youth to transition into successful and fulfilling lives. Inspired by personal experiences with foster children, Ms. Morales highlighted the challenges they face and her mission to provide tools, education and personalized coaching to build self-identity and resilience.
