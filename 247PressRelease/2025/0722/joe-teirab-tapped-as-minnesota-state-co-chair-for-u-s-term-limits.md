# Joe Teirab Tapped As Minnesota State Co-Chair For U.S. Term Limits

ST. PAUL, MN, July 22, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Today, U.S. Term Limits announces that Joe Teirab has agreed to be the Minnesota State Co-Chair for U.S. Term Limits. Teirab brings a depth of both private and public service to this role. He brings a strong understanding for the need to enact congressional term limits to break the dysfunction we see in Washington, D.C.  "I am honored to serve as the Minnesota State Co-Chair for U.S. Term Limits," said Joe Teirab. "Now more than ever before, the need for term limits is crystal clear. Polls show that the one issue that unites all Americans regardless of political affiliation is the need for congressional term limits. Polling shows over 80% of Americans regardless of political affiliation support congressional term limits and want to see them enacted.   "For many, serving in Congress has become a lifelong entitlement rather than a public service," concluded Teirab. "Term limits will help improve accountability and make government work better for all Americans. I look forward to working with the Reed Olson and the team from U.S. Term Limits to ensure that the Minnesota legislature adopts a resolution calling for congressional term limits."  "We are honored to have Joe leading our efforts in Minnesota with Reed to pass term limits for Congress," said Philip Blumel, President of U.S. Term Limits. "Under their guidance, I am confident we will get our term limits resolution passed in Minnesota  About Joe Teirab  Joe Teirab was born and raised in Minnesota. After graduating Cornell University and Harvard Law School, he served as an officer in the U.S Marine Corps. He deployed to Iraq, helping commanders defeat ISIS.   After active duty, Joe served as a state prosecutor in Nicollet County, and later, as a federal prosecutor, putting Minnesota's worst criminals behind bars. Joe was the 2024 Republican nominee for U.S. Congress in MN-02.   Joe lives in Rosemount with his wife, Whitney and their son.  U.S. Term Limits is the largest grassroots term limits advocacy group in the country. We connect term limits supporters with their legislators and work to pass term limits at all levels of government, particularly on the U.S. Congress. Find out more at termlimits.org.
