# Diamond Ring Remount, Restyle, and Stackable Event with Rottermond Jewelers and Gabriel & Co.

BRIGHTON AND MILFORD, MI, March 25, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Rottermond Jewelers is thrilled to announce an exclusive two-day Ring Remount, Restyle, and Stackable Event at their Milford and Brighton, Michigan jewelry stores on April 4th and 5th. This special occasion will feature the latest spring arrivals from Gabriel & Co., alongside over $1 million worth of exquisite loose natural diamonds and lab-grown diamonds shown by Rottermond professionals.  Guests will have the opportunity to explore elegant settings, get assistance reimaging their heirlooms, or discover dazzling new stackable styles. A Gabriel & Co. representative will be on-site to offer personalized insights and guidance to ensure every piece of bridal jewelry and fashion jewelry by the brand reflects each client's unique style.  To enhance the experience, Rottermond Jewelers is offering a "Buy More, Save More" promotion during the event: save $100 on purchases of $1,000, $350 on purchases of $2,500, and $500 on purchases of $5,000. Additionally, those who spend $500 or more will receive a luxurious Gabriel & Co. jewelry travel case as a gift with purchase during the event.  For an added touch of appreciation, the first ten clients per store who schedule an appointment per store and attend the event will receive a $50 gift card to enjoy at either Caio Amici or Pettibone.  Celebrate the artistry of fine jewelry and luxury watches for the community of Milford, Brighton, and surrounding areas in Michigan by experiencing the exceptional service of Rottermond Jewelers. Schedule an appointment today to participate in this unforgettable event in Michigan.  About Rottermond Jewelers  Since their establishment in 1984, Rottermond Jewelers has served as a trustworthy purveyor of all things fine jewelry and luxury watches for the people in Milford and Brighton, Michigan. Rottermond Jewelers is renowned for their unparalleled inventory, top-notch customer care, expert custom jewelry design, and other services. The retailer is an active member of their community and for more than 35 years has donated over $500,000 to local charities and non-profit organizations such as the Pink Fund, the Michigan chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and ALS of Michigan, Inc. Get in touch with the jewelry retailer by sending an email to info@rottermond.com or by calling (248) 685-1150 for their Milford location and (810) 220-0015 for their Brighton location!
