# Join Craig, Kelley & Faultless in Fighting for a Cure

INDIANAPOLIS, IN, March 25, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Craig, Kelley & Faultless is honored to help Randy Ollis in his 2025 Leukemia & Lymphoma Society's Visionaries of the Year campaign.   Randy, who spent 39 years as a meteorologist with WISH-TV in Indianapolis, is a friend of our firm and a two-time guest on managing partner David Craig's podcast, After the Crash. He recently joined David on episode 54 to discuss driving safely in winter weather.   As cancer survivors, both Randy and David understand the importance of fighting cancer and know it can affect anyone. Our firm donated $5,000 as a Hero Circle Contributor, and we encourage everyone to support such a worthy cause and give back to our communities.  The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society is the largest nonprofit dedicated to fighting blood cancers. It has invested more than $1.8 billion in cancer research since 1949. To find out more about this important organization, visit the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society website at www.lls.org.  To help Randy in his campaign, make a donation here: Randy's Visionaries of the Year Fundraiser.  About Craig, Kelley & Faultless  Craig, Kelley & Faultless is headquartered in Indianapolis and has offices in Indiana and an office in St. Louis, Missouri. The firm handles cases throughout the country and has attorneys licensed in Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Kentucky, Ohio and Iowa.
