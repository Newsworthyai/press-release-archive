# BZBGEAR to Reveal Exciting Rebrand and First-to-Market 8K Tech at InfoComm 2025

ORLANDO, FL, June 10, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- BZBGEAR®, a leader in professional AV and broadcasting solutions, is making a bold statement at InfoComm 2025 in Orlando, FL, with the official unveiling of its exciting new company rebrand. Visitors can experience the next chapter of BZBGEAR firsthand at booth 2069 in the Orange County Convention Center from June 11–13, 2025.  Since its inception in 2019, BZBGEAR has rapidly become a trusted name in the AV industry—known for its award-winning technologies, future-ready product lines, and relentless focus on customer success. Now, the company is elevating its identity to reflect its exponential growth, global partnerships, and leadership in innovation.  "Our rebrand is more than a new look, it's a promise," said Eugene Bocharov, CEO and president, BZBGEAR. "We're evolving with the industry and pushing ahead with the next generation of pro AV and broadcasting technology. We're so proud of this milestone and what better place to show off our makeover than at InfoComm!"  To celebrate the rebrand reveal, BZBGEAR will have fun swag giveaways for visitors as well as a photo op backwall to capture and share the moment. Along with its primary booth, BZBGEAR will also be featured at their partners' exhibits. Catch BZBGEAR at AVL Media Group (booth 6281) and NDI (booth 3780) showcasing their latest pro AV and broadcasting gear.  InfoComm 2025 attendees will have the chance to get hands-on with BZBGEAR's newest solutions:  • BG-8K-1616MA: The industry's first-ever 8K 16x16 HDMI 2.1 matrix switcher • BG-IPGEAR-XTREME series: 8K AV over IP multicast transceivers for scalable, high-performance distribution • BG-STREAM series: 4K UHD video streaming encoders and decoders with low latency and high efficiency • BG-ADAMO series: Now with a powerful 40x optical zoom, making the most awarded PTZ camera in the market even more versatile  With a new identity that evokes energy, optimism, and innovation, BZBGEAR is reaffirming its mission: to simplify and amplify the pro AV and broadcasting worlds.  To learn more, stop by booth 2069 at the Orange County Convention Center in Orlando, FL, for InfoComm 2025 or visit bzbgear.com.  About BZBGEAR  Established in 2019 and headquartered in Sacramento, California, BZBGEAR is a leading manufacturer of end-to-end pro AV and broadcasting products. Their mission is to provide top-grade AV and broadcasting solutions focusing on design, user experience, and lifetime product support. Every solution is carefully designed by BZBGEAR hardware and software engineers to ensure unmatched quality and features. With award-winning products and industry-leading support, BZBGEAR is the number one choice for any AV and broadcasting application. Learn more at bzbgear.com.
