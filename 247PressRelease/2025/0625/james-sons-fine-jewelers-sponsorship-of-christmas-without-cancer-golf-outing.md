# James &amp; Sons Fine Jewelers Sponsorship of Christmas Without Cancer Golf Outing

CHICAGO / NAPERVILLE, IL, June 25, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- James & Sons Fine Jewelers proudly served as a sponsor for the annual Christmas Without Cancer Golf Outing held on June 6, 2025. With check-in beginning at 11:30 a.m., the event gathered members of the community for a day of camaraderie, purpose, and philanthropyâ€”all to support an organization making a profound difference in the lives of local families affected by cancer.  Since its founding, Christmas Without Cancer has been dedicated to easing the burden of the holiday season for families facing the financial and emotional stress of a cancer diagnosis. From covering medical bills and daily expenses to delivering gifts and essential supplies, their mission resonates deeply throughout the Chicago area.  As a family-owned jeweler with deep roots in the community, James & Sons is honored to support causes that reflect their values. Sponsoring the 2025 Golf Outing is just one of the ways they continue to give back and uplift the very neighborhoods they serve.
