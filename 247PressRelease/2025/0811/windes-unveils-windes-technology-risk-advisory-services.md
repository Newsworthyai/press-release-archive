# Windes Unveils 'Windes Technology &amp; Risk' Advisory Services

LONG BEACH, CA, August 11, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Windes, a premier advisory and accounting firm, has launched Windes Technology & Risk to address the rising threat of cyberattacks and increasing regulatory scrutiny facing today's businesses. The new service line delivers end-to-end cybersecurity, IT governance, and risk advisory solutions tailored for mid-market companies navigating the complex digital landscape.  Building on over 30 years of combined experience, the Windes Technology & Risk team brings comprehensive expertise in areas such as PCI DSS, HIPAA, ISO 27001, SOC reporting, virtual CISO services, security awareness training, penetration testing, incident response planning, and advanced advisory analytics. By centralizing technology and risk expertise under one roof, Windes delivers integrated, actionable solutions designed to help businesses build resilience, meet compliance standards, and extract value from data. These solutions are tailored to meet the needs of mid-market organizations across healthcare, biotech, retail, manufacturing, education, government, and beyond.  "Our clients face unprecedented challenges from cybersecurity threats, regulatory requirements, and the need for actionable data insights," said Managing Partner Sean McFerson. "With Windes Technology & Risk, we are empowering businesses to secure their operations, improve decision-making, and align IT strategy with long-term goals, ensuring they stay ahead in a rapidly evolving digital environment."  Randy Tanaka, Audit & Assurance Services Partner and Practice Leader for Windes Technology & Risk, said: "We have assembled a team with deep, specialized knowledge across cybersecurity compliance, IT governance, risk management, and analytics. Our goal is to help organizations not only protect their systems and data but also turn complex risks into opportunities for growth."  To learn how Windes Technology & Risk can support your organization's cybersecurity and compliance goals, contact the Windes Technology & Risk Team.  About Windes  Windes is a leading advisory, audit, and tax firm for growth-oriented small and mid-sized privately held companies, nonprofit organizations, and high-net-worth individuals. Our approach uses tailored expertise to proactively inform decision-making to maximize our clients' business potential. For more information on how we can be your trusted advisor, visit us at windes.com.
