# Ron Gordon Watch Repair Announces New Post on the TAG Heuer Carrera Chronograph x Porsche 963

NEW YORK, NY, August 02, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Ron Gordon Watch Repair, a top-rated watch repair shop in New York, NY, at https://www.rongordonwatches.com/, is proud to announce its latest blog post, which highlights the newly released TAG Heuer Carrera Chronograph x Porsche 963. The post explains the synergy between the two brands, one for high-performance cars and one for high-performance cars. The blog post explains the technical details of this collaboration between TAG Heuer and Porsche, a partnership that blends horology and car racing.  "Fast cars and accurate watches are a combo that never grows old. Much of our client base wants the brand identity that comes from car brands like Porsche and watch brands like TAG," explained Ron Gordon, proprietor of Ron Gordon Watch Repair. He continued, "In our blog and announcements, I like to focus attention on industry dynamics, but I remind everyone that if they are lucky enough to own a TAG Heuer, they should bring it into the shop for an inspection and tune-up. There is no greater tragedy, after all, than a quality watch sitting in a drawer because it is malfunctioning."  TAG Heuer and Porsche lovers can read the full post at https://www.rongordonwatches.com/2025/07/tag-heuer-3/.  The post provides details about this historic partnership. For example, Ron explains into the Carrera 963's race-ready design, which features a skeletonized dial, a red and black Porsche-inspired colorway, and the powerful in-house Calibre TH20-00 movement. "It's bold, it's technical, it's fast," writes Ron. "But it's also grounded in heritage." This new chronograph continues the legacy of the Carrera line, first launched in 1963, while embracing modern innovations in materials and engineering. It's a watch made for the wrist, but born on the racetrack.  Many readers may now be familiar with the storied history of TAG Heuer and Porsche. The two brands have been closely connected since the 1960s. Over the years, both have participated in car racing events. In addition, many movies have featured both Porsche and TAG Heuer over the years. Thus, a brand identity has been built around virile cars and virile watches, reflecting a fast-paced consumer lifestyle that signifies one has "made it" and remains an active participant in life itself. The TAG Heuer brand, like Porsche, is not just one that signifies wealth. These are two brands that signify the "good life," as evaluated against modern standards of masculinity. Fast cars and fine watches signify a person who has the kind of class that makes movie stars charismatic.
