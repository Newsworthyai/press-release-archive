# Marquis Who's Who Listee Brian Fricker Featured on Innovators' Journey to Discuss Staffing Partners Inc.

MILWAUKEE, WI, July 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Brian Fricker, president of Staffing Partners Inc., was featured on Innovators' Journey to discuss the unique staffing services his company offers. Under Mr. Fricker's leadership, Staffing Partners Inc. has become one of the top staffing agencies in the state of Wisconsin. Unlike other staffing companies, the agency offers transportation and PPE equipment for all employees. Through the BakPak division, the company is able to provide a warehouse space with a forklift and loading dock where clients can have assembly, packaging and fulfillment services completed. Looking to the future, Mr. Fricker hopes to expand Staffing Partners Inc. out of Wisconsin. For more information, visit https://www.staffingpartnersinc.com/.   Watch the segment here.
