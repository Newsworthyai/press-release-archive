# Celebrate Love with the Honeymoon On Us Bridal Event at Huntington Fine Jewelers

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, July 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- From July 17th to 19th and again from July 25th to 26th, Huntington Fine Jewelers invites couples to make the most of their wedding planning during the much-anticipated Honeymoon On Us Bridal Event, happening at all Oklahoma locations. With a qualifying purchase of $2,999 or more, guests will receive a free 7-night resort getaway to a dream destination of their choice, like a serene beach escape or an exciting international adventure. This limited-time offer gives newly engaged couples or long-term romances the chance to create lasting memories beyond the ring.  Find the perfect engagement ring or wedding bands while also securing the honeymoon of a lifetime. From elegant bridal jewelry to exceptional service, Huntington Fine Jewelers offers an experience as unforgettable as the moment you say "I do."  This exclusive event is available at all Huntington Fine Jewelers jewelry stores throughout Oklahoma in Oklahoma City, Norman, Stillwater, Shawnee, Yukon, and Midwest City.  Explore exquisite fine jewelry collections and take advantage of this exciting opportunity to begin your love story in style. This is also a fantastic time to upgrade your love's bridal set with a new wedding band, or an anniversary band to show your appreciation for your time together.   Visit your nearest Huntington Fine Jewelers showroom for complete Honeymoon On Us event details, expert guidance from jewelry professionals, and to discover a stunning selection of fine jewelry and luxury services.  About Huntington Fine Jewelers   For over 35 years, Huntington Fine Jewelers has served the greater Oklahoma City area with the finest engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, certified loose diamonds, and Swiss-made timepieces. Since their grand opening in Oklahoma City the retailer has grown in both square footage and staff, all while maintaining the personal level of customer service they have been known for. Today, customers can enjoy jewelry and watch collections from some of the top brands in the industry as well as the company's premier jewelry design and repair services. For more information visit their website at www.huntingtonfinejewelers.com, send an email to info@huntingtonfinejewelers.com or visit their showrooms in person.
