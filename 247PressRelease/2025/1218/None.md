# THE HEART OF GIVING HOLIDAY EVENT BRINGS COMMUNITY TOGETHER FOR A DAY OF GENEROSITY, JOY &amp; SUPPORT WITH LADIES TOUCH INC &amp; THE WEST ATHENS WESTMONT TASK FORCE

LOS ANGELES, CA, December 18, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- On December 22, 2025, Ladies Touch Inc., in collaboration with the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department South Los Angeles Station, the West Athens Westmont Task Force, and a network of nonprofit partners and community organizations, will present The Heart of Giving Holiday Event.   The celebration will take place from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM at the South Los Angeles Station front parking lot (1310 W. Imperial Hwy, Los Angeles), and all community members are invited to attend free of charge.  This festive holiday gathering offers much more than cheer: it's a coordinated effort to deliver essential resources, uplifting moments, and community connection for families in need, just in time for the holidays.  "We believe every family deserves to feel the warmth, generosity, and hope of the holiday season," said Spokesperson Ewanda Jackson, Founder of Ladies Touch Inc. "The Heart of Giving Holiday Event is about dignity, community care, and showing up for each other when it matters most."  WHAT TO EXPECT Free holiday-ready essentials — including food, clothing, and gifts for adults and children  Hot meals, cotton candy, and treats  Holiday photo-op with Santa & Elves/Luves, a dedicated holiday photo activation presented by LuvMe  Complementary gift wrapping booth for donations   Outreach tables and community resources from nonprofit organizations and service providers  Music, community spirit, and a chance to connect with neighbors, volunteers, and community leaders.  REGISTRATION REQUIRED TO RECEIVE FREE ITEMS Due to high demand and to ensure fairness and proper distribution, families must register in advance to receive gifts, clothing, food packages, and access to the Santa photo experience.  Registration details are available through Ladies Touch Inc. and partnering organizations.  COMMUNITY CALLED TO HELP FILL THE TOY NEED — AMAZON WISH LIST LINK INCLUDED This year, the need for children's toys is greater than ever.
