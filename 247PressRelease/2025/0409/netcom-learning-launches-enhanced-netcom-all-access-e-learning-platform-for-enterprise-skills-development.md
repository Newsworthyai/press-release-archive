# NetCom Learning Launches Enhanced NetCom+ All Access E-Learning Platform for Enterprise Skills Development

NEW YORK, NY, April 09, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- NetCom Learning, a global leader in IT and business training solutions, today announced the launch of its enhanced NetCom+ All Access E-Learning subscription platform. The expanded offering provides unlimited access to over 1,100 expert-led self-paced courses, masterclasses, and webinars from leading industry vendors, addressing the urgent demand for AI, cybersecurity, and IT expertise across organizations of all sizes.   As businesses navigate rapid technological change, NetCom+ All Access delivers a comprehensive, cost-effective solution starting at just $699 per learner annually. We're currently offering an introductory price of $399 for all learners, with additional flexible enterprise pricing options available for larger teams.   "The skills gap in emerging technologies continues to widen, with AI expertise becoming particularly critical for business competitiveness," said Russell Sarder, CEO of NetCom Learning. "Our enhanced NetCom+ All Access platform makes enterprise-grade technical education accessible, measurable, and immediately applicable for teams of any size."   Key Platform Features   The enhanced NetCom+ All Access platform includes:   - Expanded Course Library: 600+ premium courses and 500+ complimentary courses spanning AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and business technology from vendors including Microsoft, AWS, EC-Council, and AI CERTS   - Self-Paced Learning Model: On-demand access allowing teams to upskill without disrupting workplace productivity   - Complete AI Certification Pathways: Structured learning journeys from foundational concepts to advanced implementation.  - Included Exam Vouchers: AI CERTS exam vouchers at no additional cost, with one free retake per certification   Enterprise Learning Management: Advanced tracking and reporting through the NetCom365 portal  The platform's self-paced design addresses business needs by eliminating scheduling constraints. It allows team members to progress through training content at optimal times while maintaining workplace responsibilities.   With technology skill requirements evolving faster than ever, NetCom+ All Access represents a strategic investment for organizations committed to developing an AI-ready workforce while maximizing their training budget efficiency.  About NetCom Learning  NetCom Learning helps build innovative learning organizations by structuring a smarter workforce, supporting changes, and driving growth. With more than 26 years of experience, NetCom Learning has been empowering innovative learning organizations to adapt and drive growth in this fast-paced world by closing critical skill gaps and ensuring smooth deployment, implementation, and consumption through authorized training delivered by Certified Trainers on vendors such as Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, AI Certs, EC-Council, CompTIA, and more.
