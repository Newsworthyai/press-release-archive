# 1989 Aston Martin Lagonda Now on Display at DFW Car & Toy Museum

FORT WORTH, TX, April 09, 2025 /24-7PressRelease/ -- A true symbol of luxury, innovation, and British craftsmanship, the 1989 Aston Martin Lagonda has taken its place among the extraordinary collection at the DFW Car & Toy Museum. This rare and futuristic sedan, a standout in the world of classic automobiles, is now available for viewing at one of the most exciting automotive museums in Texas.  With only 36,000 miles on the odometer, this exquisite Lagonda is powered by a 5.3-liter DOHC aluminum V8 paired with a Chrysler-sourced three-speed automatic transmission. Designed by the visionary William Towns, the Series 4 Lagonda features a sleek, wedge-shaped body with distinctive fixed headlights and an interior that exemplifies opulence. The cabin is adorned with burled walnut veneers, Wilton wool carpeting, and plush power-adjustable leather seats. Advanced for its time, the vehicle is equipped with digital instrumentation, air conditioning, cruise control, and even a period-correct Panasonic car phone.  The DFW Car & Toy Museum proudly showcases this legendary vehicle as part of The Ron Sturgeon Collection, alongside over 200 iconic automobiles spanning multiple eras. Visitors can explore two floors of meticulously curated exhibits, immersing themselves in the beauty and engineering of some of the world's most remarkable cars.  Beyond the stunning cars, the museum offers a welcoming and unique experience for all ages. Admission is free, and for those who love to bring along their four-legged friends, the museum is proudly dog-friendly. Whether you are an automotive enthusiast, a history buff, or just looking for a fun day out, the DFW Car & Toy Museum is the perfect destination.  Come see the 1989 Aston Martin Lagonda and explore a world where automotive history meets innovation!  About DFW Car & Toy Museum  DFW Car and Toy Museum, formerly known as DFW Elite Toy Museum, was founded by Ron Sturgeon in the 1980's collecting automobile toys and cars. Until recently, it was in Haltom City, TX, and its website DFW Elite Toy Museum is still the go-to source for toy and car lovers worldwide. The museum is now located in North Fort Worth at 2550 McMillan Parkway, Fort Worth, TX 76137. (at Meacham and I35w) The new facility is 150,000 square feet with free parking & admission. It promises an entertaining visit. They offer secure climate-controlled car storage and facilities for events, including car clubs, board meetings etc. The museum is also dog friendly and encourages pictures. Hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 am- 6:00 pm, visit the museum's website at dfwcarandtoymuseum.com.
