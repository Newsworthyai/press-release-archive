# Sandy's Furniture Announces Retirement Auction After Nearly 50 Years as a Leader in BC's Furniture Industry

VANCOUVER, BC, February 02, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- After nearly five decades as one of British Columbia's most trusted and recognizable furniture retailers, Sandy's Furniture has announced its Retirement Auction, marking the conclusion of a remarkable legacy that began in 1976.  A household name across the province, Sandy's Furniture has long been known for quality craftsmanship, exceptional customer service, and thoughtfully curated home furnishings. As the owners prepare for retirement, the company will auction off over $1,000,000 in inventory through a 3-day live online auction at Direct Auctions, offering the public a rare opportunity to purchase premium furniture at auction pricing.  A Legacy Since 1976 For nearly 50 years, Sandy's Furniture has furnished homes throughout British Columbia, earning its place as one of the largest and most respected names in the BC furniture market. From timeless bedroom collections to modern living and dining pieces, Sandy's built lasting relationships with generations of customers who valued quality, trust, and service.  "This retirement auction represents the closing of an incredible chapter," says the Sandy's Furniture team. "We're extremely proud of what we've built since 1976 and deeply grateful to our loyal customers. This auction allows our inventory to move into new homes as we step into retirement."  "Sandy's Furniture is a true BC success story," says Jeff Schwarz, owner of Direct Auctions and star of the hit TV series The Liquidator. "They've been a major player in the furniture industry for decades, and it's an honor for us to manage their retirement auction. This sale gives buyers access to high-quality furniture from a retailer that's built its reputation on trust and longevity."  Auction Highlights The Sandy's Furniture Retirement Auction will feature a wide range of floor models and brand-new, in-box inventory, including: - Dining, bedroom, and living room furniture - Sofas, sectionals, and recliners - Fabric and leather seating - Mattresses and bedroom sets - Home décor and accessories - Featured brands include Decor-Rest, Sunday's, Stylus, Moe's Home Collection, Serta, Natuzzi - Italia, and Kingsdown, among others.
