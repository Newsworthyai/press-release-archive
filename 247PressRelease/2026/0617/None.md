# Simply Success with a Mental Edge Conference is Coming to Orlando, FL on September 26, 2026

ORLANDO, FL, June 17, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Philomena Moncoeur of Simply Success Group and Marcus A. Wright of WrightMentality have joined forces to produce the Simply Success with a Mental Edge Conference, a high-energy, interactive business event scheduled for Saturday, September 26, 2026, in Orlando, Florida. The conference, themed around the tagline "The Smartest Investment is YOU," promises a full day of immersive learning, strategic networking, and practical tools designed to help entrepreneurs and business owners break through plateaus, build powerful brands, and grow with intention.  The event bridges business, marketing, branding, and visibility with the framework of emotional intelligence, equipping attendees not just with strategies, but with the self-awareness and presence to execute them and sustain real growth.  About the Conference Unlike traditional sit-and-listen conferences, Simply Success with a Mental Edge is built as an active, participant-driven experience. Doors open at 8:30 AM with coffee, networking, and check-in, followed by a full program from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST, including a catered lunch. Throughout the day, attendees will cycle through interactive workshops, live practice sessions, structured networking, games, challenges, and giveaways.  Key areas of focus include: - Networking to win - Self-awareness as a sales and leadership tool - Emotionally intelligent branding — building a brand people feel, not just see - Empathy-driven marketing that converts - Visibility and presence on stage, on camera, and online - Practical EQ frameworks, scripts, and worksheets usable immediately after the event  Attendees leave with take-home tools, not just notes.  Early Bird Ticket Options — Invest in Yourself Now Tickets are now on sale at early bird pricing, giving growth-minded entrepreneurs an opportunity to secure their seat at one of Orlando's most anticipated business events of 2026.   Group discounts are available for three or more tickets. All sales are final. Early bird pricing is available for a limited time as ticket prices will increase as the event approaches. Entrepreneurs who invest early invest smartly.  Travel & Accommodation  The conference is designed to serve both local Florida professionals and destination travelers. Simply Success Group has secured a hotel room block at the newly built EVEN Hotel by IHG, conveniently located near Universal Epic Universe, the newest Orlando theme park. Rooms are available for the conference weekend and can be booked directly through the conference website at a group rate while availability lasts.  For those making a full getaway of the trip, select travel packages include black car service, a private VIP dinner, exclusive professional photos, a podcast interview feature, VIP swag, and discounted Disney and Universal theme-park tickets, making the conference weekend a complete Orlando experience.  Meet the Hosts Philomena Moncoeur is the Founder and CEO of Simply Success Group. A business marketing strategist, speaker, and visibility expert with over 25 years of experience, Philomena has helped thousands of entrepreneurs, nonprofits, and organizations grow their brands and achieve measurable results through strategic marketing, branding, AI, and automation.  Marcus A. Wright is the Founder of WrightMentality, a coach, motivational speaker, and leadership trainer with more than two decades of experience. Marcus empowers professionals, entrepreneurs, and teams to unlock their potential through emotional intelligence, leadership development, and personal growth.
