# Hamptons Fashion Week Brings Star-Studded Runway Event to West Palm Beach in collaboration with The Hampton Vintage

WEST PALM BEACH, FL, March 07, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Hamptons Fashion Week in collaboration The Hampton Vintage, A Luxury Marketplace with will bring its signature blend of luxury fashion, vintage style, and cultural influence to West Palm Beach with the highly anticipated Hampton Vintage Marketplace, an elevated fashion and shopping experience celebrating sustainability, craftsmanship, and vintage timeless design.  Headlining the event as Mistress of Ceremonies is iconic fashion personality Regina Kravitz, who will guide guests through an exciting afternoon of runway presentations, designer showcases, and curated vintage collections. The event will also welcome a distinguished group of VIP attendees including renowned philanthropist and influencer Jean Shafiroff, Gail Brophy of the hit reality series Members Only, and Suzanne Lossia of the Food Network, bringing together notable personalities from fashion, philanthropy, entertainment, and media.  At the center of the runway presentation will be the debut sustainable collection of Sunskaara, a brand dedicated to ethical production, cultural preservation, and the celebration of artisan craftsmanship. Blending timeless elegance with conscious design, Sunskaara introduces a collection rooted in sustainability and heritage techniques. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted using responsible sourcing practices, small-batch production, and traditional dyeing methods that honor generational artistry. By working directly with skilled artisans, the brand supports fair labor practices while helping preserve cultural textile traditions.  Sunskaara's debut collection reflects a refined yet purposeful aesthetic — featuring structured silhouettes, elevated materials, and versatile statement pieces designed for the modern woman who values both luxury and impact. With an emphasis on mindful production and quality over quantity, the collection represents a meaningful shift toward intentional fashion consumption.  The runway show, produced by Hampton's Fashion Week, will also spotlight an exciting lineup of participating designers and curated by Hampton Vintage Market with vintage brands including Coco Cabana Shop, VF Design, Vielles Ames, and Zingara Vintage, creating a dynamic fusion of heritage fashion and forward-thinking sustainability. Cindy Guyer, a West Palm Beach Influencer, will also be gracing the runway.  Guests are invited to enjoy a full "Shop Till You Drop" vintage marketplace from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, offering exclusive access to designer collections, rare vintage finds, and limited-edition pieces.  The official fashion show presentation begins at 1:30 PM, drawing media, tastemakers, VIP guests, and fashion insiders for a signature runway moment.   VIP attendees will enjoy curated giveaways, special gifting experiences, and exclusive access throughout the day.  More than a shopping event, Hampton's Fashion Week in collaboration with the Hampton Vintage Market represents a celebration of fashion's evolving future — where sustainability meets luxury vintage , sophistication and craftsmanship is honored across generations.  "On this International Women's Day, we proudly honor the incredible women founders who have shaped the fashion industry. Their vision, resilience, and creativity inspire us all. As we launch Hamptons Fashion Week in West Palm Beach, we celebrate their contributions and commit to empowering the next generation of female leaders in fashion." — Dee Rivera, Founder of Hamptons Fashion Week  EVENT DETAILS Hamptons Fashion Week X Hampton Vintage Market West Palm Beach Waterfront 100 N.
