# Lost Bowling Pins in Woodbury! Track Them Down and Score Big at Main Event's Grand Opening

WOODBURY, MN, July 17, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Main Event, the ultimate destination for family fun and entertainment, is preparing for the grand opening of its first-ever location in Minnesota at the Tamarack Hills (540 Bielenberg Drive) this August. However, the team faces a challenge: 10 bowling pins have disappeared just days before the big day. Now, the Woodbury Main Event is calling on the community to help solve the mystery.   The missing shipment never reached its final stop, and time is running out before the August 3 opening. Starting July 19 through July 28, locals can visit https://mainevent.com/woodbury-lost-pins/ for daily clues as to where the pins are hidden for the chance to win prizes. Each recovered pin unlocks a VIP invite for five and a full year of free games. Whoever finds the Golden Pin takes home the ultimate prize of Main Event for life.   "We are so excited to bring the Main Event experience to Minnesota, but we need the community's detective skills to help us get our lanes ready for action," said Michael Frisby, General Manager of the Woodbury Main Event. "We've hidden these pins throughout Woodbury, and we can't wait to see who can track them down and claim their prize before our grand opening on August 3!"   Main Event Woodbury will bring nonstop entertainment with activities including laser tag, bowling, arcade games, and more. Guests can enjoy a full-service restaurant and bar featuring dishes such as the Buffalo Ranch Chicken Sandwich, Funnel Cake Fries, and drinks like the Cotton Candy Shirley Temple and Margarita Flight. The new venue invites friends and families to enjoy interactive games, friendly competition, and great food and drinks in one high-energy space.   For complete rules and eligibility, visit https://mainevent.com/woodbury-lost-pins/.  About Main Event Entertainment   Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Coppell, Texas, Main Event Entertainment, Inc. operates over 60 locations in 23 states across the country. Offerings include state-of-the-art bowling, laser tag, hundreds of games, virtual reality and an in-center restaurant Family Kitchen, making it the perfect place for families to connect and make memories.   Main Event is owned and operated by Dave & Buster's Entertainment, Inc., which offers premier entertainment and dining experiences to guests now through two distinct brands: Dave & Buster's and Main Event. Dave & Buster's has 182 stores in 43 states, Puerto Rico and Canada, and offers guests the opportunity to "Eat, Drink, Play and Watch," all in one location.   Each store offers a full menu of entrées and appetizers, a complete selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages and an extensive assortment of entertainment attractions centered around playing games and watching live sports and other televised events.    For more information about each brand, visit www.daveandbusters.com and www.mainevent.com.
