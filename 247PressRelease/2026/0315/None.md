# Surge Airbnb Management Extends Five-Star Service to Four Additional Southern and Western Markets

HOUSTON, TX, March 15, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Surge Airbnb Management, a short-term rental management company distinguished by its five-star reviews and exceptional property owner satisfaction, announced today its expansion into Alabama, South Carolina, Oklahoma, and New Mexico.  Founded by Humberto Marquez, a seasoned real estate investor and short-term rental operator, Surge continues its strategic growth by entering markets that demonstrate strong fundamentals for vacation rental investment and property owner success.  "Alabama, South Carolina, Oklahoma, and New Mexico offer property owners compelling opportunities in growing vacation rental markets," said Marquez. "Our expansion into these states brings our proven five-star service model to owners who want professional management without compromising on quality or performance."  Surge has earned its reputation through consistent delivery of superior results for property owners and memorable experiences for guests. The company's comprehensive management approach addresses every aspect of short-term rental operations, from initial listing setup through ongoing revenue optimization and guest relations.  Property owners in these four new markets will benefit from Surge's established operational excellence, including professional property marketing, intelligent pricing strategies, guest screening and communication, quality control systems, and compliance management. This holistic approach has generated consistently positive outcomes across all Surge-managed properties.  "Our five-star reviews tell the real story," Marquez noted. "They represent property owners who trust us with their most valuable assets and guests who choose our properties for their most important trips. That's a responsibility we take seriously in every market we serve."  The short-term rental landscape continues to evolve, with professional management becoming increasingly essential for property owners seeking to maximize their investments while maintaining compliance with local regulations and hospitality standards. Surge's entry into Alabama, South Carolina, Oklahoma, and New Mexico provides property owners in these markets with access to institutional-quality management previously unavailable to many individual investors.  Each of these markets offers distinct advantages for short-term rental investors, from Alabama's Gulf Coast appeal to South Carolina's tourism economy, Oklahoma's emerging rental market, and New Mexico's unique cultural and recreational attractions. Surge's management expertise helps property owners capitalize on these opportunities while avoiding common pitfalls.  Property owners interested in partnering with Surge can expect personalized service backed by proven systems, transparent performance reporting, and the operational excellence that has made the company a trusted name in short-term rental management.  About Surge Airbnb Management  Surge Airbnb Management specializes in professional short-term rental management for property owners committed to achieving superior returns and five-star guest experiences. Under the leadership of founder Humberto Marquez, an experienced real estate investor and rental operator, Surge has built a reputation for excellence through consistently satisfied property owners and highly rated guest experiences.
