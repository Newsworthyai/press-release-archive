# MARITIME PERSONAL INJURY ATTORNEY SPENCER ARONFELD NAMED A 2026 FLORIDA SUPER LAWYER

MIAMI, FL, July 13, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Spencer Aronfeld, founder of Aronfeld Trial Lawyers and a board-certified civil trial lawyer, has been selected for inclusion in Florida Super Lawyers 2026 in the practice area of Transportation and Maritime Law. This marks Aronfeld's 13th consecutive year on the Florida Super Lawyers list, from 2014 through 2026. He was also selected in 2006, 2007, 2009, and 2010.  "I am honored to be selected to this year's list of Super Lawyers," said Spencer Aronfeld, founder of Aronfeld Trial Lawyers. "I am grateful to my colleagues for their nominations and to the Super Lawyers selection panel for recognizing my professional achievements. This recognition not only reflects my work, but our firm's continued commitment to achieving the best possible results for our clients and upholding the highest standards of the legal profession."   Spencer Aronfeld is a board-certified civil trial lawyer who has earned international recognition as a "lawyer for the people." Born in Chicago and raised in Wichita, Kansas, Aronfeld graduated cum laude from the University of Miami School of Law in 1991. After graduation, he founded Aronfeld Trial Lawyers, based in Coral Gables, Florida. He has dedicated his career to representing individuals against major corporations, including Carnival Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean Cruise Line, Celebrity Cruise Line, Disney Cruise Line, Norwegian Cruise Line, Holland America Cruise Line, Bayer, Johnson & Johnson, Stryker, Target, Publix, Walmart, Mt. Sinai Hospital, State Farm Insurance Company, United Automobile Insurance Company, Baptist Health South Florida, South Miami Hospital, and International Airplane Leasing.  Aronfeld has been named one of Florida Magazine's "20 Most Intriguing Floridians" and has appeared nationwide as a legal expert in national media. He runs the popular TikTok account @cruiseshiplawyer, which has more than 160,000 followers and millions of likes. Through the platform, he educates travelers about maritime law, passenger rights, and vacation safety. He also explains real-life cases and offers a behind-the-scenes look at lawsuits against major cruise lines, including Carnival, Royal Caribbean, and Disney. Aronfeld is a published author and frequent blogger whose work has appeared in the American Association for Justice's Trial Magazine and other national legal and consumer publications.  Community service and helping others are important parts of Aronfeld's life. In 2010, he founded Lawyers to the Rescue, a nonprofit humanitarian initiative that provides legal aid and supports civil rights activism for people in need.  ABOUT SUPER LAWYERS Super Lawyers recognizes outstanding attorneys across more than 70 practice areas who have achieved significant peer recognition and professional success. The list represents the top 5 percent of Florida lawyers. Annual selections are based on a patented multiphase process that includes a statewide lawyer survey, independent candidate research, and peer evaluations by practice area. The result is a credible, comprehensive, and diverse list of exceptional attorneys. Super Lawyers lists are published nationwide in Super Lawyers Magazine and in leading city and regional magazines and newspapers across the country.  ABOUT ARONFELD TRIAL LAWYERS Miami-based Aronfeld Trial Lawyers was founded in 1991 by Managing Partner and board-certified trial lawyer Spencer Aronfeld. The firm represents clients with serious injury claims involving maritime law, automobile accidents, premises liability, cycling accidents, slip-and-fall incidents, wrongful death, medical and dental malpractice, product liability, workplace injuries, sexual assault, and other matters. Every client receives the benefit of the firm's experience, resources, and commitment. Personal injury law is the firm's calling, and its nationally recognized legal advocates work for clients—not big business. All attorneys and staff members at the firm are bilingual in English and Spanish. To learn more, visit https://www.aronfeld.com/.
