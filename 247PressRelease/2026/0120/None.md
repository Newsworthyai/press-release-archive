# McCready Law Adds Indiana Trial Attorney Kellie Clark to Indianapolis Office

INDIANAPOLIS, IN, January 20, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- McCready Law has hired Kellie Clark as an associate attorney in the firm's Indianapolis office, expanding its personal injury litigation team in Indiana.  Clark represents individuals and families in personal injury cases and handles matters involving children, schools, institutional abuse, sexual assault, premises liability and animal attacks. She also brings a defense-side foundation from early in her career and has tried cases to verdict, including a federal jury trial.  "Kellie is a trial lawyer who understands what clients carry into these cases and what it takes to move them forward," said Michael McCready, founder and managing partner of McCready Law. "Her background in high-stakes litigation and focus on protecting vulnerable people will strengthen our work in Indiana."  Clark has been recognized by Indiana Super Lawyers (2023â€“2026), The National Trial Lawyers Top 40 Under 40, and was previously named an Indiana Rising Star from 2017 to 2020. Clark is a member of the Indiana State Bar Association, the American Bar Association and the Indiana Trial Lawyers Association.   "People come to a personal injury lawyer after something has changed their lives. It's my mission to take on the legal fight and fight for the case in court when that's what justice requires," Clark said.   Clark earned her Juris Doctor, cum laude, from the University of Dayton School of Law. While in law school, she served as Chief Justice of the Moot Court and worked as a staff writer for the University of Dayton Law Review. She also received honors tied to academics, intellectual property and pro bono work, including the Brother Raymond Fitz Student Leader for Justice Award.  Clark will be based at McCready Law's Indianapolis office at 8200 Haverstick Rd., Suite 210, Indianapolis, IN 46240.  For more information about Clark or McCready Law's services, visit https://mccreadylaw.com/.  About McCready Law McCready Law was founded in 1999 and handles all fields of personal injury law, including car accidents, slip and fall incidents, wrongful death, premises liability, product liability and medical malpractice cases. The firm has recovered more than $300 million for clients and maintains a policy of no fee unless the client wins.
