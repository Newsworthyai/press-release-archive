# Friendly Recovery Center Releases Educational Guide on Seasonal Depression

TUSTIN, CA, March 08, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Friendly Recovery Center has published a new blog resource titled "What Is Seasonal Depression?" to help readers understand the symptoms and causes of seasonal affective disorder (SAD).  The article discusses how reduced daylight exposure, biological rhythm disruption, and environmental changes can influence mood and energy levels. It outlines common symptoms such as fatigue, withdrawal, and changes in sleep or appetite patterns.  The blog also reviews therapeutic approaches that may be recommended, including light therapy, counseling, and structured outpatient programs for those whose symptoms significantly impact daily functioning.  Friendly Recovery Center provides regional services including outpatient mental health treatment in Costa Mesa for individuals seeking flexible care options.
