# Mariah Siner Releases Christian Teddies Coloring: A Gentle, Scripture-Filled Coloring Experience for Children and Families This Thanksgiving Season

PEMBROKE, PINES, FL, February 06, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- As families across the country gather for Thanksgiving and reflect on gratitude, faith, and togetherness, author and illustrator Mariah Siner introduces a heartfelt new release crafted especially for children and believers of all ages. Her latest book, Christian Teddies Coloring, brings Scripture to life through charming hand-drawn teddy bears and stuffed animals living out the Word of God in their everyday world.  Centered on faith, creativity, and quiet reflection, Christian Teddies Coloring invites children to learn God's Word in a playful, meaningful way. Each page pairs adorable illustrations with powerful verses, making the book a gentle tool for spiritual growth and family bonding during the holiday season.  "This book was created with love and purpose," Mariah shares. "I wanted families, especially young children, to engage with Scripture in a way that feels joyful, comforting, and full of wonder. Coloring is peaceful, and when paired with God's Word, it becomes something truly special."  Designed to support both learning and devotion, Christian Teddies Coloring offers: • 21 original hand-drawn illustrations • 25 bubbled Scriptures from the Sword of the Spirit • 1 heartfelt prayer  From playful teddy bears to whimsical stuffed animal scenes, every illustration is intentionally paired with a verse meant to uplift, inspire, and plant seeds of truth in young hearts. The book also serves as a comforting activity for parents, grandparents, teachers, and ministry leaders who want to nurture faith through hands-on engagement.  As families look for meaningful, screen-free activities for the holidays, Christian Teddies Coloring stands out as both wholesome entertainment and a beautiful introduction to Scripture. Whether shared around the table after Thanksgiving dinner or used during quiet time at home, the book encourages children to pause, color, learn, and reflect on God's living Word.  With this new release, Mariah Siner continues her mission to create faith-centered resources that help children grow spiritually while enjoying the simple joys of creativity. Christian Teddies Coloring is a reminder that learning the Bible can be both engaging and deeply comforting one teddy bear at a time.
