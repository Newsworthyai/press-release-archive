# Dynamic Foot Ankle Center Welcomes Nurse Practitioner Natalie Collins, MSN, FNP-C.

FREDERICKSBURG & LORTON, VA, January 27, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Dynamic Foot & Ankle Center is pleased to announce the addition of Natalie Collins, MSN, FNP-C, to its clinical team. Natalie joins the practice as a full-time Nurse Practitioner. She will see patients at both the Fredericksburg and Lorton offices, broadening the center's capacity to treat whole-body musculoskeletal conditions.  Natalie Collins, MSN, FNP-C Natalie is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with specialized training and extensive clinical experience in orthopedic and musculoskeletal care. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Drexel University and her Master of Science in Nursing from Marymount University. Natalie worked for 7.5 years as a registered nurse, caring for orthopedic, surgical, and trauma patients at Level I Trauma Centers across the DMV. That background allows her to approach patient care with a strong understanding of both surgical and non-surgical orthopedic management.  Clinical focus and services Natalie will provide comprehensive, evidence-based musculoskeletal care emphasizing patient education, shared decision-making, and individualized treatment plans. Services she will offer at both locations include:  - Joint injections  - Trigger point injections - Bracing - Evaluation and non-surgical management of knee, back, shoulder, and other joint pain  "We're excited to expand our scope of care to treat the body as a whole," said Dr.Danielle VonDerLinden, CEO. "Natalie's strong orthopedic background and patient-centered approach align perfectly with our mission to help patients return to the activities they love."  About Dynamic Foot & Ankle Center  Dynamic Foot & Ankle Center provides specialized foot care, ankle, and musculoskeletal care with patient-centered treatment plans. With convenient locations in Fredericksburg and Lorton, the center is committed to high-quality, evidence-based treatment for acute and chronic conditions.  Patients in Fredericksburg and Lorton may schedule appointments with Natalie Collins by calling 540-898-6500 in Fredericksburg or 571-418-8670 in Lorton, or by visiting www.dynamicfootandanklecenter.com.
