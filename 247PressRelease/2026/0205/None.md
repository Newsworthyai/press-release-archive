# "Fear Struck" Wins Again: Kay A. Oliver Claims 2026 International Impact Award

LOS ANGELES, CA, February 05, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Award-winning and critically acclaimed, "Fear Struck" has been honored with the 2026 International Impact Award for Crime Fiction - Psychological Thriller, reinforcing its status as a standout in contemporary psychological thrillers and making the audience proud to be associated with such a recognized work.  Having previously received top honors at the Fall 2025 BookFest Awards and dual recognition at the 2025 Best Book Awards, "Fear Struck's" multiple awards and International recognition inspire admiration and trust in its quality and impact.
