# REI Accelerator Reviews Releases Free Multifamily Readiness Checklist for New Investors

NEW YORK, NY, July 10, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- REI Accelerator Reviews has announced the release of its free Multifamily Readiness Checklist, a practical resource designed to help individuals assess whether they have the habits, systems, and preparation needed before pursuing multifamily real estate opportunities.  The checklist is intended for people who want a structured way to review their investing process without replacing professional financial, legal, or tax advice. It walks users through key questions about goal setting, accountability, networking, lead generation, education, and long-term planning.  The resource reflects the philosophy that has guided REI Accelerator Reviews since its founding in 2022: consistent action produces better results than collecting more information.  "We saw talented people getting stuck," the team says. "The information was out there, but they needed someone to help them put it into action."  According to the founders, the checklist encourages users to focus on preparation before pursuing opportunities.  "Our goal isn't just to teach concepts. We want people executing every week and moving closer to their next deal."  The resource also emphasizes accountability, one of the principles that has shaped the company's approach to helping investors grow.  "We measure success by action. You can have great information, but if you never execute, nothing changes."  Beyond investing, the checklist encourages readers to think about their long-term purpose and the impact they hope to create.  "We don't want success to stop with one person. We want it to benefit families and future generations."  Why Preparation Matters  Many people interested in real estate investing spend months—or even years—consuming books, podcasts, and videos without developing a repeatable action plan. While education is valuable, preparation often determines whether someone consistently follows through.  Several studies highlight the cost of poor planning and inconsistent execution:  Research from the U.S. Small Business Administration has found that inadequate planning is one of the leading contributors to business failure. A study by Dr. Gail Matthews at Dominican University of California found that people who write down specific goals and report progress to an accountability partner are significantly more likely to achieve them than those who simply keep goals in mind.  Gallup has reported that employees and business owners who receive meaningful coaching and accountability demonstrate higher engagement and productivity than those who work without structured support.  The National Association of REALTORS® consistently reports that networking and referrals remain among the most effective ways people identify real estate opportunities, highlighting the importance of relationship-building over waiting for opportunities to appear.  Use This in 15 Minutes  The Multifamily Readiness Checklist is designed to be completed in about 15 minutes.  Users are encouraged to:  Define one clear long-term investing goal. Review whether they have consistent weekly action steps. Evaluate how they generate new opportunities. Identify gaps in accountability. List three relationships they can strengthen this month. Choose one action to complete within the next seven days.  The completed checklist can then serve as a baseline for future progress reviews.  Common Mistakes People Make  According to REI Accelerator Reviews, many aspiring investors encounter the same avoidable obstacles:  Consuming information without creating an action plan. Trying to manage every task alone instead of building systems. Waiting for the "perfect" opportunity before taking consistent action. Neglecting relationship-building until capital or opportunities are urgently needed. Failing to review progress and adjust goals regularly.  The new checklist encourages users to identify these habits early and replace them with simple, repeatable routines.  The free Multifamily Readiness Checklist is available for anyone who wants to better understand their current level of preparedness and create a practical roadmap for consistent improvement.  Readers are encouraged to download the checklist, complete all sections in one sitting, identify one improvement they can make this week, and revisit the checklist every month to measure their progress over time.  REI Accelerator Reviews is an online real estate investing company based in Columbus, Georgia, and founded in 2022 by Jonathan Cronin and Larry Kite. The company helps real estate investors accelerate their journey through multifamily investing with one-on-one coaching, education, accountability, virtual assistant support, capital raising guidance, and services that help investors source off-market apartment opportunities. Guided by faith-based values, the company aims to help investors build generational wealth while creating lasting generational impact.
