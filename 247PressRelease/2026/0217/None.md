# Betsy H. Lambert's The Winding Path: A Poetic Journey Offers a Reflective, Faith-Rooted Look at Aging, Renewal, and the Courage to Begin Again

RALEIGH, NC, February 17, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Ahead of her featured interview on The Chris Voss Show on February 18, 2026, inspirational poet and author Betsy H. Lambert spotlights her poetry collection, The Winding Path: A Poetic Journey—an 84-page work exploring aging, acceptance, and renewal with spiritual depth and emotional clarity. Published in August 2025, the book is available now on Amazon in paperback, with official updates here and on Facebook (search: The Winding Path).  "The Winding Path is a refreshing, light-hearted poetic journey that examines the joys and challenges of aging."  Moving through themes of gratitude, grief, identity, and inner strength, The Winding Path pairs spiritual reflection with lived experience—especially for readers navigating transition, reinvention, and the later seasons of self-discovery.  "This collection is my awakening to acceptance of my seasoned self and a hopeful look toward what comes next," Lambert said.  Book Details  Title: The Winding Path: A Poetic Journey Author: Betsy H. Lambert Publication Date: August 2025 Format: Paperback Page Count: 84 pages ISBN-13: 979-8-34-995875-5 List Price: $23.00 Amazon: Click here to purchase  A Poetry Collection for Readers Seeking Peace, Purpose, and Reflection  The Winding Path is especially meaningful for:  · Readers navigating grief, reinvention, or transition  · Women entering a new season of life  · Faith-rooted audiences seeking spiritual encouragement  · Legacy-minded families and gift-givers  It also makes a thoughtful gift for mothers, grandmothers, retirees, and loved ones who appreciate poetry grounded in lived experience.  A Legacy Story Rooted in Family and Courage  Lambert's work carries a deeply personal foundation shaped by generational history. The book is dedicated to her mother, Ann Russ Holaday—a Women Airforce Service Pilots (WASP) veteran who ferried bombers across the country for the U.S. Army Air Forces. Holaday later became an artist and master woodcarver, leaving an enduring creative influence that continues to echo through her family.  Inspiration and Literary Credibility  Lambert is a published poet whose work has been recognized in literary circles and faith communities. She is a contributor to Women Living on Purpose, a 2021 Amazon International Best Seller, and her poetry has been selected for publication in multiple anthologies, including the 2023 and 2024 Bards North Carolina Poetry Anthologies and the 2025 Eastern Sea Bards Anthology. She continues to write sermon-based verse and additional poetry rooted in spiritual reflection, lived wisdom, and present-day themes.
