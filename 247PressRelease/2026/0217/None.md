# Jana Kramer Releases Emotional New Single "Nothing Without You," Written by Gretchen Rossi

LOS ANGELES, CA, February 17, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Country music artist Jana Kramer unveils her powerful new single, "Nothing Without You," a faith-driven ballad written by television personality and songwriter Gretchen Rossi and produced by acclaimed producer Bryan Todd. Recorded at the iconic Blackbird Studios in Nashville, the track is now available on all major streaming platforms worldwide.  Blending heartfelt storytelling with soaring vocals, "Nothing Without You" explores themes of resilience, healing, and the strength found through faith and love. The emotionally charged release reflects a deeply personal narrative brought to life through Kramer's signature vocal style and cinematic production.  Early momentum around the single has been strong, with the track gaining attention across both Country and Christian music audiences and generating positive response from listeners internationally. Warner Music Group has secured European distribution, with the song already reaching radio audiences in Germany.  The collaboration between Kramer, Rossi, and producer Bryan Todd grew out of a shared vision to bring meaningful storytelling back to the forefront of modern country music. With its honest lyrics and powerful delivery, "Nothing Without You" aims to connect with listeners navigating their own journeys of growth, loss, and renewal.
