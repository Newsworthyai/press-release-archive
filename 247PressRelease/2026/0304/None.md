# Brian Banner Named Attorney of the Month for Attorney Journals Orange County

ORANGE COUNTY, CA, March 04, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- In Attorney Journals Orange County (Volume 239, 2026), Brian Banner, founder of Banner Attorneys, APLC, is recognized as Attorney of the Month for his deeply personal, client-focused approach to personal injury law.  Banner's path to becoming an attorney was anything but traditional. Raised in a blue-collar family, he developed a strong work ethic and a passion for standing up for the underdog. Although he never originally planned to become a lawyer, his career trajectory shifted after working in a law firm and discovering his passion for litigation and helping injured individuals navigate complex legal and insurance systems.  A pivotal moment in his life came just weeks before taking the bar exam, when Banner survived a serious head-on collision. The accident left him with long-term injuries requiring surgery and extensive treatment. That experience profoundly shaped his practice. Having personally endured the physical pain, emotional stress, and insurance frustrations that many of his clients face, Banner brings empathy and patience to every case.  In 14 years, Banner grew his firm from a one-man operation working at his kitchen table into a thriving practice with multiple attorneys and support staff. The firm has recovered more than $10 million in verdicts and settlements, including multiple six-figure results for auto accidents, premises liability, and property damage cases. Beyond the numbers, Banner emphasizes meaningful client outcomes—like helping a seriously injured single mother secure a $1.5 million settlement that allowed her to buy a home and rebuild her life.  Unlike large, volume-driven firms, Banner built his practice around personalized attention and authentic client relationships. His philosophy centers on discipline, gratitude, and treating every client as a person—not a case file. He hires self-motivated team members, avoids micromanagement, and fosters a culture focused on service and results.  Outside the courtroom, Banner remains committed to community involvement. His firm supports youth athletics, sponsors local schools and programs, partners with charitable organizations, and actively serves the Latino community, with much of the staff fluent in Spanish.  At its core, Banner's success stems from a simple belief: when people's lives are turned upside down by injury, they need someone who listens, genuinely cares, and fights to restore stability. Through empathy, discipline, and unwavering advocacy, Brian Banner has built a firm that truly lives up to its promise—putting clients first.
