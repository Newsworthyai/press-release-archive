# One Brave Challenger Takes on the Mega Pancake Challenge at Ol' South Pancake House

FORT WORTH, TX, February 11, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Stacks were high and the energy even higher Thursday morning as Ol' South Pancake House – Fort Worth hosted a Mega Pancake Challenge at its iconic University Drive location. Beginning at 8:30 a.m., a single determined participant stepped up to take on the ultimate breakfast feat: 8 pounds of pancakes in one hour. Click to Watch Video  As the challenge unfolded, the dining room quickly filled with cheers, laughter, and encouragement from fellow guests and staff. What started as one person's challenge became a full-restaurant experience, turning an ordinary morning into a memorable moment of Fort Worth fun.  "This was such a great reminder of why we love what we do," said Rex Benson, owner of Ol' South Pancake House. "One person took on the challenge, but the whole restaurant got behind them. That sense of community—mixed with good food and a little friendly pressure—is what Old South has always been about."  Known for its World-Famous German Pancakes and 24/7 service at the Fort Worth location, Ol' South Pancake House has been a gathering place for generations. The Mega Pancake Challenge added a playful twist to the restaurant's long-standing tradition, giving guests something to cheer for while enjoying hot coffee and classic comfort food.  Whether finishing the challenge or simply giving it a valiant effort, the participant earned plenty of applause and left with bragging rights—and a morning that guests won't soon forget.  Ol' South Pancake House – Fort Worth is open 24 hours a day and welcomes guests anytime for breakfast, lunch, or late-night favorites. Ol' South has a second location in Burleson.  For more information, visit www.olsouthpancakehouse.com.
