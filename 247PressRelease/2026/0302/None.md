# Universal Casualty RRG Inc Attends NIADA National Leadership Conference

OKLAHOMA CITY, OK, March 02, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Universal Casualty Risk Retention Group, Inc (UCRRG Inc), an Oklahoma-based risk retention group dedicated to serving the auto service industry, proudly attended the National Leadership Conference hosted by the National Independent Automobile Dealers Association (NIADA).  For nearly a decade, UCRRG Inc has maintained a strong relationship with NIADA and its members, delivering tailored insurance solutions designed specifically for the unique needs of independent auto dealers nationwide.  "Universal Casualty RRG Inc has enjoyed a nine-year relationship with NIADA, and during that time we have saved members millions of dollars in insurance costs," said President Tim Derham. "Our average savings for NIADA members is approximately 30 percent compared to their prior insurance policies."  As a specialized risk retention group, UCRRG Inc focuses exclusively on understanding the operational risks, regulatory requirements, and coverage needs of independent auto dealerships. This focused approach allows the organization to provide competitive pricing, stability, and coverage structures designed specifically for the independent dealer market.  By participating in the NIADA National Leadership Conference, UCRRG Inc continues to strengthen its commitment to supporting independent dealers through education, collaboration, and cost-effective risk management solutions.  About Universal Casualty Risk Retention Group, Inc:   Universal Casualty Risk Retention was founded in 2017 for the purpose of serving the Auto Service industry.
