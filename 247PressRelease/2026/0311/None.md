# A Crown, A Mission, A Leader, Brittney Quarterman Wins Ms. Florida Corporate America 2026

ORLANDO, FL, March 11, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- In an inspiring evening celebrating leadership, purpose, and the power of professional women, Brittney Quarterman of Orlando was crowned Ms. Florida Corporate America 2026 on Saturday, March 7, 2026, during the official state competition held at the elegant Rosen Plaza Hotel.  Women from across the State of Florida gathered to compete for the prestigious title, each representing excellence in career achievement, community service, and leadership. After a day of interviews and an evening of competition, Quarterman earned the honor of representing Florida on the national stage with Ms. Corporate America.  The 2026 competition embraced the theme Blooming Elegance, An All White Soiree, symbolizing growth, transformation, and the beauty of women stepping boldly into their purpose. Guests arrived dressed in white with shades of purple, creating a visually stunning atmosphere that reflected unity, empowerment, and celebration.  The Ms. Corporate America Florida competition recognizes women who exemplify professional excellence while also demonstrating a commitment to service and community impact. Contestants competed in several segments that highlight both leadership and presence, including professional interviews, community platform presentations, and evening wear.  "Brittney Quarterman represents the essence of what Ms. Corporate America stands for," said Philomena Moncoeur, Ms. Corporate America Florida President. "She embodies confidence, professionalism, and a passion for uplifting others. We are proud to see her represent Florida as she advances to the national competition."  As Ms. Florida Corporate America 2026, Quarterman will serve as an ambassador for the organization throughout the year, promoting leadership, community involvement, and professional development. She will also represent the state at the national competition hosted by Ms. Corporate America Organization, where she will join titleholders from across the country.  The evening brought together community leaders, entrepreneurs, supporters, and advocates for women's empowerment. The event was made possible through the support of generous sponsors and partners committed to uplifting women in business and leadership.  Sponsors for the 2026 competition included Simply Success Group, Tap-In Magazine, Ori Ara, CPA Orlando, Midnighties, It's Island Time Snacking Co, One Bless Acre Farm, Flawless Events Florida, along with several other women led businesses dedicated to supporting leadership, entrepreneurship, and community impact.  The Blooming Elegance evening concluded with a celebratory crowning moment, honoring the accomplishments of all contestants and welcoming Brittney Quarterman into her new role as the official representative for Florida.  ABOUT MS. FLORIDA CORPORATE AMERICA Ms. Corporate America Florida is the official state organization affiliated with Ms. Corporate America. The program celebrates professional women who demonstrate leadership, service, and excellence in their careers while making meaningful contributions to their communities. Titleholders serve as ambassadors who inspire others through their achievements, advocacy, and dedication to empowering women.  For more information about the organization or upcoming opportunities, visit: https://floridamscorporateamerica.com
