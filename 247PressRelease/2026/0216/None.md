# Wade Casstevens Launches Personal Website Highlighting Real Estate Leadership, Community-Focused Development, and Long-Term Investment Vision

ARLINGTON, VA, February 16, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Real estate investor and developer of workforce and affordable housing Wade Casstevens today announced the launch of his official website at wadecasstevens.com.   The new site offers an overview of Casstevens' professional background, his approach to disciplined investment and development, and his role as founder and principal of Linden Property Group, a real estate investment and development firm focused on long-term value creation and responsible asset management.  "This website is a place to clearly articulate how I think about real estate, risk, and stewardship," said Wade Casstevens. "Development isn't just about buildings. It's about longevity, accountability, and creating assets that work for communities as well as investors."  The site also provides insight into Casstevens' work through Linden Property Group, including the firm's investment strategy, development portfolio, and commitment to pragmatic growth across residential and mixed-use properties. Emphasis is placed on fundamentals-driven decision-making, operational discipline, and a hands-on ownership approach.  In addition to professional highlights, wadecasstevens.com features a regularly updated section for insights and commentary, offering visitors a deeper look at Casstevens' perspective on real estate markets, development trends, and long-term investment planning.  "Real estate rewards patience and long-term planning," Casstevens added. "This platform allows me to share not only what we build, but why we build the way we do."  About Wade Casstevens Wade Casstevens is a real estate investor and developer of workforce and affordable housing across the Mid-Atlantic region. He is the founder and principal of Linden Property Group, a multifamily real estate investment firm based in Arlington, Virginia. Mr. Casstevens focuses on the acquisition, development, and management of real estate assets with an emphasis on durability, operational efficiency, and long-term value.
