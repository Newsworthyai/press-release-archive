# Keith Hall Shares Tax-Saving Secrets in New Book Hire Your Kid

ORLANDO, FL, January 14, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- In his new book, Hire Your Kid, Keith Hall reveals an innovative tax strategy designed for small business owners and parents looking to save thousands on their tax returns while teaching their children valuable life lessons. With his straightforward approach, Hall explains how you can hire your children to work in your business and save over $5,000 in taxes annually.  Born from Hall's own experience, Hire Your Kid offers a practical guide that turns everyday family expenses into business tax deductions. The book walks readers through the process of creating a job for their child, filing the necessary tax paperwork, and taking full advantage of tax benefits. Hall's unique strategy allows parents to pay their children a reasonable wage, making the wages tax-deductible, and then use the savings to fund their child's future through options like Roth IRAs.  Keith Hall's no-nonsense approach explains how small business owners can save big without adding any extra financial burden. Drawing from his 40 years of experience, Hall has helped countless entrepreneurs navigate the complex world of taxes, and now he's sharing his secrets in this easily digestible format.  The book also brings in a personal element, as Hall shares his own journey of using this strategy to benefit his own family. By following the simple steps outlined in Hire Your Kid, readers will be equipped not only to reduce their tax liability but also to strengthen their family's bond through meaningful work and shared growth.  About the Author Keith Hall, CPA, is a tax and accounting professional with over 40 years of experience helping small business owners maximize their profits and minimize their tax liabilities. He has worked with thousands of entrepreneurs to simplify complex tax strategies and deliver practical financial solutions. Through Hire Your Kid, Hall aims to equip parents and small business owners with the tools they need to save money and set their children up for a successful future.  Hire Your Kid will be available soon in both print and digital formats.
