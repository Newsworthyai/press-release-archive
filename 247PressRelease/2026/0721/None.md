# Maggiano, DiGirolamo &amp; Lizzi P.C. Supports Youth Creativity Through the 2026 K-Story Magazine Art Contest

PALISADES PARK, NJ, July 21, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Maggiano, DiGirolamo & Lizzi P.C. proudly supported the 2026 K-Story Magazine Children's Art Contest, reaffirming its commitment to encouraging creativity, education, and community engagement among young artists. Held in partnership with K-Story Magazine, the fifth annual event brought together children, families, and community members for a celebration of artistic talent and cultural pride.  This year's contest invited participants to create artwork inspired by the FIFA World Cup 2026, giving children the opportunity to express their creativity through one of the world's most celebrated sporting events. The submissions showcased a wide range of artistic styles, highlighting themes of teamwork, sportsmanship, cultural diversity, and global unity. From energetic soccer scenes to imaginative interpretations of international friendship, the artwork reflected the enthusiasm and originality of the participating students.  The exhibition and awards ceremony took place on June 20, 2026, at the Korean American Civic Empowerment Center (KACE) in Palisades Park, New Jersey. The event featured an impressive display of student artwork and recognized 20 young artists for their creativity, dedication, and artistic achievement. Family members, friends, and community supporters gathered to celebrate the accomplishments of the participants, creating an atmosphere of encouragement and inspiration.  Representing Maggiano Law Firm at the event were Michael Maggiano and Attorney Jusun Yook, who joined families and community leaders in recognizing the students' accomplishments. Their participation reflected the firm's belief that investing in youth programs and community initiatives helps create meaningful opportunities for the next generation.  For Maggiano Law Firm, supporting the Children's Art Contest is part of a broader commitment to serving the communities beyond legal representation. The firm believes that encouraging artistic expression, recognizing young talent, and supporting cultural organizations contribute to stronger, more connected neighborhoods. Attorney Jusun Yook's close relationship with the Korean American community further reinforces the firm's dedication to initiatives that bring people together and provide children with opportunities to showcase their abilities.  Maggiano Law Firm extends its appreciation to K-Story Magazine for organizing the annual contest and to KACE for hosting this year's exhibition and awards ceremony. The firm also congratulates all 20 award recipients and every student who participated, recognizing the creativity, passion, and hard work displayed throughout the competition.  By supporting programs that celebrate young talent and strengthen community connections, Maggiano Law Firm remains committed to making a positive impact both inside and outside the courtroom.  About Maggiano, DiGirolamo & Lizzi P.C.  Maggiano, DiGirolamo & Lizzi P.C. is a New Jersey personal injury law firm dedicated to protecting the rights of individuals and families. In addition to providing experienced legal representation, the firm actively supports community initiatives that promote education, creativity, and cultural engagement. Through meaningful partnerships and local outreach, Maggiano Law Firm strives to make a lasting difference in the communities it serves.
