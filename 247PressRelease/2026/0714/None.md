# Linda Keen Helps Readers and Seekers Reconnect With Their Inner Wisdom

PORTLAND, OR, July 14, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- As many people search for greater meaning, balance, and personal direction, Linda Keen continues helping individuals reconnect with their inner wisdom through her work as an author, intuitive guide, musician, and teacher.  Founder of Keen Intuition, Linda has spent years supporting people through periods of transition, self-discovery, and personal growth. Through private sessions, workshops, guided imagery, and educational resources, she encourages individuals to develop a deeper trust in themselves and their own intuitive abilities.  "People come to me when they're ready to hear their own inner voice again," Linda says. "There's always a moment when a person realizes, I'm not broken. I'm waking up."  Linda's work is rooted in the belief that intuition is not a rare gift but a natural ability available to everyone. Her approach focuses on helping people gain clarity, strengthen self-awareness, and navigate life's challenges with greater confidence and understanding.  Visitors to KeenIntuition.com can explore a collection of books focused on intuition, emotional growth, and spiritual development. Among them is Intuition Magic: Understanding Your Psychic Nature, which offers readers practical insight into understanding and trusting their intuitive experiences. Her novel, John Lennon in Heaven: Crossing the Borderlines of Being, combines storytelling and reflection while exploring themes of healing, connection, and personal transformation.  Beyond her writing, Linda's creative work includes music. Her album, Out on the Ocean, recorded with acclaimed guitarist Ray Frank, features 18 tracks of traditional melodies and vocals that reflect many of the same themes found throughout her books and teachings. Available through Amazon Music and major streaming platforms, the album continues to introduce listeners to a blend of storytelling, reflection, and musical artistry that complements her broader body of work.  The project also highlights the work of the late Ray Frank, an accomplished guitarist whose career included collaborations with respected musicians and recording experience within New York's music community. Together, the recordings serve as a musical expression of the values that continue to shape Linda's creative and educational work. The album's widespread availability preserves a collaboration that continues to showcase both Linda's creative vision and Ray Frank's enduring musical legacy.  Linda believes that meaningful change begins by listening more carefully to ourselves and trusting the wisdom that already exists within.  "People don't need to become someone new," she says. "They need permission to trust who they already are."  In addition to her books and music, Linda's website features educational content, testimonials, and information about her services. Visitors can also access resources designed to support personal growth, self-reflection, and intuitive development.  As more people seek authentic ways to navigate uncertainty and create greater balance in their lives, Linda Keen continues offering a thoughtful and compassionate path toward self-discovery and inner clarity.  About Linda Keen  Linda Keen is an author, intuitive guide, musician, and founder of Keen Intuition. Through books, workshops, private sessions, and creative works, she helps individuals strengthen self-awareness, trust their intuition, and cultivate a deeper connection to themselves and the world around them.
