# Southwest Michigan Celebrates Three Beaches Ranked Among Michigan's Top 10 by USA TODAY Readers

ST. JOSEPH, MI, July 14, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Southwest Michigan is making waves this summer, with three area beaches earning spots on USA TODAY's 2026 10Best Readers' Choice list of Michigan's Top 10 Beaches. Silver Beach County Park and Tiscornia Beach in St. Joseph, along with Warren Dunes State Park in Sawyer, were all recognized among the state's best beach destinations.  The recognition highlights Southwest Michigan's unique concentration of award-winning shoreline experiences, all located within a short drive of one another along the Lake Michigan coast. Silver Beach County Park ranked No. 4, Tiscornia Beach ranked No. 7, and Warren Dunes State Park ranked No. 10 in the statewide readers' poll.  "Having three beaches ranked among Michigan's Top 10 is an incredible honor for Southwest Michigan and speaks to the exceptional quality and variety of experiences available along our Lake Michigan shoreline," said Marcy Simpson, Executive Director of Visit Southwest Michigan. "What makes this recognition especially meaningful is that visitors don't have to choose just one. All three award-winning beaches are within easy driving distance of St. Joseph, Benton Harbor and Stevensville, making it possible to experience them all during a single visit."  The trio of beaches showcases the diversity of experiences available throughout Southwest Michigan. Visitors can climb towering dunes and explore wooded trails at Warren Dunes State Park, enjoy the vibrant waterfront atmosphere and family attractions at Silver Beach County Park and take in the scenic shoreline and lighthouse views at Tiscornia Beach.  "Few destinations can offer this kind of beach-hopping experience," Simpson added. "Visitors can discover three nationally recognized beaches and enjoy the charming downtowns, local restaurants, attractions and hospitality that make Southwest Michigan such a memorable summer destination."  The USA TODAY 10Best Readers' Choice Awards are determined through public voting following nominations by a panel of travel experts. The annual rankings recognize outstanding destinations and attractions across the country.  For more information about planning a Southwest Michigan beach getaway, visit swmichigan.org.  About Visit Southwest Michigan  Visit Southwest Michigan promotes, develops and advocates for destination experiences in St. Joseph, Benton Harbor, Stevensville and the surrounding area. Visit Southwest Michigan connects visitors and locals with award-winning beaches, outdoor recreation, arts and culture, farm and craft beverage experiences, events and year-round adventures.
