# DM Injury Law Earns Dual 'Best Law Firm' Honors in Kansas City

KANSAS CITY, MO, July 15, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- DM Injury Law, a personal injury and workers' compensation law firm based in Kansas City, has secured two regional legal distinctions from local publications. The firm was selected as the top provider in the "Best Law Firm" category for both the KC Favorites awards, presented by The Kansas City Star, and the Best of KC awards, published by The Pitch.  Both recognitions followed consecutive announcements earlier this summer. The Pitch officially released its annual Best of KC honorees on June 25, marking the second consecutive year that DM Injury Law has achieved top placement in its reader-voted legal category. The Kansas City Star subsequently made its KC Favorites selections public on July 12, confirming the firm's placement at the top of the metropolitan area's legal directory.  The annual awards evaluate regional service providers based on civic feedback, client sentiment, and overall community impact within the greater Kansas City metropolitan area. For the regional legal sector, these distinctions reflect sustained client satisfaction, structural professionalism, and consistent outcomes in multi-jurisdictional personal injury and workplace litigation.  The firm handles cases arising from motor vehicle collisions, workplace accidents, catastrophic injuries, and wrongful death claims. It operates across multiple jurisdictions, including Missouri and Kansas, where varying statutes of limitations and regional evidentiary standards govern corporate negligence and civil liability.  Local awards and community recognition can also help people identify trusted legal resources when deciding who to turn to during their recovery. After an accident, legal claims often involve insurance companies and workers' compensation laws. Having a lawyer who understands the local courts, laws, and claims process can make a difference for injured people seeking restitution.   The collective evaluation process by both media outlets spans several months of public nominations, community auditing, and final data verification. The comprehensive regional rankings serve as reference materials for local residents identifying qualified legal professionals in personal injury, negligence, and employment-related liability fields.  About DM Injury Law  Founded in 2013 and headquartered in Kansas City, DM Injury Law is a law firm focused on personal injury and workers' compensation law. The firm represents plaintiffs in legal matters involving automobile accidents, motorcycle collisions, slip-and-fall incidents, workplace injuries, and wrongful death claims. Operating with a collective team of trial lawyers, the firm manages civil claims against commercial insurers and corporations across Missouri, Kansas, and surrounding states. The practice is dedicated to securing appropriate compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, and long-term damages while providing individual guidance throughout the litigation lifecycle.
