# A Doorway to Imagination: Marva Washington Debuts 'Dimension Z' at the 2026 LA Times Festival of Books

LOS ANGELES, CA, June 29, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Adventure and the power of friendship came to life at the 2026 Los Angeles Times Festival of Books as MainSpring Books VIP Author Marva Washington introduced readers to Jaxon Discovers Dimension Z during a featured book signing and author interview on April 18–19 at the University of Southern California campus.  As one of the nation's premier literary events, the festival welcomed more than 150,000 readers, authors, educators, and book lovers to celebrate storytelling in all its forms. Among the many featured works showcased throughout the weekend, Jaxon Discovers Dimension Z stood out as a creative children's adventure that combines imagination, friendship, and personal growth.  Inspired by Washington's great-nephew, the story follows an energetic and curious young boy named Jaxon whose visit to his grandmother's vegetable garden leads to an unexpected discovery. After venturing into a forbidden tool shed, Jaxon encounters a mysterious being from another dimension and soon finds himself immersed in a mission that challenges his courage, problem-solving skills, and understanding of friendship.  Blending fantasy with relatable childhood experiences, the book encourages young readers to embrace curiosity while exploring themes of kindness, cooperation, healthy habits, and personal responsibility. Through Jaxon's journey, readers are invited into a world where imagination opens doors to new possibilities and where helping others becomes a pathway to growth and success.  During her interview, Washington shared the inspiration behind the book and discussed how Jaxon's real-life personality influenced the story's development. She reflected on the joy of writing a character whose energy, curiosity, and unique traits became the foundation for an adventure designed to entertain while offering meaningful lessons for readers of all ages.  Festival attendees also learned about the deeper themes woven throughout the narrative, including friendship, acceptance, and the importance of working together despite differences. The evolving relationship between Jaxon and his unexpected companion demonstrates how trust and understanding can emerge even from unlikely beginnings.  The appearance offered readers an opportunity to connect with an author passionate about creating stories that spark imagination while encouraging positive values. Through both the book signing and interview, Washington engaged audiences in conversations about storytelling, childhood curiosity, and the enduring impact of books that inspire young minds to dream beyond the familiar.  As the celebration continues its tradition of bringing diverse voices and stories to readers, MainSpring Books remains committed to supporting authors whose works encourage creativity, learning, and meaningful connections across generations.
