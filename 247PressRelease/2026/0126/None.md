# John Buys Your House Announces Office Relocation to Uptown Charlotte

CHARLOTTE, NC, January 26, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- John Buys Your House, a Charlotte-based real estate investment company, has officially relocated its office to a new permanent address in Uptown Charlotte.  The company's new office location is:  John Buys Your House 227 W 4th St, Suite 480 Charlotte, NC 28202  The move reflects the company's continued growth and long-term commitment to serving homeowners throughout Charlotte and the surrounding areas. Operations will continue as normal, and there is no change to the company's services or contact information.  Founded by local real estate investor John Swann, John Buys Your House specializes in purchasing residential properties directly from homeowners, offering flexible solutions for those looking to sell quickly or without traditional listing processes.  "Our move to Uptown Charlotte gives us a centralized, permanent location as we continue to expand our operations," said John Swann. "We're excited to establish this office as our long-term home base."
