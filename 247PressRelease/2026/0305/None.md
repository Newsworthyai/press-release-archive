# Business Lawyer Sasha Begum Earns Certification from Harvard Law School's Negotiation and Leadership Program

SAN ANTONIO, TX, March 05, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Sasha Begum, managing partner of Begum Pelaez-Prada Business Law, has earned certification from Harvard Law School's prestigious Negotiation and Leadership program. This certification marks a significant milestone in Begum's ongoing commitment to delivering top-tier legal representation and advocacy for clients in high-stakes negotiations.  The Negotiation and Leadership program, held from June 9-12, 2025, in Cambridge, MA, was an immersive in-person combined program. This comprehensive training offered legal professionals powerful frameworks for navigating intricate negotiation matters while sharpening leadership skills to handle tough dynamics and deliver lasting agreements.  "I completed Harvard Law School's Program on Negotiation to better advocate for my clients when the stakes are highest. In complex, high-pressure negotiations, my clients deserve more than instinct — they deserve a strategic, disciplined approach grounded in proven negotiation frameworks," said Sasha Begum, managing partner of Begum Pelaez-Prada Business Law.   As part of her professional development, Begum now possesses the physical certificate from Harvard Law School, affirming her successful completion of this prestigious program. Photos of the certificate, along with her headshot, are available upon request.  Begum added that this certification allows her to bring even greater strategic depth to negotiations on behalf of her clients. "This training strengthened my ability to protect their interests, anticipate risk, manage difficult dynamics, and create durable agreements that align with their long-term goals. I pursued this program so I can deliver stronger outcomes, greater clarity, and confident leadership at the negotiating table on their behalf."  Begum Pelaez-Prada Business Law, based in San Antonio, Texas, focuses on negotiating contracts, corporate restructuring, M&A, business formations, contracts, estate planning, wills and trusts, and domestic and international transactions. Led by Begum, who brings nearly 20 years of experience in business and international law, the firm emphasizes personalized, strategic legal solutions with a commitment to integrity, and offers bilingual services in English and Spanish.
