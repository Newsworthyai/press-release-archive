# Dr. Lynn Puana Grant for Veterans Announces 2027 Scholarship Cycle for Undergraduate Military Veterans Nationwide

ENGLEWOOD, CO, June 13, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- The Dr. Lynn Puana Grant for Veterans today opens applications for its 2027 scholarship program, awarding $1,000 to an undergraduate U.S. military veteran pursuing a degree aligned with a military career path. Founded by practicing pain physician Dr. Lynn Puana, the grant serves as a direct investment in veterans who have completed their service and now seek higher education to advance their professional trajectories.  To qualify for the Dr. Lynn Puana Grant for Veterans, applicants must meet four criteria. First, they must hold U.S. military veteran status, including those who have completed service in active duty, the Reserves, or the National Guard. Second, they must be currently enrolled or officially accepted as an undergraduate student at an accredited college or university within the United States. Third, they must pursue an undergraduate degree that follows a career path in the military. Fourth, applicants must agree to the terms and conditions of the essay contest, which forms the core of the selection process.  The $1,000 award aims to reduce financial barriers for veterans transitioning from service to academic life. Whether covering tuition, textbooks, or other educational expenses, the grant provides tangible support to those who have already given significantly to their country. By focusing specifically on veterans pursuing military-aligned career paths, the Dr. Lynn Puana Grant for Veterans recognizes the unique expertise and discipline that service members bring to fields such as logistics, cybersecurity, healthcare administration, engineering, and leadership development.  Dr. Lynn Puana, who earned her medical degree from Creighton University School of Medicine and completed her internal medicine residency at Baylor Scott & White Hospital, established this grant as an extension of her clinical mission. With more than two decades of experience treating patients with chronic pain, trauma, and addiction, Dr. Lynn Puana understands the profound challenges veterans face during reintegration into civilian life. The grant reflects her belief that education serves as a powerful tool for rebuilding identity and purpose after service.  The essay contest requires applicants to articulate how their military experience has shaped their academic and professional goals, as well as how they plan to contribute to their communities following degree completion. Dr. Lynn Puana emphasizes that personal narrative and self-reflection are essential components of growth, making the essay format a fitting vehicle for selecting a recipient who embodies resilience and determination.  Key dates for the 2027 scholarship cycle are as follows: the deadline to apply is May 15, 2027. The scholarship winner will be announced on June 15, 2027. While the grant is administered from Englewood, Colorado, no geographic restrictions apply, and eligible veterans from any U.S. state or territory may apply.  For complete application instructions, essay prompts, and submission guidelines, interested individuals should visit the official scholarship website. The Dr. Lynn Puana Grant for Veterans does not charge any application fees and encourages all qualifying undergraduate veterans to apply.
