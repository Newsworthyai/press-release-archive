# Unique Lighting &amp; Home Decor Closes 2025 with over $7,500 In-Kind Donation to the National Affordable Housing Network

BUTTE, MT, January 05, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Unique Lighting & Home Decor, a women-owned lighting retailer headquartered in uptown Butte, MT, is closing out 2025 with a significant community contribution, donating  over $7,500 in lighting fixtures, accessories, hardware, and bulbs to the National Affordable Housing Network (NAHN).  Housing affordability continues to tighten across the state. In 2024, 28.9% of Montana households spent 30% or more of their income on housing and utilities, meeting the federal definition of cost-burdened households, according to the U.S. Census Bureau.  The timing of Unique's contribution aligns directly with NAHN's mission to expand long-term affordable homeownership opportunities, strengthen community housing initiatives, and improve financial literacy for households navigating housing cost pressures.  "After two decades of working with Affordable Housing, I am so excited to be able to provide this level of contribution to an organization that I wholeheartedly believe in," said Kellie Gribben, Owner and Founder of Unique Lighting & Home Decor.  Gribben continued: "They're [NAHN] work ensures housing affordability isn't just about lowering upfront costs, it's about creating homes and communities that remain sustainable for families over time. Their mission impacts entire neighborhoods and the families that live in them. I'm proud to support that momentum for Southwest Montana as these economic pressures continue to challenge households across the area."  The lighting materials provided by Unique Lighting & Home Decor will support NAHN-aligned housing initiatives across Southwest Montana in 2026, helping partner organizations maximize build budgets and reduce material cost barriers at a regional level.  This contribution, coordinated in December 2025, represents the largest in-kind nonprofit donation Unique Lighting & Home Decor has made in the company's history, reinforcing the role local businesses continue to play in supporting nonprofits advancing affordable housing outcomes.  About the National Affordable Housing Network (NAHN)  Founded in 1994, the National Affordable Housing Network is a nonprofit organization based in Butte, Montana, dedicated to advancing affordable, high-performance housing. NAHN supports long-term homeownership through subdivision development, historic building housing initiatives, and financial literacy programs that strengthen community housing stability. The organization also provides technical assistance and educational support to nonprofits and community housing partners throughout the United States, including Southwest Montana.  About Unique Lighting & Home Decor  Unique Lighting & Home Decor is a women-owned lighting retailer headquartered in Butte, Montana, serving communities across Southwest Montana. The store specializes in lighting fixtures and customized lighting design through one-on-one consultations, with a long tradition of local community support.  Website: https://buttelighting.com
