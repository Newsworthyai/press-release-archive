# Award-Winning Author Xuemo's Latest Philosophical Work Tops Charts,Offering a Blueprint for Authentic Living

NEW YORK, NY, March 01, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Xuemo's latest English book, Reclaim the Throne of Your Heart, has reached No. 1 New Release in Eastern Philosophy on Amazon. Centered on the idea of "mastering your mind to shape your destiny," the book addresses how individuals can build clarity, independence, and resilience in an age of information overload and constant pressure.  The work presents a structured five-step framework focused on cognitive awareness and personal development. It guides readers to reassess external influences, clarify personal values, strengthen self-observation, improve concentration and insight, and refine behavioral patterns in daily life. Through a practical and systematic approach, the book aims to help readers reduce emotional drain, enhance decision-making capacity, and maintain mental clarity in complex environments.  Xuemo has published more than 80 books, with his works translated into over 20 languages. His novel Curses of the Kingdom of Xixia won the 2024 NYC Big Book Award for World Literature. The novel Wild Fox Ridge won the 2024 International Impact Book Awards and the 2025 NYC Big Book Award, and was honored with the 2025 International Book Awards.. His writings are increasingly recognized in the fields of international literature and cross-cultural studies. The release of this new book marks a further expansion of his work into the areas of personal development and cognitive management.  Now !
