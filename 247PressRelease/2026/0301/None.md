# Surge Airbnb Management Announces Expansion to Four High-Growth States

HOUSTON, TX, March 01, 2026 /24-7PressRelease/ -- Surge Airbnb Management, recognized for its exceptional five-star ratings and property owner satisfaction, today announced expansion into four sought-after markets: Arizona, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Hawaii.  The expansion reinforces Surge's position as a premier short-term rental management company, bringing founder Humberto Marquez's expertise as a real estate investor and experienced rental operator to property owners seeking professional management partners in these thriving markets. "Arizona, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Hawaii represent incredible opportunities for short-term rental property owners," said Marquez. "These markets combine strong tourism fundamentals with growing demand for professionally managed vacation rentals. Our track record of happy property owners and five-star guest reviews positions us perfectly to serve these communities."  Surge has built its reputation on delivering measurable results for property owners while maintaining the highest standards of guest service. The company's data-driven approach to pricing, marketing, and operations has consistently generated superior returns for property owners while earning exceptional guest feedback.  Property owners in these four new markets will gain access to Surge's comprehensive management platform, including professional listing optimization, dynamic revenue management, round-the-clock guest services, quality assurance protocols, and experienced local market expertise.  "What sets Surge apart is our commitment to both sides of the equation," Marquez explained. "Property owners see better financial performance, and guests experience the five-star service that has become our hallmark. This dual focus has created sustainable success across all our markets."  The company's expansion into Arizona, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Hawaii comes as property owners increasingly recognize the value of professional management in optimizing their short-term rental investments. Surge's proven systems help owners navigate local regulations, maintain property quality, and achieve occupancy rates that exceed market averages.  Each market offers unique advantages: Arizona's year-round appeal, North Carolina's mountain and coastal destinations, Tennessee's tourism growth, and Hawaii's premium vacation rental landscape. Surge's management approach is customized to leverage each market's specific strengths while maintaining consistent service standards.  Property owners interested in Surge's management services can expect transparent communication, detailed financial reporting, proactive property maintenance, and the peace of mind that comes from partnering with a company known for its five-star reputation.  About Surge Airbnb Management  Surge Airbnb Management delivers professional short-term rental management services that maximize property owner returns while ensuring exceptional guest experiences. Led by founder Humberto Marquez, a real estate investor and short-term rental expert, Surge has established a track record of five-star reviews from both property owners and guests.
