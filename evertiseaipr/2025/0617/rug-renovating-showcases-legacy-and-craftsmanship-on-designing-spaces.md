# Rug Renovating Showcases Legacy and Craftsmanship on Designing Spaces

Rug Renovating, a name synonymous with rug restoration and cleaning in the United States, recently graced the screens of Designing Spaces, an award-winning home improvement show. This feature not only underscores the company’s enduring legacy but also shines a light on the art and science behind rug restoration, a craft that has been perfected over 125 years. The segment, which aired in the New York market, offered viewers a rare glimpse into the meticulous processes that go into restoring and preserving rugs of all types. From antique handmade pieces to modern designer rugs, Rug Renovating’s team of master craftspeople demonstrated their unparalleled expertise and dedication to bringing these textiles back to their former glory.

 Laura Iskyan, General Manager at Rug Renovating, expressed pride in the company’s feature on Designing Spaces, viewing it as a testament to their commitment to excellence and craftsmanship. This exposure is significant not just for the company but for the preservation of cultural heritage, as many of the rugs restored by Rug Renovating are not only family heirlooms but also pieces of historical importance.

 The importance of this feature extends beyond the company’s reputation. It highlights the value of preserving traditional crafts in an age where mass production often overshadows artisanal skills. For homeowners, designers, and collectors, Rug Renovating’s work ensures that the beauty and history embodied in these rugs are not lost to time. The segment serves as a reminder of the importance of choosing expert care for valuable and sentimental pieces, ensuring they can be enjoyed by future generations.

 As Rug Renovating continues to set the gold standard in rug services, its feature on Designing Spaces not only celebrates its history but also educates the public on the importance of proper rug care and restoration. This is a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and the enduring value of preserving beauty and history through expert care.
