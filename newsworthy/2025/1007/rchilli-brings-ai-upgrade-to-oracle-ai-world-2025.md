# RChilli Brings AI Upgrade to Oracle AI World 2025

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Oct 7, 2025 @ 8:00 AM Pacific — RChilli, a leading provider of AI-driven recruitment solutions and an Oracle partner, is again exhibiting at Oracle AI World, October 13-16, 2025, at The Venetian Convention & Expo Center in Las Vegas.

As a trusted Oracle partner, RChilli consistently evolves its product suite to meet the dynamic needs of hiring teams. Its advanced technology enhances the recruitment process through automation, enriched data quality, and bias-free evaluation—all of which can be integrated with Oracle Fusion Cloud Human Capital Management (HCM).

RChilli’s product suite offers significant value to organizations through its integration with Oracle Cloud HCM solutions. Oracle AI World is a perfect opportunity for companies to explore first-hand RChilli’s AI-powered solutions, including Candidate Profile Import, Essential Data Enhancer, Full Database Reprocessing, List Of Values, Customizable Taxonomy, Redact & Design, Browser Assistant, Email Importer, and Bulk Data Import.

“We’re excited to introduce RChilli’s AI Agents for Recruitment at Oracle AI World 2025,” said Josh Adams, Director of ERP Operations, RChilli. “These advanced virtual assistants are designed to automate crucial hiring tasks such as profile analysis, pre-screening, data validation, and interview scheduling. With a strong focus on reducing bias and supporting inclusive hiring practices, RChilli AI agents streamline the recruitment process and ensure that every candidate is evaluated fairly and efficiently, enabling organizations to make smarter, faster hiring decisions.”RChilli has a long history of engineering solutions that support recruitment processes for both recruiters and candidates. With automated extraction and standardization of data, RChilli has helped organizations maintain well-structured and enriched candidate databases, contributing to improved decision-making and reduced time-to-hire.

“At RChilli, we’ve built our solutions on the foundation of responsible AI, ensuring that every innovation enhances trust, transparency, and real business value,” said Vinay Johar, CEO, RChilli. “Our collaboration with Oracle allows us to deliver intelligent recruitment automation at scale—helping organizations achieve cleaner data, reduce manual work, and accelerate smarter hiring decisions.”Meet RChilli in PersonAttendees at Oracle AI World are invited to visit the RChilli booth #6029 to experience live demos, explore real-world success stories, and meet one-on-one with product experts.

Those interested can also pre-book meetings to discuss their recruitment goals and discover how RChilli can help them achieve greater efficiency, reduce bias, and enhance candidate experiences using AI-powered solutions purpose-built for Oracle environments.

For more information about RChilli participation in Oracle AI World 2025 or to schedule a meeting during the event, please visit “Meet RChilli At Oracle AI World 2025.”
