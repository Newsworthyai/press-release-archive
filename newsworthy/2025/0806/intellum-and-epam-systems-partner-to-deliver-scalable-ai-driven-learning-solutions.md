# Intellum and EPAM Systems Partner to Deliver Scalable, AI-Driven Learning Solutions

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Aug 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading provider of scalable learning management solutions, today announced a strategic partnership with EPAM Systems, Inc. (NYSE: EPAM), a global leader in digital engineering and consulting services. This collaboration aims to empower organizations to deliver personalized, AI-driven learning experiences that drive meaningful business outcomes.

EPAM brings over 30 years of experience in software engineering, digital platform development, and AI-enabled transformation services. Their expertise complements Intellum’s robust platform capabilities, enabling organizations to scale engaging learning experiences across diverse audiences.

“EPAM’s leadership in AI-enabled transformation perfectly complements our mission to innovate how learning drives growth," said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum. "Together, we’re redefining what’s possible by combining cutting-edge AI with scalable learning experiences—helping organizations deliver more personalized, impactful education at speed and scale."Through this partnership, organizations can leverage EPAM’s AI-driven engineering expertise with Intellum’s advanced learning platform to build smarter, more adaptive education programs. By uniting powerful personalization, real-time insights, and intelligent automation, the collaboration empowers enterprises to deliver learning experiences that continuously evolve to meet the needs of users and the demands of the business.

“At EPAM, we are committed to helping enterprises harness the power of AI and digital innovation to achieve meaningful transformation,” said Angela Masella, VP, Workforce Experience Consulting - EPAM Systems. “Partnering with Intellum allows us to extend that vision into the learning space. Together, we’re enabling organizations to deliver smarter, more adaptive learning experiences that drive real business impact.”“Today’s enterprises need learning solutions that are as intelligent and agile as the challenges they face,” said Eric Zenor, Workforce Experience Consulting- EPAM Systems. “By combining Intellum’s scalable platform with our expertise in AI and digital engineering, we’re helping clients create personalized learning ecosystems that adapt in real time and drive measurable performance at scale.”About IntellumIntellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights.

About EPAM SystemsEPAM Systems, Inc. (NYSE: EPAM) is a leading global provider of digital engineering, cloud, and AI-enabled transformation services. Since 1993, EPAM has leveraged its software engineering expertise to become a trusted partner for global enterprises and startups, delivering innovative solutions that drive business value. Learn more at www.epam.com.
