# Join the Mission to Support Rare Diseases at the 2025 Rose Gala by To Cure A Rose Foundation

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Aug 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” Trey Morris, auctioneer from Morris Auctioneers, is set to lend his talent to the upcoming Rose Gala, a spectacular event dedicated to raising funds for the To Cure A Rose Foundation. The gala, scheduled for Saturday, August 9, promises an evening filled with hope, music, and impact at the Omni Barton Creek Resort in the heart of Texas Hill Country.

"I am honored to be a part of this incredible event that aims to make a real difference in the lives of children battling rare diseases," expressed Trey Morris.The 2025 Rose Gala will commence with a Cocktail Reception and Silent Auction at 5:30 PM, followed by a Dinner Program at 6:30 PM. The night will reach its peak with the Live Auction and Mission Paddle Raise at 8:00 PM, leading into an After Party Celebration at 9:00 PM. The event calls for all attendees to don their finest Cocktail Attire for an evening of elegance and purpose for a good cause.

Highlighting the festivities, the gala will pay tribute to Dr. Z. Leah Harris, the esteemed Chair of Pediatrics at Dell Medical School, with the prestigious 2025 Patient First Impact Leadership Award, shedding light on Dr. Harris's groundbreaking work in advancing pediatric care and rare disease research.

The evening's entertainment will be headlined by the talented Casey McPherson, lead singer of Alpha Rev and Founder of To Cure A Rose Foundation. "Music has the power to amplify our mission and bring hope to families facing rare diseases," Casey McPherson shared.

Emceed by Lauren Petrowski, a familiar face from Good Day Austin and Spotlight ATX on KVUE, the gala promises inspirational stories of resilience, exciting live and silent auctions featuring exclusive experiences, and a Mission Paddle Raise to further genetic treatments for children in need.

Attendees can expect an unforgettable experience filled with exquisite dining and inspiring narratives for a good cause. With the support of generous patrons, the Rose Gala is dedicated to changing the future of rare diseases.

For more information and to secure tickets for the 2025 Rose Gala, visit To Cure A Rose's official website at www.tocurearose.org.
