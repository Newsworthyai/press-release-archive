# 2025 HRO Today Forum North America Begins Wednesday, April 30 in San Antonio

San Antonio, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Apr 29, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Eastern — SharedXpertise Media, publishers of HRO Today digital magazine, HRO Today EMEA and APAC, producers of the Baker’s Dozen Customer Satisfaction Ratings, and organizers of the HRO Today Forums and Educational Events, welcomes HR leaders and executives, as well as a host of practitioners, technology experts, and business innovators, to the 2025 HRO Today Forum North America at the JW Marriott San Antonio Hill Country Resort & Spa.

The HRO Today Forum annually brings together HR leaders, practitioners, technology experts, and innovators who are building the workforces and setting the policies that will be the future of North American and global business.

Our 2025 Forum North America will be the event of the year for HR leaders, featuring:

* A diverse program of sessions and panels on key topics such AI, Talent Acquisition, Employee Engagement and Retention, diversity and inclusion, and more.
* The announcement of the MSP Baker’s Dozen Customer Satisfaction Ratings.
* Keynote address on employer branding from expert and professor Sasha Strauss

The highlight of the event will be the annual CHRO of the Year Awards Gala and Dinner, where innovators and trailblazers in HR leadership are celebrated with awards in several categories, including Lifetime Achievement.

“The 2025 CHRO of the Year finalists exemplify these key leadership qualities, enabling growth and supporting their workforces through periods of uncertainty,” wrote SVP/Editorial Director Debbie Bolla in the April issue of HRO Today. Click here for more on the finalists.
