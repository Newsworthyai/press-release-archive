# Hope Partnership for Education to Host “The Excellence of Hope” Friendraiser Event in Philadelphia on Oct. 30th

Philadelphia, PA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 1, 2025 @ 4:00 AM Pacific — Hope Partnership for Education, an independent middle school and education center in North Philadelphia, invites the community to its annual Friendraiser titled “The Excellence of Hope” on Thursday, October 30th at 6:00 pm in the Ballroom at the Ben located at 832 Chestnut Street in Philadelphia. This heartfelt event celebrates the inspiring achievements of students, staff and alumni while raising crucial funds to continue providing high-quality, values-based education that transforms lives in Philadelphia.

“Our annual Friendraiser is a beautiful gathering of individuals who embody our core values, a space to recognize honorees who steward our founding principle – that all Philadelphians deserve a quality educational experience," said Mr. Virgil Sheppard, President & CEO of Hope Partnership for Education.This year, Hope Partnership is proud to honor Mrs. Marjie Quint and Mrs. Nancy Tabas, two dedicated champions who have inspired students, staff and the broader Philadelphia community. Both have served as trustees, committee members and art enrichment teachers at Hope Partnership School for many years. Their unwavering commitment has significantly contributed to providing a transformational education to youth in Fairhill. In addition to celebrating the achievements of the Hope Partnership School's students, staff, champions and alumni, the community is invited to attend for a joy-filled evening of live music, dinner and a silent auction.For those who want to support but not able to attend, donations can be made to support the mission of Hope Partnership for Education and more information can be found at the event website: "Excellence of Hope" Friendraiser Event. Support through various ways including purchasing event tickets, monetary donations, scholarship donations and event sponsorships. Hope Partnership thanks all who have already donated and helped sponsor this event so far. More information is available for those interested in event Sponsorship.

About [Hope Partnership for Education](https://www.hope-partnership.org/):Hope Partnership for Education is a non-profit organization located in North Philadelphia, dedicated to providing high-quality, values-based education. Our mission is to nurture compassionate citizens and courageous leaders through a transformative and joy-filled educational experience that supports students from adolescence to adulthood.
