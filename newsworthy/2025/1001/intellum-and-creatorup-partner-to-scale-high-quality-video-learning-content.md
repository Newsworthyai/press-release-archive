# Intellum and CreatorUp Partner to Scale High‑Quality Video Learning Content

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 1, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading AI-first learning platform, today announced a strategic partnership with CreatorUp, a global video content studio that makes content creation easy for education, training, and storytelling initiatives. This collaboration empowers organizations to integrate impactful video and media assets directly into their learning programs, accelerating engagement, clarity, and learner retention.

CreatorUp enables teams to produce studio‑grade video content affordably using a network of trained creators, subject‑matter experts, and a leading‑edge, generative AI-powered platform. With offerings spanning animation, live‑action, interactive media, livestreaming, and turn‑key video production, CreatorUp supports everything from training modules to promotional content for learning initiatives.

“Content is the cornerstone of modern learning experiences. Video does more than deliver information; it brings stories to life and makes learning memorable,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By partnering with CreatorUp, we are providing our clients with the ability to deliver powerful video content at scale.”Through this partnership, organizations using Intellum will be able to order video content from CreatorUp directly through integrated workflows, embed video assets in learning paths, and track completion, impact, and engagement—all within a unified system. This integration delivers efficiencies in production, consistent content quality, and deep alignment between content and learning outcomes.

“The future of corporate learning is visual and scalable, and our partnership with Intellum makes that future accessible today,” shared Michael Tringe, Co-Founder and CEO of CreatorUp. “We're pairing a global studio-as-a-service supported by a Universal AI Engine™ with the Intellum platform, allowing customers to include studio-quality content that captivates learners and drives measurable business results.”About [Intellum](https://www.intellum.com/) Intellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.

About [CreatorUp](https://creatorup.com/) CreatorUp is a leading provider of digital media content for businesses, educational institutions, and organizations of all sizes. Its services include all types of content production, for live action, livestreaming, animation, immersive experiences, and generative AI.CreatorUp is dedicated to helping its clients succeed in the digital world by transforming the ways they create and purchase compelling content and reach their audiences. This includes The CreatorUp Store [an eCommerce marketplace of more than 900 content solutions], a global network of 10,000+ creators, and a universal generative AI platform.Trusted by over 250 global brands including Google, YouTube, TikTok, IBM, AirBnB, Stride, Kaplan, The Special Olympics and more, CreatorUp has completed work in over 20 countries.The company is headquartered in Los Angeles, with a presence in Athens, Dubai, Mumbai, and Singapore.Visit us online at creatorup.com.
