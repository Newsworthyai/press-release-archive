# RChilli Achieves CCPA Compliance, Strengthens Commitment to User Data Privacy

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Apr 9, 2025 @ 12:00 PM Central — RChilli, a global leader in AI-powered HR Tech solutions, proudly announces that it has achieved full compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This major milestone reaffirms RChilli’s unwavering commitment to data privacy, transparency, and the responsible use of user information across its platforms.

In an age where data privacy has become critical to public trust, RChilli’s CCPA compliance marks a significant advancement in ensuring user data is handled with the highest standards of care and accountability. By aligning its processes with CCPA regulations, RChilli empowers users with greater control over their personal information while reinforcing its dedication to ethical data management practices.

The California Consumer Privacy Act, one of the most robust privacy laws in the United States, grants California residents enhanced rights over their data. RChilli’s adherence to these regulations underscores its proactive approach to creating a secure and transparent digital environment for clients and users worldwide.

“Achieving CCPA compliance is more than a legal milestone—it’s a reflection of our company’s culture and commitment to responsible innovation,” said Jitender Thakur, Head - IT Infrastructure and Information Security at RChilli Inc. “We take our responsibility seriously when it comes to protecting user data. This achievement demonstrates that our users’ trust in us is well placed.”Over the past few years, RChilli has become synonymous with trust in the HR Tech space, offering reliable solutions for HR tech industry. With the addition of CCPA compliance, RChilli adds yet another layer of assurance for clients across the U.S., particularly those operating in or doing business with California-based entities.

This certification follows RChilli’s previous achievements of ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, and GDPR compliance, making it one of the few companies in its domain to hold such a comprehensive suite of data security and privacy certifications. These benchmarks not only assure users of RChilli’s security infrastructure but also emphasize the company’s proactive pursuit of global data protection standards.

With businesses increasingly shifting toward cloud-based recruitment platforms and AI-driven HR technologies, the importance of handling data ethically and securely cannot be overstated. Ensuring compliance with regional data privacy laws like CCPA enables clients to use RChilli’s services confidently, knowing that their users’ personal data is treated with the utmost integrity.

As the HR Tech landscape continues to evolve, RChilli remains committed to fostering an environment that values privacy, security, and transparency. The company's latest CCPA compliance not only ensures adherence to legal requirements but also aligns with its broader mission—to build future-ready, privacy-first recruitment technologies that help companies hire better talent, faster.

About RChilliRChilli empowers organizations with AI-driven solutions that automate data tasks, streamline decision-making, and enable HR teams to focus on hiring the right talent faster. Our innovative products enhance recruitment efficiency while ensuring compliance with ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2 Type II, NYC AEDT LAW, HIPAA, and GDPR. RChilli is available on Oracle Cloud Marketplace and Salesforce AppExchange, and it ensures global accessibility and trusted performance for organizations worldwide.
