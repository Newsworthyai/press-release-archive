* Weekly trading momentum indicates higher lows with increasing volume, suggesting renewed institutional buying.

3. BrainChip Holdings (ASX: BRN) - Early Entry into AI’s Next FrontierBrainChip is a pioneer in neuromorphic computing, building chips that mimic the way human brains process information. Its technology is designed for low‑power AI applications, including robotics and autonomous vehicles, sectors expected to explode in the coming decade.

The stock has spent the past year consolidating around $0.20, forming what chartists would describe as a “spring coil” pattern - tight compression often preceding powerful moves.

* Short‑term target: A break above $0.40 could signal the start of a new bullish cycle.
* Breakout clues: Sideways trading with rising volume hints at accumulation before a breakout.
