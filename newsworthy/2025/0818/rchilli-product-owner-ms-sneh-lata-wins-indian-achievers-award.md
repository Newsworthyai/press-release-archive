# RChilli Product Owner Ms. Sneh Lata Wins Indian Achievers’ Award

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Aug 18, 2025 @ 1:50 PM Central — RChilli Inc. is excited to announce that Ms. Sneh Lata, Product Owner, continues to advance AI-driven recruitment technology and create a more equitable future of work. With over 17 years of experience, Ms. Lata’s work has significantly impacted recruitment practices and the broader AI ecosystem, helping organizations across the globe enhance their recruitment processes with intelligent, inclusive, and bias-free solutions.

Ms. Lata holds a Post Graduate Diploma in International Business from a prestigious institution in India and a B.Sc. from Delhi University. Her diverse experience spans across key global markets, having worked in the United States and Canada in leadership roles that include spearheading the design and delivery of RChilli multilingual, AI-first recruitment platform.

This innovative solution replaced 15 legacy systems, introduced plug-and-play AI plugins, and set new benchmarks for faster deployment and improved accessibility.

“AI has the potential to make hiring processes more inclusive and fair, removing biases while driving operational efficiency,” said Ms. Sneh Lata, Product Owner. “At RChilli, we are committed to leveraging AI to create scalable and ethical solutions that can transform how organizations make hiring decisions.”In addition to her work at RChilli, Ms. Lata has contributed extensively to the broader enterprise technology space, including cloud-native platform adoption, digital workflow modernization, and scalable system architecture. She has led complex system integrations, collaborated with global teams, and helped organizations achieve greater operational efficiency through advanced technology.

Ms. Lata is a recognized advocate for responsible AI. She has driven initiatives that ensure ethical AI adoption and promote fairness in hiring. Her expertise in scalable SaaS architecture, predictive analytics, and AI-driven decision-making continues to empower global HR teams to make smarter, unbiased hiring decisions.

Beyond her leadership at RChilli, Ms. Lata holds a Big Data certification from UC Berkeley and has trained as a business excellence assessor by the Confederation of Indian Industry and the Export-Import Bank of India. She remains an active participant in global AI and technology summits, ensuring that she stays at the forefront of AI innovation and leadership.

Now based in Canada, Ms. Lata continues to drive purposeful innovation, helping organizations worldwide adopt AI solutions that are not only smart and inclusive but transformative. Her commitment to democratizing AI and advancing equitable hiring practices remains a core focus of her work.

About [RChilli](https://www.rchilli.com/)RChilli empowers organizations with AI-driven solutions that automate data tasks, streamline decision-making, and enable HR teams to focus on hiring the right talent faster. Our innovative products enhance recruitment efficiency while ensuring compliance with ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2 Type II, NYC AEDT LAW, HIPAA, and GDPR.

RChilli is available on Oracle Cloud Marketplace, SAP SuccessFactors, and Salesforce AppExchange, and it ensures global accessibility and trusted performance for organizations worldwide
