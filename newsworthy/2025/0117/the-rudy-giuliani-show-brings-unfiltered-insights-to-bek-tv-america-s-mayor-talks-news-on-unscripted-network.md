# The Rudy Giuliani Show Brings Unfiltered Insights to BEK TV America's Mayor Talks News on Unscripted Network

Bismarck, ND (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Jan 17, 2025 @ 3:00 AM Central — BEK TV is proud to announce the addition of a bold and timely new program to its lineup. The Rudy Giuliani Show, featuring America’s Mayor, Rudy Giuliani, underscores BEK TV’s commitment to bringing viewers fearless patriots who deliver unfiltered and insightful commentary. To learn more on how to watch, visit BEK Buzz.

 Rudy Giuliani, renowned for his extensive career as a prosecutor, former mayor of New York City, and trusted advisor, offers viewers an unparalleled perspective on the latest news and events shaping America. Drawing on his decades of experience and steadfast commitment to truth, Giuliani provides sharp analysis and in-depth discussions on critical topics affecting the nation.

 "BEK TV is thrilled to bring his unscripted, uncensored insights directly to our audience," says Jordan Hassler, BEK TV Chief Operations Officer. "Rudy's reputation as a fearless leader and his willingness to tackle tough issues head-on make him a perfect fit for BEK TV’s mission of delivering programming that informs, inspires, and challenges viewers to think critically." With the addition of The Rudy Giuliani Show, BEK TV continues to solidify its position as a leading voice for viewers seeking honest discussions in today’s media landscape.

 Catch The Rudy Giuliani Show, weekdays at 4 p.m. CT and weekends at 3 p.m. CT, at no charge on BEK TV+.

 For more information about BEK TV programming, visit BEK TV+, or follow us on social media: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X
