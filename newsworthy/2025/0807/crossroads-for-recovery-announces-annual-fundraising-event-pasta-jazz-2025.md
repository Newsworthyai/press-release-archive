# Crossroads for Recovery Announces Annual Fundraising Event: Pasta &amp; Jazz 2025

Los Angeles, California (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Aug 7, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” The Crossroads Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping individuals overcome addiction, has announced its annual fundraising event, Pasta & Jazz 2025, to be held at 5:30 pm on September 19th, 2025, at the Courtyard Marriott Liberty Station in San Diego.

The event aims to raise awareness and funds to support Crossroads for Recovery's mission to provide counseling, treatment, and support services to women battling addiction. This year's theme, "Celebrating Resilience," highlights the strength and determination of individuals on their journey to recovery.

Patricia Sanders, the Executive Director of Crossroads for Recovery, expressed her enthusiasm for the event: "Pasta & Jazz is a fantastic opportunity for our community to come together and show support for those in need. This event is not just about raising funds; it's about celebrating the resilience of individuals overcoming addiction and the hope that recovery brings."

Guests at Pasta & Jazz 2025 can look forward to an evening of delectable pasta dishes, accompanied by smooth jazz music performed by talented local musicians. The event will also feature a silent auction with a variety of unique items and experiences up for bid, with all proceeds going towards supporting Crossroads for Recovery's programs.

In addition to enjoying a night of delicious food and live music, attendees will have the chance to hear inspiring stories of recovery from women whose lives have been positively impacted by the services provided by Crossroads for Recovery.

Lisa, a former beneficiary of Crossroads for Recovery's services, shared her experience: "Crossroads for Recovery not only helped me overcome my addiction but also supported me in rebuilding my life. The services they offer are truly life-changing, and events like Pasta & Jazz play a crucial role in ensuring that others receive the help they need."

Tickets for Pasta & Jazz 2025 are now available for purchase on the Crossroads for Recovery website, with various sponsorship opportunities also open for businesses and organizations looking to support the cause.

Join Crossroads for Recovery at Pasta & Jazz 2025 for an evening of great food, soulful music, and heartwarming stories of resilience and hope.
