# Inland Marine Underwriters Association (IMUA) Board of Directors Appoints Matthew Derrick as President and Chief Executive Officer

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Jan 28, 2025 @ 11:30 AM Eastern — The Board of Directors of the Inland Marine Underwriters Association (IMUA) is excited to announce the appointment of Matthew Derrick as its new President and Chief Executive Officer. With over 20 years of experience in leadership, Matt’s vision aligns perfectly with IMUA’s commitment to innovation, professional development, and digital transformation.

“We are thrilled to welcome Matt Derrick to the IMUA team,” said Tony Falcone, President of Intact Inland Marine. “Matt’s exceptional track record in spearheading digital transformation, developing learning platforms, and fostering professional growth makes him the ideal leader to build on IMUA’s recent initiatives, such as our new website and learning portal. His vision and leadership will enhance the value we deliver to our members.”

Prior to joining IMUA, Matt served as CEO of Kodeco.com, where he led a global team of over 300 employees and contractors, oversaw a successful company rebrand, and developed innovative, customizable learning assessments that advanced professional certifications. Under his leadership, the company significantly expanded its reach and impact in professional education.

“I am honored to join IMUA and work alongside its talented team and dedicated members,” said Matt Derrick. “As the inland marine insurance industry continues to evolve, I’m excited to build on IMUA’s strong foundation to promote innovation and drive professional development, training, and networking opportunities for inland marine insurance professionals.” “I hope to meet many of you at our annual meeting in April.”

With Matt at the helm, IMUA is poised to strengthen its role as a leader in the inland marine insurance industry, driving innovation and delivering unparalleled value to its members.

About IMUA

IMUA is the national association for the commercial inland marine insurance industry. IMUA serves as the voice of its member companies representing over 90 percent of all commercial inland marine insurers. The association provides its members with comprehensive training and educational programs, including research papers and bulletins, industry analysis, and seminars. IMUA was founded in 1930.
