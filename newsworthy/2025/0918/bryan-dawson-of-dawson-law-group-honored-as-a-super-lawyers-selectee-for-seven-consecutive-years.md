# Bryan Dawson of Dawson Law Group Honored as a Super Lawyers Selectee for Seven Consecutive Years

Portland, Oregon (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Sep 18, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Pacific — Dawson Law Group proudly announces that its Principal Attorney, Bryan Dawson, has been named to Super Lawyers for the seventh consecutive year. This recognition highlights his continued excellence in personal injury, wrongful death, and complex litigation, and affirms his standing as one of Oregon’s most trusted trial lawyers.

With years of courtroom experience, Bryan Dawson has represented clients in some of the most challenging and high-stakes cases in Oregon. His practice focuses on personal injury litigation, including catastrophic car and trucking accidents, wrongful death, medical malpractice, motorcycle accidents, and slip and fall injuries. Over the course of his career, Dawson has litigated, arbitrated, and settled hundreds of cases, argued before the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, and practiced in nearly every Oregon Circuit Court.

Mr. Dawson’s recognition by Super Lawyers underscores not only his professional achievements but also the unique perspective he brings to every case. Early in his career, Dawson represented insurers, experience that now allows him to anticipate defense strategies and maximize results for injury victims. Today, he channels that knowledge into delivering exceptional advocacy for individuals and families facing life-altering circumstances.

Dawson’s peers and clients alike recognize him as a skilled, client-focused advocate. He has earned additional accolades, including an AV Preeminent Rating from Martindale-Hubbell, a 10/10 Avvo Rating, and the Albert Nelson Marquis Lifetime Achievement Award. Beyond his professional work, Dawson actively mentors young attorneys, teaches continuing legal education (CLE) courses, and coaches high school mock trial teams—contributing to the growth of the legal community in Oregon.

Clients describe working with Dawson as both reassuring and empowering. One client, Dustin Legg, shared: “Over the past few months of working with Dawson, I have experienced much relief, and gained confidence and understanding that I did not have before. I felt like I was lost and doomed to lose! Bryan has been so clear, considerate, concise, and communicative. He really puts in the extra effort, is very pleasant to speak with, and has gifted me with the feeling that I can relax and let my case ride backseat to my thoughts and worries. The best representation I could hope for.”

About Bryan DawsonBryan Dawson earned his J.D. from the University of California, Berkeley School of Law in 1996, after completing graduate studies at the University of Chicago and undergraduate studies at Claremont McKenna College. Since returning to Oregon in 1999, he has led Dawson Law Group in Portland with a commitment to providing transparent, effective, and compassionate representation.

About Dawson Law GroupFounded in Portland, Dawson Law Group represents individuals and families across Oregon in personal injury, wrongful death, and complex litigation. The firm is known for its clear communication, deep trial experience, and commitment to client success.
