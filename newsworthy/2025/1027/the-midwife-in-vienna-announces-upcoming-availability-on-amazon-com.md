# The Midwife in Vienna Announces Upcoming Availability on Amazon.com

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Oct 27, 2025 @ 6:00 AM Pacific — Following its successful U.S. retail expansion through OneLavi.com, The Midwife in Vienna, a wellness brand founded by Austrian midwife Sarah Lares, announced today that its line of organic maternity and baby products will soon be available on Amazon.com.

Known for its clean, minimalist approach to maternal and infant wellness, The Midwife in Vienna continues to build momentum among American consumers seeking natural, European-crafted solutions for pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and newborn care.

“As a practicing midwife, I’ve seen how much peace of mind comes from knowing what you’re putting on and into your body,” said Sarah Lares, founder of The Midwife in Vienna. “Amazon’s upcoming launch will make it even easier for families to access our trusted, organic products—no matter where they live.”Handcrafted in Austria using certified organic, food-grade ingredients, The Midwife in Vienna’s growing product line includes soothing herbal teas, restorative bath blends, and gentle baby skincare—all guided by Lares’ nearly two decades of midwifery experience.

The brand’s U.S. expansion underscores a rising trend among health-conscious families seeking authentic, European-quality wellness solutions. By combining traditional midwifery wisdom with modern sustainability standards, The Midwife in Vienna bridges the gap between ancient care practices and contemporary consumer needs—offering products that are as safe and simple as they are effective.

With its upcoming debut on Amazon.com, the brand continues to expand its U.S. presence while staying true to its founding mission: to provide simple, natural, and sustainable care for mothers and babies—delivered with the heart of a midwife.

Follow @diehebammeinwien on Instagram to stay updated on the official launch date and explore the full collection of products made with love in Austria.

About [The Midwife in Vienna](https://www.diehebammeinwien-shop.at/)Founded by Austrian midwife Sarah Lares, The Midwife in Vienna is a wellness brand inspired by nearly 20 years of hands-on birth and postpartum experience. The company crafts organic, minimalist products for mothers and babies, blending time-honored midwifery knowledge with modern sustainability. Every product is made in Austria using certified organic ingredients—many vegan and food-grade—reflecting the brand’s core values of simplicity, trust, and natural care.
