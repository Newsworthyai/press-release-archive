# Intellum and Promovre Partner to Power Scalable, Managed Learning Solutions

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 22, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading AI-first platform for scalable learning solutions, today announced a strategic partnership with Promovre, a global end-to-end managed services provider with deep capabilities in learning development, consulting, trust & safety, technical services, and AI. This collaboration empowers organizations to combine Intellum’s learning platform with Promovre’s operational services to deliver high-quality, reliably managed learning at scale.

Promovre brings over a decade of experience as an E2E managed services provider, combining consulting, digital transformation, content moderation, learning & development, and technical services across global markets. Their expertise in managing operations, quality, and scale complements Intellum’s platform strengths, creating a seamless solution for clients who want both a powerful LMS and trusted execution support.

“Delivering learning at scale is more than content and systems. It requires disciplined operations and managed support,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By partnering with Promovre, we’re giving clients not just the tools to deliver education but the operational backbone to run it effectively across geographies and functions.”Through this partnership, clients can benefit from end-to-end managed learning programs: Promovre will support everything from content creation, localization, moderation, and deployment to learning operations, governance, QA, and technical maintenance. Intellum’s platform will serve as the delivery engine, enabling rich personalization, reporting, and learner engagement—all seamlessly supported by Promovre’s managed services.

"Promovre is focused on providing customizable solutions with a high level of operational expertise to our clients,” said Michele Mar, VP - Global BPO Operations at Promovre. “This strategic partnership with Intellum allows Promovre to further its L&D options to meet customer needs and deliver an even higher caliber of operational solutions to Enterprise and SMB clients globally."
