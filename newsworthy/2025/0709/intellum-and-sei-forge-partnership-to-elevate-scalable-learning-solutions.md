# Intellum and SEI Forge Partnership to Elevate Scalable Learning Solutions

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 9, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading provider of scalable learning management solutions, announced today a strategic partnership with SEI, a management consulting firm renowned for delivering fresh perspectives and reliable results. This collaboration aims to empower organizations to implement effective learning strategies that drive meaningful business outcomes.

SEI brings over 30 years of experience in management consulting, specializing in areas such as AI & Technology, Data & Analytics, and Strategy & Operations. Their expertise in aligning people, processes, and technology complements Intellum’s robust platform, enabling organizations to scale engaging learning experiences across diverse audiences.

"SEI’s commitment to delivering tailored consulting solutions aligns perfectly with our mission to turn learning into growth," said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum. "Together, we can offer our clients a powerful combination of strategic insight and scalable delivery, ensuring that learning initiatives not only engage but also deliver measurable business impact."Through this partnership, clients can leverage SEI’s consulting services alongside Intellum’s comprehensive learning platform. This synergy ensures that learning programs are not only tailored to the unique needs of each organization but also delivered efficiently and effectively at scale.

"We’re excited to partner with Intellum to integrate their AI-driven learning platform with our consulting expertise," said Bill Gallagher, CEO at SEI. "Together, we’ll provide clients with personalized learning experiences that adapt to individual needs, ultimately driving performance and growth."Gallagher added that the partnership will deepen SEI’s impact on client growth and transformation, stating, "This collaboration underscores our commitment to helping organizations foster continuous development and achieve lasting success."
