# Global Trial Lawyer Guilherme “Gui” Vasconcelos Joins Treviño Law, Bringing Major Verdict Wins and Multilingual Advocacy

Austin, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Nov 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Mountain — Treviño Law is proud to announce the addition of Guilherme “Gui” Vasconcelos, an internationally trained trial lawyer with a record of securing major verdicts for injured Texans. Vasconcelos brings deep courtroom experience, multilingual communication skills, and a career devoted exclusively to personal injury law.

 Originally from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Vasconcelos earned both a Juris Doctor and a Master of Laws focused on Constitutional Law. After building a strong litigation foundation in Brazil, he moved to the United States in 2016, completing his LLM (Master of Laws) at the University of Texas School of Law and graduating with Pro Bono Honors for extensive volunteer legal work.

 Vasconcelos is licensed to practice in both Texas and Brazil. He has been a member of the Texas Bar College since 2018, an honor reserved for attorneys who demonstrate exceptional commitment to legal education and professionalism. Before joining Treviño Law, he managed and litigated more than 500 cases at DJC Law Firm in Austin, resulting in more than $8 million in gross settlements for injury clients. He also served as Managing Attorney at Ramos James Law Firm in Austin, where he led legal teams and supervised complex litigation.

 Vasconcelos is known for his courtroom performance and trial outcomes. In 2024, he secured a $4.5 million verdict in Travis County, followed by a $1.3 million verdict in Dallas County in 2025. He is an alumnus of the Trial Advocacy College of Texas (TACT) and a current member of both the Texas Trial Lawyers Association and the National Trial Lawyers Top 40 Under 40.

 Vasconcelos is fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese. His multilingual background allows Treviño Law to serve clients across diverse communities, ensuring more injury victims have access to a trial-tested lawyer who can communicate directly, without barriers.

 About Treviño Law Treviño Law represents people across Texas and Colorado who have suffered serious injuries due to negligence. The firm is dedicated to aggressive case preparation, trial excellence, and securing full justice for injured clients.
