# Intellum and HexaLearn Partner to Deliver End-to-End, Scalable Learning and Technology Solutions

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Nov 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading AI-first enterprise learning platform, today announced a strategic partnership with HexaLearn, a global provider of custom learning solutions and software services. This collaboration enables organizations to pair Intellum’s powerful platform with HexaLearn’s full-service development, design, and LMS support to streamline and scale enterprise learning programs.

 HexaLearn, an ISO-certified company with more than 14 years of global experience, delivers customized eLearning content, mobile-first training, blended learning, LMS development, and technical support. From ideation to execution, their integrated teams help businesses launch and maintain learning programs that are modern, effective, and aligned with business objectives.

 “Great learning programs require more than just a platform. They require scalable, flexible support behind the scenes,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “With HexaLearn’s proven content and development capabilities, our clients can expand faster and deliver high-quality learning experiences with greater efficiency.”

 Through this partnership, organizations can rapidly deploy and localize learning content, build custom interfaces, and extend their training programs across audiences—all while maintaining consistency, accessibility, and engagement through the Intellum platform. Whether clients need technical integrations, multi-device content, or a complete LMS setup and migration, HexaLearn complements Intellum’s technology with the services that bring it to life.

 “HexaLearn is thrilled to announce its partnership with Intellum. This collaboration represents a significant step forward in our commitment to delivering innovative learning solutions to clients,” said Rachna M. Das, Co-Founder & CFO at HexaLearn. “With Intellum’s AI Learning Platform, our clients will now be able to leverage innovative & transformative learning technology solutions that will enable them to achieve significant business growth. This move is a strategic decision that aligns with the evolving needs of the market.”

 About IntellumIntellum is the leading AI-first enterprise learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.

 About HexaLearnHexaLearn is a global learning and software-solutions partner with over 13 years of experience delivering custom eLearning development, LMS services, and mobile-ready, blended learning programs. ISO-certified and client-centric, HexaLearn helps organizations scale impactful training programs with speed and consistency. Learn more at www.hexalearn.com.
