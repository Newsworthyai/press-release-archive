# Intellum and Studion Partner to Build Deeply Engaging, Scalable Digital Learning Experiences

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Dec 17, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading AI‐first enterprise learning platform, today announced a strategic partnership with Studion, a global tech‐services firm specializing in designing, building, and supporting deeply engaging digital learning and training experiences. This collaboration empowers organizations to deliver learning programs that are not only scalable—but immersive, learner‐centered, and built for sustained engagement.

 Studion brings decades of experience creating digital learning experiences at scale. Their High Engagement at Scale® approach combines learning science, program design, technology, and ongoing support to deliver experiences that engage customer and partner audiences.

 “Great learning is about creating experiences that learners love and return to,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By partnering with Studion, we’re giving our clients not just a powerful LMS, but a full‐service partner to deliver learning that engages, scales, and delivers real results.”

 Through this partnership, organizations can leverage Intellum’s robust platform along with Studion’s full-service learning design and development capabilities. Whether launching a new partner training program, reviving an existing learning program, or building a global learning initiative, customers will benefit from:

 * Custom digital learning experiences designed for engagement and scale
* Platform‐agnostic build and integration capabilities
* Ongoing maintenance, updates, and support to ensure learning evolves with business needs

 This collaboration ensures that enterprise learning programs are not only scalable, but enduring, adaptable, and aligned with organizational goals.

 “Research shows that just 10 hours of training can drive as much as 7% improvement in channel partner performance. Intellum’s platform delivers at scale, while Studion’s expertise makes channel partners want to participate. Our proven High Engagement at Scale™ framework ensures partner training excellence,” said Furqan Nazeeri, CEO at Studion.

 About IntellumIntellum is the leading AI-first enterprise learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.

 About Studion

 Studion is a technology services firm dedicated to creating highly engaging digital experiences in Learning and the Life Sciences. After 15+ years partnering with leading brands to launch over 350 programs reaching millions of learners, Studion’s Learning practice creates programs that deliver high engagement at scale. Their Channel Sales Training and Partner Education solutions drive measurable business outcomes for enterprise organizations with complex, indirect sales organizations. To learn more, visit https://gostudion.com.
