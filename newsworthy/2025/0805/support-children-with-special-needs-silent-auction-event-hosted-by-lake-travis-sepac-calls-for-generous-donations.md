# Support Children with Special Needs: Silent Auction Event Hosted by Lake Travis SEPAC Calls for Generous Donations

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 5, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” The Lake Travis Special Education Parent Advisory Council (SEPAC) is pleased to announce its upcoming silent auction event to raise funds in support of educational programs for children with special needs. The council is seeking generous donations from businesses and individuals to contribute auction items that will help further its mission of providing resources and support to these students.

According to Janet, a member of the SEPAC organizing committee, "We are thrilled to invite the community to participate in our silent auction event by donating items that will help us continue to offer valuable services to children with special needs. Your support will make a real difference in their educational journey."

The silent auction showcases a diverse range of items available for bid, including:

* Mexico Dreams for 2
* Jungle Lux in Turquoise Tulum for 4 with a Cenote Tour experience
* An exclusive 2026 PGA Tour package for golf enthusiasts, providing attendees with the opportunity to support a worthy cause while also securing exciting prizes for themselves or their loved ones.
* Other options include unique experiences, such as "Meet the Cowboys" for 2, or a luxurious stay in Park City.

"We are grateful for the generosity of any community members and businesses who choose to donate to our silent auction," said Sarah, another SEPAC committee member. "Your contributions will enable us to enhance the educational experiences of children with special needs and empower them to reach their full potential."The Lake Travis Special Education Parent Advisory Council is dedicated to advocating for the needs of students with disabilities and ensuring they have access to high-quality educational opportunities. By participating in the silent auction or donating items, individuals and organizations can play a vital role in supporting the council's efforts to make a positive impact in the lives of these students.

For more information on how to donate silent auction items or participate in the event, please visit www.ltsepac.org/donate or contact Rachel Stanley at rachelstanley422@gmail.com.
