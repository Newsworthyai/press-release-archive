# JABCares Unites Country Music Stars for Deep in the Heart Benefit Concert Supporting Kerr County Flood Relief

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 5, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central — JABCares, the philanthropic arm of the acclaimed Josh Abbott Band, is proud to announce its upcoming charity event, the Deep in the Heart Benefit Concert, in collaboration with ACL Live and Cowboy Auctioneer. This initiative aims to raise funds and awareness to aid in the ongoing flood relief efforts in Kerr County following the devastating floods that struck the Texas Hill Country in July.With their continued commitment to supporting deserving causes, JABCares has brought together an extraordinary lineup of country music stars for an unforgettable evening of live music in support of the Kerr County community. Artists such as Josh Abbott Band, Carly Pearce, Casey Donahew, Kaitlin Butts, The Panhandlers, Wade Bowen, and others will grace the stage, uniting for a cause that resonates deeply with the heart of Texas.Cowboy Auctioneer, a stalwart supporter of the JABCares' mission, has lent its expertise and services to enhance the fundraising efforts for the event. Heath Hale, Founder, President & Auctioneer of Cowboy Auctioneer, expressed his enthusiasm for the collaboration, stating, “Our hearts go out to the families and communities affected by the recent floods. We are proud to stand alongside JABCares in supporting the recovery efforts and are inspired by the outpouring of support from the community. We look forward to a night of music, generosity, and unity as we come together to make a difference.”The Deep in the Heart Benefit Concert is set to take place on September 8, 2025, at ACL Live in Austin, Texas. For more information and to purchase tickets, please visit https://www.acllive.com/event/2025-09-08-deep-in-the-heart-benefit-concert-at-8-pm-1. Join JABCares, ACL Live, and Cowboy Auctioneer in supporting Kerr County flood relief efforts by attending the Deep in the Heart Benefit Concert for an unforgettable night of music and community giving.
