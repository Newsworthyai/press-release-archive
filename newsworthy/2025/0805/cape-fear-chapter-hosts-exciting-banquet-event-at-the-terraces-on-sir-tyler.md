# Cape Fear Chapter Hosts Exciting Banquet Event at the Terraces on Sir Tyler

Wilmington, North Carolina (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 5, 2025 @ 10:00 AM Pacific â€” The Cape Fear Chapter of the Coastal Conservation Association invites supporters and members to join them at the highly anticipated 2025 Cape Fear Chapter Banquet. The event will take place on Thursday, August 14th, from 6:00 pm to 10:00 pm at the Terraces on Sir Tyler, located at 1826 Sir Tyler Dr., Wilmington, NC.

Guests can expect an evening filled with fun, camaraderie, and a shared passion for marine conservation. The banquet will feature a variety of activities, including live auctions, raffles, and delicious food and drinks. Attendees will have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who are dedicated to preserving and enhancing coastal habitats.

According to Chapter President, Sarah Green, "Our annual banquet is a fantastic way for our community to come together and show support for the important work being done by the Cape Fear Chapter. It's a night of celebration and unity, all in the name of a cause we are truly passionate about."

The Cape Fear Chapter works tirelessly to protect and conserve North Carolina's marine resources, and events like the annual banquet help raise critical funds to support these efforts. From habitat restoration projects to advocacy work, the Chapter is committed to ensuring the future health and sustainability of our coastal ecosystems.

Tickets for the 2025 Cape Fear Chapter Banquet can be purchased online at the 2025 Cape Fear Chapter Banquet - CCA North Carolina webpage or by contacting the Chapter directly. Whether you are a long-time supporter or a newcomer to the cause, all are welcome to attend and make a positive impact on the conservation of our beloved coastal environment.

Join us on August 14th at the Terraces on Sir Tyler for a memorable evening that combines philanthropy, community, and a shared love for our oceans. Together, we can make a difference in the preservation of our coastal resources.

For more information about the Cape Fear Chapter and the 2025 Banquet, please visit ccanc.org/events.
