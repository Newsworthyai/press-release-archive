# ALPHAWHISPERERS™ House Launches at the United Nations During UNGA80, Activating a New Breed of Global Leaders

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Sunday Sep 28, 2025 @ 12:25 PM Eastern — On September 22, 2025, ALPHAWHISPERERS™ hosted its premier global leadership convening, ALPHAWHISPERERS™ House New York, at the United Nations Delegates Dining Room during UNGA80. The event gathered global executives, innovators, and cultural leaders under the theme: “Powering the Future: A New Breed of Exponential Leaders.”

ALPHAWHISPERERS™ is on a mission to cultivate and activate a new breed of Alpha Whisperers globally—strategists, advisors, and quiet influencers who represent the unseen power behind the power.

“I believe that the present world could be better, and the future even better – with the help of a new breed of Alpha Whisperers,” said Janet C. Salazar, Founder & CEO of Alpha Whisperers International Network (AlphaWIN) - www.alphawhisperer.com

Event HighlightsThe afternoon featured performances, fireside chats, and panels that blended leadership insights with cultural inspiration:

* Opening remarks from Janet C. Salazar and Dr. Joshua W. Walker, President & CEO of Japan Society.
* The Frequency School, led by Grammy-nominated artist Maejor, opened with a sound-therapy performance alongside Martina Fuchs, Kingsley M, Brandon Lee, and Aaron Dawson.
* Generational Leadership Spotlight: Tomonari Abe, 10th Generation CEO of Abe Yoando Pharma, shared how legacy fueled his leadership “moonshot.”
* Fireside chats with Demee Koch, Founder & CEO of DE MOI, and Amantia Muhedini, UBS Head of Sustainable & Impact Investing Americas.
* Keynotes from Dr. Kellyann Petrucci, bestselling author and longevity expert, and Dr. Hidekazu Yamada, President of the Japanese Society of Anti-Aging Medicine.
* Performances by Grammy Award-winning artist Roy “Gramps” Morgan uplifted the audience with “People Like You.”
* Panel discussions featured leaders including Shifra Ansonoff (BlackRock), Annabelle Manalo-Morgan, PhD (Masaya Medical), Hiroshi Abe (Abe Yoando Pharma), Lindsay O’Neill (Wellness Eternal & Biohacking Index), Nick Akira Yoshida (Abe Yoando Pharma), and Kelly Gushue (financial coach).

A Call to ActionClosing remarks were delivered by Janet C. Salazar, Victoria Kar (Master of Ceremony and Chair of the AlphaWhisperers Global Council), and members of the AlphaWhisperers Global Council, ending with a final performance by The Frequency School.

Salazar emphasized the movement’s urgency: “Reframing how power and influence operates at the highest levels of our society is the only way to democratize and humanize decision-making at the top—leading to systemic, generational, and sustainable change that could shape a better future, and save our civilization.”

About ALPHAWHISPERERS™ALPHAWHISPERERS™ is a breakthrough global leadership engine founded by Janet C. Salazar, social innovator, strategist, and Resident Fellow of the Rockefeller Foundation Bellagio Residency. Its mission is to humanize and democratize leadership decision-making by empowering Alpha Whisperers—those who advise and influence the world’s most powerful decision-makers.

Next StepsFollowing the success of the New York convening, ALPHAWHISPERERS™ House will expand to Davos, Washington, DC, and Athena, continuing to activate a new breed of exponential leaders.
