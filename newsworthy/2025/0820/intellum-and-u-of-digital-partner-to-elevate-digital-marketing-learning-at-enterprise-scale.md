# Intellum and U of Digital Partner to Elevate Digital Marketing Learning at Enterprise Scale

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Aug 20, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading enterprise learning management system, today announced a strategic partnership with U of Digital, a premier education provider specializing in digital marketing and ad tech. This partnership enables organizations to embed U of Digital’s expert-led workshops, on-demand courses, and industry insights directly into the Intellum platform—creating richer, real-time learning experiences tailored for the modern marketer.

Since 2018, U of Digital has worked with brands, publishers, and agencies to build marketing expertise through immersive workshops, curated digital courses, and a thought leadership newsletter. Their expert network — drawn from leading companies like Google, Amazon, Yahoo, and The Trade Desk — delivers content that is credible, digestible, and designed to boost digital acumen across teams at all levels.

“Marketing professionals need learning experiences that reflect the fast‑evolving nature of their work and industry,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By integrating U of Digital’s real‑world, expert-led content into our platform, we’re giving organizations the ability to develop digital literacy and marketing confidence with precision, speed, and scale.”Through this collaboration, clients will be able to offer targeted professional development—combining U of Digital’s workshops like AI Accelerator and Digital Advertising Essentials with Intellum’s powerful personalization, analytics, and delivery capabilities—ensuring learners build real-world skills that align with business goals.

“Partnering with Intellum allows us to scale our mission of elevating digital and AI literacy across the marketing and advertising industry,” said Myles Younger, Chief Growth Officer at U of Digital. “By embedding our expert-led content into Intellum’s robust platform, we’re enabling companies' in-house teams, external partner networks, and end clients to learn faster, work smarter, and stay ahead in a rapidly changing digital landscape.”About [Intellum](https://www.intellum.com)Intellum is the leading learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at[www.intellum.com](https://www.intellum.com).About [U of Digital](https://uof.digital/)U of Digital is the leading education partner focused on digital marketing, advertising, and AI. Founded in 2018, the organization delivers on-demand learning, expert-led workshops, curated newsletters, and custom education tracks to companies like Google, Nielsen, Pinterest, Reddit, Roku, Yahoo, and TikTok. The U of Digital Expert Network includes hundreds of career professionals from leading media platforms, brands, publishers, and tech companies around the world.
