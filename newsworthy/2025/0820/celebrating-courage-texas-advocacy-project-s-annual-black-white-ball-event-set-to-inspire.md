# Celebrating Courage: Texas Advocacy Project's Annual Black &amp; White Ball Event Set to Inspire

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Aug 20, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central — Texas Advocacy Project (TAP), a leading organization dedicated to eradicating dating and domestic violence, sexual assault, child abuse, stalking, and human trafficking in Texas, will host its annual Black & White Ball on September 20th, presented by DaBella, at the prestigious JW Marriott Austin.

With a mission to empower survivors through free legal and social services, TAP has been a beacon of hope for countless individuals seeking justice and freedom from abusive situations. The Black & White Ball serves as a glamorous black-tie gala that not only raises vital funds for TAP's critical programs but also celebrates the organization's 40+ years of unwavering commitment to ending power-based abuse across the state. This year's event will include the prestigious presentation of the annual Courage Award, which recognizes a former TAP client who exemplifies the remarkable courage displayed by TAP's clients on a daily basis.One of TAP's long-standing partners in this noble cause is Cowboy Auctioneer, who has been a part of the Black & White Ball success for nearly a decade. Heath Hale, Founder & President, shared his enthusiasm for the partnership, stating, "At Cowboy Auctioneer, our entire team is motivated by a passion for making an impact. When partnering with a mission as life-changing as Texas Advocacy Project, we’re inspired to use our gifts to go above and beyond to achieve record-breaking results; results that a partner like TAP needs to continue providing these critical services for courageous survivors."

The dedication of TAP's staff, board members, volunteers, and generous donors has been instrumental in the success and growth of the Black & White Ball over the years. Together, they have raised awareness, instigated change, and provided critical support to survivors in need.

To learn more about Texas Advocacy Project and its impactful work, visit https://www.texasadvocacyproject.org/.

For event details and tickets for the Black & White Ball, please visit https://www.texasadvocacyproject.org/black-white-ball.

To explore Cowboy Auctioneer's commitment to supporting missions like TAP, visit https://www.cowboyauctioneer.com/.
