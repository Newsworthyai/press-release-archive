# Intellum and ARQ Innovation Partner to Advance Immersive, AI-Driven Learning Through Learning Engineering

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 29, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading AI-first learning management solution, and ARQ Innovation, a learning technology company specializing in immersive design and adaptive learning delivery, today announced a strategic partnership to accelerate the adoption of immersive and gamified learning experiences for enterprises worldwide.

 The collaboration brings together Intellum’s enterprise-scale delivery platform with ARQ Innovation’s learning engineering methodology—integrating skills data, immersive storytelling, gamified simulations, and experience analytics to drive measurable performance improvement.

 “Learning needs to go beyond content delivery to deliver experiences that truly change behavior,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By integrating ARQ’s learning engineering and immersive storytelling expertise into our platform, we're enabling our clients to deliver smarter, more engaging learning that scales and performs.”

 Through this partnership, organizations will gain access to immersive digital learning experiences developed using ARQ’s learning engineering approach and authored with tools like Intellum Evolve, a key component of ARQ’s immersive content technology suite. These experiences are designed to integrate seamlessly with Intellum’s ecosystem, while aligning with ARQ Innovation’s ongoing work in adaptive and gamified learning through its ARQ Waypoint platform.

 “We’re excited to collaborate with Intellum to showcase how immersive design, AI, and data-driven learning can coexist across modern ecosystems,” said Simon Carter and Nirupam Sarkar, Co-Founders at ARQ Innovation. “This partnership underscores our shared commitment to advancing the science of learning while maintaining openness, interoperability, and cross-platform collaboration at the heart of our technology approach.”

 About Intellum Intellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com

 About ARQ Innovation ARQ Innovation is a learning technology company combining AI, immersive storytelling, learning sciences, and data analytics to create scalable learning ecosystems. Its upcoming ARQ Waypoint platform is built around Future of Work skills and competencies, with a flexible enterprise version that can be tailored to any organization’s own content and capability framework. Learn more at www.arq-innovation.com
