# Virtual Intensive Event Welcomes Emily Hardt as Guest Speaker

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Saturday Sep 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — The much-anticipated Virtual Intensive Event hosted by Mia the Event Diva on September 23rd & 24th is proud to announce Emily Hardt as one of its distinguished guest speakers. This virtual event aims to provide valuable insights, education, and networking opportunities for individuals in the event planning industry.

With years of experience in the event space, Emily Hardt brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the Virtual Intensive Event. Sharing her excitement about participating in the event, Emily states, “I am thrilled to be a part of the Virtual Intensive Event and look forward to engaging with fellow professionals to share insights and ideas.”

In addition to her role as a guest speaker, Emily Hardt serves as the Vice President of Cowboy Auctioneers, where she plays a pivotal role in driving the company’s success. Her leadership and vision have been instrumental in shaping Cowboy Auctioneer’s reputation as a trusted and reliable partner in the auctioneer industry.

The Virtual Intensive Event offers a unique opportunity for event planners and professionals to connect, learn, and grow in a virtual setting. With a diverse lineup of speakers and engaging sessions, attendees can expect to broaden their horizons and gain valuable insights that will benefit their careers and businesses.

Mia the Event Diva is excited to welcome Emily Hardt as a guest speaker at the Virtual Intensive Event and is confident that her contribution will add tremendous value to the overall experience for attendees. Whether you are a seasoned event professional or a newcomer to the industry, this event promises to be a rewarding and enriching experience.

For more information about the Virtual Intensive Event and to secure your spot, please visit events.miaeventdiva.com.
