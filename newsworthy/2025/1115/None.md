# The Building Texas Show's Episode 101 Features Glen Rose Mayor Joe Boles

Glen Rose, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Saturday Nov 15, 2025 @ 10:05 AM US/Central — The Building Texas Show has released Episode 101, spotlighting the charm of one of Texas’ most beloved small towns, Glen Rose. Host Justin McKenzie sits down with Glen Rose Mayor Joe Boles to explore the town’s community spirit, its growing appeal as a regional destination and its deep-rooted Christmas traditions. This year, the Glen Rose Christmas Parade kicks off their holiday festivities on November 29th at the Downtown Square followed by the Tree Lighting.

 Located just over an hour southwest of Dallas – Fort Worth, Glen Rose offers visitors a unique blend of Texas heritage and small-town warmth. In the episode, Mayor Boles shares what first drew him to Glen Rose, the town’s welcoming attitude, and how it has grown as a regional getaway.

 “Glen Rose can’t be done in one day,” said Mayor Boles. “Come for the Christmas parade. Stay the night. Enjoy the dinosaur tracks the next morning. There is something here for everyone.”

 Celebrating Christmas in Glen RoseA central theme of the episode is Glen Rose’s dedication to preserving an authentic Christmas experience. Mayor Boles shares the town’s efforts—featuring new decorations, a handcrafted nativity scene on the courthouse lawn, volunteer church choirs, and return appearances by Santa and Mrs. Claus.

 The highlight is the Glen Rose Christmas Parade on November 29. Festivities begin at 3 p.m., with the parade rolling at 5:30 p.m., followed by a tree lighting, hot chocolate, children’s activities, a walk-through snow globe, and a train circling the courthouse square.

 Weekend Getaway Destination:

 Beyond the Christmas festivities, Glen Rose offers a variety of attractions such as:

 * Dinosaur Valley State Park, home to the longest visible dinosaur tracks in the U.S.
* Fossil Rim Wildlife Center, a renowned drive-through animal preserve
* Historic downtown shops and museums
* Lodging options including cozy bed and breakfasts and resorts like Rough Creek Lodge and La Familia Resort
* Family-friendly dining, shopping, and walkable experiences

 The Building Texas Show Episode 101 Now AvailableEpisode 101 of The Building Texas Show is available now on YouTube, podcast platforms and at buildingtexasshow.com. Watch the full episode on YouTube.

 Mayor Boles wraps up the episode with a warm invitation: “Come to Glen Rose. See what we have. Enjoy the time. And have a very Merry Christmas with us.”

 About [The Building Texas Show](https://www.buildingtexasshow.com/about-the-show) The Building Texas Show, hosted by Justin McKenzie, is a statewide platform dedicated to telling the stories of Texas communities, entrepreneurs, and visionaries shaping the future of the state. From small towns to regional leaders, the show showcases how innovation, community effort, and purpose are redefining Texas’ growth and cultural identity.

 About [Glen Rose](https://visitglenrosetx.com/) Glen Rose is a vibrant small town in the Texas Hill Country with a rich history and a reputation for natural beauty and adventure. Known for its dinosaur tracks, wineries, and historic downtown, Glen Rose offers a welcoming atmosphere and memorable experiences for families, tourists, and locals alike.
