# Intellum and ELM Learning Partner to Elevate Engagement Through Custom, Gamified Learning

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 17, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the AI-first enterprise learning management system, today announced a strategic partnership with ELM Learning, a custom eLearning development and learning experience design firm known for its proprietary Neurolearning™ approach and creating tailored learning programs—including gamification, microlearning, instructor-led training, and blended solutions—all designed to make learning stick. This collaboration empowers organizations to enrich their learning programs with engaging, tailored experiences that drive retention, performance, and business impact.

Founded in 2013 and based in St. Clair Shores, MI, ELM Learning brings deep expertise in crafting learning experiences. By combining modern cognitive science, design thinking, and storytelling, ELM ensures learning is both Relevant & Meaningful (Smart) and Visually Compelling (Beautiful) (“Smart + Beautiful"). Their solutions are purpose-built to engage, motivate, and scale across diverse learner populations.

“Content is never one-size-fits-all, especially when you're trying to drive real business outcomes,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By embedding ELM Learning’s engaging, gamified, custom experiences into our platform, we’re helping our clients deliver learning that sticks.”Through this partnership, organizations leveraging Intellum can seamlessly incorporate ELM’s bespoke learning assets—such as gamified knowledge checks, bite-sized mobile lessons, and blended learning modules—directly into their learning paths. The result: more engaging, personalized education that aligns with performance goals and adapts to real-world workflows.

“At ELM, we believe learning should be both Smart and Beautiful— grounded in science, elevated by design, and unforgettable through storytelling,” said Andrew Fayad, Managing Partner of ELM Learning. “Together with Intellum, we’re empowering organizations to transform training into experiences that truly resonate with their people and translate into measurable results.”About [Intellum](https://www.intellum.com/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)Intellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.

About [ELM Learning](https://elmlearning.com/?utm_source=intellum&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=press_release&utm_term=strategic_partnership)Founded in 2013, ELM Learning creates meaningful learning experiences using its proprietary Neurolearning™ framework, which blends cognitive science, design thinking, and storytelling. With expertise in custom eLearning, instructional design consulting, instructor-led training, gamification, microlearning, and blended solutions, ELM helps organizations deliver training that engages, inspires, and performs.
