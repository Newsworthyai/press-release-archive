# Historic Augusta's 60th Anniversary Celebration: Perfectly Aged Benefit Auction Set for September 18, 2025

Augusta, Georgia (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Aug 21, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” Historic Augusta invites the community to join in celebrating its 60th anniversary at the eagerly anticipated Perfectly Aged Benefit Auction on September 18, 2025. The event, set to take place at Saint Paul's River Room from 6:00 pm to 9:00 pm, promises an enchanting evening filled with delicious libations, music, and catering.

Guests are encouraged to attend in black tie attire, with masks and tiaras adding an extra touch of elegance to the festivities. The event will feature online ticket sales and bidding, with items available for pickup by noon on September 19th.

The Perfectly Aged Benefit Auction is made possible through the generous support of various organizations and individuals, including Keystone sponsors such as the Knox Foundation and The Kru, as well as Gold sponsors Laurie McRae Interiors and Stokes Hodges Auto Group. Silver and bronze sponsors have also contributed to the success of the event.

"Your participation and support are vital in helping continue the mission of preserving and promoting Augusta's architectural and cultural heritage. Together, we can make a lasting impact on this community," remarked a representative of Charity Ace, who will be assisting with the fundraising efforts.Attendees can show their support for Historic Augusta by donating, becoming a member, or volunteering their time. Don't miss this opportunity to join in the celebration, give back to the community, and support the preservation efforts of Historic Augusta at the 60th-anniversary Perfectly Aged Benefit Auction.

For more information and to purchase tickets, visit the Historic Augusta website.
