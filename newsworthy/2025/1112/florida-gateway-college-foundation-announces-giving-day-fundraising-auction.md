# Florida Gateway College Foundation Announces Giving Day Fundraising Auction

Lake City, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Nov 12, 2025 @ 12:15 PM US/Eastern — The Foundation for Florida Gateway College announces its 3rd Annual Giving Day Experience Auction, with a goal to raise $140,001 to support scholarships, student programs, and campus initiatives. The auction opens on Monday, November 11, 2025, and closes Tuesday, November 18, 2025 providing the community with a meaningful opportunity to invest in student success.

 The Foundation invites alumni, friends and supporters to take three impactful actions:

 Give directly to the Foundation for Florida Gateway CollegeEvery dollar counts! Make a direct contribution to the Foundation at www.fgc.edu/give.

 Bid on Exclusive ExperiencesExplore and bid on unforgettable adventures, local treasures and exclusive packages in our online auction at at fgc.winningbidder.com/event

 Establish a New EndowmentCreate a lasting legacy by setting up a new endowment fund. Thanks to generous matching opportunities, any general scholarship or general vocational scholarship endowment starting at $12,500 will be matched dollar-for-dollar, instantly becoming a $25,000 fund. Many donors choose to honor or memorialize loved ones through these meaningful gifts.

 "We believe students should have every opportunity to attend Florida Gateway College regardless of their financial situation,” said Rebecca Golden, Director of Resource Development at the Foundation. “So we build relationships with YOU, with companies, with anyone who shares the passion for helping Florida Gateway College students.” This year's auction features a diverse array of exciting items including a flight experience with a military fighter pilot in a vintage warbird aircraft and luxurious travel vacation packages at local and international destinations, as well as an Ultimate Pro Sports Fan Getaway package, Hand crafted goods and much more.

 Donor participation helps fund scholarships, campus improvements and initiatives that empower students and enrich the community. Join the effort to unlock potential and support the next generation of learners.

 About the [Foundation for Florida Gateway College](https://www.fgc.edu/alumni/the-foundation/index.html): The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to expanding educational opportunities and supporting student achievement at Florida Gateway College. Through scholarships, community partnerships and innovative programs, the Foundation makes higher education accessible and fosters lifelong success.

 About [Florida Gateway College](https://www.fgc.edu/index.html) Florida Gateway College is a comprehensive, regionally accredited community college in Lake City, Florida. Offering pathway programs, technical training, transfer degrees, and workforce development, the college provides accessible education that empowers students to achieve their academic and career goals. Committed to community engagement and lifelong learning, Florida Gateway College prepares students to succeed in a diverse and dynamic workforce.
