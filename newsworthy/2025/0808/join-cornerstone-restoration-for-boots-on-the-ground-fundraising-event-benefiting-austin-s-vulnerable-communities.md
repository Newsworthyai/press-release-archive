# Join Cornerstone Restoration for 'Boots on the Ground' Fundraising Event Benefiting Austin's Vulnerable Communities

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 8, 2025 @ 11:00 AM Central — Cornerstone Restoration, a non-profit organization dedicated to restoring hope to the vulnerable in the Austin area, is gearing up for its first live auction event at Star Hill Ranch in Austin, Texas. The event, "Boots on the Ground," will raise funds to support construction projects aimed at serving the physical and spiritual needs of various marginalized groups in the community.Founded on the principle of showcasing God's love through tangible actions, Cornerstone Restoration focuses on reaching out to the elderly, widows, single parents, children, veterans, disabled individuals, as well as survivors of human trafficking or sexual exploitation. By providing construction-based projects, the organization aims to make a lasting positive impact and bring hope and healing to those in need.In a heartwarming display of support, Cowboy Auctioneer has generously donated their services to the live fundraising portion of the event. Heath Hale, Cowboy Auctioneer, expressed his excitement about the collaboration, stating, "We have been following the incredible work and faith-filled growth of Cornerstone Restoration for years and we’re so honored to partner with their team this year as strategic fundraising consultants and impactful auctioneers. It’s an honor to help bring the hope, healing, and restoration to those in our community that Cornerstone Restoration serves."The fundraising event promises a unique opportunity for attendees to participate in a live auction and donation appeal while enjoying an engaging and meaningful evening. Funds raised during the event will go towards furthering the mission of Cornerstone Restoration in providing hope in Jesus through construction projects that make a difference in the lives of those facing challenges.To learn more about Cornerstone Ranch and the impactful work of Cornerstone Restoration, visit https://www.cornerstonerestoration.org/. For details about the "Boots on the Ground" event and registration, please visit https://app.betterunite.com/cornerstonerestoration-2025fundraiser-bootsontheground.
