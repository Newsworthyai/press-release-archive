# "Let Freedom Swing" Charity Golf Tournament Hosted by the Colorado Veterans Project Set to Benefit Local Veterans

Denver, Colorado (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 8, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” The Let Freedom Swing charity golf tournament, organized by the Colorado Veterans Project, is scheduled to take place on September 17th, 2025, at the prestigious Ridge at Castle Pines North. The event aims to raise funds to support local veterans and their families, honoring their service and sacrifices.

The tournament, now in its fifth year, has been a beacon of hope for veterans in the local community. According to the event organizers, "We are excited to host the Let Freedom Swing golf tournament once again and continue our mission of giving back to those who have given so much for our country."

Proceeds from the tournament will benefit local organizations that provide essential services to veterans, including mental health support, housing assistance, job training, and more. The event offers participants an opportunity to make a tangible difference in the lives of veterans while enjoying a fun and competitive day on the golf course.

This year's tournament promises to be bigger and better than ever, with a range of exciting activities planned for participants. From a competitive golf tournament with various prizes to a silent auction featuring exclusive items, attendees can look forward to an engaging and memorable experience.

"We are deeply grateful for the generous support of our sponsors, volunteers, and participants who make this event possible," said one of the organizers. "Their contributions play a crucial role in ensuring that we can continue to provide much-needed assistance to our local veterans."In addition to the golf tournament, the Let Freedom Swing event will also feature a special awards ceremony and a reception where participants can network and connect with fellow supporters of the veteran community. The tournament provides an excellent opportunity for individuals and businesses to come together in support of a noble cause.
