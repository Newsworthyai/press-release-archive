# Hope for Houston Ball Raises Funds to Support AIH Houston's Mission

Houston, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 8, 2025 @ 9:00 AM Pacific â€” The annual Hope for Houston Ball, organized by AIH Houston, brings together philanthropists, community leaders, and supporters to raise funds for the organization's mission of empowering individuals battling HIV. The event, held at The Revaire, will be a night filled with hope, inspiration, and generous contributions.

"We are thrilled to see the continued support from our community for the important work we do at AIH Houston," remarked Tchernavia Sessum, the Executive Director of AIH Houston. "The Hope for Houston Ball is not just a fundraiser; it is a celebration of advocacy for individuals who deserve the same opportunities as everyone else." The evening will kick off with a cocktail reception, where guests can bid on an array of silent auction items generously donated by local businesses, and is followed by an exciting live auction, featuring exclusive experiences and luxury items. In addition to the auctions, guests will be treated to a gourmet dinner and captivating entertainment, including live music and a dance performance by individuals supported by AIH Houston.

"We are grateful for the outpouring of support from our donors and sponsors," shared Sessum. "Their contributions will directly impact the lives of individuals with HIV, providing them with access to vital resources and opportunities for growth." "Together, we are making a difference and building a brighter future for individuals with this disease," Sessum said, "The Hope for Houston Ball serves as a reminder that when we come together, we can create positive change and transform lives." For more information about AIH Houston and future events, please contact Tchernavia Sessum at sessumt@aihhouston.org or visit www.aihhouston.org.
