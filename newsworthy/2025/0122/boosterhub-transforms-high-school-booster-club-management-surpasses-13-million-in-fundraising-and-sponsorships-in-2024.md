# BoosterHub Transforms High School Booster Club Management, Surpasses $13 Million in Fundraising and Sponsorships in 2024.

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jan 22, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central â€” BoosterHub, the leading digital platform for high school booster club management, announced today that its member organizations have collectively raised over $13 million in 2024, marking a significant milestone in supporting extracurricular activities including sports, fine arts and marching bands.

The innovative software platform, designed specifically for volunteer-led booster clubs, has revolutionized how these essential organizations operate, reaching more than 50,000 members across 400+ clubs nationwide. In 2024 alone, BoosterHub helped coordinate over 200,000 volunteers, making it one of the fastest-growing sports and band support applications in the education sector.

"BoosterHub was created to empower the dedicated volunteers who are the backbone of high school sports and band programs," said Robin Eissler, CEO at BoosterHub. "Our platform's success reflects the critical need for modern, efficient tools that help these organizations thrive."

The all-in-one platform streamlines three critical aspects of booster club operations:

* Volunteer engagement and coordination
* Fundraising campaign management
* Financial oversight and best practices implementation

For booster club leaders like Mark Kincaid, Volunteer at Broadneck Music Boosters, the impact has been transformative: "This year, for the first time ever, 100% of our members paid their fees.
