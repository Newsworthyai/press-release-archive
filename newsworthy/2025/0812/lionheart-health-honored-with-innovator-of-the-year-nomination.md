# Lionheart Health Honored with "Innovator of the Year" Nomination

Newport Beach, California (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 12, 2025 @ 7:30 AM Pacific â€” Lionheart Health, a leader in regenerative health, has been nominated for the Orange County Business Journal's eleventh annual "Innovator of the Year" Awards. This prestigious recognition celebrates individuals and organizations in Orange County demonstrating exceptional brilliance and leadership in innovation.

Lionheart Health is at the forefront of extending healthy lifespans through its groundbreaking bioelectric and biologics technology. The company's innovative platform focuses on teaching the body to renew and restore itself, moving beyond symptom treatment to address the root causes of aging and decline.

"This nomination is a testament to the innovative efforts our entire Lionheart Health team has poured into developing our bioelectric and biologic technology platform for regenerative aesthetics and for increasing longevity and healthspan," said Howard J. Leonhardt, Executive Chairman and CEO.

Pioneering Regenerative SolutionsLionheart Health's commitment to innovation is evident in its suite of FDA-cleared products. Backed by rigorous clinical studies, these solutions are currently available globally through licensed clinics and address key areas of health and wellness, including skin regeneration, hair regeneration, sexual health, joint pain relief, and rapid body toning for weight loss without muscle loss. The company's flagship research clinic, training center, and MedSpa are conveniently located in Newport Beach, California.

A Legacy of Longevity and InnovationLionheart Health's dedication to extending healthy life is deeply rooted in the four-decade legacy of its parent company, Leonhardt Ventures. This long-standing commitment to longevity and regeneration has driven every treatment, therapy, and advancement.

Further solidifying its innovative standing, Lionheart Health was recently named one of 40 semi-finalists out of 600 competitors in the $101 million XPRIZE Healthspan competition. This global challenge aims to demonstrate the ability to extend healthy life by 20 years, a mission that perfectly aligns with Lionheart Health's core objectives.
