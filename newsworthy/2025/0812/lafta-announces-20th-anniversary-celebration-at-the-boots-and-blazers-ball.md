# LAFTA Announces 20th Anniversary Celebration at the Boots and Blazers Ball

Lampasas, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 12, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — The Lampasas Association for the Arts (LAFTA) is excited to invite the community to save the date for the highly anticipated 2025 Boots and Blazers Ball, scheduled for Saturday, October 11. This year's event will mark LAFTA's 20th anniversary and promises to be a night of celebration and entertainment for a good cause.

The Boots and Blazers Ball is LAFTA’s annual fundraising event, designed to expand opportunities for everyone to experience the arts. The highlight of the evening will be a performance by the Drew Fish Band, ensuring a night of lively music and dancing to commemorate this special milestone for LAFTA.

In anticipation of the event, LAFTA President Jennifer Smith shared her enthusiasm, stating, "We are thrilled to celebrate our 20th anniversary at the upcoming Boots and Blazers Ball. This event is not just a fundraiser but a celebration of the vibrant arts community that LAFTA has fostered over the years."

The Boots and Blazers Ball will also feature the opportunity to explore the Hannah Spring Sculpture Garden, showcasing visiting sculptures as well as the permanent sculpture collection. Additionally, the event will serve as a call for artists to contribute to the growing artistic landscape of Lampasas.

For more information or ticketing options, visit www.lafta.org/boots-and-blazers-ball/
