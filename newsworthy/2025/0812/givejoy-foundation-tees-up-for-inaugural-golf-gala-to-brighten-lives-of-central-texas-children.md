# GiveJoy Foundation Tees Up for Inaugural Golf &amp; Gala to Brighten Lives of Central Texas Children

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 12, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central â€” The GiveJoy Foundation has announced the launch of its inaugural fundraising event, The Inaugural GiveJoy Classic Golf & Gala, set to take place at the esteemed Fazio Foothills Golf Course in Austin, Texas, on September 16th.

The event's schedule is packed with exciting activities, including a thrilling round of golf, a delightful cocktail hour, a sumptuous dinner, and a live auction fundraiser led by the renowned Cowboy Auctioneer team. Funds raised during the event will directly contribute to GiveJoy's impactful initiatives, such as Joyful Soles, JoyBoxes, and the Grow & Give Back Holiday Drive, which offer vital resources and opportunities to underserved children and families.

"The GiveJoy Foundation was created to provide essential resources to children to ensure that they are able to experience joy, wellness, and care to the fullest. At Cowboy Auctioneer, taking care of children and the vulnerable in our community is one of our biggest passions, and we couldn't be more thrilled to be a part of the GiveJoy Foundation's inaugural fundraiser," shared Emily Hardt, Cowboy Auctioneer Vice President.

The GiveJoy Foundation invites all individuals and organizations passionate about making a difference to join them at The Inaugural GiveJoy Classic Golf & Gala for a day of philanthropy, community, and fun.

For more information and sponsorship opportunities, visit www.givejoyfoundation.org/
