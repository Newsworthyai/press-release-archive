# Pulse Signal Agency Launches to Redefine Marketing Performance in Regulated and Emerging Industries

San Francisco, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Aug 12, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Eastern — Pulse Signal Agency today announced its official launch, bringing a next-generation marketing model to industries that demand speed, precision, and compliance. The agency blends bold creative, integrated strategy, and AI-powered automation to help brands drive measurable outcomes across the entire marketing funnel.

At the helm is Rose Fulton, a 20-year marketing veteran, award-winning strategist, and advocate for ethical innovation. As the founder and CEO of Pulse Signal and the creator of its proprietary adtech platform ReceptivIQ, Fulton has positioned the agency as a trusted partner for brands navigating complex markets.

“Pulse Signal was built to help brands scale smarter,” said Fulton. “We deliver marketing that adapts in real time, connects with real people, and drives results that can be felt, clicked, and measured.”ReceptivIQ is a proprietary AI engine that automates audience segmentation, creative optimization, and cross-channel deployment. Key features include dynamic persona mapping, predictive creative intelligence, and real-time KPI feedback loops, all designed to boost ROI, reduce waste, and increase campaign speed to market.

Pulse Signal Services Include:* Strategy & Planning
* Market Research
* Digital Advertising (Programmatic, OTT/CTV, SEM, Organic & Paid Social)
* Content & Creative Development
* Influencer & Sponsorship Campaigns
* PR & Communications
* Email, OOH, and Direct Mail Campaigns
* Full ReceptivIQ AI Integration

Industry Expertise:Pulse Signal specializes in regulated and high-growth verticals, including:

* Healthcare & Wellness (HIPAA-compliant acquisition and awareness campaigns)
* Cannabis & Regulated Markets (compliance-focused media strategy)
* Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) (full-funnel retail and ecommerce growth)
* Technology & SaaS (lead-gen and nurture programs)
* Travel & Entertainment (geo-targeted and loyalty-building campaigns)
* Finance & Fintech (data-secure performance marketing and customer onboarding)
* Retail & Lifestyle Brands (omnichannel activations and digital-first brand strategy)

Early client results include a 40% drop in cost-per-lead for a B2B platform, 6x ROAS for a travel brand in under eight weeks, and compliant nationwide acquisition campaigns for major healthcare networks.

About Rose Fulton:Rose Fulton is a business executive and marketing leader with two decades of experience across media, advertising, and consumer strategy. A respected voice in ethical AI, ecoethics, and media equity, Fulton regularly consults and speaks on impact-driven innovation. Her background spans Fortune 500s, startup launches, and national advocacy initiatives.
