# Rhug Wild Beauty Expands U.S. Reach — Now Available on Amazon

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Nov 10, 2025 @ 6:00 AM Pacific — Rhug Wild Beauty, the award-winning luxury organic skincare brand from the historic Rhug Estate in North Wales, announced its expanded availability in the United States through Amazon, giving American consumers convenient access to the brand’s acclaimed collection of high-performance, nature-powered skincare.

 Founded by Lord Robert Newborough, a pioneer in organic and sustainable farming, Rhug Wild Beauty represents a new standard in clean beauty — uniting clinically proven efficacy with the purity of nature. Each product is meticulously crafted using a blend of wild-foraged botanicals from the Rhug Estate and hand-selected natural actives sourced globally for their scientifically validated skin benefits. The result is skincare that delivers visible results while maintaining integrity, sustainability, and authenticity.

 “Amazon allows us to reach more consumers who value sustainable, results-driven skincare,” said Lord Newborough, Founder of Rhug Wild Beauty. “We’re excited to share our estate’s organic philosophy and our commitment to combining nature with science for truly efficacious skincare.”

 Rhug Wild Beauty’s collection includes its best-selling Deep Cleansing Balm, Protecting Day Cream, Active Treatment Serum, and Reviving Skin Tonic, each COSMOS Organic certified, vegan, cruelty-free, and halal. Every formula is clinically tested across skin types to hydrate, protect, and restore balance — delivering real, measurable results through the power of natural ingredients.

 Recognized by Vogue, Tatler, and Country & Town House for redefining luxury organic skincare, Rhug Wild Beauty continues to set itself apart with its guiding principle — “Rooted in Nature, Refined by Science.”

 The brand’s entry into Amazon marks an important milestone in its U.S. expansion and aligns with its mission to make efficacious, sustainable skincare accessible to a wider audience.

 In addition to Amazon, Rhug Wild Beauty products are also available on OneLavi.com, providing another trusted destination for American consumers to explore the brand’s award-winning skincare range.

 About [Rhug Wild Beauty](https://rhugwildbeauty.com/) Rhug Wild Beauty is a luxury organic skincare brand rooted in the wild landscapes of North Wales. Each formula is crafted using a thoughtful combination of wild-foraged botanicals from the Rhug Estate and meticulously selected natural actives sourced globally for their proven efficacy. Hand-crafted in the UK, the brand’s COSMOS-certified products are vegan, cruelty-free, and halal. Founded by Lord Robert Newborough, Rhug Wild Beauty embodies clean beauty that is ethical, sustainable, and scientifically proven to deliver visible results.
