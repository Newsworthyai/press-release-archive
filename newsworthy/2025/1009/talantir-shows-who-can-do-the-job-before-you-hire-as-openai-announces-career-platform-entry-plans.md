# Talantir Shows Who Can Do the Job Before You Hire as OpenAI Announces Career Platform Entry Plans

Lisbon, Portugal (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Oct 9, 2025 @ 12:00 PM Europe/Lisbon — While OpenAI recently announced plans to expand into employment and career services, European startup Talantir is already operational, providing a comprehensive AI-powered platform that serves both employers struggling to identify qualified candidates and early-career professionals seeking to demonstrate AI-era skills.

The announcement comes as the career technology sector undergoes rapid transformation, with major AI companies recognizing the critical need for new solutions in hiring and career development. OpenAI's recent statement about "expanding economic opportunity with AI" signals growing industry attention to this €10+ billion European market opportunity.

EUROPE LEADS AS AI TRANSFORMS HIRING AND CAREERS"OpenAI's announcement validates what we've been building for the past year," said Andrew Zupko, CEO of [Talantir](https://talantir.ai). "The future of career development is happening now, not in 2026. While others announce plans, we're already helping European companies hire smarter and European talent work better in the AI era."Talantir's platform addresses the urgent crisis created by AI's impact on traditional hiring signals: with 60% of job applications now containing AI-generated content, employers can no longer reliably evaluate candidates through resumes and interviews alone, while talented individuals struggle to stand out in an ocean of AI-polished applications.

TWO-SIDED SOLUTION: EMPLOYERS AND TALENT BOTH WINUnlike announced future platforms that focus on a single side of the market, Talantir serves both constituencies simultaneously:

FOR EMPLOYERS (B2B):* Work Simulation Platform: Shows actual candidate capabilities before interviews through 15-45 minute job-specific simulations
* Process Intelligence: Captures not just what candidates produce, but how they work—including AI usage, verification methods, and decision-making patterns
* AI Fluency Assessment: Tests candidates' ability to effectively use and manage AI tools in realistic work scenarios
* Verified Shortlists: Delivers ranked, work-proven candidates with full analytics in 48-72 hours

Early results from first customers demonstrate significant impact:* completion rate—double the industry benchmark
* 5× faster candidate shortlisting (days instead of weeks)
* Reduction in cost-per-hire
* 15 recruiter hours saved per hire

"First assessment tool our candidates actually complete," said a Head of Talent Acquisition at a European tech scaleup. "We see real work quality before wasting interview time. It's transformed our early-career hiring."FOR TALENT (B2C):* Real-World Job Simulations: Practice authentic workplace scenarios designed by industry professionals
* AI Career Mentorship: Personalized guidance powered by AI to navigate career decisions
* Skills Verification: Demonstrate actual capabilities, not just credentials
* Career Roadmaps: Tailored development paths based on individual goals and market demand
* AI Fluency Development: Learn to work effectively with AI tools— the most in-demand skill in today's job market

"Too many talented people are invisible because everyone's CV looks perfect now," added Zupko. "Our platform lets real skills shine through. Candidates who complete our simulations show employers they can actually do the work—and they develop AI fluency in the process."THE AI FLUENCY ADVANTAGEA critical differentiator in Talantir's approach is measuring AI fluency—how effectively candidates use AI tools to enhance their work rather than replace their thinking. Recent data shows professionals with strong AI skills earn 28% more on average, yet most assessment platforms either ban AI use or cannot evaluate it.

"We don't penalize AI use—we measure quality AI use," explained Zupko. "The future of work isn't human versus AI. It's humans who work well with AI versus those who don't. We identify and develop the former."The platform tracks:

* Prompt quality and iteration
* Information verification and fact-checking
* Appropriate tool selection
* Critical thinking in AI-assisted workflows
* Ethical AI usage patterns

MARKET TIMING AND EUROPEAN ADVANTAGETalantir's operational status gives it a significant first-mover advantage as the AI career services market emerges. The company has:

* Live platform serving customers across the EU
* Proven product-market fit through successful pilots
* EU University partnerships launching in Q4 2025 to create talent pipelines
* Full compliance with EU AI Act requirements from day one

The European early-career hiring market represents a €10+ billion annual opportunity, with approximately 1.2 million hires per year among companies hiring 20+ entry-level roles. Talantir specifically targets the €6 billion segment of high-volume hirers (20+ roles annually).

"Europe is leading in responsible AI innovation," said Zupko. "While we welcome competition from Silicon Valley companies, we've built deep expertise in the European market, European regulations, and European talent development needs. We're not planning to enter this market, we're already here, already working, already delivering results."THE FUTURE OF WORK IS HEREAs major technology companies announce intentions to transform career services, Talantir's message to both employers and job seekers is clear: the tools to thrive in the AI era are available now.

For employers, this means:

* Stop wasting time on AI-polished resumes
* See real work before making hiring decisions
* Find candidates who can actually deliver in AI-augmented roles
* Reduce hiring costs and time-to-hire significantly

For early-career professionals, this means:

* Demonstrate real capabilities, not just credentials
* Develop and prove AI fluency
* Get personalized career guidance
* Stand out in a crowded, AI-homogenized job market

"The career development market is undergoing its biggest transformation in 50 years," concluded Zupko. "We're not waiting for that future—we're building it today, with European companies and European talent."ABOUT TALANTIRTalantir is a European AI technology company providing a work simulations platform for the future of work. For employers, Talantir delivers work simulation-based hiring that reveals actual candidate capabilities and AI fluency before interviews. For early-career professionals, Talantir provides AI-powered career development, skills verification, and mentorship to succeed in AI augmented workplaces.

Founded in 2025 and based in Portugal, Talantir serves customers across the EU with full compliance with the EU AI Act.
