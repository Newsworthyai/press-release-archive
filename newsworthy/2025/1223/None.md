# Healthipops Brings Fun, Science-Backed Wellness to Cold &amp; Flu Season — Now Available on Amazon

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Dec 23, 2025 @ 5:00 AM Pacific — As families prepare for the busy holiday season and the onset of cold and flu months, Healthipops, the award-winning family wellness brand known for its science-backed vitamin lollipops, is making it easier for parents to support everyday health. Healthipops is now available on Amazon, bringing its playful, plant-powered approach to wellness directly to U.S. households nationwide.

 Founded by parents of four Maxi and Nick, Healthipops was created to solve a common family challenge: getting children to actually enjoy wellness products. By blending pharmaceutical-grade botanical extracts with essential vitamins and minerals, Healthipops delivers functional nutrition in a format kids love — without the fuss of pills, gummies, or syrups.

 “Cold and flu season can be stressful for families, especially during the holidays,” said Maxi, co-founder of Healthipops. “We wanted to create something that supports everyday wellness while still being fun, comforting, and easy for kids to enjoy.”

 Healthipops growing product lineup includes: * Healthipops Winter Wellness with marshmallow root, lime flower and elderberry. Soothing the symptoms of coughs and colds.
* Healthipops Throat Soother with marshmallow root and elderberry.
* Healthipops Travel with ginger root, lime flower and chamomile.
* Healthipops Ear Popper Stopper with Chamomile, marshmallow root and peppermint leaf. To help ease the anxiety of flying.
* Healthipops Kids Multivitaminn, supporting immunity with vitamins and minerals.

 Making them especially useful during seasonal travel, school routines, and winter months.

 Unlike many sugar-free alternatives, Healthipops consciously uses a natural sugar base rather than sugar alcohols, helping avoid digestive discomfort while maintaining great taste. All products are vegan, sustainably sourced, and crafted with natural flavorings to ensure a smooth, enjoyable experience from start to finish.

 Parents want wellness products they can trust, and kids want something that tastes good,” said Nick, co-founder of Healthipops. “Making Healthipops available on Amazon allows families across the U.S. to access our products easily during one of the busiest times of the year.

 Healthipops’ expansion on Amazon reflects the brand’s continued momentum in the U.S. market as more families seek approachable, science-backed alternatives to traditional children’s supplements — especially during peak seasonal wellness periods.
