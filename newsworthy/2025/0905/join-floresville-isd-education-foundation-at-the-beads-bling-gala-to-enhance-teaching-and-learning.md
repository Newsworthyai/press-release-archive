# Join Floresville ISD Education Foundation at the Beads &amp; Bling Gala to Enhance Teaching and Learning

Floresville, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Sep 5, 2025 @ 7:20 AM Pacific â€” The Floresville ISD Education Foundation (FISDEF), an independent non-profit organization dedicated to supporting educational initiatives in the Floresville Independent School District, is thrilled to announce the upcoming Beads & Bling Gala, themed "It's a Mardi Gras Thing," on September 27, 2025.

The Beads & Bling Gala serves as a vital fundraising effort to provide grant opportunities for teachers, enabling them to implement creative initiatives that go beyond traditional classroom learning. Every donation, no matter the size, will play a crucial role in supporting the educational growth of students within the Floresville ISD.

Donors who generously contribute to the gala will be recognized at the event and on the FISDEF website, showcasing their commitment to the students and educators of Floresville ISD.

"Your participation in the FISDEF gala sends a powerful message of support for the educational community in Floresville ISD," added a representative from Charity Ace, who will be assisting with the fundraising efforts.

The primary goals of the Floresville ISD Education Foundation include providing foundation grant opportunities through monetary gifts to foster innovative teaching practices and enrich the educational experience for all students.
