# The Center to Host 'Beneath the Hill Country Sky' Benefit Concert Featuring William Beckmann in Boerne, Texas

Boerne, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 15, 2025 @ 5:00 AM Central — The Center, a vital nonprofit dedicated to enriching the lives of aging adults in Boerne and Kendall County, announces its exclusive benefit concert, "Beneath the Hill Country Sky," set for Thursday, November 1, 2025, at Estancia at Thunder Valley. This elegant evening features an intimate performance by acclaimed rising Texas singer-songwriter William Beckmann, offering sweeping Hill County views and a memorable experience for attendees.

A Night of Fine Dining, Outstanding Music and Community ImpactIn place of its annual gala, The Center is hosting a unique fundraising concert event designed to bring together the community while supporting essential programs for aging adults. “In lieu of our annual gala, this year we’re creating a truly unforgettable evening – a lavish night under the Hill Country skies, featuring William Beckmann, one of today’s most exciting rising stars in country music,” said Olivia Burdick, CEO of The Center. “Come join us ‘Beneath the Hill Country Sky’ – An Evening Concert Event Benefiting The Center.’”All proceeds from the event support The Center’s mission to improve quality of life for aging adults through programs that foster independence, dignity and community connection. The Center serves the Texas Hill Country community through a number of enriching programs including Meals on Wheels, daily lunches and activities at Kronkosky Place, as well as health and wellness classes.

Guests will enjoy gourmet dinner catering served by Don Strange Catering and opportunities to support The Center’s impactful community work by participating in additional fundraising activities throughout the evening.

Tickets for Beneath the Hill Country Sky are available exclusively to sponsors and VIP guests. Every dollar raised benefits programs that promote health, independence and community engagement for local aging adults and seniors. Tickets will be on sale soon – please visit the event website for updates and additional details.

About [The Center](https://thecenterboerne.org/)The Center is committed to enriching the lives of older and aging adults in Boerne and Kendall County. Through essential services such as Meals on Wheels, social activities at Kronkosky Place, as well as health & wellness programs., The Center fosters independence, dignity and connection, creating a supportive environment for all community members.Contact:
