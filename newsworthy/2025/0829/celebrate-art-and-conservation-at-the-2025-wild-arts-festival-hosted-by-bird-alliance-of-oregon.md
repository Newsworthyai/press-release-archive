# Celebrate Art and Conservation at the 2025 Wild Arts Festival Hosted by Bird Alliance of Oregon

Portland, Oregon (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 29, 2025 @ 8:00 AM Pacific â€” The Bird Alliance of Oregon is thrilled to announce the return of its highly anticipated 45th Wild Arts Festival, set to take place on December 6 and 7, 2025, at the Wingspan Event Center located at the Washington County Fairgrounds. Building on the success of the previous year's event, the Festival promises a weekend of celebrating art, literature, and conservation.

"The Bird Alliance of Oregon is dedicated to fostering a deep connection between people and nature, and the Wild Arts Festival serves as a wonderful platform to showcase this commitment and raise crucial funds," said a representative of Charity Ace, who will be assisting with the fundraising efforts.

The Wild Arts Festival offers a unique opportunity to explore a diverse selection of artwork, nature-inspired books, and exclusive silent auction items, all while supporting the Bird Alliance of Oregon's vital mission of inspiring love and protection for birds, wildlife, and the environment.

Attendees can look forward to engaging with 62 talented artists, meeting 25 accomplished Northwest writers, and participating in a silent auction featuring over 100 retail items, art pieces, vacations, and experiences.

Presented by the Backyard Bird Shop & Sauvie Island Natives, the Wild Arts Festival promises a memorable experience for all visitors, with its new location at the Wingspan Event Center offering convenient access via light rail and ample parking.

Join the Bird Alliance of Oregon in their pursuit of conservation and appreciation for the natural world at the 2025 Wild Arts Festival. Mark your calendars for December 6 and 7, and immerse yourself in a celebration of artistry and environmental stewardship.

For more information and updates on the event, visit the event website and subscribe to the email list.
