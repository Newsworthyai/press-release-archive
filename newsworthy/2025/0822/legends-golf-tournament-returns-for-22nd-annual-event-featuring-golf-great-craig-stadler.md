# Legends Golf Tournament Returns for 22nd Annual Event, Featuring Golf Great Craig Stadler

Sedalia, Colorado (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 22, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” The 22nd Annual Legends Golf Tournament, hosted by Volunteers of America, is set to take place on Wednesday, September 10, 2025, at the exclusive Sanctuary Golf Club. This year's legendary guest is none other than Craig Stadler, a distinguished PGA champion with an impressive list of accolades, including victories in prestigious tournaments such as the 1982 Masters Tournament and the 1991 Tour Championship.

"We are thrilled to have Craig Stadler join us at this year's tournament," says an event organizer. "His presence will undoubtedly add to the excitement and prestige of the event, making it a memorable day for all participants."

The tournament aims to raise funds in support of Volunteers of America's essential services across Colorado. With team entries priced at $6,000 and limited to 30 foursomes, participants can look forward to a day filled with golfing excellence, camaraderie, and philanthropy. Each team entry includes brunch, snacks, drinks, and dinner, along with green fees, carts, and access to the driving range.

In addition to the golfing action, the tournament schedule includes a Player's Brunch, team photos with Craig Stadler, a shotgun start, a team dinner, and various player favors and prizes, including gift cards for the top-performing teams.

Sponsorship opportunities are also available for businesses looking to support a worthy cause.

Don't miss this opportunity to tee off for a good cause and enjoy a day of golfing with a legend. Join the Volunteers of America Legends Golf Tournament and make a difference in the lives of Veterans and individuals in need across Colorado.

For more information and to register, visit the event website.
