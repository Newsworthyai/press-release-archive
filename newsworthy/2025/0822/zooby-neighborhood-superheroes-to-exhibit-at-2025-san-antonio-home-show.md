# Zooby Neighborhood Superheroes to Exhibit at 2025 San Antonio Home Show

San Antonio, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 22, 2025 @ 3:50 PM Central — Zooby Neighborhood Superheroes Inc., a trusted leader in San Antonio home services, announced today that it will be an exhibitor at the San Antonio Home Show, taking place Saturday, August 23 through Sunday, August 24, 2025, at the Freeman Coliseum Expo Hall A.

The two-day event runs from 10:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. on Saturday and 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Sunday, offering homeowners across the region a chance to explore the latest innovations in renovation, remodeling, and home improvement.

At the Zooby booth, attendees can meet Elmer Zubiate and his “Neighborhood Superheroes” in person. These technicians and service experts are committed to delivering reliable, flat-rate home solutions with integrity. Zooby will showcase its customer-first approach, including:

* How Zooby offers customers no hourly billing and no contractor drama. Just honest, flat-rate services done right the first time.
* Zooby provides home repairs without the headaches, maintenance without the mystery, and wonderful upgrades without the upsell.

“We love being part of events that celebrate San Antonio homeowners,” said Elmer Zubiate, CEO of Zooby Neighborhood Superheroes. “The Home Show is a chance to connect face-to-face, answer questions, and show families how we make home care simpler, fairer, and more reliable. We want every visitor to walk away knowing they don’t have to settle for contractors who play games. They can choose a superhero team that treats their home like their own.”

The San Antonio Home Show brings together dozens of leading home improvement professionals in an interactive environment designed to inspire, motivate, and excite. Whether attendees are planning a small update or a major remodel, they’ll leave with confidence and ideas for making their homes everything they want them to be.

Zooby invites visitors to stop by their exhibit at the Freeman Coliseum Expo Hall A to talk with its Neighborhood Superheroes, learn about exclusive show specials, and get expert advice on upcoming home projects.

Event Details:

* What: San Antonio Home Show
* When: Saturday, August 23 (10:00 a.m.–8:00 p.m.) and Sunday, August 24 (10:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.), 2025
* Where: Freeman Coliseum Expo Hall A, San Antonio, TX

For more information about the Home Show, visit https://freemancoliseum.com/event/san-antonio-home-show-2/ []. To learn more about Zooby Neighborhood Superheroes, visit https://zooby.com.
