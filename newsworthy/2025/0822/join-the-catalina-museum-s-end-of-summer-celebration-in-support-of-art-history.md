# Join the Catalina Museum's End of Summer Celebration in Support of Art &amp; History

Santa Catalina Island, California (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 22, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — The Catalina Museum for Art & History is thrilled to announce its upcoming "End of Summer Party," scheduled to take place on September 20th from 4:00 to 7:00 PM. The event aims to pay tribute to Dr. Richard Denney, a cherished figure in Catalina Island's community, while also raising crucial funds to support the museum's mission of preserving and celebrating the island's rich history.

“We are thrilled to help with the End of Summer Party and pay tribute to Dr. Richard Denney. His commitment to the well-being of animals on the island has touched the hearts of many,” said a representative of Charity Ace, who will be assisting in the fundraising efforts.

The End of Summer Party offers a range of sponsorship opportunities, from the prestigious Top Dog Sponsorship at $10,000 to the accessible Animal Lover Sponsorship at $1,500. These sponsorships provide an array of benefits, including reserved tables, recognition, museum memberships, and exclusive event invitations.

Individual tickets are available at $200 for members and $250 for general admission, with discounted rates for museum members. For those unable to attend, donations of any amount are warmly welcomed to assist in furthering the museum's mission.

To secure tickets, sponsorships, or make a donation, interested parties are encouraged to reach out to Natasha Neufeld Lerner at natashanl@catalinamuseum.org or by phone at (310) 850-6492.

Don't miss this opportunity to support the Catalina Museum while enjoying a memorable evening of festivities and appreciation for the island's beloved veterinarian, Dr. Richard Denney.
