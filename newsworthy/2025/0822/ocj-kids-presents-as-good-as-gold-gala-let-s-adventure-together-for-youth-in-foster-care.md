# OCJ Kids Presents As Good As Gold Gala: Let's Adventure Together for Youth in Foster Care

Scottsdale, Arizona (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Aug 22, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” OCJ Kids is thrilled to announce their premier annual fundraising event, the As Good As Gold Gala: Let's Adventure Together, taking place on October 4th, 2025, at the Orange Tree Golf Resort. This exciting evening promises a blend of music, delectable food, program updates, and inspirational guest speakers, all in support of youth in foster care across Arizona.

OCJ Kids is a nonprofit 501 (c) (3) organization dedicated to equipping and empowering Arizona's foster and at-risk youth. "We provide essential resources, transitional support, and life-skills programs to help them thrive during one of the most challenging times in their lives. What makes OCJ Kids unique is our hands-on, relationship-driven approach- partnering directly with group homes, caseworkers, and community members to meet urgent needs quickly and consistently, ensuring no child in the foster care system feels forgotten," said an OCJ Kids representative.

The gala serves as a platform for former foster youth to share their transformative journeys, shedding light on the impact of OCJ Kids' programs and community support. Every dollar raised during the event will contribute to the continuation of life-changing initiatives, essential resources, and unwavering assistance for youth in need.

Attendees can register as individual fundraisers, secure a spot at the event, or opt for sponsorships to support OCJ Kids in their mission. By partnering with OCJ Kids, organizations can further amplify their impact and demonstrate their commitment to the community.

Don't miss this opportunity to be part of a transformative evening that will leave a lasting impact on the lives of vulnerable youth. Register today and embark on an adventure of giving back with OCJ Kids.

For more information and sponsorship options for the As Good As Gold Gala, please visit the OCJ Kids website.
