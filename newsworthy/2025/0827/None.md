# Intellum and Honorlock Partner to Elevate Assessment Integrity with AI-Enhanced Proctoring

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Aug 27, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading provider of scalable learning management solutions, today announced a strategic partnership with Honorlock, a leader in online proctoring and integrity solutions. This collaboration delivers a unified platform that ensures secure, fair, and accessible assessments for enterprise clients using online certification, compliance, and skills-based programs.

Honorlock combines advanced AI with live human proctors to deliver the best of both worlds - 24/7/365 proctoring and support with no scheduling - to uphold exam security and test-taker confidence. The seamless integration with Intellum allows exams to launch directly within the learning environment, eliminating additional logins and offering detailed reports and time-stamped recordings back in the LMS dashboard. This ensures a frictionless experience for both the test-taker and the administrator.

“Without integrity there is no validity,” said Dan Hynes, SVP of Partnerships at Intellum. “By embedding Honorlock’s secure, AI‑driven proctoring within our platform, we’re enabling clients to confidently administer assessments without sacrificing user experience, compliance, or integrity.” Together, Intellum and Honorlock empower enterprises to deliver assessments that combine adaptive learning journeys with secure, verifiable testing. Learners can validate skills through on-demand exams, while organizations benefit from flat-rate pricing, 24/7/365 multi-lingual human support, and actionable analytics through Honorlock’s Analytics Hub™.

“Organizations need complete confidence in their online assessments,” said Patrick Devlin, VP of Professional Education at Honorlock. “By embedding our proctoring solution directly into their platform, Intellum is enhancing integrity and creating a seamless and user-friendly experience that removes unnecessary hurdles for test-takers and administrators alike. This partnership empowers organizations to confidently verify skills and knowledge, ensuring their learning programs truly drive verifiable performance."
