# Intellum and OpenSesame Announce Strategic Partnership to Accelerate Scalable Learning for Modern Workforces

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 16, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the pioneer in Education-Led Growth and a leader in enterprise learning platforms, and OpenSesame, a leading provider of online training courses, today announced a strategic partnership to deliver comprehensive, easily accessible learning solutions to organizations aiming to boost performance, retention, and growth through impactful training.

Organizations can now directly integrate OpenSesame’s extensive, high-quality course catalog with Intellum’s powerful learning experience platform, enabling businesses to deliver the right training to the right audiences faster and at scale. This collaboration is especially valuable for companies that manage complex learning initiatives addressing the diverse needs of employees, customers, and partners.

Meeting the moment: Why this partnership matters Today’s companies need to onboard quickly, upskill global teams, and demonstrate the impact of their training programs. The demand for scalable, data-rich, and user-friendly learning solutions has never been higher. Intellum’s customizable platform and advanced analytics, combined with OpenSesame’s thoughtfully curated content, deliver powerful solutions that help learning leaders increase productivity, reduce churn, and demonstrate ROI.

“Organizations today are being asked to do more with less—faster,” said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum. “This partnership with OpenSesame allows our clients to combine world-class content with the industry’s most flexible learning platform to deliver meaningful, measurable learning at scale.” Key features and benefits of the partnership include: * Curated course access: With OpenSesame Plus, instantly access over 40,000 courses—covering compliance, leadership, technical skills, and more—and easily import the content most relevant to your learning program into the Intellum platform.
* Simplified integration: Reduce manual effort and shorten deployment timelines for training courses.
* Real-time insights: Track learner engagement and performance across custom and off-the-shelf content through unified reporting.
* Scalable learning across audiences: Efficiently support employees, customers, and partners through a single solution.

“At OpenSesame, we believe that the most powerful learning experiences happen when high-quality content meets effortless delivery,” said Rebeca Mesa, VP of Partnerships at OpenSesame. “By partnering with Intellum, we’re enabling organizations to quickly deploy engaging, targeted training that not only closes skill gaps but also genuinely supports people in their professional growth. It’s about empowering learners and driving tangible results together.” About [Intellum](https://www.intellum.com/) Intellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.
