# Influential Experts Collaborate to Redefine Leadership Development Through Coaching and Mentoring

Jacksons Point, Ontario, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Nov 20, 2025 @ 8:00 AM America/Toronto — The HR Research Institute (HRRI) has announced the establishment of its Future of Coaching and Mentoring for Leadership 2026 Advisory Board, bringing together a distinguished group of practitioners committed to advancing leadership excellence. This board will guide organizations on modern coaching and mentoring — balancing AI-driven tools with essential human connection, shaping policies and processes for responsible AI use, and defining the evolving role of leaders in an AI-enabled workplace.

 This expert advisory panel will direct HRRI's extensive research project and virtual event, exploring how organizations can harness coaching and mentoring to build robust leadership pipelines and accelerate professional growth. Their combined knowledge will reveal effective methodologies for implementing and scaling coaching cultures that foster leader readiness and organizational resilience.

 The initiative will provide evidence-based recommendations and actionable insights for HR professionals aiming to enhance leadership development programs, optimize mentor-mentee relationships, and create sustainable pathways for nurturing high-potential talent.
