# Intellum and Skillable Join Forces to Bring Hands-On, Experiential Learning to the Enterprise

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 30, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading enterprise learning management solution, today announced a strategic partnership with Skillable, the trusted pioneer and innovator in hands-on learning and skill validation. This collaboration brings together Intellum’s scalable learning platform with Skillable’s real-world, lab-based training experiences, empowering organizations to build, practice, and validate technical skills at scale.

Skillable’s scenario-based labs enable learners to gain job-readiness in domains like cloud computing, cybersecurity, AI, and digital transformation. With over 1,200 customizable lab templates and an advanced authoring studio, Skillable provides organizations with the tools to modernize their learning paths with scalable experiential training and validate learner performance in real-time.

“Today’s workforce doesn’t just need to know skills, they need to prove they can apply those skills,” said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum.“By integrating Skillable’s hands-on experiences into the Intellum platform, we’re helping our clients go beyond knowledge transfer to skill validation. It’s a powerful way to accelerate growth, reduce time-to-competency, and deliver true business impact.”Through this partnership, enterprise customers will be able to embed Skillable labs and challenges into Intellum learning paths, creating seamless learner journeys that include practice, performance-based assessments, and skills intelligence data. Together, Intellum and Skillable are redefining what effective enterprise learning looks like.

Sarah Noe-Danzl, Chief Marketing Officer at Skillable, said, “For learners to understand how to apply their skills on the job, they need a bridge between theory and real-world performance. By integrating Skillable within Intellum learning journeys, this last-mile learning is seamless for organizations to deliver, providing learners with safe environments to experiment and explore new technical skills, while validating that they can perform a skill in the workplace.”

About Intellum

Intellum is the leading AI-first learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights.

About Skillable

Skillable is the trusted pioneer and innovator in hands-on learning and skill validation. In job and organization-tailored scenarios, people develop and validate their skills through live learning experiences that accelerate job readiness and produce performance-based skill data and intelligence. Since 2004, more than 400 global customers have created and launched 50 million labs, using Skillable to practice and validate their skills. To learn more, visit skillable.com.
