# Comedy Village Celebrates Pride Month with Hey Gay Improv Group

NEW YORK, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Jun 3, 2025 @ 3:30 PM Eastern — The Comedy Village, a renowned hub for comedic talent in Times Square, is thrilled to announce its special event in celebration of Pride Month. The beloved Hey Gay Improv Group will take the stage every Thursday in June, showcasing the best of LGBTQ+ comic and performers.

In a statement, the Comedy Village organizer expressed, "We are excited to honor Pride Month by hosting Hey Gay Improv Group at our iconic venue. It's a time of celebration, laughter, and inclusivity, and we are proud to be a part of it." HEY GAY is an interactive musical cabaret style improv comedy show featuring David Lee Morea, Andrea Hernandez Mieres, Ryan Hudzik and Ashley Albinus plus special guests.

Attendees can look forward to a unique experience with specially crafted drinks such as the "Harvey Milkshake" and "Rainbow Rum Punch" adding to the celebratory atmosphere. The shows will kick off at 7 pm each Thursday in June, welcoming audiences of all backgrounds and preferences to enjoy a night filled with laughter and camaraderie.

"We wanted to create a space where the LGBTQ+ community can come together, have a great time, and feel represented on stage,” said a member of the Hey Gay Improv Group. "Comedy has the power to bring people together, and during Pride Month, it's even more impactful."

The Comedy Village's commitment to showcasing diverse comedy acts and supporting the LGBTQ+ community shines through this special event lineup. The Hey Gay Improv Group promises a night of non-stop laughter and entertainment, showcasing not only talent but also the spirit of unity and acceptance.

For a night of laughter, love, and celebration, mark your calendars for the Hey Gay Improv Group's performances at the Comedy Village in Times Square every Thursday in June. Tickets are selling fast, so be sure to secure your spot and join in on the fun!

For more information and ticket reservations, visit www.comedyvillage.com
