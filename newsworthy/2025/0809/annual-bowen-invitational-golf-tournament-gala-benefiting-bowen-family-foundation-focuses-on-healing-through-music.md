# Annual Bowen Invitational Golf Tournament &amp; Gala Benefiting Bowen Family Foundation Focuses on Healing Through Music

Horseshoe Bay, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Saturday Aug 9, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central â€” The Bowen Family Foundation, with its steadfast belief that Music Heals, is gearing up for its annual Bowen Invitational at the picturesque Horseshoe Bay Resort. The September 15th event, presented by Shiner Bock, is a testament to the foundation's unwavering commitment to supporting children and families in Central Texas who face adversity.

Driven by their mission of providing love, resources, and opportunities to those in need, the Bowen Family Foundation's team has dedicated itself to finding impactful ways to support and connect with the community, aiming to set them on a path to a brighter future.

The Bowen Invitational promises a day filled with excitement as it showcases a competitive golf tournament alongside incredible musical performances. The highlight of the event is the generosity of attendees at the lively live auction conducted by the renowned Cowboy Auctioneer team.

Seth Hale, Cowboy Auctioneer, expressed his enthusiasm for the long-standing partnership with the Bowen Family Foundation. "We have had the pleasure of partnering with the Bowen Family Foundation for the last five years in support of children and families in our community. We share the sentiment that music heals, and it's an honor to partner with Wade, his family, his incredibly talented friends, and so many generous supporters to have a great time making an impact. It's a standout event for an incredible mission!"

Guests attending the Bowen Invitational can look forward to a day of sportsmanship, camaraderie, and giving back, all while being surrounded by the beautiful landscapes of Horseshoe Bay. The event serves as a reminder of the transformative power of music and community support in effecting positive change in the lives of those in need.

For more information about the Bowen Family Foundation and its impactful initiatives, please visit https://www.bowenfamilyfoundation.org/.
