# Director’s Corner Unveils Ask Mort™ – A Revolutionary AI Assistant for End-of-Life Care Providers

Houston, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 23, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Central — Today, Director’s Corner announced the launch of Ask Mort™, a groundbreaking AI agent designed exclusively for funeral homes, crematories, cemeteries, hospice providers, and pet aftercare services. By automating routine inquiries and administrative tasks, Ask Mort™ empowers staff to dedicate more time to meaningful, compassionate care for grieving families.

Transforming Client Care with AIIn an industry where empathy and precision are essential, providers often struggle with repetitive questions and after-hours requests. Ask Mort™ addresses these challenges by delivering instant, accurate responses to common inquiries—such as "What are my cremation options?" or "What paperwork is needed for a burial?"—while maintaining a consistent, brand-aligned voice.

Key Features & Benefits:24/7 Automated Support – Instantly answers FAQs, reducing call volume and after-hours workload. Personalized Onboarding – Guides families through initial questions, gathering essential details before human interaction. Smooth Escalation – Transfers complex conversations to staff with full context. Custom-Trained for Accuracy – Learns directly from each provider’s website, pricing lists, and policies for precise, relevant responses. Effortless Price List Automation – Instantly sends requested documents via chat, eliminating manual follow-ups. Data-Driven Insights – Tracks top questions, sentiment trends, and staff efficiency with a comprehensive analytics dashboard.

"Ask Mort™ is more than a chatbot—it’s a conversational AI built for the nuances of funeral service," said Angela Human, Founding Partner of Director’s Corner. "By handling routine tasks, it allows directors and staff to focus on what truly matters: supporting families with empathy and care."About Director’s CornerA women-owned digital marketing agency specializing in end-of-life care, Director’s Corner combines deep industry expertise with cutting-edge tools—from AI-powered solutions to multimedia storytelling—helping providers modernize their outreach while honoring tradition.
