# Intellum and Cognizant Partner to Deliver Scalable, Outcome-Driven Learning Solutions

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 3, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading enterprise learning management solution, today announced a strategic partnership with Cognizant, a global professional services company renowned for its consulting and technology services. This collaboration aims to deliver customized learning experiences that drive measurable business outcomes across various industries.

Cognizant brings decades of experience in enterprise process, technology, and transformation consulting, helping organizations reimagine their operations and take practical steps to turn big visions into reality. Their expertise in digital transformation, AI, and data analytics complements Intellum’s robust learning platform, enabling organizations to scale engaging learning experiences across diverse audiences.

"We are thrilled to announce this strategic partnership," said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum. "Together, we can offer our clients a powerful combination of strategic consulting and scalable learning technology, ensuring that learning initiatives not only engage but also deliver measurable business impact."Through this partnership, clients can leverage Cognizant’s strategic consulting services alongside Intellum’s comprehensive learning platform, including personalized learning paths, advanced analytics, and seamless integrations. This synergy ensures that learning programs are not only tailored to the unique needs of each organization but also delivered efficiently and effectively at scale.
