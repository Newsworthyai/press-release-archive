# Church Army USA Announces 5th Annual Golf Outing at Beaver Valley Golf Club

Beaver Falls, Pennsylvania (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 3, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” The Church Army USA has announced the highly anticipated 5th Annual Golf Outing, in partnership with the Uncommon Grounds Cafe. The event is set to take place at the picturesque Beaver Valley Golf Club on Friday, September 5th, 2025.

According to a representative from Church Army USA, "We seek to do the work of serving, reaching and loving broken, rejected, sad, disconnected and hurting people with a message of hope, love, and joy. Every Church Army in the world lives out the calling of our founder, Wilson Carlile, to reach out to those outside of the church with the Good News of Jesus Christ, to serve the poor and to resource the wider church in evangelism."

Attendees are invited to join the event at Beaver Valley Golf Club, located at 725 Sixth Avenue, Patterson Heights, Beaver Falls, PA 15010. Registration will begin at 7:30 AM, with a shotgun start scheduled for 9:00 AM.

The 5th Annual Golf Outing promises a day filled with camaraderie, friendly competition, and most importantly, a chance to contribute to a worthy cause. Participants can look forward to enjoying a day of golf while supporting the mission of Church Army USA in spreading hope and love to those in need.

Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of a meaningful event that combines passion for golf with a commitment to making a positive impact in the community. Join Church Army USA at the 5th Annual Golf Outing for a day of fellowship and support for a noble cause.
