# HomeAid Austin to Host Inaugural Gala, "Unlock the Magic"

Austin, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 3, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” HomeAid Austin, a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing housing solutions for individuals and families experiencing homelessness in Central Texas, is proud to announce its inaugural fundraiser gala, "Unlock the Magic," to be held on October 17, 2025, at the Renaissance Hotel in Austin.

The gala will feature a reception hour with a magician, a silent auction, and mission moments that will tug at the heartstrings. Beyond entertainment, the event aims to bring about transformation, highlighting the importance of building new beginnings for those facing homelessness in the community.

Each year, HomeAid Austin constructs safe and dignified spaces to help individuals and families rebuild their lives with hope and stability. The funds raised at "Unlock the Magic" will directly support this crucial work, enabling the organization to respond to urgent needs and continue making a positive impact.

"HomeAid cannot do this work alone," a representative of Charity Ace, who will be assisting with the fundraising efforts, emphasized. "Your participation, whether through sponsorship, attending the event, bidding on auction items, or spreading the word, plays a vital role in unlocking futures for those in need."

For more information on how to be part of this impactful event and support HomeAid Austin's mission, visit the organization's website or contact them directly.
