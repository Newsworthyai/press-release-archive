# CASA Kendall County Shines Bright at the Gold Standard Gala 2025

Kendall County, Illinois (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 3, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — The 2025 CASA Kendall Gala, known as the Gold Standard Gala, is set to take place on October 11th from 5:00 pm to 11:00 pm. Guests are invited to an evening that promises to be more than just a gala - it's a night that changes lives.

"Every seat filled brings CASA Kendall County closer to giving a child in crisis the voice they deserve," says a representative from [Charity Ace](https://charityace.com/), who will be assisting in the fundraising. "You're invited to join for an unforgettable experience that directly impacts the children of Kendall County who need us most."The event will feature thrilling live and silent auctions, exciting raffles, and an exclusive plated dinner with VIP bottle service. Attendees will have the opportunity to step into the 360° video booth, capturing memories that truly matter.

The Gold Standard Gala aims to raise awareness and funds for CASA Kendall County, advocating for children in need. Guests are encouraged to reserve their tables today to secure their spot at this impactful event.

For more information on how you can make a difference and be a voice for a child, visit www.casakendallcounty.org or follow the hashtag #GoldStandardGala #CASAKendallCounty #AdvocateForAChild on social media.

Don't miss this opportunity to shine for the future and help make a lasting impact on the lives of children in Kendall County!
