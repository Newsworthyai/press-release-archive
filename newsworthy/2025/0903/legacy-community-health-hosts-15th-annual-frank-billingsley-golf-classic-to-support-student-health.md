# Legacy Community Health Hosts 15th Annual Frank Billingsley Golf Classic to Support Student Health

Sugar Land, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 3, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific â€” Legacy Community Health is excited to announce the 15th Annual Frank Billingsley Golf Classic, presented by Equitable, Hawkins Laakso Wealth Management, and Pinnacle Environmental Services. The event is set to take place on October 13, 2025, at the prestigious Sweetwater Country Club in Sugar Land.

The Golf Classic promises a day filled with camaraderie and goodwill, starting with a shotgun start at 9:00 a.m. Participants can look forward to a morning of golf, breakfast, exciting giveaways, mulligans, and a special luncheon reception.

"The gracious support from sponsors and participants helps to raise crucial funds to provide affordable primary, behavioral, and wellness care to students," said a representative of [Charity Ace](http://charityace.com), who will be assisting in the fundraising efforts.Various sponsorship opportunities are available for businesses looking to support student health while gaining exposure for their brands. For those who prefer not to golf, donating auction or raffle items is also encouraged to contribute to the success of the event.

All proceeds from the Frank Billingsley Golf Classic will go directly towards ensuring that students have access to essential healthcare services, enabling them to stay healthy, focused, and engaged in their education.

For more information on how to get involved or support this important cause, please contact Isabella Villarreal at Villarreal@legacycommunityhealth.org.

Legacy Community Health looks forward to a successful event that will make a lasting impact on the well-being of students in the community.
