# Never Miss a Customer: XealAI Unveils Always-On Voice AI Marketing Suite

Tulsa, OK. USA (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Jan 6, 2025 @ 7:00 AM America/Costa_Rica — XealAI, a pioneering provider of AI-powered solutions, has launched its advanced AI Marketing Suite, a unified platform that combines intelligent voice AI, chatbots, and comprehensive marketing automation. The platform enables businesses to maintain round-the-clock customer engagement while automating their entire marketing and sales operations through a single, powerful interface.

"Our AI Marketing Suite represents a breakthrough in business automation," says Tony Darrick Baker, CEO of XealAI. "We've created a fully integrated system where voice AI and chatbots work seamlessly with custom sales funnels, appointment scheduling, and automated marketing campaigns. This means businesses can engage customers 24/7/365, automatically nurture leads, and drive conversions without manual intervention."The platform offers a comprehensive suite of features, including unified omnichannel communication, customizable sales funnels, intelligent appointment scheduling, and advanced CRM capabilities. Businesses can create high-converting landing pages, automate email and SMS campaigns, manage their online reputation, and track performance through detailed analytics—all from a single dashboard.

XealAI serves diverse sectors, including attorneys, siding contractors, flooring specialists, roofing companies, and dental practices. The platform's AI-powered automation handles everything from initial customer contact to appointment scheduling and follow-up nurturing campaigns, seamlessly integrating with remarketing efforts across all channels.

"What sets our platform apart is its ability to create a fully branded experience while automating every aspect of customer interaction," adds Baker. "Whether it's through voice AI, chatbots, email, or SMS, businesses can maintain consistent communication with their customers while significantly reducing manual tasks. This level of automation and integration is transforming how businesses operate and grow."To schedule an appointment to discover XealAI and its AI Marketing Suite, visit www.xealai.com.
