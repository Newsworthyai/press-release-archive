# Rhug Wild Beauty Showcased on National Television Program Coffee with America

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Oct 16, 2025 @ 11:30 AM Pacific — Rhug Wild Beauty, the award-winning luxury organic skincare brand from the iconic Rhug Estate in North Wales, was recently featured on the nationally syndicated television show Coffee with America. The segment, which aired in September 2025, introduced millions of U.S. viewers to Rhug Wild Beauty’s uniquely sustainable approach to skincare — “rooted in nature” and crafted from wild-foraged and carefully selected natural ingredients sourced for their proven skin benefits.

Broadcast to more than 200 million households across the United States, Coffee with America spotlights the latest in lifestyle, wellness, and consumer trends. During the segment, Rhug Wild Beauty captivated audiences with its story of how organic farming, sustainability, and scientific excellence can harmoniously coexist to create highly efficacious, results-driven skincare.

“Being featured on Coffee with America was a wonderful opportunity to share our philosophy with U.S. consumers who value authenticity, transparency, and sustainability,” said Lord Robert Newborough, founder of Rhug Wild Beauty and a pioneer in organic farming. “Our products are created with both wild-foraged botanicals from our estate and other exceptional natural actives chosen for their efficacy, ensuring each formula delivers visible results while maintaining purity and integrity.”The brand’s appearance coincides with its continued expansion in the United States, where Rhug Wild Beauty is now available on Amazon. American shoppers can now easily experience the brand’s acclaimed collection, including its cleansing balm, moisturizer, skin tonic, and serum — each infused with a potent blend of estate-grown botanicals, nutrient-rich plant actives, and mineral-charged spring water.

Certified COSMOS Organic, vegan, cruelty-free, and halal, Rhug Wild Beauty combines the power of nature with modern science to deliver clinically backed performance with organic purity. Every formulation is rigorously tested across all ages and skin types to restore, protect, and enhance skin vitality. Since launching in 2020, the brand has earned praise from Vogue, Tatler, and Country & Town House for redefining luxury clean beauty.

To learn more, visit www.rhugwildbeauty.com or shop Rhug Wild Beauty directly on Amazon. Follow Rhug Wild Beauty on Instagram and Facebook for updates, tips, and behind-the-scenes insights from the Rhug Estate.

About [Rhug Wild Beauty](https://rhug.co.uk/)Rhug Wild Beauty is a luxury organic skincare brand rooted in the wild landscapes of North Wales. Each formula is crafted using a thoughtful combination of wild-foraged botanicals from the Rhug Estate and meticulously selected natural actives sourced globally for their proven efficacy. Hand-crafted in the UK, the brand’s COSMOS-certified products are vegan, cruelty-free, and halal. Founded by Lord Robert Newborough, Rhug Wild Beauty embodies clean beauty that is ethical, sustainable, and scientifically proven to deliver visible results.
