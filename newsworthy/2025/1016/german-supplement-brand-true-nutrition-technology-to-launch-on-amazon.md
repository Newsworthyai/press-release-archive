# German Supplement Brand True Nutrition Technology to Launch on Amazon

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Oct 16, 2025 @ 6:00 AM Pacific — True Nutrition Technology (TNT), one of Germany’s leading sports nutrition brands, announced today that its premium line of evidence-based supplements will soon be available to U.S. consumers on Amazon. The move marks a major step in TNT’s expansion beyond Europe and reinforces its commitment to bringing German-engineered quality and scientific transparency to athletes and health-minded individuals worldwide.

Founded by former professional athletes Michael Weigl and Markus Himmelstoss, TNT was created out of a passion for performance and integrity. What began as a single sports supplement shop in Regensburg has evolved into a trusted global brand known for formulating products that meet the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and transparency.

“At True Nutrition Technology, we only bring products to market that we can fully stand behind,” said Michael Weigl, CEO and co-founder of TNT. “Every ingredient, every label, and every test reflects our belief that supplements should deliver exactly what they promise—and nothing less.”German-Made Quality Meets Global ReachAll TNT products are manufactured and packaged in Germany under HACCP, IFS, and TÜVNORD quality standards and are tested by independent laboratories for purity, nutritional accuracy, and microbiological safety. Certificates verifying these analyses are made available to the public for complete transparency.

The company’s product lineup includes performance-driven innovations such as Creatine Monohydrate Creapure®, widely regarded as the world’s purest creatine; Micronutrients, a multivitamin powder combining 24 essential vitamins and minerals with natural boosters like green tea extract, spirulina, and CoQ10; and Vegan Basics, a comprehensive vegan supplement that also includes creatine for energy support.

TNT’s upcoming presence on Amazon will make it easier for U.S. consumers to experience the same high-quality formulations trusted by athletes and fitness enthusiasts across Europe.

“Bringing TNT to Amazon is an exciting milestone in our journey to introduce our German-crafted supplements to a broader audience,” added co-founder Markus Himmelstoss. “We want every customer—from serious athletes to everyday individuals—to experience the difference that proven quality and honest formulation can make.”About [TNT (True Nutrition Technology)](https://www.tnt-supplements.de/)Founded in Germany by former athletes Michael Weigl and Markus Himmelstoss, TNT (True Nutrition Technology) is a leading sports nutrition brand trusted by more than 400,000 customers. Since 2012, TNT has produced premium supplements under strict German quality standards, with every product independently lab-tested for safety, purity, and transparency. From advanced protein blends to innovative vegan formulations, TNT develops evidence-based nutrition designed to help athletes and everyday individuals perform at their best. More than just supplements, TNT represents a passion for sport, health, and performance. Built on German precision and a culture of honesty, TNT continues to expand globally with one mission: delivering the highest quality nutrition—for us, and for you.
