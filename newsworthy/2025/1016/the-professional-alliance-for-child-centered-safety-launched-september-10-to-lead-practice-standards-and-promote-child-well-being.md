# The Professional Alliance for Child Centered Safety Launched September 10 to Lead Practice Standards and Promote Child Well-Being

Hershey, PA (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Oct 16, 2025 @ 5:00 AM Pacific â€” The Professional Alliance for Child Centered Safety (PACCS) officially launched on September 10, 2025, as a practitioner-led network dedicated to advancing education, training and unified practice standards that prioritize the emotional, psychological and physical well-being of children. Built to serve clinicians, attorneys, evaluators, researchers and advocates, PACCS offers members exclusive resources, ongoing education and vital opportunities for collaboration, all grounded in child-first principles and evidence-based standards.

"Now, almost 50 years into private practice and thousands of hours of sworn testimony in high-conflict custody disputes, I have inspired and led like-minded advocates in turning fulfillment of my vision for advocacy for, prevention of and healing of traumatized children and their dysfunctional family systems into a shared mission represented as the Professional Alliance for Child Centered Safety." said Steven P. Lindenberg, Ph.D., founder of PACCS.

Maximize Your Practice with PACCS Membership BenefitsPACCS provides members with a comprehensive suite of tools and resources designed to support ethical, trauma-informed practice, including:

* Access to a curated research library on assessment, coercive control, psychological harm, legislation and best-practice interventions
* Exclusive attendance at practitioner-focused trainings, case rounds, panel discussions, continued education certifications and roundtables
* A professional directory to connect with local experts and peers nationwide
* Weekly Lunch & Learn with Lynn Brooks LMFT
* Monthly Reunification Seminar Series with Linda Gottlieb, LMFT & LCSW-R and Loretta Maase, LPC
* The new PACCS Membership Circle App, a digital platform for connecting, resource sharing, and staying updated on practice standards and reform initiatives

Join as a Founding Member or New MemberPACCS is actively seeking founding members to help shape the future of child-first standards in family court practices. Early members will receive priority access to trainings, research insights and community leadership opportunities. The alliance also welcomes new members committed to ethical reform and collaborative practice.

Visit paccs.global to join the founding cohort or register as a new member. Be part of a trusted network dedicated to improving outcomes for children and families through innovative practice and reform.

About [PACCS](https://www.paccs.global/about-paccs)The Professional Alliance for Child Centered Safety is a 501 (c) (3) non-profit practitioner-led network dedicated to protecting children and promoting family healing through evidence-based standards grounded in child-first principles. Established by experts with lived experience and scientific expertise, PACCS aims to foster collaboration, elevate practice, and drive reform across family court systems nationwide.
