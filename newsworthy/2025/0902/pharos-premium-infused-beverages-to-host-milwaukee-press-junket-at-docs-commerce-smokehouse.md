# Pharos Premium Infused Beverages to Host Milwaukee Press Junket at Doc’s Commerce Smokehouse

Milwaukee, WI (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Sep 2, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — Pharos Premium Infused Beverages will host a historic press junket on Monday, September 8, 2025, at Doc’s Commerce Smokehouse (754 N 4th Street, Milwaukee, WI 53203), marking the debut of Wisconsin’s first THC-infused pint on tap.

Event Schedule4:00–5:00 PM | Press & Media Hour – Private space for interviews, media sampling, and Q&A with the Pharos founders and distributor.

5:00–7:00 PM | Public Open Bar – One complimentary Pharos pint on tap (5 mg THC per pint) for guests and unlimited pours for press.

Who to Meet:Mary Bernuth, Co-Founder & President, visionary behind Pharos’s mission of mindful cannabis consumption.

Rhyno Stinchfield, Co-Founder & CEO, bringing operational insight and brand vision.

Jerry Zavorka, President of Capitol Husting Distributor, the partner helping to bring THC beverages to taps across Wisconsin.

“As a Wisconsin distributor, I’m proud to bring the first THC drink on tap to our local scene. This is the future of beverages,” said Zavorka, as featured in Forbes.Press Guests Will Receive:Complimentary pours of Pharos Blood Orange Lime (5 mg THC per pint)

Food provided for media by Doc’s Commerce Smokehouse

Exclusive swag bags with Pharos-branded shirts, stickers, and more

Access to one-on-one interviews with the founders and distributor

“This is a huge milestone for the Midwest beverage scene,” said Bernuth. “We’re reshaping how people experience cannabis: safe, social, and innovative.”About Pharos BrandsPharos Brands is a premium infused beverage company offering a line of 5 mg and 10 mg THC sparkling drinks inspired by the ancient Pharos lighthouse of Alexandria—a symbol of guidance and clarity. The company focuses on flavor, quality, and transparency, helping consumers across the country explore and experience cannabis safely and enjoyably.
