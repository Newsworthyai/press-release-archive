# HR.com Forms New Employee Productivity and Engagement Advisory Board to Provide Insights for 2025 Research and Events

Jacksons Point, Ontario, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Feb 3, 2025 @ 8:00 AM America/Toronto — HR.com, the largest global network of HR professionals, is proud to announce the formation of its new Employee Productivity and Engagement Advisory Board. Continuing to grow with new members, this expert panel will provide invaluable insights and guidance for HR.com’s upcoming research initiatives, webcasts, and events dedicated to driving workplace productivity and engagement in 2025 and beyond.

The advisory board, composed of thought leaders and industry experts, will focus on uncovering innovative strategies to help organizations enhance employee performance, foster engagement, and create thriving workplace cultures. The board's collaboration will inform HR.com’s efforts to equip HR professionals with actionable insights and best practices through its programs and resources.

The research findings from this initiative will be published in a comprehensive report offering detailed insights and recommendations from the HR Research Institute. Additionally, a condensed two-page infographic will provide a quick reference for key points. The next upcoming virtual event will include two days of informative sessions from a variety of subject matter experts in this area.

The newly appointed State of Employee Productivity and Engagement 2025 Advisory Board includes the following industry thought leaders:

* Bridgett Hart, Vice President, Employee Experience, Ibex
* Kristen Kruttgaizer, Chief People Officer, Igloo Software
* Cassandra Lowery, Global Human Resources Manager, Liminal

“Productivity and engagement remain at the core of every successful organization,” said Debbie McGrath, CEO of HR.com. “These experts will help us uncover fresh insights and solutions to these critical challenges. Together, we aim to empower HR professionals with the tools and knowledge they need to create impactful change.”

The HR Research Institute releases annual “State of the Industry” reports in a variety of HR topic areas, including this past research report, HR.com’s State of Employee Productivity and Engagement 2024, on the same topic.

This and other free research reports and infographics can be downloaded at hr.com/researchinstitute.

About HR.com and the HR Research Institute

The HR Research Institute (HRRI) helps you keep your finger on the pulse of HR! Powered by HR.com, the world’s largest HR community, the HRRI identifies key trends and best practices to help more than 2 million HR professionals and their organizations make strategic decisions with informed and insightful research findings. Among the most productive and respected HR research institutes globally, the HRRI publishes dozens of high-quality reports every year across a wide array of HR topics. HR.com’s free membership offers many benefits, including access to over 250 exclusive primary research, state-of-the-industry reports, and infographics. These resources are published based on surveys developed with the assistance of a panel of thought leaders and industry experts on the advisory boards.
