# Rhug Wild Beauty Featured on National TV Show American Trends &amp; Lifestyle

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Nov 13, 2025 @ 6:30 AM Pacific — Rhug Wild Beauty, the award-winning luxury organic skincare brand from the historic Rhug Estate in North Wales, was recently featured on the nationally syndicated television program American Trends & Lifestyle. The segment spotlighted Rhug Wild Beauty as a leader in the clean beauty movement—celebrating the brand’s commitment to sustainability, transparency, and proven efficacy.

 Reaching millions of households across the United States, American Trends & Lifestyle delivers the latest in fashion, beauty, wellness, and consumer innovations. The program’s feature on Rhug Wild Beauty introduced viewers to the brand’s holistic philosophy and its rare blend of organic purity and scientific performance.

 During the segment, American Trends & Lifestyle highlighted Rhug Wild Beauty’s origins on the pristine 12,000-acre Rhug Estate in North Wales, where every product is handcrafted in the UK using certified organic and natural ingredients. Formulations include wild-foraged botanicals, estate spring water, honey, and beeswax—all combined to deliver visible, measurable results through nature’s finest elements.

 The show also showcased several of the brand’s standout products, including the Deep Cleansing Balm with Wild Berries, Nourishing Eye Cream with Elderflower, and Active Treatment Serum with Hyaluronic Acid—each designed to hydrate, protect, and revitalize skin with clinically proven performance.

 “Being featured on American Trends & Lifestyle was a wonderful opportunity to share our story with viewers who appreciate both luxury and authenticity,” said Lord Robert Newborough, Founder of Rhug Wild Beauty. “Our mission has always been to combine nature with science to create clean, sustainable skincare that truly delivers results.”

 Available in the U.S. on Amazon and OneLavi.com, Rhug Wild Beauty’s collection is COSMOS Organic certified, vegan, cruelty-free, and halal, ensuring transparency and integrity at every step. Each product embodies the brand’s guiding principle — “Rooted in Nature, Refined by Science.”

 In addition to its television reach, American Trends & Lifestyle extends its audience through BEOND TV, a dedicated streaming channel offering daily updates on beauty and lifestyle innovations.

 About Rhug Wild Beauty

 Rhug Wild Beauty is a luxury organic skincare brand rooted in the wild landscapes of North Wales. Each formula is crafted using a thoughtful combination of wild-foraged botanicals from the Rhug Estate and meticulously selected natural actives sourced globally for their proven efficacy. Hand-crafted in the UK, the brand’s COSMOS-certified products are vegan, cruelty-free, and halal. Founded by Lord Robert Newborough, Rhug Wild Beauty embodies clean beauty that is ethical, sustainable, and scientifically proven to deliver visible results.
