# Intellum and Mint Partner to Deliver Bespoke Digital Learning Solutions at Scale

Atlanta, GA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 2, 2025 @ 7:00 AM US/Eastern — Intellum, the leading provider of scalable learning management solutions, today announced a strategic partnership with Mint, a UK-based digital learning agency specializing in bespoke eLearning, video, and LMS solutions. This collaboration empowers organizations to deliver personalized, engaging learning experiences that drive meaningful business outcomes.

Mint brings over two decades of experience in crafting custom digital learning solutions, including eLearning modules, animated videos, and accessible content, largely leveraging Intellum’s content authoring tool, Evolve. Their expertise in blending creative storytelling with instructional design complements Intellum’s robust platform capabilities, enabling organizations to scale engaging learning experiences across diverse audiences.

"Mint’s commitment to creating engaging and accessible learning content aligns perfectly with our mission to turn learning into growth," said Dan Hynes, VP of Partnerships at Intellum. "Together, we can offer our clients a powerful combination of strategic content design and scalable delivery, ensuring that learning initiatives not only engage but also deliver measurable business impact."Through this partnership, clients can leverage Mint’s award-winning content development services alongside Intellum’s comprehensive learning platform, which offers personalized learning paths, advanced analytics, and seamless integrations. This synergy ensures that learning programs are not only tailored to the unique needs of each organization but also delivered efficiently and effectively at scale.

“Intellum’s powerful learning platform paired with their commitment to excellence and bold vision for scalable, impactful education makes this partnership a natural fit for Mint. By partnering their technology with our creative and strategic approach to digital learning, we’re further enabling organisations to deliver deeply personalised experiences at scale,” said Wesley Atkinson, Chief Technology Officer at Mint. About IntellumIntellum is the leading learning platform designed to drive business outcomes through scalable education for customers, partners, and employees. Intellum helps companies turn learning into growth with a captivating learner experience, effortless administration, and unmatched data insights. Learn more at www.intellum.com.

About MintMint is a UK-based digital learning agency specializing in bespoke eLearning, video, and LMS solutions. With a focus on creating engaging and accessible learning experiences, Mint serves clients across various sectors, including retail, healthcare, technology, and finance. Their award-winning solutions combine creative storytelling with instructional design to drive meaningful change. Learn more at www.mint-interactive.com.
