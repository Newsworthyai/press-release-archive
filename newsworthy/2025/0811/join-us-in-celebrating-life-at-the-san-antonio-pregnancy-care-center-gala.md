# Join Us in Celebrating Life at the San Antonio Pregnancy Care Center Gala

San Antonio, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Aug 11, 2025 @ 9:00 AM Pacific â€” The San Antonio Pregnancy Care Center, an affiliate of Heartbeat International, is on a mission to provide a Christ-centered plan for those facing unplanned pregnancies with their upcoming "Celebrating Life Gala." The event will take place on October 28th at the Witte Museum in San Antonio and will be aimed at raising awareness and funds to support their vital work in the community

"We at the San Antonio Pregnancy Care Center are dedicated to offering compassionate support and practical resources to individuals navigating unplanned pregnancies," says a representative of the center. "Our goal is to empower individuals with the information and care they need to make informed decisions."The center offers a range of services, including pregnancy testing, ultrasounds, parenting classes, and material assistance. Additionally, they provide emotional support and guidance to help individuals facing unexpected pregnancy situations.

"We are committed to ensuring that everyone who walks through our doors feels valued, respected, and supported," the representative adds. "Our team of dedicated staff and volunteers strives to create a safe and welcoming environment for all individuals seeking our assistance."For more information on how to get involved, donate, or volunteer with the San Antonio Pregnancy Care Center, visit their website or contact them directly at (210) 614-5433. Join them in making a difference in the lives of individuals facing unplanned pregnancies.
