# German Sports Nutrition Brand True Nutrition Technology Expands U.S. Presence with Upcoming Launch on OneLavi.com

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Nov 4, 2025 @ 11:20 AM Pacific — True Nutrition Technology (TNT), one of Germany’s most trusted names in sports nutrition, announced today that its products will soon be available on OneLavi.com, a specialty health, wellness, and lifestyle platform that features high-quality, science-backed brands. The upcoming launch follows TNT’s growing U.S. retail expansion, which includes availability on Amazon and an upcoming Walmart.com debut.

 Founded in Germany by former professional athletes Michael Weigl and Markus Himmelstoss, TNT is known for its evidence-based formulations and uncompromising quality. Each product is manufactured and packaged in Germany under HACCP, IFS, and TÜVNORD quality standards and verified through independent laboratory testing to ensure purity, safety, and accurate nutritional content.

 “Our upcoming launch on OneLavi.com is another step forward in making TNT’s proven, German-engineered supplements accessible to American consumers,” said Markus Himmelstoss, CEO and co-founder of TNT. “We’re excited to align with a retailer that shares our focus on health, transparency, and performance.”

 As TNT continues to grow internationally, the brand remains committed to its founding mission: to create supplements backed by science and verified by transparency. With its expansion into the U.S. market, TNT aims to bring the precision and quality of German manufacturing to a broader audience of athletes and wellness-focused consumers seeking products they can truly trust.

 TNT’s growing U.S. presence reflects its global reputation for integrity and innovation in sports nutrition. The company’s portfolio includes bestsellers such as Creatine Monohydrate Creapure®, Micronutrients, and Vegan Basics—formulas trusted by athletes and health-conscious individuals seeking measurable results.

 About [TNT (True Nutrition Technology)](https://www.tnt-supplements.de/) Founded in Germany by former athletes Michael Weigl and Markus Himmelstoss, TNT (True Nutrition Technology) is a leading sports nutrition brand trusted by more than 400,000 customers. More than just supplements, TNT represents a passion for sport, health, and performance. Built on German precision and a culture of honesty, TNT continues to expand globally with one mission: delivering the highest quality nutrition—for us, and for you.
