# The Lapsed Fan 10th Anniversary Podcast Wrestling Comedy Show.

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Jul 31, 2025 @ 3:00 PM Eastern — The Lapsed Fan Podcast, known for its deep dives into pro wrestling's history infused with humor, is set to celebrate its 10th anniversary with a special live event during WWE SummerSlam weekend. Hosted by two best friends, one an investigative journalist and the other an actor, The Lapsed Fan has garnered acclaim for its unique blend of rigorous research and audacious humor.

"We are thrilled to mark this milestone by bringing our brand of romp and revelation to The Comedy Village," said one of the co-chairmen of The Lapsed Fan Podcast.The event promises to offer fans an afternoon filled with insider insights and exclusive revelations about professional wrestling. Attendees will have the opportunity to interact with the podcast's characters, including the enigmatic "Lapsed Vince."

"We are excited to share our passion for wrestling and nostalgia with our dedicated listeners in a live setting," added the other co-chairman.The Lapsed Fan Podcast has been recognized for its excellence in the podcasting industry, winning the Sports Podcast Group’s Best Wrestling Podcast award in 2025. With a focus on wrestling's most memorable moments, characters, and events, The Lapsed Fan has established itself as a go-to source for both fans and enthusiasts of the sport.

Don't miss this one-of-a-kind experience with The Lapsed Fan Podcast at The Comedy Village during WWE SummerSlam weekend. Get ready to laugh, learn, and immerse yourself in the world of professional wrestling like never before.

For more information and tickets, visit The Lapsed Fan Podcast's official website.
