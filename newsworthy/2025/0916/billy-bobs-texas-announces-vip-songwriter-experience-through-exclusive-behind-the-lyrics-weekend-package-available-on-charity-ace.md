# Billy Bob’s Texas Announces VIP Songwriter Experience Through Exclusive "Behind the Lyrics Weekend" Package Available on Charity Ace

Fort Worth, Texas (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Sep 16, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Central — Billy Bob’s Texas, “The World’s Largest Honky Tonk” located in the Fort Worth Stockyards, is giving country music fans and songwriters an extraordinary chance to hear the stories behind the music directly from the musicians that write the songs. The Behind the Lyrics Weekend is a VIP experience available through Charity Ace's exclusive consignment package. The two-day package is available as part of Billy Bob's Legendary 81 Club’s Behind the Lyrics Songwriter’s Round event coming up this October 17-18.

“This is an incredible opportunity for fans of songwriting to experience and interact with the best in the world, and then have a VIP concert experience like no other on Saturday night!" says Justin McKenzie, COO of Charity Ace.

[VIP Songwriter Experience includes](https://charityace.com/package/behind-the-lyrics-weekend):Friday night guests will attend a Nashville-style writer’s round featuring songwriting hitmakers Josh Phillips, Brock Berryhill and Andy Albert, the writers behind chart-topping songs for Jason Aldean, Luke Combs, Kane Brown, and more. Throughout the evening enjoy Texas BBQ bites, an open bar and powerful performances from Operation Encore veteran songwriters.

Saturday morning guests will be treated to an exclusive VIP Songwriting Brunch, where guests will learn the craft of songwriting, collaborate on an original song and even earn an official songwriting credit. In the afternoon guests will go on a private VIP tour of Billy Bob’s Texas, have a chance for shopping at the Stockyards’ premier western boutiques, fine dining and a private evening concert with a featured artist to be announced soon.

About [Billy Bob’s Texas](https://billybobstexas.com/):Billy Bob’s Texas opened April 1, 1981 with national attention featuring with it’s 100,000 square feet entertainment center, more than 30 bar stations, country music’s biggest stars, real Pro Bull Riding, and a Texas size dance floor. It became known as “The World’s Largest Honky Tonk”, where more than 6,000 fun loving folks of all ages could enjoy entertainment every day and night.

About [Charity Ace](https://charityace.com/): Charity Ace is a leading provider of innovative fundraising solutions, including unique consignment travel packages and cutting-edge marketing services for nonprofits. With a mission to empower charities to amplify their voice and maximize fundraising success, Charity Ace delivers risk-free, results-driven tools that create meaningful impact.
