# Wooten Center Hosts Inaugural John Lapham Golf Classic Supporting LA Youth Education Programs

Los Angeles, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Nov 7, 2025 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — The Al Wooten Jr. Youth Center, AKA the Wooten Center, an organization offering after-school programs for youth in Los Angeles, proudly announces its first-ever John Lapham Golf Classic, honoring the legacy of its long-time board member and chairperson, John G. Lapham III. The fundraising event, formerly known as the Wooten Golf Classic, has been renamed this year to celebrate John’s contributions and to continue its mission of supporting youth college and career readiness. The inaugural John Lapham Golf Classic will take place on Monday, November 17, at the Porter Valley Country Club in Northridge, California.

 "This tournament brings together the heart of our community to honor John’s legacy and invest in our youth. His commitment to education and opportunity continues to guide our work every day," said Corey Dantzler, Executive Director, Al Wooten Jr. Youth Center.

 Event DetailsThe event supports vital college and career readiness programs serving thousands of youth at the Wooten Center. Attendees will enjoy a full day of golf, a putting contest, lunch on the course and a silent auction with an awards dinner beginning at 5 p.m.

 Registration and SponsorshipSponsorship opportunities are available at several levels, which include signage and foursomes. To participate, sponsors can register via the online RSVP form or review sponsorship details at the event website.

 Sponsor Levels

 * Sapphire: $50,000 (4 foursomes)
* Diamond: $25,000 (4 foursomes)
* Platinum: $10,000 (4 foursomes)
* Gold: $5,000 (3 foursomes)
* Silver: $2,500 (2 foursomes)
* Bronze: $1,500 (1 foursome)

 Additional contest entries and individual tickets are also available.

 About the [Al Wooten Jr. Youth Center](https://www.wootencenter.org/) The Wooten Center, also known as the Al Wooten Jr. Youth Center, has a long-standing history of providing a safe and nurturing environment for youth in Los Angeles. It offers after-school programs focused on college and career readiness, empowering youth to succeed academically and personally. This annual event continues to serve as a vital platform for building hope, opportunity and a brighter future for thousands of students in our community.
