# Innovative New Podcast Series Launches: CODE Conscious Conversations

Tampa, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Aug 1, 2024 @ 8:00 AM Eastern — CODE Health is proud to announce the launch of its groundbreaking new podcast series, "CODE Conscious Conversations" (C3). Hosted by renowned entrepreneurs and health visionaries, Dr. Lisa Piper and Wendy Cohn-Osborne, this weekly series delves into the realms of conscious living, personal growth, technology, and holistic wellness.

Dr. Lisa Piper and Wendy Cohn-Osborne, co-founders of CODE Health, bring their expertise and passion for transformative health solutions to the podcasting world. Each episode will spotlight the latest trends and breakthroughs in health and technology, featuring insights from leaders and practitioners in biohacking, holistic health, and wellness.

"With CODE Conscious Conversations, we aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge therapeutic solutions and everyday well-being," said Dr. Lisa Piper. "Our goal is to inspire listeners to embrace a more conscious and purposeful life."

Wendy Cohn-Osborne added, "We're excited to create a platform where people can explore holistic wellness and personal growth. This podcast is for anyone who wants to stay informed about the latest in health innovation while also nurturing their own well-being."

Listeners can expect engaging discussions, expert advice, and innovative perspectives that cater to both seasoned professionals and curious individuals alike. Whether you're seeking the latest in therapeutic advancements or exploring holistic well-being, C3 promises to inform, inspire, and ignite a passion for a healthier future.

Subscribe now to join Dr. Lisa Piper and Wendy Cohn-Osborne on a journey towards conscious living. Tune in to CODE Conscious Conversations on Apple Podcasts, Spotify or wherever you like to listen to podcasts, and be part of a community dedicated to health, wellness, and personal growth.
