# HR.com Celebrates 25 Years as the Leading Online Community for HR Professionals

Jacksons Point, Ontario, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Sep 9, 2024 @ 8:00 AM America/Toronto — HR.com, the largest online community of human resources (HR) professionals, is celebrating its 25th anniversary. Over the past quarter-century, HR.com has become an indispensable hub for HR professionals seeking to enhance their knowledge, skills, and careers – establishing itself as the premier go-to resource site for HR credit webcasts, tools, certification preparation, research, magazine articles, and compliance updates.

Founded by CEO Debbie McGrath in 1999, HR.com has grown into a vibrant and diverse community, offering unparalleled access to a vast array of HR-related resources. Trusted by over 2 million HR professionals, HR.com continues to lead the industry by providing the most comprehensive and up-to-date information on HR practices, policies, and trends.

Key Accomplishments:

* HR Content Library: HR.com has built an extensive library with thousands of articles, white papers, webcasts with HR certification credits, and videos covering every aspect of human resources management, from recruitment and employee engagement to compliance and legal issues.
* Certification Preparation: As the trusted resource for HR certification preparation, HR.com has helped thousands of HR practitioners achieve certifications like SHRM-CP, SHRM-SCP, PHR, and SPHR. The program provides the support, study materials, practice exams, expert-led courses, and a money-back guarantee to help HR professionals.
* Cutting-Edge Research: Through the HR Research Institute (HRRI), HR.com has delivered over 250 in-depth research reports and infographics on the latest HR trends, challenges, and best practices, helping professionals stay ahead of industry developments.
* Compliance Updates: HR.com ensures that members stay informed about the latest legal and regulatory changes, providing timely updates and analysis on employment laws, workplace safety, and other compliance issues.
* Networking and Community Engagement: With credit webcasts, virtual events, and networking opportunities, HR.com fosters a collaborative environment where HR professionals can connect, share insights, and support each other in their careers.
* Annual In-Person HR Conference: This event has evolved over time to become today’s version, HRWest, held each year in Northern California's Bay Area. It has grown into a highly anticipated event, attracting HR practitioners from across the nation to meet, learn, and explore the latest trends, strategies, and technology in human resources.

“We are incredibly proud to celebrate 25 years of serving the HR community,” said Debbie McGrath, Chief Instigator and CEO of HR.com. “Our mission has always been to empower HR professionals with the resources and knowledge they need to excel in their roles and to support them as they navigate the ever-changing HR landscape.”

As HR.com enters its next chapter, the platform will continue to innovate and expand its offerings to meet the evolving needs of HR professionals. Whether through new digital tools, advanced learning opportunities and certification support, or cutting-edge research, HR.com is dedicated to being the ultimate resource for HR excellence.
