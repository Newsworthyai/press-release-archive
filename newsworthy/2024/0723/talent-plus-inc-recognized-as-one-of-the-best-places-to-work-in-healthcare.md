# Talent Plus, Inc. Recognized As One of the Best Places to Work in Healthcare

Lincoln, Nebraska (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Jul 23, 2024 @ 6:00 AM Pacific — Talent Plus, Inc., a leading global consulting firm headquartered in Lincoln, has been selected by Modern Healthcare as one of the 2024 Best Places to Work in Healthcare.

“With all the economic uncertainty, staffing challenges and folks wanting more flexible work setups, every company has to get clever about how they attract and hold onto talent,” noted Dan Peres, President of Modern Healthcare. "The winners of the 2024 Best Places to Work awards really get it. They show us how vital it is to understand what employees want and need. . . finding the right people is a top priority, and the winners know that keeping their team happy is the key to business success.”

This recognition reflects Talent Plus’ longstanding commitment to a diverse, equitable, inclusive, belonging, caring and respectful workplace. Its comprehensive benefits empower colleagues to be self-directed in their work-life balance and well-being.

Talent Plus President Makenzie Rath offers, “It’s about layering intention around values, selection and onboarding, investing and empowering, and celebrating wins. Many practices in place (e.g. increased transparency, honor vacation days, investment solutions) we’ve learned along the way by listening. Being a Best Place to Work is not a destination but a journey. We listen, refine and grow in the culture we have and what feels rewarding to colleagues.”

Modern Healthcare’s award program identifies and recognizes outstanding employers in the healthcare industry nationwide.
