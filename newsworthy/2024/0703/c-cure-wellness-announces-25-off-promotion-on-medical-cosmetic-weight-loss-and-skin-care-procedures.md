# C-Cure Wellness Announces $25 Off Promotion on Medical Cosmetic, Weight Loss, and Skin Care Procedures.

New City, New York (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jul 3, 2024 @ 12:00 PM Eastern — C-Cure Wellness, a premier medical spa renowned for its holistic approach to skin health, is excited to announce a special promotion for new clients. From now until the end of July, new clients can enjoy $25 off any service, including medical, cosmetic, weight loss, and skin care treatments.

Located at 60 South Main Street, Suite 3, New City, NY 10956, C-Cure Wellness is dedicated to promoting healthy skin from the inside out. The spa’s team of certified nurses and experienced medical professionals are committed to providing thorough medical assessments and personalized treatments, ensuring every visit is a comprehensive and outstanding experience. The health and vitality of clients’ skin are top priorities at C-Cure Wellness.

“Our mission is to offer exceptional care and expert advice, creating a tranquil and intimate setting where personalized care and a comprehensive approach to wellness are at the forefront,” said Dr. Lesia Christen, DNP, FNP-BC, a board-certified family nurse practitioner and primary care provider at C-Cure Wellness. “Unlike larger spas, we integrate traditional spa services with alternative therapies to emphasize overall health and well-being. Our smaller scale allows for more individualized attention, fostering strong client-practitioner relationships and enhancing the therapeutic experience.”

Dr. Lesia Christen is a skilled clinician who utilizes evidence-based philosophies in her approach to preventative health, chronic disease management, and medical aesthetics. She incorporates complementary and alternative modalities to meet each client’s unique wellness and aesthetic needs.

To take advantage of this limited-time offer, book an appointment by visiting www.ccurewellness.com or contact C-Cure Wellness directly at info@ccurewellness.com or 845-729-6965.
