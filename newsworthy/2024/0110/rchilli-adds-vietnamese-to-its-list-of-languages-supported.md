# RChilli Adds Vietnamese to its List of Languages Supported

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jan 10, 2024 @ 6:00 AM Central — RChilli proudly announces the addition of Vietnamese to its supported languages, furthering its commitment to providing diverse linguistic solutions. As part of its mission to help companies hire the right talent, RChilli aims to empower hiring managers in their talent acquisition efforts by providing them with a broader candidate pool within Vietnamese-speaking regions. Building on the success of incorporating 40+ languages, RChilli's advanced parsing technology now empowers businesses in Vietnam and those engaging with Vietnamese-speaking candidates.

This integration is backed by best-in-class technology in the system to understand Vietnamese intricacies. This ensures the accurate extraction of vital candidate details, such as contact information, educational background, skills, work experience, etc. This new inclusion allows hiring managers to expand their candidate search in Vietnamese-speaking regions.

Apart from Vietnamese, RChilli provides full parsing in English, French, German, Spanish, Polish, Turkish, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Chinese(Simplified), Japanese, Russian, Arabic (Right to Left), Indonesian, Danish, Swedish, Romanian, Korean, Hebrew, Thai, Hungarian, Ukrainian, and Norwegian. The company also provides parsing in an additional 15+ languages.

Inder Pal Singh, Sr. Director-Development at RChilli, shared, "Adding Vietnamese to our supported languages is a big step. It reflects our promise to deliver top-notch solutions that meet the linguistic needs of our clients."

He emphasized, "Enhancing our products with advanced multilingual features is a top priority. We aim to reach users globally, offering the benefits of automation. Adding Vietnamese to our supported languages is another big step toward achieving this goal."
