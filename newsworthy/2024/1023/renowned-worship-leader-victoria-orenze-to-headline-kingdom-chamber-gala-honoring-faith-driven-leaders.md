# Renowned Worship Leader Victoria Orenze to Headline Kingdom Chamber Gala Honoring Faith-Driven Leaders

Cherry Hill, NJ (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Oct 23, 2024 @ 3:30 PM US/Eastern — The Kingdom Chamber of Commerce is excited to announce its 20th Anniversary Gala, a milestone celebration of faith, community, and impactful leadership. This transformative event will be held on Friday, October 25th, 2024, at 7:00 PM EST at The Merion, marking two decades of empowering businesses and individuals through faith-driven initiatives.

Experience a Night of Inspiration and Worship

Prepare for an evening of inspiration, powerful worship, and fellowship featuring the acclaimed recording artist and worship leader Victoria Orenze. Guests will experience a night of worship, moving testimonies, and supernatural encounters in God’s presence.

Celebrating Success and Recognizing Partners

The event will also include a special awards ceremony to honor key partners who have been instrumental in the Kingdom Chamber’s growth and impact over the last 20 years. These individuals have demonstrated exceptional dedication to advancing God's Kingdom in the marketplace.

A Message of Transformation

“This Gala is more than just a celebration,” said Dr. Angela Pipersburgh, President of the Kingdom Chamber of Commerce. “It’s a time to reflect on our incredible journey, honor those who have made it possible, and step into a new season of purpose, prosperity, and God’s blessings together.”

Event Details:

* Date: Friday, October 25th, 2024
* Time: 7:00 PM EST
* Location: The Merion, Cinnaminson, NJ 08077

About the Kingdom Chamber of Commerce:

The Kingdom Chamber of Commerce is a faith-based organization committed to advancing God’s Kingdom in the marketplace. The Chamber empowers individuals and businesses to pursue their God-given purpose, fostering a community of faith and integrity.
