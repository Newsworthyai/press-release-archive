# ERE Media Launches New Recruiting Innovation Summit for Talent Acquisition Professionals

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Dec 16, 2024 @ 7:00 AM Pacific — ERE Media, the longest-running and most trusted source of information about talent acquisition, recruiting, sourcing, and candidate experience, today announced the launch of their new conference the ERE Recruiting Innovation Summit, as well as updated recruiting news distribution and related talent acquisition programs.

The ERE Recruiting Innovation Summit is a new event that unites two exceptional brands – the ERE Recruiting Conference and SourceCon. This combined conference has a commitment to TA leaders, recruiters, and sourcers – to help them understand what comes next for talent acquisition and to build relationships that matter.

The ERE Recruiting Innovation Summit will be April 29-30, 2025, in Las Vegas, NV, and the early agenda will be announced soon. Conference tracks will include:

* Strategic Talent Acquisition Leadership: Discover peer-driven insights and proven strategies that will shape the future of talent acquisition.
* Critical Sourcing Tactics: Find tomorrow’s talent today through peer-led learning and connections.
* Innovative Recruiting Technology: Optimize recruiting and hiring efficiencies with the most innovative technologies on the market today and a focus on Artificial intelligence.

The ERE Recruiting and SourceCon newsletters will also be merged into one that will bring its readers the latest news and insights on sourcing, recruiting, and candidate experience. The ERE Media learning and training programs SourceCon Academy, Recruiting with AI, Talent Advisor, the CandE Benchmark Research Program, and comprehensive webinars will also continue to upskill TA professionals and help improve recruiting and sourcing acumen.

“I founded ERE Media nearly 25 years ago as a place for talent acquisition professionals to find unbiased recruiting information and resources from their peers,” said Founder & CEO, David Manaster. “Today, ERE Media is one of the most trusted sources for TA leaders, offering recruiting events, news, research, benchmarking, and training. I’m excited about the launch of the ERE Recruiting Innovation Summit in 2025 to bring together the best in talent acquisition.”To learn more about the ERE Recruiting Innovation Summit, visit: https://www.ererecruitingconference.com/.

About ERE Media

ERE Media was initially conceived nearly 25 years ago as a forum for talent acquisition professionals to find recruiting information and resources online from an unbiased source - their peers. Today, ERE Media has grown to become the most trusted and world renowned source of recruiting publications and conferences, offering trends, insights and networking opportunities for recruitment and HR professionals. More information can be found at https://www.eremedia.com.
