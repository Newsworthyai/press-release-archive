# Introducing candidate.fyi's Scheduling Product: Automating Interview Coordination for Talent Teams and Candidates

Key features of candidate.fyi's scheduling product includes:

* Self-booking interviews: Candidates can self-book interviews or submit their availability, aligning with your available interview slots for a hassle-free scheduling experience.
* Weekly interview limits: Set weekly interview limits and rotate interviewers to balance workloads.
* Tailored calendar invites: Create tailored calendar invites for interviewers and candidates, packed with the right information for a smooth scheduling process.
* Integration with candidate.fyi portal: Integrate scheduling directly within the candidate.fyi portal for a cohesive experience, or use as a standalone scheduling page.
* Automatic calendar holds: Send automatic calendar holds to candidates, reserving their interview time slots. These holds are updated to confirmed appointments once all confirmations are in place.
