# RChilli Expands Global Hiring Capabilities with Support for Multiple Languages

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Jun 12, 2024 @ 6:00 AM Central — RChilli, a leading provider of resume parsing, semantic matching, and data enrichment solutions, is thrilled to announce the addition of Greek to its extensive list of supported languages. Additionally, RChilli Search and Match Engine now supports parsing in Czech, Slovak, and Slovenian, significantly enhancing its capabilities for global users.

With the ever-growing need for diverse language support in the recruitment industry, RChilli remains committed to ensuring its AI-powered solutions are inclusive and accessible to a broader audience.

Adding support for Greek, Czech, Slovak, and Slovenian languages is a substantial step towards this goal. It will enable companies to streamline their hiring processes more efficiently and accurately, regardless of language barriers.

Why This Matters

* Expand the Talent Pool: Whether companies are hiring in Greece or exploring talent in Central Europe, the new language support opens doors to a broader range of candidates.
* Streamline the Hiring Process: Companies can now say goodbye to manual data entry and language-related delays. The enhanced parsing capabilities ensure that accurate, actionable data is processed faster.
* Make Better Hiring Decisions: With precise data extraction across multiple languages, companies can make more informed decisions, leading to better hires and a stronger workforce.

The ability to parse resumes in multiple languages saves time and enhances the accuracy of candidate data, leading to better hiring decisions.

“We are thrilled to announce the expansion of our language support to include Greek, Czech, Slovak, and Slovenian. At RChilli, we constantly strive to improve our solutions and make them more inclusive for our global clients. Adding these languages will enable our users to parse resumes more effectively, ensuring they can access the best talent from various regions. This development reflects our commitment to innovation and excellence in the HR tech industry,” said Vikesh Kumar, Director- Development at RChilli.
