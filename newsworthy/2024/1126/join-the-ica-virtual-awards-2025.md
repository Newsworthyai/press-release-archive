# Join the ICA Virtual Awards 2025

Barcelona, Spain (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Nov 26, 2024 @ 1:25 PM Eastern — The International Cannabis Awards (ICA) is thrilled to announce the 2025 ICA Virtual Awards, a one-of-a-kind online event that celebrates the achievements and innovations driving the cannabis industry forward globally. This year’s fully virtual awards ceremony will bring together an impressive lineup of top brands, distributors, breeders, and other cannabis industry pioneers for an engaging, interactive experience designed to recognize and inspire the global cannabis community.

How It WorksThis year's ICA Awards invites industry enthusiasts and experts to participate in a two-phase nomination and voting process, accessible from anywhere in the world:

1. Nominations (Open until December 16): We invite you to nominate your favorite cannabis brands, wholesale distributors, seed banks, vape devices, and innovators. Simply fill out our online form and help shine a spotlight on the very best in cannabis.
2. Voting (Begins December 16): Once nominations close, we’ll announce a shortlist of nominees. Use our secure online platform to cast your votes and influence next year’s winners.

Enhancing Winner Visibility All YearIn partnership with our media collaborators, ICA is dedicated to amplifying winners’ success well beyond the awards. Our 2025 initiatives include:

* Dedicated Winner Profiles: Each winner will have a profile on a special media page featuring photos, interviews, and links to connect audiences with their stories.
* Monthly Winner Spotlights: Our “Winner of the Month” series will feature one winner per month, showcasing their progress and achievements since the awards.
* Interactive Winner Badges: Digital award badges will include QR codes or links to the winners’ profiles, driving visibility and engagement.
* Quarterly Recap Publications: Quarterly publications in digital and print will feature updates on winners, keeping their achievements in the public eye.
* Co-Marketing with Winners: Collaborative blog posts, videos, and social media features will boost engagement, providing winners with greater exposure and connecting them with the ICA audience.

About [International Cannabis Awards (ICA)](http://www.internationalcannabisawards.com/):Founded in Barcelona, Spain in 2023. Join us for the ICA Virtual Awards 2025 and be part of an event that honors outstanding contributions in the cannabis industry. Visit www.internationalcannabisawards.com for more information and follow us on Instagram at @internationalcannaawards to stay updated.
