# RChilli Sets A Strong Foothold in Complete Oracle Ecosystem With Taleo Integration

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Feb 9, 2024 @ 7:00 AM Central — RChilli, a leader in the HR Tech space proudly announces a significant milestone in its journey towards transforming the HR Tech domain by adding Taleo to its list of Oracle integrations alongside Recruiting Booster, ORC, & Peoplesoft.

Along with being available in ERP giant marketplaces like Salesforce, SuccessFactor, MS Dynamics and many others, RChilli has cemented its position as a trailblazer in the industry by becoming a category leader in the Oracle ecosystem and capturing the complete ERP space.

This major breakthrough makes Taleo, HCM, & Peoplesoft users pave the way for improved candidate engagement, more efficient hiring processes, and an improved recruiter experience. In an effort to empower organizations in forging stronger connections with candidates and streamlining top talent acquisition, the dynamic talent acquisition solution, aims to revolutionize the approach of talent acquisition teams towards candidate engagement and recruitment.

The customers of Oracle solutions can leverage intelligent resume extraction with RChilli’s intuitive interface and robust features. This empowers candidates to efficiently complete applications in global languages, enhancing inclusivity and personalization in the application process. Along with this, users of Taleo can now experience remarkable changes in their talent acquisition landscape.

Expressing his thoughts on RChilli becoming a category leader in Oracle ERP space, Josh Adams, Director of ERP Operations at RChilli, emphasizes that RChilli stays true to the company's reputation for customer delight & loyalty, and offering customer-centric innovations in the HR Tech space. He highlights RChilli's commitment to timely service delivery, establishing the company as a trusted partner for its customers.

Vinay Johar, CEO RChilli said, “I'm excited to share that RChilli has become the category leader in the Oracle ecosystem. With our recent integration across all Oracle products like Oracle HCM, Recruiting Booster, PeopleSoft, and Taleo, we offer a unique advantage in this segment. If you're not using RChilli with Oracle, you may be missing out on a superior candidate experience aligned with industry standards.

Our partnership with Oracle, initiated a few years ago, has propelled us forward. Our success is driven by cutting-edge technology, customer-centric processes, and an unwavering commitment to exceptional experiences.

We're passionate about supporting our customers, offering the latest tech, and ensuring unmatched value. Our offerings include pioneering AI integration, 24x7 global support, globally located servers, and adherence to compliance standards like HIPAA. We aim to be partners, not just vendors, in our clients' success.

For more details and information about RChilli and its innovative solutions, please visit the RChilli website.

About RChilli

RChilli is the trusted partner for parsing, matching, and data enrichment, providing companies, in 50+ countries, with solutions built for the future of technology and recruiting. RChilli’s innovative products, backed by an industry-leading tech stack, power the processing of 4.1 billion+ docs a year for 1600+ top global recruiting platforms. Enabling companies to hire better talent faster since 2010, RChilli is ISO 27001:2013, SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA certified, and GDPR compliant.
