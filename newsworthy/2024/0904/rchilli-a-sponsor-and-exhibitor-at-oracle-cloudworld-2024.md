# RChilli- A Sponsor and Exhibitor at Oracle CloudWorld 2024

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Sep 4, 2024 @ 1:50 PM Pacific — RChilli, a leading innovator in AI-driven recruitment solutions, will sponsor and exhibit at Oracle CloudWorld 2024 in Las Vegas from September 9 to 12, 2024.

As a trusted Oracle partner, RChilli continues to innovate the recruitment landscape with its suite of intelligent AI-powered products designed to enhance the hiring process.

The AI-powered solutions of RChilli include advancements in recruitment AI, data hygiene, unbiased hiring, and recruiter hub/connectors, which are poised to bring significant value to businesses leveraging Oracle HCM.

The participation of RChilli at Oracle CloudWorld 2024 underscores its commitment to providing advanced solutions that seamlessly integrate with Oracle’s ecosystem.

Attendees at Oracle CloudWorld 2024 will have the opportunity to explore RChilli’s advanced product suite, including candidate profile import, essential data enhancer, full database reprocessing, list of values, customizable taxonomy, redact & design, browser assistant, email importer, and bulk data import.

These solutions are designed to optimize the hiring journey for recruiters and candidates alike. By automating the accurate extraction and normalization of data, RChilli empowers organizations to maintain clean, enriched candidate databases, resulting in faster hiring.

“Our collaboration with Oracle reflects our shared vision of transforming the recruitment landscape through innovation and technology,” said Josh Adams, Director of ERP Operations at RChilli.

Further, he added, “With our AI-powered solutions, businesses can unlock the full potential of their recruitment data, ensuring they attract the best talent while fostering a more inclusive and efficient hiring process.”

Oracle CloudWorld 2024 attendees are invited to visit RChilli’s booth to experience live demos, engage in custom consultations, and discover the latest AI advancements in recruitment technology.

This is an unparalleled opportunity to connect with RChilli’s experts and learn how to leverage these AI-powered solutions to meet your organization’s unique needs.

For more information about RChilli’s participation in Oracle CloudWorld 2024 or to schedule a meeting during the event, please visit “Meet RChilli At CloudWorld2024.
