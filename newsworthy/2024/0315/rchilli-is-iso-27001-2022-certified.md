# RChilli is ISO 27001:2022 Certified

San Ramon, CA (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Mar 15, 2024 @ 9:00 AM Central — RChilli proudly announces its successful attainment of the ISO 27001:2022 certification. This prestigious certification showcases RChilli’s commitment to maintaining the highest information security standards for its operations.

ISO 27001:2022 is the latest version of the internationally recognized information security standard. It provides a complete framework for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and improving an organization's information security system. By achieving this certification, RChilli demonstrates its dedication to safeguarding sensitive information and ensuring data confidentiality, integrity, and availability for its clients and stakeholders.

"We are thrilled to announce that we are ISO 27001:2022 certified," said Jitender Thakur, IT - Head of Infrastructure and Information Security / CISO at RChilli. "This achievement defines our commitment to providing our clients with cutting-edge HR technology solutions while prioritizing the best data protection and security standards."

The company received this certification after a rigorous evaluation of its information security management systems, policies, procedures, and controls by an independent third-party certification body. The certification validates that RChilli has implemented a robust and effective ISMS that aligns with international best practices.

Key benefits of ISO 27001:2022 certification for RChilli and its clients include

* enhanced risk management
* improved information security awareness
* and increased confidence in the company's ability to handle sensitive data securely.

The certification also positions RChilli as a trusted partner for organizations seeking advanced HR technology solutions like resume parser, taxonomy, search & match engines with a strong focus on data protection.

As cyber threats continue increasing, organizations worldwide recognize the importance of prioritizing information security. RChilli's ISO 27001:2022 certification exemplifies the company's proactive approach to addressing the ever-changing data protection and privacy challenges.
