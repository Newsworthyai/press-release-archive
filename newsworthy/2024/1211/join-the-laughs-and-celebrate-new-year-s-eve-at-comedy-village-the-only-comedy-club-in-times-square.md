# Join the Laughs and Celebrate New Year's Eve at Comedy Village the Only Comedy Club in Times Square

Times Square, New York (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Dec 11, 2024 @ 6:00 AM Pacific — As New Year's Eve approaches, comedy enthusiasts and tourists alike are in for a treat at Comedy Village, the only comedy club near the iconic NYE Ball Drop in Times Square. With two shows at 8 PM and 10 PM featuring renowned comedians Nick Simmons, PJ Landers, Krissy Gregory, and Nick Callas, attendees can expect an evening filled with laughter and entertainment.

"We are thrilled to celebrate New Year's Eve with comedy fans from all over," expressed the club's owner PJ Landers in a statement. "At Comedy Village, we offer a unique experience of combining laughter with the excitement of the NYE Ball Drop, making us a must-visit tourist attraction."In addition to the comedy shows, guests can indulge in a champagne toast, appetizers, and mingle with fellow comedy club enthusiasts. The fun doesn't stop with the shows – an after-party will keep the celebration going late into the night at the venue's cocktail bar.

Having been a fixture in the New York comedy scene for over two decades, Comedy Village has built a reputation for showcasing top talent and creating a vibrant comedy community. The club's recent move to Times Square has further solidified its status as a premier destination for comedy lovers.

To stay updated on upcoming shows and events, attendees are encouraged to connect with Comedy Village on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and X.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to ring in the new year with laughter and joy at Comedy Village in Times Square. Join us for a night of comedy, celebration, and unforgettable moments!

For more information and ticket reservations, visit the Comedy Village website or contact pj@comedyvillage.com.

About Comedy Village

Comedy Village is a long-standing comedy club located in the heart of Times Square, New York. With nightly shows, an array of talented comedians, and a lively atmosphere, the club promises an unforgettable entertainment experience for both locals and visitors.
