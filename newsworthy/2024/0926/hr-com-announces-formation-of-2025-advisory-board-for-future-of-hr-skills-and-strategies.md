# HR.com Announces Formation of 2025 Advisory Board for Future of HR Skills and Strategies

Jacksons Point, Ontario, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Sep 26, 2024 @ 8:00 AM America/Toronto — HR.com is proud to announce the formation of the 2025 Future of HR Skills and Strategies Advisory Board. This esteemed panel, composed of industry-leading experts, will guide the future of HR proficiencies, training, and development, helping businesses to prepare their HR professionals for the evolving demands of the workplace.

The board members will collaborate closely with HR.com’s HR Research Institute (HRRI) to offer essential insights into how organizations can enhance reskilling, upskilling, and development initiatives for HR practitioners. As businesses navigate rapid changes in technology, employee expectations, and workforce dynamics, the need for agile, forward-thinking HR professionals has never been greater.

The research findings from this initiative will be published in a comprehensive report offering detailed insights and recommendations. Additionally, a condensed two-page infographic will provide a quick reference for key points. The upcoming virtual event will include a day of informative sessions from a variety of subject matter experts in this area.

"As the future of work continues to evolve, so must the skills of our HR leaders," said HR.com’s CEO and Chief Instigator, Debbie McGrath. “These experts will play a key role in shaping how businesses equip their HR teams with the skills and strategies needed to manage tomorrow’s workforce effectively."

The HR Research Institute releases annual “State of the Industry” reports in a variety of HR topic areas, including this past research report, The State of HR Skills & Education, on the same topic.

This and other free research reports and infographics can be downloaded at hr.com/researchinstitute.
