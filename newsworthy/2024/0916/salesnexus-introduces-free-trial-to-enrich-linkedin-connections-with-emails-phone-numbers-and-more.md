# SalesNexus Introduces Free Trial to Enrich LinkedIn Connections with Emails, Phone Numbers, and More

Houston, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Sep 16, 2024 @ 7:00 AM Central — SalesNexus, a leading CRM and email marketing solution, is excited to announce a powerful new feature for users who sign up for a free trial: the ability to enrich LinkedIn connections with valuable contact information, including email addresses, phone numbers, mobile numbers, mailing addresses, company information, and more.

As part of the free trial, users can now enhance their LinkedIn email prospecting efforts by enriching up to 200 LinkedIn connections with complete contact data, making it easier to build meaningful relationships and increase outreach efficiency.

“SalesNexus is committed to helping professionals build their network and streamline their outreach,” said Craig Klein, SalesNexus, CEO. “By enriching LinkedIn connections with detailed contact information, we empower users to take their lead generation to the next level.”

Key Benefits of SalesNexus LinkedIn Enrichment:

* Free Trial Access: Users can enrich up to 200 LinkedIn connections at no cost.
* Comprehensive Contact Information: Gain access to email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, company details, and more.
* Enhanced Lead Generation: Efficiently expand your marketing and sales outreach with enriched data.

This feature makes it easier for businesses to target leads, engage in personalized outreach, and accelerate the sales process. LinkedIn users can now save time and effort by having enriched contact lists at their fingertips.

How It Works:

1. Create a Free Trial on SalesNexus.com

2. Upload Your LinkedIn Connections for enrichment.

3. Receive a Detailed List with up-to-date contact information for up to 200 connections.
