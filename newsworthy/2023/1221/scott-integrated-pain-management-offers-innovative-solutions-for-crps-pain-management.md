CRPS, also known as reflex sympathetic dystrophy (RSD), is a chronic pain condition that typically affects one limb and is often characterized by intense burning pain, swelling, and changes in skin color and temperature. Scott Integrated Pain Management offers a personalized treatment plan for each patient, which may include trigger point injections, joint and bursa injections, and Ketamine infusions, tailored to their unique needs.

Additionally, the clinic is proud to be one of the few providers that accept insurance for Ketamine treatments, making it more accessible to patients seeking relief from their CRPS symptoms. They are in-network with Medicare, United Healthcare, Independence Blue Cross and Blue Shield, Cigna, and Highmark Blueshield, ensuring affordable and convenient treatment options.

"We are committed to providing exceptional care to our patients and ensuring that accessing innovative treatments is not a barrier for them," stated Dr. Scott. "We believe that every patient deserves the opportunity to explore all available options for pain management."

Scott Integrated Pain Management also offers a multidisciplinary approach to the treatment of pain, encompassing not only physical interventions but also medical management and depression treatments for those experiencing the emotional toll of chronic pain. With a focus on opioid alternatives, the clinic strives to find individualized solutions for some of the most challenging pain conditions.
