Washington, DC (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Dec 22, 2023 @ 12:30 AM Eastern â€” Vital TMS Therapy, a leading healthcare provider in Washington, DC, is pleased to announce the availability of Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) therapy, an alternative treatment option for patients suffering from mental health problems and chronic pain conditions. TMS is a safe and painless therapy that utilizes magnetic energy to stimulate nerve cells in the brain, resulting in an improved mood and overall well-being.

"TMS therapy has revolutionized the way we approach treatment-resistant depression and other mental health conditions," said Dr. Sarah Thompson, the medical director at Vital TMS Therapy. "By boosting the release of neurotransmitters like dopamine and serotonin, TMS has shown remarkable effectiveness in improving the lives of our patients who haven't responded well to other treatment methods."

In addition to its effectiveness for treatment-resistant depression, TMS therapy has been found beneficial for a range of conditions including anxiety, addiction, PTSD, OCD, chronic pain, fibromyalgia, and migraines. The treatment process is noninvasive, does not require anesthesia or sedation, and is completely pain-free. During a session, patients sit comfortably while a coil positioned above their head delivers magnetic pulses to the brain. Patients may experience a slight tapping sensation and hear a clicking noise during the treatment.

"At Vital TMS Therapy, we prioritize patient comfort and well-being throughout the treatment process," continued Dr. Thompson. "Our team of specialists is dedicated to ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for each patient, monitoring their progress closely, and addressing any concerns they may have."

With the aid of CloudTMS technology, an advanced system that securely stores practice data in the cloud, Vital TMS Therapy can maximize the benefits of TMS treatment and provide personalized care to its patients. On average, most patients require approximately five TMS treatment sessions to achieve optimal results.
