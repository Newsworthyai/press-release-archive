# Santos Muscle Nutrition Preparing for Amazon Launch in the United States

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Mar 17, 2026 @ 5:00 AM Eastern — 

 Santos Muscle Nutrition, the Netherlands-based sports nutrition brand focused on muscle growth and athletic performance, announced plans to introduce its supplements to U.S. consumers through Amazon, marking the company’s first step toward broader availability in the American market.

 Amazon has become one of the largest online marketplaces for sports nutrition products, giving brands the ability to reach athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and wellness-minded consumers nationwide. By preparing for its Amazon debut, Santos Muscle Nutrition aims to bring its European-developed performance supplements to one of the world’s most active fitness communities.

 “Our vision has always been to help people build stronger, healthier bodies through high-quality supplements,” said Mike de Groot, Founder of Santos Muscle Nutrition. “Launching on Amazon will allow us to introduce the Santos brand to a much wider audience in the United States.”

 A European Performance Brand Expanding Internationally Founded in the Netherlands, Santos Muscle Nutrition was created to provide athletes with supplements designed to support muscle growth, training intensity, and recovery.

 The company continues to expand its product lineup with formulas designed to support:

 * Muscle growth and recovery
* Strength and workout performance
* Energy and endurance during training
* Daily nutritional support for active lifestyles

 Santos Muscle Nutrition focuses on developing performance-driven supplements that combine quality ingredients with practical benefits for athletes and active individuals.

 Preparing for U.S. Market Entry The upcoming Amazon launch represents an important milestone in the company’s international growth strategy. The United States remains the largest sports nutrition market in the world, making it a key destination for global supplement brands seeking to expand their reach.

 “Entering the U.S. market is an exciting step for Santos Muscle Nutrition,” de Groot added. “We look forward to sharing our products with American consumers who value strength, performance, and quality supplementation.”

 Additional updates regarding product availability and launch timing on Amazon are expected in the coming months.

 About [Santos Muscle Nutrition](https://santosmusclenutrition.nl/) Santos Muscle Nutrition is a Netherlands-based sports nutrition company focused on developing supplements intended to support structured fitness routines, muscle development, and consistent training habits. The company offers a growing range of products and plans continuous expansion based on consumer interest and performance trends.
