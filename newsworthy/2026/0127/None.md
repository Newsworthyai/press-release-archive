# Limitless Beauty Launches Scalp Micropigmentation in Bellingham, Bringing Advanced Hair Restoration Closer to Home

Bellingham, Wa. (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday Jan 27, 2026 @ 11:59 AM Pacific — FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

 Bellingham, WA — Limitless Beauty announces the launch of Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP), an advanced, non-invasive hair restoration procedure now available locally. Until now, residents seeking SMP typically had to travel south to Everett or Seattle for treatment.

 Scalp Micropigmentation creates the appearance of fuller, thicker hair by depositing pigment into the scalp to replicate natural hair follicles. The procedure is effective for men and women experiencing hair thinning, alopecia, pattern baldness, or general hair loss. Most clients complete treatment in approximately three sessions.

 Pricing for SMP typically ranges from $3,000 to $6,500, depending on the level of coverage and individual needs. Payment options are available to make treatment more accessible.

 To celebrate the launch, Limitless Beauty is offering a 15% Valentine’s Day launch special, representing $450 to $975 in savings. This offer is available to new SMP clients who mention this press release and book their consultation by January 15, 2026. The promotional pricing is valid through Valentine’s Day.

 Limitless Beauty founder and lead practitioner Keely brings more than 22 years of experience in pigment-based cosmetic procedures, with four years specializing in Scalp Micropigmentation. She has completed over 100 SMP treatments and also teaches the technique, giving clients confidence they are working with an experienced and highly trained provider.

 “My goal with this work is to help people regain the confidence they had before hair loss,” said Keely. “Even halfway through the process, clients often tell me they already feel like themselves again. Seeing that change is why I do this.”

 SMP is a cosmetic, non-surgical procedure with minimal downtime and long-lasting results. Before-and-after images are available during consultations to help clients understand realistic outcomes and expected results.

 Scalp Micropigmentation consultations are available now by appointment. For more information or to schedule a consultation, visit https://thelimitlessbeauty.com.
