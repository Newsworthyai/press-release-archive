# TNT Educates U.S. Athletes on Creatine, Clear Whey, and Pre-Workout Performance

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Mar 20, 2026 @ 4:00 AM Eastern — 

 True Nutrition Technology (TNT), the German sports nutrition brand trusted by more than 500,000 customers, is spotlighting three of the most widely used supplement categories among athletes—creatine, clear whey protein, and pre-workout formulas—as the company continues building awareness ahead of broader U.S. product availability.

 Sports nutrition has grown significantly in recent years as more consumers incorporate structured training and recovery routines into their daily lives. While the supplement industry continues to expand, certain products remain foundational in athletic nutrition due to their consistent use across training disciplines.

 “Our philosophy has always been to focus on products athletes actually use every day,” said Markus Himmelstoss, Managing Director of True Nutrition Technology. “Creatine, protein, and pre-workout supplements remain the backbone of performance nutrition for many athletes.”

 Creatine: A Foundation of Strength and Performance Creatine monohydrate is one of the most widely researched supplements in sports nutrition and is commonly used to support strength, muscular power, and cellular energy during high-intensity training.

 TNT’s Creatine Monohydrate with Creapure® is manufactured in Germany and known for its exceptional purity and quality standards.

 Key characteristics include:

 * 99.99% pure Creapure® creatine
* Manufactured exclusively in Germany
* Micronized for improved solubility
* Widely used for strength training and performance support

 Clear Whey: A Lighter Alternative to Traditional Protein Shakes Protein remains one of the most essential components of athletic recovery and muscle development. While traditional whey protein shakes are typically thick and creamy, Clear Whey offers a lighter alternative that mixes into a refreshing beverage.

 TNT CLEAR WHEY features:

 * 81% whey protein isolate
* Extremely low fat and carbohydrates
* Lactose-free and easy to digest
* High levels of naturally occurring amino acids and BCAAs

 Why TNT’s Clear Whey Tastes Better To address the common problem of dry or thin-tasting Clear Whey formulas, TNT uses a specialized blend of isolates that improves taste, reduces foaming, and creates a smoother mouthfeel.

 Pre-Workout Supplements for Energy and Focus Pre-workout formulas are widely used by athletes seeking to support focus, endurance, and training intensity.

 TNT’s Synapsensause Pre-Workout is designed for sustained performance during demanding workouts and competition training.

 Key formulation highlights include:

 * 400mg total caffeine (200mg pure caffeine + 200mg from guarana extract)
* 8g L-Citrulline for nitric oxide support
* 6g L-Tyrosine for focus and mental performance
* Manufactured and tested in Germany

 German Quality Standards All TNT supplements are manufactured in Germany under strict quality control standards and are routinely tested by independent laboratories for purity, safety, and consistency.

 By emphasizing transparency, ingredient quality, and evidence-based formulations, the company aims to bring its performance-focused supplement philosophy to a broader international audience.

 “German sports nutrition is built around precision and consistency,” Himmelstoss added. “Our goal is to deliver supplements athletes can trust every day.”

 Additional announcements regarding TNT’s U.S. retail availability are expected in the coming months.

From advanced protein blends to innovative vegan formulations, TNT develops evidence-based nutrition designed to help athletes and everyday individuals perform at their best. More than just supplements, TNT represents a passion for sport, health, and performance. Built on German precision and a culture of honesty, TNT continues to expand globally with one mission: delivering the highest quality nutrition—for us, and for you.
