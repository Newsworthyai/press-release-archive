# Join the HRO Today Talent Acquisition Summit in Atlanta, GA

HRO Today is thrilled to announce the Talent Acquisition Summit, scheduled for September 15-16, 2026, at the Loudermilk Conference Center in Atlanta, GA. This premier event will gather top talent acquisition (TA) leaders, HR experts, and practitioners to explore innovative strategies and solutions in recruiting top-tier talent.

 Attendees will have the opportunity to engage with renowned speakers, including Sunil Asija, Director of Human Resources, Oakland County, and Jeff Battinus, Head of Talent Acquisition, Dental Care Alliance, among others. The summit promises a rich agenda with keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities that will empower HR professionals to excel in today's competitive job market.

 "The HRO Today Talent Acquisition Summit is a pivotal event for HR leaders to reimagine recruiting strategies," said Berthony Poux, VP, Events, HRO Today. "We aim to provide a platform that not only showcases innovative solutions but also celebrates excellence in talent acquisition."

 Highlights of the event include the Talent Acquisition Strategy sessions, an Awards Reception to honor outstanding contributions in HR, and a myriad of networking opportunities. Participants can also explore sponsorship packages and learn how to become a speaker to further engage with industry leaders.

 For organizations with multiple attendees, special discounts are available. Interested parties are encouraged to contact Arturo Aguero for more information. Join us in Atlanta and be part of the transformation in HR and workforce management.

 About HRO Today: HRO Today is a leading provider of news, research, and conferences for the HR industry. Our events bring together global HR leaders to share best practices and explore the latest trends in talent acquisition and management.
