# ORGANA International Marks Independence Day by Celebrating America's Entrepreneurial Spirit


As Americans celebrate Independence Day, ORGANA International is recognizing the entrepreneurial spirit that has long been a driving force behind innovation, small business growth, and the pursuit of the American dream.

 For Larry Mills, CEO of ORGANA International, entrepreneurship has always been about more than building a company. It has been about creating products that help people live healthier lives while steadily growing a business founded on integrity, customer relationships, and a long-term commitment to wellness.

 "Independence Day reminds us that one of America's greatest strengths is the freedom to pursue an idea and turn it into something meaningful," said Mills. "Building a business isn't about overnight success. It's about staying committed to your vision, serving your customers, and continuing to improve year after year."

 Founded in 1997, ORGANA International has grown from a wellness company focused on natural health products into a recognized brand serving customers across the United States while expanding into international markets. The company continues to broaden access to its portfolio of plant-derived wellness products through leading online marketplaces and global distribution initiatives.

 Mills said ORGANA's growth reflects the opportunities available to entrepreneurs who remain focused on long-term value rather than short-term results.

 "We've been fortunate to grow step by step over the years," Mills said. "Every milestone has been made possible by loyal customers, dedicated partners, and the opportunity to build a business in a country that encourages innovation and entrepreneurship."

 Built on Wellness, Guided by Purpose Since its founding, ORGANA International has remained committed to developing wellness products rooted in natural ingredients and everyday health support. Its product portfolio includes Fulvic Acid Trace Mineral Drink, Liquid Oxygen, Essiac Tea, magnesium creams, and other wellness formulations designed to complement healthy lifestyles.

 As consumer interest in natural wellness continues to grow, ORGANA remains focused on educating consumers while expanding access to its products both in the United States and internationally.

 ORGANA Wellness Products - Available on Amazon Consumers can purchase ORGANA's Fulvic Acid Trace Mineral Drink, Liquid Oxygen, Essiac Tea, and other wellness products through the company's website and on Amazon. The company continues to expand availability while remaining committed to the natural wellness philosophy that has guided ORGANA for nearly three decades.

 "Entrepreneurship is ultimately about creating something that improves people's lives," Mills added. "We're grateful for the opportunity to continue serving our customers, and Independence Day is a meaningful reminder of the freedom that makes journeys like ours possible."

 About [ORGANA International](https://organa.net/) Founded in 1997, ORGANA International is a wellness company committed to delivering natural, bioavailable products that help people live healthier lives. From trace minerals and liquid oxygen to magnesium creams and detoxification teas, ORGANA’s product line is designed to restore vital elements often missing in modern lifestyles. Headquartered in Springville, Utah, ORGANA is a customer-first company with a 5-star BBB rating and nearly three decades of dedication to natural wellness.
