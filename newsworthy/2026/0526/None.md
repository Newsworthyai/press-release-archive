# ORGANA International Highlights Summer Travel Fatigue and Oxygen Support

ORGANA International is highlighting the growing impact of summer travel fatigue, heat exposure, and active lifestyles on energy and recovery while emphasizing the importance of oxygen-support wellness routines during the summer months.

 Long travel days, outdoor activities, warmer temperatures, and disrupted routines can contribute to feelings of fatigue and low energy during the summer season. While many consumers focus on hydration and nutrition, ORGANA International says oxygen-support wellness products are increasingly becoming part of broader daily wellness and recovery routines.

 ORGANA International, a wellness company specializing in plant-derived minerals and natural wellness formulations, says modern lifestyles often place additional demands on energy balance and recovery, particularly during periods of increased activity and travel.

 “Summer can be one of the most physically demanding times of the year,” said Larry Mills, CEO of ORGANA International. “Travel, heat, outdoor activity, and busy schedules may all impact how people feel day to day. Many consumers are looking for wellness routines that help support energy, hydration, and overall vitality.”

 What May Contribute to Summer Fatigue * Increased outdoor activity and heat exposure
* Travel-related fatigue and disrupted routines

* Hydration and wellness challenges during warmer months

 According to ORGANA International, consumers are increasingly exploring wellness products that support active lifestyles, recovery routines, and overall energy balance as part of their daily health practices.
