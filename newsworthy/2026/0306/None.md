# ORGANA International Expands Availability on Amazon and Walmart.com — Entering the World’s Largest E-Commerce Marketplace

Springville, UT (Newsworthy.ai) Thursday Mar 5, 2026 @ 5:00 PM US/Mountain — ORGANA International, a wellness company known for its natural mineral-based supplements, announced expanded availability of its products through Amazon and Walmart.com as the company continues to grow its digital retail presence in the United States.

 Together, Amazon and Walmart.com represent two of the largest online retail platforms serving American consumers. With U.S. e-commerce sales projected to surpass $2 trillion within the next several years, the expansion places ORGANA within one of the fastest-growing retail channels in the world.

 “Our goal has always been to make natural, plant-derived minerals more accessible to people who want to support their health naturally,” said Larry Mills, CEO of ORGANA International. “Expanding our presence on Amazon and Walmart.com allows more consumers to discover ORGANA and experience the benefits of these essential nutrients.”

 Addressing Modern Nutrient Gaps Modern agricultural practices, soil depletion, and fast-paced lifestyles have contributed to widespread mineral deficiencies in today’s food supply. Many people unknowingly fall short on important trace minerals that support energy production, cellular function, hydration, and overall wellness.

 ORGANA’s product line focuses on replenishing these nutrients through natural, plant-derived mineral supplements designed for optimal bioavailability.

 Unlike many synthetic mineral supplements, ORGANA minerals are derived from decomposed plant matter rich in naturally occurring trace minerals and fulvic acid — compounds known to help transport nutrients into cells where they are needed most.

 ORGANA’s Mineral-Based Wellness Products Several of ORGANA’s most popular products are now accessible to consumers through major online marketplaces.

 Organa Trace Minerals Liquid Supplement

 * Up to 78 naturally occurring trace minerals
* Plant-derived minerals designed for high bioavailability
* Contains fulvic acid to support mineral transport at the cellular level

 Organa Mineral Drink

 * Liquid mineral formula designed to replenish essential nutrients
* Supports hydration, energy, and overall wellness

 Organa Liquid Oxygen

 * Oxygen-enhanced liquid formula used internally or topically
* Designed to support stamina, endurance, and daily vitality

 Together, these products reflect ORGANA’s mission to deliver natural supplements that help restore vital nutrients often missing from modern diets.

 Expanding Access to Natural Wellness As e-commerce continues to reshape how consumers discover and purchase wellness products, ORGANA believes online platforms provide an important opportunity to connect directly with individuals seeking natural health solutions.

 “People today are more aware than ever of the role nutrition plays in overall health,” Mills added. “By expanding across major e-commerce platforms, we can reach more families, athletes, and wellness-focused individuals who want natural ways to support their bodies.”

 Consumers can now find ORGANA products through both Amazon and Walmart.com, making the brand’s mineral-rich supplements easier to access nationwide.

From trace minerals and liquid oxygen to magnesium creams and detoxification teas, ORGANA’s product line is designed to restore vital elements often missing in modern lifestyles.
