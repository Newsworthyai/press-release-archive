# Family Friend Roofing Donates Roof to Central Texas Veteran in Community Holiday Initiative

Round Rock, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Mar 9, 2026 @ 2:00 AM Central — Family Friend Roofing and Construction in Round Rock, TX donated a new roof to a local veteran this holiday season. Inspired by community partnerships including USA Cares and ShareTheWill Foundation, the initiative reflects the company’s commitment to serving veterans across Central Texas and Dallas, with future nominations now planned.

 On November 18, a Central Texas veteran named Rico received news that would change his holiday season, a brand-new roof fully donated by Family Friend Roofing and Construction.

 The Round Rock-based roofing company stepped forward after learning that the same veteran had recently received a power wheelchair through community partner, ShareTheWill Foundation. WA simple morning call sparked a defining moment in the company's mission.

 Why Did Family Friend Roofing Decide to Donate a Roof to a Veteran? “When we launched, we knew giving back would be a pillar of our company,” said Lindsay Shapee, co-owner of Family Friend Roofing and Construction. “It’s who we are.”

 Lindsay, along with co-owners Josh Bovino and Aaron Bernard, built the company with service at its core. While early community buy-in developed gradually beyond the Round Rock Chamber and the Five Point Board of Realtors, one value quickly unified the leadership team, supporting military members and veterans.

 During a team call, Josh shared what Lindsay now fondly calls “another bright idea.”

 “He said, ‘I really want to donate a roof to a veteran for Christmas. How can we make that happen?’”

 What followed was weeks of coordination, conversations, and careful vetting to ensure the right family would receive the support.

 How Did Local Organizations Help Make This Roof Donation Possible? The Family Friend Roof and Construction team reached out to Matt Maples of USA Cares, a nonprofit dedicated to assisting post-9/11 veterans and their families. Though the process required thoughtful coordination, the introduction led to Rico, a veteran who already received assistance in the form of a power wheelchair through community advocates including Will Williams and ShareTheWill Foundation.

 Together, these organizations created a moment bigger than any one company.

 Family Friend Roofing coordinated a surprise reveal at Café Java, where Rico learned about the roof donation alongside community members and even Santa Claus. The event was both practical and deeply personal.

 “Being part of this was more than we asked for,” Lindsay said. “Watching our partners connect with Rico, hear his story, and truly understand his journey changed us.”

 What Does This Roof Donation Mean for the Round Rock Community? For Family Friend Roofing, this initiative was not just about shingles and decking. It was about connection.

 Serving homeowners across Central Texas and Dallas, the company specializes in residential roof repair and replacement, storm restoration, and insurance claim guidance. Leadership says community impact is equally foundational.

 Lindsay is active with the Five Point Board of Realtors, and the company is engaged with the Round Rock Chamber, strengthening ties with local business leaders and families throughout Williamson County.

 “Real change happens when people connect beyond the surface,” Lindsay shared. “Telling stories, sharing time, and building relationships, that’s what brings a team closer.”

 Will Family Friend Roofing Donate More Roofs in the Future? Yes.

 The company plans to accept nominations for future veteran roof donations as it continues to grow. The goal is to expand the initiative annually, creating a lasting tradition of service during the holiday season and beyond.

 This first roof marks the beginning of what leadership hopes will become a sustained giving program across the regions they serve.

 Key Details * Company: Family Friend Roofing and Construction
* Location: Round Rock, TX
* Service Areas: Central Texas and Dallas
* Initiative: Donated roof to local veteran
* Date Veteran Notified: November 18
* Partners Referenced: USA Cares, ShareTheWill Foundation
* Future Plans: Accepting nominations for additional veteran roof donations

 About Family Friend Roofing and Construction Family Friend Roofing and Construction is a Round Rock-based roofing company serving Central Texas and Dallas. The company provides residential roof repair, roof replacement, storm damage restoration, and insurance claim assistance.

 Built on the belief that business should strengthen community, the company integrates local engagement into its core operations, partnering with area organizations and veteran-focused nonprofits.
