# HR.com Announces 2026 HR Training Solution Award Winner

Jacksons Point, Ontario, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Mar 18, 2026 @ 10:00 AM Eastern — The winner of the Best HR Training Solution Award for 2026 was announced during HR.com's Future of HR Skills and Strategies Virtual Event on March 18. The award honors outstanding achievements in advancing and enhancing skills, improving performance, and helping organizations build stronger, more capable HR teams.

 Best HR Training Solution Award: Rackspace Technology

 The AI Institute at Rackspace Technology equips employees at all levels with the skills to confidently adopt, apply, and innovate with AI. Combining role-based learning, practical application, and Responsible AI principles, the program delivers scalable, ethical, and measurable workforce transformation — from foundational literacy to advanced mastery. It provides certification, digital badges, and progress tracking, enabling employees to contribute to innovation while advancing organizational AI readiness.

 View All HR.com Awards: www.hr.com/awards

 "HR training is the cornerstone of building high-performing, future-ready HR teams,” said HR.com CEO Debbie McGrath. “We are proud to recognize this innovative, scalable AI learning program that equips employees with the skills to drive real impact and advance organizational success."
