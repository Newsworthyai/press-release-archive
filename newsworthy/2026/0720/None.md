# Catch Nathan Macintosh's Hilarious Pacific Northwest Tour This Summer!

Nathan Macintosh, one of stand-up comedy's fastest-rising voices, returns to the Pacific Northwest this summer with three performances, including a one night appearance in Portland and two nights in Seattle.

 Macintosh will perform Wednesday, July 29 at 8:00 p.m. in the Neon Room at Helium Comedy Club in Portland, followed by performances at Laughs Comedy Club in Seattle on Friday, July 31 and Saturday, August 1.

 Known for his intelligent observations, quick pacing, and distinctive perspective on everyday life, Macintosh has built an international following through stand-up specials, television appearances, podcasts, and sold-out comedy clubs across North America. His performances resonate with audiences through compelling storytelling, insightful self-awareness, and fresh takes on everyday scenarios.

 "Every audience brings something different," said Nathan Macintosh. "That's what keeps stand-up exciting. I'm looking forward to getting back to Portland and Seattle and seeing what happens."

 The Pacific Northwest has become one of Macintosh's favorite touring regions, with audiences that appreciate his conversational delivery and carefully crafted writing.
