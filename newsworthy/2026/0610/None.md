# HR.com Names Winner of the 2026 Best Rewards and Recognition Platform Award

HR.com is pleased to recognize organizations advancing employee engagement and workplace culture through effective rewards and recognition platforms. As part of this year’s HR Awards, HR.com is proud to announce Awardco as the winner of the Best Rewards and Recognition Platform Award, recognizing its excellence in helping employers celebrate employee contributions, strengthen appreciation efforts, and drive higher engagement, satisfaction, and retention across the workforce.

 Best Rewards and Recognition Platform Award: Awardco

 Awardco helps HR teams boost engagement, motivation, and retention through automated, meaningful recognition. By connecting appreciation to company values and performance goals, Awardco delivers measurable insights, global reward flexibility, and seamless HRIS integration - making recognition scalable, impactful, and easy to manage.

 “We’re honored to receive this award from HR.com,” said Steve Sonnenberg, Co-Founder and CEO of Awardco. “It’s a reflection of the passion our team brings to helping companies create moments of recognition that build connection, strengthen culture, and make work more meaningful.”

 “We’re pleased to recognize this achievement in HR,” said Debbie McGrath, CEO of HR.com. “Awardco has elevated employee recognition by enabling organizations to deliver meaningful appreciation at scale. Their innovation and focus on measurable workforce impact continue to advance best practices across the HR industry.”
