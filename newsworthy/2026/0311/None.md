# Discover How NYC Bodegas Are Turning Into Media Hubs

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Mar 11, 2026 @ 4:50 PM Central — Bodegas TV today announced the launch of a Smart TV streaming network designed specifically for New York City bodegas, combining local news, entertainment programming, and community-driven advertising on screens inside neighborhood stores.

 The platform brings together local reporters, creators, and AI-powered content production to deliver programming tailored to the communities that bodegas serve every day.

 The initiative aims to install screens in 500 bodegas by 2026, with a long-term goal of reaching 2,000 stores across New York within the next three years.

 Bodegas TV is designed to turn neighborhood stores into community media hubs, delivering hyperlocal news and entertainment while creating new revenue opportunities for store owners.

 A New Model for Local Media Inside Neighborhood Stores Bodegas TV combines AI-powered content production with local storytelling to create a continuous streaming channel designed for in-store viewing.

 The programming mix includes:

 50% Local News Content

 * Breaking local news
* Community stories
* Updates relevant to neighborhood residents
* Content produced by local reporters and creators

 50% Entertainment Programming

 * Short-form entertainment
* AI-generated content
* Micro-dramas and creative video series
* Community features and cultural programming

 This combination allows stores to provide engaging content for customers while supporting local storytelling and journalism.

 Built for Bodega Owners Bodegas TV is designed to be simple and completely hands-off for store owners.

 Hardware Installation

 * Smart TV hardware provided at no cost to the retailer

 Advertising Revenue

 * Programmatic advertising
* Direct-sold local advertising

 Passive Income Model

 * Store owners participate in a revenue-sharing system
* Advertising revenue generates passive income for participating bodegas
* No operational work required by the store

 All installation, technical maintenance, and ongoing support are handled by the Bodegas TV team.

 Empowering Local Creators and Community Journalism Bodegas TV also creates opportunities for local reporters, creators, and storytellers to produce content that reaches audiences directly inside their communities.

 By combining AI-assisted production tools with local journalism, the platform dramatically reduces the cost of producing and distributing video news.

 The result is a scalable model that supports community-driven storytelling while expanding access to local information.

 Expanding Across New York The network will begin its rollout in New York City bodegas, with a target of 500 participating stores by 2026 and expansion to 2,000 bodegas across the region within three years.

 Each participating store becomes part of a growing network delivering hyperlocal content, community news, and culturally relevant entertainment to customers throughout the day.

 About Bodegas TV Bodegas TV is a Smart TV streaming network designed for neighborhood bodegas and small businesses. The platform combines AI-powered content production, local reporting, entertainment programming, and digital advertising to create a new media channel inside community stores.

 By providing hardware at no cost and offering a revenue-sharing model, Bodegas TV enables store owners to generate passive income while supporting local news and community storytelling.
