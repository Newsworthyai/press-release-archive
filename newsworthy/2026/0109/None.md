# Nathan Macintosh Returns to Zanies Chicago for Five-Show Run January 15–17

Chicago, IL (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Jan 9, 2026 @ 8:00 AM Pacific — Acclaimed stand-up comedian Nathan Macintosh returns to Zanies for a five-show engagement from January 15 through January 17. With just 145 seats per show, fans are encouraged to secure tickets early ahead of what is expected to be a high-demand weekend.

 Macintosh is a Juno Award–nominated comedian whose stand-up has generated more than 40 million views online. Known for sharp, clean comedy rooted in real-life storytelling, his work resonates with both casual comedy fans and dedicated stand-up audiences. His recent hour-long special, Down With Tech, continues to gain traction through widely shared clips and strong word of mouth.

 The Chicago dates follow a national tour that includes recent sold-out shows in San Diego and New Jersey. This appearance marks Macintosh’s return to Zanies Chicago, where he has performed multiple times and built a loyal local following. The January run is limited to one weekend, with no additional Chicago dates scheduled.

 Show Details:

 Nathan Macintosh performs five shows at Zanies Chicago from January 15–17.

 Tickets are available for

 * Thursday, January 15 at 7:00 PM,
* Friday, January 16 at 7:00 PM,
* Friday, January 16 at 9:15 PM,
* Saturday, January 17 at 7:00 PM, and
* Saturday, January 17 at 9:15 PM. Seating is limited to 145 guests per show.

 VenueZanies Chicago1548 N Wells StChicago, IL

 Fans can preview Macintosh’s stand-up through clips from his Down With Tech special and find additional tour information at https://nathanmacintosh.com.

 In addition to his stand-up work, Macintosh is a familiar voice to comedy fans through podcast appearances on Are You Garbage, Stav’s Podcast, and Being Ian With Jordan. His growing national profile continues to translate into packed rooms across North America.

 For Chicago comedy fans looking for a smart, laugh-heavy night out, this weekend at Zanies offers a rare chance to catch Nathan Macintosh in an intimate club setting. Hurry before it sells out.
