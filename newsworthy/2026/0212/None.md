# Blimburn Seeds Announces Brand Partnership with Cultivation Icon Kyle Kushman

New York, NY (Newsworthy.ai) Wednesday Feb 11, 2026 @ 7:45 PM Eastern — Blimburn Seeds, a globally recognized cannabis seed bank known for its premium genetics and international footprint, today announced a new brand partnership with renowned cultivator, educator, and cannabis advocate Kyle Kushman.

 The partnership brings together Blimburn’s decades-long expertise in genetics and breeding with Kushman’s deep-rooted knowledge of cultivation, plant health, and cannabis education. Together, they aim to elevate grower education, highlight the importance of strong genetics, and support cultivators at every stage of their journey, from home growers to large-scale operators.

 As part of the collaboration, Kyle Kushman will work closely with Blimburn to spotlight genetics-driven cultivation practices, contribute to educational content, and engage directly with the global grower community through events, media, and digital storytelling.

 Kyle represents everything we value in this industry, respect for the plant, dedication to education, and an unwavering commitment to quality,” said Sergio Martinez, CEO of Blimburn Seeds. “This partnership is about more than a name; it’s about aligning with someone who understands that great cultivation starts with great genetics. Together, we’re focused on empowering growers with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed.

 Kushman, widely recognized for his decades of hands-on experience and his role in advancing cultivation best practices, sees the partnership as a natural alignment rooted in shared philosophy.

 I’ve spent my life teaching growers that the foundation of every successful harvest is the genetics you start with,” said Kyle Kushman. “Blimburn understands that at a fundamental level. Their commitment to quality, consistency, and global education makes this partnership a powerful opportunity to help growers grow better cannabis, no matter where they are.

 The collaboration will include joint educational initiatives, content series, appearances at industry events, and ongoing efforts to bring practical, genetics-first cultivation insights to growers worldwide.

 The partnership marks another step in Blimburn Seeds’ mission to lead with education, innovation, and authenticity while strengthening connections across the global cannabis community.

 About [Blimburn Seeds](https://blimburnseeds.com/) Founded in Barcelona, Spain in 2002, Blimburn Seeds is a pioneer in global cannabis seed and clone distribution with a mission to provide the highest-quality seeds for both commercial and home growers worldwide. With a focus on education and sustainability, Blimburn Seeds empowers growers to cultivate cannabis responsibly and successfully. For more information about Blimburn Seeds and its partnership with Steve DeAngelo, please visit www.blimburnseeds.com.

 About Kyle Kushman Kyle Kushman is a cannabis cultivator, educator, and longtime advocate known for his contributions to modern cultivation practices and grower education worldwide.
