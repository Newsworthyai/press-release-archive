# APEX Adventure Plex Coming Soon to Winnipeg: Open Late March

Winnipeg, MB, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Saturday Feb 21, 2026 @ 9:00 AM Canada/Central — APEX Adventure Plex, a Canada-owned and operated indoor trampoline park and active entertainment destination, is coming soon to Winnipeg. Located at 1150 Nairn Ave #12, Winnipeg, MB R2L 0Y5, the new facility is expected to open late March 2026, bringing a one-of-a-kind mix of high-energy attractions, group experiences, and family-friendly fun to the city.

 Built for all ages and designed to keep people moving, APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg will be a go-to destination for birthday parties, team windups, graduation parties, Christmas parties and corporate bookings.

 “Winnipeg has a strong appetite for fun, active experiences, and we’re excited to bring APEX Adventure Plex to the community,” said Luke Shaheen, Managing Partner of APEX Adventure Plex and a Board Member of the International Association of Trampoline Parks (IATP). “We’ve designed this park to be a true destination; an experience families can count on for unforgettable memories and groups can rely on for celebrations that actually feel exciting.”

 What makes APEX Adventure Plex different in Winnipeg Unlike traditional amusement facilities, APEX Adventure Plex is built around active entertainment—experiences that feel like play, but get people moving in a big way. The Winnipeg location is designed to serve multiple audiences at once:

 * Kids burn off energy in a safe, purpose-built environment
* Teens get a high-energy place to hang out and stay active
* Adults can jump in, play with kids and make real memories
* Parents can relax while the whole group is entertained

 With attractions engineered for engagement, APEX aims to deliver a better group experience than typical “walk-around” entertainment options, more movement, more interaction and more reasons to come back.

 Key features coming to APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg The Winnipeg park will include a stacked lineup of attractions, including:

 * The Longest Trampoline in the World
* Bubble Soccer
* Ninja Warrior Course
* Super Trampolines
* Redemption Arcade
* And much more

 Grand opening timing and location APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg is expected to open late March 2026 at:

 APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg1150 Nairn Ave #12Winnipeg, MB R2L 0Y5

 More details on opening events, booking availability and early access announcements will be shared soon.

 About APEX Adventure Plex APEX Adventure Plex is a Canada-owned trampoline park and active entertainment brand built for families, groups and guests of all ages. With locations designed to deliver high-energy attractions, memorable celebrations and a one-of-a-kind indoor experience, APEX continues expanding across Canada, bringing premium active entertainment to communities looking for something better than ordinary amusement.
