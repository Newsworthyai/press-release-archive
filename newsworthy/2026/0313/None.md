# APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg - Grand Opening and Sneak Peek

Winnipeg, MB, Canada (Newsworthy.ai) Friday Mar 13, 2026 @ 8:15 AM Central — APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg is officially opening its doors on Thursday, March 26, welcoming guests into 40,000 sq. ft. of indoor adventure at 1150 Nairn Ave #12. The park will open at 4:00 PM and close at 9:00 PM on grand opening day.

 At the heart of APEX Winnipeg is a truly headline-worthy feature: the world’s longest trampoline, stretching an incredible 192 feet end-to-end. On grand opening day, an official adjudicator will be on-site to certify the record attempt, turning the moment into a live, once-in-a-lifetime milestone for Winnipeg. The big question is simple: who will be the first to jump across the record-breaking 192’ trampoline? The new length surpasses the widely reported benchmark of roughly 170 feet from a trampoline-track installation in Russia.

 To celebrate the grand opening, APEX Winnipeg will also run a grand opening giveaway for attendees: guests who enter will have the chance to win 1-year VIP passes to APEX.

 From March 26 through April 5, APEX Winnipeg will operate on a walk-in only basis, with admission available first come, first served. During this initial launch window, online bookings will not be available.

 About APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg APEX Adventure Plex Winnipeg is an indoor adventure park built for high-energy fun, movement and unforgettable experiences.
