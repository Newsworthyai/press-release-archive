# FastBlast Founder William King Shares Nutrition Philosophy and Brand Journey in Citybiz Q&amp;A

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday May 5, 2026 @ 6:00 AM Eastern — 

 FastBlast today announced that its founder, William King, has been featured in a Q&A interview with Citybiz, where he discusses the personal experiences that led to the creation of the brand, as well as his philosophy on nutrition, convenience, and long-term wellness.

 Citybiz is a digital publisher and news aggregator focused on business, commerce, and economic activity across 20 major U.S. markets, serving C-level executives, entrepreneurs, and senior managers through regional news coverage, CEO interviews, and industry-focused content.

 In the interview, King shares how a personal health crisis and years of research into nutrition and lifestyle changes shaped his approach to building FastBlast. His journey, which included overcoming the side effects of prescribed medications and exploring the impact of diet on overall health, became the foundation for the company’s mission to simplify access to nutrient-dense, whole-food nutrition.

 “FastBlast was really born out of my own experience trying to rebuild my health,” said King. “I realized that a lot of people want to eat better, but they don’t have the time or consistency to prepare high-quality meals every day. We wanted to create something that removes that barrier.”

 Insights from Global Longevity and Everyday Nutrition During the Q&A, King also discusses his research into global longevity regions, where populations tend to live longer, healthier lives. He highlights the importance of consistent dietary patterns, whole foods, and nutrient absorption, particularly the role of gut health and fermented nutrients in supporting overall wellness.

 These insights directly influenced the development of FastBlast products, which are designed to combine organic fruits, vegetables, superfoods, and fermented nutrients in a convenient, easy-to-use format.

 A Focus on Simplicity and Consistency King emphasizes that one of the biggest challenges consumers face is maintaining consistency with healthy eating habits. FastBlast addresses this by offering products such as its Banana Berry Smoothie pouches and Daily Essentials blend, both designed to provide balanced nutrition in a portable, accessible format.

 The Banana Berry Smoothie is a vegan product made with real fruit and no added sugar, formulated to deliver a combination of carbohydrates, protein, healthy fats, and fiber. The inclusion of fermented nutrients is intended to support digestion and nutrient absorption, helping consumers get more out of what they consume.

 Expanding Availability Across the U.S. In the interview, King also notes that FastBlast has expanded its availability across major online platforms, including Amazon and Walmart.com, making the brand more accessible to consumers nationwide.

 The company continues to focus on expanding distribution while maintaining its core commitment to organic, whole-food ingredients and simplified nutrition.

 A Practical Approach to Better Health King concludes the interview by encouraging consumers to focus on small, consistent improvements rather than drastic lifestyle changes, reinforcing the brand’s philosophy that sustainable habits are key to long-term wellness.

 “You don’t have to completely overhaul your life to start making better choices,” King said. “Small, consistent improvements can make a big difference over time.”

 About [FastBlast](https://fastblast.com) FastBlast develops organic, whole-food nutrition products designed to support everyday health and wellness. Inspired by research into global longevity regions where people regularly consume superfoods, probiotics, and fermented foods, FastBlast products are formulated to deliver nutrient-dense ingredients in convenient formats that fit modern lifestyles.

 Founded by entrepreneur William King, FastBlast focuses on combining organic fruits, vegetables, and fermented nutrients to help make healthy nutrition simple and accessible for busy families and individuals. The company’s products emphasize whole-food ingredients, natural flavors, and no added sugar.
