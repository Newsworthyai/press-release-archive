# Qiari.ai Launches Personalized Remote Quantum Energy Sessions for Mobile and Desktop

Austin, TX (Newsworthy.ai) Tuesday May 12, 2026 @ 3:00 PM Pacific — Qiari.ai officially announces the launch of its personalized remote quantum energy platform, designed to deliver customized frequency sessions to users through mobile and desktop devices.

 Built for individuals seeking flexible access to frequency wellness, Qiari.ai allows users to experience remote quantum energy sessions on demand, without specialized equipment or scheduled in-person appointments. The platform delivers personalized sessions tailored to each user's preferences, accessible from virtually anywhere in the world.

 By removing the limitations of traditional setups, Qiari.ai makes remote quantum energy experiences part of an everyday wellness routine. The platform is designed to support users who want frequency-based experiences integrated seamlessly into modern life.

 Qiari.ai is accessible across iOS, Android, and desktop browsers, giving users flexibility to start a session at home, while traveling, or during short breaks throughout the day. The remote-first architecture also makes the platform suitable for individuals, couples, and entire households on a single account.

 With growing global interest in personalized wellness technologies, Qiari.ai represents a new direction for how frequency-based experiences can be delivered at scale through digital infrastructure.

 "People want wellness experiences that fit into modern life, not the other way around," said David Wong, Founder of Qiari.ai. "Qiari.ai was built so personalized remote quantum energy sessions are available anytime, from virtually anywhere."

 The launch reflects Qiari.ai's continued focus on expanding access to innovative wellness technologies through user-friendly digital platforms.

 Users can now access Qiari.ai across mobile and desktop devices.
