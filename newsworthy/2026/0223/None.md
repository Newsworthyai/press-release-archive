# Santos Muscle Nutrition: European Performance Supplement Brand Announces Expansion into the United States

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Feb 23, 2026 @ 12:57 PM America/Phoenix — Santos Muscle Nutrition, a performance-focused sports supplement company headquartered in the Netherlands, has announced plans to expand into the United States market.

 The brand focuses on foundational fitness supplementation designed to support training consistency, recovery routines, and structured nutrition programs. The U.S. introduction will begin with four core products — T-Booster, Vitaminpack, Creatine, and Whey Protein — with additional products expected to follow as the company builds its presence.

 The company’s strategy centers on establishing a complete performance nutrition system rather than launching a single flagship item, introducing supplements intended to complement exercise habits, nutrition planning, and evolving fitness trends.

 Foundational Product Line for U.S. Launch The initial rollout will include:

 * T-Booster — capsule-based formulation featuring botanical extracts and minerals
* Vitaminpack — powdered daily micronutrient blend
* Creatine — pure creatine monohydrate performance supplement
* Whey Protein — fast-absorbing protein powder for post-training nutrition

 The company plans to expand its product catalog continuously, introducing additional supplements based on consumer demand and training trends.

 Phased Market Entry Strategy The U.S. rollout will follow a staged availability approach beginning with online distribution channels before expanding into broader retail opportunities. This phased strategy allows the brand to build recognition and gather consumer feedback prior to wider placement.

 “Our goal is to create a structured foundation for athletes and active consumers,” said Mike de Groot, founder of Santos Muscle Nutrition said. “We want products that work together as part of a routine — not isolated supplements.”

 Long-Term Performance Focus Santos Muscle Nutrition aims to develop an evolving supplement ecosystem with ongoing product additions aligned to training needs and fitness trends.

 The expansion marks the company’s first major entry outside Europe as it introduces its approach to performance nutrition to American consumers.

 About [Santos Muscle Nutrition](https://santosmusclenutrition.nl/) Santos Muscle Nutrition is a Netherlands-based sports nutrition company focused on developing supplements intended to support structured fitness routines, muscle development, and consistent training habits. The company offers a growing range of products and plans continuous expansion based on consumer interest and performance trends.
