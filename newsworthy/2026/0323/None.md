# Santos Muscle Nutrition Preparing for OneLavi Launch in the United States

Boca Raton, Florida (Newsworthy.ai) Monday Mar 23, 2026 @ 4:00 AM Eastern — 

 Santos Muscle Nutrition, the Netherlands-based sports nutrition brand focused on muscle growth and athletic performance, announced plans to expand its U.S. presence through OneLavi, a growing e-commerce platform connecting emerging global brands with American consumers.

 The upcoming OneLavi availability represents another step in Santos Muscle Nutrition’s broader international growth strategy, following its previously announced plans to enter the U.S. market through major online platforms.

 “Our goal has always been to build a brand that supports real athletes and real training,” said Mike de Groot, Founder of Santos Muscle Nutrition. “Launching on OneLavi gives us the opportunity to introduce Santos to a new audience in the United States while continuing to grow globally.”

 Expanding Through Emerging E-Commerce Platforms As the digital retail landscape evolves, platforms like OneLavi are creating new opportunities for international brands to establish a presence in the U.S. market. By offering curated product selections and streamlined access for consumers, these platforms provide an alternative path to market entry for emerging wellness and performance brands.

 Santos Muscle Nutrition’s upcoming launch on OneLavi is designed to help build brand awareness and accessibility among U.S. consumers seeking performance-focused supplements.

 A European Brand Built for Performance Founded in the Netherlands, Santos Muscle Nutrition develops supplements designed to support:

 * Muscle growth and recovery
* Strength and training performance
* Energy and endurance during workouts
* Daily nutrition for active lifestyles

 The company continues to expand its product lineup with a focus on practical, performance-driven formulations tailored to athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

 “We’re focused on building a complete range of supplements that support every stage of training,” de Groot added. “This is just the beginning of our expansion into the U.S.”

 Continuing U.S. Expansion The OneLavi launch represents an important step in Santos Muscle Nutrition’s entry into the U.S. market, with additional platform availability and product announcements expected in the coming months.

 As the brand builds momentum internationally, Santos Muscle Nutrition aims to establish itself as a trusted name in performance nutrition for athletes seeking quality, consistency, and results.

 About [Santos Muscle Nutrition](https://santosmusclenutrition.nl/) Santos Muscle Nutrition is a Netherlands-based sports nutrition company focused on developing supplements intended to support structured fitness routines, muscle development, and consistent training habits. The company offers a growing range of products and plans continuous expansion based on consumer interest and performance trends.
