# Olé All Day! Celebrate the FIFA Club World Cup Finals at Hilton West Palm Beach

Fútbol fans looking for the perfect FIFA Club World Cup Finals watch party on Sunday, July 13, can cheer on their favorite teams at Hilton West Palm Beach — no tickets required.

 Hilton’s al fresco eatery, La Playa will showcase a FIFA Club World Cup menu packed with global flavors, including Steak Sliders ($24) with smoked gouda and horseradish cream, Tuna Tostada ($24) with guacamole and watermelon radish, and blackened Grouper Tacos ($18) with avocado and creamy tomatillo-grilled pineapple salsa. Wash down your feast with salud-worthy sips like the West Palm Julep ($18) loaded with bourbon, mint, lemon, and raspberry; Mint & Melon Mojito ($16) with rum, mint, and Midori liqueur garnished with cantaloupe melon balls; or the adorably Instagrammable Classic Pina Colada ($30) served in a chilled coconut.

 Family-friendly festivities and outdoor entertainment make Hilton West Palm Beach the place to be. From 1–5 PM, the resort comes alive with Lazy Sundays featuring a live DJ spinning vacation-mode beats, poolside dancing, and tiki-style food and drink specials.

 Purchase a ResortPass day pass, which includes access to Lazy Sundays and the hotel’s premium amenities, here.

 Hilton West Palm Beach is located at 600 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach, FL. For more information, call (561) 231-6000 or visit hiltonwestpalmbeach.com.
