# CyrusOne Appoints Krupal Raval as Chief Strategy Officer

DALLAS–( BUSINESS WIRE )– CyrusOne, a leading global data center developer and operator specializing in delivering cutting-edge digital infrastructure solutions, today announced that Krupal Raval has joined the company’s senior management team as Executive Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer. In this newly created position, Krupal will lead the company’s rapidly growing development program in the United States and drive strategic collaborations with hyperscale customers.

 “CyrusOne has earned an outstanding reputation in the industry for its execution and innovation, and I’m delighted to be joining this experienced and talented team,” said Raval. “I look forward to contributing my experience to further advance the company’s vision and strategic goals.”

 Prior to joining CyrusOne, Krupal served as Managing Director, xScale, at Equinix, where he led the hyperscale data center business and coordinated several multi-billion dollar joint ventures that impacted the Americas, EMEA, and APAC markets. His strategic vision and execution skills previously drove growth as Chief Financial Officer for Asia Pacific at Digital Realty.

 

“Krupal brings exceptional industry experience and proven leadership to CyrusOne,” said Eric Schwartz, CEO of CyrusOne. “With more than a dozen new data centers under construction and an aggressive expansion strategy, Krupal’s deep relationships with hyperscalers and strategic vision will be instrumental in accelerating our growth and strengthening our market position.”

 About CyrusOne

 CyrusOne is a leading global data center owner, developer, and operator, providing advanced digital infrastructure solutions worldwide. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, the company operates over 55 data centers in the United States, Europe, and Japan. CyrusOne specializes in comprehensive solutions for hyperscale and enterprise companies, enabling its customers to achieve their unique business and sustainability goals by meeting the complex requirements of AI-driven applications and service workloads. CyrusOne data centers offer unparalleled flexibility, enabling customers to modernize, simplify, and respond quickly to changing needs. CyrusOne delivers customized build-to-suit, colocation, and interconnection solutions that meet its customers’ ever-evolving digital needs. For more information, visit cyrusone.com .
