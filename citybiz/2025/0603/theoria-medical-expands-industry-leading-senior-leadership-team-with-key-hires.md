# Theoria Medical Expands Industry-Leading Senior Leadership Team with Key Hires

NOVI, Mich.–(BUSINESS WIRE)–Theoria Medical (“Theoria” or the “Company”), a leading physician-led organization transforming the delivery of care across post-acute and senior living settings, today announced the following appointments to its senior leadership team in support of the Company’s long-term vision to elevate standards, unify clinical operations, and achieve outcomes that matter for patients, partners, and payers.

 * Brendan McNamara has been appointed Chief Operating Officer. He has a distinguished track record of operational leadership across multi-state care delivery systems, most recently serving as Regional President at Main Street Health and previously as CEO of Sound Telemedicine. A former U.S. Army Ranger, he brings a disciplined, team-oriented leadership approach and deep experience scaling high-performing organizations and improving care outcomes.

 * Svetlana Vinokur has been appointed Chief Financial Officer. She has extensive expertise leading financial organizations in FP&A, capital strategy, and corporate development, having led financial transformation initiatives across major healthcare and consumer organizations including VillageMD and NielsenIQ.

 * Manny Raposo has been appointed Chief Growth Officer. He joins following a highly successful tenure as Chief Sales Officer at PharmScript, where he built a high-performing commercial organization and helped drive sustained growth and market expansion.

 * Kevin Murphy has been appointed Chief of Value-Based Care. As a recognized leader in senior care innovation, he has extensive experience launching and managing value-based care models across ACOs, direct contracting entities, and in-home care organizations. He was appointed to the Theoria Board of Directors in January 2025, where he has since brought valuable clinical and operational perspectives that have helped to shape and strengthen Theoria’s commitment to high-quality, value-based care delivery.

 * Marcia Siguenza has been appointed Chief Human Resources Officer. She has more than two decades of experience in physician practice management and HR leadership, having held prior roles as Chief People Officer and CHRO across multi-state medical groups.

 Setting a New Standard in Post-Acute Care

 This established team brings deep industry expertise, operational precision, and proven leadership experience to support Theoria’s mission of building the most comprehensive and innovative post-acute care delivery platform.

 With an expanded executive team, Theoria is positioned to accelerate its mission of unifying care across the post-acute patient journey from hospital discharge, skilled nursing, and assisted living through to the home-based setting. Leveraging its physician-led model and proprietary technology infrastructure, Theoria is committed to delivering superior health outcomes to a complex patient population through enhanced care coordination and patient satisfaction.

 “Within a fragmented and often reactive healthcare landscape, Theoria is focused on building a differentiated approach to care across the post-acute continuum. Our model is rooted first and foremost in high-touch patient care, underpinned by evidenced-based and high-quality clinical care that leads to better outcomes for our patients,” said Dr. Justin Di Rezze, CEO of Theoria Medical. “This team reflects the caliber of leadership required to execute on our long-term vision of delivering integrated, value-based care solutions at scale. Together, we will continue to focus on innovation and redefining what’s possible in post-acute care, while prioritizing investments in our team and organizational infrastructure.”

 About Theoria Medical

 Theoria Medical is a tech-enabled management service organization supporting a comprehensive medical group practice dedicated to serving patients across the care continuum with an emphasis on post-acute care and primary care. Theoria serves facilities across the United States with a multitude of services to improve the quality of care delivered, refine facility processes, and enhance critical clinical relationships. Theoria’s affiliated medical groups offer a broad scope of services including multispecialty physician services, telemedicine, chronic care management and remote patient monitoring.
