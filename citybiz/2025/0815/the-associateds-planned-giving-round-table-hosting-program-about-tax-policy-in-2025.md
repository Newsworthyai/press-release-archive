# The Associated’s Planned Giving Round Table Hosting Program About Tax Policy in 2025

On September 9th, The Associated: Jewish Federation of Baltimore’s Planned Giving Round Table (PGRT) will host an informative program on Tax Policy in 2025 with featured Speaker, Aharon Friedman, Director and Senior Tax Counsel with Federal Policy Group. Learn more about this program and how PGRT can help you network, develop technical skills and learn from a wide array of experts.
