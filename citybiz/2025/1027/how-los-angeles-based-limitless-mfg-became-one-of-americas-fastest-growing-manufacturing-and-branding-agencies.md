# How Los Angeles-Based Limitless MFG Became One of America’s Fastest-Growing Manufacturing and Branding Agencies

In the heart of Los Angeles, where creativity and business collide, Limitless MFG has built one of the most dynamic full-service manufacturing and branding agencies in the country. Founded by Miller McCoy, Stephen Mangini, and Brett Schwers, the company has been recognized by Inc. Magazine as one of the fastest-growing private companies in America, ranking #129 nationwide on the 2025 Inc. 5000 list. Limitless MFG also secured #6 in U.S. manufacturing, #22 in California, and #8 in Los Angeles, solidifying its reputation as a national leader in creative manufacturing.

 A Full-Service Manufacturing and Branding Agency in Los Angeles

 What began as an in-house operation has evolved into a vertically integrated agency that brings creative vision to life. Limitless MFG offers a full range of services including product design, manufacturing, fulfillment, e-commerce, and tour merchandising, allowing brands and creators to move from concept to market quickly and efficiently. By combining creative direction, technical expertise, and operational precision, the company provides clients with a single, seamless production experience from start to finish.

 “From day one, our mission has been to redefine how brands in entertainment, fashion, and lifestyle industries bring products to market,” said Miller McCoy, CEO of Limitless MFG. “This recognition by Inc. is proof of what’s possible when you combine a talented team, loyal clients, and relentless focus on quality and execution.”

 How Limitless MFG Scaled From Los Angeles Startup to National Manufacturing Leader

 Limitless MFG’s 2,911 percent growth over three years is a reflection of both its operational excellence and deep understanding of what modern brands need to succeed. The company serves clients who range from global touring artists and viral influencers to established consumer brands, all seeking a partner that can handle creative, production, and logistics under one roof.

 Among their most notable partners and clients are Nordstrom Rack, B.B. Simon, Stevenson Ranch, and Gin & Juice by Dre and Snoop. These collaborations reflect Limitless MFG’s ability to operate across retail, fashion, and entertainment while maintaining the same level of quality and innovation for each.

 Meet the Founders Behind One of America’s Fastest-Growing Manufacturing Companies

 The company’s rapid rise is driven by three co-founders with complementary expertise. Miller McCoy, as CEO, leads the strategic vision and overall growth. Stephen Mangini focuses on creative development and brand strategy, ensuring each project captures cultural relevance and authenticity. Brett Schwers manages production systems, logistics, and fulfillment, maintaining the level of precision that keeps Limitless MFG running at scale.

 Together, they have created a model that blends design, production, and brand-building into one ecosystem that supports creators and companies alike.

 The Future of Lifestyle Manufacturing and Merch Fulfillment

 With a growing global production network and an expanding client base, Limitless MFG continues to set new standards for how brands create and distribute products. Its Los Angeles headquarters keeps the company connected to the heart of entertainment and fashion while serving clients around the world.

 The recognition from Inc. marks more than just a milestone; it represents the rise of a new kind of agency that understands both the artistry and infrastructure behind brand success. As McCoy puts it, “Limitless isn’t just our name. It’s the mindset that drives everything we do.”
