# NeighborWorks Housing Solutions Receives $40,000 Donation from Wells Fargo Foundation

Foundation named Neighborhood Sponsor for November 6th Gala

 NeighborWorks Housing Solutions (NHS) is grateful to the Wells Fargo Foundation for a recent donation in the amount of $40,000.

 The donation positions the Foundation as the Neighborhood Sponsor of the NHS “Opening Doors, Changing Lives” Gala on November 6th at Granite Links Golf Club in Quincy. The annual event is the housing service provider’s signature fundraiser, with proceeds supporting the programs and people NHS serves.

 The Wells Fargo Foundation is the philanthropic arm of Wells Fargo, focused on creating positive, lasting change to strengthen communities. Funding is prioritized to activities and programs that have a broad reach and support the needs of underserved communities and advance racial equity.

 “We cannot overstate how thankful we are to the Wells Fargo Foundation not only for this generous donation, but also for its commitment to our local communities and appreciation of the services NHS provides,” said Robert Corley, CEO of NeighborWorks Housing Solutions. “For many years, the Foundation has championed our mission to develop safe, affordable housing and foster vibrant, resilient communities.”

 About NeighborWorks® Housing Solutions

 NeighborWorks® Housing Solutions is the leading housing service provider in southern Massachusetts. NHS has more than 70 years of combined experience in the community development field and provides services to more than 20,000 households in over 75 cities and towns across the region. In addition to building and managing nearly 900 units of housing for veterans, homeless families and others in need, the agency offers a wide range of housing services, including foreclosure prevention counseling, home rehabilitation loans and grants, financial coaching and first-time homebuyer workshops.
