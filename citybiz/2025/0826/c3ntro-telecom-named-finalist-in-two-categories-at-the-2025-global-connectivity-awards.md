# C3ntro Telecom Named Finalist in Two Categories at the 2025 Global Connectivity Awards

C3ntro Telecom (C3ntro), a leader in global telecommunications and fiber infrastructure provider, today announced that it has been shortlisted for this year’s Global Connectivity Awards in two categories. The company has been included as a finalist for best Latin American/Caribbean Connectivity Provider as well as project of the Year – Subsea, Terrestrial, Satellite and/or Wireless for its revolutionary Tikva Project. This recognition reflects C3ntro’s ongoing commitment to delivering innovative digital infrastructure and services across Latin America and beyond.

 “We’re extremely proud to have been recognized in two highly competitive categories among such esteemed global peers,” said Simon Masri, President & CEO of C3ntro Telecom. “Making the shortlist for both categories is a testament to our team’s hard work, vision, and relentless pursuit of innovative connectivity solutions.”

 The Global Connectivity Awards, now in their 21st year, celebrate the standout achievements and breakthroughs defining the digital infrastructure landscape. In the Best Latin American/Caribbean Connectivity Provider category, outstanding performance in the Latin American and/or Caribbean connectivity markets are recognized. Project of the Year – Subsea, Terrestrial, Satellite and/or Wireless is a special recognition category reserved for the most innovative and impactful digital infrastructure project of the year, with no limit to the size, scope, or location of the project. This nomination goes to world-changing initiatives that have environmental, societal, state and government implications, like the Tikva Project. This project establishes a state-of-the-art, 2,500-kilometer fiber optic network between Phoenix, Arizona, and Querétaro, Mexico, enhancing cross-border connectivity and marketing the first project of its kind in over 25 years. The award ceremony will be held on October 22nd at Indigo at The O2 in London.

 About C3ntro Telecom

 C3ntro Telecom operates a state-of-the-art carrier-grade fiber network across Mexico’s major cities, offering dark fiber and wavelength services for low-latency, high-performance data center interconnectivity. Designed for hyperscalers, carriers, and cloud service providers, our AI enabled network features reliable, redundant infrastructure to support current and future needs. With over 30 years of experience, we also provide voice, SMS, virtual numbers, and traffic management services, making us a trusted partner for carriers, CPaaS providers, and enterprises.
