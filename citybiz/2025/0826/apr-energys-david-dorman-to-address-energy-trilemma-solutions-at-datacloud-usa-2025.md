# APR Energy’s David Dorman to Address Energy Trilemma Solutions at Datacloud USA 2025

APR Energy, a global leader in specialized energy solutions, will be participating in Datacloud USA, taking place September 16-17, 2025 in Dallas, Texas. David Dorman, Director of Commercial Operations at APR Energy, will join industry experts to share insights on how innovative energy strategies are powering data center reliability, affordability, and stability in today’s rapidly evolving digital landscape.

 Datacloud USA is the leading event for data center, cloud and digital infrastructure leaders. Attendees can expect dynamic discussions and networking with executives and innovators across every sector of the North American digital infrastructure market. Dorman will participate in the panel, “Solving the energy trilemma: availability, affordability, stability”, which will examine how data centers can secure reliable, cost-effective power in a climate of rising energy demands. Industry leaders will discuss balancing availability, affordability, and stability to support digital infrastructure growth and enable new AI-driven workloads.

 WHAT: “Solving the energy trilemma: availability, affordability, stability”

 WHERE: Datacloud USA Stage, Austin Marriott Downtown, Austin, Texas

 WHEN: Tuesday, September 16, 2025, 2:30 to 3:15 p.m. CT

 WHO: Panelists:

 * David Dorman, Director of Commercial Operations, APR Energy
* Bob Kinscherf, Vice President, National Accounts, Constellation
* John Chesser, Chief Executive Officer, Quantica Infrastructure
* Ihab Chaaban, Data Centers and Hydrogen Commercialization Director, GE Vernova
* Thomas Seeber, Managing Director, Data Center Power Solutions, INNIO Jenbacher

 Chairperson:

 * Meghan Griffiths, Partner, Austin, Jackson Walker

 To learn more about APR Energy, visit: www.aprenergy.com

 For more information on Datacloud USA, visit: www.datacloud-usa.com

 About APR Energy

 APR Energy, based in Jacksonville, Florida, provides rapidly deployable mobile power solutions to utility and data center operators, offering emergency, bridging, and permanent energy options. For more than 20 years, APR Energy has partnered with customers around the world to deliver fast, reliable power using mobile gas turbines. The company creates unique value by executing large-scale projects in weeks or months—far faster than the 2–5 years typically required to construct permanent infrastructure. For more information, please visit www.aprenergy.com.
