# Family Trust Federal Credit Union Named Best Credit Union in South Carolina By Forbes

Continuing its recognition as an award-winning financial institution, Family Trust Federal Credit Union (Family Trust) announced that it has been named the Best-In-State Credit Union in South Carolina by Forbes. The recognition accompanies the credit union’s position on Newsweek’s 2025 list of America’s Best Regional Banks and Credit Unions and its top (5-star) rating from BauerFinancial for the 28th consecutive quarter.

 To identify the best credit unions in each state, Forbes partnered with Statista to survey 26,000 U.S. consumers on financial institutions based on trust, customer service, digital tools, financial advice and fee transparency. Additionally, Forbes analyzed over 500,000 online reviews from February 2022 through March 2025. The survey results accounted for 80% of each company’s score, and the reviews comprised 20%.

 “It’s an incredible honor to be one of 222 credit unions nationwide recognized for our commitment to serving members with excellence, care and integrity,” said Ryan Harvey, president and CEO of Family Trust. “This achievement directly reflects our team, whose dedication and compassion make all the difference for our members and community.”

 Family Trust serves over 55,000 members across seven locations throughout York County and one in Greenville County, SC. To learn more about Family Trust and its offerings, visit www.familytrust.org.

 About Family Trust Federal Credit Union

 Family Trust Federal Credit Union has been in business since 1957. With over 55,000 members and eight branches, its mission is to help people achieve all their financial goals. Family Trust has received the top (five-star) rating for financial strength and stability from Bauer Financial for 28 consecutive quarters. Family Trust offers personal checking, savings, investments, loans, credit cards, and mortgages.
