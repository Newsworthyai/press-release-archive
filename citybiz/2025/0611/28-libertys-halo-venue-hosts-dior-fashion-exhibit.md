# 28 Liberty’s Halo Venue Hosts Dior Fashion Exhibit

Halo, the new event venue at 28 Liberty Street, hosted a UBS House of Craft x Dior exhibition showcasing the work of all seven of Dior’s creative directors across eight decades. Curated by Carine Roitfeld and photographed by Brigitte Niedermair, the exhibition highlighted each designer’s unique perspectives and their influence on couture fashion.
