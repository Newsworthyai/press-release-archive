# Ross Dress for Less to Expand in the Detroit Area

Dublin, California, October 7, 2025 – Today, Ross Stores, operator of the largest off-price retail chain in the United States, announced that four new Ross Dress for Less stores will open in the Detroit area on October 11th as part of a broader wave of new stores opening nationwide.

 “We’re excited to grow our presence in the Detroit area with the addition of four new stores across the local community.” said Richard Lietz, Executive Vice President, Property Development. “This expansion is a step forward in our efforts to reach more customers with high quality merchandise, great brands, and incredible savings. Ross continues to invest in the communities where we operate stores and as a result, the Company plans to donate a total of $20,000 to local Boys & Girls Clubs and The Carpenter Elementary School via First Book Marketplace.”

 Store addresses and hours are:

 * 23647 Greenfield Rd., Southfield, Michigan 48075  Sunday – Thursday 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Friday – Saturday 9:00 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.
* 3181 Fairlane Dr., Allen Park, Michigan 48101  Sunday 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Monday – Saturday 9:00 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.
* 8393 W. Grand River Ave., Brighton, Michigan 48116  Sunday – Saturday 9:30 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.
* 3675 Washtenaw Ave., Ann Arbor, Michigan 48104  Sunday – Saturday 9:30 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.

 In support of the grand openings, the Company will also make donations to local charities, including:

 * The Boys & Girls Clubs of Southeastern Michigan – Lloyd H. Diehl Club
* The Boys & Girls Clubs of Southeastern Michigan – Dick & Sandy Dauch Campus NFL/YET
* The Boys & Girls Club of Greater Flint – Peak Extension
* The Carpenter Elementary School via First Book Marketplace

 The four stores range from 22,000 to 23,500 square feet in size. Each new store is expected to create on average 55-60 jobs, consisting of both full- and part-time employees. After the October openings, Ross Dress for Less will have 23 stores in the state of Michigan.

 About Ross Stores, Inc. Ross Stores, Inc. is an S&P 500, Fortune 500, and Nasdaq 100 (ROST) company headquartered in Dublin, California, with fiscal 2024 revenues of $21.1 billion. The Company operated Ross Dress for Less® (“Ross”), the largest off-price apparel and home fashion chain in the United States with 1,873 locations in 44 states, the District of Columbia, Guam, and Puerto Rico as of August 2, 2025. Ross offers first-quality, in-season, name brand and designer apparel, accessories, footwear, and home fashions for the entire family at savings of 20% to 60% off department and specialty store regular prices every day. The Company also operated 360 dd’s DISCOUNTS® stores in 22 states as of August 2, 2025 that feature a more moderately-priced assortment of first-quality, in-season, name brand apparel, accessories, footwear, and home fashions for the entire family at savings of 20% to 70% off moderate department and discount store regular prices every day. Additional information is available at www.rossstores.com.
