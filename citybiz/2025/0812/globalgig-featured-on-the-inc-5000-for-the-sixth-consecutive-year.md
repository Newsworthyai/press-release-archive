# Globalgig Featured on the Inc. 5000 for the Sixth Consecutive Year

Globalgig, a leading provider of globally managed communication and connectivity solutions, has been recognized on the Inc. 5000 list of America’s fastest-growing private companies for the sixth year in a row. Ranked 3,808 with 99% revenue growth, Globalgig joins an exclusive group of U.S. businesses noted for consistent expansion and meaningful impact in today’s evolving digital landscape.

 In the past year, Globalgig has accelerated innovation across its AI-driven network management and security offerings, addressing the demands of distributed workforces and intensifying cyber threats. The company’s enhanced managed security portfolio now gives organizations access to smarter, more adaptive networks.

 The last three years have seen Globalgig broaden its service suite, optimize operations, and add top talent. Central to this progress is Orchestra, Globalgig’s next-generation agentic AI platform that goes beyond traditional SaaS. Orchestra provides intelligent inventory management and reporting across all services—serving as the digital backbone of Globalgig’s managed communication solutions.

Orchestra Insight the intelligence engine of its managed services offerings, equips enterprises with real-time, actionable network intelligence across the entire IT estate. Its contextual monitoring tools, AI-ops automation, and customizable dashboards provide unified, real-time visibility into network health, security posture, and risk—across legacy and next-generation devices, cloud environments, and security applications. These enhancements reflect Globalgig’s commitment to continuous improvement and delivering exceptional service to all its global clients.

 “This recognition reflects our team’s strong commitment to solving today’s challenges while helping enterprises prepare for the future,” said Ernest Cunningham, CEO of Globalgig. “At Globalgig, we focus on delivering outcomes that empower organizations to grow, adapt, and succeed. As technologies like agentic AI continue to reshape the business landscape, we are helping our clients build the capabilities they need to stay ahead. This includes unifying legacy and modern networks and providing real-time visibility across devices, clouds, and applications.”
