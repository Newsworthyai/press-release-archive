# Measured Among Inc. 5000 Fastest Growing Companies in U.S.

AUSTIN, Texas–(BUSINESS WIRE)–Measured, the AI-powered marketing effectiveness platform trusted by enterprise brands, today announced it has been named one of the fastest growing companies in the U.S. by Inc. 5000. This is the third time Measured has been recognized for its rapid growth driven by enterprise adoption of its combined Media Mix Modeling and incrementality testing technology.

 This year’s Inc. 5000 recognition follows a period of significant growth for Measured across retail, CPG, financial services, and many fast-growing omnichannel companies seeking a scalable and unbiased measurement solution. As marketers face mounting pressure to prove ROI and grow tired of traditional Media Mix Modeling pitfalls, Measured’s innovative methodology has become the trusted gold standard for efficiency and growth in media investments.

 “Being recognized for the third time on the Inc. 5000 list is a proud moment for the entire Measured team,” said Trevor Testwuide, Co-founder and CEO of Measured. “Our goal has always been to provide marketers with the truth about their media investments through incrementality measurement and optimization. This recognition validates that mission and the measurable impact we deliver to our enterprise customers as we continue to scale across the global marketing ecosystem. Achieving this milestone, again, speaks to the strength of our technology, the trust we’ve earned with leading brands, and the relentless dedication of our world-class team.”

 “Measured’s third appearance on the Inc. 5000 list highlights the critical role accurate measurement plays in today’s marketing landscape,” said Will Post, VP of Growth for Measured. “These are companies that not only achieved extraordinary revenue growth but that are shaping the future of their industries through innovation and execution. Measured’s inclusion on this coveted list of super-star companies underscores our consistent performance, innovation, and resilience in delivering a mission-critical platform for marketers.”

 About Measured

 Measured is the AI-powered marketing effectiveness platform trusted by enterprise brands. Since 2017, leading marketers have used our all-in-one platform to manage, test, plan, and optimize over $20 billion in full-funnel media investments. Measured’s unique combination of analyst-grade expertise and triangulated data helps marketers prove the incremental impact of their advertising and maximize ROI with unmatched ease, accuracy, and efficiency.
