# Consulting Solutions Again Ranked Among SIA’s Largest US Staffing Firms

Consulting Solutions, a nationally recognized leader in technology workforce and consulting services, announced today that it has again been named in the Largest Staffing Firms in the United States research report by Staffing Industry Analysts (SIA), the global research and advisory firm focused on staffing and workforce solutions. This year’s report highlights 224 companies that generated at least $100 million in U.S. staffing revenue in 2024. Consulting Solutions has now been named to the SIA list of largest staffing firms for seven consecutive years. Consulting Solutions also made SIA’s list of largest IT staffing firms based on 2024 revenue.

 “Earning a spot on SIA’s list for the seventh straight year reflects the continued strength of our teams and the trust our clients place in us,” said Brian Schonacher, Chief Revenue Officer of Consulting Solutions. “This accomplishment continues to reinforce our commitment to delivering outstanding value and service across every engagement.”

 Collectively, the firms listed in this year’s report generated $126.4 billion in U.S. staffing revenue in 2024, making up an estimated 68% of the market.

 Consulting Solutions’ inclusion in the Largest Staffing Firms in the United States: 2025 Update follows the company also receiving a 2025 Healthiest Companies Award at the Platinum Level from the First Coast Worksite Wellness Council (FCWWC).
