# AArete Strengthens Healthcare Consulting with Appointment of Pam Price and Gina Hedstrom as Managing Directors

AArete, a leading global management and technology consulting firm, announced today the addition of two managing directors, Pam Price and Gina Hedstrom. Price’s and Hedstrom’s appointments further position the firm’s healthcare consulting practice for continued growth and excellence.

 “With decades of healthcare experience, Pam and Gina bring invaluable perspective to AArete clients,” said AArete CEO Loren Trimble. “Their expertise enhances our ability to deliver data-driven, AI-powered solutions that address the most complex challenges facing healthcare organizations today.”

 Pam Price, managing director: Price brings more than 35 years of healthcare experience helping payers and providers achieve measurable improvements through actionable insights. She provides a rare blend of strategic vision and operational insight, having worked across the Medicare Advantage landscape in areas ranging from revenue optimization to provider engagement and financial operations. Price’s deep understanding of CMS regulations, data analytics, risk adjustment and Stars methodologies enables clients to build high-performing teams that prioritize both regulatory compliance and member engagement.

 Gina Hedstrom, RN, managing director: Hedstrom is a seasoned healthcare consultant with over two decades of experience transforming payer and provider operations across the U.S. Throughout her career, she has served as trusted advisor to many U.S. health payers, driving improvements in policy optimization, claims adjudication, utilization management, credentialing and medical record audits. As a registered nurse, she integrates clinical, technical and executive expertise to serve clients. Hedstrom will serve as managing director in AArete’s Payment Intelligence® practice, leading strategic initiatives in payment accuracy, automation, operational strategy and healthcare data innovation.

 AArete’s commitment to excellence is demonstrated by industry honors, including being globally recognized for four consecutive years as a Forbes World’s Best Management Consulting Firm, and being named one of Forbes’ America’s Best Management Consulting Firms 2025 in the category of Healthcare & Life Sciences. In addition, Modern Healthcare recognized the firm as one of the Largest Healthcare Management Consulting Firms in 2025.

About AArete

 AArete is a global management and technology consulting firm specializing in driving profitability improvement, digital transformation, and strategy & change for clients. AArete’s solutions are powered by a digital-first mindset, market intelligence and data-driven approach to deliver purposeful change, actionable insights and guaranteed results.
