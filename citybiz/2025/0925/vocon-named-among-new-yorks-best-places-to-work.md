# Vocon Named Among New York’s Best Places To Work

Vocon, a leading national architectural and interior design firm, has been named among Crain’s 2025 Best Places to Work in New York City. The firm, founded in 1987, continues to expand its presence nationally while fostering a dynamic workplace culture in its New York office.

 Crain’s New York Business partners annually with Best Companies Group to conduct surveys of thousands of employees at New York City-based companies. The program identifies and honors the top 100 organizations based on employee evaluations of workplace policies, practices, culture, and work-life balance.

 “Our people are at the center of everything we do,” said Debbie Donley, Vocon’s Chief Experience Officer. “Collaboration, creativity, and care define our culture, and we’re proud that our employees feel valued and supported.”

 Vocon ranked on this year’s list with 214 employees nationwide and 83 based in New York. The firm was recognized for its strong employee-focused policies, including flexible hybrid work options, and benefits that emphasize professional growth and well-being.

 Vocon’s voluntary turnover rate of just 13% reflects its commitment to building long-term relationships with its employees. Team-building activities, professional development, and an emphasis on work-life balance are core to Vocon’s culture.

 Since its founding, Vocon has established itself as a leader in architectural and interior design by creating an environment where employees thrive professionally and personally. The firm believes that engaged employees deliver exceptional results for clients across industries.

 About Vocon Established in 1987, Vocon is a leading national architectural and design firm specializing in corporate headquarters, mixed-use developments, multi-family residential, retail, corporate interiors, and corporate account management. With offices in New York City and Cleveland, Vocon is known for creating spaces that foster energy, creativity, and collaboration. The firm’s diverse client base includes organizations such as WeWork, PwC, KeyBank, Goodyear, and Willis Towers Watson. Services include architecture, interior design, workplace strategy, and experiential brand design. For more information, visit www.vocon.com.
