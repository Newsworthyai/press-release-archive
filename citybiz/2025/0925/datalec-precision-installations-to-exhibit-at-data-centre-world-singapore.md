# Datalec Precision Installations to Exhibit at Data Centre World Singapore

Datalec Precision Installations (DPI), a global leader in delivering world-class data centre solutions, is pleased to announce its participation as an exhibitor at Data Centre World Singapore 2025, taking place from 8 – 9 October at Marina Bay Sands, Singapore.

 With a steadfast commitment to precision, quality, and innovation, DPI continues to expand its global footprint, particularly across the APAC region, bringing cutting-edge expertise to meet the evolving demands of the data centre industry.

 DPI offers a comprehensive suite of end-to-end services, including mechanical and electrical (M&E) engineering, connectivity design, precision bespoke manufacturing, and installation services. The company also provides specialist consultancy, technical cleaning, electronic security, and managed services, all tailored to deliver scalable, high-performance infrastructure solutions that meet each client’s unique requirements.

 Peter Cole, DPI’s Executive Vice President, will be joined by other senior members of the leadership team to showcase the company’s full range of capabilities and demonstrate how DPI is enabling the future of data centre infrastructure. Their presence highlights DPI’s commitment to delivering solutions that align with both global standards and regional needs.

 Discover how Datalec’s innovative approach and expert craftsmanship are shaping the next generation of global data centres.

 WHAT: Data Centre World Singapore

 WHEN: 8-9 October 2025

 WHERE: Marina Bay Stands, Booth G115
