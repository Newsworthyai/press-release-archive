# Party Like a Rocket Man: 4th of July at Plantation Walk

Celebrate Independence Day at Plantation Walk with a full evening of free, family-friendly fun! Enjoy live music across two stages, including American Elton, The Tribute to Elton John, performing hits like “Tiny Dancer,” “Crocodile Rock,” and “Bennie and the Jets.” The party also features DJ ViVi, DJ Mack, and The Radio Band, along with street performers, a festive street market, and complimentary glow sticks and American flags (while supplies last). The fun starts at 4 p.m. and will run until 11 p.m.

 At 9 p.m., head to the 6th floor of the Plantation Walk Parking Garage for an incredible rooftop view of the City of Plantation’s fireworks show, followed by the main concert performance.

 Admission and parking are completely free. Open to the public; all ages welcome. Rain or shine. No outside food, drinks, coolers, or chairs allowed.

 Located in the heart of Broward County, Plantation Walk is a vibrant mixed-use destination offering modern urban living, shopping, dining, entertainment, and office space. With more than 30 shops and restaurants set in beautifully designed, pedestrian-friendly streetscapes, it also includes 700 luxury apartments at The Rise, blending lifestyle and convenience.
