# Serotonin Centers Now Open in Jacksonville’s Ortega Neighborhood

Serotonin Anti-Aging Centers (Serotonin), the fast-growing anti-aging/longevity franchise, recently opened its first Jacksonville location at Ortega Park (4495 Roosevelt Blvd, Ste 403). The center and offers a variety of innovative treatments designed to promote health, vitality, and overall well-being.

 Serotonin Anti-Aging Centers are lauded for their concierge-style health optimization and anti-aging services including:

 * Medical weight loss
* Hormone replacement therapy (HRT)
* Sexual Wellness
* Body contouring (CoolSculpting/EmSculpt Neo)
* Medical aesthetics (Botox, Fillers, Microneedling with PRP, and Hydrafacials)
* IV drip therapy including NAD+
* Restorative services like red-light therapy and hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBOT)

 The new Serotonin in Ortega is locally owned by Darnice O’Leary and led by General Manager Mary Daniel. O’Leary brought Serotonin to the Ortega community to introduce a wellness concept that truly meets people where they are. She noticed many individuals feel overlooked by traditional options, whether they are deeply invested in their health or just getting started. Serotonin offers a more thoughtful approach, giving clients the tools, encouragement, and direction they need to take control of their wellness journey.

 “I saw a real need in Ortega for something more thoughtful and approachable,” said O’Leary. “Wellness looks different for everyone, and too often people feel left out of the process. Serotonin creates space for people to feel seen and supported, no matter where they are in their journey.”

 Each center staffs licensed medical practitioners and personal health coaches that work one-on-one with each member to design personalized prescriptive health plans. This empowers clients to take control of their health needs and work directly with their provider. Services focus on four main modalities – hormone restoration, aesthetic enhancement, weight control, and immunity – to help optimize the health and happiness of members, who are guided along by Serotonin’s team of experts at every juncture of their unique health journey.

 “When I was looking for a business opportunity, I knew I wanted to be involved in something that would help people feel better and boost their confidence, and Serotonin was the perfect fit,” said O’Leary. “Serotonin Centers’ innovative treatments and commitment to improving overall well-being made it an ideal choice for me, and I’m excited to bring this amazing service to the Ortega community.”

 Aiming to rapidly expand into new markets across the U.S., Serotonin Centers is positioned to lead the Medspa and anti-aging industries as demand in the sector continues to flourish. The opening of the brand’s new location in Ortega marks the next step in the brand’s continued expansion.
