# Sax on The Beach Hits The Right Note At Chichería Mexican Kitchen

New Friday Music Series Blends Coastal Flavors with Live Saxophone and Summer Patio Vibes

 WHAT: Chichería Mexican Kitchen has introduced Sax on the Beach, a new weekly live music series every Friday evening. Guests can enjoy the smooth sounds of Latin saxophonist Manny Quero from 6-9 p.m. while savoring Chichería’s coastal Baja-inspired Mexican cuisine.

 Chichería’s gluten free menu boasts fresh, seafood-forward flavors, including award-winning crispy fish tacos, snapper ceviche and handmade blue corn tortillas. This is the perfect excuse to relax on the patio, sip refreshing margaritas and take in the ideal summer vibe.

 WHEN: Fridays from 6-9 p.m.

 WHERE:

 Chichería Mexican Kitchen 202 Chattahoochee Row, Suite C Atlanta, GA 30318 404-343-0134 chicheriamx.com
