# TASTES of Thai SELECT Festival Brings Authentic Thai Flavors to 28 Liberty Plaza

28 Liberty Event Venue Halo Welcomes Thai SELECT Certified Restaurants, Cultural Performances, and Cooking Demonstrations

 On Thursday, September 18, 28 Liberty will welcome the TASTES of Thai SELECT festival in conjunction with Thai SELECT and the Department of International Trade Promotion. The event, organized through the 28 Liberty event venue Halo, will take place on 28 Liberty Street’s South Plaza from 11:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.

 The free-admission, all-day cultural and culinary celebration will feature 14 Thai-SELECT-certified restaurants and vendors, each recognized for authentic Thai flavors, high-quality ingredients, and traditional preparation methods. Attendees can enjoy freshly prepared Thai dishes, along with live cooking demonstrations, traditional Thai dance performances, and music.

 In addition to food tastings, the festival will feature four cultural booths: Team Thailand, the Tourism Authority of Thailand, Topthai (a Thai product line available on Amazon), and a Thai Traditional Costume booth. Visitors can try on traditional attire or take photos with dancers in costume while experiencing Thailand’s rich cultural heritage.

 The TASTES of Thai SELECT festival is part of the global Thai SELECT initiative, an official certification program awarded by the Ministry of Commerce of Thailand. The seal signifies authenticity, ensuring diners experience true Thai cuisine at restaurants worldwide.

 Spanning 30,000 square feet, Halo is designed for a wide array of events with unparalleled flexibility, accommodating up to 750 guests. The venue expands 28 Liberty’s existing events space, complementing the expansive plaza and Union Square Hospitality’s Manhatta, a restaurant and event space on the top floor of the property.

 Halo at 28 Liberty has emerged as a premier event venue in Lower Manhattan, showcasing world-class programming, including the UBS House of Craft x Dior exhibition and the 1-54 Contemporary African Art Fair.

 About Thai SELECT

 Thai SELECT is an official certification mark awarded by the Ministry of Commerce of Thailand to recognize and promote authentic Thai cuisine worldwide. The seal assures diners that a restaurant or food product offers true Thai flavors, high-quality ingredients, and traditional preparation methods. Whether at a fine-dining destination or a casual neighborhood eatery, Thai SELECT guides consumers toward Thai culinary experiences that reflect excellence in taste, authenticity, and hospitality. Explore Thai SELECT restaurants for Thai cuisine experiences via this website: https://www.thaiselect.com/restaurants. The event is organized by the Department Of International Trade Promotion Ministry Of Commerce, Thailand, and Velvet.Co, and NU Media.

 About 28 Liberty

 28 Liberty, at the heart of downtown NYC, is the go-to destination in Lower Manhattan for recreation and best-in-class dining. The office tower’s 2.5-acre Fosun Plaza hosts a variety of community events throughout the year, from food festivals to arts programs to educational experiences and more. The 38,000-square-foot penthouse is home to restaurateur Danny Meyer’s latest endeavor, Manhatta, a restaurant and bar with sweeping panoramic views of the NYC skyline, and featuring a multifunctional event space accommodating up to 800 guests at three simultaneous gatherings. Alamo Drafthouse Cinema, Court 16 and the upcoming Socceroof are among the tenants of the new 200,000-square-foot retail space at the base of the building. Whether exploring the latest cultural event or sipping cocktails at sunset from the 60th floor, 28 Liberty always offers something new to discover.
