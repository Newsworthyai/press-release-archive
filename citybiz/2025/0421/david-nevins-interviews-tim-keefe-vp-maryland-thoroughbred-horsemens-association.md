# David Nevins Interviews Tim Keefe, VP, Maryland Thoroughbred Horsemen’s Association

Tim Keefe is the Vice President of the Maryland Thoroughbred Horsemen’s Association (MTHA) and has been an integral figure in Maryland’s racing community since he began his training career in 1992. He served as President of the MTHA from 2014 to 2023, where he played a key role in advocating for horsemen and fostering relationships within the state’s racing industry. Throughout his tenure, Keefe helped launch several important initiatives, including the Horsemen’s Health System and the Beyond The Wire aftercare program.

 Keefe has also worked to strengthen the collaboration between the MTHA, the Maryland Jockey Club, and the Maryland Horse Breeders Association, helping to drive the revitalization of the state’s racing industry. He has been deeply involved in legislative efforts, including his work on the Racing and Community Development Act of 2020. Tim continues to advocate for the future of Maryland racing, focusing on sustainable growth and development of the industry.
