# Emburse Named to 2025 Inc. 5000 List of Fastest-Growing Private Companies in the U.S.

Emburse, the company defining Expense Intelligence through AI-powered travel and spend orchestration, today announced its inclusion in the 2025 Inc. 5000, a prestigious list of the fastest-growing private companies in the United States. Emburse is ranked #485 in software companies. 2025 is the company’s third year ranking, demonstrating strong growth and momentum.

 The significant honor reinforces Emburse as a category leader and innovator in intelligent finance technology. Despite continued economic volatility and disruption across the enterprise software industry, the company has steadily grown its revenue by helping thousands of organizations to transform how they control and optimize business spend.

 “The inclusion of Emburse in the 2025 Inc. 5000 is a powerful recognition of our people, our values, and our progress as a company,” said Marne Martin, CEO of Emburse. “We’re not just growing—we’re scaling a bold vision to reimagine how finance teams operate. From embedded AI technology to predictive automation, our platform empowers organizations to act in real-time and proactively control spend with deep insight. This recognition belongs to every Emburse employee working hard to deliver tremendous value for our customers every day.”

 The company’s return to the Inc. 5000 reflects a strategic effort to define and lead a new category: Expense Intelligence. Unlike legacy expense management tools, Emburse delivers an intelligent, unified platform that combines embedded AI, intuitive user experiences, and strategic visibility into spend. Through smarter decision-making, finance is transformed from a cost center into a growth enabler that moves the business forward.

 Recognition as an Inc. 5000 company reinforces several of Emburse’s core strengths:

 * A mission-driven culture that attracts and retains top talent
* A future-ready platform grounded in AI and actionable insights
* A loyal and growing customer base across industries and geographies
* A commitment to making finance smarter, faster, and more human

 About Emburse

 Emburse delivers Expense Intelligence—transforming reactive expense management into forward-thinking financial confidence. Expense Intelligence harnesses AI to orchestrate corporate spend across travel booking, procurement, reimbursements, and payments, embedding dynamic policy controls and predictive insights directly into workflows. This real-time approach empowers organizations with the agility to adapt, control risks, and strategically optimize spend.

 Trusted globally by more than 12 million finance leaders, travel managers, and professionals, Emburse serves over 20,000 organizations in 120 countries—including Global 2000 enterprises, SMBs, public sector agencies, and nonprofits.

 By proactively managing and accurately validating spend, Emburse ensures robust financial governance, enhanced compliance, and unsurpassed visibility into spend behaviors—all while dramatically streamlining the process for every employee.

 At Emburse, Expense Intelligence is more than a feature—it’s a framework for transformation, reshaping the role of finance teams from administrators to strategic drivers of organizational success.

 For more information, visit emburse.com or follow our social channels at @emburse.

 About the Inc. 5000

 The Inc. 5000 ranks the fastest-growing private companies in the United States based on verified revenue growth over a three-year period. Companies on the 2025 list are recognized for resilience, innovation, and leadership in their industries. Full results and company profiles can be found at www.inc.com/inc5000.
