# Say Cheese! Celebrate National Cheeseburger Day in South Florida

Whether the mood calls for a classic cheeseburger or a chef-inspired masterpiece, South Florida’s culinary scene is ready to deliver this National Cheeseburger Day.

 Burger lovers in South Florida have plenty to bite into on National Cheeseburger Day, on September 18.

 Two Palm Beach County hotspots – Hilton West Palm Beach and The Mall at Wellington Green – are inviting guests to indulge in one of America’s most beloved comfort foods, with a range of mouthwatering options that cater to both classic cravings and gourmet taste buds.

 At Hilton West Palm Beach, order the Smash Burger ($24-26) for lunch at Mezze or dinner at Galley. The popular handheld features a double beef patty with cheese, onion, pickles, and tomato aioli. Pair it with a cold beer from Michelin-starred Moody Tongue Brewery or a refreshing cocktail from Galley’s drink menu, such as the Golden Goose ($19), made with Hendrick’s Grand Cabaret Gin, St-Germain, cassis, gooseberry shrub, lemon, lime, and yuzu tonic.

 

 Hilton West Palm Beach is located at 600 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach. For information, call (561) 231-6000 or visit hiltonwestpalmbeach.com.

 California Pizza Kitchen at The Mall at Wellington Green is flipping up a West Coast Burger ($18.49), an adventurous blend of wagyu, chuck, and brisket beef, topped with melted American cheese, applewood smoked bacon, caramelized onions, tomatoes, pickles, lettuce, and homemade burger sauce, complemented by crispy smashed potatoes.

 California Pizza Kitchen at The Mall at Wellington Green is located at 10300 Forest Hill Blvd., Wellington. For information, call (561) 793-1601 or visit cpk.com/locations/california-pizza-kitchen-wellinton-green.
