# Tompkins Community Bank Appoints Paul Palladino as Business Development Officer

In an expansion of its retail banking team, Tompkins Community Bank (Tompkins) has welcomed Paul Palladino as business development officer for Syracuse, NY. Palladino, a 25-year veteran of the banking and finance industry, will cultivate relationships with individuals and businesses throughout Tompkins’ Central New York footprint and promote the company through community involvement.

 “I look forward to seeing Paul’s impact on our market,” said Helen Talty, community banking division manager at Tompkins. “His background in financial sales and business development, accompanied by his commitment to serving his community, makes him an ideal fit for our team.”

 Palladino previously worked as a sales manager and loan officer for Supreme Lending and Syracuse Securities. Palladino serves Second Chance Animal Shelter’s board of directors as a fundraising coordinator and volunteers with various animal shelters and veteran organizations in Onondaga County. He currently resides in Fayetteville, NY, with his wife.

 About Tompkins

 Founded in 1836, Tompkins Community Bank serves the Central, Western, and Hudson Valley regions of New York, and the Southeastern region of Pennsylvania. Through their unique local decision-making model, the bank offers personalized service and exceptional responsiveness while delivering a broad range of products and services for consumers and businesses. Insurance services are offered through Tompkins Insurance Agencies, and wealth management services are available through Tompkins Financial Advisors. Further information is available at www.tompkinsbank.com.
