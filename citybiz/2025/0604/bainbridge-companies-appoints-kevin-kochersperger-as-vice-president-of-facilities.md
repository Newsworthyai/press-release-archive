# Bainbridge Companies Appoints Kevin Kochersperger as Vice President of Facilities

The Bainbridge Companies (Bainbridge), a premier owner, developer and manager of multifamily communities, announces that Kevin Kochersperger has joined the leadership team as Vice President of Facilities.

 Kochersperger brings over 30 years of experience across both public and private sectors, as well as numerous esteemed qualifications, including Certified Professional Facilities Manager (CPFM), FEMA (CIMS/CERT) Coordinator, OSHA Safety Officer, Journeyman Electrician, and Stationary Engineer. He is also an active and dedicated member of the International Facility Management Association (IFMA) and the Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA).

 In his new position, he leads capital improvement initiatives, strategically oversees renovation projects, and manages the daily maintenance operations across the company’s diverse portfolio of luxury communities. His focus on operational efficiency, sustainability, and adherence to safety standards and regulatory compliance aims to elevate Bainbridge’s commitment to providing exceptional living environments.

 “Kevin’s arrival brings not only a significant depth of technical knowledge and proven leadership capabilities, but also a genuine passion for achieving excellence in facilities management,” stated Richard Schechter, Chairman, CEO, and Managing Principal of Bainbridge Companies. “We are confident that his expertise will be invaluable as we continue to elevate the quality and performance of our communities nationwide.”

 Beyond his professional pursuits, Kochersperger actively contributes to his community through volunteer work with organizations such as American Humane, Palm Beach Harvest, and Meals on Wheels.

 About Bainbridge Companies LLC:

 The Bainbridge Companies LLC (Bainbridge) is a multifamily real estate company that has developed and acquired more than 43,000 apartment homes representing more than $8 billion in transactions since its inception in 1997. With over 650 associates nationally, Bainbridge engages in every step of the real estate process, from development and construction, acquisition, and disposition to asset management and property management of multifamily real estate. Bainbridge is headquartered in Wellington, Fla., and has offices in Atlanta, Austin, Bethesda, Charlotte, Dallas, Orlando, Raleigh, and Tampa.
