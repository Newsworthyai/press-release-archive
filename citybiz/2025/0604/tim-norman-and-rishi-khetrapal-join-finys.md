# Tim Norman and Rishi Khetrapal Join Finys

TROY, Mich., June 3, 2025 /PRNewswire/ — Finys, a leading provider of insurance software solutions, today announced the appointment of Tim Norman as Chief Growth Officer and Rishi Khetrapal as Director of Finance. These key leadership additions are part of the company’s strategic plan to accelerate growth and scale operations.

 Following its investment from Serent Capital in November 2024, Finys has focused on expanding its capabilities, delivering even greater value to customers, and driving innovation across its core insurance suite. The additions of Norman and Khetrapal mark a significant milestone in that journey.

 Norman brings over 20 years of experience in insurance technology, with a deep background in property and casualty, as well as life and health insurance markets. As Chief Growth Officer, he will lead Finys’ go-to-market strategies and support the company’s expansion into new markets.

 Khetrapal joins Finys with more than a decade of experience in financial operations and planning. As Director of Finance, he will oversee financial strategy, helping to guide sustainable, long-term growth.

 “We’re thrilled to welcome Tim and Rishi to Finys,” said Kurt Diederich, CEO of Finys. “Their experience and leadership will be instrumental as we build on our momentum and continue delivering the high-quality service and innovation our customers expect.”

 About Finys, LLCFinys offers a modern enterprise platform, the Finys Suite, for property and casualty insurers. Finys includes components for core administration (policy, billing, and claims), business intelligence, portals, and more. Supporting personal, commercial, and specialty lines, the suite is used by insurers to reduce operational costs and improve time to market.
