# All Treats and No Tricks in Norman Love Confections’ 2025 Halloween Chocolate Collection

Prepare for a spine-tingling treat this Halloween with the launch of the 2025 Halloween Chocolate Collection, available starting online on Oct. 3 and in stores by Oct. 6. This exclusive collection features five enchanting flavors, each one carefully crafted to transport chocolate lovers to a haunting world of Halloween magic and spellbinding sweetness.

 From the tangy zing of Blood Orange to the nostalgic satisfaction of Candy Bar, this collection offers something for every craving. Box options include 10-piece, 16-piece, and 25-piece selections, providing the perfect size for every occasion. Whether gifting to friends, family or indulging yourself, the 2025 Halloween Collection is the perfect treat for spooky season.

 The 2025 Halloween Collection Flavors:

 * Blood Orange (dark chocolate): A dangerously decadent blend of sweet and tart blood orange, encased in dark chocolate ganache. Wicked Dark (dark chocolate): A rich, bittersweet 76% dark chocolate in a dark chocolate shell, speckled with purple and green accents. Candy Bar (milk chocolate): A nostalgic nod to a trick-or-treat favorite. Roasted peanut-infused caramel wrapped in milk chocolate. Raspberry Caramel (white chocolate): Silky raspberry-flavored caramel inside a white chocolate shell, hand-painted to resemble a creepy eyeball. Peanut Butter Cup (milk chocolate): A reimagined classic: roasted peanut butter and creamy milk chocolate combine for a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth experience.

 Box Size Pricing:

 * 10-piece box for $34
* 16-piece box for $49
* 25-piece box for $68

 For more information or to place an order, please visit NormanLoveConfections.com.

 About Norman Love Confections

 Founded in 2001, Norman Love Confections creates and distributes handcrafted artisanal chocolates from its corporate headquarters at 11380 Lindbergh Blvd. in Fort Myers, Florida. The award-winning Fort Myers-based chocolatier has been lauded more than a dozen times by a leading consumer ratings magazine, including recognition six times for producing the best ultra-premium chocolates in the nation. Forbes.com included Norman Love Confections BLACK line on its list of the nation’s top 10 truffles; The Huffington Post listed it among the six best U.S. destinations in its Chocolate Lover’s Travel Guide; and USA Today named Love as one of its top 10 entrepreneurs for 2014. Love was named the inaugural inductee of Dessert Professional Magazine’s Chocolatier Hall of Fame, partnered with local chefs to present a six-course meal at the James Beard House in New York and Norman Love Confections was ranked among the top 10 shops in the world for chocolate lovers by U.K. media outlet Daily Telegraph. In addition to new locations coming soon to Florida’s east coast, Norman Love Confections has Chocolate Salons in Fort Myers, Estero, Naples, Sarasota and Delray Beach. Norman Love Confections’ chocolates, gift baskets, sipping chocolate and specialty items also can be ordered online at www.normanloveconfections.com.
