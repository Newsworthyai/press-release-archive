# Evocative Secures Spot on Inc. 5000 List for Sixth Year in a Row

Evocative, a global provider of Internet infrastructure, today announces its inclusion on Inc. Magazine’s prestigious Inc. 5000 list of the United States’ fastest-growing private companies for 2025. Evocative ranks No. 3372, marking its sixth consecutive year of recognition.

 The Inc. 5000 list provides a comprehensive look at the most successful companies by growth within the United States’ private businesses. Companies are ranked according to percentage revenue growth from the past three years.

 “We are thrilled to once again be recognized on the esteemed Inc. 5000 list,” said Derek Garnier, CEO at Evocative. “This achievement reflects our team’s unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional digital infrastructure solutions and our continued focus on innovation in areas to support the accelerating requirements for AI workloads. As we navigate an increasingly complex technology landscape, this recognition validates our strategic approach to growth and our dedication to empowering businesses with the infrastructure they need.”

 The 2025 Inc. 5000 companies collectively generated significant economic impact. Evocative’s consistent presence on the list underscores the company’s sustained growth trajectory, both organic and inorganic, and market leadership in the Internet infrastructure sector.

 Over the past year, Evocative has continued to strengthen its market position through strategic initiatives and service expansions. Its launch of Megaport services and expansion in several key US markets signifies Evocative’s focus on continuously developing its customer-centric approach. The company’s global footprint of interconnected data centers and Points of Presence across North America, Europe, and Asia continues to support enterprise workloads, positioning it well in the evolving digital infrastructure market.

 To learn more about Evocative’s digital infrastructure solutions, visit www.evocative.com.

 About Evocative

 Evocative is a global leader in Internet infrastructure, providing enterprise-class data center, network, and bare metal solutions that enable exceptional digital experiences. Evocative’s global footprint includes interconnected data centers and Points of Presence across strategically located metros in North America, Europe, and Asia regions. Through organic growth and strategic acquisition, the company continues to expand its presence to power enterprise workloads at the edge. Evocative is dedicated to helping drive digital businesses forward, now and in the decades to come.

 To learn more about Evocative’s digital infrastructure solutions, visit www.evocative.com.
