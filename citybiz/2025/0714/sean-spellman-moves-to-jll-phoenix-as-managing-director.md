# Sean Spellman Moves to JLL Phoenix as Managing Director

The Phoenix office of JLL has announced the addition of Sean Spellman as Managing Director. An Arizona native, Spellman brings 18 years of office leasing expertise to the company’s award-winning Phoenix Agency Leasing team.

 “Sean’s collaborative style and results-driven mindset make him a powerful resource for our clients and the market,” said JLL Executive Managing Director and Phoenix market lead Pat Williams. “He has long been very respected in our industry and we are excited to have him join us.”

 Spellman’s career spans leadership roles with the industry’s largest brokerage firms, where he regularly achieved top broker status and honed his skills in the marketing and leasing of institutional-quality metro Phoenix office properties.

 Spellman holds a bachelor’s degree in Finance from the University of Arizona and is an active member of NAIOP Arizona, the Men’s Art Council and Executive Council Charities.

 He joins a larger JLL Phoenix workforce employing more than 600 of the region’s most recognized industry experts. This includes specialists in office, industrial, retail, healthcare and data center brokerage, tenant representation, facility and investment management, capital markets, multifamily investments and development services, and related services within the real estate leasing, investment and management process.
