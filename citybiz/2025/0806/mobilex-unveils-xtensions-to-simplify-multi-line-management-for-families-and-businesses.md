# MobileX Unveils Xtensions to Simplify Multi-Line Management for Families and Businesses

MobileX, the most customizable wireless service designed to save consumers and businesses money, today introduced Xtensions, a powerful new feature that makes managing multiple lines easier than ever. Xtensions lets users seamlessly oversee and control numerous lines in the MobileX app, delivering greater convenience for families, businesses and anyone juggling more than one line.

 Xtensions offers a smarter, more transparent approach to managing multiple wireless lines. Users can easily monitor usage, switch plans, update payment methods and activate new lines – all from the MobileX app on their mobile phones. Each line benefits from MobileX’s personalized experience, with the flexibility to mix and match plans, including fully customized AI-powered options starting at $3.48/month, or unlimited data on one of the fastest 5G networks in America.

 “Xtensions isn’t your typical family or business plan. It’s full-service wireless management made simple,” said Peter Adderton, founder and CEO of MobileX. “Whether you’re keeping your household connected or managing a team, Xtensions gives you complete control at a fraction of the cost of most carriers. From adding a new line to adjusting data, everything is at your fingertips with no hassle and no surprises.”

 Key Features of Xtensions:

 * Centralized Control. Primary account holders can easily link existing MobileX phone and tablet lines or add new ones with a few taps, sending invitations directly through the app.
* Effortless Management. Update payment methods, monitor usage and adjust plan settings for each line in real-time – no need to log in and out of separate accounts.
* Simplified Onboarding. Activate new lines via eSIM or physical SIM card through the app – ideal for families or business teams.
* Smart Financial Visibility. Backed by MobileX’s AI technology, each line is optimized for the most cost-effective plan available.

 Xtensions is now available with the latest MobileX app update. New customers can get started by downloading MobileX on the Apple App Store and Google Play. MobileX SIM starter packs can also be found at over 3,700 Walmart stores and Walmart.com. For more information on MobileX and Terms and Conditions of Service, including its fair use policy, visit mymobilex.com.

 About MobileXHeadquartered in Orange County, California, MobileX is the world’s most customizable mobile carrier, delivering the ultimate in choice and cost control. MobileX is a unique service that uses artificial intelligence to predict how much data customers need, delivering a dramatic reduction in cost while ensuring reliable speed and service. MobileX was founded by Peter Adderton, who also founded both Boost Mobile and Digital Turbine. For more information, please visit mymobilex.com.
