# Let Flavor and Freedom Ring with 4th of July Weekend Dining in Atlanta

Atlanta’s Top Eateries Serve Food, Music and Fun all Weekend Long

 This Fourth of July weekend, let freedom (and flavor) ring. Whether craving a classic burger and beer, ready to dive into red, white and blue sweets or looking to spark some fun with games and DJ vibes, Atlanta’s favorite restaurants are serving up all the essentials for a festive and flavor-packed holiday. Here’s where to eat, drink and celebrate Independence Day in style.

 Westside

 * Your 3rd Spot is rolling out the red carpet for a four-day celebration dubbed “Red, White & Play,” running July 3-6. The 30,000-square-foot entertainment venue will offer chef-curated specials, festive cocktails and over 100 curated games—from bowling to arcade favorites—all included with a reservation and Experience Pass. The fun kicks off Thursday, July 3, with extended hours and DJ-driven energy during “We Celebrate the 3rd.” With extended hours on Friday, July 4, early guests can score free bowling during the Beat the Clock bowling bonus from 1-2 p.m., followed by an evening of eats, beats and non-stop action. All weekend, guests can enjoy a seven-course Taste & Explore menu crafted for the holiday, highlighting red, white and blue ingredients in each dish. View full details and book experiences online.

 Dunwoody

 * Steak & Grace invites guests to enjoy dinner over the long weekend in a setting that redefines the modern steakhouse experience. Open on July 4, the restaurant welcomes diners to enjoy a fresh take on the classic steakhouse with fire-cooked steaks, seafood, sandwiches and large salads. Make reservations via OpenTable.

 Multiple locations

 * At all HOBNOB Neighborhood Tavern locations, patriotic flavors take center stage on Friday, July 4, with two star-spangled specials: the Americana Burger & Beer combo for $17.76 (pictured below)—featuring an all-American burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, secret sauce and a HOBNOB house lager—and the Red, White & Blue waffle for $9.95, topped with strawberries, blueberries and whipped cream. At HOBNOB Peachtree City, guests can enjoy an afternoon patio party with live music and $5 beers, while HOBNOB Halcyon celebrates with live music Friday night. Reservations can be made online.

 * Marlow’s Tavern is offering Insiders a free Red, White and Blue burger when they spend $15 or more on July 4. This special burger features Gorgonzola cheese, caramelized onions and red peppers on a sesame brioche bun, served with Freedom fries. Guests can sign up online to become a Marlow’s Insider and enjoy this patriotic deal, along with a refreshing cocktail like the Blueberry Buck.
