# Wylie &amp; Rum Gets Loud and Proud with Drag Bingo on June 24

Guests are Invited to Celebrate Pride Month and Support Joining Hearts with Bingo, Beats, and Tropical Treats

 WHAT:

 Wylie & Rum lures guests to celebrate Pride Month with flair on Tuesday, June 24. From 7:30-10:00 p.m., participants will enjoy a lively Drag Bingo night hosted by the dazzling Ella/Saurus/Rex, with music by Brad Gibson of Neon Horror. The unforgettable night will be filled with high-energy performances, tropical cocktails, flavorful bites and nonstop pride-filled fun.

 A portion of the night’s proceeds and performer tips will benefit Joining Hearts, an Atlanta nonprofit supporting those living with HIV/AIDS.

 Guests are invited to show their pride, play to win and support a vital cause—all while enjoying a night of fierce entertainment and community celebration.

 

 WHEN:

 Tuesday, June 24, 2025

 7:30-10:00 p.m.

 ABOUT WYLIE & RUM

 Named for its location near the corner of Moreland Avenue and Wylie Street in Reynoldstown and for its signature rum-based drink menu, Wylie & Rum serves an “unapologetically inauthentic” take on Caribbean cuisine informed by a melding of Jamaican, Cuban, European and other influences. Founded by longtime Atlanta restaurateur Tony Lewis (co-founder of the popular Bridgetown Grill) and his son David, Wylie & Rum boasts a modern, casual vibe accented with murals by Brian Steely and popular Puerto Rican artist Tommy Bronx. The restaurant is also available for catering and corporate and special events. Wylie & Rum is open from 11 a.m.-10 p.m. Sunday through Thursday and 11 a.m.-11 p.m. Friday and Saturday. For more information, please call 404-941-7877 or visit wylieandrum.com.
