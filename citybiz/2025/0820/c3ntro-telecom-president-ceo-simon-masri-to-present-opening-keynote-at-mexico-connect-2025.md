# C3ntro Telecom President &amp; CEO Simon Masri to Present Opening Keynote at Mexico Connect 2025

C3ntro Telecom (C3ntro), a leader in global telecommunications and fiber infrastructure provider, is set to participate in Mexico Connect 2025, a premier event for telecom and connectivity professionals in Mexico and the United States. Taking place September 10-11, 2025, in Mexico City, Mexico, the event brings together hundreds of leaders that are shaping the future of cross-border connectivity and more for innovative insights and valuable networking. Simon Masri, President and CEO of C3ntro, will kick off the event by speaking during the opening keynote.

 Mexico Connect offers an ideal platform for building lasting relationships that will shape the future of connectivity, uniting telecoms, ISPs, data centers, content providers, and government leaders to share their knowledge and create connections. Masri will speak on day one during the opening keynote, titled “Investing in the future of Mexico’s Telco Landscape.” This discussion will examine how investors such as private equity firms, financial institutions, and hyperscalers are fueling regional growth, as well as the role of collaborations across industries in opening new market opportunities and enhancing customer experiences. Masri and his fellow speakers will also discuss how digital infrastructure in Mexico is evolving and how innovation can continue to further growth opportunities in the country.

 WHAT: Investing in the future of Mexico’s Telco Landscape

 WHERE: Conference Room, The St. Regis, Mexico City, Mexico

 WHEN: Wednesday, September 10, 9:30 – 10:15 am CST

 WHO: Speakers:

 * Simon Masri, President & CEO, C3ntro Telecom
* Daniel Elguea, CEO, Fermaca Networks
* Gabriel Navarro, CEO, EVEN GROUP
* Armando de la Peña, CEO, Axtel
* Boris Velandia, Vice President & General Manager Wholesale, AT&T Mexico

 Chairperson:

 * Gloria Palavacini, Telecom Consultant, Independent

 About C3ntro Telecom

 C3ntro Telecom operates a state-of-the-art carrier-grade fiber network across Mexico’s major cities, offering dark fiber and wavelength services for low-latency, high-performance data center interconnectivity. Designed for hyperscalers, carriers, and cloud service providers, our AI enabled network features reliable, redundant infrastructure to support current and future needs. With over 30 years of experience, we also provide voice, SMS, virtual numbers, and traffic management services, making us a trusted partner for carriers, CPaaS providers, and enterprises.
