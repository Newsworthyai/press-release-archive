# MCS Named to 2025 Inc. 5000 List of America’s Fastest-Growing Companies

Inc., the leading media brand and playbook for the entrepreneurs and business leaders shaping our future, announced MCS, the national property services company founded in 1986,was included on its Inc. 5000 list, the most prestigious ranking of the fastest-growing private companies in America.

 This annual ranking spotlights the nation’s most successful independent businesses. Companies are ranked according to their revenue growth over a three-year period, and the list has become a hallmark of entrepreneurial achievement, spotlighting brands that are reshaping their industries and driving the economy forward.

 Based in Lewisville, TX, MCS delivers its suite of property preservation and related services to clients nationwide through a hybrid service model featuring over 25 self-performing markets, 30,000+ service partners and industry-leading technology. The company was recognized by Inc. for its numerous accomplishments over the past year including key acquisitions in support of its national growth program, an expansion of its regional self-performing network, and additional initiatives that allowed MCS to capture new market share.

 “Being named to the prestigious Inc. 5000 list for the first time is a testament to our rapid expansion, industry leadership, and the entire MCS team’s shared commitment to success and dedication to customer service,” notes Craig Torrance, CEO of MCS. “MCS is honored to be included on this celebrated ranking and we look forward to continuing our growth and supporting all facets of the commercial, residential rental, mortgage and property preservation sectors.”
