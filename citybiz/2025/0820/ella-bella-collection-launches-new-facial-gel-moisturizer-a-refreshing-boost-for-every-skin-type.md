# Ella Bella Collection Launches New Facial Gel Moisturizer – A Refreshing Boost for Every Skin Type

Ella Bella Collection, the beloved skincare destination located at The Mall at Wellington Green, proudly announces the launch of its newest innovation: the Ella Bella Facial Gel Moisturizer ($29, 1 oz.). Expertly crafted to hydrate, refresh, and prime the skin, this lightweight formula is designed to elevate any skincare routine.

 Infused with aloe vera, vitamin C, chamomile, calendula, and a blend of nourishing botanical oils – with rosehip taking center stage – the Facial Gel Moisturizer delivers powerful hydration and a natural glow. Its gentle, non-greasy texture makes it ideal for all skin types, offering both a soothing feel and a perfectly smooth base for makeup.

 “We created this moisturizer to be a daily essential,” says Leila Galbiatti Najjar, founder of Ella Bella Collection. “It’s clean, effective, and gives your skin that refreshed, radiant, and dewy look we all love – while also serving as the perfect primer.”

 This launch marks a continued commitment by Najjar to offer skincare that combines nature’s best ingredients with modern needs. Customers can discover the new Facial Gel Moisturizer in-store and online.

 Ella Bella Collection is located at The Mall at Wellington Green at 10300 Forest Hill Blvd., Wellington, Florida. For more information, visit ellabellacollection.com or call (561) 797-9570.

 About Ella Bella Collection Created by Leila Galbiatti Najjar, Ella Bella Collection is a curated skincare and wellness boutique offering high-quality, natural products designed to enhance beauty from the inside out. Located at The Mall at Wellington Green, Ella Bella is known for its personalized service and thoughtfully selected product line.
