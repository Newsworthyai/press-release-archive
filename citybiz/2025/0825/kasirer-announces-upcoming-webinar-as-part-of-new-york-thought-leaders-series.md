# Kasirer Announces Upcoming Webinar as Part of ‘New York Thought Leaders’ Series

As part of its New York Thought Leaders Series, Kasirer, New York City’s top lobbying and government relations firm, is hosting a free webinar titled: Get Ahead: Why Proactive Liquor Licensing Strategy Matters for Real Estate Success. The virtual session, hosted on September 15 at 10am, will bring together Suri Kasirer, President at the eponymous firm, and Max Bookman, Partner at Pesetsky & Bookman PC, to discuss how forward-looking licensing strategies can streamline development projects, support amenity planning, and reduce regulatory risks. Attendees will also receive supplementary materials, including regulatory resources and key takeaways.

 The event is geared towards:

 * Real estate developers, property owners, and project managers
* Restaurant, hotel, and hospitality professionals
* Real estate and compliance attorneys
* Investment professionals in real estate development

 Key topics of discussion include: Updates to New York’s liquor licensing laws, including temporary license changes; the new amenity space liquor license category and its project impacts; guidance on outdoor dining, private clubs, and amenity planning approvals; industry trends and case studies from major developments; and compliance challenges and best practices.

 Across New York City’s boroughs, Kasirer has helped navigate the complex liquor licensing landscape, supporting clients including Smorgasburg and The James New York, among many others. Interested attendees can register for the webinar here: https://lnkd.in/eY5B7xsx
