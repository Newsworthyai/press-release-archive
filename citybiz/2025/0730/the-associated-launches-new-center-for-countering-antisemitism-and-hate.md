# The Associated Launches New Center for Countering Antisemitism and Hate

The Associated: Jewish Federation of Baltimore launched its new Center for Countering Antisemitism and Hate. Building on years of work by The Associated and its agency, the Baltimore Jewish Council, this new initiative addresses the growing surge in antisemitism and focuses on education, security, coalition building and countering misinformation.
