# Danielle Delongchamp of Concorde Honored as 2025 CEO of the Year at Wealth Management Industry Awards

Concorde President and CEO Danielle Delongchamp has been named Chief Executive Officer of the Year at the 2025 Wealth Management Industry Awards (“The Wealthies”). The honor reflects Concorde’s commitment to equipping financial professionals with an integrated ecosystem of solutions that help them deliver meaningful results for clients.

 “I’m honored and humbled to receive this prestigious award from WealthManagement.com,” said Delongchamp. “More importantly, it affirms the strength of Concorde’s mission: providing financial professionals with the support, tools, and resources they need to serve their clients with excellence. This recognition is a testament to the dedication and expertise of our entire team, who work every day to empower advisors to achieve stronger outcomes for the investors they serve.”

 Now in its 11th year, the Wealth Management Industry Awards are the wealth management industry’s premier recognition program, celebrating innovation and excellence in support of financial advisor success. The 2025 program set a record with 1,209 nominations from more than 400 firms, with nearly 300 companies named finalists, including 88 first-time honorees.

 Winners were selected by a panel of independent judges comprised of industry leaders, consultants, and media representatives, who evaluated initiatives and executives on both quantitative measures (scope, scale, adoption, feature set) and qualitative measures (innovation, creativity, delivery).

 “On behalf of the Concorde board of directors, we congratulate Danielle on this well-deserved recognition,” said Michael Rollins, board spokesman. “Her vision and leadership have positioned Concorde for sustainable growth, while ensuring that our financial professionals receive the resources and guidance needed to thrive in a competitive marketplace. We are proud to support her as she continues to chart the firm’s future success.”

 About Concorde

 Concorde is a nationally recognized, full-service broker-dealer and SEC-Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) and insurance firm committed to supporting financial professionals and their clients. Through a thoughtfully integrated ecosystem of investment, advisory and insurance solutions, the firm provides the tools and expertise needed to help reach financial goals and objectives.

 Founded by entrepreneurs, the Concorde team is proud to serve a company made up of like-minded individuals driven by passion, purpose and a shared commitment to excellence. With a personalized approach, the firm offers a high-caliber experience tailored to each advisor’s and client’s unique goals. Concorde’s integrated broker-dealer and wealth management platforms are designed to maximize potential, providing customized support and forward-looking resources that allow financial professionals unlock their full potential.

 Headquartered in Michigan, Concorde Holdings, Inc. is the parent company of Concorde Investment Services, Concorde Asset Management and Concorde Insurance Agency. The firm serves clients across all 50 states with a clear focus on long-term partnership and purposeful progress. To learn more, visit concordeis.com.
