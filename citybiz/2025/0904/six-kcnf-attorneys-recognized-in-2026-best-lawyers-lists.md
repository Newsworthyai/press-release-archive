# Six KCNF Attorneys Recognized in 2026 Best Lawyers Lists

Kalijarvi, Chuzi, Newman & Fitch, P.C. (KCNF) is proud to announce that six of the firm’s attorneys have been selected by their peers for inclusion in the 2026 edition of The Best Lawyers in America® and Best Lawyers: Ones to Watch® in America.

 The attorneys included in the 2026 edition of The Best Lawyers in America are:

 * George M. Chuzi– Employment Law – Individuals
* Elaine L. Fitch– Civil Rights Law; Employment Law – Individuals; Litigation – Labor and Employment
* Mary E. Kuntz– Employment Law – Individuals
* Stephen B. Pershing– Employment Law – Individuals

 The attorneys included in the 2026 edition of Best Lawyers: Ones to Watch in America are:

 * Sacred B. Huff– Labor and Employment Law – Employee; Litigation – Labor and Employment
* Aaron H. Szot– Litigation – Labor and Employment

 Since it was first published in 1981, The Best Lawyers in America has become widely regarded as the definitive guide to legal excellence. The Best Lawyers® recognitions are based entirely on peer-review evaluations and highlight outstanding attorneys across more than 150 practice areas in 76 countries.

 About KCNF

 For 50 years, the lawyers of Kalijarvi, Chuzi, Newman & Fitch, P.C.
