# Kevin Yaryan Returns to SRS Real Estate Partners as Senior Vice President on the Capital Markets Team in Florida

SRS Real Estate Partners announces that Kevin Yaryan has rejoined the firm as Senior Vice President on the Capital Markets team in Florida. Yaryan brings more than 20 years of experience in Florida and California working across property types with a specialization in shopping centers and net lease properties.

 Throughout his career, Yaryan has served as lead broker on hundreds of transactions, navigating complex markets through pivotal economic cycles including the Great Recession, the COVID-19 pandemic, and recent inflationary conditions. Prior to rejoining SRS, he was a managing partner at Sun Realty Partners. His earlier career includes key roles at Equity Investment Services, Marcus & Millichap, and the Orlando office of SRS, where he worked until 2023.

 A graduate of the University of Southern California with a bachelor’s degree in business, Yaryan has developed a reputation for maximizing value for sellers while identifying strategic opportunities for buyers. His passion for connecting clients has enabled him to consistently achieve high transaction efficiency by understanding the unique intricacies of each deal and tailoring solutions to meet both buyer and seller objectives.

 “Kevin’s deep market knowledge, proven leadership, and unwavering dedication to client success make him an invaluable addition to our Capital Markets team,” said Patrick Nutt, Senior Managing Principal at SRS Capital Markets. “We’re thrilled to welcome him back to SRS and look forward to the impact he will have in Florida and beyond.”

 Yaryan’s return underscores SRS Real Estate Partners’ continued commitment to growing its national Capital Markets platform and strengthening its presence in Florida.
