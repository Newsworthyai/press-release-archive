# Quantica Infrastructure CEO John Chesser to Speak at Datacloud USA 2025

Quantica Infrastructure, LLC (Quantica), developer of integrated digital infrastructure combining renewable power, fiber conduit networks, and data center campuses, announced announced today that Chief Executive Officer John Chesser will participate in a panel discussion at Datacloud USA 2025, where the company will also serve as a Silver Sponsor. The event will take place September 16–17, 2025, in Austin, Texas, bringing together leaders from across the digital infrastructure and energy industries.

 Datacloud USA serves as a key forum for the data center and energy sectors to address how AI and hyperscale growth are reshaping infrastructure needs. Chesser will participate in the panel Solving the energy trilemma: availability, affordability, and stability to discuss technologies that support resilient and secure operations, the impact of AI workloads on backup power, advances in battery storage, and the potential of behind-the-meter and microgrid solutions.

 WHAT: Panel: Solving the energy trilemma: availability, affordability, and stability

 WHERE: Datacloud USA Stage | Austin Marriott Downtown | Austin, TX

 WHEN: Tuesday, September 16, 2025 | 2:30 p.m. – 3:15 p.m. CT

 WHO: Panelists:

 * John Chesser, Chief Executive Officer, Quantica Infrastructure
* Bob Kinscherf, Vice President, National Accounts, Constellation
* David Dorman, Director of Commercial Operations, APR Energy
* Thomas Seeber, Managing Director – Data Center Power Solution, INNIO Jenbacher
* Ihab Chaaban, Data Centers and Hydrogen Commercialization Director – GE Vernova

 Moderator:

 * Meghan Griffiths, Partner, Austin, Jackson Walker
