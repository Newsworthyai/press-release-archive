# Gilat Secures Over $7 Million Contract to Supply Transportable SATCOM Terminals to U.S. Department of Defense

Gilat Satellite Networks Ltd. (NASDAQ: GILT, TASE: GILT), a worldwide leader in satellite networking technology, solutions and services, announced today that Gilat DataPath was awarded a contract to provide Transportable SATCOM Terminals in support of the U.S. Department of Defense through a prime contractor. The award includes multiple units of Gilat DataPath’s popular DKET 3421 transportable terminals and support services totaling over $7 million. Deliveries are expected by the end of 2025.

 Gilat DataPath’s DKET 3421 is a fully field-proven, rugged solution to its customers’ needs for a high quality, reliable terminal. The DKET 3421 supports multi-carrier operations with a scalable modem architecture of up to 32 modems. Demand for transportable SATCOM solutions continues to grow as defense organizations seek rapid-deployment systems that ensure resilient, high-capacity communications in dynamic environments.

 “Gilat DataPath takes great pride in being a supplier of cutting-edge DKET solutions to the government and military community for almost 30 years,” said Nicole Robinson, President of Gilat DataPath. “This award reinforces Gilat DataPath’s ability to deliver mission-critical communications solutions to our customers’ stringent requirements.”
