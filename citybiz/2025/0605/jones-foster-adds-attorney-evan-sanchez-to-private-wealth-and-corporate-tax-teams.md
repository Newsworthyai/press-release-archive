# Jones Foster Adds Attorney Evan Sanchez to Private Wealth and Corporate &amp; Tax Teams

Jones Foster, a commercial and private client law firm with offices in West Palm Beach, Palm Beach, and Jupiter, Florida, is pleased to announce the addition of attorney Evan A. Sanchez to its Private Wealth, Wills, Trusts & Estates and Corporate & Tax teams. Mr. Sanchez will be based in the firm’s West Palm Beach office.

 “Evan is a talented attorney with an outstanding academic background, and we are pleased to have him join our team,” said William G. Smith, Vice Chair of the firm’s Corporate & Tax and Private Wealth Departments and Chair of the firm’s Associate Development Committee. “Our firm is committed to developing exceptional lawyers into future leaders who will preserve and foster the long-term relationships that we’ve built with our clients – particularly in complex areas such as wealth transfer and tax planning. I look forward to seeing Evan thrive here and make valuable contributions as he builds his practice.”

 Evan Sanchez will focus his practice on tax planning, estate planning, and corporate law. He will work with clients to develop customized strategies for the efficient preservation and transfer of assets. Mr. Sanchez will also counsel individuals and businesses on a wide range of corporate governance matters, including entity selection and formation and drafting shareholder agreements.

 Mr. Sanchez graduated cum laude with a Juris Doctor from Florida International University College of Law and went on to earn a Master of Laws (LL.M.) in Taxation from the Graduate Tax Program at the University of Florida Levin College of Law. During his law school studies, Mr. Sanchez was an Articles Editor on the executive board of the FIU Law Review and served as Treasurer of the Tax Law Society. He is also the recipient of a CALI Book Award in Seminar: Banking Law & Regulation. Mr. Sanchez gained valuable experience as a Private Client Services Intern at the West Palm Beach affiliate of a global advisory firm providing tax, valuation, financial, and consulting services to high-net-worth individuals and family offices.

 About Jones Foster

 Jones Foster is a full-service commercial and private client law firm headquartered in West Palm Beach, Florida, with offices in Palm Beach and Jupiter. Tracing its roots back to 1924, the firm has served as an integral part of South Florida’s growth and prosperity. Through a relentless pursuit of excellence, Jones Foster delivers original legal solutions that help clients, colleagues, and the community to move forward. A significant number of attorneys have received the designation of Board-Certified Specialist by The Florida Bar in their specific practice area. The firm’s practice groups include Complex Litigation & Dispute Resolution; Corporate & Tax; Land Use & Governmental; Private Wealth, Wills, Trusts & Estates; Real Estate; and Trust & Estate Litigation.
