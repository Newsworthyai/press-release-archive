# L&amp;A and Five Partners Recognized for Patent Practice in 2025 IAM Patent 1000: The World’s Leading Patent Professionals

The publication lauds the firm for client service and “a level of rigor and foresight that is second to none.”

 Lando & Anastasi, LLP (L&A) is pleased to announce that the firm has been ranked for the 14th consecutive year among some of the world’s largest and most prominent law firms in the 2025 IAM Patent 1000: The World’s Leading Patent Professionals.

 The publication, one of the most extensive guides to leading patent professionals and firms around the world, called out L&A’s excellence in patent law and client-focused approach. “[The firm is] highly responsive and consistently delivering high-quality work within tight deadlines, bringing a level of rigor and foresight that is second to none. They have a proactive, client-focused approach that ensures [clients] are always a step ahead of potential issues.”

 L&A is one of only a few firms, and the only IP boutique of our size, to be ranked for excellence in all three patent practice areas: transactions, prosecution, and litigation. In addition to the firm rankings, five firm partners also were named by IAM to this year’s list for their patent practices.

 

John N. Anastasi is recognized for his prosecution practice. A cornerstone of the firm, John “leverages his extensive network of industry professionals to provide top-tier guidance on patent strategy, ensuring essential protection for patrons’ innovations and helping them achieve their business goals.”

 Peter C. Lando is recognized for his prosecution and transactions practices. IAM notes, Peter comes “highly recommended” by clients and “is “renowned for his expertise in handling patent matters in complex technology areas. One of Peter’s standout qualities is his ability to provide insightful, well-reasoned guidance that is both clear and actionable.” The publication adds, Peter’s “precise and thorough approach instils confidence in his clientele. [His] extensive experience and deep understanding of patent law, combined with his pragmatic business sense, make him an indispensable partner.”

 Keith F. Noe is recognized for his prosecution practice. Keith’s approach to client service is appreciated by a range of clients, including large multinationals and notable manufacturing companies. Clients benefit from Keith’s corporate leadership experience, and “his in-house tenure, ensuring he gets to grips with commercial concerns and provides practical, actionable advice.”

 Craig R. Smith is recognized for his litigation practice. In its stellar review, IAM writes, “For contentious matters, clients rely on Craig Smith, a tenacious advocate who draws on more than two decades of wisdom to make all the right strategic plays.”

 Thomas M. Sullivan is recognized for his prosecution practice. IAM points out that Tom’s extensive experience “marries deep technical expertise with qualified business acumen.” Tom also “provide[s] tailored, insightful counsel to a diverse clientele” which includes large multinational conglomerates and growing high tech companies.

 About IAM

 IAM, a trusted source of worldwide IP news and analysis, publishes the annual IAM Patent 1000: The World’s Leading Patent Professionals, identifying the top patent professionals in key jurisdictions around the globe. Their extensive research process is based on interviews with more than 1,800 attorneys in private practice and in-house counsel, together with client feedback. Only those identified by market sources for their exceptional skill sets and profound insights into patent matters are selected for the guide.

 About Lando & Anastasi, LLP

 Lando & Anastasi, LLP (L&A) partners with clients to protect their innovations through sophisticated and innovative intellectual property strategies related to patent, trademark, and copyright protection, licensing and associated transactions, counseling, trade secrets, and litigation.
