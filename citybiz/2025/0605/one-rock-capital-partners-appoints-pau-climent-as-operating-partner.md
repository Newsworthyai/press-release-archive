# One Rock Capital Partners Appoints Pau Climent as Operating Partner

NEW YORK–(BUSINESS WIRE)–One Rock Capital Partners, LLC, a value-oriented, operationally focused private equity firm, is pleased to announce the addition of Pau Climent to its team of Operating Partners. Mr. Climent has significant experience in creating and optimizing supply chains for consumer and industrial businesses.

 Mr. Climent joins One Rock’s Operating Partner team with over 20 years of experience working in businesses supplying chemicals, food & beverage and cosmetics & perfumery. Most recently, Mr. Climent served as the Chief Supply Chain Officer of GPD Companies (“GPD”), a One Rock portfolio company. Prior to GPD, he held executive roles at consumer and industrial companies that were implementing supply chain and operations improvements, including Pepsico Europe and AkzoNobel. Earlier in his career, Mr. Climent held roles at Volkswagen Group, Altran and Accenture.

 “Through Pau’s work at GPD, we found him to be an experienced and action-oriented executive who drove positive change for One Rock’s portfolio company,” said One Rock Managing Partner Tony W. Lee. “We look forward to collaborating with Pau on more opportunities across our portfolio.”

 

“As a former executive of a One Rock portfolio company, I have witnessed the impact of One Rock’s Operating Partners in improving industrial businesses in multiple key functional areas,” said Mr. Climent. “As I join the One Rock Operating Partner team, I look forward to contributing to meaningful change across our portfolio company supply chains, with the goal of creating value for our companies and investors.”

 Working alongside Operating Partners has been a key part of One Rock’s strategy since its inception. Mr. Climent joins a growing team, which now includes 30 Operating Partners at One Rock.

 ABOUT ONE ROCK CAPITAL PARTNERS, LLC

 One Rock makes investments in companies with potential for growth and operational improvement using a rigorous approach that utilizes highly experienced Operating Partners to identify, acquire and enhance businesses in select industries. The involvement of these Operating Partners is designed to afford One Rock the ability to conduct due diligence and consummate acquisitions and investments in all types of situations, regardless of complexity. One Rock works collaboratively with company management and its Operating Partners to develop a comprehensive business plan focused on growing the enterprise and its profitability to enhance long-term value.
