# Compu Dynamics President &amp; CEO to Participate in DICE: Midwest Panel on Data Center Cooling

Compu Dynamics, a leading provider of data center infrastructure construction and operations services, announces today its participation in the Data Center Investment Conference & Expo (DICE): Midwest, which takes place September 25, 2025 in Chicago, Illinois. Steve Altizer, President and CEO at Compu Dynamics, will discuss advancements in data center cooling as AI transforms the industry.

 DICE Midwest by Bisnow will cover emerging development trends in Chicago and the Midwest, innovative cooling and power strategies for next-gen chips, facility efficiency techniques, flexible construction approaches, and more. Attendees will gain actionable insights while networking with industry leaders including CEOs, investors, engineers, and developers. Altizer will participate in the panel titled, “Data Center Cooling Innovations: Preparing for the AI Era,” which will explore innovations in data center cooling fueled by AI such as liquid-based solutions like immersion and direct-to-chip cooling. The session will also examine how emerging technologies like intelligent thermal control systems are enabling facilities to handle dense AI clusters more efficiently and reliably.

 About Compu Dynamics

 Compu Dynamics is one of North America’s leading data center solutions providers for colocation, hyperscale cloud, and AI-infrastructure organizations. Since 2002, the company has developed the widest breadth of solutions and services for mission critical data centers, including white space integration; liquid cooling solutions; modular/edge data center builds; and electrical, mechanical and low voltage installation services. Headquartered in Chantilly, Virginia, Compu Dynamics supports clients throughout the US and Canada, including hot markets such as Atlanta, Dallas, Phoenix, Portland, Chicago, Denver, Salt Lake City, Richmond, Raleigh, Columbus, Toronto, and Montreal.
