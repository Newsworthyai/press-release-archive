# Pep Boys Named to Forbes List of America’s Best-In-State Employers 2025 in Pennsylvania

Pep Boys, a leading U.S. automotive service provider, has been named to the Forbes list of America’s Best-In-State Employers 2025, ranking No. 83 in Pennsylvania. The annual list, compiled in partnership with Statista, recognizes companies that stand out for employee satisfaction and workplace culture. View the full list here.

 To identify the top employers, Forbes and Statista conducted an independent survey of more than 160,000 U.S. employees working at companies with at least 500 employees. The ranking considers over 2 million employer evaluations, with feedback coming directly from employees as well as the public.

 “We are honored to be recognized among Pennsylvania’s best employers,” said Joe Auriemma, CEO of Pep Boys. “This recognition is a reflection of the passion and dedication of our team members, who keep our customers moving every day. Creating a workplace where our employees can grow, feel valued and thrive remains central to who we are.”

 Pep Boys was founded in Philadelphia in 1921 and has proudly called the city home for more than a century. Today, the company continues to invest in its people through career development, competitive benefits and a strong culture of teamwork, while serving customers at locations across Pennsylvania and the U.S.

 About Pep Boys

 Pep Boys is a trusted leader in automotive services, dedicated to keeping drivers on the road with reliable maintenance and repair solutions. With over 800 locations across the U.S. and Puerto Rico, our team of skilled professionals, including many ASE-certified technicians, serves millions of customers each year, ranging from everyday drivers to commercial fleets.

 Founded in 1921 by Navy veterans Manny, Moe and Jack, Pep Boys is built on a foundation of passion, trust and safety. More than a century later, we continue to honor that legacy by providing expert care, exceptional service and a strong commitment to the communities we serve.
