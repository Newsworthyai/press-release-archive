# Big Dawg Party Rentals Named to Inc. 5000 and Inc. Best Workplaces 2025

BROOKLYN, N.Y.–(BUSINESS WIRE)–Big Dawg Party Rentals, a leading full-service event rental company headquartered in Brooklyn, today announced it has been named to both the Inc. 5000 list of fastest-growing private companies in America and Inc. Best Workplaces 2025.

 The dual recognition underscores Big Dawg’s momentum in the rental sector — combining rapid growth with an employee-first culture that fosters innovation, loyalty, and operational excellence.

 “Being named to both the Inc. 5000 and Inc. Best Workplaces lists this year is a tremendous honor,” said Brendan Quinlan, founder and CEO of Big Dawg Party Rentals. “We’re proud to support Fortune 500 companies, global brands, and major sporting events with world-class service — but what truly sets us apart is the culture we’ve built within our team. Growth means nothing without people.”

 The honors follow other recent achievements: Quinlan was recognized with the American Rental Association’s 12 Under 40 Award, spotlighting industry leaders across North America, Canada, Europe, and beyond, and Big Dawg secured a position in the Cuse 50 Awards, a worldwide recognition of the fastest-growing businesses led by Syracuse University alumni.

 Big Dawg Party Rentals provides equipment and services for thousands of events annually, ranging from weddings and cultural festivals to large-scale corporate activations and headline sporting events. With a reputation for reliability, scale, and client-first execution, the company continues to establish itself as one of the most trusted event partners in the industry.

 About Big Dawg Party Rentals

 Big Dawg Party Rentals is a full-service event rental company based in Brooklyn, NY. With an extensive inventory and a client roster that includes Fortune 500 companies, global brands, and leading venues, Big Dawg delivers rentals for hundreds of events every week. The company is recognized for its commitment to operational excellence, customer service, and workplace culture.
