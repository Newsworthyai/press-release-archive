# Rosedale Center Welcomes New Mix of Tenants

Rosedale Center, a 1,142,972-square-foot shopping center located in the heart of the Twin Cities, announced today that it has signed six new tenants slated to open this year.

 Several new retailers and restaurants who are scheduled to open at Rosedale Center in the coming weeks include Kendra Scott, J.Crew Factory, Johnston & Murphy, Lolli & Pops, Paris Baguette, BRKTHROUGH. Additionally, Dick’s Sporting Goods, Legendary Spice, & Boba X, Kukuri recently opened at the center.

 “With consumers’ increased appetite for new brands, experiences and evolving retailers, we are focused on providing additional choices to complement our existing tenants at Rosedale Center,” said Lisa Crain, Vice President Retail / General Manager at Rosedale Center. “The vision by our leasing, construction, operations, and development teams continues to ensure that the dynamic entertainment center will remain the Twin Cities’ premiere shopping destination for years to come.”

 Holly Rome, JLL Executive Vice President of National Retail Leasing, and Lane Walsh, JLL Vice President of National Retail Leasing lead the leasing effort at Rosedale Center and secured the leases.

 About Rosedale Center Rosedale Center is centrally located between the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul. Rosedale Center is a 1,142,972 square foot shopping center featuring over 150 retailers including AMC Theatres, JCPenney, Macy’s, Rose & Loon, and Von Maur. Rosedale Center attracts more than 12 million visitors annually and is rated a top shopping destination in Minnesota. For more information on stores, events, and programs, please visit rosedalecenter.com.
