# Guy Harvey Foundation’s Annual ‘Love the Blue’ Gala to Benefit Ocean Conservation and Marine Science Education Initiatives

Outstanding leaders in marine science education and environmental stewardship to be honored

 The Guy Harvey Foundation (GHF) will host its 17th annual “Love the Blue” gala, presented by Norwegian Cruise Line, beginning at 6 p.m. on Wednesday, October 29 at the Lauderdale Yacht Club, 1725 SE 12th Street in Fort Lauderdale. Proceeds from Love the Blue benefit the Foundation’s ocean research and education initiatives.

 Guests will enjoy a cocktail reception, dinner, and silent and live auctions featuring original Guy Harvey artwork, exclusive trips and unique gift items. In addition, the Foundation will pay homage to the 50th anniversary of the blockbuster film, Jaws, which was adapted from author Peter Benchley’s novel of the same name, and to this day still sparks global awareness of the ocean’s top predator. Benchley’s wife, renowned marine and environmental conservation advocate Wendy Benchley, will be the keynote speaker, sharing her insights and experiences in continuing her late husband’s legacy of ocean stewardship.

 World-renowned artist, scientist, conservationist and GHF Founder and Chair Emeritus, Dr. Guy Harvey, will join his daughter, Foundation CEO Jessica Harvey, in sharing captivating stories of their recent ocean explorations and highlighting a year that was marked by groundbreaking research and transformative education initiatives.

 “It is always so inspiring to host this event and celebrate with a community that supports our vital efforts in preserving our oceans and educating the next generation of ocean stewards,” said Jessica Harvey. “With the continued support of our new and longtime partners, donors and friends, we are driving positive change and creating a brighter future for our oceans and all who depend on them.”

 Each year, the Foundation builds on Dr. Harvey’s decades of scientific expertise to enhance its marine science curriculum for elementary, middle and high school students. By providing field trip grants to go out in nature, the Foundation helps educators bring marine and environmental science to life for their students as they explore nature together and students have the chance to actively engage with real-world case studies of environmental challenges and conservation successes from around the globe.

 “The health of our oceans depends on bold action and collaboration,” said Dr. Harvey. “We are incredibly grateful to everyone whose generosity and shared passion for protecting the marine environment allows us to continue and expand our work in education and conservation.”

 To learn more about the GHF’s educational, research and conservation initiatives, or to get involved, visit www.GuyHarveyFoundation.org. Follow the Foundation on Facebook, Instagram and X.com at @GuyHarveyOcean.

 About Guy Harvey: Guy Harvey is a world-renowned marine wildlife artist, scientist, diver, angler, conservationist and explorer, fiercely devoted to his family and his love of the sea. His childhood passion for the ocean and its living creatures not only inspired him to draw but fueled a burning interest that prompted a formal education in marine science and later, earned his Ph.D. from the University of the West Indies. Though he gave up a budding career as a marine biologist for that of a highly acclaimed artist, Dr. Harvey has continued his relentless pursuit to unravel the mysteries of the sea, traveling the world to better understand the habits and habitats of the marine wildlife he paints. The Guy Harvey brand brings the undersea world into everyday life through various licensed products including apparel, artwork, home goods and gift items that can be found in various retailers within the US, Caribbean and Central America. With more than one million followers on social media, the Guy Harvey brand has a wide demographic base of outdoor and water enthusiasts and helps benefit marine conservation through the research and education work of the Guy Harvey Foundation, which receives a portion of all proceeds from officially licensed Guy Harvey apparel and merchandise. To learn more about Guy Harvey apparel and merchandise, please visit www.guyharvey.com.

 About the Guy Harvey Foundation: With a focused mission to better understand and conserve the ocean environment, the Guy Harvey Foundation (GHF) collaborates with local, national and international organizations to conduct scientific research and provides funding to affiliated researchers who share this objective The GHF also develops and hosts cutting-edge educational programs that help educators to foster the next era of marine conservationists, ensuring that future generations can enjoy and benefit from a properly balanced ocean ecosystem. www.GuyHarveyFoundation.org.
