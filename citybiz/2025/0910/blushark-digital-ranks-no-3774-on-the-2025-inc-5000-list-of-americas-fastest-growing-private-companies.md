# BluShark Digital Ranks No. 3774 on the 2025 Inc. 5000 List of America’s Fastest-Growing Private Companies

Inc. announced that BluShark Digital LLC ranks No. 3774 on its annual Inc. 5000 list, the most prestigious ranking of the fastest-growing private companies in the United States. BluShark also holds rank No. 27 in Washington, D.C., No. 233 in the Washington-Arlington-Alexandria metro area, and No. 329 in the advertising, marketing, and public relations category.

 The 2025 list recognizes companies that achieved significant revenue growth from 2021 to 2024. BluShark Digital reported a 99.47% increase during this period.

 “What started as a way to help my law firm stand out online has grown into a separate company that now helps hundreds of businesses across the nation expand their digital footprint,” said Seth Price, founder and CEO of BluShark Digital. “We’re proud to be recognized for our growth, but more importantly, we’re proud of the results we deliver for our clients every day.”

 Founded to help businesses enhance their digital presence, BluShark Digital offers services in local, organic, and paid search. The agency is known for its results-driven approach, using advanced SEO strategies, content development, link-building, and technical optimization to help firms grow. In 2024 alone, BluShark helped clients generate more than 500,000 new leads.

 This accomplishment marks a significant milestone for BluShark Digital, as the agency secures a spot on the Inc. 5000 list for the sixth consecutive year. While BluShark is best known for its work within the legal industry, it also partners with a diverse range of professional service firms across the country.

 This year’s Inc. 5000 honorees have shown notable resilience and innovation, with the top 500 companies achieving a median three-year growth rate of 1,552 percent and creating over 48,000 jobs. The full list is available at www.inc.com/inc5000.

 About BluShark Digital LLC BluShark Digital is a digital marketing agency that helps businesses grow through strategic search engine optimization and online visibility. Founded by attorney and entrepreneur Seth Price, the company applies proven marketing strategies to support professional service firms, particularly in the legal sector. Learn more at www.blusharkdigital.com.
