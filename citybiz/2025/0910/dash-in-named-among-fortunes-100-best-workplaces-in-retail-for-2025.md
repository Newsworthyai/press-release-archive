# Dash In Named Among Fortune’s 100 Best Workplaces in Retail for 2025

Dash In announced today it was recognized as one of Fortune’s 100 Best Workplaces in Retail, ranked #9 on the 2025 list. This marks the third consecutive year Dash In has earned a place alongside leading retail organizations, affirming the company’s commitment to building an exceptional workplace culture.

 “Being named a Fortune Best Workplace for a third year is a proud achievement that is a reflection of the everyday dedication of our team,” said Blackie Wills, President and Chief Executive Officer of Wills Group, parent company of Dash In. “Our commitment to our team members is foundational to the Dash In guest experience. By fostering a culture where every team member can thrive, we deliver outstanding experiences to our guests and the communities we serve.”

 Great Place to Work®, Fortune’s analytics partner, surveys employees from across the nation using their Trust Index to measure key factors that create outstanding workplace environments. Dash In’s recognition on this prestigious list underscores the company’s focus on trust in management, company culture, and professional development.

 Earlier this year, Dash In and Wills Group earned the Great Place to Work Certification for the fourth straight year, with 85% of employees reporting Dash In as a great place to work – well above industry averages.

 Fortune’s recognition of Dash In highlights many of the factors that differentiate Dash In: a people-first approach, continuous investment in professional development, and a dedication to community engagement and supporting local communities.

 To learn more about careers at Dash In and its parent company, Wills Group, please visit willsgroup.com/careers.

 About Dash In

 Dash In is on a mission to redefine convenience by elevating the experience. Serving customers at more than 57 locations throughout Delaware, Maryland, and Virginia, Dash In is passionate about quality, innovation, cleanliness, and community engagement.

 About Wills Group, Inc.

 Headquartered in La Plata, Maryland, Wills Group is Great Place to Work Certified and has over 300 retail locations across the Mid-Atlantic region, including Dash In, Splash In ECO Car Wash, and SMO Motor Fuels. A family-owned company since 1926 with expertise in convenience retailing, fuels marketing, and commercial real estate, the Wills Group prides itself on keeping guests’, team members’ and communities’ Lives in Motion. For more information about the Wills Group, visit willsgroup.com.
