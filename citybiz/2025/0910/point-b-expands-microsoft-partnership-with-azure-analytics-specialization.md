# Point B Expands Microsoft Partnership With Azure Analytics Specialization

SEATTLE–(BUSINESS WIRE)–Business and technology innovation firm Point B announced today that it has been recognized by Microsoft as a designated Solutions Partner for Data & AI (Azure) with a Specialization in Analytics on Microsoft Azure.

 Specializations are awarded to partners whose capabilities have been validated by Microsoft or a third-party auditor on their ability to meet Microsoft’s highest standards of service delivery in a specific solution area.

 “Point B’s recognition by Microsoft as a designated Solutions Partner for Data & AI and our specialization in Analytics on Microsoft Azure is based on its extensive experience and track record of customer success in planning, designing, building and implementing advanced analytics solutions on the Azure platform,” said Tory Tolton, Senior Vice President, Technology & Digital, Point B.

 The certification process evaluated Point B’s ability to help clients drive Azure adoption, implement and support advanced Azure data and AI solutions, and maintain a team of highly certified Microsoft technology experts.

 “This Specialization underscores Point B’s technical expertise in helping customers realize the full potential of their data using Microsoft Azure,” continued Tolton. “Our customers benefit because they get better analytics solutions with less risk, as they can rest assured that their data is handled securely.”

 To learn more about Point B and our strategic alliance with Microsoft, visit pointb.com.

 About Point B

 Point B is a business innovation firm that takes the guesswork out of transformation. Point B engineers customers’ futures by combining industry expertise and advanced technologies to help mid-market and enterprise organizations in North America reimagine their business and its processes to get ahead and stay ahead. Founded in 1995, Point B is based in Seattle with offices across the U.S.
