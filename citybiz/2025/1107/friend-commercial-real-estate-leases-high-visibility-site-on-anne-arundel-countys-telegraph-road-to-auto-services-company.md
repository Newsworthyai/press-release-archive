# Friend Commercial Real Estate Leases High-Visibility Site On Anne Arundel County’s Telegraph Road to Auto Services Company

Friend Commercial Real Estate, a commercial real estate advisory and brokerage firm headquartered in Millersville, MD, is pleased to announce the successful lease of the corner parcel at 8274 Telegraph Road in Odenton, Maryland.

 Matthew Antonis, SIOR of Friend Commercial Real Estate represented the landlord and procured the tenant, an auto services company.

 The property’s remaining 1.5 +/- acres is available for lease, offering flexible outdoor storage or contractor yard opportunities within the OTC-I zoning district, a 1,600-square-foot office, and excellent visibility and accessibility – given its proximity to Route 32 and the MARC Odenton station.

 For leasing information, please contact Matthew Antonis or Eugenia Antonis of Friend Commercial Real Estate at mantonis@friendre.com or eantonis@friendre.com.

 About Friend Commercial Real Estate

 Friend Commercial Real Estate is a full-service commercial real estate brokerage dedicated to helping clients buy, sell, lease, and manage properties throughout Maryland, Virginia, and Washington, D.C.

 Through its affiliate, Friend Property Management, the firm also offers tailored management solutions for property owners.
