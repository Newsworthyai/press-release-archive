# Hollywood Casino Perryville Celebrates 15 Years of Entertainment, Community, and Excitement

Hollywood Casino Perryville, a property of PENN Entertainment, is proud to announce a major milestone, its fifteenth anniversary of delivering top-tier entertainment, gaming, and community impact. Since opening its doors in September 2010, Hollywood Casino Perryville has become a cornerstone of entertainment in Northeastern Maryland.

 “Reaching 15 years is an extraordinary achievement that reflects the dedication of our passionate team and the loyalty of our guests,” said Amy Brennan, General Manager of Hollywood Casino Perryville.

 Over the past fifteen years, Hollywood Casino Perryville has made a meaningful impact across Cecil County and beyond—supporting local nonprofits, creating hundreds of jobs, and serving as a proud partner in dozens of community events and initiatives.

 

 “We’re more than just a casino—we’re part of the fabric of this community,” Brennan added. “This anniversary is a moment to thank our guests, employees, and partners for helping us reach this milestone. The best is ahead.”

 For more information on Hollywood Casino Perryville, visit www.HollywoodCasinoPerryville.com or follow us on social media.

 About Hollywood Casino Perryville

 A PENN Entertainment property, Hollywood Casino Perryville is the leading gaming and entertainment destination in Northeastern Maryland. Conveniently located off I-95 Exit 93 in Perryville, the casino is open 24/7 and features over 700 slot machines, live table games, a poker room, off-track betting, and the ESPN BET Sportsbook, complete with live tellers and 24-hour betting kiosks.
