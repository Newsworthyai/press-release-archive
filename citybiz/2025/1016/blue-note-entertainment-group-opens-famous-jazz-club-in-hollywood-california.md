# Blue Note Entertainment Group Opens Famous Jazz Club in Hollywood, California

Confirming strong demand for entertainment venues and attractions in Los Angeles, JLL announced that Blue Note Entertainment Group has opened its famous jazz club at The Dome Entertainment Center, which is home to the world-famous Cinerama Dome, located at 6372 Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood, California. This 8,558-square-foot venue features an A-room for the headliners and a B-room for developing artists, surprise jams and podcasts.

 Blue Note Entertainment Group is a multi-faceted entertainment company that owns, operates, licenses and/or programs Blue Note Jazz Clubs worldwide, including New York, NY; Tokyo, Japan; Milan, Italy; Waikiki, Hawaii; Beijing & Shanghai, China; Napa, California; and Rio de Janeiro & Sao Paulo, Brazil. The Blue Note features the biggest names in Traditional Jazz, Contemporary Jazz, Blues, R&B, Latin Jazz, Big Bands, Cabaret and other related genres of music, in an intimate 250 seat club environment.

 JLL’s Scott Burns and Liam Bredberg represented the landlord in the lease. Ira Spilky of Ira Spilky & Associates represented Blue Note Entertainment Group.

 “This lease is an excellent indicator of the strength of Hollywood and its entertainment draw,” said Liam Bredberg, JLL Associate. “Blue Note had a choice to open anywhere in the Los Angeles area but they chose this location in Hollywood.”

 According to JLL’s Q2 Los Angeles Retail Report, the market remains health with vacancy currently at 5.8 percent and very little construction.
