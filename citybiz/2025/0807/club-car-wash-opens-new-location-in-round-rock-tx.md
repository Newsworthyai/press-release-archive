# Club Car Wash Opens New Location in Round Rock, TX

Club Car Wash is proud to announce the grand opening of its newest Texas location at 1051 N A.W. Grimes Blvd, Round Rock, TX 78664.

 To mark the occasion, Club Car Wash is offering its premium $25 MVP Wash for just $1 for a limited time. All proceeds from the $1 washes will be donated to the Round Rock Serving Center, a local nonprofit organization that provides food, clothing, financial assistance, and community programs for families in crisis.

 The new Round Rock location features state-of-the-art wash technology, powerful vacuums, free microfiber towels, and Unlimited Wash Membership options, offering a fast, convenient, and consistent clean for every vehicle.

 “Every time we open a new location, our goal is to make an immediate and meaningful impact,” said Cory Via, Chief Operating Officer at Club Car Wash. “Round Rock is a community that gives generously, and we’re proud to do our part by supporting the incredible work of the Round Rock Serving Center.”

 To learn more, visit www.clubcarwash.com.
