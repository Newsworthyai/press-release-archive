# Jh Wall Paints and Precision Aerial Services Sign Leases at Business Park in Ranch Mission Viejo, California

Confirming demand for multi-tenant industrial space in Orange County, California, JLL announced today that it has secured two industrial leases at phase two of Los Patrones Business Park encompassing 12,922 square feet and located at 28600-28650 Airoso Street in the South Orange County submarket of Rancho Mission Viejo, California. JH Wall Paints signed a lease for 3,620 square feet with Precision Aerial Services Inc. signing for 2,030 square feet.

 JH Wall Paints, a lime wash paint company, will use the space for ecommerce distribution and administration functions. Precision Aerial Services, the premiere crane and aerial equipment service provider, will use the space for equipment storage.

 JLL’s Kris Smith and Louis Tomaselli represented the landlord, Los Patrones Business Park Partners II, LLC, in the leases. Phase two of Los Patrones Business Park offers move-in ready units ranging from 1,824 square feet to 5,702 square feet.

 “We continue to see strong demand at multi-tenant industrial facilities in Orange County and throughout Southern California,” said Kris Smith, JLL Vice President. “Both companies were attracted to Los Patrones Business Park because of its strategic location, secured facility and availability for immediate occupancy.”

 Los Patrones Business Park is ideally located with nearby access to Interstate 5 Freeway, CA 241 Toll Road and State Route 74 (Ortega Highway) which provide convenient access to neighboring communities. Developed in 2024, phase two of the business park offers 18-foot clear height, private and secured yard and loading area, and 3/1,000 parking.
