# Nason Yeager Gerson Harris &amp; Fumero Represents Unusual Machines, Inc. in $48.5 Million Registered Direct Offering

Nason Yeager Gerson Harris & Fumero, P.A. represented Unusual Machines, in the company’s closing of its $48.5 million registered direct offering of 5,000,000 shares of common stock at a purchase price of $9.70 per share which closed on July 15, 2025. The Orlando-based manufacturer and distributor of drone parts intends to use the net proceeds from the offering to purchase drone motor manufacturing equipment and for general corporate purposes, including working capital.

 The Nason Yeager team was led by shareholder Michael Harris and included attorneys Ed Schauder and Constantine Christakis of the firm’s Securities practice group. They advised Unusual Machines on all aspects of the transaction. This included the preparation and filing of the registration statement on Form S-3 and the prospectus supplement with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), as well as drafting and negotiating transaction documentation, and providing guidance on regulatory and corporate governance matters.

 This latest transaction builds on Nason Yeager’s ongoing representation of Unusual Machines in securities and corporate matters. Unusual Machines is a U.S.-based manufacturer and distributor of drone components and systems, with a growing portfolio that includes Fat Shark, a market leader in FPV (first-person view) goggles, and Rotor Riot, a popular direct-to-consumer brand for drone enthusiasts. The company is positioning itself as a major component supplier within the evolving U.S. drone and global defense sectors.

 Nason Yeager’s Securities practice group have vast experience and proficiency in representing public and private companies, hedge funds, and family offices in a wide range of capital markets, regulatory, and M&A transactions. The firm has offices in Palm Beach Gardens, Fort Lauderdale, and Boca Raton.

 About Nason Yeager Gerson Harris & Fumero, P.A. Founded in 1960, Nason Yeager Gerson Harris & Fumero, P.A. is one of Florida’s oldest law firms, providing strategic legal counsel and representation to a wide array of public and private sector clients located throughout Florida and beyond. The firm’s attorneys have earned a reputation for their results-driven work, utilizing innovative legal strategies to help clients achieve their goals. For more information visit www.nasonyeager.com.

 About Unusual Machines, Inc. The Company manufactures and sells drone components and drones across a diversified brand portfolio, which includes Fat Shark, the leader in first-person view (“FPV”) ultra-low latency video goggles for drone pilots. Several of the Company’ products have been approved for and listed on the Defense Innovation Unit’s Blue Framework list. The Company also retails small, acrobatic FPV drones and equipment directly to consumers through the curated Rotor Riot ecommerce store. With a changing regulatory environment, The Company seeks to be a dominant component supplier to the fast-growing multi-billion-dollar US drone industry and the global defense business. According to Fact.MR, the global drone accessories market is currently valued at $17.5 billion and is set to top $115 billion by 2032.
