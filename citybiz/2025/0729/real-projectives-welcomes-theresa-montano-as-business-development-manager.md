# Real Projectives Welcomes Theresa Montano as Business Development Manager

Real Projectives, a leading real estate advisory and management firm, is pleased to announce that Theresa Montano has joined the company as Business Development Manager. Ms. Montano brings years of marketing and business development experience, with extensive expertise gained through her work with regional and statewide chambers of commerce in Maryland and beyond.

 “We are thrilled to welcome Theresa to the Real Projectives team,” said Jonathan Williams, CEO and Founder of Real Projectives. “Her proven track record of supporting economic prosperity and business growth through strategic partnerships makes her an invaluable addition as we continue to expand our reach and serve more clients across the United States.”

 In her new role, Ms. Montano will focus on client acquisition through strategic networking, relationship building, and partnership development. Her approach combines driving sales initiatives with educational outreach and innovative solutions that have consistently exceeded company goals and expectations throughout her career.

“What attracted me to this role is the opportunity to continue making communities here in Maryland, and around the country, better for work and living,” said Ms. Montano. “I don’t just see commercial buildings; I see jobs and people working. When we work on manufactured housing improvement projects, we help people find nice homes where it might have been impossible before.”

 Prior to joining Real Projectives, Ms. Montano built her foundation in the non-profit sector, where she dedicated herself to helping communities and businesses thrive. This experience shaped her commitment to finding opportunities to support others, making her transition into real estate a natural progression that aligns with her passion for contributing to business success.

 Throughout her career, Ms. Montano has demonstrated exceptional ability to foster business growth, magnify consumer engagement, and generate record-breaking revenue results. She is known for her integrity, strong work ethic, and continuous pursuit of real-world knowledge advancement.

 Ms. Montano will be expanding Real Projectives’ reach in Maryland and across the U.S. by building strategic relationships and proudly serving more clients. Her expertise and dedication will undoubtedly contribute to the company’s mission of providing trustworthy advice and management to investors, owners, and users of real estate.

 About Real Projectives

 Real Projectives® is a national real estate consulting firm with broad expertise and an agile approach that helps minimize headaches, protect reputations, and maximize returns.
