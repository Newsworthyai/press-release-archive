# Williams Jones Wealth Management Named to Barron’s 2025 Top 100 RIA Firms List

Williams Jones Wealth Management, LLC, an SEC-registered investment adviser and wealth management firm with offices in New York City and Palm Beach, Florida, has been named as one of Barron’s Top 100 RIA Firms for the fourth consecutive year. Barron’s rankings aim to highlight the nation’s best financial advisors. Williams Jones is ranked No. 50 by Barron’s for 2025.

 Williams Jones offers a wide range of services, including investment management, financial planning, retirement planning, estate planning, and philanthropic guidance.

 “We are honored to be on this prestigious list for the fourth year in a row,” said William P. Jones, Jr., Founder and Chairman of Williams Jones. “This recognition underscores our continuing commitment to our clients to deliver customized investment and financial planning advice.”

 Barron’s 10th annual ranking of independent advisory companies is based on assets under management, technology spending, succession planning, and other metrics. To view the complete 2025 list of Barron’s Top 100 RIA Firms, please visit barrons.com. The full methodology for the list can be found here.
