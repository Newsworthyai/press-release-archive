# CO/AI Launches Partner Perks Program to Drive AI Adoption and Innovation

Venice, California– February, 21st, 2025 – CO/AI, an AI-powered business solutions platform, is excited to announce the launch of its Partner Perks Program, bringing together some of the most innovative AI startups to provide exclusive discounts and special offers to businesses looking to integrate AI into their operations.

The CO/AI Partner Perks Program is designed to accelerate the adoption of cutting-edge AI solutions by connecting high-intent businesses with AI tools that streamline processes, automate workflows, and drive growth. By offering exclusive deals, this initiative helps businesses adopt AI solutions at a reduced cost while giving AI startups a direct channel to new customers.

CO/AI’s Founding Partner Perks Members Include:

Reportable Inc., Make, Zeni AI, AdCreative.ai, Octoparse, Refuel AI, Fathom, Tenable, Fireflies, Looka, Maven AGI, Quartile, Pangea, Todoist, Browse AI, Notion, Veed, CoLoop, Pictory, Parallel Health, GPT for Work, Litmaps, BIGVU, Vidnoz AI, SimplyPut AI, HumanX, DailyBot, NightCafe, SOUNDRAW, AI Mavericks, Unbounce, Brainforge, Gumloop, Perplexity, Xena Foresight, Whoop, and Byword.

﻿These companies provide cutting edge ai solutions to power marketing, automation, analytics, cybersecurity, content creation, and productivity creating a powerful ecosystem of AI tooling within CO/AI’s Partner Perks Program.

AI companies interested in joining the program can apply here: https://getcoai.com/partnernow

What is CO/AI?

At CO/AI, we love technology—but we love humans more.

AI is not just a passing trend; it is a transformational force that is already redefining industries, automating major work flows, and shifting how businesses operate. However, many organizations struggle to assess their AI-readiness, governments grapple with policy challenges, and media narratives often fail to capture both the risks and opportunities AI presents.

CO/AI was founded to provide clarity.

We help businesses:

* Identify and adopt AI tools that enhance efficiency and drive growth
* Improve AI literacy so leaders can make informed decisions
* Connect AI innovators with businesses that need cutting-edge solutions

"The future of AI isn't about replacing humans—it’s about enhancing what we can achieve together. Through our Partner Perks Program, we are making AI adoption easier and more accessible than ever before,” said Shane Robinson , CEO of CO/AI.
