# UGI to Begin Upgrades in Camp Hill Next Week

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Release Date: September 26, 2025

UGI Utilities, Inc. is scheduled to begin a natural gas main replacement project in Camp Hill on Wednesday, October 1. The system upgrades are part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service. The work is also an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.

Underground work will happen on the following streets:

* Scarsdale Drive between Rockaway Drive and Plainview Road
* Rockaway Drive, Cedarhurst Lane and Plainview Road Intersections at Scarsdale Drive

Construction is scheduled Monday through Friday from 7:00am to 5:00pm. Drivers, residents, and business owners may experience road closures, detours, and parking restrictions. There will be some interruptions to natural gas service. This will require UGI to have access to properties to inspect and relight equipment.

The installation of the new natural gas main is expected to be completed by the end of October. The completion date may change due to weather and working conditions. Final paving and restoration will occur later.

UGI encourages anyone interested in converting to natural gas to contact the company at 800-276-2722 or gasconversion@ugi.com.

UGI Utilities, Inc. is a natural gas and electric utility company that serves more than 760,000 customers. Additional information about UGI Utilities, Inc. is available at www.ugi.com.
