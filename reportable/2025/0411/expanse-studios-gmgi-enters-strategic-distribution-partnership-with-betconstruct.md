# Expanse Studios (GMGI) Enters Strategic Distribution Partnership with BetConstruct

Valletta, 11 April - 2025 - - Expanse Studios, the iGaming division of Golden Matrix Group Inc. (NASDAQ: GMGI), has announced a strategic partnership with BetConstruct, one of the globally leading iGaming aggregators and technology providers.

This integration will enable the distribution of Expanse Studios’ 55+ proprietary games—including slots, crash games, and table games—via BetConstruct’s extensive B2B network, reaching hundreds of operators across regulated markets worldwide. This includes advanced access to U.S.-based sweepstakes and social casino operators, where GMGI’s portfolio is already live in over 40 states.

* Accelerated Global Distribution: The partnership immediately scales Expanse’s distribution by tapping into BetConstruct’s well-established aggregation infrastructure, creating broader visibility and faster onboarding with global operators
* Strengthened U.S. Market Reach: The integration deepens Expanse Studios’ presence in the fast-growing U.S. social casino gaming space, supporting GMGI’s strategy to expand its domestic footprint without dependency on third-party content
* Improved Monetization and Margins: Leveraging proprietary content across BetConstruct’s B2B partner network supports cost efficiency and drives recurring, margin-accretive revenue for GMGI

"Joining forces with BetConstruct is a strategic move that aligns with our commitment to expanding market access and delivering high-quality gaming content to a wider audience," said Damjan Stamenkovic, CEO of Expanse Studios. "This partnership strengthens our presence in key regions and provides additional value to operators looking for innovative, player-focused gaming solutions."

Ruzanna Yolchyan, BetConstruct’s Head of Gaming Operations, expressed tremendous excitement about the partnership: “We are very pleased about partnering with Expanse Studios. Their impressive gaming content coupled with captivating design offers a truly immersive experience to players worldwide, which is what we always deliver with BetConstruct.”

About Expanse Studios

Expanse Studios, a subsidiary of Golden Matrix Group Inc. (NASDAQ: GMGI), is a leading B2B iGaming content provider, specializing in slots, crash games, table games, and gamified casino experiences. With a growing portfolio of 55+ proprietary games, Expanse Studios delivers premium gaming content for regulated markets, sweepstakes platforms, and social casinos worldwide. For more information, visit expanse.studio or contact contact@expanse.studio.

About BetConstruct

BetConstruct is a global award-winning technology and service provider for the online and land-based gaming industry. With over 20 years of experience, the company offers innovative products such as online and retail sportsbook, classic and live casino, poker, skill games, and a social gaming platform, all managed through its pioneering business management environment, SpringBME. BetConstruct holds licenses from reputable authorities, including the UK Gambling Commission and the Malta Gaming Authority, ensuring compliance and security across its operations. As part of the SoftConstruct family, BetConstruct is committed to removing barriers to industry entry and facilitating profitable expansion for its partners.
