# UGI Set to Begin Additional System Upgrades Next Week in Lancaster

UGI Utilities, Inc. is scheduled to begin natural gas main replacement projects in Lancaster on Monday, October 13. The system upgrades are part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service. The work is also an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.

Underground work will happen on the following streets:

* South Lime Street between East Vine Street and Howard Avenue
* Howard Avenue at South Lime Street
* Seymour Street between Fairview Avenue and South Prince Street
* Fairview Avenue between 138 Alley and Seymour Street
* South Prince Street Intersection at Seymour Street
* 700 Block of South Prince Street

Construction is scheduled Monday through Friday from 7:00am to 5:00pm. Drivers, residents, and business owners may experience road closures, detours, and parking restrictions. There will be some interruptions to natural gas service. This will require UGI to have access to properties to inspect and relight equipment.

Installation of the new natural gas mains is expected to be completed by the end of October. Temporary restoration of the area will occur immediately after the underground work is completed. Final paving will happen at a later date.

UGI encourages anyone interested in converting to natural gas to contact the company at 800-276-2722 or gasconversion@ugi.com.

UGI Utilities, Inc. is a natural gas and electric utility company that serves more than 760,000 customers. Additional information about UGI Utilities, Inc. is available at www.ugi.com.
