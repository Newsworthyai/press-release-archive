# 2025 Wilson Sonsini Entrepreneur & Investor Life Sciences Summit Opens Its Doors on Thursday, March 13, 2025 in Salt Lake City

SALT LAKE CITY March 11, 2025 – BioUtah, Utah's life sciences trade association, and the University of Utah Technology Transfer Office invite attendees to the 2025 Wilson Sonsini Entrepreneur & Investor Life Sciences Summit, March 13-14, 2025. This premier event brings together key players in Utah's thriving life sciences ecosystem, offering a unique platform for networking, collaboration, and investment opportunities.

For more information on the agenda and to register visit https://eilifesciencessummit.org/

Why Utah?

Utah's life sciences industry is experiencing remarkable growth, with a GDP of $22.6 billion and employing 180,000 Utahns. The state fosters a collaborative environment where research institutions, entrepreneurs, and investors come together to drive innovation and commercialization.

Why Investors Should Attend:

* Access to Promising Startups: Discover emerging life sciences companies with groundbreaking technologies and therapies.
* Gain Market Insights: Hear from industry experts and thought leaders on the latest trends and investment opportunities in life sciences.
* Network with Industry Leaders: Connect with entrepreneurs, investors, researchers, and service providers in Utah's vibrant life sciences community.

Why Companies Should Attend:

* Showcase Innovations: Present your company's latest technologies and products to a targeted audience of investors and potential partners.
* Gain Funding: Network with investors in attendance.
* Learn from Experts: Attend sessions led by experienced entrepreneurs, investors, and legal experts to gain valuable insights on building and growing a successful life sciences company.

Event Highlights:

* Keynote Speakers: Monica DiCenso of J.P. Morgan and Eb Bright of Stanford Mussallem Center for Biodesign and Exploramed will share their expertise on the investment landscape and healthcare entrepreneurship.
* Shark Tank-like Pitch Competition: Utah life sciences companies will pitch their innovations to a panel of experts. Presentations will be divided into categories such as medical devices, diagnostics, pharmaceuticals, therapeutics, biotechnology, and digital health.
* Networking: The Summit provides ample opportunities for attendees to connect and collaborate.
* Ski Day: A networking ski day and luncheon at Snowbird Ski Resort will follow the Summit.

Register Today:

Register now at www.eilifesciencessummit.org to secure your spot at this premier life sciences event.

 About BioUtahBioUtah is an independent 501(c)(6) trade association serving Utah’s life sciences industry. Its member companies reflect a broad spectrum of the industry with strengths in medical device manufacturing and services, research and testing, biotechnology, biopharmaceuticals, and diagnostics, amongst others; and are a key driver of Utah’s economy and advancing healthcare.About the University of Utah Technology Licensing OfficeThe University of Utah Technology Licensing Office (TLO) is dedicated to fostering innovation and creating value through effective technology transfer. As a leader in managing intellectual property, the TLO empowers researchers and inventors by providing the resources and support needed to transform groundbreaking ideas into real-world solutions. By facilitating the journey from discovery to commercialization, the TLO plays a critical role in driving economic growth and societal impact.
