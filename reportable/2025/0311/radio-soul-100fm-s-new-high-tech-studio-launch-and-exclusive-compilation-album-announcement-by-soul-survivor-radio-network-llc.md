# Radio Soul 100FM's New High-Tech Studio Launch and Exclusive Compilation Album  Announcement by Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC

Title: Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC Unveils New Studio Home for Radio Soul 100FM and Announces Compilation Album Release

Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC, the parent company of Radio Soul 100FM, has made significant strides in their commitment to delivering top-tier "Soul Funk & Jazz for the Generations"music to fans across the globe. The company is completing the construction of a broadcast/production studio in Las Vegas which will serve as the new home for KYRS-DB Radio Soul 100FM Las Vegas.!

The transition to the new studio is a strategic move that underlines the company's mission to consistently provide high-quality broadcast music, podcast hosting, album releases and more. This represents a significant milestone for the station, which has grown significantly since its inception. The new studio boasts modern broadcasting equipment to ensure seamless transmission of the soulful sounds that Radio Soul 100FM is known for.

In addition to the unveiling of the new studio, Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC has also announced that the station’s compilation album Radio Soul 100FM Presents The Artists Volume I, is now available on Bandcamp and on the stations website This album is a unique collection of 'Soul" from various talented artists who have graced the station's airwaves over the years. It is an embodiment of the station's rich history and dedication to promoting soul music.

Music lovers and fans of Radio Soul 100FM should anticipate a vibrant, soulful, and varied collection of songs in the compilation album. Featuring Bobby Caldwell, Raymond Barton, Kathy Kosins and others.The compilation promises to be an audio journey that rekindles the love for the soul funk and jazz, while also introducing listeners to new artists and sounds like Leon Neal and Sista Soul.

It is an exciting time for the station and its listeners, as they collectively contribute to the growth and preservation of the soul music genre.

Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC is confident that the move to the new studio home for Radio Soul 100FM and the release of the compilation album will serve as a catalyst for growth, as the station seeks to expand its reach and influence in the music industry. These developments are also a testament to the station's dedication to its listeners, who have been supportive of the station's journey.

The new studio home and the compilation album are significant achievements for Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC. They underline the company's commitment to providing high-quality soul music and supporting the artists who create it. As Radio Soul 100FM continues to grow, these developments will play a crucial role in shaping the station's future and its impact on the world of soul music.

In the coming weeks, listeners can expect a stellar line-up of programs and music from the new studio. They can also look forward to the official release of the compilation album, which promises to be a thrilling collection of soul music from some of the genre's finest artists.

This is a remarkable time for Soul Survivor Radio Network LLC, Radio Soul 100FM, and its large community of listeners. The new studio home and the compilation album represent exciting milestones that are set to enhance the quality of broadcast and the overall listener experience.

For more information about the new studio home of Radio Soul 100FM or to order the compilation album, please visit https://radiosoul100fm.com/.
