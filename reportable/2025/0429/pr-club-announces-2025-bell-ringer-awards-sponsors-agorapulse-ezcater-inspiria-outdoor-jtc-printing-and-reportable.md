# PR Club Announces 2025 Bell Ringer Awards Sponsors: Agorapulse, ezCater, Inspiria Outdoor, JTC Printing and Reportable

Boston, MA – April 29, 2025 –The PR Club, New England’s leading communications trade organization, today announces its sponsors for the 57th Annual Bell Ringer Awards on Tuesday, June 3, 2025 at the Museum of Science. Five industry-leading organizations, Agorapulse, ezCater, Inspiria Outdoor Advertising, JTC Printing, and Reportable, will support the annual event, which brings together PR, marketing and communications professionals from across New England to celebrate excellence in the field.

As sponsors, each organization brings a unique perspective and expertise to the event, which recognizes outstanding achievements in PR across categories like content marketing, media relations, social media, and communication campaigns.

“We are thrilled to have Agorapulse, ezCater, Inspiria Outdoor, Reportable, and JTC Printing join us as sponsors of the Bell Ringer Awards this year,” said Kaitlynn Cooney, President of the PR Club. “Their support demonstrates the continued strength of the PR industry despite significant changes to the media ecosystem in the past year.”

Agorapulse, a leading social media management platform, joins the roster of 2025 Bell Ringers sponsors to champion the evolving role of digital engagement in PR success.

“Today’s PR teams are on the front lines of social media, and we’re proud to equip them with the tools they need for communications campaigns,” said Rémi Malenfant, VP of Marketing at Agorapulse. “Sponsoring the Bell Ringer Awards is our way of celebrating the social-first strategies that drive reputation, relationships, and results.”

ezCater, the #1 food tech platform for workplaces in the US, will be one of the sponsors for this year’s Bell Ringer Awards. As a company that makes it easy for any organization to manage its food needs, ezCater provides 24/7 service and business-grade reliability to its clients in the communication industry, helping them celebrate achievements like those recognized at the Bell Ringer Awards.

Inspiria Outdoor Advertising, a top provider of out-of-home advertising, will also support the 2025 Bell Ringer Awards. Known for their innovative and effective outdoor media solutions, Inspiria Outdoor helps businesses increase brand recognition and exposure through strategic outdoor advertising placements anywhere in the United States– from airport lobbies to Times Square.

“Inspiria Outdoor is thrilled to sponsor and support this year's Bell Ringer Awards and to partner with these amazing PR firms and agencies throughout New England,” said Ronnie Ram, President and CEO of Inspiria Outdoor. “New England agencies are some of the top in the country and we look forward to establishing great relationships with these distinguished members and award winners.”

JTC Printing, a trusted provider of high-quality printing services, has also signed on as an in-kind sponsor of the Bell Ringer Awards. Specializing in a wide range of printing solutions,, JTC Printing helps businesses create standout materials that leave a lasting impression.

“PR professionals rely on high-quality printed materials to help convey their messages clearly and effectively,” said John Luz of JTC Printing. “We’re proud to sponsor the Bell Ringer Awards and support the work of these talented communicators by providing in-kind donations of printed materials for the event. Their success is our success, and we’re excited to help bring their amazing work to life.”

Reportable, a leader in business intelligence and communications tools for the digital age, will be an in-kind sponsor of the Bell Ringer Awards. Reportable’s cutting-edge media monitoring and PR metrics solutions use AI-based web crawling technology to help PR professionals measure communications campaigns, while its newsrooms and releases offer companies a media-rich solution to manage communications with external audiences.

“As a company built on the power of data, we know how important it is to track, measure, and understand the effectiveness of PR work,” said Karen Reynolds, Head of Sales at Reportable. “The Bell Ringer Awards are a great way to celebrate the most successful, data-driven PR campaigns of the year.”

For more information about the 2025 Bell Ringer Awards and our sponsors, visit https://prclub.org/bell-ringer-awards/

 About the PR ClubFounded in 1948, the PR Club (formerly the Publicity Club of New England) has been a cornerstone of the communications industry for decades. Through its events, resources, and Bell Ringer Awards, the organization fosters community and celebrates excellence in public relations, marketing, and communications.To learn more about the PR Club and the 57th Bell Ringer Awards, visit PRClub.org and follow the PR Club on LinkedIn, X, and Instagram.
