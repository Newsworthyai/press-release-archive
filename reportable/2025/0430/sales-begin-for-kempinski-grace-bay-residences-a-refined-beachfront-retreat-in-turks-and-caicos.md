# Sales Begin for Kempinski Grace Bay Residences, a Refined Beachfront Retreat in Turks and Caicos

Providenciales, Turks & Caicos Islands – April 30, 2025 – Kempinski Grace Bay, the much-anticipated luxury resort and branded residence being developed on a serene stretch of Grace Bay Beach, has officially launched its sales phase. This milestone signals a new era of refined beachfront experiences and vacation ownership opportunities in the Turks and Caicos Islands.

Thoughtfully envisioned by global developer JTRE and managed by Kempinski Hotels — Europe’s oldest luxury hospitality brand — the property comprises 119 private residences and four oceanfront villas. Nestled across 11 acres of unspoiled shoreline, a low-density design ensures both privacy and a sense of tranquility that honours the natural beauty of Grace Bay.

JTRE, the developer of Kempinski Grace Bay, is a leading European real estate firm with offices in Providenciales and an expanding international footprint. The project reflects JTRE’s long-term commitment to the islands and its focus on creating enduring, high-quality developments that respect both the community and environment.

“This is a meaningful moment for our team in Providenciales,” said Marek Baca, Chief Representative for the Americas at JTRE. “Kempinski Grace Bay offers the opportunity to be part of something very special – something only a few in the world will experience. As we open this new chapter, we invite future owners to experience the essence of Grace Bay in a way only Kempinski can provide.”

“Our presence on Grace Bay reflects Kempinski’s belief that true luxury lies in rarity and timelessness,” added Xavier Destribats, Chief Operating Officer EMEA and the Americas, Kempinski Group. “This is not simply a new resort. It is a continuation of a story that began in 1897—and an invitation to live each moment with grace, ease, and enduring beauty.”

A Rare Coastal Sanctuary

Kempinski Grace Bay blends the quiet elegance of European hospitality with the gentle rhythm of Caribbean life. Every detail — from architecture and interior finishes to the resort’s timeless amenities — is crafted to connect land, sea, and soul.

Future owners may choose from studio to five-bedroom residences, each offering expansive living spaces and a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor life. A limited number of six-bedroom beachfront villas complete the offering, each with direct beach access and sweeping ocean views.

The resort will also feature:

* Signature fine dining restaurant
* Beach club restaurant
* Exquisite 3,000 square foot wine cellar
* European spa and fully equipped fitness area with an ocean view
* On-site exclusive boutique
* Sports pavilion with padel and pickleball courts
* Kids club

Presented by Leading Local Real Estate Firms

Sales are exclusively represented by Christie’s International Real Estate Turks & Caicos and Engel & Völkers Turks and Caicos — firms known for their local expertise and global reach. Pre-construction pricing is available for a limited time, with private appointments now being scheduled.
