# UGI System Upgrades in Manheim Township to Impact Traffic on Pleasure Road

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Release Date: March 4, 2025

UGI Utilities, Inc. is scheduled to begin additional work on a gas main replacement project along Pleasure Road in Manheim Township, Lancaster County on March 10.

The work, which is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service, will extend along Pleasure Road from McGrann Boulevard to Crest Lane. During the expected month-long construction, Pleasure Road will be open one way for westbound traffic. East bound traffic will be detoured onto Grandview Boulevard and McGrann Boulevard. There may be some parking restrictions along Pleasure Road.

The work is scheduled Monday through Friday from 7:00am-4:00pm. Weather and working conditions may impact the completion date.

UGI Utilities, Inc. is a natural gas and electric utility company that serves more than 760,000 customers. Additional information about UGI Utilities, Inc. is available at www.ugi.com.
