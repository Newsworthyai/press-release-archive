# UGI System Upgrades Set to Get Underway in Manheim Borough

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Release Date: October 1, 2025

UGI Utilities, Inc. is scheduled to begin a natural gas main replacement project Thursday, October 2 in Manheim, Lancaster County. The system upgrades are part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service. The work is also an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.

Underground work will happen on the following streets:

* North Grant Street between West Colebrook Street and West High Street
* West Gramby Street between North Grant Street and North Clay Street
* North Clay Street between West Colebrook Street and West Gramby Street
* West Chestnut Street between North Graham Alley and North Grant Street

Construction is scheduled Monday through Friday from 7:00am to 5:00pm. Drivers, residents, and business owners may experience road closures, detours, and parking restrictions. There will be some interruptions to natural gas service. This will require UGI to have access to properties to inspect and relight equipment.

Following the installation of the new natural gas main, temporary restoration of the area will occur with final paving happening at a later date.

UGI encourages anyone interested in converting to natural gas to contact the company at 800-276-2722 or gasconversion@ugi.com.

UGI Utilities, Inc. is a natural gas and electric utility company that serves more than 760,000 customers. Additional information about UGI Utilities, Inc. is available at www.ugi.com.
