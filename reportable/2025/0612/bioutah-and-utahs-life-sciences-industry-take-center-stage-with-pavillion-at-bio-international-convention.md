# BioUtah and Utah’s Life Sciences Industry Take Center Stage with Pavillion at BIO International Convention

Salt Lake City, Utah – June 12, 2025 – BioUtah, Utah’s life sciences trade association, is proud to announce that President and CEO, Kelvyn Cullimore, along with key partners – the Governor’s Office of Economic Opportunity, World Trade Center Utah, the University of Utah Technology Licensing Office, Utah Inland Port Authority, The Point Utah, Utah Innovation Fund, Salt Lake City Dept of Economic Development, and Utah Health Entrepreneurs – will co-host the Utah Pavilion at booth #783 during the BIO International Convention, held June 16-19 in Boston, MA.

The Utah Pavilion will showcase the state's thriving life sciences sector - one of the fastest growing in the nation. Bringing together industry, government, and university tech transfer, the booth epitomizes the collaboration that uniquely defines Utah’s life sciences community. U.S. News and World Report recently named Utah the #1 state for business for the third year in a row.

“Utah is a hotbed for life sciences innovation, so it’s no surprise that we’ve gained national and international recognition,” said Cullimore. “In addition to the state’s natural beauty, national parks, and outdoor recreation, Utah boasts a highly educated workforce, pro-business environment, and a mature ecosystem essential to a robust bioscience hub.”

The Utah Pavilion offers attendees a valuable opportunity to:

* Learn about Utah's dynamic life sciences industry.
* Discover the diverse range of companies, research institutions, and resources that fuel that state’s bio ecosystem.
* Connect with leaders, innovators, key decision-makers, researchers, and entrepreneurs who are shaping the future of life sciences in the Beehive State.
* Explore potential partnerships and investment possibilities.

Whether you're a seasoned industry professional or a curious attendee, the Utah Pavilion welcomes you to experience the vibrancy of Utah's life sciences sector. Visit us at booth #783 and discover how Utah can propel your life sciences endeavors forward.

 For more information contact: Kelvyn Cullimore, President and CEO, BioUtah at kelvyn@bioutah.orgAbout BioUtahBioUtah is an independent 501(c)(6) trade association serving Utah’s life sciences industry. Its member companies reflect a broad spectrum with strengths in medical device manufacturing and services, research and testing, biotechnology, biopharmaceuticals, and diagnostics, amongst others; and are a key driver of Utah’s economy and advancing healthcare.
