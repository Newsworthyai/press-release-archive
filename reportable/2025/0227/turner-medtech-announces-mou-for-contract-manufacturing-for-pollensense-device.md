# Turner MedTech Announces MOU for Contract Manufacturing for PollenSense Device

OREM, Utah - February 27, 2025 - Turner MedTech, a leading medical device contract manufacturer, has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with PollenSense to manufacture its innovative pollen detection device.

The PollenSense device uses advanced sensor technology to provide real-time pollen monitoring, helping allergy sufferers manage their symptoms and improve their quality of life.

This partnership brings together a certified medical device manufacturing company with a groundbreaking solution to a common problem for millions of people.

“We are excited to partner with PollenSense to bring this much-needed product to market,” said Charles Jensen, VP/GM of Turner MedTech. “We are confident in our ability to manufacture the PollenSense device to the highest quality standards and are eager to contribute to its success.”

David Brent Noel, CEO of PollenSense, added, “Turner MedTech’s expertise in medical device manufacturing will be invaluable as we scale up production.”

The partnership is expected to create new jobs and drive economic growth in Utah, with units going into production for sales in Q2 2025.

 About Turner MedTechTurner MedTech is a leading contract manufacturer of medical devices headquartered in Orem, Utah. The company specializes in the design, development, and manufacture of complex medical devices, including implants, surgical instruments, and diagnostic equipment. Turner MedTech has a team of experienced engineers and scientists dedicated to developing innovative products that meet the needs of healthcare professionals and patients.Turner MedTech is an ISO 13485 certified contract manufacturer. The company provides comprehensive support throughout the product lifecycle, including design support, rapid prototyping, process development, and product assembly & testing. Their proven track record accelerates product development and manufacturing for medical, military, dental, and sports solutions. About PollenSensePollen Sense, headquartered in Provo, Utah, is a company that has revolutionized pollen monitoring with its innovative technology. They have developed automated sensors that provide real-time pollen and mold counts, offering a significant improvement over traditional manual methods. This technology allows for hyperlocal reporting, giving users detailed information about the pollen levels in their specific area.
