# BioUtah to Hold the 2025 Life Sciences Day on the Hill at the Utah State Capitol

SALT LAKE CITY -– January 29, 2025 – BioUtah, Utah’s trade association dedicated to the life sciences community, in partnership with the Utah Life Sciences Innovation Caucus and the Governor’s Office of Economic Opportunity, will hold the 2025 Life Sciences Day on the Hill January 30, 11:00 am to 2:00 pm MT, at the Utah State Capitol, in Salt Lake City.

Featuring exhibits from local life sciences companies in the capitol rotunda, the Life Sciences Day on Capitol Hill provides an exceptional opportunity for innovators to engage directly with state legislators and showcase Utah’s diverse bio industry, from medical devices and diagnostics to biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.

“The gathering turns the spotlight on our industry for a day of education and impactful conversations with policymakers,” said Kelvyn Cullimore, president and CEO of BioUtah. “As one of the fastest-growing healthcare innovation hubs in the nation, legislators will learn about an array of new technologies that are changing patients’ lives.”

With more than $22B in GDP and more than 1,600 companies employing 180,000 Utahns, Utah’s life sciences industry has become a major force in the state’s economy.

Its member companies reflect a broad spectrum of the industry with strengths in medical device manufacturing and services, research and testing, biotechnology, biopharmaceuticals, and diagnostics, amongst others; and are a key driver of Utah’s economy and advancing healthcare.
