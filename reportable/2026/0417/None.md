# UGI Makes “Easiest Utilities to Do Business With” List With High Customer Scores

for immediate Release - april 17, 2026

UGI Utilities (UGI) – Gas Division has been recognized as an “Easiest to Do Business With” utility driven by high scores for billing and payment experiences for customers. The recognition comes from Escalent’s Cogent Syndicated 2026 Utility Trusted Brand & Customer Engagement: Residential ™ study. Escalent is an AI-enabled market research and advisory firm with extensive energy, utility, and brand experience.
