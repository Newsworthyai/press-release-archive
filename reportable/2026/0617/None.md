# UGI System Upgrades Set to Start Next Week in Quakertown Borough and Richland Township

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - JUNE 17, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to begin system upgrades in Quakertown Borough and Richland Township on Monday, June 22, 2026. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
