# UGI Begins System Upgrade Project in Harrisburg and Swatara Township

for immediate release - april 6, 2026

UGI Utilities just began system upgrades in Harrisburg and Swatara Township. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
