# Scam Proof: New Book Empowers Readers to Recognize, Avoid, and Stop Modern Scams Practical Guide Offers Clear Strategies to Protect Personal Data, Finances, and Digital Identity

New York, NY Feb 9, 2026

As online scams, fraud, and impersonation schemes continue to rise across email, text messages, phone calls, and social media platforms, Scam Proof for Seniors, a new book by Dr. Juan Rodriguez, provides readers with practical, easy-to-understand tools to protect themselves and their loved ones from modern scams.

To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

Key Takeaways:* New book explains how modern scams operate and why they succeed across email, phone, text, and social platforms. A
* Guide offers practical, non-technical steps to identify scams, verify legitimacy, and respond if compromised. The book addresses a wide range of threats, including phishing emails, fake tech support calls, romance scams, and impostor government messages.
* Written for seniors and families, the book focuses on education, empowerment, and real-world scam scenarios.
