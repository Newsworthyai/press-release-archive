# Award-Winning Alberta Distillers Limited® Releases Two New Premium Expressions: Rare Batch Issue No. 2 and Alberta Premium Cask Strength 10 Year Old

TORONTO – February 9, 2026

Alberta Distillers Ltd., an award-winning leader in 100% prairie rye whisky, introduces two new limited-edition expressions, Alberta Premium Cask Strength 10 Year Old and Rare Batch Issue No. 2. The releases arrive in a milestone year for the Calgary distiller, which marks its 80th anniversary in 2026. Together, the new expressions capture Alberta Distillers Ltd.’s dedication to craft, with precision in production to strengthen the character of these products, and patience in aging.
