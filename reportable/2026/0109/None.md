# BioUtah to Attend the J.P. Morgan 44th Annual Healthcare Conference

SALT LAKE CITY – January 9, 2026

BioUtah, the trade association, common voice, and flag bearer for Utah’s life sciences community, today announced that it will participate in the J.P. Morgan 44th Annual Healthcare Conference, January 11-16, 2026, in San Francisco. BioUtah President and CEO, Kelvyn Cullimore, will attend on behalf of the association, representing the more than 1,600 life sciences companies across the state. Utah boasts strengths in medical device manufacturing, diagnostics, biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and digital health.
