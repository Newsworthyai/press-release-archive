# UGI Beginning Night Work in Easton on Sunday

for immediate release - April 10, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to do night work Sunday, April 12 through Friday, May 1 in Easton. The ongoing system upgrade project is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service.
