# First Orion Introduces SIP Redirect Call Authentication Integration to Expand Access to Trusted, Logo Branded Calling

April 30, 2026

NORTH LITTLE ROCK, Ark., April 30, 2026 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- First Orion, a leading provider of branded communications solutions, today introduced a new patented SIP redirect-based, end-to-end call authentication integration method, enabling enterprises to more easily and securely authenticate outbound calls while delivering trusted calls at scale.

To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

Key Takeaways:* First Orion today introduced a new patented SIP redirect-based, end-to-end call authentication integration method, enabling enterprises to more easily and securely authenticate outbound calls while delivering trusted calls at scale.
* Call Authentication, the technology that delivers INFORM® with Logo and SENTRY® Call Blocking, is now available through this new Call Authentication integration.
* This capability enables enterprises to verify that calls are valid as they reach carrier networks, branding only authenticated calls with INFORM or blocking unauthenticated calls with SENTRY.
