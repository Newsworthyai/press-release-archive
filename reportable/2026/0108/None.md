# First Orion Partners with Deutsche Telekom to Launch Branded Calling to Combat Call Fraud in Germany

NORTH LITTLE ROCK, Ark., Jan. 08, 2026

First Orion, a leading provider of branded calling solutions, today announced a new go-to-market collaboration with Deutsche Telekom to introduce branded calling services to enterprise customers across Germany. Through this initiative, Deutsche Telekom will offer First Orion’s branded calling solution to its business customers, enabling companies to display a verified brand identity directly on a customer’s mobile device when placing calls over Deutsche Telekom’s network.
