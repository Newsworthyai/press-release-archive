# Boldin Launches AI Planner Assistant, Signaling New Era of Technology-Enabled DIY Financial Planning

San Francisco, CA — February 25, 2026 —

Boldin, the financial confidence company, today announced the launch of its AI Planner Assistant. Users can ask Boldin’s AI any financial planning question and get personalized answers based on their own fully modeled, end-to-end financial plan. AI Planner Assistant is available in beta to all Boldin subscribers.

To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

Key Takeaways:* Boldin's AI Planner Assistant enables users to ask any financial planning question and get personalized answers based on their own fully modeled, end-to-end financial plan. AI Planner Assistant is available in beta to all Boldin subscribers.
* Early engagement with the AI Planner Assistant has been strong and highly interactive, as more than 15,000 users have submitted over 190,000 questions during beta testing.
* Boldin’s product team is evolving the Assistant daily — enhancing conversational clarity, strengthening financial domain accuracy, and deploying multi-agent validation systems that review and vet responses before they are delivered.
