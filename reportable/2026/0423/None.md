# New Insights from MarginBusiness Reveal Why Amazon Europe Expansion Falls Short for U.S. Brands

Boca Raton, Florida, April 23, 2026

MarginBusiness, an Amazon growth partner specializing in European marketplaces, today released new insights explaining why many U.S.-based Amazon brands struggle to replicate their domestic success across Europe, highlighting fundamental differences in customer behavior, search intent, and conversion dynamics.

To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

Key Takeaways:* MarginBusiness says many U.S.-based Amazon brands underperform in Europe because Amazon Europe comprises distinct national markets with different customer behavior, search intent, and conversion dynamics.
* The company reports that the biggest performance gap across European marketplaces is conversion, as literal translations and misaligned keywords drive impressions without sales and push advertising costs higher.
* Brands that succeed rebuild listings for each country, align keywords to real local purchasing intent, and run advertising that supports conversions rather than clicks.
