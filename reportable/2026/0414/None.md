# UGI Issues Another Scam Alert to Protect Customers as Reports of Rip-offs Continue

for immediate release - april 14, 2026

UGI Utilities, Inc. is again seeing an increase in scams tricking customers into making payments they shouldn’t make. In several recent cases, UGI customers were told by scammers they had to pay a fee to start service. Some of them lost hundreds of dollars.
