# Almabase Recognizes 50 Alumni Relations and Advancement Champions of 2025

San francisco, california, january 22, 2026

Almabase, a donor engagement platform for non-profits, has announced the winners of its second iteration of 50 Champions in Advancement, showcasing the best in the alumni relations and advancement field. The winners were vetted through a rigorous process from June through December 2025, and represent a variety of prestigious educational institutions across the world.

To view the full announcement, including downloadable images, bios, and more, click here.

Key Takeaways:* Almabase concluded AB50C 2025 by revealing the 50 best professionals in Advancement
* AB50C is a program that recognizes the impact made by advancement professionals around the world
* Champions hailed from prestigious institutions including Cornell, East Stroudsburg, and Erasmus University
