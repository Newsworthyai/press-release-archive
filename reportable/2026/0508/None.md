# UGI System Upgrades Set to Begin Next Week in Salem Township

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - MAY 8, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to begin a system upgrades project in Salem Township, Luzerne County on Monday, May 11, 2026. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
