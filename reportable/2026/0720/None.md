# UGI System Upgrades Beginning in Lyons Borough

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - JULY 20, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to begin system upgrades in Lyons Borough, Berks County on Monday, July 20, 2026. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
