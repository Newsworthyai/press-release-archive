# UGI Beginning System Upgrades Next Week in Mount Carmel

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - JULY 1, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to begin system upgrades in Mount Carmel on Monday, July 6, 2026. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
