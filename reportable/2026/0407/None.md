# Novelist Predicted US Repression

New Jersey

Barry H. Cohen, author of the science fiction trilogy, Soul Switchers began work on his series at the start of the first Trump administration. Part I of the three novels did not publish until 2022. However, Cohen claims he saw the early warning signs of coming repression right from the start.
