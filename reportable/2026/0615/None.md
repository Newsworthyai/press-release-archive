# UGI System Upgrades Project Now Underway in Sellersville

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE - JUNE 15, 2026

UGI Utilities just began system upgrades in Sellersville Borough, Bucks County. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
