# UGI Scheduled to Start System Upgrades in Lititz

for immediate release - march 31, 2026

UGI Utilities is scheduled to begin system upgrades in Lititz, Lancaster County on April 1. The work is part of UGI’s commitment to provide safe and reliable service and is an opportunity for some homes and businesses along the project route to convert to natural gas.
