# BOSTON REALTY ADVISORS MANAGES SALE OF 961 COMMONWEALTH AVENUE

BOSTON – Boston Realty Advisors, the largest independent commercial real estate brokerage in the Boston area, represented 961 Commonwealth Avenue LLC in the sale of the retail property at 961 Commonwealth Ave., home to Boston Halal Restaurant and Brigham & Women’s Hospital.

"We saw an incredible level of interest in this property, underscoring the strong demand for prime retail investment opportunities in Boston," said Joe Wagner, Managing Director of Boston Realty Advisors, who led the sale. "Our team excels at connecting clients with ideal buyers, and this sale is a testament to that expertise."

The property benefits from a fresh 15-year lease for Boston Halal restaurant at competitive terms, while Brigham and Women’s Hospital has been a stable tenant since 1993.

The transaction highlights Boston Realty Advisors’ capabilities in managing diverse retail property sales and reinforces the firm’s dedication to creating value-driven, client-focused solutions.

 ABOUT BOSTON REALTY ADVISORS Founded in 2001 by Jason S. Weissman, Boston Realty Advisors is a Boston-based, privately held, locally operated real estate firm. BRA is a leader in the middle markets, focused on investment sales, corporate real estate and retail. For more information, please go to www.bradvisors.com .
