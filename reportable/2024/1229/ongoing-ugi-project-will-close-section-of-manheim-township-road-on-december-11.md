# Ongoing UGI Project Will Close Section of Manheim Township Road on December 11

UGI Utilities, Inc. continues work on installing a new natural gas main in Manheim Township, Lancaster County and will have to close a section of Fountain Avenue to northbound traffic on Wednesday, December 11.

The one-day closure on Fountain Avenue will extend north from New Holland Avenue to Louise Avenue. Drivers who need to use Fountain Avenue’s southbound lane in this section will have to access it from Pleasure Road.

A message board will notify drivers of the detour. The lane closure is expected to last from 7am until 5pm.

UGI Utilities, Inc. is a natural gas and electric utility company that serves more than 740,000 customers. Additional information about UGI Utilities, Inc. is available at www.ugi.com.
